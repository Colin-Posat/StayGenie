HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical Atmosphere with Modern AmenitiesFors Hotel seamlessly blends Istanbul's rich history with contemporary comforts. Situated near iconic landmarks like the Blue Mosque and Grand Bazaar, this quaint hotel offers a unique cultural experience.Breathtaking Roof Terrace and Dining OptionsGuests can savor panoramic city views and the Sea of Marmara from the open roof terrace (available seasonally). The on-site restaurant serves delectable buffet breakfasts, afternoon teas, and dinners, while the bar lounge and terrace are perfect for unwinding with a drink.Compact Rooms with Essential FeaturesThe cozy rooms at Fors Hotel are thoughtfully designed, featuring room service, flat-screen TVs, minibars, and complimentary Wi-Fi. After exploring nearby attractions like St Sophia and the Archeological Museum, guests can rely on convenient services like transfers, car rentals, and a tour desk for a hassle-free stay.Book your stay at Fors Hotel now to immerse yourself in Istanbul's charm and convenience.
