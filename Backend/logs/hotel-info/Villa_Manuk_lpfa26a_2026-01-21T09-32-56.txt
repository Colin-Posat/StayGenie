HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Surrounded by NatureVilla Manuk offers a serene retreat amidst lush greenery, just a short distance from Sekumpul Waterfall. The outdoor swimming pool, fed by natural spring water, provides a chemical-free oasis for relaxation.Personalized ComfortEach room at Villa Manuk boasts a private terrace or balcony with stunning views of the pool, garden, and mountains. The en suite bathrooms are stocked with complimentary toiletries, and guests have access to a communal kitchen for added convenience.Convenient ServicesWhether arranging car rentals, organizing hiking excursions, or utilizing laundry facilities, the attentive staff at Villa Manuk ensures a seamless stay. Indulge in a culinary journey at Cenik Restaurant, offering a delightful array of Balinese and Indonesian delicacies.Book your stay at Villa Manuk for an unforgettable experience in the heart of nature.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The property requires a deposit payment to secure the booking. Payment can also be done via PayPal. Staff will contact guests directly for payment instructions.

Please note that the property will empty the pool for general cleaning once a week, therefore pool is closed for guests' use.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
No internet access available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 10 years old stay for Rp 150,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 11 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 3,000,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 154,883 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
