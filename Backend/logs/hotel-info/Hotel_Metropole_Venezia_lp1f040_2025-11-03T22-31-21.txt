HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Metropole Venezia offers 5-star luxury with allergy-free rooms, garden views, and free WiFi. Each room is elegantly furnished with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and some even feature a private terrace.Exceptional ServicesGuests can enjoy concierge services, a tour desk, and a 24-hour front desk for convenience. The hotel also provides room service, luggage storage, and currency exchange, ensuring a seamless stay for all visitors.Delightful Dining and Nearby AttractionsStart your day with a delicious breakfast offering continental, Italian, and American options. Explore popular attractions like Doge's Palace, Basilica San Marco, and La Fenice Theatre, all within walking distance. Conclude your day with a drink at the in-house bar, making your stay at Hotel Metropole Venezia truly unforgettable.Book your stay now at Hotel Metropole Venezia for a luxurious and memorable experience in Venice!

HOTEL POLICIES:

Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for € 200 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
