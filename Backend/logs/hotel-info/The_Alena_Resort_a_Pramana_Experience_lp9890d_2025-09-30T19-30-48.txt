HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsEach room at The Alena Resort is a sanctuary of comfort and style, featuring modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and plush four-poster beds. The en suite bathrooms are equipped with complimentary toiletries, ensuring a pampering stay for every guest.Relaxation and DiningIndulge in a culinary journey at the on-site restaurant or opt for private dining with 24-hour room service. Unwind with a rejuvenating massage at the spa center or soak up the sun by the outdoor pool. The resort's serene ambiance promises a truly relaxing getaway.Convenient ServicesWith complimentary WiFi and a free shuttle service to nearby attractions, The Alena Resort goes above and beyond to cater to its guests' needs. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 11 years old to 16 years old stay for Rp 600,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
