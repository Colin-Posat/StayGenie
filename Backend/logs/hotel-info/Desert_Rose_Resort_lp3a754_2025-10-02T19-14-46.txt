HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Las Vegas StripDesert Rose Resort offers a peaceful retreat just moments away from the vibrant Las Vegas Strip. Escape the casino atmosphere and relax in our oasis-like setting with a heated outdoor pool surrounded by palm trees.Modern Suites with Full KitchenOur contemporary suites are designed for comfort and convenience, featuring free WiFi, a private balcony, and a fully equipped kitchen. Enjoy your favorite shows on the TV with cable channels and brew a fresh cup of coffee with the in-room coffee machine.Amenities for Relaxation and ConvenienceIndulge in our whirlpool and stay active in the fitness center. Take advantage of the barbecue area, convenience store, and on-site laundry services. Join us at Ellington’s Bar and Grill for cocktails and delicious lighter fare in a laid-back atmosphere.Ready to experience the perfect blend of tranquility and excitement at Desert Rose Resort? Category Ratings:

• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally positive, although some guests noted issues with wait times and specific requests.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and accessibility to public transport were highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were comfortable overall, but some guests mentioned maintenance issues and outdated decor.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with some positive mentions of the gym and pool, but highlighted a lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received generally positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel and would recommend it to others.
