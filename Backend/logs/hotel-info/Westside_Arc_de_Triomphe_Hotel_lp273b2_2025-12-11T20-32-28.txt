HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationIndulge in the comfort of our air-conditioned rooms with stunning views of Paris. Enjoy free Wi-Fi and satellite channels on the LED TV. Sip on complimentary tea/coffee from the courtesy tray in your room.Delicious Dining OptionsStart your day with a delicious breakfast served in your room or choose from the buffet. Unwind with a drink at the lounge bar or order from our convenient room service menu throughout the day.Convenient Location and ServicesExplore Paris with ease using the nearby Pereire Metro Station and let our concierge service assist you with local sights and tours.
