HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Vatican CityJust 100 meters from Rome's Cornelia Metro Station, Hotel Club House Roma offers a tranquil stay just 3 stops away from Vatican City. Explore nearby attractions like the Trevi Fountain and the Spanish Steps with ease.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesRelax in air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, satellite TV, and a private bathroom. Some rooms even feature a charming balcony. Wake up to a delightful buffet breakfast of fresh cakes and pastries.24-Hour Assistance and Chic BarEnjoy round-the-clock multilingual staff assistance and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Unwind at the bar with a selection of wines, cocktails, and snacks after a day of sightseeing. Book your stay now for a memorable Roman getaway at Hotel Club House Roma.
