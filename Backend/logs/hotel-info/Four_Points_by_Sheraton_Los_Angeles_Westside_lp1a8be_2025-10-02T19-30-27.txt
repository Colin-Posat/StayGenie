HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 27 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.6/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the rooms.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with a few complaints about old elevators and staff knowledge.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's convenient location near key landmarks, the airport, and a shopping mall was highlighted by guests.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable, clean, and well-equipped, with some mentioning small bathrooms.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with positive mentions of the fitness room but complaints about extra charges for parking.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Varied opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling the pricing was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, with some guests wishing for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to return to the hotel.
