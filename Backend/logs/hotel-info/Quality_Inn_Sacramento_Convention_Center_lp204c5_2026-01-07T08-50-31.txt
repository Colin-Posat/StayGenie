Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were kept clean overall, although some reviews mentioned cleanliness issues like stains on sheets and brown stains.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Staff members were friendly and provided good customer service, with a few exceptions of dismissive behavior reported.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location was convenient for guests, being close to key attractions and events, although some mentioned noise and homeless people in the vicinity.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the comfortable beds and amenities provided in the rooms, but some reviews highlighted maintenance issues like noisy AC and musty smells.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - Amenities were satisfactory overall, with some positive mentions of included services like breakfast and parking. However, reviews raised concerns about noise levels and the lack of bottled water in rooms.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the offered services and others finding it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests wished for more variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience, praising the friendly staff and convenient location, but some experienced issues with noise levels and maintenance problems.
