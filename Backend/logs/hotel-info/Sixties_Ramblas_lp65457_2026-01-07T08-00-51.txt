HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
1960’s Vintage VibeExperience a blast from the past at Sixties Ramblas, where each room is adorned with retro décor straight from the 1960s. Immerse yourself in nostalgia while enjoying modern comforts in the heart of Barcelona.Convenient LocationSituated just a stone's throw away from the iconic Ramblas, our hotel offers easy access to Barcelona's top attractions. With the nearest Metro Station a mere 2 minutes away, exploring the city is a breeze.Charming AmenitiesIndulge in our on-site bar and restaurant, or seek recommendations from our knowledgeable staff for the best local spots. Take advantage of our 24-hour reception, bike rental services, and even challenge a friend to a game on our stylish billiards table.Book your stay at Sixties Ramblas now and make your Barcelona getaway truly unforgettable!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that parking is limited and cannot be guaranteed or reserved in advance. Larger cars will not fit since it is a small car park. Please contact the hotel for requirements and restrictions.

If you are not the owner of the credit card used to make the reservation, you must provide a copy of the credit card, a copy of the passport, and signed copy of the authorization from the cardholder for the total cost of the reservation.

Please note that a child's cot is available upon previous request and must be confirmed by the property.

Please note, when booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.﻿ The property will contact the guest after the reservation is confirmed.

Please note that it is forbidden to bring scooters and electric bikes into the hotel.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
