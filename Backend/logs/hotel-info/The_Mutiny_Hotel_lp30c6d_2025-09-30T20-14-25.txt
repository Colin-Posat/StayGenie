GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Clean and enjoyable pool
• Refurbished rooms are comfortable

Areas for Improvement:
• Inconsistent cleanliness
• No clear signage in the restaurant
• Noise from air conditioning units

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 4.8/10 - Reviews are mixed regarding cleanliness. While some guests found the rooms clean and well-cared for, others highlighted serious cleanliness issues such as mold, dirty carpets, and issues with kitchen cleanliness.
• Service: 7/10 - Service was generally positive, with most guests acknowledging the friendly and helpful staff. However, there were instances of rude and dismissive behavior by some staff members, impacting the overall experience for a few guests.
• Location: 7.5/10 - The hotel's location was appreciated by guests, being close to key landmarks and public transport. However, the lack of clear signage, operating hours, and menu in the restaurant was seen as a missed opportunity.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Guests found the refurbished rooms comfortable and enjoyable. However, issues such as noisy air conditioning units and cleanliness problems in some units detracted from the overall room quality.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with positive comments about the clean and enjoyable pool. However, the lack of proper cushion covers for loungers was noted as an area for improvement.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling that the hotel fell short in terms of cleaning, maintenance, and basic guest comfort. Others suggested that the price was not justified given the issues encountered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received. However, guests expressed disappointment over the lack of clear signage, hours, and menu in the on-site restaurant.
• Overall Experience: 6.9/10 - While most guests had a positive overall experience, there were recurring issues related to cleanliness, maintenance, and staff behavior that affected the overall enjoyment of the stay.
