HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesTowering over Marina Bay, Mandarin Oriental, Singapore offers a luxurious stay with an outdoor pool boasting stunning cityscape views, a lavish spa, and a well-equipped gym. Guests can also enjoy rejuvenating Yoga classes during their stay.Prime Location and AccessibilityDirectly linked to Marina Square Shopping Centre and just a short walk from City Hall MRT Station, Mandarin Singapore provides easy access to shopping and transportation. With Orchard Road a quick drive away and Singapore Changi Airport within a 20-minute ride, guests can explore the city with convenience.Dining and Entertainment OptionsIndulge in Italian cuisine at Dolce Vita or savor afternoon tea and cocktails at MO BAR, both offering breathtaking views. With additional Japanese and Cantonese dining options, Mandarin Oriental ensures a delightful culinary experience for guests.Book your stay at Mandarin Oriental, Singapore for a truly unforgettable and luxurious experience.

• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Food options, especially at breakfast, received positive feedback overall, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - The majority of guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing exceptional service and location.
