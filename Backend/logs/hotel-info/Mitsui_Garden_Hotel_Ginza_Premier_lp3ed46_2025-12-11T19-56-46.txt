HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
City Views and Stylish RoomsEnjoy breathtaking views of Tokyo Bay and the city skyline from high-floor rooms at Mitsui Garden Hotel Ginza Premier. The chic and stylish rooms, renovated in 2013, offer modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, fridge, and air purifier/humidifier.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsSituated just minutes away from Shinbashi Station and the vibrant Ginza area, our hotel offers easy access to transportation and shopping. Explore Tsukiji Fish Market and Hama Rikyu Park within walking distance, or take a direct train to Haneda Airport from nearby Shinbashi Station.Exceptional Services and Dining OptionsIndulge in massage services, laundry/dry cleaning facilities, and free internet PCs at our 24-hour front desk. Start your day with a buffet breakfast at RISTORANTE E’VOLTA -il cielo- and unwind at Karin Bar with drinks and light refreshments. Book now for a memorable stay at Mitsui Garden Hotel Ginza Premier.
