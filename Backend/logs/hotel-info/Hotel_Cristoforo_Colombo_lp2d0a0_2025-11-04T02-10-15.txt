HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Convenient AmenitiesExperience the epitome of comfort at Cristoforo Colombo in Rome's vibrant EUR district. Enjoy direct access to Euroma2 shopping center and Rome's GRA ring road. The hotel boasts free WiFi, parking, and a seasonal outdoor pool.Delightful Dining OptionsSavor a culinary journey at the on-site restaurant offering a delectable selection of Roman, Italian, and international dishes. Start your day with a sumptuous continental buffet breakfast, ensuring a perfect beginning to your Roman adventures.Prime Location with Easy Access to TransportationBenefit from excellent transport links with a nearby bus stop connecting you to Rome's metro line B. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 9/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas.
• Service: 8.3/10 - The majority of guests appreciated the friendly and helpful staff, with a few mentioning minor issues with service time.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's proximity to public transport and shopping centers was highlighted as a major advantage.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests generally found the rooms comfortable, although some reported maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with positive mentions of the pool and gym, but some guests noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high relative to the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast received positive feedback overall, although some guests suggested improvements in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
