HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesCrowne Plaza Florya offers an indoor and seasonal outdoor pool, spa, wellness centre, and hammam. Guests can also enjoy free WiFi, private parking, and a children's pool for the little ones.Dining ExperiencesIndulge in a variety of culinary delights at Salamina Restaurant, Atrox Restaurant Lounge, Sardinia Winter Garden Restaurant, and Orca pool bar. From breakfast to international cuisine and snacks, there's something for every palate.Convenience and AccessibilityWith a fitness centre, garden, terrace, and services like private airport transfers, concierge, and 24-hour front desk, Crowne Plaza ensures a comfortable stay. Plus, its proximity to Istanbul's top attractions and transportation hubs makes exploring the city a breeze.Book now for a memorable stay at Crowne Plaza Florya!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that the property can only accommodate pets with a maximum weight of 5 kg or less.
All pets staying at this property must have up-to-date vaccinations.
Pets should not be left unattended in the room, they must be on a leash or in a bag at all time in the public areas or in the restaurant. Wild animals are not accepted.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 11 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
