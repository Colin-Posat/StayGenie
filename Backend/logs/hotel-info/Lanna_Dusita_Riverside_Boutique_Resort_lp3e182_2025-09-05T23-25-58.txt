HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Riverside Oasis:Nestled along the picturesque Ping River, Lanna Dusita Hotel provides a tranquil escape with complimentary WiFi and private parking. Indulge in the serene ambiance as you unwind by the infinity-edge pool or pamper yourself with a rejuvenating massage.Luxurious Accommodations:Each room at Lanna Dusita Hotel boasts stunning views of the river or lush garden, accompanied by a spacious balcony. Enjoy modern amenities including cable TV, minibar, and a tea/coffee maker, ensuring a comfortable stay. The private bathrooms add a touch of luxury to your experience.Convenient Services:Enhancing your stay, the hotel offers convenient services such as airport transfers, car rentals, and tour arrangements for a seamless travel experience. Located just a short drive from downtown Chiang Mai, guests can also take advantage of the complimentary shuttle van service to explore the city effortlessly.Experience serenity and convenience at Lanna Dusita Hotel - book now for an unforgettable stay!
