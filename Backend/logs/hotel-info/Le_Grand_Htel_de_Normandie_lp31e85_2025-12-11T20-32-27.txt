HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ParisLocated in the 9th arrondissement, our hotel offers 24-hour service in the heart of Paris. Just a 2-minute walk from the Saint Lazare Train Station, guests can easily explore the city with free Wi-Fi access.Comfortable and Convenient RoomsEach soundproofed room features en suite bathroom facilities for your privacy. Upgrade to a room with a private balcony and enjoy a charming view of the street below.Near Iconic Landmarks and ShoppingStart your day with a delicious breakfast at Le Grand Hôtel de Normandie before visiting the Louvre (2.2 km) or strolling to the Opéra Garnier (10 minutes away). Shop at the famous Galeries Lafayette, just a 9-minute walk from our hotel.Ready to experience Parisian charm? Book your stay with us today!
