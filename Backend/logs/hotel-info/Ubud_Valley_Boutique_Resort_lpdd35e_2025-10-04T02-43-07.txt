HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience comfort and style in our air-conditioned rooms with private bathrooms, flat-screen TVs, and some with stunning pool views.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy complimentary bikes and WiFi, relax by the outdoor swimming pool, and indulge in a continental breakfast at our in-house restaurant serving International cuisine.Explore UbudTake advantage of our scheduled shuttle to Ubud centre, and let us assist you with car hire and local area information at our reception. The resort is ideally located near popular attractions like Ubud Palace and Monkey Forest.Book your stay at Ubud Valley Boutique Resort for a memorable and relaxing getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Ubud Valley Boutique Resort in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for Rp 1,000,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
