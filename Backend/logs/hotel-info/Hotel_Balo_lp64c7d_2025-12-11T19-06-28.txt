HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Balo, located in Istanbul near Taksim Square, offers 4-star accommodation with air-conditioned rooms featuring modern amenities like a coffee machine, flat-screen TV, and private bathroom. Some rooms even boast balconies with city views, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Convenient FacilitiesGuests at Hotel Balo can enjoy the convenience of a fitness centre, private parking, garden, and terrace. The hotel also provides a concierge service, tour desk, 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service, and free WiFi, catering to all their needs during their stay.Prime LocationWith Taksim Metro Station, Istiklal Street, and Galata Tower in close proximity, Hotel Balo is the perfect choice for travelers looking to explore the vibrant city of Istanbul. • Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location received positive feedback for being central and convenient to landmarks, despite being in a noisy area with nightclubs.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but some guests reported issues like sewer smells and noise disturbances.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests highlighting positives like gym and pool while others mentioned broken gym facilities.
• Value for Money: 4.2/10 - Poor rating on value for money, as some guests felt the services did not justify the high prices.
• Food and Beverage: 4.4/10 - Negative feedback on food and beverage, with complaints about breakfast quality and behavior of serving staff.
• Overall Experience: 3.8/10 - Mixed reviews on the overall experience, with some guests having a positive stay while others had numerous complaints about various aspects of the hotel.
