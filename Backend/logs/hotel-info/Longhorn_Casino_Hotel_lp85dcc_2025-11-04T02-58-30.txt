• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Rooms were described as basic and clean by some guests, while others noted the outdated facilities and lack of amenities like coffee machines.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - The amenities offered were deemed satisfactory by some guests, with positive mentions of the onsite restaurant, but others noted the lack of certain amenities like water bottles.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Opinions on the value for money were divided, with some guests feeling that the hotel offered good service despite being older and others expressing disappointment with the facilities given the price.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Food and beverage options were generally acceptable, with positive comments on the restaurant's food quality but also some complaints about prices and variety.
• Overall Experience: 6.9/10 - Guests had mixed overall experiences with some mentioning pleasant stays and helpful staff, while others were dissatisfied with the cleanliness and outdated facilities.
