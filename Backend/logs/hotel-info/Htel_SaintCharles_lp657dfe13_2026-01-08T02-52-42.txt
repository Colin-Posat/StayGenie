HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with AmenitiesThe guest rooms at Hôtel Saint-Charles feature a modern décor, wooden floors, flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, and en suite bathrooms. Elevators are available for convenience.Delicious Breakfast and Relaxing BarStart your day with a buffet breakfast including fresh pastries in the breakfast room or on the terrace.
