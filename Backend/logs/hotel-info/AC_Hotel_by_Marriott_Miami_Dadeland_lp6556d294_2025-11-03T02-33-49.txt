Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.7/10 - Guests consistently highlighted the cleanliness of the hotel, many reviews mentioned the rooms and common areas were very clean.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Reviews generally praised the friendly and helpful staff, with specific mentions of excellent service at the front desk.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was considered ideal by guests, especially for its proximity to key areas and safety.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness and comfort of the rooms, although some reviews highlighted inconsistencies in room cleanliness.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews regarding amenities, with some guests mentioning positive aspects like free parking while others highlighted issues with lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests finding the pricing high relative to services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with minor complaints about service and variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel.
