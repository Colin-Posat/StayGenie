HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Sheraton New York Times Square Hotel offers a comfortable work area with a window and ergonomic chair, ensuring a productive stay.Dining and Fitness OptionsIndulge in a breakfast buffet at Hudson Market or savor prime Angus burgers at Hudson Market Burger. Stay active with personalized dining and workout programs at the on-site fitness center.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy complimentary Wi-Fi at the computer center, and utilize fax, copy, and printing services at the 24-hour FedEx Office Print &amp; Ship Center. With a location just steps away from popular attractions like Rockefeller Center and Times Square, make your stay unforgettable.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The credit card provided for payment must be presented at check-in and the cardholder and guest name must match.

Following amenities are included in daily resort fee:
2 tickets per stay for Central Park Bike Rental (2 hour rental),
$30 F&B credit per day,
Premium WiFi,
Local and Long-distance calls.

All breakfast inclusive rates are for 2 adults maximum, any additional guests will be an additional charge.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
