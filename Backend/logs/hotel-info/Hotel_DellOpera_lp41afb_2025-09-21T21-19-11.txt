HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Location and Elegant RoomsHotel Dell'Opera, located next to the renowned La Fenice Theatre and a short walk from St. Mark's Square, offers elegantly decorated rooms inspired by 18th-century Venetian-style décor. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, flat-screen satellite TV, and picturesque views of Venice's historic buildings or rooftops.Rooftop Bar and DiningIndulge in summer drinks with stunning city views at the hotel's rooftop bar. Start your day with a delicious breakfast buffet in the dining room, and unwind at the 24-hour bar for a relaxing evening.Exceptional Services and Special OffersThe friendly staff at Dell'Opera Hotel is ready to assist with reservations for museums, tours, and cultural events. Take advantage of special rates at nearby restaurants and a partner garage for a seamless stay in Venice.Book your stay at Hotel Dell'Opera for a memorable Venetian experience!
