Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.1/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the hotel, although a few mentioned issues with dirty carpets and open showers.
• Service: 8/10 - The staff received positive feedback for being warm, friendly, and helpful, although there were some complaints about communication regarding curfews.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location in downtown was appreciated for its convenience, but some guests found the neighborhood to be gentrified yet sketchy.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the rooms for their design, modern vibe, and amenities. However, there were occasional mentions of maintenance issues and low beds.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities with some guests appreciating the pool, gym, and laundry facilities, while others mentioned missing spa services and insufficient communication on curfews.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - There were varying opinions on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified for the services offered, while others felt it was expensive compared to similar options.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to return, despite some minor issues mentioned in specific categories.
