Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Most guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, although one review highlighted the absence of toothbrush and toothpaste.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was highly appreciated overall, with special mentions for the staff going above and beyond to correct booking errors. However, some negative comments about late checkout and poor communication were noted.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's proximity to LAX and shuttle service were mostly well-received, with one review mentioning it as a drawback.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally described as comfortable and spacious, but some issues like lack of coffee facilities and uncleaned rooms were reported.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback was received regarding amenities, with some guests praising the soundproofing and others citing the lack of response by security.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services received positive feedback, although a few guests suggested improvements in variety and responsiveness.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with special mentions for outstanding staff interactions and warm welcomes.
