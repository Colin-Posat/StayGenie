Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.

People no matter the age stay for £100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.

• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
