HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Public Bath: Mitsui Garden Hotel Kyoto Shijo offers modern rooms with free WiFi, air-conditioning, and heating facilities. Guests can relax in the public bath and enjoy free bath amenities and slippers in each room.Convenient Location: Situated just 600 meters from Karasuma Station, with easy access to Shijo Station Exit 2 on the Karasuma Subway Line, guests can explore Kyoto with ease. The hotel is also close to popular attractions like Higashihongan-ji Temple and Nijo Castle.Dining and Services: Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast at the on-site restaurant. The hotel also offers massage services, laundry, and dry cleaning facilities for a comfortable stay. Additionally, guests can enjoy beverages from the drinks vending machine.Book now for a memorable stay at Mitsui Garden Hotel Kyoto Shijo!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
