Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the hotel, although there were some reports of mold and brown water in drains in one room.
• Service: 7.3/10 - The staff were described as friendly and helpful by most guests, but there were complaints about elevator issues and slow response to requests.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel's proximity to Times Square and other key landmarks was a highlight for many guests, although some found it challenging to get taxis or ride shares.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Spacious and comfortable rooms were appreciated, but some guests experienced elevator malfunctions and noise disturbances.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Basic amenities were provided, with a positive mention of the outdoor patio, but there were complaints about the broken elevator and lack of heat in rooms.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the daily fee and lack of complimentary items did not justify the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - While some guests enjoyed the food and beverage options, others found the breakfast disappointing in comparison to the price.
• Overall Experience: 6.8/10 - Overall, guests had a mixed experience with the hotel, with issues ranging from elevator problems to smoking fee disputes impacting their stay.
