IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
Upon check-in photo identification and credit card is required. All special requests are subject to availability upon check-in. Special requests cannot be guaranteed and may incur additional charges.

Please be advised the property also offers valet parking at a cost. Contact hotel for details.

Please note the Club Lounge is open Sunday evening through Friday morning. The lounge is closed on the weekends.

Please note that breakfast included rates are based on 2 adults and 2 children under 12 years of age.

HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
People no matter the age stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.
