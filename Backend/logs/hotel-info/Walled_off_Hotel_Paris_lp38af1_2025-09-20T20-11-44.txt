Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.5/10 - Most guests noted good cleanliness standards, but there were concerns about bed bugs, mildew in showers, and general maintenance.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Staff were frequently commended for their friendliness and helpfulness, though some guests experienced issues such as badge malfunctions.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its proximity to key areas and safety, although noise from the street was a concern for some guests.
• Room Quality: 5.8/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness and comfort of the beds, but criticized the lack of climate control, noise insulation, and room amenities.
• Amenities: 4.5/10 - A lack of in-room bathrooms and poor lighting were significant drawbacks, despite some satisfaction with room cleaning services.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Some guests felt the hotel was a good deal, specifically noting its location, but overall experience suggests room for improvement in value.
• Food and Beverage: 5/10 - Mixed feedback regarding breakfast availability and policies suggests an average rating, with no extensive praise or complaint.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Guests generally had a positive experience with notable highlights being the staff and location, though several concerns have impacted the overall satisfaction.
