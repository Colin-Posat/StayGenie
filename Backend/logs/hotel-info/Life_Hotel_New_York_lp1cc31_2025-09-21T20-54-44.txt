HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Historical SettingLife Hotel, nestled between Broadway and 5th Avenue, offers a unique stay in the historic building that once housed LIFE Magazine. Immerse yourself in the charm of the past with modern comforts.Elegant Room FeaturesIndulge in the luxury of wrought iron bed frames, Carrera marble counters, and rain shower heads. Relax on pillow-top mattresses with Egyptian cotton sheets. Each room is a blend of classic elegance and contemporary style.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy complimentary access to the fitness centre with Peloton bikes, concierge services, and filtered water and ice stations. Stay connected with free WiFi and digital newspapers. Let us pamper you with our turn-down service.Book now to experience the perfect blend of history, luxury, and convenience at Life Hotel.
