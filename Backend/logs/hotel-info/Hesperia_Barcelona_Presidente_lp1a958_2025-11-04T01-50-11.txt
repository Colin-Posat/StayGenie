HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation: Experience spacious and air-conditioned rooms at Hesperia Barcelona Presidente, complete with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs for your comfort.Spectacular Views: Enjoy stunning city views from the gym and terrace, providing a perfect backdrop to your stay.Convenient Location: Situated on Diagonal Avenue, this hotel is near shopping areas, sightseeing bus stops, and metro stations, making exploring Barcelona a breeze.Book your stay at Hesperia Barcelona Presidente for a truly unforgettable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Hesperia Barcelona Presidente in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
The name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property.

Guests under 18 can only stay when having an authorization from their parents or legal tutors, or if they are accompanied by them.

Please note that there is a surcharge per pet per day of 22€.
