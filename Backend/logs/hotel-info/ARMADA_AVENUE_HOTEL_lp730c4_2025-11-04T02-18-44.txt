HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Spa and Recreational Amenities:Unwind at our full-service spa with rejuvenating massages and body treatments. Take a dip in the outdoor pool, relax in the steam room, or work out at the fitness center.Dining and Entertainment:Indulge in delectable meals at the restaurant, enjoy drinks at the bar/lounge or poolside bar. With 24-hour room service, savor delicious dishes in the comfort of your room.Convenience and Comfort:Our hotel offers complimentary shuttle service to the beach and shopping center, along with free valet parking. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi, and enjoy modern amenities like LCD TVs, minibars, and rainfall showers in our air-conditioned rooms.Book your stay at ARMADA AVENUE HOTEL for a luxurious and convenient experience in Dubai!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM.

* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* A car is required for transportation to and from this property.
