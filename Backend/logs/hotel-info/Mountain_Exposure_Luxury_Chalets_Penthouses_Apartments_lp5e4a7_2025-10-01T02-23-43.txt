HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to Mountain Exposure Luxury Chalets &amp; Penthouses, where breathtaking views meet unparalleled comfort in the heart of Zermatt.Relax and Unwind: Treat yourself to invigorating onsite massages or rent a bicycle to explore the stunning surroundings. Achieve the ultimate relaxation with our spa-like amenities designed for your rejuvenation.Fully Equipped Accommodations: Enjoy the convenience of our spacious guestrooms, featuring fully-equipped kitchens with full-sized refrigerators, ovens, and microwave. Relax with your favorite show on flat-screen televisions, or soak away your worries in deep soaking bathtubs.Exceptional Services: Our dedicated team offers concierge services and babysitting options, ensuring every need is met. Plus, enjoy the ease of complimentary Wi-Fi and express check-out to keep your stay smooth and enjoyable.Don't miss your chance to experience the luxury and adventure that awaits you—book your stay today!
