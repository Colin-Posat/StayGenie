HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with AmenitiesThe hotel offers modern rooms with flat-screen cable TV, small fridge, work desk, and free toiletries. Perfect for a relaxing evening after exploring downtown LA.Book now for a memorable stay at Doubletree by Hilton Los Angeles Downtown, where convenience and comfort meet in the heart of the city.
