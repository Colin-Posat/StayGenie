HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the enchanting Disney Hotel New York - The Art of Marvel, where art and luxury meet just moments away from Disneyland® Paris!Recreational DelightsUnwind in our state-of-the-art health club, take a refreshing dip in the indoor pool, or relax in the soothing hot tub. With a complimentary shuttle service to nearby theme parks, your family's adventure is just a ride away!Culinary ExperiencesSavor delectable international cuisine at our Downtown restaurant or indulge in 24-hour room service for a cozy night in. Start your day with an inviting buffet breakfast, and unwind in our stylish bar/lounge with a drink in hand.Comfort and ConvenienceOur spacious rooms offer flat-screen TVs, complimentary Wi-Fi, and private bathrooms equipped with luxurious toiletries. Benefit from complimentary self-parking and express check-in/out for a seamless experience.Book your stay now and immerse yourself in the magic!
