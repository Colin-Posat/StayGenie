HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical Charm and Prime LocationHyatt Regency London Blackfriars, a Grade II listed hotel on the former grounds of King Henry VIII’s Bridewell Palace, offers a unique blend of history and luxury. Situated in the heart of the City of London, guests enjoy easy access to iconic landmarks and transportation hubs.Elegance and ComfortFeaturing 205 sophisticated guest rooms and suites, the hotel boasts modern design, marble bathrooms, Hyatt Luxury Mattresses, and high-tech amenities. Each room offers a blend of style and functionality for a comfortable stay.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's four restaurants and bar. From Chinese classics with a modern twist at Chinese Cricket Club to eclectic upscale menus at NYnLon, and exotic cocktails at Leaf &amp; Cane, guests are treated to a diverse range of dining experiences.Ready to immerse yourself in history and luxury at Hyatt Regency London Blackfriars? Book now for an unforgettable stay!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for £100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
