IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 21 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

Condos or apartments have a maximum occupancy of 2 to 4 adults plus children under the age of 16, depending on the unit. Only registered guests are allowed in the unit, please note that visitors and gatherings are not permitted.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 39 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in public areas and costs  USD  9.95 per  24 hours.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
