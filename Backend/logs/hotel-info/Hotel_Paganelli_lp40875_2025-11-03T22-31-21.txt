HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Setting and Relaxing AtmosphereHotel Paganelli, a former convent turned into a stylish retreat, offers a sophisticated lobby and a charming bar for your relaxation. The serene atmosphere is perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring Venice.Luxurious Accommodations with Historic CharmChoose between luxurious rooms in the main building or the modern annex, each offering a unique experience. The annex boasts art-dèco decorations and renovated antique ceilings, adding a touch of history to your stay.Convenient Location and Exceptional ServiceLocated within walking distance of iconic landmarks and vaporetto stations, Hotel Paganelli ensures you can easily explore Venice. Count on the friendly staff for personalized recommendations and assistance throughout your stay.Experience the charm of Venice at Hotel Paganelli - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.
