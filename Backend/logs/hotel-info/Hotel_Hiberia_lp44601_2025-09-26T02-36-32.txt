HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Trevi FountainHotel Hiberia is a charming property located a stone's throw away from the iconic Trevi Fountain. Immerse yourself in the heart of Rome with attractions like the Coliseum just a short walk away.Comfortable Rooms and AmenitiesExperience comfort in our air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs. Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast at our restaurant offering a variety of Italian and international dishes.Convenient ServicesOur multilingual staff is at your service 24/7, ensuring a seamless stay. Take advantage of our luggage storage room and easy access to transportation with buses to Roma Termini Train Station nearby.Book your stay at Hotel Hiberia for an unforgettable Roman getaway.
