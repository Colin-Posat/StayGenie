HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 4-Star Accommodation in Balinese ArchitectureIndulgent Amenities: From the indoor pool to the library, The Sungu Resort &amp; Spa offers a range of facilities for guests to unwind and relax. Enjoy complimentary WiFi throughout the property.Unique Experiences: Take advantage of free trekking in the picturesque rice fields and shuttle service to downtown Ubud. Explore Rudana Museum and Fine Arts Gallery, or let your children unleash their creativity in the kids painting class.Tranquil Oasis: Nestled in the artistic village of Penestanan, the resort provides spacious air-conditioned rooms with private courtyards and lush gardens. Immerse yourself in Balinese culture with outdoor showers and a sunken bath in each room.Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and Balinese hospitality at The Sungu Resort &amp; Spa.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 5 and older are allowed.
Children 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 390,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
