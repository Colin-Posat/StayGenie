HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Locationvoco Paris Montparnasse is ideally situated in Paris’s 14th arrondissement, just steps away from Gaîté Metro station and a short stroll from Montparnasse Train Station.Modern AmenitiesThe hotel boasts contemporary air-conditioned rooms with a flat-screen TV, minibar, and complimentary WiFi. Guests can enjoy tea/coffee facilities and a private bathroom for added convenience.Dining and RelaxationIndulge in a delicious buffet breakfast at Le Parnasse restaurant or unwind with a drink at The Scarlet Bar's terrace or lobby.Experience the best of Paris with easy access to major attractions and transportation options. Book your stay at voco Paris Montparnasse for a memorable trip!
