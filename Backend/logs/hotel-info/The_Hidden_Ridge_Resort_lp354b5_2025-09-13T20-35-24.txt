Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Guests mentioned clean rooms as a positive aspect, but some reviews noted cleanliness issues such as dim lighting.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Service was generally appreciated with some guests mentioning friendly staff, but there were complaints about noise and delayed room servicing.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its convenience and tranquil surroundings, but some guests found the rooms disappointing due to being outdated.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with some guests liking spacious rooms and comfortable beds, while others complained about dated furniture, uncomfortable beds, and lack of kitchen amenities.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Amenities like the pool, hot tub, and sauna were well-received, but there were complaints about limited laundry facilities and lack of AC in rooms.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others appreciated the free bus pass and parking.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally good, but there were some complaints about the lack of variety and kitchen amenities.
• Overall Experience: 7.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience with highlights on the location and amenities, but some were disappointed with room quality and value for money.
