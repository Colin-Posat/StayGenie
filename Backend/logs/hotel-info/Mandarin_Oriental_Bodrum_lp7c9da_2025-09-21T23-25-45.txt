HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to the exquisite Mandarin Oriental, Bodrum, where luxury meets tranquility.Serene Spa RetreatUnwind at our full-service spa, offering rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials that will melt your worries away. Enhance your relaxation with our 2 stunning outdoor swimming pools and a private beach just steps away.Culinary DelightsSavor delectable dishes at one of our 4 restaurants, or indulge in 24-hour room service for a cozy night in. Enjoy daily complimentary receptions and let loose at our beach bar and poolside lounges with refreshing drinks available.Comfort &amp; ConnectivityRetreat to one of our 137 individually decorated guestrooms, each featuring premium bedding, private balconies, and exclusive designer toiletries. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and enjoy additional services, including concierge and express check-in, ensuring your stay is seamless.Book your getaway at Mandarin Oriental, Bodrum today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Up to 2 children 6 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* Cashless payment methods are available for all transactions.
* Contactless check-out is available.

• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests with friendly and supportive staff mentioned.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to key landmarks and public transport, making it convenient for guests.
• Room Quality: 8.5/10 - Rooms were praised for being spacious, comfortable, and well-facilitated, with room upgrades offered.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the facilities, while others mentioned shared walking paths with buggy traffic.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some feeling the services were premium while others found pricing high for dining options.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast buffet and restaurant services were well-received, with some limitations on dining options mentioned.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hospitality and premium resort experience. Minor cons included limited dining options and shared walking paths with buggy traffic.
