HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Unique AmenitiesHotel Saint Cecilia in Austin offers a truly luxurious experience with record players in each room and complimentary WiFi. The 5-star hotel boasts rooms with stunning pool views and access to an outdoor swimming pool with exclusive poolside bungalows.Exceptional Dining and Relaxation OptionsIndulge in a delicious breakfast with à la carte and American options, and savor mouthwatering American cuisine at the on-site restaurant. Unwind on the terrace or take advantage of the 24-hour front desk for all your needs.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsSituated near popular landmarks like Austin Convention Center and Barton Springs Pool, Hotel Saint Cecilia provides easy access to Capitol Building and the University of Texas. Book your stay now at this exquisite hotel for an unforgettable experience.
