HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Hotel on Rome's Via VenetoDesigner Furnishings and Free SpaIndulge in luxury at the Grand Hotel Palace with rooms featuring designer furnishings, LCD TVs, and marble bathrooms with VIP toiletries. Relax at the free spa after a day of exploring the nearby Villa Borghese.Fine Dining and Wellness CenterSavor fine Italian cuisine at the Cadorin Restaurant and enjoy a lavish American buffet for breakfast. Unwind at the wellness center with a sauna, hot tub, massages, and a fitness center to rejuvenate your senses.Stylish Restored Building and Ideal LocationExperience the charm of a 1920s building designed by Marcello Piacentini, now stylishly restored by Italo Rota. Conveniently located near Piazza Barberini and Barberini Metro Station, the hotel is just a short walk from the iconic Spanish Steps.Book your luxurious stay at the Grand Hotel Palace now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 50 per  day.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 99 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable with spacious layouts, though some guests noted minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - While some guests enjoyed the amenities, others found them lacking, particularly mentioning the absence of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.2/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the pricing, with some feeling it was too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - While breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, some guests felt there was room for improvement in terms of variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
