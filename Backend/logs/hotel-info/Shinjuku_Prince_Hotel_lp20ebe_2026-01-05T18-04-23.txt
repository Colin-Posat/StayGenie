HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near KabukichoLocated just a stone's throw away from the vibrant Kabukicho area, Shinjuku Prince Hotel offers convenience and excitement at your doorstep. With a mere 5-minute walk from JR Shinjuku Station, this hotel is the perfect base for exploring Tokyo.Luxurious Amenities and ServicesIndulge in the comfort of elegantly designed rooms equipped with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV and a safe. Unwind with a relaxing massage or savor authentic Japanese cuisine while enjoying breathtaking city views at the hotel's restaurant.Convenient Facilities and Dining OptionsBenefit from internet stations, luggage storage, and a 24-hour front desk for all your needs. Explore the culinary delights of Restaurant Fuga-Wafu and Prince Marche, offering a variety of delectable dishes to satisfy your cravings.Book your stay at Shinjuku Prince Hotel now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience in Tokyo.
