HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesThe Elser Hotel Miami offers a range of luxurious amenities including an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, and terrace. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of private parking, free WiFi, and a 24-hour front desk with concierge service.Elegant and Comfortable RoomsEach guest room at The Elser Hotel Miami is designed for comfort and style, featuring air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Certain rooms even boast city views and balconies, providing a touch of elegance to your stay.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary experience at the hotel's restaurant, offering a variety of American, Italian, and Mediterranean cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free, and halal options are also available upon request, catering to every guest's palate.Experience luxury, comfort, and exceptional service at The Elser Hotel Miami. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Miami.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
Please inform The Elser Hotel Miami - An All-Suite Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of USD 200 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note the resort fee includes: Highspeed Wi-Fi Access in rooms, suites, and all common areas; Fitness/Yoga Classes ; Local and 1-800 Phone Calls; 24- Hour Concierge Services; Two In-Room Refillable PATH Water Bottles; Two Complimentary drinks per reservation.

There is a 25 daily fee for making the sofa bed in the rooms that the sofa bed has been used.
