HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortExperience the blend of historic charm and modern comfort at Hotel de Lutèce, a 17th-century building on Ile Saint-Louis. The 23 soundproofed, air-conditioned rooms offer a cozy retreat with private bathrooms, satellite TV, and free Wi-Fi.Convenient LocationSituated just a 10-minute walk from iconic landmarks like the Panthéon and Georges Pompidou Centre, Hotel de Lutèce provides easy access to Parisian attractions. Explore the city with nearby public transport to the Louvre and Musée d’Orsay.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind in style at Hotel L de Lutèce, featuring a wellness area with a hydromassage bath and sauna. Indulge in relaxation after a day of sightseeing in the heart of Paris.Book your stay at Hotel de Lutèce now to enjoy a memorable Parisian getaway!
