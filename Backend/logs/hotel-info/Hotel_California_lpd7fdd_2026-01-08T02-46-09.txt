HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in Barcelona's Gothic DistrictHotel California, nestled in the heart of Barcelona’s Gothic District, is a mere 2 minutes’ walk from the vibrant La Rambla. Immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of the city right from your doorstep.Comfortable Rooms with Balconies and Free Wi-FiRelax in our soundproofed rooms featuring balconies, air conditioning, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, wardrobe, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Enjoy a cozy and peaceful stay in the midst of the bustling city.Convenient Amenities and Nearby AttractionsStart your day with a delicious breakfast buffet, unwind at the hotel bar, or opt for room service. Explore the lively La Boqueria Market just 300 meters away, stroll to Plaça Catalunya in 10 minutes, or take a leisurely walk to La Barceloneta Beach in just 20 minutes.
