HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Colonial Building Accommodation:Fairmont The Norfolk is a luxurious retreat nestled in tropical gardens, offering elegantly decorated rooms and spacious suites with modern amenities and garden views.Dining Variety and Wellness:Indulge in a culinary journey at 6 restaurants including a modern American steak house, a wine bar, and a poolside eatery. Unwind at the fitness centre with sauna, heated outdoor pool, and pampering massage facilities.Convenience and Cultural Exploration:Enjoy free WiFi, shopping tours, and cultural excursions arranged by the 24-hour front desk. Immerse yourself in Kenyan history at the nearby National Museums or opt for a shuttle to explore Nairobi or catch a flight from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Fairmont The Norfolk!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$0 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for US$0 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
