IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests must be aged 21 or older to check in at this property.

The resort fee includes beach towels, WiFi, business center access and local calls.

The service charge is non refundable.

The property will make an additional authorization for USD 100 per day at check-in for incidentals. The authorization is refundable and subject to damage inspection of the accommodations.

Please note the property is undergoing light renovations regarding decorations and general repairs. No noise is expected.

The credit card used for booking must be presented at check-in. The name on the credit card must match the name on the reservation and the card holder must be present along with a government-issued photo ID.

When booking 5 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Free Happy Hour daily from 5 PM to 6 PM.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
