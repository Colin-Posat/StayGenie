HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel SB Icaria offers stylish modern rooms with wooden floors and adjustable lighting. Each room is equipped with a minibar, safe, and a private bathroom, providing the utmost comfort for guests.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can enjoy the gym, sauna, outdoor pool, sun terrace, and hot tub, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring Barcelona. The hotel's serene garden terrace is also a great spot to relax with a drink.Ideal LocationConveniently situated just 300 metres from Nova Icaria Beach, Hotel SB Icaria is in the vibrant Olympic Port area. With popular attractions like Ciutadella Park and Barcelona Zoo within walking distance, and easy access to public transportation, guests can effortlessly explore the city's highlights.Book your stay at Hotel SB Icaria for a luxurious and convenient Barcelona experience.
