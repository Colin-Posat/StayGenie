HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationAvenue is a 3-star hotel located just 350 metres from Amsterdam Central Railway Station, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking for easy access to transportation.Comfortable RoomsThe rooms at Avenue Hotel are equipped with satellite TV, air conditioning, and a work desk, providing guests with a comfortable and functional space to relax and unwind.Local Attractions and ServicesWithin a short walking distance from Dam Square, guests can explore the Royal Palace and Madame Tussauds. Additionally, the hotel offers a packed lunch service and tour desk to enhance guests' local experiences.Book your stay at Avenue Hotel now to enjoy a convenient location, comfortable rooms, and easy access to local attractions and services.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of EUR 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected as a cash payment. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full in cash, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that when your booking is non-refundable, the credit card holder needs to be one of the guests staying at the hotel.

Please be informed that you have to show your credit card that you used for the payment, matching your ID upon check-in.

Between 07:00 - 21:00 there is a valet parking and outside of these hours an additional fee applies. Other parking facilities are available in the vicinity.
