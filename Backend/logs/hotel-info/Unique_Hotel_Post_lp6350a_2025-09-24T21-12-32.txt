IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
This property may host on-site functions and some rooms may be affected by noise.
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi. The train ride from Täsch to Zermatt takes 15 minutes. The hotel is a 5-minute walk from the train station.

Please inform the hotel at least 10 minutes before your arrival in Zermatt if you want to make use of the free transfer service.

Please note that all the hotel's restaurants and bars are only open during the winter season. Limited opening times apply in other seasons.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 9 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 100 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 9 years old and over stay for CHF 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
