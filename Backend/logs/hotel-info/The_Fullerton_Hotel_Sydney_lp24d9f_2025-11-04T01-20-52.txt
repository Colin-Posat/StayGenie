Maintain your fitness routine while enjoying your stay in the heart of Sydney.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at The Fullerton Hotel Sydney with a range of dining options from modern Australian cuisine to Southeast Asian signature dishes and handcrafted cocktails. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Payments by Credit Card shall incur Merchant Service Fee of 1.85% and 1.40% for debit cards.

Please note that The Fullerton Hotel Sydney requires a $100 per night, credit card pre-authorisation upon check in to cover any incidental charges.

Please note entry to the fitness centre is for registered guests only over the age of 18. Guests aged 16-18 years are only permitted entry with a consent form signed and presented by a parent or guardian. For more information, please contact the property, using the contact details found on the booking form.

Photo ID provided must match personal details on Credit Card. Credit card used at time of reservation must be presented upon check in at the hotel, or check-in will be denied.

We partner with two nearby public parking facilities. Secure Parking at 171 Pitt Street (located under the hotel) has self parking $68 per 24 hours and Valet parking $115 per 24 hours. The second option is Wilson Parking at 123 Angel Place (Pitt Street) for a charge of $25 per entry per 24 hours on weekdays and $18 per entry per 24 hours on weekends. Please note both options must have the ticket validated by the hotel Concierge.

Prices are subject to change.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  AUD 66 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 12 years old and over stay for AUD 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Cleanliness of the hotel was generally appreciated, although some guests mentioned specific issues like bathroom blinds and desk chair stains.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Most guests found the staff to be lovely and accommodating, with prompt responses to issues encountered.
• Location: 8.9/10 - The hotel's central location near key landmarks and public transport was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were spacious, well-appointed, and comfortable, but some aspects felt dated to certain guests.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory, with guests appreciating aspects like the gym and breakfast offerings.
• Value for Money: 7.4/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel.
