HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Outdoor Waterpark and Shuttle ServiceExperience the thrill of a 6,000 m² outdoor waterpark right at your doorstep, just minutes away from Universal Orlando Resort. Enjoy the convenience of a complimentary shuttle service to the theme park and stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the property.Family-Friendly AccommodationsIndulge in vibrant and cozy rooms equipped with modern amenities such as a 55-inch flat-screen TV, coffee maker, work desk, and ironing facilities. Perfect for families, the hotel also offers a variety of room options to suit your needs.Dining Options and EntertainmentStart your day with a delicious breakfast at TradeWinds Restaurant before heading to the thrilling water slides and jungle gym area in the water park. Unwind at the Wet Rooster Bar, try your luck at the arcade, or grab essentials from the convenience store on-site.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Coco Key Hotel &amp; Water Park Resort in Orlando!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Guests under the age of 21 can only check-in with a parent or official guardian.

Water park hours subject to change without notice and are weather permitting. Please contact the property in advance for further details.

The standard resort fee includes water park admission for 4 people, which is valid on both check-in and check-out days.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 10 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on food quality and pricing, with many guests expressing dissatisfaction with the cost and quality of food offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the water park and staff, despite some issues with cleanliness and amenities.
