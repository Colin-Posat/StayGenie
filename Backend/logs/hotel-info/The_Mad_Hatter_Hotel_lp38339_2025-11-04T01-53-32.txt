HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building with Modern ComfortsExperience a stay at The Mad Hatter Hotel, a charming 19th-century building turned into a cozy retreat in the heart of Southwark. Once a hat factory, this historic gem now offers comfortable rooms with free Wi-Fi and essential amenities.Authentic Dining ExperienceIndulge in delicious home-cooked pub dishes at the friendly bar and restaurant on-site. From English classics to specialty pies, savor a range of authentic meals in a warm and inviting atmosphere.Prime Location for SightseeingLocated just steps away from the River Thames and the Tate Modern art gallery, and a short walk from London Waterloo Station, our hotel provides easy access to London's top attractions. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for £5.50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for £35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
