HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover The Monsaraz San Diego, Tapestry Collection by Hilton, where contemporary art meets luxurious comfort in the heart of Point Loma.Recreational OasisUnwind with our 24-hour fitness center or explore the stunning surroundings by renting our bicycles, ensuring an invigorating stay tailored to both relaxation and adventure.Culinary DelightsSavor exquisite Californian cuisine at our onsite restaurant, Westerly Public House, or enjoy room service for a perfect in-room dining experience. Don't miss our full breakfast offering every morning, available for an energizing start to your day.Convenience at Your FingertipsExperience seamless travel with express check-in/out and complimentary high-speed internet in all 92 air-conditioned rooms, equipped with all modern amenities and designer toiletries for your comfort.Escape to luxury and book your stay at The Monsaraz San Diego today!
