HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel Yasmak Sultan, nestled in Istanbul's vibrant Sultanahmet district, offers spacious rooms with free WiFi. Each room is elegantly furnished, featuring a private bathroom with marbled countertop basin and a bathtub. Some rooms even boast a cozy seating area, perfect for relaxation.Relaxation and WellnessIndulge in the hotel's traditional Turkish spa, indoor pool, and panoramic restaurant. The marbled Turkish hammam and dry sauna in the spa offer a rejuvenating experience. Guests can unwind with a massage or stay active in the gym, ensuring a balanced stay.Spectacular Dining ExperienceThe Olive Restaurant presents a delightful blend of Turkish and international cuisine against a backdrop of Topkapi Palace and the Bosphorus Strait. For a more casual setting, the Lobby Bar offers a selection of beverages and snacks.Experience luxury, relaxation, and exquisite dining at Hotel Yasmak Sultan. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Istanbul getaway.

Category Ratings:

• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and clean, with a few mentions of small bathrooms and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7/10 - A mixed review of amenities with positive comments on the breakfast buffet and pool, but some criticism for the lack of spa services and the condition of the fitness area.
• Value for Money: 7.1/10 - Guests had mixed feelings about the value for money, with some finding it reasonable given the location, while others felt the price was slightly high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - The breakfast options were well-received, although there were some suggestions for more variety. The hotel's restaurant also received positive feedback.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience with the hotel and would recommend it to others.
