HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the charm and elegance of Hotel Jerome, Auberge Collection, a true gem in the heart of Aspen.Luxurious AccommodationsEnjoy absolute comfort in one of our 93 exquisitely designed rooms with pillowtop beds, down comforters, and Egyptian cotton sheets. Every detail, from designer toiletries to spacious private bathrooms, is crafted for your indulgence.Gourmet Dining OptionsSavor delectable cuisine at our three on-site restaurants or opt for the convenience of in-room dining. Wind down at one of our cozy bars or cafes, perfect for enjoying a beverage after a day of adventure.Rejuvenating AmenitiesUnplug and recharge with our luxurious spa services, outdoor pool, hot tub, and fully equipped fitness center. Our family-friendly atmosphere allows you to create lasting memories while enjoying concierge services and a fun-filled arcade/game room.Book your stay today for an unforgettable experience!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, although there were a few mentions of minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was consistently praised by guests for being friendly and efficient.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The central location of the hotel near key landmarks and amenities was a highlight for many guests.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Guests found the rooms to be spacious, comfortable, and well-appointed, with minor maintenance mentions.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - While some guests enjoyed the amenities such as the gym and pool, others were disappointed by the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Guests generally praised the breakfast and restaurant services, with minor suggestions for improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a highly positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to return.
