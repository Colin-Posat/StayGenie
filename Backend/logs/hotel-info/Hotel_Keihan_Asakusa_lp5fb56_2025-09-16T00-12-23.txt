HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Asakusa StationHotel Keihan Asakusa is a mere 2-minute walk from Tsukuba Express Asakusa Station, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore the vibrant neighborhood. Just 700 meters away from iconic attractions like Sensoji Temple and Kaminarimon Thunder Gate, the hotel offers easy access to the cultural heart of Tokyo.Modern Comforts and AmenitiesThe hotel boasts modern rooms equipped with free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms. Guests can unwind in air-conditioned rooms featuring cheerful red accents, glass-top desks, and high-tech toilets. The option for massages adds an extra touch of relaxation to your stay.Delicious Breakfast Buffet and Nearby AttractionsStart your day with a delightful breakfast buffet offering a mix of Western and Japanese delicacies, including fresh-baked bread. With Asakusa Subway Station nearby, exploring popular areas like Ueno, Ginza, and Shibuya is convenient for guests. Additionally, the proximity to Kokugikan Sumo Stadium ensures easy access to exciting events.Book your stay at Hotel Keihan Asakusa for a memorable Tokyo experience!
