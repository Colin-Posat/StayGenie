HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern and Stylish AccommodationsExperience the elegance of our bright, air-conditioned rooms with wooden floors and flat-screen satellite TV. Choose rooms with stunning views of the sea or city for a truly memorable stay.Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in our hotel's sauna, steam baths, and well-equipped gym for an additional fee. Treat yourself to a relaxing massage or explore the city with our convenient bicycle rental service. Our 24-hour reception ensures all your needs are met promptly.Prime LocationConveniently situated in the 22@ Technological District, Hotel 4 Barcelona is a short walk from Bogatell Beach and Llacuna Metro Station.
