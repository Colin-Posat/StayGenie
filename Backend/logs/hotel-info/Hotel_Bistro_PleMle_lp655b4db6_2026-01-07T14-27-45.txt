HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Family-Run Hotel in the Heart of BernIndividually Decorated Rooms: Enjoy uniquely designed rooms with free Wi-Fi and TV, some with private shower facilities.On-Site Restaurant and Terrace: Sample homemade sweets at the hotel's restaurant and relax on the terrace with views of the Aare River.Convenient Location: Just a 5-minute walk from the Old Town and near the Bear Pit, with a grocery store and tram stop within minutes.Book now to experience the charm and hospitality of Hotel Landhaus in Bern!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that check-in until 21:00 is only possible on weekdays. On weekends and public holidays, the reception is open until 18:00. If you arrive later, please inform the property in advance.

Please note that there is no elevator at the property. The rooms are only accessible by an antiquated and narrow staircase.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 7 years old and over stay for CHF 19.50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
