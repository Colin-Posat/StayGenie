HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location and Stunning ViewsSituated on the South Bank of the Thames, Park Plaza Westminster Bridge London offers breathtaking views of the Houses of Parliament and Big Ben. Just a short walk away from iconic landmarks like the London Eye, guests can immerse themselves in the vibrant heart of the city.Luxurious Amenities for Ultimate RelaxationIndulge in the hotel's 24-hour gym, a refreshing 15-metre swimming pool, sauna, and steam room. The spacious, modern rooms are equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay, from a flat-screen TV to a well-stocked minibar.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentSavor exquisite dining at Brasserie Joël and Ichi Sushi &amp; Sashimi, or enjoy a cup of authentic Italian coffee at illy Caffè. End your day with live entertainment at Primo Bar, adding a touch of glamour to your evenings.Experience luxury and convenience at Park Plaza Westminster Bridge London - book your stay now for an unforgettable London getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The credit card holder must be present upon arrival along with the card used to make the booking. The hotel does not accept third party credit card payments.

Guests are required to show photo ID and the credit card used upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

Please note that different terms and conditions and policies, which may include pre-paid deposits, will apply to group bookings of more than 9 rooms. The property will contact the guest following their reservation.

If travelling as a family, please remember to add your children to the booking so that we can prepare for your arrival.

Please note that the swimming pool is open from 6:30 am until 10 pm daily. Kids times : 8 am until 8 pm.

Please note that Pool Booking system is in place and a 30 minutes slot must be booked in advance.
