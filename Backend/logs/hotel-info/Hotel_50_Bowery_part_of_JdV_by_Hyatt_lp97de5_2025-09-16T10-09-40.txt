HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and AmenitiesHotel 50 Bowery offers elegantly designed rooms equipped with a desk, bathrobes, Lefroy Brooks rain shower head, and bath amenities from Jonathan Adler. Each room also features an in-room safe for your valuables and is adorned with original artwork, creating a unique and comfortable stay.Convenient FacilitiesEnjoy complimentary WiFi throughout the hotel, a 24-hour fitness center to stay active during your visit, and an onsite restaurant offering delicious dining options. Whether you're here for business or leisure, these facilities cater to all your needs.Prime LocationSituated in the heart of the city, Hotel 50 Bowery provides easy access to popular neighborhoods like Lower East Side, Tribeca, and Soho. With iconic landmarks like Brooklyn Bridge and City Hall just a stone's throw away, your stay promises to be both convenient and memorable.Book your stay at Hotel 50 Bowery now and experience luxury and convenience in the vibrant city of New York.
