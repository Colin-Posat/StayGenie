HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Panoramic ViewsExperience luxury in the heart of Istanbul's historic Sultanahmet district at Hotel Ipek Palas. Indulge in the beautifully decorated rooms with free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and traditional touches. The terrace restaurant offers breathtaking Bosporus views, making dining a memorable experience.Delicious Dining OptionsStart your day with a delightful buffet breakfast at Payitaht Restaurant, featuring homemade jams and organic produce. For lunch and dinner, savor traditional Turkish and international dishes at the terrace restaurant. The a-la-carte menu ensures a culinary journey during your stay.Convenient Services and Prime LocationBenefit from 24-hour reception assistance for tour arrangements and enjoy laundry facilities, car rental, and currency exchange services. With top attractions like Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace within walking distance, Hotel Ipek Palas is the ideal choice for your Istanbul adventure. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 11 years old stay for € 13 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 7 years old to 11 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
