HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Stay in TokyoConvenient Location: Located just a minute walk from JR Sugamo Station, APA Hotel Sugamo Ekimae offers easy access to transportation and nearby attractions like Rikugien Garden.Comfortable Rooms: Enjoy air-conditioned rooms with original mattresses, flat-screen TVs, and en suite bathrooms with free toiletries.Relaxation and Services: Unwind in the public bath or open-air bath, take advantage of massage treatments, and enjoy convenient amenities like free WiFi, luggage storage, and newspaper services.Book now for a relaxing and convenient stay at APA Hotel Sugamo Ekimae in Tokyo!
