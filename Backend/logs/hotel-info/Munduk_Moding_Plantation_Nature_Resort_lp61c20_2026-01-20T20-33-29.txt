HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Escape to Munduk Moding Plantation Nature Resort, a tranquil haven nestled amidst the lush landscapes of Gobleg.Spa IndulgenceRelax and rejuvenate at our full-service spa, where expert therapists offer a range of massages, body treatments, and facials tailored just for you.Recreational BlissDive into leisure with two stunning outdoor swimming pools, an outdoor tennis court, and a soothing sauna.
