HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Impressive Mountain ViewsOVERLOOK Lodge by CERVO Zermatt offers Alpine-style apartments with breathtaking mountain views, just a short distance from Sunegga Bahn Funicular. Enjoy the beauty of Matterhorn glacier paradise only 900 meters away while staying connected with free WiFi throughout the property.Comfortable AccommodationRelax in the cozy seating area of our apartments, some equipped with flat-screen TVs. Prepare your meals in the fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances. Each unit includes a private bathroom and complimentary bed linen for a comfortable stay.Ski and AdventureTake advantage of the ski storage facilities and explore exciting activities like skiing and hiking in the picturesque surroundings. With Matterhorn and Klein Matterhorn nearby, adventure awaits just outside our doors.Book your stay at OVERLOOK Lodge by CERVO Zermatt for an unforgettable Alpine experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Payment before arrival via bank transfer is required. The property will contact you after you book to provide instructions.
Please inform OVERLOOK Lodge by CERVO Zermatt in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note that the main property CERVO Mountain Boutique Resort is closed from 22 April 2018 to 28 June 2018.

Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.
