HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 19th-Century Building:Granvia offers a luxurious stay in a stunning 19th-century building, blending historic charm with modern comfort. The high ceilings and stylish decor of each room create an elegant atmosphere for guests to enjoy.Convenient Location:Situated just 100 meters from the vibrant shops and restaurants of Passeig de Gràcia, Granvia provides easy access to the heart of the city. With Plaza de Catalunya, Las Ramblas, and iconic Gaudí landmarks within walking distance, guests can immerse themselves in the beauty of Barcelona.Terrace and Buffet Breakfast:Guests can start their day with a delicious buffet breakfast and later unwind on the charming terrace with a refreshing drink. The perfect spot to relax after a day of exploring the city's wonders.Experience luxury and convenience at Granvia - Book your stay now!
