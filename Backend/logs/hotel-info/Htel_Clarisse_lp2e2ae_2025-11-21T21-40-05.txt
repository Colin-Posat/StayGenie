HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Renovated RoomsClarisse, a chic hotel in Paris, offers modern rooms renovated in September 2018. Each room features free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom for your comfort.Convenient LocationSituated just 700 metres from Parc des Expositions Exhibition Centre and 350 metres from Metro Porte de Vanves, Clarisse provides easy access to key attractions like Invalides and Saint-Lazare. The T3 tramway stop is conveniently located just opposite the hotel.Delicious BreakfastStart your day right with a delicious breakfast served daily in Clarisse’s dining room. Enjoy a variety of options to kickstart your Parisian adventures.Book your stay at Clarisse now and experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience in the heart of Paris.
