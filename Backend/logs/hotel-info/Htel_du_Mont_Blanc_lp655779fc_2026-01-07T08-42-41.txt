HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in the Latin QuarterLocated in the vibrant Latin Quarter, surrounded by restaurants and bars, Hôtel du Mont Blanc offers a convenient stay just a short stroll away from Notre-Dame Cathedral.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesThe air-conditioned rooms feature LCD TVs with satellite channels and private bathrooms. The simple yet cozy décor ensures a comfortable retreat after a day of exploring the city.Convenient On-Site FacilitiesIndulge in traditional cuisine at the on-site restaurant or enjoy a buffet breakfast in the charming arched stone cellar.
