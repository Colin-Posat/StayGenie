HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Modern RoomsExperience comfort and style just a block away from Waikiki Beach at Renew. Each room features a 32-inch flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a private balcony with stunning views. Relax in the seating area or pamper yourself in the en suite bathroom with plush bathrobes and premium amenities.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy complimentary newspapers, beach towels, and beach gear during your stay. Everything you need for a perfect beach day is right at your fingertips at Renew.Nearby AttractionsExplore the vibrant surroundings with Honolulu Zoo and Ala Wai Golf Course just a short drive away. Don't forget to visit the iconic Diamond Head Crater Park, located a mere 4.3 km from the hotel.Book your stay at Renew now for an unforgettable Hawaiian getaway.
