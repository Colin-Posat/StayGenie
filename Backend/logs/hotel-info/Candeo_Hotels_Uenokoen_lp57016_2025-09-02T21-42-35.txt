HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant heart of Tokyo at Candeo Hotels Uenokoen, where comfort meets convenience.Modern AmenitiesStay connected with complimentary wireless and wired internet access in our 268 well-appointed guestrooms, equipped with refrigerators and LCD televisions. Enjoy relaxation in elegant private bathrooms featuring bidets and complimentary toiletries.Dining ExperienceStart your day right with our buffet breakfast served daily from 7 AM to 9:30 AM, along with delicious dining options available at our on-site restaurant.Convenient LocationDiscover nearby attractions such as Ueno Park, Ueno Zoo, and Sensoji Temple, all within walking distance. Our multilingual staff at the 24-hour front desk ensures a welcoming experience, while self-parking is available for your convenience.Make your reservation now to enjoy an unforgettable stay!
