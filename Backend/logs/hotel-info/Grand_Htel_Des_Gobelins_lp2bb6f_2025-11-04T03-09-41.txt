CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Friendly staff
• Comfortable beds
• Spacious rooms

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• No spa services
• Mixed opinions on pricing and room sizes

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, although some minor maintenance issues were reported.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff. Some guests reported slow responses and confusion with requests.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel is centrally located with easy access to key landmarks, public transport, restaurants, and shops.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and clean, with spacious options available. Some guests mentioned small room sizes and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory, with some positive feedback on the gym and pool. Additionally, some guests expressed a desire for more amenities such as spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Opinions on pricing varied, with some guests finding it expensive for the services offered, while others considered it reasonable given the central location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, although some guests suggested improvements in variety and pricing.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the location, staff friendliness, and cleanliness. Many guests expressed a desire to return.
