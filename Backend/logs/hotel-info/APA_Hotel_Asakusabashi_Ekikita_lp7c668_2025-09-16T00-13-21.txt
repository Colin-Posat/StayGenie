HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Comfort and ConvenienceThis hotel, opened in June 2015, offers modern rooms with unique features like an original mattress for comfort and a 40-inch flat screen TV for entertainment. The en suite bathrooms are equipped with a bathtub and free toiletries for a relaxing stay.Convenient Location and AmenitiesConveniently located near Asakusabashi subway Station, guests can easily explore Tokyo with a 15-minute train ride to Tokyo Station and Tokyo Skytree. The hotel also offers a 24-hour front desk, free WiFi, and free luggage storage for added convenience.Dining and Nearby AttractionsGuests can enjoy dining at the on-site restaurant for breakfast. Explore nearby attractions like the Edo-Tokyo Museum and Ginza shopping area, both just a short train ride away. Don't miss the chance to visit Haneda International Airport, just a 40-minute train ride from the hotel.Book your stay at APA Hotel Asakusabashi-Ekikita for a modern and convenient Tokyo experience.
