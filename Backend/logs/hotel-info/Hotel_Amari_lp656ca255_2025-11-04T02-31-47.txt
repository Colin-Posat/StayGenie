Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests mentioned the cleanliness of the hotel positively, although some reviews highlighted minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated with helpful and friendly staff, although there were mentions of slow response times and restrictive hotel regulations.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location was a standout feature with easy access to public transit and close proximity to major landmarks and dining options.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and quiet, but there were complaints about old furnishings and maintenance issues like noisy heaters and malfunctioning toilets.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - While some guests appreciated the quiet and safe environment, others noted that the amenities were outdated and lacked certain conveniences like consistent heating and disposable slippers.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the hotel offered good value for its location, while others mentioned that the facilities were outdated for the price paid.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - The breakfast offerings and restaurant services received positive feedback, although some guests desired more variety and eco-friendly practices in the food and beverage options.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing the convenient location and cleanliness as key strengths.
