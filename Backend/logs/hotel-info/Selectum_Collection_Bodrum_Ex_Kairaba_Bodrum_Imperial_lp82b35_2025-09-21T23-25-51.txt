HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the ultimate getaway at Selectum Collection Bodrum, where luxury meets tranquility by the sea.Relaxation and WellnessIndulge in rejuvenating treatments at our onsite spa, offering a variety of massages designed to melt your stresses away. After venturing to our private beach, unwind with various recreational amenities, including outdoor tennis courts and a thrilling waterslide.Culinary DelightsSavor the flavors of the Mediterranean at Olympos and our other exquisite dining venues. Enjoy complimentary daily breakfast and indulge in all-inclusive rates that cover meals and beverages at select restaurants, making dining a delightful experience.Comfort and ConvenienceRetreat to one of our 375 air-conditioned rooms, complete with private balconies, LCD televisions, and minibars. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and enjoy a range of featured amenities, including a 24-hour front desk and free self-parking.Don’t miss out on this incredible escape—book your stay at Selectum Collection Bodrum now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* The seasonal pool will be open from April to November.
* The property allows pets in specific rooms only and has other pet restrictions (surcharges apply and can be found in the Fees section). Guests can arrange to bring pets by contacting the property directly, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
