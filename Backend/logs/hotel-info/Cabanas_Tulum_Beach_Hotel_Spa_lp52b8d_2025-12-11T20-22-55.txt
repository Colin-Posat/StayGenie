HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Beachfront Location in TulumSecluded Oasis with Beach Club AccessEscape to Cabanas Tulum for a tranquil getaway. Enjoy private terraces with hammocks and the option for beachfront rooms. Indulge in a rooftop terrace room with a hot tub for the ultimate relaxation.Exquisite Dining and Beach Club AmenitiesSavor international cuisine at Fresco's restaurant, catering to all ages with a children's menu. Unwind at Ziggy's Beach Club with refreshing cocktails and beachside beers, creating the perfect beach escape.Concierge Services and Naj Naay SpaLet our bilingual staff curate eco-friendly tours to Sian Ka'an or cenotes, or explore nearby archaeological sites like Cobá and Chichen Itza. Rejuvenate at Naj Naay Spa for a pampering experience.Book now for a memorable beachfront retreat at Cabanas Tulum!
