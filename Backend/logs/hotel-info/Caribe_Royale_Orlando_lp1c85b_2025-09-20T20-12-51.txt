HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThe Caribe Royale Orlando resort boasts an outdoor pool, hot tub, fitness center, children's playground, and game room. Villas feature fully equipped kitchens and screened patios, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Exciting ActivitiesGuests can indulge in the waterfall pool with a 75-foot waterslide, relax in hot tubs, or rent private cabanas. The resort also offers fishing, bike rentals, and a Sport Court, providing entertainment for all ages.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a blend of Latin and Caribbean flavors at Calypso’s Pool Bar &amp; Grille, or savor contemporary steaks and seafood at The Venetian Chop House. With a selection of wines to complement your meal, dining at the resort is a true delight.Experience luxury, fun, and exquisite dining at Caribe Royale Orlando - your perfect retreat near Walt Disney World®.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
The resort fee is $35 plus taxes per night and includes:

- in-suite standard WiFi (4 devices).
- Shuttle to Disney Parks.
- In-suite coffee; fitness center access.
- Pickleball & Padel Tennis (1p-5p with reservation).
- Unlimited bike use, fishing, and basketball.
- Movies Under the Stars (weekends).
- Running trail.
- Local and 800# calls.

Please note that WiFi is available for up to 2 additional devices for a charge of USD 4.95 per day.

Please note that reservations are recommended for the on-site restaurant, The Venetian Chop House.

Non-registered Hotel Day guest parking price: $32.00 per day (plus tax, per vehicle)
Hotel guest parking price: $29.00 plus taxes, per vehicle
