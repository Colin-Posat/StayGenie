HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Terrace with Hot Tub and PoolRiad Al Rimal boasts a stunning rooftop terrace with a hot tub, perfect for relaxing under the Moroccan sun. The central courtyard houses a refreshing pool, offering a tranquil oasis in the heart of Marrakech Medina.Traditional Moroccan Decor and CuisineImmerse yourself in the rich culture of Morocco with the traditional decor of Riad Al Rimal's rooms. Indulge in a delicious daily breakfast and savor authentic Moroccan dishes upon request in the dining room, providing a true taste of the local cuisine.Convenient Amenities and ServicesFrom organizing tour excursions to facilitating airport transfers, Riad Al Rimal ensures a seamless stay for its guests. Unwind in the traditional lounge or explore nearby attractions such as the iconic Jemaa El Fna Square and the historic Koutoubia Mosque.Experience the charm of Marrakech at Riad Al Rimal - Book now for an unforgettable stay!
