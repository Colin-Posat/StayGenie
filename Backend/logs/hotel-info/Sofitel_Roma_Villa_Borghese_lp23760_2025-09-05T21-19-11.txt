Enjoy cocktails and tapas at the panoramic rooftop lounge with breathtaking views of the city.Prime LocationLocated near Villa Borghese gardens, this hotel is in Rome's prestigious area, close to Via Veneto and Spanish Steps. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests noticing stains on bedding and raising complaints, while others found the hotel clean.
• Service: 8.9/10 - Generally exceptional service highlighted by helpful and friendly staff, with some discrepancies in the experience with bell staff.
• Location: 9.2/10 - Highly praised location close to key landmarks, with easy access to public transportation and attractions.
• Room Quality: 8.7/10 - Rooms described as comfortable, well-lit, and with modern decor, though some minor maintenance issues were mentioned.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - Satisfactory amenities with positive mentions of the gym, pool, and rooftop bar, although some guests desired additional services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, although some guests suggested more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hotel for its location, service, and comfortable accommodations.
