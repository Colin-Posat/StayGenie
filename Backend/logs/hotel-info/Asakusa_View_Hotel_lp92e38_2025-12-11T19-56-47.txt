HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Art Deco Style and Convenient LocationAsakusa View Hotel boasts an elegant art deco style and is conveniently connected to Asakusa Station. Just a short walk from Tawaramachi Station, this hotel offers easy access to Tokyo's attractions.Modern Rooms and Relaxing AmenitiesThe warmly lit rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows at Asakusa View Hotel provide a cozy retreat. Guests can unwind in the attached bathroom with a bathtub and enjoy complimentary toiletries. For relaxation, there's an indoor swimming pool, massage services, and currency exchange facilities.Dining with a View and Cultural DelightsIndulge in French cuisine at Makie or savor traditional Chinese dishes at Karakurenai while enjoying panoramic city views. Musashi offers a buffet spread with stunning city views. Don't miss the chance to visit Senso-ji Temple and Asakusa Shrine, both just a short walk away.Book your stay at Asakusa View Hotel for a memorable Tokyo experience!

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  JPY 2000 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for ¥5,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
