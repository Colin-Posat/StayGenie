HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Ottoman ExperienceImmerse yourself in the charm of a restored Ottoman mansion at HHK Hotel. Indulge in the opulence of palace-like interiors, traditional ornaments, and a Turkish bath, all while enjoying breathtaking views of Bosphorus and Golden Horn from the rooftop terrace.Elegant Rooms and Spectacular ViewsEach uniquely decorated room at HHK Hotel offers a blend of Ottoman style and modern amenities, including air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and stunning sea views. Relax in comfort surrounded by luxurious beddings, chandeliers, and lavish bathrooms.Culinary Delights and RelaxationSavor delicious meals on the terrace, accompanied by panoramic views and a glass of wine.
