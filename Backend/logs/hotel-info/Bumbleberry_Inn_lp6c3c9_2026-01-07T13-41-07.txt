HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Scenic Views of Zion MountainsExperience the beauty of Springdale, Utah with views of the Zion Mountains right from your private balcony at Bumbleberry Inn. Each room offers a cozy retreat with modern amenities including free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a microwave.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by the seasonal outdoor heated swimming pool and hot tub, or take advantage of the free shuttle service to Zion National Park for a day of adventure. After exploring the outdoors, return to Bumbleberry Inn to lounge by the pool and soak in the serene surroundings.Convenient LocationLocated just 5 minutes from the entrance to Zion National Park, Bumbleberry Inn also offers easy access to Coral Pink Sand Dunes State Park and Bryce Canyon for further exploration. Book your stay now and make unforgettable memories in the heart of Utah's natural beauty.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The seasonal pool and hot tub at this property are open from April to September but dates may very. Contact the property for more information.

Please note that reception is open from 0800 to 1800 during November through February and from 0800 to 2200 during March through October. If you are arriving after hours, a phone number will be available on the entry door.

Please note this hotel does not have an elevator.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for US$10 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
