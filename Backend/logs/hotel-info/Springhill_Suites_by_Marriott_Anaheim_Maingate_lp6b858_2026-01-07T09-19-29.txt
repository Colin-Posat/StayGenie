• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location, while others found it high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Feedback on the breakfast and dining options was generally positive, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to return.
