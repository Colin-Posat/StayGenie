HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for US$100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 105 per  day.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Great location
• Comfortable beds

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited spa services
• Issues with room quality
• Unfriendly and inattentive rooftop bar staff

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although there were some complaints about staff in the rooftop bar being unfriendly and inattentive.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Guests experienced issues with room quality, including the need to switch rooms multiple times due to various issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with guests praising the comfortable beds and spacious bathrooms, but expressing disappointment over the limited spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the experience provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, with some guests suggesting more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
