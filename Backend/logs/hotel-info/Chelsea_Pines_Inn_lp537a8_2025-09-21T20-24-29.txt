HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Film-themed CharmThe Chelsea Pines Inn, nestled in Chelsea and the Meatpacking District, is a unique haven for film enthusiasts. Decorated with vintage Hollywood film posters, this five-story walk-up offers a nostalgic ambiance that transports guests to the Golden Age of Hollywood.Cozy AmenitiesGuest rooms at the Inn are thoughtfully equipped with modern conveniences such as a flat-screen TV, iPod docking station, and complimentary WiFi. The provision of a refrigerator, safe, and ironing essentials ensures a comfortable stay, while the private bathroom stocked with complimentary toiletries adds a touch of luxury.Relaxation and ConvenienceWith 24-hour concierge services, a charming lounge, and an indoor greenhouse, guests can unwind in style. The seasonal inner garden provides a tranquil escape, complemented by all-day snacks and easy access to popular attractions like Chelsea Market and the 14th Street subway station.Experience the allure of old Hollywood at Chelsea Pines Inn - book your stay now for a memorable and cozy retreat.
