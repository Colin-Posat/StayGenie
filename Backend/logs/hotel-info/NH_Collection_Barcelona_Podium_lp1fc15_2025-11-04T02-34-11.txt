HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Modern Accommodation in Central BarcelonaNH Collection Barcelona Pódium offers a blend of 19th-century charm and contemporary design, located in the serene heart of Barcelona. Luxurious Amenities for Relaxation include a sauna, gym, and a rooftop terrace with a picturesque swimming pool offering stunning city views. The spacious, air-conditioned rooms feature a choice of pillows, a minibar, and a Barcelona city guidebook for your convenience.Prime Location for ExploringSituated near iconic landmarks like Plaça Catalunya and the Ramblas, the hotel provides easy access to Barcelona's Gothic Quarter, Ciutadella Park, and the Olympic Port. Convenient Transport Options are available with the Arc de Triomf Metro Station just a stone's throw away.Delightful Dining ExperienceIndulge in Mediterranean delicacies at the hotel's restaurant, unwind at the lounge bar, and start your day right with a healthy breakfast. Book your stay at NH Collection Barcelona Pódium for an unforgettable Barcelona experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that The Bistro Restaurant is closed during the month of August. The rest of the year it is open every day from 11:00 to 13:00.

Please note that for reservations of 8 rooms or more, special conditions and charges apply.

The name on the credit card used for the booking must be presented on arrival and it should correspond to the guest staying at the property.

Dogs and cats are allowed with a maximum weight of 25 kg. A charge of EUR 35 per night will be applied (maximum of 2 pets per room).

Guide dogs are allowed free of charge.

Pets can not stay alone in the room, except for the time the owner is using other services in the Hotel (Breakfast, Dinner, Gym…). Please call the hotel for full information and pet rules.

• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was a highlight for many guests, with staff being friendly, accommodating, and going above and beyond. However, a few guests had complaints about long wait times.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The central location near key attractions and public transport was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned issues with cleanliness and room maintenance.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - While some guests appreciated the provided amenities such as the gym and pool, others felt there was a lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Views on value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was too high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with many willing to recommend the hotel to others.
