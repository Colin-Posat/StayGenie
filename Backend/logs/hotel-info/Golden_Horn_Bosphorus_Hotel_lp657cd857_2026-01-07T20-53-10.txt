HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Golden Horn Bosphorus Hotel offers 40 rooms, including accessible options, family rooms, and presidential suites, all designed to provide a blend of comfort and opulence.Prime LocationNestled behind the historic Sirkeci GAR, now the Marmaray metro station, the hotel provides breathtaking views of the iconic Bosphorus and easy access to both European and Asian sides of Istanbul.Ideal for FamiliesWith its spacious rooms and proximity to popular landmarks like the Hagia Sophia and Grand Bazaar, the hotel is a convenient choice for families looking to explore Istanbul's rich history and vibrant culture.Book now to experience luxury, convenience, and unforgettable views at the Golden Horn Bosphorus Hotel in Istanbul!

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 9 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
