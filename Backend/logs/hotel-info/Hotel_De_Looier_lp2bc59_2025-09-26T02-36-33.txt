HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in AmsterdamHotel de Looier is nestled in a tranquil street at the heart of Amsterdam, just a short 5-minute stroll to the vibrant Leidseplein filled with bars and cafés.Comfortable Rooms and AmenitiesEach room at our hotel features a private bathroom and convenient coffee and tea facilities, ensuring a cozy and relaxing stay. Start your day right with a delicious continental breakfast served in our breakfast room.Proximity to Major AttractionsWith Amsterdam's top attractions within a 15-minute walking radius, including the museum area and the iconic Anne Frank House just 10 minutes away, exploring the city couldn't be more convenient. Plus, easy access to public transport near the hotel adds to the overall accessibility.Experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Hotel de Looier - book your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please inform Hotel De Looier in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of EUR 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that guests are required to show the credit card used to make the booking upon arrival. If this is not possible, guests should have an authorization form, signed by the credit card holder if he/she is not traveling.

If guests cannot provide an authorization form, they are required to pay a deposit of EUR 250 in cash upon arrival at the property.
