HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLess than 10 minutes' walk to the Oregon Convention Center and The Rose Garden Arena, the Crowne Plaza Hotel Portland-Downtown Convention Center offers easy access to key attractions and venues in Portland.Modern AmenitiesEach room at the hotel is equipped with a Keurig coffeemaker, a microwave, and a mini-refrigerator, ensuring guests have all the comforts of home. Additionally, enjoy entertainment with a DVD player and access to a DVD lending library, as well as cable TV.On-Site FacilitiesGuests can take advantage of the indoor pool, sports court, 24-hour fitness center, and rental car desk during their stay. With MAX Light Rail and Streetcar stops right in front of the hotel, exploring the city is convenient and hassle-free.Book your stay at the Crowne Plaza Hotel Portland-Downtown Convention Center for a memorable and comfortable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
If you book a room with breakfast included the rate, please note this only applies to two guests staying in the room. Any additional guests will pay the full price for breakfast.

Please note: The property provides complimentary pick up at the airport and provides drop off service for a minimal fee. Shuttle service is based on availability and it is best to contact the hotel beforehand to confirm availability.

Upon arrival the hotel will authorize the debit/credit card provided for room and tax plus $75 for incidentals. Prepaid reservations will be authorized for the $75 incidental hold. This is a temporary hold and will be released after payment at checkout. Please allow 3-7 business days to see the hold disappear from the account depending on your financial institutions policies.
