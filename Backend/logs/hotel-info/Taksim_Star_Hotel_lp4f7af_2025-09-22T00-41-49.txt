HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Centrally Located Near Istiklal AvenueTaksim Star Hotel is conveniently situated just 50 metres from the vibrant Istiklal Avenue, offering easy access to numerous dining, entertainment, and shopping options.Comfortable Rooms with Stunning ViewsThe hotel features air-conditioned rooms equipped with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and minibars. Some rooms even boast balconies with breathtaking views of the Bosphorus or Istanbul city centre.Relaxation and Convenience at Your FingertipsGuests can unwind in the indoor pool, indulge in rejuvenating massage treatments, and enjoy steam baths at a nearby facility. Additionally, the hotel provides services such as currency exchange and airport shuttle for a hassle-free stay.Book your stay at Taksim Star Hotel now to experience the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and luxury.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 7 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
