IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
When sending packages and luggage to the property, please write the booking number, check-in date and full name (English alphabet) of the guest used to make the reservation on the packing slip. Mail without the above information may not be accepted by the property.

Please note, on-site luggage storage is available only on the day of check-in and check-out.

The full amount of the reservation must be paid at check-in.

Guests arriving after check-in hours must inform the property in advance. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
