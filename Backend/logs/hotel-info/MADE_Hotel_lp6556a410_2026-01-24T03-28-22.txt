HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Accommodations with City ViewsMADE Hotel in New York offers select rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows and modern amenities like air conditioning, bathrobes, and Samsung® flat-screen TVs. Some units even boast stunning city views.Diverse Dining OptionsGuests can enjoy an American breakfast at the property, savor tapas at Debajo, grab coffee at Paper, and unwind with craft cocktails at the Lobby Bar. The hotel provides a range of culinary experiences to suit every palate.Rooftop Lounge and PatioWith a patio on the 2nd floor and the Good Behavior lounge on the 18th floor, guests can relax in style while taking in panoramic views of New York City. Indulge in Tiki-inspired cocktails and craft beers at this chic urban retreat.Ready to experience the vibrant atmosphere of MADE Hotel? Book your stay now!
