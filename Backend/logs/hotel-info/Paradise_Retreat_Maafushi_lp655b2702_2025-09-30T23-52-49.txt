Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests generally appreciated the clean rooms, but some mentioned issues like blocked sinks and old facilities.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was highlighted as exceptional by most guests, with friendly and helpful staff, but some reviews mentioned communication issues and delayed responses.
• Location: 7/10 - The location was seen as convenient by some, but others found it far from the main areas of interest with unappealing surroundings.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Room quality received mixed reviews with comments on spaciousness, but concerns about aging facilities, discolored ceilings, and the need for renovations.
• Amenities: 6.2/10 - Some guests appreciated the amenities like the gym and pool, while others found them lacking with complaints about no juice for breakfast and additional spa services.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some feeling the price was justified by the services offered, but others found it expensive for what was provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests desired more variety and quality in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Despite mixed reviews across categories, most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
