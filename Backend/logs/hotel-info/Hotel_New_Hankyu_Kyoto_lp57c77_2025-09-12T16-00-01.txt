HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Kyoto Station:Hotel New Hankyu is conveniently located right across from Kyoto Station, making it the perfect base for exploring the city. With easy access to transportation options, guests can easily navigate around Kyoto and beyond.Diverse Dining Options and Amenities:The hotel boasts 5 restaurants offering a variety of cuisines, from traditional Kaiseki meals to freshly grilled teppanyaki dishes. Additionally, guests can enjoy the convenience of a tour desk, 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and currency exchange services.Comfortable Rooms with Modern Amenities:Each air-conditioned room at Hotel New Hankyu Kyoto is equipped with essential amenities such as an air purifier, flat-screen TV, and a private bath. Guests can also enjoy free WiFi throughout the property and access to drinks vending machines for added convenience.Experience the convenience and comfort of Hotel New Hankyu Kyoto. Book your stay now for a memorable trip to Kyoto!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
From Kansai International Airport (KIX), take the Haruka directly to JR Kyoto Station. The hotel is located across the street from Kyoto Station's Central Gate and one block from Kyoto Tower.

On-site private parking is available. Guests must make reservations in advance. Please contact the property for details.
