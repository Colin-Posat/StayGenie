HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Wi-Fi AccessHotel Le Twelve offers modern rooms decorated in neutral tones and patterned fabrics. Each room features an LCD TV, a private bathroom, and views of the street or a furnished terrace, perfect for relaxing with a drink.Convenient LocationLocated in the 12th district of Paris, Hotel Le Twelve is just a short walk from popular landmarks like Place de la Nation, Place de la Bastille, and Bois de Vincennes. With Porte de Vincennes Metro Station nearby, exploring attractions such as Le Louvre and Disneyland Paris is easy.Delicious Breakfast and Nearby Dining OptionsStart your day with a delightful continental breakfast served daily. Additionally, private parking is available within 900 meters, and a variety of restaurants are within walking distance for a taste of local cuisine.Experience comfort and convenience at Hotel Le Twelve - book your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hotel Le Twelve in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please note that for stays of 5 nights or more a security deposit will be requested. The property will contact you directly to organise this.

Guests arriving outside reception opening hours will require a code to access the hotel. To receive the code, please contact the hotel prior to arrival using the contact details found on the booking confirmation.

Secure private parking is available on request at a location 900 metres from the hotel. Guests are advised to reserve a place by contacting the hotel prior to arrival.

Please note that an extra bed can only be accommodated in the Superior Double Room.

Please note the hotel close at midnight, you have to contact the property if you arrive after that time.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 19 per  day.
