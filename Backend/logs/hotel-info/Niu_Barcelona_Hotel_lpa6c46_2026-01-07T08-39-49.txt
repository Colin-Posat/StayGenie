HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Pool with a ViewNiu Barcelona Hotel offers a unique experience with its rooftop outdoor pool, providing guests with a stunning view of the city while they relax and unwind.Comfort and ConvenienceEach room at the hotel is equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, air conditioning for the summer months, and heating for the winter. The private bathrooms feature a shower and a hairdryer for added convenience.Exceptional ServiceWith a 24-hour front desk, guests can expect top-notch service throughout their stay. As a welcoming gesture, each guest receives a complimentary bottle of water upon arrival.Experience luxury and comfort at Niu Barcelona Hotel. Book now to enjoy a memorable stay in the heart of Barcelona!

Please note that the terrace and pool will remain closed from 1 October to 31 March.

When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies may apply and the hotel reserves the right to charge a 50% nonrefundable deposit in advance.

If your reservation is nonrefundable, you will receive an email from the establishment with instructions to complete the payment quickly and safely after confirmation. The hotel can initiate the process of canceling the reservation in the event that the payment is not completed within the next 48 hours.

The air conditioning system provides hot air in winter and cool air in summer.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
