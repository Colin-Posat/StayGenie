Convenient Location near San Antonio River WalkLess than 1.5 km from the iconic San Antonio River Walk, La Quinta Inn and Suites offers a prime location for your stay in San Antonio. Explore the city with ease and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere just steps away from the hotel.Relaxing Amenities for a Comfortable StayUnwind in the outdoor pool, stay active at the fitness centre, and stay connected with free WiFi in all rooms. Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.

Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
