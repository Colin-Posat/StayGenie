HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Free WiFiAmsterdam ID Aparthotel, located just 200 meters from Amsterdam Sloterdijk Station, offers spacious and modern apartments with free WiFi. The bright, non-smoking apartments feature a blend of modern décor and authentic Dutch elements, including a fully equipped kitchen and a flat-screen TV.Shared Facilities and Dining OptionsGuests can relax in the lounge area, work out in the fitness center, or unwind in the sauna. The hotel also offers a bar, secured parking, and laundry facilities. Dining is convenient with on-site restaurant The 51 serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. For entertainment, there's a playroom with a foosball table, pool table, and dartboard.Convenient Location and Meeting SpacesSituated just a 6-minute train ride from Amsterdam’s historic city center, the hotel is also close to Westergasfabriek, Schiphol airport, and the RAI convention center. With meeting spaces available for various group sizes, Amsterdam ID Aparthotel caters to both leisure and business travelers.Book your stay at Amsterdam ID Aparthotel for a blend of modern comfort and Dutch charm.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Amsterdam ID Aparthotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of EUR 100 is required. The host charges this 7 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that the credit card used to make the booking must be presented upon check-in as a guarantee. If applicable, the property will use these credit card details for incidentals and extras. If you are using a third-party credit card, please contact Amsterdam ID Aparthotel for more information.
Above all, any prepayment from €1100 may need to be verified with a payment link.

Please note that Amsterdam ID Aparthotel does not accept any cash payments.

The main booker must be minimum 21 years old and the name of the main booker has to be the same as on the credit card. The credit card will be checked upon arrival.

Please note that the apartment will not be cleaned daily. When staying 7 days or longer, the apartment is cleaned once a week.

Linens are also changed once a week. Towels are changed after 3 days.

Guests will receive 1 welcome package upon arrival. This includes a dishcloth, dish towel, detergent, one dishwasher tablet, amenities and toilet paper. Please note that this will only be provided once.

Please note that breakfast is available upon request for a surcharge. This can be arranged upon check-in.

Please notify the property in advance if you wish to bring children.

Please note that drug usage and noise disturbances are not allowed and may result in immediate eviction from the property without refund.

The deposit may vary per room type. The guest will be notified at check-in.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 16 years old stay for € 35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
