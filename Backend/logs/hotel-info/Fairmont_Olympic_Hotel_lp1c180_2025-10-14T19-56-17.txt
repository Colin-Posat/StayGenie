HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Culinary ExcellenceListed on the National Register of Historic Places, the Fairmont Olympic Hotel in downtown Seattle offers a truly unique experience. Indulge in multiple award-winning dining options while surrounded by French-inspired furnishings and breathtaking city views in your room.Luxurious Amenities and Personalized ServiceEach guest room is equipped with Cable TV, a minibar, designer toiletries, and terry robes for your comfort. Enjoy fresh air through the windows and make use of the fitness centre, indoor pool, and 24-hour concierge service. Shop to your heart's content without leaving the hotel premises.Prime Location and Nearby AttractionsConveniently located, the hotel is steps away from the 5th Avenue Theatre, a short walk from the Convention Center, and within minutes of Pike Place Market. Immerse yourself in the vibrant city life while staying at the Fairmont Olympic Hotel.Book now for an unforgettable stay at the Fairmont Olympic Hotel and experience luxury at its finest.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and costs  USD  36.38 per  24 hours.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 81.09 per  day.
