HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Charming Ambiance: Hotel Amalfi is conveniently located in the heart of Amalfi, steps away from Saint Andrew's Cathedral. Relax in the peaceful garden surrounded by citrus fruit trees.Comfortable Accommodation: Each guest room is equipped with satellite TV and air conditioning, with some rooms offering a private balcony for a cozy retreat. Start your day right with a delicious breakfast served on the terrace overlooking the city.Relaxing Common Areas: Unwind in the reading room or catch up on your favorite shows in the TV lounge. Whether you're here for leisure or business, Hotel Amalfi provides a welcoming atmosphere for all guests.To book your stay at Hotel Amalfi and experience the charm of Amalfi, make your reservation today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hotel Amalfi in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Payment before arrival by secure online link is required. The property will contact you after you book to provide instructions.

Please note the entire booked amount will be charged in case of early departure.

Please note that parking is available only upon request and must be confirmed by the property.

Please note that the property is accessed via a flight of 20 stairs.

Please note that payment is due before arrival, Hotel Amalfi will provide detailed payment instructions, for example a link to a secured payment platform.

The Hotel stands in a Restricted Traffic Area (ZTL). Please do not access by car.
