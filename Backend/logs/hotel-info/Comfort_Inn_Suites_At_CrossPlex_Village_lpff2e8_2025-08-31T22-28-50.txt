HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AccommodationsComfort Inn &amp; Suites at CrossPlex Village in Birmingham offers stylish and comfortable 3-star accommodations. Each guest room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a spacious desk for your convenience.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy a range of amenities during your stay, including a fitness centre, free private parking, and a shared lounge. Indulge in buffet and continental breakfast options each morning to start your day off right. Plus, take advantage of room service, a 24-hour front desk, and complimentary WiFi throughout the property.Nearby AttractionsLocated just a short distance from Legion Field and BJCC, this non-smoking hotel also provides easy access to Samford University and Alabama Splash Adventure. With Birmingham-Shuttlesworth International Airport nearby, your travel plans are made simple. Book your stay at Comfort Inn &amp; Suites at CrossPlex Village for a memorable experience.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Most guests highlighted the cleanliness of the rooms, although a few mentioned issues like filthy floors.
• Service: 8.4/10 - The hotel staff received positive feedback for being friendly and helpful, with only a few complaints about slow check-in and water pressure.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks was appreciated, although a guest noted it was not as expected in one case.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Guests generally found the rooms comfortable and well-equipped, but there were comments about furniture wear and cleanliness issues.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Some guests praised the amenities like the gym and pool, while others noted the lack of a spa and maintenance issues in rooms.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions on value varied, with some feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally received positive feedback, with some guests suggesting more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, with only a few complaints impacting the rating.
