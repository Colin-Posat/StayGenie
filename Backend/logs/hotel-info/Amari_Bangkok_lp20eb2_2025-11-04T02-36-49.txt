HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 3 years old and over stay for THB 1,766 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the pricing, with some guests feeling it was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, but some guests wanted more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
