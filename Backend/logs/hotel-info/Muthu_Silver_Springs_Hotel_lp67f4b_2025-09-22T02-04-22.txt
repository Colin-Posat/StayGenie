HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationModern AmenitiesDining OptionsBook your stay at Silver Springs Hotel for a luxurious experience just steps away from Nairobi National Park. Enjoy modern amenities such as air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, a fitness centre, and a restaurant serving delicious meals. Indulge in all-day dining options and relax in spacious suites with elegant decor. Explore the local area with excursions arranged by the tour desk, or visit Nairobi Hospital just 100 metres away. Don't miss out on the opportunity to experience comfort and convenience at Silver Springs Hotel.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 10 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 11 years old stay for US$40 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
