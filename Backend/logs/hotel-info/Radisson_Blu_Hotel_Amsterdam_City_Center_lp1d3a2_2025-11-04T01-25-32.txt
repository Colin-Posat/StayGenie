HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Unique Themes: Experience the charm of our spacious rooms decorated with colourful themes like the Golden Age and Naturally Cool, offering free Wi-Fi and a cozy seating area with an LCD TV.Convenient Location: Located in a quiet part of Amsterdam's center, just a short walk from Dam Square, our hotel provides easy access to popular attractions like Waterlooplein and the Kalverstraat shopping street. The nearby Spui Tram Stop connects you to key business districts and other parts of the city.Dining Options: Indulge in international cuisine and a generous breakfast buffet at our on-site restaurant, or unwind with a drink at the bar. With additional dining choices just a stone's throw away, culinary delights are always within reach.Book your stay at Radisson Blu Hotel Amsterdam for a memorable and comfortable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please contact the Radisson Blu Hotel Amsterdam directly if you would like to stay with more than two people in one room and if you have any other special requests. Contact details are stated in the booking confirmation. Extra beds are only available upon request and against a supplement charge. All special requests are only guaranteed after confirmation from the hotel. The hotel will check the validity of the credit card. If the credit card is proven to be invalid, you will be provided 24 hours to provide the hotel with a new valid credit card. The credit card holder must be present upon arrival with the credit card used for the booking. The hotel only accepts credit card authorization forms and payments from travel agents and companies. Making a reservation for the private parking garage is recommended. A maximum height of 190 cm is allowed in the parking and it costs EUR 62.50EUR per day.

Please note, the Radisson Blu Hotel, Amsterdam City Center is a cashless hotel and only accepts card and contactless payments.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 62.50 per  day.
