Please note the method of payment used to make the reservation must be presented upon check-in. Contact hotel for details.

Guests must be at least 21 years of age to check-in.

Roll-away beds can only be accommodated in king rooms for a fee and must be booked in advance of the guest's stay.  Please contact the hotel for more information.

A Daily Destination Fee of $25 plus tax (17.4%) be added to the room rate, which includes the following:
 •	Daily Enhanced Internet
•	Daily F&B Credit of $25
•	2 passes daily admission to the Chicago Architectural Center
•	2 passes daily admission to the Chicago Sports museum

Destination Fees will be charged at the hotel. For pre-paid reservations, only the prepayment for room and tax will be charged upon booking and the destination fee plus taxes will be due at the hotel after check in.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 4.7/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as an issue, with complaints about old linens, dirty sheets, and sticky items.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service was appreciated by most guests, but there were complaints about hidden fees and lack of flexibility in date changes.
• Location: 9.3/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its proximity to key landmarks and the riverwalk, making it ideal for exploring the city.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, with some complaints about small room size and discrepancies between advertised features and actual room.
• Amenities: 5.5/10 - Some guests found the amenities lacking for the high price, with issues such as non-functioning refrigerator and extra charges for WiFi.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for basic services and hidden fees.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with positive comments on the quality of food.
• Overall Experience: 6/10 - Mixed overall experience, with positive feedback on location and staff, but negative comments on cleanliness, hidden fees, and room quality discrepancies.
