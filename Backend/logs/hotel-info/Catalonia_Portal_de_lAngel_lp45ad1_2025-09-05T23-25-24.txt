HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortExperience the perfect blend of history and modern amenities at Catalonia Portal de l'Angel. Housed in a 19th-century building, this hotel offers comfortable rooms with air conditioning, satellite TV, and free Wi-Fi.Outdoor Pool OasisRelax in style at the outdoor pool surrounded by beautiful painted murals. Enjoy a refreshing swim or soak up the sun in this tranquil oasis in the heart of Barcelona.Prime LocationLocated between Plaza de Catalunya and the Gothic Quarter, Catalonia Portal de l'Angel is just steps away from Las Ramblas and Barcelona Cathedral. With easy access to public transportation, exploring the city is a breeze.Book your stay now at Catalonia Portal de l'Angel for an unforgettable Barcelona experience!
