HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Victorian Elegance and Modern ComfortsExperience the charm of a restored mansion in the trendy Woollahra district. The Hughenden Boutique Hotel offers a unique blend of elegant Victorian interiors and modern amenities, catering to all your needs.Art and Entertainment at Your DoorstepImmerse yourself in culture with antique and art galleries surrounding the hotel. Conveniently located near Sydney's top entertainment and sports venues, including the Sydney Cricket Ground and the Hordern Pavilion, the Hughenden ensures you'll never have a dull moment.Pet-Friendly ParadiseAt the Hughenden, we welcome your furry friends with open arms. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Hughenden Boutique Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of AUD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Guests must pre-book parking directly with The Hughenden Boutique Hotel to ensure a space is available (charges apply). Please contact the property for more information, using the contact details found on the booking confirmation.
