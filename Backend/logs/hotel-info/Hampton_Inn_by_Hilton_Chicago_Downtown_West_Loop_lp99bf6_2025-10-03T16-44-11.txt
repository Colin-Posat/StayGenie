HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in Downtown ChicagoLocated just 1 mile from the vibrant Magnificent Mile, Hampton Inn by Hilton Chicago Downtown West Loop offers easy access to top shopping, dining, and entertainment options. Explore the bustling West Loop neighborhood and enjoy the bustling city life right at your doorstep.Modern Amenities for Ultimate ComfortEach room is equipped with a flat-screen LCD HDTV for your entertainment, and some rooms even offer stunning views of downtown Chicago. Stay connected with free WiFi access and start your day right with a complimentary hot breakfast. Keep up with your fitness routine at the on-site fitness center.Convenience at Your FingertipsTake advantage of the 24-hour business center for all your work needs, and satisfy your cravings anytime with the 24-hour snack shop. Unwind at the end of the day at Fulton Tap, the hotel's onsite bar, and enjoy a selection of craft beers at your own pace.Experience the best of Chicago at Hampton Inn by Hilton Chicago Downtown West Loop. Book your stay now for a memorable city getaway.
