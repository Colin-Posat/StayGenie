HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationLocated in Bedugul, Giriwood Hotel &amp; Villa offers a luxurious stay with an outdoor swimming pool, garden, and a shared lounge. The property boasts a restaurant serving delectable dishes and a bar where guests can unwind.Comfort and ConvenienceEach room at the 4-star hotel is elegantly furnished with a desk, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. Guests can enjoy free WiFi, coffee machine, and scenic mountain views from select rooms. The hotel also provides recreational activities like billiards, table tennis, and darts for entertainment.Convenient AmenitiesWith 24-hour room service and a front desk, guests' needs are promptly attended to. Additionally, car hire services are available for exploring the area conveniently. Book your stay at Giriwood Hotel &amp; Villa for a memorable experience.
