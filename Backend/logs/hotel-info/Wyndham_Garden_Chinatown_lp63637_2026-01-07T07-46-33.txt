HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience comfort and style at Wyndham Garden Chinatown. Each room features a flat-screen TV, refrigerator, and premium rooms even offer a balcony with stunning city views. The en suite bathrooms are equipped with all the essentials, including a hairdryer.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in a culinary delight at The Bowery Beer Garden, offering a wide selection of draft beers and delicious American cuisine. For a more upscale experience, head to Rooftop 93 Bar &amp; Lounge on the 19th floor, where you can enjoy craft cocktails with a view of the iconic Empire State Building. Don't forget to check out the vibrant Wu Room for cocktails, music, and dancing.Convenient LocationSituated in the heart of Chinatown, our hotel provides easy access to popular attractions like the National September 11 Memorial &amp; Museum. With the Grand Street subway station just steps away, exploring the city is a breeze.Book your stay at Wyndham Garden Chinatown now and elevate your NYC experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:

PETS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 55 per  day.
