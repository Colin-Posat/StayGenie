HOTEL DESCRIPTION:

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Overall, guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, although some reviews mentioned issues like hair in the shower.
• Service: 7.3/10 - Service at the hotel received mixed feedback, with some guests praising the helpful staff and others noting indifferent or unhelpful behavior.
• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's location near Joshua Tree National Park was highlighted as a positive aspect by many guests, providing easy access to the park.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests generally found the rooms to be spacious, clean, and comfortable, with some minor noise and maintenance issues mentioned.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating the cleanliness and comfort while others mentioned noise from appliances and a lack of water at breakfast.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the hotel offered a good deal while others found certain aspects overpriced for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Feedback on food and beverage was mixed, with breakfast quality being a point of contention. Some guests praised the breakfast while others found certain items lacking in taste.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, citing aspects such as cleanliness, helpful staff, and a convenient location.
