HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AccommodationsThe Hotel Indigo San Antonio-Riverwalk offers modern rooms with 42-inch flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi. Each room features wooden floors, CD players, refrigerators, and comfortable seating areas. Opt for a river view room for a picturesque stay.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy Southern American cuisine at Phi Restaurant and Bar, take a dip in the outdoor pool, or stay active in the gym. The hotel also provides a business center, 24-hour reception, and easy access to nearby attractions like the San Antonio Art Museum and the Henry B. Gonzalez Convention Center.Ideal LocationLocated just 0.7 miles from The Alamo, Hotel Indigo is a short drive from the San Antonio Zoo and Aquarium. Experience the best of San Antonio with a stay at our conveniently located hotel.Book your stay at Hotel Indigo San Antonio-Riverwalk now for a memorable experience!
