HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location on Champs Élysées Avenue: Experience luxury and convenience at Fraser Suites Le Claridge Champs-Elysées, offering fully equipped suites with free Wi-Fi and stunning views of either the Avenue des Champs-Élysées or the serene patio.Spacious and Well-Equipped Suites: Enjoy the comfort of separate living and dining areas, a fully equipped kitchen, and modern amenities including satellite TV and a DVD player in each suite.Explore and Indulge: Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast and discover a variety of dining options nearby.
