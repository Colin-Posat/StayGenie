HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Garden and Meeting RoomsHotel Alimara offers a unique experience with its central garden, providing a serene oasis in the bustling city of Barcelona. The hotel also boasts several meeting rooms, making it an ideal choice for business travelers looking for a professional yet relaxing environment.Modern Rooms and Fitness CentreGuests can enjoy the comfort of modern rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and heating at Hotel Alimara. Additionally, the hotel features a fitness centre for guests to stay active and energized during their stay.Culinary Delights and Convenient LocationIndulge in Mediterranean cuisine with a modern twist at Alimara's restaurant, while children can enjoy specially crafted menus. For a more casual setting, the hotel's bar, SUMMUM, offers a terrace serving a variety of food, drinks, and snacks. Conveniently located near Mundet Metro Station, exploring the vibrant city of Barcelona is effortless. Book your stay at Hotel Alimara for a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and culinary delights.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hotel Alimara in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
If you require an invoice when booking a prepaid rate, please write this request and your company details in the <b>Ask a question</b> box.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please note that you must contact Hotel Alimara directly to request extra beds. Unless you have written confirmation from the hotel, your extra bed is not guaranteed.

When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Please note that air conditioning is available in the summer season.

Please note that heating is only available in the winter season.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Rooms and common areas were generally clean, although there were a few mentions of cleanliness issues in some reviews.
• Service: 8/10 - Most guests appreciated the service provided by the staff, but there were complaints about unfriendly staff and service issues in some reviews.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location near public transport and key landmarks was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and clean, but some reviews mentioned maintenance issues and uncomfortable beds.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews about amenities provided, with some guests appreciating them while others mentioned shortcomings like lack of swimming pool and poor gym equipment.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Several guests felt that the hotel was overpriced for the services offered, citing issues with room comfort, amenities, and value for cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Food options were generally well-received, with a few complaints about limited breakfast selection.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, but a significant number mentioned service issues, cleanliness problems, and value for money concerns.
