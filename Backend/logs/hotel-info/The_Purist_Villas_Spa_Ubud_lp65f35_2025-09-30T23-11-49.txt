HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Balinese-Style Villas: Experience a tranquil stay surrounded by lush greenery in modern Balinese villas at The Purist Villas and Spa. Indulge in the landscaped pool and sundeck, offering the perfect spot for relaxation.Cultural Experiences and Wellness: Immerse yourself in Balinese culture with traditional craftwork and wood furnishings in tastefully designed villas. Enjoy spa treatments, cooking lessons, yoga classes, and organized day trips for a holistic retreat.Gastronomic Delights and Relaxation: Delight your taste buds at The Living Room Restaurant with delectable meals on lounge sofas. Savor Balinese snacks and fresh juices at The Pool Cafe. End your day with a refreshing cocktail by the pool.Book now to embark on a rejuvenating getaway at The Purist Villas and Spa.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform The Purist Villas & Spa Ubud in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 750,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 8.4/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
