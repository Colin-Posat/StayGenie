HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rydges Sydney Harbour offers a prime location in the historic Rocks district, with a rooftop swimming pool providing stunning views of Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge.Modern Comforts: Enjoy amenities like flat-screen TVs, refrigerators, and a choice of soft or firm pillows in every room, along with tea/coffee making facilities.Convenient Attractions: Just a short walk from Circular Quay and iconic landmarks like the Sydney Harbour Bridge and Opera House, the hotel is perfectly situated for sightseeing.Relaxation and Dining: Unwind in the spa pool or on the rooftop terrace, and savor cafe-style meals and Australian beverages at the Lobby Lounge and Bar.Book your stay at Rydges Sydney Harbour for a memorable Sydney experience.
