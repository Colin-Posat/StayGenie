HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 22 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Overall, guests highlighted the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas. Some minor issues were mentioned, such as stained carpets.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Guests appreciated the friendly and accommodating staff, though a few complaints were noted about wait times and noise levels.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel's proximity to Disneyland and restaurants was a major highlight for most guests, despite some noise concerns.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Comfortable beds and well-presented rooms were frequently mentioned, with some comments on outdated decor and bathroom cleanliness.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Overall satisfaction with amenities, with positive feedback on the pool and friendly staff. Some guests mentioned limited parking and lack of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on the price value, with some guests feeling it was on the expensive side for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some suggestions for improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
