HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Central LocationLocated just a stone's throw away from King's Cross and St Pancras International stations, The Elmwood Hotel offers a budget-friendly stay in the heart of London. Perfect for exploring top attractions like the British Museum, Piccadilly Circus, and Covent Garden.Newly Decorated RoomsEnjoy comfort and style in one of the 35 en suite rooms, recently refurbished to provide a cozy retreat after a day of sightseeing. Free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the property, ensuring you stay connected during your stay.Delicious Breakfast OptionsStart your day right with a hearty breakfast selection featuring ham, cheese, boiled eggs, fruit, yogurts, cereals, toast, and more. Whether you prefer a hot or cold drink, the Elmwood Hotel has you covered.Book your stay at The Elmwood Hotel for a memorable and convenient London experience.
