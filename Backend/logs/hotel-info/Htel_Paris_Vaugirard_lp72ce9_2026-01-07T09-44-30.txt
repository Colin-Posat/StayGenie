HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Modern AmenitiesHotel Paris Vaugirard, just 50 meters from Porte de Versailles, offers free WiFi and soundproofed rooms with flat-screen TVs. The hotel's continental breakfast and 24-hour front desk enhance your stay.Comfortable AccommodationsEach room features simple décor, with the option of courtyard or street views. Enjoy the convenience of a private bathroom and satellite channels during your stay.Accessible and Well-ConnectedWith a metro station only a 2-minute walk away, explore Paris effortlessly. Whether heading to Montparnasse or Montmartre, Hotel Paris Vaugirard's proximity to public transport makes sightseeing a breeze.Book your stay at Hotel Paris Vaugirard now for an unforgettable Parisian experience!
