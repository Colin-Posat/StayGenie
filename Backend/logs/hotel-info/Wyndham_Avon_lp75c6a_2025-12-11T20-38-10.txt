HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Heated Indoor Pool and Full-Service RestaurantWyndham Avon boasts a heated indoor pool and a full-service restaurant, ensuring guests can relax and dine in style during their stay.Modern Amenities and ServicesEach guestroom at this Avon Cambria Hotel is equipped with a flat-screen TV, microwave, refrigerator, and a tea/coffee maker. Guests can unwind in the hot tub, utilize the fitness center, and make use of the business center with fax/photocopying services.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsConveniently located in the French Creek District, this hotel is a short drive from Tower City Center and popular attractions like Progressive Field and the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. With Westgate Mall just a stone's throw away, guests can indulge in some retail therapy.Book your stay at Wyndham Avon today for a memorable and comfortable experience!
