HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Exotic Beachfront Cabins: Nestled within Tulum National Park, our thatched cabins offer stunning sea views and modern amenities like free WiFi and private bathrooms.Eco-Friendly Oasis: Immerse yourself in nature at our eco-friendly property powered by sustainable energy sources, where you can unwind in cabins surrounded by lush gardens and palm trees.Culinary Delights and Convenience: Indulge in delectable tapas at the beachfront Mivida restaurant or explore nearby dining options in Tulum town, just a short drive away.Experience the perfect blend of nature and comfort at Punta Piedra Beach Posada. Book your stay now for an unforgettable beach getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that the use of wind generated energy will cause some noise in case of storms.

Standard Rooms do not have a sea view.

The property will place a pre-authorization of the total amount including taxes. You can contact the property for more information.

The Estimate time of cash back, depend of the Owner Bank of the Credit Card.

The hotel does not have parking. In the area you will find private parking for 10 usd per night, you can also park on the street. The private parking does not belong to the hotel.
