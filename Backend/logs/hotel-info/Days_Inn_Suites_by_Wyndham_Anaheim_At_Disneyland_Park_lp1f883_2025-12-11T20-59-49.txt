Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.1/10 - Guests had mixed feedback about cleanliness, with some mentioning issues like ants and roaches in the rooms.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although some guests mentioned indifferent or dismissive staff behavior.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Guests highly praised the convenient location, especially for Disneyland visits and easy access to key amenities.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were described as clean, comfortable, and updated, but some guests reported issues like ants and uncomfortable beds.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - The amenities were a mixed bag, with some guests appreciating the shuttle service and complimentary breakfast, while others mentioned issues like wifi problems and restrictions in the pool area.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Guests had varied opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the services did not match the price paid.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast options received mixed reviews, with some guests wanting more choices and better quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Overall, guests had a satisfactory experience with positive feedback on staff, cleanliness, and convenience, although some negative incidents affected their stay.
