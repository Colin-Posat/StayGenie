HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Setting with Modern ComfortsExperience the charm of a small noble residence combined with modern amenities at Hotel Canal Grande. The 4-star boutique hotel seamlessly blends historic elegance with contemporary comforts for a unique stay in Venice.Luxurious Rooms and SuitesIndulge in spacious and beautifully decorated accommodations at Hotel Canal Grande. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, heating, and satellite TV in every room, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing stay.Convenient Amenities and ServicesFrom a small terrace overlooking the Grand Canal to meeting rooms, a bar, and 24-hour room service, Hotel Canal Grande offers a range of amenities for guests' convenience. Additionally, take advantage of the private dock for easy access via water taxi or gondola.Experience the perfect blend of history and luxury at Hotel Canal Grande. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience in Venice.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 50 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Adults  stay for € 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.9/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
