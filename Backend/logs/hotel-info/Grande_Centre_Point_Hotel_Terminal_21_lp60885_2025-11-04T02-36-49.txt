HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to the Grande Centre Point Hotel Terminal 21, where luxury meets convenience in the heart of Bangkok.Indulgent Relaxation: Unwind at our full-service spa, offering rejuvenating massages and body treatments. For the active traveler, enjoy our outdoor pool, hot tub, and tennis court.Culinary Delights: Savor delectable meals at BlueSpice or relish the comfort of room service. Our bar/lounge and poolside bar are perfect spots to enjoy your favorite drinks, while children up to three years enjoy our complimentary breakfast.Comfortable Accommodations: Choose from 498 well-appointed guestrooms, equipped with refrigerators, microwaves, and luxurious bedding. Enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi and cable programming to keep entertained during your stay.Experience the vibrant culture of Bangkok and book your stay with us today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Reservations are required for spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 11 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* Some facilities may have restricted access. Guests can contact the property for details using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
