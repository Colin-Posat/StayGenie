HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not possible) and costs  EUR 45 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable, but some guests found them small relative to the price paid.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - Guests were generally satisfied with amenities, with some highlighting gym and river views, but others noted the lack of a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing; some guests felt the cost was high for the room size and breakfast, while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, with guests enjoying the variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and highly recommended the hotel, praising the location, cleanliness, and friendly staff.
