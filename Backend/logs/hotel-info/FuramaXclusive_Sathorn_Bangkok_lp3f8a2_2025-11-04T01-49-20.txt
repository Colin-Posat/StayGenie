HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in SilomFuramaXclusive Sathorn, nestled in the heart of Silom, offers boutique-style accommodation with free Wi-Fi. Enjoy the outdoor swimming pool and sundecks just steps away from the vibrant Chong Nonsi BTS skytrain station.Chic and Comfortable RoomsExperience the cosy contemporary decor of rooms at FuramaXclusive, featuring warm lighting and elegant wood flooring. Each air-conditioned room boasts a balcony, a well-equipped pantry, and a seating area. Entertainment options include satellite TV, a DVD player, and a work desk for your convenience.Exceptional Services and DiningBenefit from a 24-hour front desk and concierge services during your stay. Indulge in international cuisine at the L’avenue Restaurant, offering indoor and outdoor seating for a delightful dining experience.Book your stay at FuramaXclusive Sathorn for a memorable Silom getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 1 and older are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 1 year old to 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 17 years old stay for THB 1,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 5 years old and over stay for THB 1,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
