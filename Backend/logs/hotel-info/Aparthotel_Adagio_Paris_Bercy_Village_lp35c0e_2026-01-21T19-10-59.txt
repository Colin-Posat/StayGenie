HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location in ParisAparthotel Adagio Paris Bercy Village is situated in the vibrant 12th district, steps away from Cour Saint-Emilion Metro Station, making it an ideal base for exploring the city.Fully Equipped ApartmentsThe studios and apartments at Adagio Paris Bercy Village cater to 2 to 4 guests, featuring well-appointed kitchens, cozy seating areas with satellite TV, and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a comfortable stay for all.Abundance of AmenitiesEnjoy the convenience of 24-hour reception, ticketing services, and additional facilities like laundry and dry cleaning. Wake up to a delicious buffet breakfast and explore the nearby Bercy Village with its array of dining and shopping options.Book your stay at Aparthotel Adagio Paris Bercy Village for a memorable Parisian experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that upon check-in guests will be requested to show a photo ID and a credit card. The details of theses cards must match the reservation's holder ones.

A baby kit (cot, high chair) is available on request according to availability.

For stays up to 7 nights, optional housekeeping service is available upon request : at an additional cost, guests can request their bed linen and towels to be changed or a full housekeeping service. For stay of 8 nights and more, weekly housekeeping is provided and included in the rates

The accommodations include a vacuum cleaner and cleaning kit.

Please note that a pet can be accommodated with a EUR 10 extra fee.

Please note that children under 12 years old can enjoy breakfast for a reduced rate.
