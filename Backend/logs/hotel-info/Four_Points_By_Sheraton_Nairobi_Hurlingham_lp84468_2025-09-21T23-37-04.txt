HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesBoasting a fitness centre and an outdoor swimming pool, Four Points By Sheraton Nairobi Hurlingham offers a luxurious stay in Nairobi. Guests can relax by the heated pool or indulge in a round of golf at the nearby Royal Nairobi Golf Club.Culinary DelightsEnjoy a delightful culinary experience at the on-site restaurant, serving Middle Eastern cuisine with vegetarian options. Start your day with a continental or buffet breakfast to fuel your adventures in the city.Exceptional ServiceWith multilingual staff available 24/7 at the front desk, you can expect exceptional service throughout your stay. • Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were generally described as comfortable and well-furnished, with occasional complaints about room assignments and paint smells.
• Amenities: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests enjoying the pool and restaurants, while others found them expensive or lacking certain services.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food quality and services received mixed feedback, with some guests highlighting good food and drinks while others complained about high prices and slow service.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising friendly staff, comfortable rooms, and good food.
