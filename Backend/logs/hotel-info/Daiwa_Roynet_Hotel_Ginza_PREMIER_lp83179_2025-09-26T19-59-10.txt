HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationDaiwa Roynet Hotel Ginza, open since December 2015, is ideally situated just a 1-minute walk from Ginza Itchome Station on the Yurakucho Line. Perfect for both leisure and business travelers, the hotel's proximity to transportation makes exploring Tokyo a breeze.Modern AmenitiesEach room at Ginza Daiwa Roynet Hotel is equipped with a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and a cozy en suite bathroom stocked with complimentary toiletries. The property also boasts a 24-hour front desk, offering convenience and peace of mind to guests throughout their stay.Nearby AttractionsLocated within walking distance of Tsukiji Fish Market and Ginza Mitsukoshi department store, guests can easily immerse themselves in the vibrant local culture and shopping scene. With Haneda Airport just 13 km away, traveling to and from the hotel is a breeze.Book your stay at Daiwa Roynet Hotel Ginza for a seamless Tokyo experience!
