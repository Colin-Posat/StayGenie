Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness overall, although some mentioned noisy cleaners in one review.
• Service: 9.1/10 - Service received high praise across multiple reviews, especially for reception and staff helpfulness.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and its proximity to shops were well-liked by guests in several reviews.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and adequate for short stays, though some guests found them small.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with positives about storage and organization but negatives about worn rugs and awkward toilet placements.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Varied opinions on value for money, with some guests finding the rates expensive compared to services received.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining services were generally well-received, with some comments on price and lack of variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience with the hotel, praising staff, location, and comfort.
