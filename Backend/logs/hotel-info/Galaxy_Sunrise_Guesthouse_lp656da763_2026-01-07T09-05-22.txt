HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Beachfront relaxation and culinary delightsEscape to a tranquil oasis at Galaxy Sunrise Guesthouse, where you can unwind on a private beach and revel in the stunning garden views. Indulge in delectable Italian cuisine at AYA Cafe &amp; Restaurant with a picturesque ocean backdrop, and savor a complimentary local breakfast each morning.Luxurious accommodations and attentive serviceExperience personalized service with multilingual staff, luggage storage, and lockers. Each of the 9 elegantly decorated guestrooms offers modern amenities like rainfall showers, bidets, and complimentary Wi-Fi to ensure a comfortable stay.Convenience and explorationConveniently located near Rihiveli Beach, and just a short drive from Velana Intl. Airport, Galaxy Sunrise Guesthouse is the perfect gateway to a memorable island getaway. Book now for an unforgettable experience.
