IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Meliá Desert Palm Member of Meliá Collection in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
All cots are subject to availability.

An additional deposit amount will be taken upon check in for extra charges during your stay - (AED 500 for Rooms & Suites, AED 1000 for Pool Villa and Polo Pool Villa, AED 2000 for Pool Residences and AED 5000 for Villa Layali - additional deposit amount per night.

The name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property. For reservations made by a third party, you will need to complete an authorisation form and present a copy of the person's ID and credit card.

In-Villa parties or Group gatherings are not permitted without prior approval & coordination with Hotel Sales team following Hotel policy.

When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements will apply.
