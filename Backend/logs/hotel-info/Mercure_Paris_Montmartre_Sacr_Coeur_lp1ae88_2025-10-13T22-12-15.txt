HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views of ParisMercure Paris Montmartre Sacré Coeur offers air-conditioned rooms with sound-proofing, providing stunning views over Paris. Each room features a flat-screen TV and en suite bathroom, ensuring a comfortable stay.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy a buffet breakfast or have it in their room. The hotel's bar, SpeakArt, serves snacks and drinks. Additionally, the open-space coworking facility caters to remote workers, offering free WiFi, a wireless printer, and a Nespresso coffee machine.Prime LocationLocated in the vibrant Montmartre district, the hotel is within walking distance of iconic sights like Sacré Coeur and Moulin Rouge. With easy access to the metro, guests can explore the city with ease.Experience the charm of Montmartre at Mercure Paris Montmartre Sacré Coeur. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Parisian getaway.

Please note that the open-space working area can be used for EUR 6 per hour for a solo worker or EUR 24 per hour for a private meeting room (1-5 people).

Please note that children under 12 years old can enjoy breakfast for free.

A Wojo corner coworking space is dedicated to clients who wish to work with us! Our bar area and our terrace welcome you only to enjoy a drink or a board to share.
