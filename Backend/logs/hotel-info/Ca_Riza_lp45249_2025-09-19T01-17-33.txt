HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience the charm of Venice at Ca' Riza, offering air-conditioned rooms and apartments with free Wi-Fi in the historic center. Indulge in traditional Venetian décor, complete with modern amenities such as a TV, minibar, safe, and desk. Apartments even include a convenient cooking area for added comfort.Convenient LocationLocated just a 20-minute stroll from St Mark’s Square, Ca' Riza provides easy access to the city's iconic landmarks. With Venice Marco Polo airport a short 15 km away and Santa Lucia Train Station within a 10-minute walk, your travels are sure to be stress-free.Exceptional AccessibilityWhether you are exploring the city or catching a train, Ca' Riza's prime location ensures you can make the most of your time in Venice. Book now for an unforgettable stay at Ca' Riza.
