HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Pool and Panoramic ViewsHotel Catalonia Barcelona Plaza boasts a seasonal rooftop pool and a terrace offering breathtaking city views, perfect for relaxation and unwinding after a day of exploring.Gastronomic DelightsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's diverse dining options, from the avant-garde Simultáneo Gastrobar to the modern Catalan cuisine at Filigrana restaurant and the Japanese delights at Kurai.Business and Leisure BlendWith a fitness room, beauty salon, free Wi-Fi, and spacious rooms featuring Smart TVs and Chromecast apps, the hotel seamlessly combines business amenities with leisure offerings, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury, convenience, and top-notch service at Hotel Catalonia Barcelona Plaza!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Only one dog or cat under 20 kg per room is allowed (on request). Supplement of EUR 25 per night per animal and deposit of EUR 200.

Please note that children under 18 years of age must be accompanied by an adult. Children under 14 cannot access the spa.

Credit cardholder must match guest name or provide authorisation.

Book your room with half board and enjoy the haute cuisine of the Simultaneous Gastrobar for lunch or dinner.

As with reservations of more than 4 rooms, special conditions and supplements will apply to those of more than 8 nights.
