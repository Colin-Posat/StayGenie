HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the charm of Marrakech at Hotel Oudaya &amp; Spa, where comfort meets Moroccan elegance.Unwind in StyleOur hotel boasts air-conditioned rooms with either a balcony or terrace, offering a peaceful retreat after a day of exploration. Each well-appointed room features satellite TV and en suite bathrooms with modern amenities.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a delectable continental breakfast before heading out to explore. For lunch or dinner, savor meals on our terrace overlooking the pool, or unwind with a drink at our inviting bar area.Relaxation AwaitsTreat yourself to rejuvenating massages at our spa, or simply relax in our Moroccan-style lounge. With a 24-hour reception and complimentary Wi-Fi, your comfort is our priority.Discover the essence of Marrakech—book your stay at Hotel Oudaya &amp; Spa today!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
