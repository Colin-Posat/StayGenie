HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Eiffel Tower Views and 19th-Century CharmExperience Parisian elegance at Hôtel La Comtesse, where every room offers stunning views of the iconic Eiffel Tower. Set in a historic 19th-century building, our hotel in the 7th district exudes classic Hausmannian charm.Luxurious Amenities and ServicesIndulge in modern comforts with TV, WiFi, and a minibar in every room. Relax in our fitness room and hammam, or savor French cuisine in the on-site café. Our buffet breakfast and express breakfast options ensure a delightful start to your day.Prime Location and Cultural AttractionsLocated steps from the Champs de Mars and near the École Militaire Metro Station, our hotel provides easy access to Parisian landmarks. Explore the Rodin Museum and the Eiffel Tower, both within walking distance. Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hôtel La Comtesse.
