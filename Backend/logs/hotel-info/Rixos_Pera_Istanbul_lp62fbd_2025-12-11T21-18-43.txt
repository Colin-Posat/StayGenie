HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Convenient LocationRixos Pera is a gem in the heart of Istanbul, just steps away from Istiklal Avenue and Demiroren Shopping Mall. The hotel's proximity to transportation hubs makes exploring the city a breeze.Gourmet Dining and RelaxationIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's three a-la-carte restaurants, offering a variety of global cuisines. Unwind at the Rixos Royal Spa with exclusive massage sessions, sauna, and Turkish bath facilities.Fitness and ConvenienceStay active at the fully-equipped fitness centre or pamper yourself at the on-site barber shop/hairdresser. With high-speed WiFi, 24/7 front desk service, and easy access to popular attractions, Rixos Pera ensures a seamless stay in Istanbul.Experience luxury and convenience at Rixos Pera Istanbul.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
A prepayment deposit via 3D secure system is required to secure your reservation according to payment and cancellation policy of your reservation.The property will contact you after you book to provide instructions.

Rixos Pera Istanbul charges the first night of your accommodations to your credit card as a deposit to secure your booking. This deposit is refundable if the booking is canceled within more than 24 hours' notice.

Please note that any extra bedding depends on availability. Extra beds/cots should be requested in the Special Requests box during booking and need to be confirmed by the hotel.

You can benefit from a 15% discount on spa facilities throughout the year.

Please note that public off site parking lot (reservation is not needed) is available and EUR 15 is applicable for each entrance.

Please contact the property if you want to benefit from transfer service to/from Ataturk Airport. Contact details can be found in booking confirmation.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 22 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 60 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
