HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ZermattHotel Butterfly, BW Signature Collection is nestled in the heart of car-free Zermatt, a mere 250 meters from the Gornergratbahn train station and ski bus stop.Luxurious Rooms and DiningIndulge in rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms. Wake up to delightful continental and buffet breakfast options, and savor a 4-course dinner at the hotel's restaurant.Relaxing Wellness AreaUnwind in the wellness area offering a Finnish sauna, infra-red cabin, and steam bath. In the summer, pamper yourself with these facilities available upon request.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hotel Butterfly, BW Signature Collection in picturesque Zermatt!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hotel Butterfly in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note that the spa area is not available in summer.

Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 15 years old stay for CHF 65 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.
