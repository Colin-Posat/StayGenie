HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AccessibilityLocated in the vibrant south of Paris, Hotel Du Lion is just steps away from Denfert-Rochereau Square and Metro Station. The easily accessible lift ensures all guests can enjoy the stunning views of Paris from the upper floors.Comfort and AmenitiesEach soundproofed room is thoughtfully equipped with modern conveniences like free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a private bathroom. Upgrade to rooms with air conditioning and a balcony for an extra touch of luxury. The 24/7 front desk ensures all your needs are met promptly.Delightful Dining and RelaxationStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast served in the cozy breakfast room or in the comfort of your own room. Unwind in the common living room with satellite TV or explore the city effortlessly with the nearby metro station. Book now to experience Paris at Hotel Du Lion.
