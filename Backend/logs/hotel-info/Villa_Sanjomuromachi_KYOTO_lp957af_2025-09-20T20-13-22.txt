HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationVilla Sanjomuromachi KYOTO, opening in July 2016, offers a tranquil stay just a short walk from Karasuma Oike Subway Station. The carpeted rooms are equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and free WiFi, ensuring a comfortable experience for guests.Concierge Services and City GuideManaged by a city guide publisher, the property provides excellent concierge services to assist guests in making the most of their stay in Kyoto. Whether you need local recommendations or help with booking activities, the dedicated staff is there to enhance your experience.Convenient LocationSituated near popular attractions like Nishiki Market and Nijo Castle, Villa Sanjomuromachi KYOTO offers easy access to key destinations in Kyoto. Explore the city with ease and return to a welcoming atmosphere at the hotel.To experience luxury and convenience in the heart of Kyoto, book your stay at Villa Sanjomuromachi KYOTO today.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for ¥4,400 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for ¥7,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
