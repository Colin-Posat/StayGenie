HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views and Luxurious AmenitiesSerene Surroundings: Domaine des Remparts, nestled in a 2-hectare park in Marrakech, provides a picturesque backdrop of the Atlas Mountains.Wellness and Dining: Enjoy the convenience of a fitness centre, two restaurants including Le Tibipt serving authentic Moroccan cuisine, and a spa offering rejuvenating treatments like hammam sessions and aromatherapy baths.Relaxation and Recreation: Unwind in rooms and suites featuring terraces, private gardens, and fireplaces. Take a dip in one of the two swimming pools, practice yoga on the platform, or challenge a friend to a game of tennis.Book now to experience the epitome of luxury and tranquility at Domaine des Remparts.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Domaine Des Remparts Hotel & Spa in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests need to arrive at check-in from 15:00. Departure form the residence is from 12:00.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 13 years old stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.9/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
