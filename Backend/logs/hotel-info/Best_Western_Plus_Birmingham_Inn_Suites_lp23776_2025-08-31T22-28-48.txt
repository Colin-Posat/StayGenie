Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.6/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel, with multiple positive mentions across reviews.
• Service: 8.2/10 - While service was generally appreciated, some complaints were noted regarding check-in wait times and customer service at the front desk.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and ease of access to public transport received positive feedback from guests.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, although some guests reported maintenance issues like rattling AC units and worn-out tubs.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the pool and breakfast, though some guests noted the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the offerings, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Generally positive feedback on breakfast and dining services, though a desire for more variety was expressed by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
