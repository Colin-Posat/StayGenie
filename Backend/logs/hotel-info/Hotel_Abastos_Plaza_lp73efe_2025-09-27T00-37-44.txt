HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Spa Facilities and Modern AmenitiesHotel Abastos Plaza, just a short 10-minute drive from Mexico City International Airport, offers a luxurious stay with spa facilities, Wi-Fi access, and a chic restaurant and bar. The modern-style rooms feature all the comforts you need, including air conditioning, flat-screen cable TV, and a laptop safe.Delicious Dining Options and Convenient ServicesIndulge in the buffet or à-la-carte service at the on-site restaurant, or grab a drink at the bar. With vending machines for snacks and drinks, as well as room service, your cravings are always satisfied. Explore nearby dining options or venture to the Historic Centre just 11 km away.Exploring Mexico City's Cultural GemsImmerse yourself in Mexico City's rich culture by visiting iconic museums and landmarks such as the National History Museum and The Anthropology Museum. Conveniently located near transportation options, including Iztacalco Metro Station, you're just moments away from unforgettable experiences.Book your stay at Hotel Abastos Plaza now for a truly memorable getaway!

• Location: 7.6/10 - Mixed feedback on location, with some guests appreciating proximity to the airport and shopping complex, while others found it lacking pleasant walking areas.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Spacious and modern rooms with some amenities missing. Mixed feedback on bed and pillow comfort.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with guests appreciating some but noting the lack of others like coffee making facilities and spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed opinions on the value for money, with some feeling it was reasonable given the location, while others found it expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Most guests enjoyed the buffet breakfast and restaurant services, though some felt the breakfast was overpriced and not of high quality.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
