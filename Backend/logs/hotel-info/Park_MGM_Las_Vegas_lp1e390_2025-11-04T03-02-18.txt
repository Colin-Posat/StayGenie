For a taste of the South of France, visit Primrose restaurant for garden-inspired dishes in a charming setting.Convenient Facilities and ServicesWith a casino, sportsbook, and concierge desk for bookings, guests have access to entertainment and assistance at their fingertips. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.2/10 - Guests had mixed experiences with cleanliness, with reports ranging from very clean rooms to dirty and smelly rooms.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with some guests highlighting exceptional staff members, while others mentioned issues with room change requests and unresponsive staff.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location was praised for being close to main attractions and public transport options, making it convenient for guests.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with some guests loving the comfortable beds and spacious rooms, while others reported broken furniture and dusty conditions.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Guests were satisfied with the amenities provided, with some mentioning the lack of certain amenities like a mini fridge in the room.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining options received positive feedback overall, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
