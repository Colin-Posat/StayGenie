HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic CharmHotel Kette, housed in a 16th-century building, exudes historic charm and character. The Venetian-style rooms with large windows offer a glimpse into the city's past, while the LCD TVs with satellite channels provide modern comfort.Elegant AccommodationsThe rooms at the Kette are elegantly decorated with traditional wallpaper and fine fabrics, creating a luxurious and inviting atmosphere. Guests can enjoy amenities such as air conditioning and a minibar for a comfortable stay.Convenient Location and ServicesLocated just 100 meters from the Fenice Theatre and a short walk from iconic landmarks like Piazza San Marco, the hotel offers a prime location for exploring Venice. Additionally, the buffet breakfast featuring a variety of delicious options and shuttle services to the airports enhance the overall convenience for guests.Experience the perfect blend of history and luxury at Hotel Kette. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Venice.
