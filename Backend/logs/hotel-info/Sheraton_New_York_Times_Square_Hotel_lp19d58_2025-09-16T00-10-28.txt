Local and Long-distance calls.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Friendly staff
• Clean rooms

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• No spa services
• Mixed cleanliness reviews
• Varied room quality

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests praising the spotless rooms and attentive cleaning staff, while others mentioned issues like dirty towels, crowded lobby, and lack of clean facilities.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Overall positive feedback on service, with guests appreciating the friendly and accommodating staff. However, some guests encountered surly and unhelpful reception staff, leading to mixed reviews.
• Location: 8.3/10 - High rating for the prime location, with the hotel being close to popular landmarks and convenient transport options. This was consistently mentioned as a major advantage by most guests.
• Room Quality: 6.3/10 - Varied opinions on room quality, with some guests enjoying spacious rooms and comfortable beds, while others complained about tired and outdated furniture, low-pressure showers, and uncomfortable mattresses.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests satisfied with the gym and pool, while others expressed disappointment at the lack of essential services like drinking water and spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Guests had mixed feelings about the value for money, with some finding the pricing justified by the location, while others felt the hotel was overpriced, especially considering additional fees not clearly communicated.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Generally positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some guests suggesting more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some specific issues mentioned in other categories.
