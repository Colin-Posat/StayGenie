IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Meritage Resort and Spa in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
The resort fee includes:

- bottle of signature wine (must be 21 or older)
- WiFi
- fax and local calls
- daily bottled water
- fitness studio access
- in-room coffee and tea
- daily shuttle service to/from downtown Napa
- daily electronic newspaper access via PressReader.

Resort fee is subject to applicable taxes.

Breakfast package allows a maximum of $60 credit towards breakfast per room, per day.

• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on amenities, with some highlighting the lack of spa services and issues with pool towels and food availability.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests finding the price high for the services offered and others considering it reasonable for the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed reviews, with some guests praising the breakfast and restaurant while others were disappointed with the options and service.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite mixed feedback in other categories.
