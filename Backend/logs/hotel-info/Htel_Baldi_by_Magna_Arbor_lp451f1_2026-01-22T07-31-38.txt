HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience comfort and style at Hotel Baldi, located just minutes away from iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Tour Montparnasse. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with modern amenities such as LCD TVs, minibars, and free toiletries.Convenient Dining OptionsStart your day right with a delicious breakfast available for dine-in or to take away. Whether you prefer to savor your meal in the cozy ambiance of your room or grab it on the go, we've got you covered.Top-Notch FacilitiesEnjoy complimentary Wi-Fi access throughout the hotel and the convenience of a 24-hour reception. With metro stations nearby, exploring the city is a breeze. Stay at Hotel Baldi, a proud recipient of the CLEF VERTE label for 2020.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Housekeeping is offered every three days for stays of more than 3 nights. For stays of less than 3 nights, it remains suspended, except for exceptional requests.

The breakfast reservation is made the day before, in order to be able to respect the maximum capacity of our breakfast room and to allow everyone to take a breakfast without having to wait.

Breakfast can also be asked to go, in a bag, from 7 to 10AMGuests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 27 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 15 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
