HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 5 years old and over stay for THB 1,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Overall, guests mentioned cleanliness positively, but some reviews reported issues such as dust and cleanliness of carpets.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with some reviews mentioning helpful and kind staff, while a few others noted less friendly interactions.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The central location was highlighted positively by most guests for easy access to key landmarks and public transportation.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were generally spacious and comfortable, although a few reviews mentioned outdated furniture and minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating the pool and gym, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the pricing, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the offered services and others finding it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received mixed feedback, with some guests praising the variety and quality, while others suggested improvements.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
