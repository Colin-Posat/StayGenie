HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Downtown Chicago's North Loop Business DistrictLocated just 4 blocks from the famous Magnificent Mile, Hilton Garden Inn Chicago Downtown Riverwalk offers a prime location for shopping, dining, and entertainment. Business travelers will appreciate the proximity to the North Loop business district, while leisure guests can explore nearby attractions like Millennium Park and Navy Pier.Modern Amenities for Comfort and ConvenienceEach room at the hotel is equipped with a 40-inch HDTV, premium cable channels, a microwave, and a small refrigerator. Guests can stay productive with a work desk and ergonomic chair, and enjoy free WiFi access throughout the property. The 24-hour fitness center and on-site dining options provide added convenience.Business-Friendly Services and FacilitiesWith a 24-hour business center, meeting boardroom, and room service, Hilton Garden Inn caters to the needs of business travelers. Whether hosting meetings or staying connected with work, guests can rely on the hotel's business amenities for a seamless stay.Book your stay at Hilton Garden Inn Chicago Downtown Riverwalk for a perfect blend of comfort and convenience in the heart of Chicago.
