HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and FeaturesTownePlace Suites Sacramento Airport Natomas provides a comfortable stay with an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, and a garden. Enjoy the convenience of free private parking and a 24-hour front desk for all your needs.Modern and Well-Equipped RoomsEach guest room is thoughtfully designed with air conditioning, kitchen amenities, and a private bathroom. Relax with free toiletries, cozy bed linen, and towels provided during your stay.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated just 8 km from Sacramento International Airport, this 3-star hotel is close to Discovery Park and California State Railroad Museum.
