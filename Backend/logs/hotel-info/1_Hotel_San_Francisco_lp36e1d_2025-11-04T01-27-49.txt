HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Waterfront ViewsLocated on San Francisco’s Embarcadero waterfront, 1 Hotel San Francisco offers breathtaking views and a serene atmosphere. Enjoy the outdoor rooftop terrace overlooking the city skyline.Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in the comfort of plush bathrobes, slippers, and boutique toiletries provided in each guest room. Stay entertained with a flat-screen cable TV and unwind with a drink from the honour bar. The spacious work desk and ergonomic chair cater to your business needs.Convenient FacilitiesTake advantage of the 24-hour business centre and fitness centre during your stay. With Oracle Park, the Golden Gate Bridge, and Chase Center nearby, 1 Hotel San Francisco is the perfect choice for your visit to the city.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at 1 Hotel San Francisco.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Our priority is and always has been the health and well-being of our guests and team members. Learn about the steps we're taking to ensure your health and safety here. In compliance with local regulations and CDC guidelines, some amenities and services may be limited or unavailable during your stay. Please note that signing a COVID-19 Acknowledgement, Waiver and Release is required at check-in.

1 Hotel San Francisco is a smoke-free property. Smoking in guestrooms or suites will incur a fee of $750 per guestroom or $1,000 per suite. Additional deep cleaning may incur a $500 cleaning fee.

The Guest Amenities Fee includes:

- Tier 1 WiFi access in guest rooms and common areas of the hotel
- Complimentary 24 hour Fitness centre (Located on the 2nd Floor)
- Daily passes available to the YMCA (walking distance) - experience exclusive classes (yoga and fitness) and 25-meter lap pool
- Access to Outdoor Terraces (Located on the 5th, 7th & 8th Floors)
- Coffee and Tea service starting at 04:00 in Americano Restaurant
- Nespresso machines (upon availability)
- Nightly Shoe Shine as requested

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 90.06 per  day.
