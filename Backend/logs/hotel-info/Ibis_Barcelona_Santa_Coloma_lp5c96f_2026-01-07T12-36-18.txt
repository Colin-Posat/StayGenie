Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.1/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 6.8/10 - While some guests appreciated the service, there were complaints about attitude and wait times.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to key landmarks and public transport, although some found the metro station distance inconvenient.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally spacious and comfortable, with a few minor inconveniences like water pressure issues and lack of fridge.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities, although some mentioned the absence of a spa and limited options like no mini bar or kettle in the rooms.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money, with some guests finding it expensive for offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally received positive feedback, but some indicated a lack of variety or poor coffee quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience, with compliments on friendly staff and cleanliness.
