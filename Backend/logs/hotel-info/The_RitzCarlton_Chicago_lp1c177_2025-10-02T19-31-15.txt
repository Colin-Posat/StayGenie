HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsLocated atop Water Tower Place in Chicago’s Gold Coast, the Ritz-Carlton offers artfully decorated guest rooms with city or lake views. Each room includes a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and a marble bathroom with deluxe toiletries.World-Class AmenitiesGuests can enjoy a swim in the lap pool, relax in the spa, or dine at one of the hotel's two restaurants and bar. With 24-hour concierge services, a modern fitness center, and a business center, every need is catered to at the Chicago Ritz Carlton.Prime Location for ExploringSituated near the Magnificent Mile, Navy Pier, and the Museum of Contemporary Art, this hotel is the perfect base for exploring Chicago's top attractions.Book your stay at the Ritz-Carlton, Chicago for an unforgettable luxury experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
