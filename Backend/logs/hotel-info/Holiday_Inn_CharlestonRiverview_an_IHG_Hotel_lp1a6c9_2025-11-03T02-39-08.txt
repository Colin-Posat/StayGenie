Areas for Improvement:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.1/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness with some guests reporting issues like dust, chipped paint, and dirty windows.
• Service: 7.1/10 - Staff were generally friendly and helpful, though some guests experienced delays in addressing issues.
• Location: 9/10 - High praise for the convenient location close to key landmarks, although access may be challenging for some due to the hotel's positioning on a major bridge.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Rooms were described as outdated by some guests, with reports of maintenance issues like broken shower rods and malfunctioning electrical outlets.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with positive mentions of the restaurant and pool, but complaints about the lack of spa services and outdated facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Views were appreciated, but some guests felt that the hotel was overpriced for the quality of services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on food quality, with compliments for dinner and breakfast but also complaints about limited breakfast options.
• Overall Experience: 6.8/10 - Guests had varying experiences, with some expressing disappointment in cleanliness and maintenance, while others highlighted the friendly staff and convenient location.
