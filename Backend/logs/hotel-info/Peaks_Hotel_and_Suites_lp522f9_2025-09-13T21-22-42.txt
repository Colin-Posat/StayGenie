Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of CAD 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
