HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central LocationLocated in the heart of Memphis, La Quinta by Wyndham Memphis Downtown offers a convenient stay for guests looking to explore the city. With popular attractions such as Memphis Rock n Soul Museum and Memphis Music Hall of Fame just a stone's throw away, guests can immerse themselves in the vibrant culture of the city.Comfort and ConvenienceGuests can enjoy a comfortable stay in the hotel's well-equipped rooms, each featuring a private bathroom. The property also boasts free WiFi, a fitness centre, and free private parking, ensuring a hassle-free experience for all visitors. Additionally, the indoor pool and terrace provide the perfect spots to unwind after a day of sightseeing.Exceptional ServiceWith a dedicated reception offering valuable local insights and assistance, guests can make the most of their visit to Memphis. Whether it's recommendations for nearby attractions or help with navigating the area, the hotel's staff is committed to ensuring a memorable stay for every guest.Book your stay at La Quinta by Wyndham Memphis Downtown for a seamless blend of comfort, convenience, and exceptional service.
