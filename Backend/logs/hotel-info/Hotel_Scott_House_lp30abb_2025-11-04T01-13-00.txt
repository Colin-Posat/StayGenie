HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building CharmHotel Scott House Hotel is nestled on the fourth and fifth floors of a charming 19th-century building in the heart of Rome. The historic setting adds a unique touch to your stay, providing a blend of old-world charm with modern comforts.Central LocationConveniently situated next to Termini Train and Metro Stations, this hotel offers easy access to Rome's top attractions. Explore the Coliseum with just a short Metro ride or take a leisurely stroll to the Spanish Steps. Additionally, with buses to the airports departing from Termini Station, your travels are made hassle-free.Modern AmenitiesEnjoy contemporary amenities during your stay at Hotel Scott House Hotel. From free Wi-Fi throughout the property to well-equipped rooms featuring flat-screen TVs with Sky channels, air conditioning, minibars, and private bathrooms, your comfort is ensured.Plan your Roman getaway at Hotel Scott House Hotel for a memorable and convenient stay.
