HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to Club Wyndham Desert Blue, your ultimate getaway in the heart of Las Vegas!Relaxation and RecreationDive into one of our two outdoor swimming pools or unwind in a hot tub after a fun day exploring. With a well-equipped health club, you can keep up with your fitness routine while enjoying the vibrant atmosphere.Comfort and ConvenienceStay in one of our 281 air-conditioned rooms that come with kitchenettes, perfect for preparing snacks, and enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi for seamless connectivity. Each room is designed with your comfort in mind, featuring modern amenities like cable TV and coffee/tea makers.Dining and NightlifeSavor delightful bites from our snack bar/deli or enjoy a refreshing drink at our poolside bar. With a bar/lounge atmosphere, you can truly unwind and bask in the Las Vegas nightlife.Don't miss out—book your stay at Club Wyndham Desert Blue today!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Overall, guests mentioned cleanliness positively, but some reviews noted issues like mold and mildew smell and rooms not being cleaned properly.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, though a few experienced delays and unprofessional behavior at the front desk.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location was highlighted as a major advantage, with guests appreciating its proximity to key landmarks and peaceful atmosphere.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and clean, although some had maintenance issues and problems with appliances.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with guests particularly enjoying the pool and jacuzzi.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services received positive feedback, with guests appreciating the breakfast and general restaurant offerings.
• Overall Experience: 6.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing friendly staff, comfortable rooms, and great location as key highlights.
