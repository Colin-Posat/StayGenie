The Legendär is only accessible by the Gornergrat train (1st stop: Legendär) or the pedestrian path (10 minutes walk from the center of Zermatt). The residence is therefore not accessible by taxi. It is possible to take the Gornergrat train from the residence to go directly to the ski slopes (Gornergrat included in the ski pass in winter).
