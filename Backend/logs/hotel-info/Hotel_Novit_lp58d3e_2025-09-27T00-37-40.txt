HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Pool with City ViewsHotel Novit, nestled in Mexico City’s vibrant business district, boasts a stunning rooftop pool providing unparalleled views of the bustling cityscape. Guests can relax and unwind while taking in the breathtaking scenery.Modern Rooms with City ViewsThe air-conditioned rooms at Novit are elegantly designed with smart, modern décor and offer panoramic city views. With amenities like free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and comfortable seating areas, guests can enjoy a luxurious stay with all the necessary comforts.Gastronomic Delights and EntertainmentIndulge in avant-garde Mediterranean cuisine at the hotel’s restaurant or sip on cocktails while enjoying live music at the outdoor lounge bar. The perfect way to unwind after a day of exploring the nearby World Trade Centre and surrounding entertainment options.Experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at Hotel Novit. HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 6 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for US$27.32 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 14 years old and over stay for US$27.32 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
