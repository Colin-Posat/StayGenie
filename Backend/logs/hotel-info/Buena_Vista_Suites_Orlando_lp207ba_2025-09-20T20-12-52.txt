HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious All-Suite Property Near Disney WorldSuite Perfection: Each suite at our hotel offers a private bedroom, bathroom, and a separate living room with a cozy sofa bed. You'll also find convenient amenities like a small refrigerator and microwave in every suite.Unbeatable Location: Just a 3-minute drive from Walt Disney World Resort, our property also provides scheduled transportation to the park, making it a breeze to enjoy all the magic Disney has to offer.Endless Convenience: Take a dip in our heated outdoor pool, savor a complimentary breakfast, and enjoy free on-site parking. With free transfers to local theme parks and proximity to Orlando's top attractions, your stay with us will be nothing short of amazing.Book your stay now and experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at Buena Vista Suites Orlando.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that the transfer service has limited hours of operation. Contact the property for details.

Hotel Resort Fee is USD 19 (including tax is USD 21.38) per night and includes the following:
- In-suite standard WiFi (4 devices);
- Shuttle to Disney Parks;
- In-suite coffee;
- Pickleball & Padel Tennis (1p-5p with reservation);
- Access to basketball court;
- Use of self-service business center;
- Unlimited local and 800# calls.

Please note that package delivery fees apply. Please contact the property for pricing and details.
