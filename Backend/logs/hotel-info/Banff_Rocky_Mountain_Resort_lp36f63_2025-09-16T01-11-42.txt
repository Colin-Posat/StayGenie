HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Suite Accommodation and Scenic LocationExperience spacious all-suite accommodation at our resort located along the beautiful Banff Rocky Mountain National Park. Enjoy breathtaking views and easy access to top-rated ski trails, scenic hiking paths, and golfing opportunities just moments away.Health and Fitness ClubIndulge in our health and fitness club offering a full gym, squash courts, outdoor tennis courts, a 15-meter indoor swimming pool, and both indoor and outdoor hot tubs. Stay active and rejuvenate during your stay with us.Alpha Bistro and EntertainmentSavor Canadian cuisine at Alpha Bistro located in the main building, providing a cozy dining atmosphere. Unwind and socialize in the presence of large screen TVs and make the most of the spacious outdoor patio for a relaxing time.Book your stay now to experience the perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and adventure at our resort!

What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited shuttle service

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Guests had mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some mentioning stains on towels and old facilities.
• Service: 7.3/10 - Staff were described as friendly and helpful, but there were complaints about slow shuttle services and infrequent shuttles to the city.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's proximity to town and key landmarks was highly appreciated by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were generally praised for being spacious, modern, and well-equipped, but some mentioned maintenance issues like loose bathroom fixtures.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests appreciated amenities such as kitchenettes, pools, and jacuzzis, but there were some complaints about the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - There were mixed reviews on the value for money, with some finding the price reasonable given the location, while others felt it was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
