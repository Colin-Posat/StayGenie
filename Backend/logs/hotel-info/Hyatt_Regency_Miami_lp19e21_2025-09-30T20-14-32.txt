HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views and Convenient LocationThe Hyatt Regency Miami offers breathtaking views of Biscayne Bay and easy access to the city's attractions, including the Miami Convention Center and the Port of Miami.Luxurious AmenitiesGuests can enjoy a heated outdoor pool, a fully equipped fitness center, and comfortable guestrooms with flat-screen TVs. The Riverview Bar and Grill serves delicious Latin cuisine and fresh fish, perfect for a relaxing meal.Convenience and ComfortWith amenities like free WiFi, a safe, and ironing facilities, guests can relax and unwind during their stay. Whether visiting Miami Beach or the Vizcaya Museum &amp; Gardens, this hotel provides the perfect blend of comfort and convenience.Book your stay at Hyatt Regency Miami for a memorable experience in the heart of Miami.
