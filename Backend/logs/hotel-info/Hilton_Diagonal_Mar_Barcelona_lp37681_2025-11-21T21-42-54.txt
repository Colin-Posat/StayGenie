HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and Prime LocationExperience luxury at its finest at Hilton Diagonal Mar Barcelona, located next to Diagonal Mar Shopping Centre and Nova Mar Bella Beach. Enjoy the seasonal outdoor pool, fitness centre, and air-conditioned rooms with LED TVs for a comfortable stay.Purobeach Barcelona and Exquisite Dining OptionsRelax at Purobeach Barcelona's terrace with outdoor pools and pampering services. Indulge in a diverse menu and classic cocktails at Purobeach Restaurant or savor Catalan cuisine at Aürt Restaurant. Treat yourself to tapas and drinks at Ma'i for a culinary delight.Convenience and AccessibilityWith 22 meeting rooms and proximity to CCIB Convention Centre, this hotel is perfect for business travelers. El Maresme-Forum Metro Station nearby offers easy access to central Barcelona. Don't miss the chance to stay at this exceptional hotel for a memorable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

It is Hilton's policy to request a credit card upon check-in and temporarily block EUR 50 per day to cover possible extra charges. 

Should you require us not to request credit cards, then you need to provide us with a corporate credit card to guarantee all incidental charges. For this purpose, please provide credit card details in advance to the hotel. The name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property. 

For reservations made by a third party, you will need to complete an authorization form and present a copy of the person's ID and credit card. Please note that Purobeach Barcelona is now closed for the season, we will be back next year. The reserved room is set for the number of guests stated in your booking. Should this number be exceeded, a different room type or an additional room may be necessary, both subject to extra charges and availabilityGuests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

• Service: 6.8/10 - Service received mixed feedback, with some guests appreciating the staff's friendliness and attentiveness, while others had complaints about slow service and unhelpful interactions.
• Location: 7.5/10 - The hotel's location was praised by many guests for its convenience, while some felt it was farther away from key places than expected.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally described as nice and well-furnished, with a few mentions of wear and tear in some areas.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests enjoying the offerings while others found them lacking or overpriced.
• Value for Money: 5.5/10 - Several guests felt the hotel was overpriced for the services provided, citing issues with room rates, additional charges for amenities, and perceived value.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, with compliments on breakfast options, but some guests mentioned issues with service speed and variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the facilities and staff, but some highlighted specific areas of improvement.
