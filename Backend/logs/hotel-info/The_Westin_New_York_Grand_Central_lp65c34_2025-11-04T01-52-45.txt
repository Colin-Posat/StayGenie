HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location in ManhattanSituated in Manhattan, The Westin New York Grand Central offers easy access to major landmarks like Grand Central Terminal and the United Nations.Modern AmenitiesThe hotel boasts a fitness centre, business centre, and on-site dining for guests to enjoy during their stay. The rooms feature flat-screen TVs, work desks, and complimentary toiletries in the en suite bathrooms.Dining and ServicesGuests can unwind at the on-site bar and lounge, indulge in a Grab &amp; Go breakfast with a variety of options, and make use of room service and concierge assistance.Experience the best of New York City with Times Square, Empire State Building, and Chrysler Building just a short distance away. Book your stay at The Westin New York Grand Central for a memorable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note, guests must be at least 21 years or older to check-in.

Please note: Crib requests are subject to availability and need to be confirmed by management.

Please note that the parking fee does not include exit and reentry.

Please note that 'breakfast included' rates include continental breakfast for 2 adults only, irrespective of room occupancy.

The destination fee includes:

- Enhanced High Speed Internet Access
- Complimentary local, long-distance, and international calls
- 35 USD food and beverage credit per room per day
- 20 USD dry cleaning credit per room per day
- One yoga class at local studio per room per day
- Citi Bike pass for two or official Grand Central self-guided audio tour for two per day
Please see the concierge on the lobby level to book any of these experiences.
