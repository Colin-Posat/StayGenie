HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views and Modern ComfortsThe elegant APA Hotel Asakusa Kuramae offers well-equipped guest rooms, just a 2-minute walk from Kuramae Station. Enjoy stunning views of the Sumida River from the public bath on the top floor.Comfortable and Convenient AmenitiesThe compact rooms feature a flat-screen TV, fridge, work desk, and an en suite bathroom with a bathtub. Guests can also experience the luxury of an original mattress designed for ultimate comfort.Delicious Dining Options and Nearby AttractionsIndulge in a daily buffet breakfast at Gin Yuba Restaurant. Conveniently located near Asakusa Sensoji Temple and just a short walk from Asakusa Station, the hotel is the perfect base for exploring Tokyo.Book now to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at APA Hotel Asakusa Kuramae.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform APA Hotel Asakusa Kuramae in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
From Kuramae Subway Station, Exits A6 and A7 are closest to the hotel. From Asakusa Subway Station, Exits A1 and A2-b are closest to the hotel.

Guests arriving after check-in hours must inform the property in advance. If the property is not informed by 00:00 on the arrival day, the booking may be treated as a no show. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

When booking for 15 guests or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

The height limit for on-site parking is 200 cm.

On-site parking is available on a first-come, first-serve basis. In case on-site parking is fully occupied, guests will be redirected to a non-affiliated public parking space.

Renovation work at the hotel will be carried out from November 29th, 2024 to March 24th, 2025. Some rooms may be affected by noise.

Renovation and painting work on the 12th floor will take place from November 18th, 2024 to November 20th, 2024, from 11:00 to 17:00. Some rooms may be affected by noise.

Please note that the air conditioning on the 3rd, 5th, 6th, 10th, and 12th floors will be unavailable on November 20th, 2024 from 11:00 to 16:00.

[Notice of change in business hours of the large public bath]
Please note that the hotel will change the opening hours of the large public bath on the following dates due to pipe cleaning in the large public bath: 26th November, 6:00AM - 9:00AM / 3:00PM - 1:00AM.
