Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, although some reviews mentioned specific instances of cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, with a few exceptions citing issues with staff communication and assistance.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its convenience, with some guests mentioning proximity to key areas while others noted the lack of nearby attractions.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Guests had mixed reviews about room quality, with some highlighting spaciousness and comfort and others pointing out maintenance issues and cleanliness problems.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Views on amenities varied, with positive comments about the gym and pool but negative feedback on the lack of spa services and kitchen appliance issues.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was justified and others finding it too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services received generally positive feedback, although some guests desired more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing factors like room comfort, location, and staff friendliness.
