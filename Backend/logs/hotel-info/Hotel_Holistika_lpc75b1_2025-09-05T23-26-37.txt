HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Wellness Oasis - Indulge in massages, body treatments, and facials at our serene retreat. Enjoy the 2 outdoor pools, health club, and complimentary bicycles for your leisure.Gastronomic Delights - Savor delightful meals at our restaurant, or grab a quick bite at the coffee shop. The hotel also offers shopping on-site for your convenience.Comfortable Accommodations - Relax in one of our 24 guestrooms with modern amenities like free Wi-Fi, ceiling fans, and daily housekeeping. Discover nearby attractions such as Tulum National Park and Cenote Crystal.Book your tranquil escape at Hotel Holistika for a rejuvenating stay amidst nature's beauty and comfort.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Hot springs access available from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 5 years old or younger stays free, per registered adult, when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* Construction is underway at a neighboring building and there may be noise disturbance from the construction work.
* Alcohol is not served at this property.
