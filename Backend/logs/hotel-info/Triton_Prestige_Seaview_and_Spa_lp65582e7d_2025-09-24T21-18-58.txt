GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Great location
• Good organization of trips

Areas for Improvement:
• Issues with jacuzzi maintenance
• Poor value for the room rate
• Inconsistent food quality

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, though a few negative instances were highlighted.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Most guests praised the staff for their friendliness and helpfulness, but some mentioned issues with refund and room allocation.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and fast transport to the island were highly valued by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.5/10 - While rooms were generally comfortable, there were recurring issues with the jacuzzi and maintenance problems.
• Amenities: 5.5/10 - Several complaints were raised about the inadequate response to issues with the jacuzzi, impacting guests' overall experience.
• Value for Money: 3.5/10 - Many guests felt the hotel did not provide value for the high room rate, especially considering the poor response to problems.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on the food quality, with some guests finding the variety lacking while others praised the good food options.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Despite issues with specific aspects, most guests had a positive overall experience and recommended the hotel.
