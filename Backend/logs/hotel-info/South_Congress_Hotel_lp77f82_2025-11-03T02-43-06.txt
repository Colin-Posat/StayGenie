HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and Convenient FacilitiesSouth Congress Hotel in Austin's vibrant South Congress District offers a year-round outdoor pool, fitness centre, and a chic terrace for guests to unwind. Indulge in delectable meals at the on-site restaurant and enjoy the convenience of private parking.Elegant Rooms with Modern ComfortsEach room at the boutique hotel is thoughtfully designed, featuring air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. Some rooms boast scenic views, while all guests are provided with plush bath robes and premium toiletries. Stay connected with complimentary WiFi available throughout the property.Exceptional Services and Nearby AttractionsExperience top-notch hospitality with a 24-hour front desk, a cash machine, and a variety of shops on-site. Explore nearby attractions like San Marcos and Round Rock, with Bergstrom Airport just a short drive away. Book your stay at South Congress Hotel for a memorable Austin getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for US$30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 45 per  day.
