Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned small room sizes and tight bathrooms.
• Service: 8.1/10 - The majority of guests appreciated the helpful and friendly staff, but there were isolated incidents of poor accountability and service mishaps.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's prime location near key attractions and public transportation was highly valued by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were generally described as clean, comfortable, and newly decorated, though some guests found them on the smaller side.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While some guests appreciated the amenities provided, others were disappointed by the absence of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Mixed feedback on the perceived value for money, with some guests finding the price reasonable given the location, while others felt it was on the higher side.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, though some guests desired more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
