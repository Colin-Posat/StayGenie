HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm in the Heart of VeniceExperience the charm of a 15th-century building at The Locanda del Ghetto. Located in the heart of the Venetian Ghetto area, this property offers rooms with elegant parquet floors and original ceiling beam decorations.Delicious Kosher BreakfastIndulge in a generous kosher breakfast at the property, served directly underneath the Schola Italiana Synagogue. Enjoy a variety of options including croissants, cereals, hot drinks, and fruit juice to start your day right.Modern Comforts and ConvenienceRelax in air-conditioned rooms equipped with a TV, minibar, and en suite bathroom. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi access during your stay. The Locanda is conveniently located within walking distance to Saint Mark's Square and Santa Lucia Train Station.Book your stay at The Locanda del Ghetto for a memorable experience in Venice.
