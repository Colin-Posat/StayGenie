HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 70 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.4/10 - Guests consistently mentioned the spotless and comfortable rooms as a positive aspect.
• Service: 9.3/10 - Service was exceptional and staff were praised for being friendly, attentive, and helpful.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel is conveniently located near key landmarks and in a nice area, offering easy access to attractions.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were described as spacious, beautifully designed, and comfortable, although some guests noted the need for room furniture upgrades.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests appreciating the thoughtful details and others mentioning the need for updates, especially in the gym and spa.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Opinions on value varied, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the services and location, while others found it expensive for what was offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with a few guests suggesting improvements in variety and pricing.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had an exceptional overall experience, praising the hotel's ambiance, staff professionalism, and luxurious feel.
