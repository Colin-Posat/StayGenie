GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location

Areas for Improvement:
• Expensive parking fees
• Poor communication regarding financial matters

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the rooms, although some minor maintenance issues were mentioned.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was appreciated by most guests, but some had complaints about response times and communication with management.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location near downtown and key landmarks was highly regarded by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were comfortable and well-appointed, with a retro vibe that was generally well-liked, although some guests felt the rooms were too small for two people.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests were satisfied with the amenities provided, including the pool and rooftop bar, but some mentioned the lack of certain facilities like a fridge in the room.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the prices were high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Overall, guests enjoyed the food and beverage options, although some felt there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed their willingness to return to the hotel in the future.
