HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsEach air-conditioned studio or suite at Cable Mountain Lodge features a seating area with a satellite TV, a microwave, and a small refrigerator for your convenience.Scenic Views and RelaxationEnjoy a seasonal outdoor heated pool and a hot tub with stunning canyon views, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring Zion National Park.Convenient AmenitiesWith free Wi-Fi, a convenience store, gift shop, picnic area, and even a private beach on-site, Cable Mountain Lodge ensures a comfortable and enjoyable stay for all guests.Book your stay at Cable Mountain Lodge today for a memorable experience near Zion National Park!
