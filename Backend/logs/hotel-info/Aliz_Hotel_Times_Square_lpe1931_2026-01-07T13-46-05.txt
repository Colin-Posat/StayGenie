HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooftop Bar and LoungeExperience breathtaking views of the Manhattan skyline at the two-story rooftop bar and lounge at Aliz Hotel Times Square. Enjoy a relaxing evening with a drink in hand while taking in the iconic cityscape.Modern Amenities and ComfortIndulge in comfort with air-conditioned rooms, flat-screen TVs, and Keurig® coffee machines. Some rooms offer terraces or river views for a truly unique stay. The private bathrooms are stocked with premium bath amenities for your convenience.Convenient Facilities and ServicesStay active at the fitness center and cycling studio with Peloton bikes® or catch up on work at the business center. The 24-hour front desk staff are always ready to assist you, ensuring a seamless stay at the heart of New York City.Book now to elevate your New York experience at Aliz Hotel Times Square.
