HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Ottoman-Style Hotel in the Heart of SultanahmetOpulent Rooms and Turkish Bath Wellness CentreDecorated in Ottoman-style architecture, Sirkeci Mansion boasts rooms with ornate wood carvings and a wellness centre offering a Turkish bath and massages. The indoor pool adds to the relaxing atmosphere, perfect after a day of exploring the nearby Topkapı Palace.Culinary Delights and Rooftop Terrace with Bosphorus ViewIndulge in Turkish cuisine at Neyzade Restaurant, with the option to dine in an elegant setting or enjoy a Bosphorus view on the rooftop terrace. The terrace bar offers refreshing drinks and a stunning panorama, ideal for unwinding after a day of sightseeing.Complimentary Activities and Convenient LocationTake advantage of free Turkish cooking classes and city tours arranged by the hotel. With key attractions like Hagia Sofia Museum, Basilica Cistern, and the Blue Mosque within walking distance, Sirkeci Mansion offers a perfect blend of luxury and convenience.Book now to experience the charm of Sirkeci Mansion and create unforgettable memories in Istanbul.

PETS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 12 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
