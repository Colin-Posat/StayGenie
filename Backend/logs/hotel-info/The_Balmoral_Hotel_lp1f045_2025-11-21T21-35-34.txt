HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation:The Balmoral, first opened in 1902, offers elegantly designed rooms with marble bathrooms and interactive TVs. Many rooms boast stunning views of Edinburgh Castle and the Old Town, providing a truly unforgettable stay.Culinary Delights:Indulge in a gastronomic experience at The Balmoral's dining establishments. From the fine dining menu at Number One Restaurant to the informal Brasserie Prince and the innovative cocktails at Bar Prince, there's something to satisfy every palate. Don't forget to enjoy a traditional afternoon tea at Palm Court.Relaxation and Wellness:Unwind and rejuvenate at The Balmoral Spa, featuring a pool, sauna, Turkish steam room, and 5 treatment rooms. With an exercise studio available, guests can stay active and pampered during their stay.Experience luxury and sophistication at The Balmoral in the heart of Edinburgh. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

Spa Update

Use of Spa Facilities
Use of our spa facilities is complimentary for hotel residents during their stay. Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for £50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for £50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  GBP 40 per  day.
