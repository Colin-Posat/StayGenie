HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience unparalleled luxury in our modern 5-star boutique hotel, The Glasshouse, Autograph Collection. Each room boasts floor-to-ceiling windows offering stunning views, along with premium amenities like Nespresso coffee makers and Highland Aromatics toiletries.Stunning Rooftop GardenIndulge in the beauty of our 2-acre rooftop garden, overlooking the picturesque Calton Hill. Whether enjoying breakfast in The Brasserie or savoring local delights in the Rooftop Garden, the panoramic views will leave you breathless.Prime LocationConveniently located near Edinburgh's top attractions, including Edinburgh Castle and Princes Street, our hotel offers easy access to the city's vibrant scene. With a tram stop nearby providing a direct link to the airport, your travel convenience is guaranteed.Book now to elevate your Edinburgh experience at The Glasshouse, Autograph Collection.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The Glasshouse Hotel is pleased to offer discounted parking at the nearby Q-Park Omni - our guests can take advantage of a 50% discount when pre-booking their parking online via https://www.q-park.co.uk/en-gb/cities/edinburgh/omni/ and using discount code GH50.

We are also pleased to offer our guests access to the Nuffield Health & Fitness Center next door in the Omni center. Passes are available for purchase with our Front Office team in order to use their Gym, Swimming Pool, Sauna and Steam Room.

Guests who are members of Marriott Bonvoy, or who enroll at the property - may be eligible to receive Bonvoy points on any additional spend at the property but will not receive any points or credit on bookings.

For guests who have booked a rate excluding Breakfast, the additional daily cost to add this on would be £30 per person for the Full Breakfast (which includes a plate from our A La Carte menu and the Continental Buffet Selection) or £15 per person for the Continental Breakfast offering only.

Please note that all guests will be requested to provide a valid Credit or Debit card for pre-authorization upon check-in as guarantee against any extras or incidentals (this will also include the stay cost for bookings that are to be paid at the hotel).Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for £75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  GBP 12 per  day.

• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, with some guests suggesting more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
