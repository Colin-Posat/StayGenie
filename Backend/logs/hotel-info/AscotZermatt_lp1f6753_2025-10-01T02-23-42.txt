HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Apartments with Matterhorn ViewsAscot-Zermatt offers modern apartments with balconies and stunning Matterhorn views, just 300 meters from the Sunnegga Funicular Station. Each unit features a fully equipped kitchen with a Nespresso Coffee Machine, a cozy seating and dining area, and a bathroom with a shower or bathtub. The highlight of these apartments are the large, south-facing windows that offer unobstructed views of the surroundings.Convenient LocationSituated in Zermatt, guests can easily explore various restaurants, a bakery, a café, and a supermarket within a short distance of 450 meters. For those looking to explore the mountains, the Gornergrat Mountain Railway is only 500 meters away, and the Matterhorn Express Cable Car is a mere 900 meters from Ascot-Zermatt.Free WiFi and EntertainmentIn addition to the breathtaking views and convenient location, guests can enjoy free WiFi throughout the property. Each apartment is equipped with a DVD player and satellite TV for entertainment purposes.Experience the beauty of Zermatt with a stay at Ascot-Zermatt.
