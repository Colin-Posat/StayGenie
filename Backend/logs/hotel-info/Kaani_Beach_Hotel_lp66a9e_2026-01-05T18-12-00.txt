HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tropical Paradise SettingNestled on Maafushi Island, Kaani Beach Hotel offers a sunny beach escape surrounded by swaying coconut palm trees.Exciting On-Site ActivitiesGuests can indulge in thrilling adventures like scuba diving, island picnics, dolphin watching, and snorkeling, ensuring an unforgettable stay.Modern Comforts and AmenitiesRelax in rooms with private balconies boasting city or sea views, equipped with a flat-screen TV, minibar, and en suite bathroom. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please share your flight details with the property before your arrival to secure your seat(s) for the transfer.

Transfers should be booked in advance, as we don’t have our boats in Male or the airport. We’ll have to send the boat to Maafushi. The travel time by speedboat is 35 minutes. The boat can be shared with other guests.

However, sharing a boat is subject to other guests' transfer requests for the same flight or time, or only if you meet people at Male's airport who want to go to Maafushi upon your arrival.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 5 years old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 17 years old stay for US$25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 17 years old stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
