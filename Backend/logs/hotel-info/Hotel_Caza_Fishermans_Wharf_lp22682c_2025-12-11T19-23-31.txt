HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Caza Fisherman's Wharf, located in San Francisco, offers a premium stay just 400 meters from Ghirardelli Square. The hotel boasts free bikes, private parking, an outdoor pool, and a fitness center for guests to enjoy.Top-Notch FacilitiesGuests can indulge in the hotel's restaurant, relax in the shared lounge, or seek assistance from the 24-hour front desk. With free WiFi throughout the property, a garden, and a sun terrace, every need is catered to with utmost care.Exciting ActivitiesFor leisure, guests can engage in a game of billiards at this 4-star establishment. Moreover, popular attractions like Alcatraz, Pier 39, and Coit Tower are just a stone's throw away, ensuring a memorable stay.Ready to experience luxury and convenience at Hotel Caza Fisherman's Wharf?

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 2 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 73.52 per  day.

Category Ratings:

• Service: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the friendliness of the staff and the helpful service, but there were complaints about responsiveness and noise levels.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location near major attractions and public transportation was highly valued by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and suitable for families, but noise insulation and specific room amenities like fridges were points of contention.
• Amenities: 8.1/10 - The hotel's amenities, especially the pool and games lobby, were well-received, but additional charges and missing amenities like fridges were noted.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on the value for money, with complaints about additional costs, parking fees, and lack of included amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - While breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, there were mentions of high prices and limited options.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Despite some issues, most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel.
