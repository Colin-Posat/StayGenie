HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Location with Stunning ViewsHotel Latinum offers a unique experience with its proximity to the iconic Porta Maggiore, allowing guests to soak in the vibrant atmosphere of Rome right from their rooms.Fascinating Interior DesignStep into a world where modern comforts meet ancient history as the hotel showcases an archaeological excavation beneath its glass floor, adding a touch of intrigue to your stay.Comfort and EleganceWith 12 modern and elegantly decorated rooms, each equipped with wooden furniture and some featuring a balcony overlooking Piazza di Porta Maggiore, Hotel Latinum ensures a comfortable and stylish retreat in the heart of Rome.Experience the allure of Rome from a different perspective at Hotel Latinum.
