HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Elegant 19th-Century Property: The 29 London offers elegant 19th-century charm with good-value accommodation and friendly service. Located just a 5-minute walk from Victoria Coach Station and nearby Buckingham Palace, our hotel provides a convenient and central stay in London.Modern Amenities: Enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi access, a 24-hour reception, and a luggage store facility during your stay. Each comfortable room is equipped with a TV, telephone, tea/coffee facilities, and a private bathroom featuring a walk-in shower for your convenience.Prime Location: With Victoria Rail and Underground Station only a 10-minute walk away, guests have excellent access to central London and the Gatwick Express for a quick 30-minute service to Gatwick Airport.Book your stay at The 29 London for a delightful blend of comfort, convenience, and historic elegance.
