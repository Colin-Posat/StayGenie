HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience serenity at Maya Ubud Resort &amp; Spa, a hidden gem nestled in a lush green valley along the Petanu River.Relaxing Spa RetreatIndulge in rejuvenating spa treatments by the river, designed to melt away your stresses and immerse you in pure tranquility.Dining DelightsSavor delicious international cuisine at Mayasari or enjoy sumptuous snacks and free afternoon tea at the River Cafe and Tree Bar, all while overlooking breathtaking views.Active Lifestyle &amp; AmenitiesStay active with our fitness center, tennis courts, and yoga classes held in an open-air pavilion, ensuring a balance of relaxation and physical rejuvenation.With free WiFi throughout and just a short drive to Ubud’s cultural treasures, this resort is your ideal escape. Don’t wait—book your unforgettable stay today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
CHILDREN are STRICTLY PROHIBITED from being in/or accessing the Infinity Riverside Pool area.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for Rp 790,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 9/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with butlers receiving special praise for going above and beyond.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's setting in the jungle and proximity to nature received high praise.
• Room Quality: 6/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with some guests mentioning uncomfortable beds and unreliable Wi-Fi.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Facilities like shared pools were often full, and guests desired more private options.
• Value for Money: 5/10 - Some guests felt that the experience fell short of expectations for the price point.
• Food and Beverage: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on dining options, with some guests mentioning mishandled bookings and limited dinner choices.
• Overall Experience: 9/10 - Many guests had a truly relaxing and exceptional experience, praising the service and the serene setting.
