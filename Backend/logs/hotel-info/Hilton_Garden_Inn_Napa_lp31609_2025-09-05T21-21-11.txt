Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Overall, guests mentioned that the rooms were clean, but a few reviews highlighted stains or cleanliness issues.
• Service: 7.8/10 - The service was generally positive, with comments on friendly staff and efficient check-in/out processes. However, there were some complaints about unhelpful or combative front desk staff.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised by guests for its proximity to key landmarks and amenities.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests generally found the rooms comfortable and well-equipped, but some mentioned issues with noise or outdated furnishings.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While some guests appreciated the amenities such as the gym and pool, others mentioned the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the hotel was overpriced for the accommodations and services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and dining options received generally positive feedback, though a few guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Guests had varied experiences, with some highly recommending the hotel for a pleasant stay while others expressed dissatisfaction with various aspects.
