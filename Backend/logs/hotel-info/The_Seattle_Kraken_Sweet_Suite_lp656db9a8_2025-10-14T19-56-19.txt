HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Situated in Seattle's Washington region, The Seattle Kraken Sweet Suite offers a charming terrace and shared amenities. Convenient Facilities such as a shared kitchen and lounge add to the comfort of guests, with free WiFi available throughout. Modern Comforts like a flat-screen TV and premium linens ensure a relaxing stay. Explore nearby attractions like Space Needle and CenturyLink Field, or venture further to Tiger Mountain State Forest. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, this non-smoking property provides a perfect retreat. Secure your spot at The Seattle Kraken Sweet Suite for an unforgettable experience.
