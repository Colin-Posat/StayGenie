HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Convenient Location:La Ginabelle, just a stone's throw away from Zermatt Train Station and cable cars, offers a perfect blend of comfort and accessibility.Indulge in Culinary Delights and Relaxation:Savor the renowned gourmet cuisine and unwind in the expansive spa area with an outdoor pool at a soothing 34°C. Enjoy complimentary afternoon coffee and tea with delicious home-made cake during the winter season.Family-Friendly Amenities and Services:Families can take advantage of the kid's club while parents make use of the shuttle service to and from the train station, ensuring a hassle-free stay.Experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at La Ginabelle - book now for an unforgettable retreat.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 9 years old to 15 years old stay for CHF 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

• Room Quality: 8/10 - Rooms were generally spacious, comfortable, and well-maintained, with some reports of maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 8.2/10 - Guests appreciated the range of amenities, particularly the spa and pool, although some desired more options like spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, though some guests suggested more variety could improve the dining experience.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
