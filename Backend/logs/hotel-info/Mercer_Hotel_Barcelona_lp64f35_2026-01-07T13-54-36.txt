HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Swimming Pool and Stylish RoomsMercer Hotel Barcelona offers a unique experience with a rooftop swimming pool and stylish rooms featuring original beamed ceilings and chic décor. Enjoy modern amenities like free Wi-Fi and flat-screen satellite TV in a historic setting next to Roman walls.Charming Courtyard and Poolside BarRelax in the hotel's interior courtyard surrounded by orange trees, or sip on a refreshing drink at the poolside bar. The terrace with loungers is perfect for soaking up the Barcelona sun.Ideal Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated in the Gothic Quarter, Mercer Hotel is a short walk from Las Ramblas and close to Jaume I Metro Station. Explore the charming streets filled with cafés and restaurants, or visit nearby attractions like Barcelona Cathedral and the trendy Born district.Book your stay at Mercer Hotel Barcelona for a blend of history, luxury, and modern comfort.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 5 years old and over stay for € 75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
