HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationThe Econo Lodge is ideally situated in downtown Sacramento, just blocks away from the Convention Center and a short distance from Old Town Sacramento. Explore nearby attractions such as the State Capitol, Sacramento Zoo, and more.Comfortable AccommodationsChoose from a selection of connecting rooms and suites, all equipped with convenient amenities like irons and ironing boards.
