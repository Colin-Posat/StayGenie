HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Home2 Suites By Hilton Sacramento At Csus, where your ideal getaway awaits.Spacious AccommodationsExperience home-like comfort in one of our 103 air-conditioned rooms, each featuring kitchenettes complete with refrigerators and stovetops. Relax with entertainment on a 50-inch Smart TV while enjoying the convenience of complimentary Wi-Fi.Unmatched AmenitiesStart your day with a complimentary buffet breakfast before diving into recreational activities. Take a refreshing dip in our outdoor pool or stay on top of your fitness goals in our 24-hour fitness center.Prime LocationConveniently located just minutes from California State University Sacramento and the Golden 1 Center, you’ll be perfectly positioned to explore Sacramento’s attractions, including Cal Expo and Arden Fair Mall.Book your stay now and indulge in an experience that feels just like home!
