Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned minor issues like slippery shower floors.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests praising the professionalism and friendliness of the staff, while others mentioned issues with certain staff members.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location was considered great for exploring the city and close to key landmarks.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, although some guests felt they were small for the price and had issues with noise insulation.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests praising the bathroom facilities and WiFi, while others found rooms lacking in certain features like microwaves and refrigerators.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Guests had varied opinions on the value for money, with some finding it reasonable given the location, while others felt services did not justify the price.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - The food and beverage services received mixed feedback, with some guests enjoying the breakfast and overall restaurant experience, while others found the options limited and expensive.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some specific issues mentioned in individual reviews.
