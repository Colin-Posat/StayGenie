HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spectacular Views and Central LocationHotel Okura offers elegant rooms with stunning views of Kyoto and is conveniently located in the heart of the city, providing easy access to popular attractions and shopping districts.Luxurious Amenities and ServicesGuests can indulge in beauty treatments, enjoy free Wi-Fi, and take advantage of concierge services. The hotel also offers an indoor swimming pool, free parking, and a variety of boutiques at Shopping Avenue Comfort.Fine Dining and Culinary ExperienceExperience exquisite French cuisine at Sky Restaurant Pittoresque while enjoying panoramic mountain and city views. The hotel also features a bakery and various restaurants, including a top-floor buffet restaurant offering Kyoto cuisine.Book your stay at Hotel Okura Kyoto for a luxurious and unforgettable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Food & beverage services at this property may be limited or unavailable due to Coronavirus (COVID-19).
As a result of Coronavirus (COVID-19), this property has temporarily suspended its shuttle services.
Please note that some of the hotel's restaurants have a dress code, and some do not allow children under 10 years of age.

The fitness centre is accessible only for adults ages 18 and above and an additional charge are required.

The hotel's Welcome Lounge at Kyoto Station is located on the ground floor of the Cerema Building. It's open daily from 09:30 until 17:00, and accessible only to staying guests.

Free shuttle locations and schedules:
- From the Welcome Lounge at Kyoto Station, the shuttle departs at the top (--:00) and bottom (--:30) of every hour between10:30 and 17:00, except at 12:30 and 15:00.
- From Hotel Okura Kyoto, the shuttle departs at --:05 and --:35 every hour between 10:05 and 16:35, except at 12:05 and 14:35.
Please note that the shuttle takes about 20-25 minutes, depending on traffic conditions. Staff will meet guests at the hotel lobby or the Welcome Lounge.

The luggage delivery service operates as follows.
Luggage received by 14:00 at the Welcome Lounge, will arrive at the hotel by 17:00.
Luggage received by 11:00 at the hotel, will arrive at the Welcome Lounge on a requested time between 14:00 and 17:00.
