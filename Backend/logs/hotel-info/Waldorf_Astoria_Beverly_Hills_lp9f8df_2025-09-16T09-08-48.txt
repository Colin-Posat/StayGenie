Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.3/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness with some guests mentioning issues with housekeeping.
• Service: 7.4/10 - While some guests appreciated the friendly staff and prompt room service, others had concerns about slow responses to requests.
• Location: 8.8/10 - Highly praised location in the heart of Beverly Hills with easy access to attractions and hotspots.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally well-received for their views, amenities, and spaciousness, although some guests noted delays in housekeeping.
• Amenities: 8.5/10 - Guests loved the La Prairie Spa and rooftop restaurant, but some mentioned a lack of turn-down service and possible staff shortages.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding the breakfast and room upgrades worth it, while others felt certain services were lacking.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the food and service at the on-site restaurants, with a few mentioning the high prices.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - The overall guest experience was positive, with a few concerns about housekeeping, turn-down service, and value for money.
