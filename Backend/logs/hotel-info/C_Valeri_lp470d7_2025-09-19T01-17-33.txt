HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building and Unique DesignThe Cà Valeri B&amp;B offers a charming stay in a historic building, just a short stroll from the iconic St. Mark’s Square. Each room is uniquely designed with classic or Venetian-style decor, providing a one-of-a-kind experience for guests.Comfort and EleganceGuests can enjoy modern comforts in the traditionally styled rooms, including a TV, minibar, and air conditioning. The private bathrooms are well-equipped with toiletries, and some rooms boast exquisite Murano glass chandeliers. Whether with tiled or wooden floors, each room exudes elegance.Convenient Amenities and LocationWith free WiFi throughout the property and antique furniture adorning the public areas and rooms, guests can immerse themselves in the rich history of Venice. Situated near the San Zaccaria water bus stop and within walking distance of Venice Giardini parkland, Cà Valeri offers both convenience and serenity.Book your stay at Cà Valeri B&amp;B for a memorable Venetian experience.
