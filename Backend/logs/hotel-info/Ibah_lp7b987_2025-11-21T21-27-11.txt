HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Balinese Retreat by the Campuhan RiverSuite Villas with Breathtaking ViewsIndulge in traditional Balinese luxury with suite villas offering stunning views of natural scenery. Relax on your private balcony/terrace and unwind in the marble bathroom after a day of exploration. Enjoy in-room entertainment with cable TV.Tranquil Oasis with Spa and PoolDiscover serenity at Ibah's outdoor pool surrounded by a stone garden. Unwind on day beds nestled into a large stone wall or pamper yourself with soothing massages at the spa. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for Rp 750,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
