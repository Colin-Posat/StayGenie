HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in RomeThe Best Roma is ideally situated by the historic Porta Maggiore, offering easy access to iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Trevi Fountain. With bus lines and the metro nearby, exploring the city is convenient for guests.Vibrant SurroundingsLocated near bustling dining spots in Via del Pigneto and the lively San Lorenzo neighborhood, guests can immerse themselves in Rome's vibrant nightlife and culinary scene.Charming AccommodationsRetreat to the hotel's cozy rooms adorned with antique-style décor after a day of sightseeing. Start your mornings with a delightful continental breakfast in the graceful hall or in the comfort of your own room.Experience the allure of Rome at The Best Roma. Book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 20 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
