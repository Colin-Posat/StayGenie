HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Breathtaking ViewsSituated in downtown Vancouver, our 31-story hotel offers stunning views of the North Shore Mountains and Vancouver Harbour.Luxurious AmenitiesEnjoy a 52-foot indoor pool, fully equipped gym, and floor-to-ceiling windows in our guest rooms. Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  CAD 45 per  day.
