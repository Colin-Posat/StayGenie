HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Accommodations with City ViewsMADE Hotel in New York offers select rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows and modern amenities like air conditioning, bathrobes, and Samsung® flat-screen TVs. Some units even boast stunning city views.Diverse Dining OptionsGuests can enjoy an American breakfast at the property, savor tapas at Debajo, grab coffee at Paper, and unwind with craft cocktails at the Lobby Bar. The hotel provides a range of culinary experiences to suit every palate.Rooftop Lounge and PatioWith a patio on the 2nd floor and the Good Behavior lounge on the 18th floor, guests can relax in style while taking in panoramic views of New York City. Indulge in Tiki-inspired cocktails and craft beers at this chic urban retreat.Ready to experience the vibrant atmosphere of MADE Hotel? Book your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform MADE Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
A damage deposit of USD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Any reservation with a length of stay greater than 21 days is subject to different policies. The property will request a signed agreement after booking.

The resort fee includes:

- complimentary cup of filter coffee in the morning, redeemable at Paper Coffee
- complimentary glass of house red/white wine in the evening, redeemable at Paper Coffee
- free WIFI throughout
- access to Good Behavior, barring private events
- Tokyo Bike X bikes available for hire, on a first-come, first-served basis and if wheather allows it.
