What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Staff was generally friendly and accommodating, with special mentions of helpfulness and service at the gondola reception.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's ski in/out location in Sunshine Village was highly appreciated, along with the proximity to key amenities.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were comfortable with positive mentions of bed quality and spaciousness, but some guests noted noise disturbances.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests enjoyed the hot tub, family room, and kids activities, but some mentioned the lack of USB ports in the rooms.
• Value for Money: 7.4/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some feeling it was reasonable for the location, while others mentioned high alcohol costs and expensive dining experiences.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Varied reviews on dining experiences, with positive mentions of restaurant food quality and negative feedback on expensive and subpar dining experiences.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a desire to return in the future, highlighting the friendliness of the staff and stunning views.
