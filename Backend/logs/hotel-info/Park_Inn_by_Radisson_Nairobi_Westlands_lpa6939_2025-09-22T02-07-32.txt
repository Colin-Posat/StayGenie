• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were well-decorated and comfortable, although some guests noted minor issues with amenities.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, particularly highlighting the gym and rooftop bar, but some mentioned the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Opinions varied regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the offerings, while others considered it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The quality of food and beverage services received positive feedback overall, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
