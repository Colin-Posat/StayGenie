HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Hôtel Dame des Arts offers standard and executive guestrooms with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and air conditioning. Some rooms and the rooftop bar provide stunning views of the city's iconic landmarks.Gastronomic DelightsStart your day with a delightful breakfast buffet served at the hotel. For a more intimate experience, opt for room service. Unwind on the garden terrace with a refreshing drink and delicious snacks from the hotel's bar and snack bar.Convenient LocationSituated just steps away from Notre Dame Cathedral and other major attractions, the hotel also offers a shuttle service and easy access to public transport via nearby Metro Stations Odéon and Saint-Michel.Experience luxury and convenience at Hotel Hôtel Dame des Arts.
