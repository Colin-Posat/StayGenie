HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Experiences and AmenitiesThe Pullman Paris Centre-Bercy, designed by Tom Dixon, offers a creative and casual atmosphere inspired by the vibrant Bercy district. Enjoy the wellness facilities, including a swimming pool, Spa by Sothys, barber shop, Power Fitness, and a summer terrace.Diverse Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in the trendy Ariette Bar &amp; Restaurant, co-working spaces, art gallery, and a private cinema by mk2. Immerse yourself in a collection of unique experiences within the hotel.Comfortable and Convenient RoomsExperience spacious and contemporary rooms with extra-large beds, big screen TVs, work desks, and tea/coffee making facilities. Relax with quality toiletries, rain showers, and enjoy the central location near major attractions like the Louvre Museum and Opera Garnier. Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hotel Pullman Paris Centre-Bercy!
