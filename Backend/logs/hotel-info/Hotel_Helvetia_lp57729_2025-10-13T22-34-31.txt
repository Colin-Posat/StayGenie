HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the enchanting Hotel Helvetia, a cozy retreat nestled in the heart of Zermatt, just moments away from the breathtaking Zermatt-Matterhorn Ski Paradise.Unmatched ConvenienceEnjoy seamless connectivity with complimentary wireless internet access throughout the hotel. Our convenient ski storage ensures you have everything you need at your fingertips, while our gift shops and newsstands provide little luxuries to enhance your stay.Delicious DiningIndulge in a delightful dining experience at Restaurant Helvetia, where you can savor a diverse menu crafted to please your palate. Start your day right with a complimentary buffet breakfast served daily, and unwind at our inviting bar/lounge with your favorite drink.Comfortable RoomsRelax in one of our 14 well-appointed guestrooms featuring flat-screen televisions, cable programming, and modern amenities such as safes and electric kettles.
