HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Villas with Private PoolsExperience ultimate relaxation in our tranquil retreat, Ubud Heaven, where most villas feature a private pool set in a beautifully landscaped garden. Enjoy the serenity and privacy during your stay.Traditional Javanese ArchitectureImmerse yourself in the charm of traditional Javanese architecture blended with Balinese rice fields at Ubud Heaven. The spacious villas offer a semi-open living area with rustic wood interiors, a well-equipped kitchen, and a dining area. The en suite bathroom with a bathtub and shower provides a touch of luxury.Convenient Amenities and ServicesIndulge in soothing massages at the spa, take advantage of the free buggy service, and relish international and continental delicacies at Warung Ubud Heaven. Our 24-hour front desk staff is ready to assist with laundry, car rental, and area shuttles. For added convenience, airport shuttle service is available for a fee.Book your stay at Ubud Heaven now for a truly unforgettable experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 1,500,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for Rp 400,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
