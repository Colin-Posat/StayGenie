HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel Eden - Dorchester Collection offers luxury rooms with classic Italian furnishings, satellite LED TV, and city views. The perfect blend of comfort and elegance awaits you in each room.Culinary ExcellenceIndulge in gourmet Mediterranean cuisine at the award-winning La Terrazza restaurant with panoramic views. Pair your dinner with exquisite wines for a truly unforgettable dining experience. Additionally, enjoy lunch and dinner at Il Giardino Ristorante, and start your day with an extensive buffet breakfast.Prime LocationLocated near Via Veneto street and within walking distance to the Spanish Steps, Villa Borghese park, and the Trevi Fountain, our hotel provides the ideal base for exploring the beauty of Rome.Experience luxury and sophistication at Hotel Eden - Dorchester Collection. Book your stay now to create unforgettable memories in the heart of Rome.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 65 per  day.
