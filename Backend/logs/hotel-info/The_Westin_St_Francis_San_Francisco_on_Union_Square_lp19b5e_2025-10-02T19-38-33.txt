HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Union Square Hotel with Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsHistoric Charm and Modern Comforts: The Westin St. Francis San Francisco on Union Square offers a unique blend of historic charm with the iconic Magneta Grandfather Clock and modern comforts in its elegantly furnished &quot;Landmark Building&quot; rooms and stylish &quot;Tower Building&quot; rooms.Dining Options and Culinary Delights: Guests can indulge in a variety of dining options, from morning coffee at Cafe Rito to exquisite American favorites at The Oak Room Restaurant. The Clock Bar and Chateau Montelena provide the perfect settings for evening drinks and wine tastings.Convenient Location and Fitness Amenities: Situated in front of a cable car stop and within walking distance to Chinatown, this hotel also boasts a WestinWORKOUT fitness center for guests to stay active. Free WiFi in the lobby ensures connectivity throughout your stay.Experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at The Westin St. Francis San Francisco on Union Square. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
Please note that the hotel allows dogs only and no other types of pets.

For guests that book a rate that includes Free Parking, please note this is limited to one standard sized vehicle per night. Oversize vehicles may require additional fees.

Our daily resort fee includes: $25 USD Daily Food & Beverage Credit*, 2 Dynamic Westin St. Francis Stainless Steel Water Bottles, 2 hour bike rental (once per stay), 2 Round trip Cable Car Tickets (once per stay), Wine Tasting Flight for 2 (once per stay), Daily Enhanced High Speed Internet Access. Please visit our Concierge Desk in the lobby for more information about these items/experiences. *Food and Beverage credit is not cumulative and cannot be accumulated for multi-day stays (credit applies to room charges only).

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 70 per  day.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Comfortable beds
• Fantastic views
• Friendly staff

Areas for Improvement:
• Expensive breakfast
• Lack of certain amenities
• Maintenance issues in rooms
• Additional fees for services

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, although a few mentioned dust in the bathroom.
• Service: 8/10 - The staff were generally friendly and accommodating, although there were instances of double charging and room allocation issues.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's central location near Union Square and key attractions was highly praised.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, but some guests noted the need for updates and maintenance.
• Amenities: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities; some guests appreciated the gym and room service, while others were disappointed by the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions were divided on the pricing, with some finding it reasonable for the location and others feeling it was overpriced, especially with additional fees.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the food options available, but some felt there could be more variety and better value for money.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hotel's convenience, comfortable beds, and friendly staff.
