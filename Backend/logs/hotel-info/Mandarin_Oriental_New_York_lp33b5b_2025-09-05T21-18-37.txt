HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsLocated in Columbus Circle’s Deutsche Bank Center, this hotel offers rooms and suites with floor-to-ceiling windows showcasing panoramic views of Central Park, the Manhattan skyline, and the Hudson River. Each room features a 55-inch flat-screen TV, a mini-bar restocked daily, and complimentary treats.Relaxation and Wellness AmenitiesGuests can unwind in the 75-foot indoor pool or indulge in a rejuvenating treatment at the full-service spa, complete with a sauna and massage rooms. Stay active at the on-site fitness center or seek assistance from the Concierge for NYC exploration.Gourmet Dining with Breathtaking ViewsMO Lounge presents delectable American cuisine and expertly crafted cocktails amidst unparalleled views of Central Park and the Manhattan skyline. Perfect for a memorable dining experience.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at Mandarin Oriental, New York.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 85 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$95 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
