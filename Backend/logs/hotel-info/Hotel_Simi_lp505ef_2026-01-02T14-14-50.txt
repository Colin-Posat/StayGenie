Spa Area for Relaxation:Indulge in luxury at Hotel Simi's spa area, featuring a sauna, steam bath, whirlpool, and solarium for the ultimate relaxation experience.Convenient Location:Located just 300 meters from Zermatt train station, our hotel provides easy access to cable cars, lifts, shops, and restaurants, ensuring you make the most of your stay without any hassle.Culinary Delights at Mountain Restaurant Simi:Savor authentic Swiss and Valais specialties at our mountain restaurant in Furi, situated 1800 meters above sea level. HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 9 years old to 16 years old stay for CHF 55 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 9 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 55 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
