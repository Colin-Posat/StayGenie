HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationMcMillan Inn in Savannah offers a 4-star bed and breakfast experience with elegant family rooms and modern amenities. Each unit is equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Some rooms even feature a fully equipped kitchen for added convenience.Charming AmenitiesGuests can start their day with a delicious buffet breakfast, including American and vegetarian options, served in a cozy setting or delivered to their room. The property also boasts a shared kitchen, concierge service, outdoor fireplace, picnic area, and a year-round outdoor pool for relaxation and enjoyment.Ideal LocationConveniently located near popular attractions like Lafayette Square and Forsyth Park, McMillan Inn is the perfect choice for travelers seeking comfort and convenience in Savannah. Book now for a memorable stay!
