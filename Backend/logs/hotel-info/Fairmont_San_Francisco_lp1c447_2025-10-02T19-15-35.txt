HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations:Indulge in high ceilings, modern amenities, and breathtaking views of San Francisco's iconic landmarks in every room and suite at Fairmont San Francisco. Enjoy the luxury of marble baths, Le Labo toiletries, and plush robes for a truly upscale experience.Dining and Entertainment:Savor the unique dining experience at Tonga Room &amp; Hurricane Bar, offering Polynesian-fusion cuisine and live entertainment on a floating barge. With two distinct dining options on-site, your culinary cravings are sure to be satisfied.Wellness and Recreation:Unwind at the full-service gym and spa, or take advantage of the health club's equipment, saunas, and exercise classes. Whether you're looking to stay active or relax, Fairmont San Francisco has all the facilities to cater to your needs.Experience luxury, dining, and wellness at their finest at Fairmont San Francisco. Book now for an unforgettable stay in the heart of the city.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 90 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$59 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and costs  USD  13.95 per  24 hours.
