Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.3/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness with some guests praising clean rooms and daily bedding change while others reported broken shower and lack of hot water.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service was generally appreciated with mentions of friendly staff, although some complaints about musty smell, outdated decor, and cleanliness issues.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Highly praised location near Surasak BTS Station, with easy access to key landmarks and perfect connections to Bangkok airport within 35 mins.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Guests enjoyed spacious rooms, comfy beds, and nice pool. Some mentioned rooms being huge and well-located, while others noted minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Mixed opinions on amenities, with some guests highlighting gym and pool while others complained about the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Varied feedback on pricing, with some guests feeling it was good value for central Bangkok while others found it expensive for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally well-received, though some guests suggested more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, though some mentioned issues like limited parking, expensive breakfast, and no spa services.
