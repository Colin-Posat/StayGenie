HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Hotel with Prime LocationLocated next to the Alamo and the River Walk, Menger Hotel offers a blend of history and modern comfort. Enjoy a meal at the Colonial Room Restaurant or take a dip in the outdoor pool.Charming AmenitiesRelax in the courtyard garden or stay active in the gym. With free WiFi, cable TV, and coffee makers in guest rooms, your stay is sure to be convenient and comfortable.Convenient and CentralAdjacent to Rivercenter Mall and close to popular attractions like the San Antonio Zoo and Botanical Garden, Menger Hotel is the perfect base for exploring the city. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 17 years old stay for US$20 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 45 per  day.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the hotel, although some minor cleanliness issues were reported.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was appreciated by most guests, with efficient and friendly staff. Some guests mentioned issues with room service and check-out times.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's prime location near key attractions like the Alamo and River Walk was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and cozy, with some complaints about room size and AC cooling.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Most guests found the amenities satisfactory, with positive mentions of the pool and breakfast buffet. Some guests desired additional amenities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed reviews, with most guests appreciating the breakfast and restaurant options.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing the location and historical charm as highlights.
