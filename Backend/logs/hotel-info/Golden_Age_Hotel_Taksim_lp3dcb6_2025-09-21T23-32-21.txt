HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Prime LocationLocated just 300 metres from Taksim Square, Golden Age Hotel Istanbul offers a prime location with easy access to the metro station and Lutfi Kirdar Convention Centre. The rooms are elegantly decorated with Turkish influences, offering modern amenities for a comfortable stay.Exquisite Dining OptionsIndulge in a delightful culinary experience at Le Coq Restaurant, serving a lavish buffet breakfast and a tempting à la carte menu. Enjoy hot drinks and light snacks at the Nostaljia Bar or sip on refreshing drinks at the Pool Bar. Don't miss the complimentary tea time cookies served by the pool.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind in the Turkish bath, pamper yourself with a rejuvenating massage, or take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool surrounded by comfortable loungers. After a day of exploring Istiklal Avenue, retreat to the hotel for a relaxing evening.Book your stay at Golden Age Hotel Istanbul for a memorable experience in the heart of Istanbul.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Guests mentioned the cleanliness positively, although some complained about insects in the rooms.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Overall, service was appreciated, with specific mentions of friendly and helpful staff. However, there are complaints about unkind receptionists and unresponsive staff to noise complaints.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for being close to key landmarks, peaceful, and ideal for accessing tourist destinations.
• Room Quality: 6.5/10 - Most guests found the rooms comfortable but criticized maintenance issues, noise disturbances, and inaccurate room allocations.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Satisfactory amenities were highlighted, though some guests felt basic necessities were lacking or not replenished daily.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some feeling prices were high for the services provided while others found it reasonable due to the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and food services received positive feedback, but some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Generally, guests had a positive overall experience with the hotel, with specific mentions of great staff, location, and cleanliness.
