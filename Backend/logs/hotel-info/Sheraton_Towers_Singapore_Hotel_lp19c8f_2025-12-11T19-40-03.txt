HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and AmenitiesThe award-winning Sheraton Towers Singapore Hotel SG Clean Staycation Approved offers 5-star accommodation with an outdoor pool, spa treatments, and a 24-hour fitness centre. The rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows, flat-screen TVs, and en suite bathrooms with a bathtub and shower. Suites even have a separate living room for added comfort.Convenient Location and TransportationLocated just 400 meters from Newton MRT station, guests have easy access to explore the city with Downtown and North-South Lines. The hotel also offers a free shuttle service to Buona Vista, making travel hassle-free. Within walking distance to Orchard Road shopping area and local food stalls at Newton Circle Food Centre, guests can immerse themselves in the vibrant culture of Singapore.Culinary Delights and RelaxationIndulge in sumptuous meals at The Dining Room overlooking waterfalls and gardens, or savor authentic Cantonese cuisine at Li Bai Cantonese Restaurant. After a day of exploring, unwind with cocktails at The Lobby Bar or The Pool Bar. For relaxation, enjoy massage therapies at the spa, or rejuvenate at the sauna and steam room.Book now for a luxurious stay at Sheraton Towers Singapore Hotel SG Clean Staycation Approved.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for S$ 119.90 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Friendly staff
• Excellent cleanliness
• Convenient to public transport

Areas for Improvement:
• Rooms showing age
• Mixed reviews on value for money
• Limited room amenities

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.5/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the rooms and facilities.
• Service: 8.3/10 - The majority of guests appreciated the friendly and helpful staff, although some mentioned minor issues with housekeeping.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's proximity to key locations such as the Newton MRT Station and food center was highly valued by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - While some guests found the rooms comfortable, others mentioned that they were starting to show signs of age and needed refurbishment.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests appreciated the facilities such as the pool and gym, but some felt that there could be more variety or upgrades.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the prices were high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and dining options were generally well-received, although some guests suggested improvements in the variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, highlighting aspects like great staff and location.
