HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in the Heart of Mexico CityHotel Ritz Ciudad de México, situated on lively Madero Street near Zócalo Square, offers classic rooms with free Wi-Fi and cable TV.Delightful Dining OptionsIndulge in authentic Mexican cuisine at the hotel's Madero restaurant or unwind with a drink at the bar.
