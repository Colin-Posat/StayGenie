HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spacious &amp; Elegant RoomsHotel Le Richemont offers spacious and elegant rooms in Paris’ 13th arrondissement, also known as the Asian Quarter. All rooms feature flat-screen TVs and free WiFi, making your stay comfortable and connected.Convenience &amp; AccessibilityThe hotel prioritizes guest convenience with easily accessible rooms for guests with disabilities. Additionally, ironing facilities are available in a dedicated room, and upon request, guests can get a travel adapter and luggage scale from the reception.Prime Location &amp; Nearby AttractionsSituated near Metro Olympiades, with direct access to key destinations like Chatelet-Les-Halles and Gare de Lyon, exploring Paris is effortless. A short walk to Bercy Village offers a variety of shopping and dining options, while the proximity to AccorHotels Arena adds excitement to your stay.Experience Paris in comfort and style at Hotel Le Richemont.
