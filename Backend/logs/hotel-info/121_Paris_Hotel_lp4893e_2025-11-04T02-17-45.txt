HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Montmarte LocationNestled in the historic Montmarte district in Paris, 121 Paris Hotel offers a charming stay just minutes away from iconic landmarks like the Sacré Coeur Basilica and the Moulin Rouge.Comfortable AccommodationsEach room at the hotel is equipped with modern amenities including a TV and complimentary WiFi. Guests can unwind in a private bathroom featuring a bath or shower, toilet, and sink. Plus, there's a specially adapted room for guests with reduced mobility.Convenient ServicesStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast in the cellar-dining area and enjoy the convenience of a 24-hour front desk. With the Brochant Metro Station just 100 meters away, exploring Paris's famous attractions like the Champs Elysees is a breeze.Ready to experience the charm of 121 Paris Hotel? Book your stay now!
