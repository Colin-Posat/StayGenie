HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationAPA Hotel Hanzomon Hirakawacho, open since June 2014, is just a 6-minute walk from Hanzomon and Kojimachi subway stations. With free WiFi throughout the property, staying connected is a breeze.Comfortable RoomsExperience optimal comfort in our compact rooms featuring double beds with original mattresses for ideal weight distribution. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, desk, and a private bathroom with a bathtub and complimentary toiletries.On-Site AmenitiesIndulge in the convenience of drinks vending machines and start your day with a delicious breakfast buffet at our restaurant. Whether exploring Shibuya, Ginza, or Shinjuku, our hotel's proximity to major attractions makes it the perfect choice for your stay.Book now for an unforgettable experience at APA Hotel Hanzomon Hirakawacho!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The full amount of the reservation must be paid at check-in.

Guests arriving after 24:00 must inform the property in advance. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

Please note that only the Single Room accommodates 1 child under 6 years old in the existing bed, free of charge. The Double Room accommodates up to 2 people, no additional children allowed.

When booking for 15 guests or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Please note that air conditioning is centrally controlled and guests cannot change the settings by themselves.
