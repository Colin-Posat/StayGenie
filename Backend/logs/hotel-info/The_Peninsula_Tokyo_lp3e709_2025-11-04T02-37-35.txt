HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Japanese InfluencesThe Peninsula Tokyo offers opulent rooms with traditional Japanese designs, featuring modern amenities like bed-side curtain controls and built-in flat-screen TVs in the marble bathrooms.World-Class Spa and Fitness FacilitiesIndulge in relaxation at the spa center, where guests can enjoy massages, beauty treatments, saunas, and aromatherapy showers. Stay active at the fitness club with a swimming pool and sun loungers overlooking the Imperial Palace Gardens.Culinary Delights and Spectacular ViewsExperience a variety of dining options, from Japanese and Cantonese cuisines to high tea at The Lobby. End your day at Peter: The Bar on the 24th floor, savoring cocktails and creative cuisine while taking in sweeping views of Tokyo.Book now to immerse yourself in luxury and hospitality at The Peninsula Tokyo.

Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Please inform The Peninsula Tokyo in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Swimming Pool is closed from Mon 20 Jan 2025 until Fri 24 Jan 2025
To use the hotel's shuttle, please make a reservation in advance. Shuttle services are charged. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

Please note that a minimum age of 16 years applies for entrance to the spa and fitness facilities.

Children between 4 years and 11 years can access the pool from 09:00-18:00 only when accompanied by a parent or guardian.

When booking 10 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Pool Closure Notice: Please be informed that the swimming pool and vitality pool will be closed from Monday 20th January 2025 until Friday 24th January 2025 for annual routine maintenance. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and thank you for your kind understanding.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 17 years old stay for ¥12,650 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  JPY 6000 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.5/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel rooms and public areas.
• Service: 8.4/10 - The attentive and helpful staff were highlighted by many guests, with some minor complaints about speed of service.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and public transport received positive feedback.
• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Guests appreciated the spacious and comfortable rooms, with some mentioning minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.9/10 - Satisfactory amenities were provided, with positive mentions of the gym and pool, though some guests desired additional spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some guests feeling it was high for the services offered while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive reviews, though some guests suggested more variety in the menus.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
