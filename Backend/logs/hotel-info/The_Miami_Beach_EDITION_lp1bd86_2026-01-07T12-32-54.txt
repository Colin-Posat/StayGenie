HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and EntertainmentIndulge in the ultimate luxury experience at The Miami Beach EDITION with an on-site ice skating rink, 2 outdoor pools, and a petite spa. Enjoy a range of activities including a bowling alley, nightclub, and fitness center, ensuring entertainment for all guests.Culinary Delights and Refreshing BeveragesSatisfy your taste buds at Matador Room offering delectable Latin cuisine and Tropicale serving light poolside snacks. The Market provides a variety of cocktails, sandwiches, and a raw bar. Unwind at the Matador Bar or the Lobby Bar with a selection of wines, champagnes, and other beverages.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated just 2.3 km from Lincoln Road Mall, The Miami Beach EDITION offers easy access to Bass Museum of Art, Art Deco District, and Bayshore Municipal Golf Course. Experience the best of Miami Beach during your stay at our exquisite hotel.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$75 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
