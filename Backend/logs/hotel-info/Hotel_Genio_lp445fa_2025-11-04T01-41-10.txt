HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Terrace with Panoramic Views:Hotel Genio offers a stunning rooftop terrace with breathtaking city views, providing the perfect spot to unwind and admire the beauty of Rome.Comfortable and Well-Equipped Rooms:All rooms at Hotel Genio are air-conditioned and feature either carpeted or parquet floors, along with amenities such as a TV and minibar, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Delicious Continental Breakfast and Prime Location:Start your day with a delightful continental breakfast buffet at Hotel Genio, offering a variety of fresh and tasty options. Situated just 120 metres from Piazza Navona, surrounded by Rome's charming streets filled with popular wine bars and restaurants, the hotel's prime location allows for easy exploration of the city's attractions.Experience the charm and convenience of Hotel Genio - book your stay now for an unforgettable Roman getaway!
