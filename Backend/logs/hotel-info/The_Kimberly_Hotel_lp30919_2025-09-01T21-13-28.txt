HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Kimberly Hotel, located in the heart of New York, offers elegant accommodation just steps away from iconic landmarks such as St Patrick's Cathedral and Rockefeller Center. Guests can indulge in the comfort of well-appointed rooms, some even equipped with convenient kitchenettes.Exceptional AmenitiesExperience top-notch amenities at The Kimberly Hotel, including a fitness centre for health enthusiasts, private parking for convenience, and a charming terrace to unwind after a day of exploration. The hotel also boasts a restaurant serving delicious meals, catering to every culinary desire.Prime LocationConveniently situated near popular attractions like Chrysler Building and Grand Central Station, The Kimberly Hotel is the perfect choice for travelers seeking luxury and convenience in the bustling city of New York. Book your stay now to experience the epitome of hospitality.
