Luxurious Spa RetreatFeaturing a full-service spa offering massages, body treatments, and facials, The Royal Corin Thermal Water Spa &amp; Resort - Adults Only is a haven for relaxation. Take a dip in one of the 3 outdoor swimming pools, 2 spa tubs, or the rejuvenating hot springs.Gastronomic DelightsIndulge in international cuisine at Basalto restaurant or opt for room service.
