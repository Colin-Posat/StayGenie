HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Beaux-Arts Hotel in Central ManhattanElegant Rooms and Amenities: The 504 rooms at Martinique New York on Broadway, Curio Collection by Hilton, boast flat-screen TVs, desks, and complimentary bottled water for the first night. Guests can also request a mini refrigerator, subject to availability.Convenient Facilities: Enjoy the 24-hour fitness centre and same day dry cleaning services during your stay at Martinique New York on Broadway.Prime Location: Situated less than 1 km from Times Square, with landmarks like Madison Square Garden, Herald Square, and the Empire State Building just steps away, this hotel offers the perfect blend of luxury and convenience.Book your stay at Martinique New York on Broadway for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Manhattan.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
