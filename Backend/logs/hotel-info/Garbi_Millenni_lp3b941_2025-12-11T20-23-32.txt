HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique SettingHotel Millenni is a chic boutique hotel located steps away from Paral.lel Metro Station. The property boasts a charming roof terrace with a relaxing hot tub and stunning city vistas.Modern AmenitiesEach room at Hotel Millenni is tastefully decorated and equipped with modern conveniences such as free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a luxurious bathroom. Guests can enjoy a delicious buffet breakfast daily and explore the vibrant bars and restaurants in the Raval district.Relaxation and EntertainmentGuests have access to a well-equipped gym, a soothing hot tub, and a rejuvenating sauna. The hotel also offers internet access on computers, a cozy library with newspapers, and a lounge for unwinding.Experience the perfect blend of comfort and style at Hotel Millenni.
