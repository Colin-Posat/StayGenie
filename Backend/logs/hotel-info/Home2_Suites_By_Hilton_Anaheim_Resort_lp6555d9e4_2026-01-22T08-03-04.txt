HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Conveniently Located Across from DisneylandModern Amenities in Every RoomConvenient Facilities and Nearby AttractionsBook your stay at the Home2 Suites by Hilton Anaheim Resort, CA for a magical and convenient Disneyland experience. Enjoy complimentary wireless internet, coffee makers, and a refreshing outdoor pool during your stay. With laundry facilities, a gift shop, and a business center on-site, your comfort and convenience are our top priorities. Plus, being minutes away from popular attractions like Knott's Berry Farm and Anaheim GardenWalk, there's never a dull moment. Experience the best of Anaheim and beyond with us.
