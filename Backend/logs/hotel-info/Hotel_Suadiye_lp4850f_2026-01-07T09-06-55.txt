HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Sea Views and Luxury AmenitiesHotel Suadiye offers a prime location by the Sea of Marmara, providing guests with breathtaking views of the sea and the Prince Islands from its terrace. Each elegantly furnished room boasts picturesque views and is equipped with modern amenities like satellite TV, air conditioning, and a minibar. Some rooms even feature a cozy seating area with a plush sofa for ultimate relaxation.Relaxation and ConvenienceGuests can unwind in the sauna, stay active in the fitness center, or indulge in a soothing massage treatment. The hotel also houses a well-equipped business center for those mixing business with leisure. Additionally, the proximity to Bagdat Avenue offers a shopping haven for guests seeking luxury brands.Culinary Delights and After-Dinner RelaxationStart your day with an extensive buffet breakfast and savor delectable dishes from the a-la-carte lunch and dinner menus either indoors or on the terrace with stunning views. Afterward, unwind at the Lobby Bar for a perfect after-dinner experience.Book your stay at Hotel Suadiye now to experience luxury by the sea!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
