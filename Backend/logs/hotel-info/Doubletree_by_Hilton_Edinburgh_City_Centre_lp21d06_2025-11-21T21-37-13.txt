Stunning Views of Edinburgh CastleDoubletree by Hilton Edinburgh City Centre offers unparalleled views of the iconic Edinburgh Castle, making it a perfect choice for a memorable stay in the heart of the city.Luxurious AccommodationsExperience comfort and style in the modern en suite rooms featuring luxury Egyptian cotton bedding and convenient amenities like hairdryers and tea/coffee facilities, ensuring a relaxing stay after a day of exploring the city.Award-Winning Dining OptionsIndulge in culinary delights at the Monboddo Bar with its stylish ambiance or savor modern and classic dishes at the award-winning Bread Street Brasserie, showcasing the best of Scottish beef and fish for a truly unforgettable dining experience.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Doubletree by Hilton Edinburgh City Centre!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not possible) and costs  GBP 18 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 17 years old and over stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Overall, guests found the rooms clean, but some reviews noted a bad smell in the room and halls.
• Service: 8.4/10 - The friendly and helpful staff were highlighted positively in many reviews, with exceptional service from specific individuals.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks such as Edinburgh Castle was praised by many guests.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests generally enjoyed spacious, clean rooms, but some mentioned dated interiors and soft pillows.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities offered were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the restaurant and friendly staff.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Several guests felt the hotel was overpriced for the services provided, citing issues with room quality, menu choices, and additional charges.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - Breakfast options were well-received by guests, with positive comments about the variety and quality of dishes.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, particularly praising the friendly staff, convenient location, and enjoyable dining options.
