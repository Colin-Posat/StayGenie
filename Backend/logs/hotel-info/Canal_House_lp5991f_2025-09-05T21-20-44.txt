HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique RoomsCanal House presents boutique-style rooms in the heart of Grachtengordel-West, just steps away from Westermarkt tram stop. The rooms boast sumptuous décor, featuring dark woods and lavish fabrics, along with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and work desks.Prime Shopping and AttractionsSurrounded by high-end shops and cultural hotspots, Canal House offers a convenient location for guests. Explore nearby attractions such as the Amsterdam Tulip Museum and Anne Frank House, both a short stroll away. Indulge in shopping at renowned stores like Marcel Wanders and Bijenkorf, all within walking distance.Central ConvenienceWith Amsterdam Central Railway Station a mere 15-minute walk from the hotel, guests enjoy easy access to transportation hubs for seamless travel. Book your stay at Canal House now for a luxurious and centrally located experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for € 75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
