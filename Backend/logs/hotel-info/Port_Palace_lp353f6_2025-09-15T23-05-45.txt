IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Port Palace in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note that the option for an extra bed is available only in the Deluxe and Suite categories.

Please note that a preauthorisation of EUR 100 will be requested on your credit card upon arrival. The amount will be released upon check-out.

Please note that guests will be asked to show the credit card used to make the reservation at check-in. If you do not have the credit card, please ensure you contact the property in advance to let them know.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Adults  stay for € 99 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 50 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, with minimal complaints.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was a highlight for many guests, although there were some mentions of rude or inattentive staff.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's location was a standout feature, providing beautiful views and easy access to key attractions in Monaco.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Guests generally found the rooms to be spacious, comfortable, and well-appointed, although some minor noise issues were reported.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests praising the rooftop restaurant and views, while others noted a lack of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions on the value for money varied, with some guests feeling the hotel was expensive for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - While breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, there were some complaints about limited options and high prices.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, citing the location, views, and staff as major highlights.
