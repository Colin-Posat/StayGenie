HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Miami AirportSituated just five minutes away from Miami International Airport, the Hampton Inn and Suites Miami Airport South/Blue Lagoon offers a complimentary shuttle service, ensuring a stress-free arrival and departure for guests.Exploration and RelaxationLocated a short drive from Miami city centre, guests can easily explore popular attractions, dine at world-famous restaurants, shop at nearby malls, and immerse themselves in the vibrant nightlife of the city. For those looking to connect with nature, the proximity to Everglades National Park allows for memorable day trips.Comfort and AmenitiesWith soundproofed windows ensuring a peaceful night's sleep, guests can wake up refreshed and indulge in a complimentary hot breakfast. The hotel's outdoor swimming pool provides a refreshing escape, while the modern fitness centre caters to wellness needs during the stay.Book your stay at the Hampton Inn and Suites Miami Airport South/Blue Lagoon for a perfect blend of convenience, exploration, and relaxation.

• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Guests found the rooms to be modern, inviting, and comfortable, with special mention of the comfortable beds.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the amenities, with some guests noting the outdated fitness center and lack of maintenance in certain areas.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money offered by the hotel, with some guests appreciating the location and shuttle service, while others expected more for the price.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast offered, but some suggested improvements in variety and shuttle service timing.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with specific praises for the staff and cleanliness.
