HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Prime LocationAmstelzicht, a 3-star hotel housed in a townhouse dating back to 1659, offers a charming stay in the heart of Amsterdam. Overlooking the scenic river Amstel, the hotel is just a stone's throw away from the vibrant Rembrandtplein and the iconic Flower Market.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesThe rooms at Amstelzicht seamlessly blend traditional Amsterdam architecture with contemporary decor. Guests can enjoy spacious box-spring beds, flat-screen TVs, and complimentary WiFi. For a touch of luxury, opt for a double room with canal views, featuring double window frames for a peaceful retreat.Convenient Facilities and ServicesComplete with a minibar, tea/coffee making facilities, and private bathrooms, Amstelzicht ensures a comfortable stay. Indulge in a Nespresso coffee while admiring the picturesque canal views. Plus, guests can relish complimentary coffee or tea in the inviting lobby.Ready to immerse yourself in the historic allure of Amsterdam? Book your stay at Amstelzicht today for an unforgettable experience.
