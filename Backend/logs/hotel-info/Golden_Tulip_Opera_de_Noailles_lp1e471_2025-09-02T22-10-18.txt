HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in ParisThe Golden Tulip Opera de Noailles offers a contemporary and family-owned hotel experience in the heart of Paris, just steps away from the iconic Opéra Garnier. Modern Amenities All rooms are air-conditioned and soundproofed, equipped with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a minibar. The private bathrooms feature a bath and/or walk-in shower, ensuring a comfortable stay. Relaxation and Wellness Guests can indulge in a complimentary buffet breakfast served in the inner garden, and access the sauna and fitness center for relaxation. Prime Location With the Louvre Museum within a kilometer, this hotel is the perfect choice for travelers interested in art, culture, and city walks. Experience Paris at its best at Golden Tulip Opera de Noailles.

Please note that the owner is completely responsible for the dog's behaviour and they cannot be left unattended at any time.

Guests booking the deluxe room should specify in the Special Request box if they will be 2 adults or 2 adults and 1 child.

When booking 4 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements will apply.
