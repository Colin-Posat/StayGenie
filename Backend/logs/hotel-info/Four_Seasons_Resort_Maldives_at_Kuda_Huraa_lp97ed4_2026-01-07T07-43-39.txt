HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in the mesmerizing ocean views and tropical blooms surrounding the rooms at Four Seasons Resort Maldives at Kuda Huraa. Enjoy the blend of modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and DVD players with traditional Maldivian open-air showers in the spacious bathrooms.Exciting ActivitiesFrom surfing and shark safaris to dolphin-watching and diving, there's no shortage of exhilarating experiences at this resort. Guests can also enjoy tennis, billiards, snorkeling in coral gardens, and rejuvenating marine-inspired treatments at The Island Spa.Dining and EntertainmentWith four restaurants, two bars, and private dining options including sandbank barbecues and overwater gazebos, culinary delights await. Whether you're seeking a romantic dinner or a casual beachside lunch, the dining experiences here are sure to impress.Book your stay at Four Seasons Resort Maldives at Kuda Huraa for an unforgettable tropical getaway!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that the property is only accessible by speedboat transfer, 25 minutes from Velana International Airport. Transfers are not included in the estimated room rates.

Shared speedboat rates, inclusive of all taxes: 
Adult (13 years and above): USD 289.58 per adult per way
Child (5-12 years): USD 141.57 per child per way
Child (0-4 years): Complimentary 

Premium and private boat options are available upon request. Please contact the property directly with your flight details to check for pricing and availability.

CHILD POLICY: 
Children under 4 years will eat for free based on the rules and meal-plan accompanied by an adult, excluding alcoholic beverages. Maximum 2 free kids per unit paid. Mandatory green tax of USD 12 per child per night will be charged. We are more than happy to accommodate kids below 8 years in our overwater accommodations, however, the parents will be required to sign a liability form.

Please note that there is a compulsory Christmas Eve Gala Dinner supplement on the 24th of December and a compulsory New Year's Eve Gala Dinner supplement on the 31st of December.

Adult (13 years and above): USD 482.63 per adult
