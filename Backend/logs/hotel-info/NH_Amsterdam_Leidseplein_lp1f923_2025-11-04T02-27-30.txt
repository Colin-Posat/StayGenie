HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Leisure Facilities and Elegant Rooms NH Amsterdam Leidseplein offers a perfect blend of leisure facilities and elegant rooms. The air-conditioned rooms boast hard-wood floors, warm crème colors, and bright accents. Enjoy complimentary tea/coffee facilities, a minibar, and satellite TV in every room, along with extra-long beds for your comfort.Convenient Location Situated in the museum and fashion district, our hotel is only 250 meters from the famous Leidseplein and a short walk to the Stadsschouwburg. Explore nearby attractions such as the P.C.Hooftstraat shopping area and Museum Square, with restaurants, bars, and theatres just steps away.Newly Refurbished Bar &amp; Kitchen Copper Unwind and socialize at our newly refurbished Bar &amp; Kitchen Copper. Sip on a refreshing drink while enjoying the vibrant ambiance of the hotel.Book your stay at NH Amsterdam Leidseplein and experience the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and style.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Children younger than 12 years may stay free of charge in their parents' room if the room size allows this.

Children younger than 12 years are charged EUR 9.95 for breakfast. Children from 0 until 4 years can have breakfast free of charge.

Please note that the city tax is not included in the price. Guests can settle this upon arrival.

When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Please note that dogs and cats are allowed upon request and subject to approval. The maximum weight is 25 kg. A charge of EUR 25 per night will be applied (max of 2 pets per room). Guide dogs stay free of charge.
