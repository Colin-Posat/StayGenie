HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience luxury at its finest at Canopy By Hilton Scottsdale Old Town. Our 4-star hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each equipped with a private bathroom for your comfort. Some rooms even feature a fully equipped kitchen for your convenience.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind in our outdoor swimming pool or indulge in a soothing soak in our hot tub. For the more active guests, hop on one of our complimentary bikes and explore the beautiful surroundings of Scottsdale. With a 24-hour front desk, we are always here to cater to your needs.Convenient LocationConveniently located just 18 km from both Copper Square and Phoenix Convention Center, and 6.1 km from Desert Botanical Gardens, our hotel is the perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers.
