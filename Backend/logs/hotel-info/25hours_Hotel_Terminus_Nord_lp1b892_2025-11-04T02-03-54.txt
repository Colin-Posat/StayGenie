HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 28 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Rooms were clean and well-maintained, with cleanliness often being noted as a standout feature.
• Service: 7.2/10 - Service received mixed feedback, with friendly staff but some complaints regarding room service and check-in procedures.
• Location: 9.2/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its proximity to transportation and local attractions, a highlight for many guests.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Guests enjoyed the funky and unique room design, though some found the rooms too small and experienced maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6/10 - Amenities were viewed as limited, with guests noting the absence of certain basics such as tea or coffee makers and poor sound isolation.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the offered amenities and room sizes.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Food quality was generally appreciated, especially the variety at breakfast, though some guests wanted more options or better coffee.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Guests generally had a positive experience, with the hotel's unique charm and friendly ambiance contributing to a favorable overall impression.
