HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThe Hampton Inn Miami Airport East offers a year-round outdoor pool and fitness centre for guests to enjoy, ensuring a relaxing and rejuvenating stay. The guestrooms are equipped with modern conveniences like a flat-screen TV, coffee-making facilities, and a private bathroom. For added comfort, select rooms come with a microwave and small refrigerator.Convenient ServicesStart your day right with a complimentary breakfast provided by the hotel. Guests can also take advantage of the on-site launderette, luggage storage, and business centre, making your stay hassle-free and convenient.Prime LocationSituated just 4 km from Miami International Airport and 12 km from the city centre, Hampton Inn Miami Airport East offers easy access to key destinations in Miami. Book your stay now and experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note the airport shuttle hours are from 06:00 until 22:00.

Guests must present the same credit card used for making the reservation at the time of check in.

Shuttle service is provided daily from 6am to 12am (midnight).

 Pickup from Miami International Airport (MIA) is located on the Second Level Departures. Please call the Hotel directly at 786-801-3136 for any questions or concerns.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 12 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
