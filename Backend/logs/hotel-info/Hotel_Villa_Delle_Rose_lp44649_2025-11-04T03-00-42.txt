HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsVilla delle Rose, a family-run hotel housed in a historic home, offers a 200 m² garden and free WiFi. The classic rooms with carpeted floors and flat-screen TVs provide a comfortable stay.Relaxing Garden OasisEnjoy drinks from the bar in the serene garden or opt for a continental breakfast served buffet-style. For a more intimate experience, have breakfast brought to your room upon request.Convenient LocationSituated a short walk from Rome's Termini Train Station, the hotel provides easy access to bus, metro, and train connections. Traveling to and from Ciampino and Fiumicino Airports is also hassle-free.Book now to experience the perfect blend of history and hospitality at Villa delle Rose.
