HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations:Staybridge Suites - Miami International Airport offers air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities, including a kitchen and a flat-screen TV. The comfortable seating area and desk in each room provide a relaxing space for guests.Convenient Facilities:Guests can take advantage of the outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, and barbecue facilities during their stay.
