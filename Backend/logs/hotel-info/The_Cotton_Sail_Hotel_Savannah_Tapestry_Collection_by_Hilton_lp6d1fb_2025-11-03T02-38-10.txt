Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews about cleanliness. Some guests mentioned spots on the floors and sheets, while others found the hotel to be clean overall.
• Service: 8/10 - Guests appreciated the helpful and friendly staff, although some experienced delays during check-in.
• Location: 8.6/10 - Highly praised location close to key landmarks and attractions, with easy access to public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms received mixed feedback, with some guests loving the room features like hardwood floors and heated bathroom flooring, while others had issues with noise and lack of natural light.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Satisfactory amenities, with some guests missing a mini fridge in the room and others mentioning the absence of a microwave.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Mixed opinions on the value for money, with some guests feeling the hotel was overpriced for the quality offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, but some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would consider staying again, citing factors like the amazing view and charming location.
