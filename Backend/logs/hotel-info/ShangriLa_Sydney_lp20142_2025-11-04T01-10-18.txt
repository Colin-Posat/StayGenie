HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsExperience waking up to unparalleled views of iconic Sydney landmarks like the Opera House and Harbour Bridge. The Shangri-La Hotel offers lavish rooms with marble bathrooms, free Wi-Fi, and plush amenities for a truly indulgent stay.Relaxation and Wellness AmenitiesUnwind and rejuvenate at the CHI, The Spa with a variety of body treatments and massages in private spa suites. Take advantage of the hotel's hot tub, sauna, and sun deck to enhance your relaxation experience.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentIndulge in exquisite dining at the award-winning Altitude Restaurant overlooking the harbour. End your day with creative cocktails at the Blu Bar on 36, offering breathtaking views of the city skyline. Book your stay now to immerse yourself in luxury at the Shangri-La Sydney.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Shangri-La Sydney in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
The name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property.

A 1.85% credit card surcharge for credit cards is applicable for all credit card transactions.

We require a credit card pre-authorisation or a prepayment of the full accommodation amount plus AU$145 per night for incidentals, for a guest to be able to check in to the hotel.

Guests who book breakfast-inclusive rate plans will receive breakfast for 2 adults and up to 2 children aged 5 and younger. Additional children under age 5 and children 6-11 years old will receive 50% off the adult breakfast price.

Please note over New Years a non-refundable, non-transferable pre-payment is required. Floor allocations will depend on the room type and date of arrival. Guests cannot request a specific floor during this time.

When booking 9 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements will apply.

The valet parking fee is AUD 135 per day for vehicles over 82.6 inches (2.1 meters) high.

Please note that Rollaway beds over NYE will be charged at $400 per night and $700 for Horizon Club Rooms and all Suites.

Operating hours for Altitude Restaurant and Blu Bar on Level 36 may vary during the Festive Season and on Public Holidays.

Please note that the Horizon club lounge is closed from 16 to 17 December 2024.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 275 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  AUD 105 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews regarding cleanliness, with some guests mentioning neat rooms, while others complained about issues like bedding not being neatly made.
• Service: 8/10 - Overall positive feedback about the staff's hospitality and service quality, although a few guests had complaints about front desk staff attitude.
• Location: 8.7/10 - Highly praised for its central location with stunning views of Sydney Harbour and close proximity to key landmarks.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Comments on rooms being spacious, comfortable with fantastic views, but some noted maintenance issues and hard mattresses.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Satisfactory amenities mentioned, including a pool, spa, and good facilities, but concerns about cold indoor pool and limited spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed opinions on the pricing, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some guests suggesting more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the location, staff hospitality, and comfortable rooms.
