HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 5 years old and over stay for Rp 575,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Beautiful gardens
• Friendly staff

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited sunbeds
• Mixed reviews on food quality
• Some noise in rooms
• Complaints about property theft handling

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although there were complaints about wait times and stolen property handling.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, but some mentioned minor maintenance issues and noise.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests enjoying the gardens, pools, and friendly staff, while others noted limited sunbeds and noise.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some feeling it was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were mixed, with some praising the breakfast buffet and staff, and others critiquing limited options and mediocre quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some shortcomings in service, room quality, and food.
