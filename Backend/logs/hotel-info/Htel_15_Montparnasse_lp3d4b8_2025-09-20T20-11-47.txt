HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the charm of Hôtel 15 Montparnasse, where Parisian elegance meets modern comfort.Luxurious Accommodations: Each of our 29 uniquely decorated guestrooms offers a touch of luxury, featuring minibars, espresso makers, and spacious areas for relaxation. Enjoy complimentary wireless internet and unwind with entertainment on 80-cm LCD televisions.Exceptional Amenities: Indulge in our full breakfast served daily, utilize our convenient room service, and experience the warmth of our cozy lobby fireplace. Our 24-hour front desk and concierge services ensure all your needs are met seamlessly.Prime Location: Nestled in the heart of the 15th Arrondissement, you are just minutes away from iconic landmarks like Montparnasse Tower, Luxembourg Gardens, and the Louvre Museum. Explore the vibrant local culture at your doorstep.Don't miss out on a delightful stay; book your escape at Hôtel 15 Montparnasse today!
