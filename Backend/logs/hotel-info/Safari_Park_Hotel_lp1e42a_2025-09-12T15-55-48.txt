HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Elegant Oasis in Nairobi's HeartNestled within 50 acres of beautifully manicured gardens, Safari Park Hotel offers a serene escape with an outdoor pool, casino, and spa. The spacious rooms, adorned with African-themed decor, provide a luxurious retreat with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and minibars.Culinary Delights from Around the WorldIndulge in a culinary journey at Safari Park Hotel with a variety of restaurants serving Italian, African, Japanese, and Chinese cuisines. Unwind at Hemingway’s lounge and bar, offering a relaxing atmosphere with garden views.Recreational Paradise and Convenient LocationEnjoy recreational activities like tennis or explore nearby attractions such as the Muthaiga Golf Club. With a 24-hour front desk and proximity to the airport, Safari Park Hotel ensures a seamless stay for all guests.Ready to experience luxury in the heart of Nairobi?
