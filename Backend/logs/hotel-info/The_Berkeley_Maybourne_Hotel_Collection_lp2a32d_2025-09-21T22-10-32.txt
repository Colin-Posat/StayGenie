HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 5-Star Hotel in KnightsbridgeAward-Winning Dining Experience: Indulge in exquisite cuisine at Marcus Wareing at The Berkeley, a Michelin-starred restaurant offering a culinary journey like no other. The Collins Room also awaits with its award-winning fashion-themed afternoon tea, a delightful experience for guests.Elegant Accommodations: The Berkeley boasts fine Italian marble bathrooms and luxurious fabrics in its bedrooms, ensuring a blend of comfort, style, and opulence for a restful night's sleep. Each room is designed to provide a beautiful and relaxing retreat for guests.Prime Location and Amenities: Situated just a short stroll from Hyde Park and Harrods, this hotel combines 5-star luxury with an excellent location. With a spa, fitness centre, and free WiFi, The Berkeley offers a perfect blend of relaxation and convenience for your stay.Book your stay at The Berkeley today for an unforgettable luxury experience in Knightsbridge.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform The Berkeley, Maybourne Hotel Collection in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
The Rooftop Pool at The Berkeley is closed from Fri 01 Nov 2024 until Fri 31 Jan 2025
Please be advised that any extra beds can be added to Deluxe King Bedded rooms only. Superior King Bedded rooms cannot accommodate additional beds.

Baby cots are upon request and need to be confirmed by the hotel.

Please note that guests staying for 7 nights or more will be required to pay a deposit.

Discounts can only be applied once per stay, back-to-back stays within a 24-hour period are considered one stay.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for £75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  GBP 65 per  day.
