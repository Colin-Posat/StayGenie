Some rooms even offer a quaint balcony with street views for a truly enchanting stay.Stylish Vuda Bar and TerraceUnwind at the chic Vuda Bar located adjacent to the hotel, where you can enjoy a refreshing cocktail or a steaming cup of coffee while watching events and music on the LCD TV.
