HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
1950s-inspired Boutique HotelLocated on US State Road 1, this boutique Miami hotel offers a unique experience with exterior hallways overlooking a mosaic pool. Each modern Vagabond Hotel room features a flat-screen TV, vibrant wall art, and hardwood floors. Guests can enjoy a fitness centre, seasonal live entertainment by the pool, and free parking. The on-site Mr. Mandolin Restaurant serves casual Mediterranean cuisine and craft cocktails. With free WiFi and just 7.6 km from Miami city centre, this hotel is the perfect blend of retro charm and modern amenities.Experience the retro charm and modern comfort at Vagabond Hotel. Book your stay now and explore Miami's vibrant attractions!
