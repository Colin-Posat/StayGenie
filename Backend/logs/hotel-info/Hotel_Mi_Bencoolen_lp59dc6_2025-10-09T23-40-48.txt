Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, although some mentioned minor cleanliness issues like dirty pillow cases.
• Service: 8.9/10 - Service was one of the highlights of the hotel, with many guests praising the friendly and helpful staff.
• Location: 9.1/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions, transportation modes, and amenities was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.7/10 - While rooms were generally comfortable, some guests mentioned issues like small room size, uncomfortable bedding material, and noisy A/C units.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating essential facilities provided, while others felt there could be more variety and additional services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was fair given the location, while others found it slightly expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, although some guests wished for more variety and added in-room tea/coffee options.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, appreciating the friendly staff, convenient location, and clean rooms.
