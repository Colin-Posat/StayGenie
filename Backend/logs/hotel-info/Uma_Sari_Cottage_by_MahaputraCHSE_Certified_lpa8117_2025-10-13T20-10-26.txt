HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Scenic Balinese-Style RetreatOverlooking the garden, Uma Sari Cottage by Mahaputra-CHSE Certified offers Balinese-style rooms with private balconies and free Wi-Fi. It features a landscaped pool, free shuttles to Ubud’s centre, and an Indonesian restaurant.Authentic Balinese ExperienceRooms open to tropical views, with finely crafted wooden bed frames and the option of a bathtub. Choose between fan-cooled or air-conditioned rooms for your comfort.Convenient Location and Leisure ActivitiesJust a short walk from Ubud’s village centre, Uma Sari is the perfect base for exploring. Enjoy afternoons at the art gallery, rent a motorbike to discover the area, or relax with on-site safe boxes and laundry services.Book now for a memorable stay at Uma Sari Cottage and immerse yourself in the beauty of Ubud.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 150,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
