HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
City-Centric ConvenienceLocated in Deira, just steps away from Union Metro Station and a short drive from Dubai International Airport, Panorama Deira Hotel offers comfortable rooms with city views and essential amenities. The hotel's proximity to Dubai World Trade Centre makes it a convenient choice for business travelers.Dining and EntertainmentGuests can start their day with a continental breakfast at Panoramic Café, and later enjoy a variety of local and international dishes. For sports enthusiasts, Booze Bar offers a vibrant atmosphere to watch games while savoring drinks and snacks.Local Excursions and LeisureWith a tour desk for car rentals and local attraction information, guests can easily explore the area. Adventure seekers can try sand surfing and camel riding, while shoppers will appreciate the hotel's proximity to Al Ghurair Shopping Centre and the beach.Book your stay at Panorama Deira Hotel for a seamless blend of comfort and convenience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Panorama Hotel Deira in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
