HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Terrace with Plunge PoolH10 Itaca boasts a stylish 700 m² terrace with a refreshing plunge pool, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring the city.Modern Rooms with Premium AmenitiesEach bright room at the hotel is equipped with free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, and a minibar, ensuring a comfortable stay. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of a private bathroom with Textures Collection amenities.Convenient Location and Complimentary OfferingsLocated near major attractions and transportation hubs, including Sants Train Station and Plaza España, H10 Itaca offers a complimentary glass of cava upon arrival. Additionally, guests can indulge in signature cuisine at the on-site restaurant and relax in the chic lobby or on the terrace.Experience the perfect blend of comfort and luxury at H10 Itaca - book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 24 per  day.
