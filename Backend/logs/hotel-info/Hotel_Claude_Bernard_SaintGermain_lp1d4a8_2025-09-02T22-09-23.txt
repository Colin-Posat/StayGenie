HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ParisHotel Claude Bernard is ideally situated just 1 km from the vibrant Saint-Germain-des-Prés district and a short 500 meters from the iconic Notre Dame cathedral. With numerous Metro stops nearby, exploring Paris's renowned attractions like the Louvre and the Eiffel Tower is effortless.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsGuests at Hotel Claude Bernard can enjoy a range of amenities including private balconies, satellite TV, air-conditioning, and modern bathrooms with both a shower and a bath. The cozy rooms provide a perfect retreat after a day of sightseeing in the city of lights.Convenient ServicesStart your day right with a delicious buffet breakfast served every morning. For added convenience, the hotel offers free WiFi internet access and an optional airport shuttle service for a hassle-free arrival and departure experience. Book your stay at Hotel Claude Bernard now for a memorable Parisian getaway.
