HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover luxury at Amwaj Rotana, Jumeirah Beach - Dubai, where relaxation meets sophistication.Unwind in StyleIndulge in rejuvenating treatments at our exquisite spa, offering a range of massages and facials tailored just for you. After a day of pampering, take a dip in the outdoor pool or relax in the sauna, all while enjoying our state-of-the-art fitness center.Gastronomic ExcellenceSavor delectable international cuisine at Horizon, All Day Dining, or opt for the convenience of 24-hour room service. With all-inclusive rates available, your culinary journey is both satisfying and stress-free, complemented by refreshing drinks from our poolside bar.Modern ComfortsExperience unparalleled comfort in our 301 well-appointed guestrooms, featuring minibars, LCD televisions, and private balconies. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and unwind with designer toiletries in your elegant private bathrooms.Book your stay today and experience the ultimate getaway!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* There is limited hot water on site.
* The seasonal pool will be open from June to December.
* Pool access available from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* Up to 2 children stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* Alcohol is not permitted at this property.
* A car is recommended for transportation to and from this property.
* Parking height restrictions apply.
