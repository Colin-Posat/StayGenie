HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Amsterdam Experience:Hotel Washington, located in an early 20th-century house near Museum Square, offers typical Amsterdam styled rooms with a charming backyard garden.Comfortable Accommodations:Benefit from rooms featuring a work desk, cable TV, and private or shared bathroom facilities. Enjoy garden terrace views from select rooms.Convenient Amenities:Start your day with an optional continental breakfast buffet and explore nearby dining options for dinner. With bicycle rental available, guests can easily visit Leidseplein by tram and explore the Albert Cuyp street market and Kalverstraat shopping area.Book your stay at Hotel Washington for a delightful Amsterdam experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Hotel Washington in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of EUR 100 is required. The host charges this 7 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
The reception is open between 08:00 and 23:00. Check-in after 23.00 is possible on request.

Please note that check-in after 00:00 is only possible upon request and against an extra fee of EUR 20.

Please note that breakfast is served from 08:00 until 10:00.

Please note that in case of a group reservation (more than 3 rooms), 14 days before date of arrival the reservation will be changed to a non-refundable reservation.

Please note that the hotel has no lift.

Guests must pay the total amount of the reservation upon check-in. Please note that reservations with a Non Refundable rate will be charged on a credit card on the day of

booking.
