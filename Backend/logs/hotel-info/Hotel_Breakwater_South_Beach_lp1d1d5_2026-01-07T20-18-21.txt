Luxurious Glass-Enclosed Pool and RestaurantIndulge in the vibrant South Beach scene at this boutique hotel with a stunning glass-enclosed pool and a restaurant featuring a full-service bar, just steps away from the beach.Modern Amenities and ServicesExperience comfort and convenience in every room with complimentary WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and an iPod docking station. Enjoy a rain-style shower and organic toiletries for a touch of luxury. Take advantage of the fitness and business centers, 24-hour reception, and bicycle hire services.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentSavor authentic Cuban cuisine at Havana 1957’s and enjoy all-day dining with nightly entertainment at Ocean's Ten.
