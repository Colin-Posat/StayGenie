HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AmenitiesFiesta Inn Insurgentes Sur offers a range of modern amenities including a gym, 24-hour reception, free Wi-Fi, and parking. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, work desk, safe, and coffee maker for your convenience.Dining with a ViewIndulge in a delicious buffet breakfast and dine at the hotel's restaurant with panoramic views. If you're looking for more dining options, the adjacent shopping center houses a variety of restaurants, bars, and cafés to explore.Convenience and ServicesEnjoy the convenience of laundry and dry cleaning services, a concierge and tour desk, and the option to request an airport shuttle for a hassle-free stay. With Barranca del Muerto Metro Station nearby, exploring Mexico City is a breeze.Experience comfort and convenience at Fiesta Inn Insurgentes Sur. Book your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that the property offers guest access with a guide dog.

• Additional nightly fee of $990.00 MXN plus taxes, per guest room. The fee includes your dog's stay and special cleaning.

• Only one small or medium dog with a maximum weight of 44 lb is allowed, or two dogs with a combined maximum weight of 44 lb.

• Your dog must always wear a leash or harness and remain within allowed areas: your guest room, circulation areas, and specially designed areas.

• Your dog cannot access the hotel's dining outlets, swimming pool, fitness center, event spaces, or restricted areas.

• Guests will be responsible for their dog's behavior. In case of any damage to the property or affectation to another guest or staff, the guest will be responsible and will pay accordingly.
