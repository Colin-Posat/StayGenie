HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Traditional Japanese ExperienceNishiyama Ryokan offers a unique Japanese-style stay with tatami rooms, kaiseki dinners, and yukata robes. Experience traditional futon bedding on woven-straw floors.Convenient LocationJust a short walk from Shiyakusho-Mae Subway Station, explore Kyoto easily with nearby access to JR Kyoto Station and popular temples like Honno-ji and Kamogawa River.Authentic Dining and RelaxationIndulge in authentic Japanese breakfast and multi-course dinners at the dining room. Unwind with in-room massages and enjoy the garden or rental bicycles for a leisurely day.Book your stay at Nishiyama Ryokan for an unforgettable Japanese hospitality experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
The property has a curfew at 24:00. Guests cannot enter or leave the property after this time.

The property recommends guests use a taxi from JR Kyoto Station, which is a 15-minute ride away.

Please be informed that the on-site restaurant is closed on Wednesdays.

Guests with children must inform the property of the age of each child in advance. Please note that adult rates are applicable to children 10 years and older, while different rates apply for children under 10 years old.

Please inform the the property in advance if you prefer Western meals for breakfast.
