HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Prime LocationExperience the charm of Paris at Le Patio Bastille, situated conveniently between Bastille and Nation. Indulge in our air-conditioned rooms and suites featuring modern amenities like satellite TV, private bathrooms, and kitchenettes.Delightful Dining and RelaxationStart your day with a delicious breakfast buffet and unwind in our serene garden and terrace. Our soundproofed accommodations ensure a peaceful stay amidst the bustling city.Convenience and ComfortBenefit from our 24-hour reception desk and private parking on-site. Explore Paris effortlessly with the Nation Metro and RER Station just 400 meters away, providing easy access to popular attractions like the Louvre Museum and Champs Elysees.Book now to elevate your Parisian getaway at Le Patio Bastille!
