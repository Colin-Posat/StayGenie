HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Amenities Hotel Globus offers modern, air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms, and entertainment options for children such as toys and a PlayStation 2 console.Convenient Services Enjoy free Wi-Fi, a large buffet breakfast, a buffet-style restaurant, and a modern business centre with printing facilities. The hotel also provides garage parking, travel assistance, and discounts at a local gym and beauty centre.Prime Location Situated in Rome's La Sapienza University district, Hotel Globus is just 2 metro stops from Termini Train Station, offering easy access to major attractions like the Coliseum and the vibrant San Lorenzo district.
