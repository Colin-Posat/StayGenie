HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesLocated just 9 miles from Miami International Airport, Holiday Inn Express Miami Airport Doral Area offers a convenient transfer service for guests. The hotel boasts a heated outdoor pool and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a comfortable stay.Comfortable AccommodationsEach guestroom is equipped with modern amenities including a small refrigerator, 42-inch flat-screen TV, and coffee-making facilities. Some rooms feature additional perks like a desk, seating area, and sofa bed, providing extra comfort and space for guests.Fitness and Business FacilitiesGuests can start their day with a delicious breakfast buffet at the hotel and later utilize the fitness center or the 24-hour business center. Additionally, golf enthusiasts will appreciate the proximity to Doral Country Club, only a 2-minute drive away.Book your stay now at Holiday Inn Express Miami Airport Doral Area and enjoy a relaxing and convenient experience.
