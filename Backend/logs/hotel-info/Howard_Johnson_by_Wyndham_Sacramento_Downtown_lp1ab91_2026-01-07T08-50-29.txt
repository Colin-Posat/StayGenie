HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location &amp; Shuttle ServiceLess than 0.8 km from the State Capitol, Howard Johnson by Wyndham Sacramento Downtown offers a free transfer service to Sacramento International Airport upon request between 4:00 am - 11:00 pm.Comfortable Rooms &amp; AmenitiesEach room features a flat-screen HD cable TV with premium channels, a coffee machine, and an en suite bathroom with a hair dryer.Relaxing Outdoor Facilities &amp; Nearby AttractionsGuests can unwind in the outdoor swimming pool and hot tub, or stay active in the fitness centre. The hotel is adjacent to the American River Bike Trail and close to attractions like the California State Railroad Museum, Crocker Museum, and Old Sacramento.Book your stay at Howard Johnson by Wyndham Sacramento Downtown for a memorable experience!
