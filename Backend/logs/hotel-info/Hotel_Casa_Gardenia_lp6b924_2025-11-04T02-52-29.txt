IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Please inform Hotel Casa Gardenia in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
When booking 5 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Guests must present a valid ID and credit card at check-in, where a €100 hold will be placed for potential damages. This amount will be refunded on the day of check-out if no damage is found and no smoking has occurred in the room.

Smoking, the use of illegal substances, or hosting bachelor/bachelorette parties or similar events is not allowed in this accommodation.

Pets up to a maximum weight of 15kg are accepted upon request, with a supplement of €25 per pet per night. A maximum of two pets is allowed per room.

Special requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
