HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationIndulge in a stay at the 4-star Carlton Hotel, located just 500 metres from the vibrant Grand Bazaar. The elegantly designed guest rooms offer modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi, and comfortable seating areas, ensuring a relaxing and enjoyable experience.Wellness and FitnessUnwind and rejuvenate with massage treatments and a sauna, or maintain your fitness routine at the well-equipped gym. After a workout, treat yourself to a traditional Turkish bath for the ultimate relaxation experience.Gastronomic DelightsSavor a delectable buffet breakfast at the hotel's restaurant, offering a variety of Turkish and European dishes for lunch and dinner. With its convenient location near major attractions, including the Blue Mosque and St. Sophia, Carlton Hotel is the perfect choice for your Istanbul getaway. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

When booking 5 rooms or more, applied policies are as following:

1. The property does not accept cash as a method of payment (card only).

2. The name of the guest needs to correspond with the name on the credit card when booking.

3. Please note that for non-refundable reservations the guests will be charged with total amount and there will be no refund in case of cancellation.

4. Please note that for refundable reservations the guests will be charged with a 50% non-refundable warrant as pre-payment.

5. The guests will be charged with total amount if they book sooner than 15 days to arrival.

6. The reservations can be cancelled until 15 days but 50% of the total amount will not be returned.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 20 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
