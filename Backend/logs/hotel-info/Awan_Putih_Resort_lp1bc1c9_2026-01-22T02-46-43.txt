HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesAwan Biru Villas in Payangan offers a sustainable 4-star accommodation with a pool boasting breathtaking views, a fitness centre, and private parking. Enjoy the full-day security and outdoor fireplace during your stay.Elegant Villa UnitsEach villa unit at Awan Biru Villas features air conditioning, a flat-screen TV with streaming services, and a private bathroom with luxurious bathrobes and slippers. Relax on your private balcony with outdoor furniture and enjoy the stunning pool views.Delightful Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in à la carte and continental breakfast options, including pancakes, fruits, and juice. Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
