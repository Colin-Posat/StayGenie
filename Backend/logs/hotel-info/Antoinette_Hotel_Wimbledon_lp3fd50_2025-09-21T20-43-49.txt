HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation - The Antoinette Hotel, located in the heart of Wimbledon, offers elegantly designed rooms equipped with modern amenities like air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and comfortable workspaces. Superior double rooms even boast private balconies for a touch of luxury.Convenient Location - Situated just 900 meters from Wimbledon mainline station, the hotel provides easy access to popular attractions like Centre Court Shopping Mall, Wimbledon Theatre, and the renowned Wimbledon tennis championships. Guests can also explore the nearby Wimbledon Village for a taste of local charm.Relaxing Amenities - Enjoy a tranquil stay with complimentary Wi-Fi, free on-site parking, and a serene roof garden perfect for unwinding after a day of sightseeing. Start your day right with a delicious Full English and Continental Buffet Breakfast served at the Rosewater Restaurant.Book your stay at the Antoinette Hotel now for a delightful Wimbledon experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:

INTERNET:

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for £15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 12 years old stay for £20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for £20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
