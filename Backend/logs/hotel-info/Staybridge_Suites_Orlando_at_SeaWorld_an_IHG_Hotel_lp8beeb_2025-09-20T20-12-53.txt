CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Clean rooms
• Friendly staff
• Great location
• Spacious rooms

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• Lack of spa services

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.1/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff mentioned by many guests. However, some reviews indicated slow response times.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to key attractions like SeaWorld and Aquatica, making it convenient for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were generally clean, spacious, and well-equipped, though some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with some guests praising the free breakfast and shuttle service, while others noted the absence of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling it was on the expensive side compared to the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, though a few guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to return, highlighting aspects like cleanliness, friendly staff, and convenient location.
