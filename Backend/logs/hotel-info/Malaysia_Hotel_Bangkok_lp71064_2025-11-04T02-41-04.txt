Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 2/10 - Guests consistently mentioned poor cleanliness as a major issue throughout the hotel.
• Service: 3.5/10 - Rude and unhelpful staff were a common complaint among guests, with many reporting a lack of customer service.
• Location: 5.8/10 - While some guests appreciated the location, others highlighted concerns about the surrounding area and limited services.
• Room Quality: 4.8/10 - Rooms were described as outdated, with uncomfortable beds and poor maintenance reported by several guests.
• Amenities: 5.3/10 - Mixed reviews for amenities, with some guests praising the pool and outdoor areas, while others noted the lack of services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - The value for money was a source of contention, with some guests feeling the price was justified and others finding it overpriced for the experience.
• Food and Beverage: 6.4/10 - Food quality received mixed reviews, with some guests enjoying the offerings while others found them lacking in variety and taste.
• Overall Experience: 1/10 - The majority of guests had an extremely negative overall experience, with many advising others to avoid the hotel entirely.
