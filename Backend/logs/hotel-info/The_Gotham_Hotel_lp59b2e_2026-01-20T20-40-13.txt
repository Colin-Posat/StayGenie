HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique Accommodation in Midtown ManhattanElegant Rooms with Loft-like Ceilings: Experience a touch of luxury in every room with loft-like ceilings, large windows, and elegant bathrooms featuring Italian glass mosaic tiles and premium toiletries.Gourmet Dining and Complimentary Amenities: Indulge in exquisite dining at our renowned gourmet restaurant and stay connected with complimentary WiFi throughout the hotel. Enjoy turn-down service upon request and take advantage of the free access to the nearby NYSC Gym.Prime Location with Nearby Attractions: Situated between Madison and 5th Avenues, The Gotham offers easy access to iconic landmarks like Rockefeller Centre and Times Square. Don't miss the chance to unwind on one of our five balconies with stunning views.Book your stay at The Gotham now for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Manhattan.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 40 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.03/10 - Some guests reported issues with cleanliness, indicating a lack of thorough cleaning in parts of the room.
• Service: 9.5/10 - Numerous reviews highlight the friendliness and helpfulness of the staff, providing excellent service.
• Location: 10/10 - The hotel’s location is frequently praised for its proximity to major attractions like Times Square and 5th Avenue.
• Room Quality: 8.54/10 - Rooms were generally described as spacious and comfortable, although some guests noted a dated feel.
• Amenities: 7.49/10 - Guests mentioned inadequate amenities, such as the absence of a dedicated fitness center.
• Value for Money: 8.02/10 - Several reviews expressed that the hotel was overpriced for the experience provided.
• Food and Beverage: 8.98/10 - The breakfast received positive feedback, contributing to the overall positive experience.
• Overall Experience: 9.03/10 - While there are some criticisms, the overall sentiment is positive, with many guests expressing a desire to return.
