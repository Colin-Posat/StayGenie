IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Best Western Mornington Hotel Hyde Park in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Payment Information (Multiple Bookings):

For reservations of 5 rooms or more, the hotel will charge your credit card 25% of the total booking cost at the time of booking. This amount is non-refundable. The remaining 75% will be charged to your credit card 14 days before your arrival date.

The credit card used to make the booking must be presented upon check-in. If the booking is made by a third party, please contact the property directly to make alternative arrangements.

Cancellation Policy (Multiple Bookings):

For reservations of 5 rooms or more, cancellations made less than 28 days before arrival will result in the hotel charging your credit card the cost of the entire reservation.

Extra beds must be requested directly with the hotel when booking to ensure availability.

Please note that the hotel will charge your credit card for the amount of 1 night, 48 hours prior to arrival.
