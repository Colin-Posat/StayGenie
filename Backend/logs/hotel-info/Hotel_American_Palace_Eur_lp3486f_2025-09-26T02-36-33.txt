HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical Property near Metro StationHotel American Palace Eur, located just 50 meters from the Laurentina Metro Station, offers a unique blend of history and modern convenience. Guests can enjoy drinks in the serene green courtyard surrounded by fruit trees, creating a peaceful oasis in the bustling city.Comfortable Rooms and 24-Hour GymThe air-conditioned rooms feature elegant wooden furnishings, satellite TV, and private bathrooms equipped with a shower and hairdryer. Additionally, the hotel provides a free 24-hour gym for guests to stay active and energized during their stay.Convenient Location and Easy Airport AccessBenefit from the hotel's quick metro connections to Rome's Termini Train Station. Moreover, the proximity to both Fiumicino and Ciampino Airports, reachable within 20 minutes by car, ensures a smooth transition for travelers.Experience the charm and convenience of Hotel American Palace Eur - book your stay now!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 6 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
