HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Venetian AccommodationHotel Marconi, located on the Grand Canal across from the iconic Rialto Bridge, offers elegantly decorated rooms in a typical Venetian style. Each room is equipped with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, minibar, and air conditioning. Some rooms feature classic designs, while others showcase refined antique Venetian furniture.Exclusive OfferingsGuests staying at Hotel Marconi can enjoy complimentary city maps and entrance to the prestigious Venice Casino. The hotel also serves a delicious buffet breakfast in its bright dining room, and guests can unwind with a drink while taking in the breathtaking views from the outdoor tables.Ideal LocationSituated amidst historic markets and authentic Veneto restaurants, Hotel Marconi provides easy access to public transportation and is a mere 700 meters from the renowned St. Mark’s Basilica. Experience the best of Venice at Hotel Marconi.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 7 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
