HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic French Quarter Inn on Market StreetLuxurious Amenities and ServicesGuests are welcomed with champagne at check-in and treated to a daily gourmet continental breakfast. Enjoy a nightly port wine and cheese reception, freshly baked cookies, and all-day flavoured iced teas and snacks. Evening turndown service with a local treat adds a special touch to your stay.Elegant Rooms with Modern ComfortsEach vintage-inspired room features free WiFi, a 40-inch flat-screen TV, Blu-Ray/DVD player, and a small refrigerator. Indulge in in-room breakfast or relax with a movie night.Convenience and HospitalityBenefit from free bike rentals, concierge services, and a 24-hour front desk. Unwind with in-room massages available upon request. Explore nearby King Street for shopping and dining or visit the South Carolina Aquarium just a short walk away.Book now to experience the charm and luxury of French Quarter Inn!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
