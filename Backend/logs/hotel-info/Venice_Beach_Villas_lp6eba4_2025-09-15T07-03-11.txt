HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience a slice of paradise at Venice Beach Villas in Florida. Indulge in the comfort of our modern accommodations featuring flat-screen TVs, fully equipped kitchens, and private bathrooms with free toiletries. Relax in style with some units offering a cozy seating area and patio to unwind.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by taking a dip in our refreshing outdoor pool or explore the area with complimentary bicycle use, perfect for cycling enthusiasts. With Venice Theatre just steps away and Venice Fishing Pier within a short distance, there is no shortage of entertainment options for guests.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy the convenience of free WiFi, private parking, and a range of kitchen appliances including a dishwasher, oven, microwave, toaster, and fridge. Start your day right with a fresh cup of coffee from the provided coffee machine. Book your stay at Venice Beach Villas for a memorable Florida getaway.
