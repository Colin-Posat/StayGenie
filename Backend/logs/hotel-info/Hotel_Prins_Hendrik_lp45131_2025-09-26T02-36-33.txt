HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Dutch City House HotelConvenient Accommodation: Hotel Prins Hendrik, located across from Amsterdam central station, provides comfortable rooms with seating areas, flat-screen TVs, and work desks. Enjoy a complimentary breakfast and WiFi during your stay.Dining Options: Indulge in a varied menu at Prins Hendrik’s restaurant, or relax on the terrace. The Amsterdam-style bar serves refreshing drinks and snacks all day.Prime Location: Explore nearby attractions like the Anne Frank House and the Royal Palace, all within a 10-minute walk. HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally well-received, but some guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
