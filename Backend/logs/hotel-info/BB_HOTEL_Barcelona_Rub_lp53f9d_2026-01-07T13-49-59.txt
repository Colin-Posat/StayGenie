HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Elegant AccommodationsThe B&amp;B Hotel Barcelona Rubi offers elegant and well-equipped rooms with modern amenities, including free Wi-Fi and a 43-inch flat-screen TV. Each room features parquet floors, a free safe, and a spacious shower in the bathroom, ensuring a comfortable stay.Convenient LocationLocated just a 10-minute walk from Rubi Ferrocarril Railway Station, our hotel provides easy access to both the business areas outside Rubi and Sant Cugat del Vallés. With a free private car park and proximity to the AP-7 Motorway, guests can explore the surrounding areas with ease.24-Hour ServicesEnjoy the convenience of a 24-hour cafeteria and buffet breakfast to start your day right. Whether you're heading to Barcelona or Sant Cugat, the Ferrocarril Railway offers quick connections for your travels.Experience comfort and convenience at B&amp;B Hotel Barcelona Rubi - book your stay now!
