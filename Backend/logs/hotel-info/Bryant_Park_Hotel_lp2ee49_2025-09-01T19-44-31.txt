Luxurious Boutique Hotel in Central ManhattanExquisite Room FeaturesIndulge in the elegance of hardwood floors, Tibetan rugs, and leather accents in each room. Enjoy entertainment with a 32-inch TV and Bose CD player. Pamper yourself in marble bathrooms with plush bathrobes and premium toiletries.Convenient AmenitiesExperience the vibrant lobby adorned with red furniture and black marble floors. Benefit from a 24/7 exclusive gym, concierge services, and access to a screening theatre and event space. Book your stay at the Bryant Park Hotel for an unforgettable Manhattan experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 200 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note, the reservation amount plus a USD 200 authorization per day on a valid credit card is required for incidentals.

Guests under the age of 21 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

Self parking is available at a location nearby and a special validated rate of USD $45 for Standard Size Vehicles and $55 for SUV per day applies. Contact the property for details.

Please note, the complimentary pastries and coffee ends at 9 AM.

HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
