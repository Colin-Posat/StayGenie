HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with AmenitiesThe Best Western PLUS Burnaby Hotel boasts modern rooms with a cable TV, refrigerator, coffee maker, and ironing facilities, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Convenient LocationSituated just a 10-minute walk from the Royal Oak Skytrain and less than 1 km from the renowned Metrotown mall, guests can easily explore the city and enjoy shopping without hassle.Business and Leisure FacilitiesWith free WiFi throughout the hotel, a hot breakfast served daily, a business center, and a complimentary newspaper, the Best Western PLUS Burnaby Hotel caters to the needs of both business and leisure travelers.Book your stay at the Best Western PLUS Burnaby Hotel now for a memorable and convenient experience!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for CAD 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
