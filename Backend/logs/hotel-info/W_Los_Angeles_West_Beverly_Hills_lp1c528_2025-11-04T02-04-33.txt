Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests mentioned mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some expressing satisfaction while others raised concerns.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service received positive feedback overall, with special mentions of exceptional front desk staff and customer focus.
• Location: 8.6/10 - Highly praised for its great location, proximity to key landmarks, and pleasant outdoor space.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms were described as somewhat dated, with issues like non-secured windows, average beds, and the need for updates.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities range from good pool service to pleasant lobby, although some services like food pricing and room updates were mentioned.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Several guests felt the hotel was overpriced considering the average rooms and some expensive services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Food options were generally well-received, particularly the lunch offerings and good service in the restaurant.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with notable mentions of the friendly staff and convenient check-in process.
