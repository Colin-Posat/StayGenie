HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Centrally Located in Paris:Experience the heart of Paris at Hôtel Brittany, just a short stroll from iconic landmarks like the Opera and Montmartre. Set in a charming Haussmann building, our hotel seamlessly blends modern comforts with classic Parisian design.Comfortable and Well-Equipped Rooms:Unwind in our air-conditioned rooms, each featuring a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, safe, and a private bathroom with deluxe amenities. Relax and rejuvenate in style.Convenient Amenities and Services:Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast and lounge in our elegant chesterfield sofas. With shops and restaurants nearby, our concierge is on hand to assist with any needs, from dining recommendations to arranging services like hairdressing or dry cleaning.Ready to immerse yourself in the charm of Paris?
