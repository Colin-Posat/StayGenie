HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThe Westin Austin Downtown offers a rooftop pool bar and an on-site restaurant, providing guests with a taste of the vibrant Austin scene right at their doorstep. Premium rooms boast stunning views of downtown Austin, ensuring a memorable stay.Culinary DelightsIndulge in American Southern cuisine and a Friday brunch at Stella San Jac, the hotel's restaurant, bar, and lounge. For a more relaxed atmosphere, head to Azul, the 20th-floor pool bar, offering light bites and handcrafted cocktails with breathtaking city views.Convenience and ComfortFrom a rooftop pool to a 24-hour front desk and business center, The Westin caters to all your needs. Additionally, guests can take advantage of the on-site mini-market, dry cleaning service, and laundry service for a stress-free stay.Experience the best of Austin at The Westin Austin Downtown. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
