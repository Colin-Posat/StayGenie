HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to The Scott Resort &amp; Spa, your luxurious retreat in the heart of Scottsdale.Unwind in StyleIndulge in our full-service spa featuring rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials. After a day of pampering, bask in one of our two outdoor swimming pools or hit the fitness center to maintain your active lifestyle.Culinary DelightsSavor authentic Cuban cuisine at The Canal Club, or enjoy a refreshing drink at the poolside bar. Our room service is available for a cozy night in, ensuring your dining experience is truly memorable.Exceptional AmenitiesWith over 14,500 square feet of event space, complimentary valet parking, and luxuriously equipped guestrooms featuring private balconies, your stay will be nothing short of extraordinary.Experience the ultimate getaway at The Scott Resort &amp; Spa. Book your stay today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the resort prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* Guests can arrange to bring pets by contacting the property directly, using the contact information on the booking confirmation (surcharges apply and can be found in the Fees section).
* A car is not required for transportation to and from this property.
* Contactless check-in and contactless check-out are available.
* This property welcomes guests of all sexual orientations and gender identities (LGBTQ+ friendly).

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Guests mentioned varying levels of cleanliness, with some highlighting the cleanliness as a positive aspect and others pointing out issues like limescale in the shower.
• Service: 7.2/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests appreciating the friendly staff and comfortable beds while others mentioned unfriendly staff and slow service.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location was praised for being in close proximity to key landmarks and having easy access to transportation, with several guests enjoying the trendy decor and vibe of the property.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally well-received for their comfort and design, although some guests experienced noise issues and maintenance issues like water leakage from the shower.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with some appreciating the pool area and bicycles available for use, while others noted the lack of heated pool and problems with the shower door.
• Value for Money: 5.4/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered and add-on charges, while others found the property unique and well-designed despite the high cost.
• Food and Beverage: 6.3/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed feedback, with some guests enjoying the breakfast and restaurant offerings, while others found the menu limited and portions too large.
• Overall Experience: 6.9/10 - The overall guest experience was varied, with some guests loving the unique decor and vibe, while others were disappointed with the value for the high price paid.
