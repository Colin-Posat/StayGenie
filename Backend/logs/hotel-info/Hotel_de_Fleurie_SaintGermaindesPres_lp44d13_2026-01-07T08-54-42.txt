HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with a ViewThe Hôtel de Fleurie, nestled in the heart of Saint-Germain-des-Prés, offers a blend of 18th-century charm and modern comfort. The air-conditioned rooms, adorned in soothing pastel hues, boast private balconies with picturesque views, perfect for a relaxing stay.French Elegance and Culinary DelightsIndulge in a continental breakfast of delectable French pastries and steaming coffee served in a charming breakfast room with century-old arches. Unwind in the wood-paneled bar, enjoying aperitifs in the cozy ambiance of classic armchairs.Convenient Transport Services and Prime LocationBenefit from the hotel's transport arrangements to and from Paris airports, ensuring a hassle-free journey. With Odéon Metro Station just a stone's throw away, explore the iconic landmarks and vibrant streets of the capital with ease.Ready to experience Parisian elegance at its finest?
