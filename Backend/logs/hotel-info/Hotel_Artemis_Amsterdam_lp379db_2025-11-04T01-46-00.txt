HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms and AmenitiesThe Dutch Design Hotel Artemis offers stylish and modern rooms designed with natural materials and Dutch art. Each room features a desk chair by a Dutch designer and a selection of video-on-demand movies. Stay connected with free WiFi during your stay.Gym and Waterside TerraceEnjoy the convenience of a gym to stay active and a relaxing waterside terrace for warm days. Whether you want to work out or unwind outdoors, Hotel Artemis Amsterdam has you covered.Business-Friendly FeaturesWith 11 multifunctional meeting areas, business guests will find everything they need for successful meetings. Benefit from modern facilities and a convenient location just a short drive from Amsterdam's city centre.Book your stay at Dutch Design Hotel Artemis for a blend of modern comfort and Dutch design inspiration.
