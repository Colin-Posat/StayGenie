HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views and ConvenienceExperience breathtaking city and mountain views at Hilton Garden Inn Cusco, located in the heart of the historic city centre. Just steps away from Cusco's main square, indulge in the on-site restaurant, room service, and a 24-hour convenience store for all your needs.Modern Amenities and ComfortRelax in rooms filled with natural light and equipped with top-notch amenities including Neutrogena toiletries, a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a safety deposit box. Some rooms even boast a private terrace for a tranquil courtyard experience.Exceptional Service and LocationWith a 24-hour front desk and a gift shop, our attentive staff ensures your stay is seamless. Explore nearby attractions like La Merced Church and enjoy easy access to Alejandro Velasco Astete International Airport. Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hilton Garden Inn Cusco.

Based on local tax laws, Peruvian citizens (and foreigners staying more than 59 days in Peru) must pay an additional fee of 18% .To be exempt from this 18% additional fee (IGV), a copy of the immigration card and passport must be presented.

Please note both documents are required for fee exemption. Guests who are not able to present both documents will be required to pay the fee.

Foreign business travelers who require a printed invoice, will also be charged the additional 18% regardless of the length of their stay in Peru. This fee is not automatically calculated in the total costs for the reservation.
