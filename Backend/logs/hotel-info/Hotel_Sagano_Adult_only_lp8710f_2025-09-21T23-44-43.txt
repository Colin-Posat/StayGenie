HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Modern AmenitiesHotel Sagano (Adult only) in Kyoto offers air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen TVs and free WiFi. The private bathrooms are equipped with bidets and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests. Some rooms even boast stunning city views, adding a touch of luxury to your experience.Convenient Services and FacilitiesEnjoy the convenience of room service, a 24-hour front desk, and free private parking at this 2-star love hotel. The shuttle service provided allows guests to easily explore the surrounding area. Additionally, take advantage of the ironing service and business facilities offered, including fax and photocopying services.Explore Kyoto's AttractionsLocated in the Ukyo Ward district, Hotel Sagano is the perfect base for exploring Kyoto. Guests can indulge in activities like hiking and visit nearby attractions such as the Arashiama Bamboo Grove and Otagi Nenbutsu-ji Temple. Book your stay now for a memorable experience in Kyoto!
