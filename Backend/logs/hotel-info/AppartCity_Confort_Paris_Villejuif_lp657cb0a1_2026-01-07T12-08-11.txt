HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Free WiFiAppart’City Confort Paris Villejuif provides a range of modern accommodations with free WiFi access and flat-screen TVs. From standard studios to 2-room suites, each room includes a well-equipped kitchenette for added convenience.Delicious Breakfast OptionsStart your day with a delicious breakfast at the residence, featuring a variety of breads, including gluten-free options, AOC Poitou-Charentes products, fresh fruits, cheeses, organic yogurt, and a wide selection of organic teas.Convenient Location and 24-Hour ConciergeLocated just outside Paris with easy access to Métro line 7, this hotel is only 15 minutes from the city center.
