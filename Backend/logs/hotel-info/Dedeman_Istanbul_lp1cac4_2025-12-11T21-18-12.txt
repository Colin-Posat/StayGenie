HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Panoramic Views and Luxurious FacilitiesLocated just an 8-minute walk from Gayrettepe Metro Station and Zorlu Center, Istanbul Dedeman offers a stunning roof bar with panoramic views of Istanbul, a semi Olympic-sized indoor pool, and top-notch spa facilities.Modern Rooms and Wellness AmenitiesThe hotel features modern rooms and suites adorned in warm colors, equipped with satellite TVs, complimentary water, tea/coffee facilities, minibars, and cozy bathrobes and slippers. Guests can also enjoy free Wi-Fi throughout the property and indulge in the Life Style Health Club's hot tub, gym, and various massage services.Culinary Delights and Convenient LocationTurkuaz Restaurant caters a variety of international and Turkish dishes, served buffet-style or à la carte. Conveniently situated near major shopping centers and business districts, Dedeman provides easy access to popular Bosphorus districts and Istanbul Airport just 50 km away. Ready to experience luxury and convenience? Book your stay now at Istanbul Dedeman!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 9 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 7 years old and over stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
