HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortHotel Tirreno, located in the heart of Rome, offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern comfort. Set in a historic building once inhabited by the famous Italian painter Il Domenichino, the hotel has been completely renovated to provide air-conditioned rooms with all essential amenities.Garden Oasis and Delicious BreakfastGuests can relax in the peaceful garden or enjoy a continental breakfast each morning, featuring delectable pastries and authentic Italian coffee. The on-site bar offers a variety of drinks that can be savored in the charming garden setting.Convenient Location and Excellent ServicesBenefiting from a central location, Hotel Tirreno provides easy access to great restaurants and popular attractions such as the Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica, Coliseum, and Roman Forum. With excellent metro links nearby, exploring the city is convenient for guests. Book now to experience the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and hospitality at Hotel Tirreno.
