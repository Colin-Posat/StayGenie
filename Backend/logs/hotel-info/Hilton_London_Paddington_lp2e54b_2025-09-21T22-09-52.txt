HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Art Deco-style RoomsExperience elegance and comfort in our Art Deco-style rooms at the 4-star Hilton London Paddington. Each room features modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, a minibar, and luxurious bathrooms. The large windows provide ample natural light, creating a welcoming ambiance for your stay.Convenient LocationConnected to Paddington Station by a footbridge, our hotel offers easy access to transportation. Explore Hyde Park and Kensington Palace within a 10-minute walk. Additionally, guests are just a short 15-minute journey from Heathrow Airport via the Heathrow Express train service, making travel hassle-free.Dining and Relaxation OptionsIndulge in culinary delights at our stylish brasserie restaurant or unwind at the 146 Paddington bar. Enjoy 24-hour room service for added convenience. For a peaceful retreat, the Executive Lounge offers a serene space for relaxation or work.Book your stay at Hilton London Paddington for a luxurious and convenient experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and charges are applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for £30 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for £30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests found the rooms spacious and comfortable, but some noted issues with room amenities and maintenance.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests enjoying the gym and pool facilities, while others mentioned the lack of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Opinions were divided on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others found it acceptable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Most guests were satisfied with the breakfast and restaurant offerings, although a few mentioned limited variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
