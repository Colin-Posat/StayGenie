HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated just a 3-minute walk from Ariake Monorail Station, Ariake Washington Hotel is easily accessible and perfect for exploring Tokyo.Modern AccommodationsThe air-conditioned rooms feature contemporary decor, private bathrooms, and free WiFi.
