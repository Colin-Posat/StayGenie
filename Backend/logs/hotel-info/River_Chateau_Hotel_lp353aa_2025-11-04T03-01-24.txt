HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationIndulge in the elegance of the River Chateau Hotel, offering sophisticated rooms adorned with luxurious fabrics and exquisite marble details. Each room is equipped with modern amenities including a minibar, free wired internet, and a flat-screen TV for your entertainment needs.Convenient LocationSituated a stone's throw away from the picturesque Tiber River and a short walk from the iconic Ponte Milvio bridge, the hotel provides easy access to Rome's popular attractions. Whether you're attending a match at the Olympic Stadium or exploring Saint Peter's Square, the hotel's prime location ensures you're never far from the action.Exceptional Dining ExperienceStart your day right with a delectable buffet breakfast served in the charming dining hall. Unwind with a refreshing drink at the hotel's bar, adding a touch of sophistication to your stay.Experience luxury and convenience at River Chateau Hotel - book your stay now for an unforgettable Roman getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 25 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
