HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and FeaturesThe Darling at The Star is a haven of luxury and entertainment, boasting a casino, day spa, and an impressive selection of 20 bars and restaurants. Guests can indulge in modern rooms with stunning city skyline views or relax in the Turkish bath and scented outdoor pool.Exceptional Room FeaturesEach room at The Darling Sydney is thoughtfully designed with a flat-screen TV, iPod docking station, and luxurious amenities like Egyptian cotton sheets and bathrobes. Whether overlooking the city or surrounding suburbs, guests are sure to enjoy a comfortable and stylish stay.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated just a short distance from Cockle Bay, The Darling offers easy access to Sydney's iconic landmarks such as the Opera House and The Rocks historic district. With the Sydney International Convention Centre within walking distance, guests can explore the city with ease.Experience luxury and convenience at The Darling at The Star - book your stay now for an unforgettable experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform The Darling at The Star in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that there is a 0.8% charge when you pay with a credit card.

Please note that the property requires a $100 per night credit card pre-authorisation at time of check-in to cover any incidental charges or damage to the property.

From Sunday 11th September 2022 until late March, there will be periods of elevated noise during the hours of 9:00 -17:00 daily as we work through the refurbishment of some of The Darling guestrooms. During this time, we will do our best to minimise any inconveniences this may cause.

The accommodation prices shown online are for bookings of up to 9 rooms only, regardless of room type. Room rates and booking terms may be different for group bookings depending on the number of rooms booked and will be cancelled should it exceed the maximum number bookable online. To make a booking of 10 rooms or more please email us directly: Sales@star.com.au

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for AUD 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
