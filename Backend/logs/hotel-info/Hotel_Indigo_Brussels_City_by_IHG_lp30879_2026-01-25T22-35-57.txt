HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Boutique Guest RoomsHotel Indigo Brussels City, located in the heart of Brussels, offers boutique guest rooms inspired by the nearby Botanical Garden. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a mini fridge, a Nespresso coffee machine, and complimentary WiFi for a comfortable stay.Fitness and Health CentreGuests at Hotel Indigo Brussels City have access to a fully equipped fitness and health centre, perfect for staying active during their stay. Whether you prefer a morning workout or an evening exercise session, the facilities cater to all your fitness needs.Serra Restaurants and Indoor GardensIndulge in a unique dining experience at the serra restaurants within indoor gardens at the hotel. Enjoy live events while surrounded by lush greenery, adding a touch of nature to your culinary adventures.Book your stay at Hotel Indigo Brussels City for a memorable experience in the vibrant city of Brussels.
