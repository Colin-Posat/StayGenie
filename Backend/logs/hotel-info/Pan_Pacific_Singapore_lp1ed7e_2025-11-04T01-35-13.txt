HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 5-Star ExperienceThe Pan Pacific Singapore offers a luxurious 5-star experience directly connected to Marina Square Mall and Suntec Convention Centre. Guests can enjoy an array of amenities including an outdoor swimming pool, 8 dining options, and a bar. Free WiFi is available throughout the hotel.Breathtaking Views and RelaxationThe air-conditioned rooms feature full-length windows with panoramic city views, flat-screen TVs, and work desks. The property also boasts a wading pool with cabanas, 2 tennis courts, a fitness centre, and the award-winning St. Gregory Spa for ultimate relaxation.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentIndulge in a variety of dining options from the Edge International Buffet to the artisanal Pacific Emporium. Unwind at the PLUME cocktail bar or enjoy Mediterranean-inspired delights at the Poolside Restaurant &amp; Bar.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Pan Pacific Singapore!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Pan Pacific Singapore in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
The name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property. The total payable price may be increased by 1% due to the GST increase in 2024.

Please note that the swimming pool will be closed until further notice due to emergency maintenance. Guests may utilise the swimming pool at our sister property, PARKROYAL COLLECTION Marina Bay, Singapore.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
