HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location Near Iconic LandmarksThis Paris hotel is located just a 2-minute walk from the Eiffel Tower, near Trocadéro and the River Seine. The Mercure Paris Centre Tour Eiffel is also conveniently close to the Parc des Princes Stadium, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and sports enthusiasts.Luxurious Amenities and Dining OptionsThe hotel offers a fitness centre for guests to stay active, a bar to unwind after a day of exploring, and air-conditioned guest rooms with satellite TV and private bathrooms for a comfortable stay. Indulge in a daily American or buffet breakfast on the terrace and savor traditional French cuisine at Le Jardin restaurant, accompanied by a selection of fine wines.Easy Access to Major AttractionsWith Bir Hakeim Metro station just 2 minutes away, guests can effortlessly explore iconic Parisian sites like Avenue Champs-Elysées, the Arc de Triomphe, and the Branly Museum during their stay at Mercure Paris Centre Tour Eiffel.Book now to experience luxury and convenience in the heart of Paris!

• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but complaints about hard beds, old bathrooms, and maintenance issues were noted.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - While some guests appreciated the amenities offered, others mentioned the lack of in-room facilities and old elevators.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and food services received positive feedback, although variety and in-room dining services were areas of improvement.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with location and friendly staff being highlighted as positives.
