CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 1 and older are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 1 year old to 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 1 year old and over stay for US$50 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas, with only a few minor mentions of maintenance issues.
• Service: 8/10 - Overall, guests found the staff to be friendly and helpful, although there were occasional complaints about slow service.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's central location near key landmarks and public transport options was a standout feature for many guests.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Guests praised the comfort and spaciousness of the rooms, with a minor suggestion for additional amenities like a lobby bar.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Reviews were mixed regarding amenities, with some guests appreciating the gym and pool but others noting the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
