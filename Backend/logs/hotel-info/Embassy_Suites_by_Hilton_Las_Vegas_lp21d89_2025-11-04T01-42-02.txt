HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated across from the University of Nevada Las Vegas and just minutes away from the vibrant Las Vegas Strip, the Embassy Suites Las Vegas offers the perfect blend of proximity and tranquility.Spacious Suites and AmenitiesEach suite at the hotel is thoughtfully equipped with 2 flat-screen TVs, a microwave, and a refrigerator. The separate living area provides ample space for relaxation, complete with a large work desk. Guests can also enjoy the outdoor pool, full-service business center, and on-site gym.Delightful Dining OptionsStart your day with a delicious cooked-to-order breakfast and unwind in the evening with complimentary snacks at the Three Palms Café. The hotel also offers an evening reception for guests to mingle and enjoy continental cuisine.Book your stay at Embassy Suites Las Vegas for a memorable and comfortable experience!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 6.4/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although some guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 7.8/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 6/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 5.6/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 6.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
