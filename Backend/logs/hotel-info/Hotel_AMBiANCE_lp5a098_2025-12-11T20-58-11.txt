HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious RenovationCompletely renovated in June 2014, Hotel Ambiance Superior in Zermatt offers free WiFi. Zermatt Train Station is a 10-minute walk away, and the Sunnegga Express funicular is only 600 metres away.Elegant Rooms with ViewsAll rooms have an en-suite bathroom and a cable TV. Most of them also feature a balcony. Some rooms offer unobstructed views of the Matterhorn.Convenient LocationNestled just a short stroll from Zermatt Train Station and Sunnegga Express funicular, Hotel Ambiance Superior offers a perfect blend of comfort and accessibility.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hotel Ambiance Superior in picturesque Zermatt!
