HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in Historic SultanahmetSpectacular Rooftop Views: Enjoy breathtaking panoramas of the Blue Mosque and the Sea of Marmara from our rooftop terrace.Culinary Delights: Indulge in Turkish specialties and international cuisine at our Terrace Restaurant and 70’s Pub, offering a nostalgic ambiance with 70’s music.Relaxation and Convenience: Unwind in our spa facilities, learn to cook traditional Turkish dishes, and take advantage of services like 24-hour reception and tour desk.Immerse yourself in the charm of Istanbul's historic district at Sarnıç Premier Hotel. Book now to experience luxury and comfort in the heart of Sultanahmet.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 5 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 6.7/10 - Limited facilities and issues with provided items like toilet paper.
• Value for Money: 7.7/10 - A mixed experience with high expectations not met, particularly in relation to cost.
• Food and Beverage: 6.2/10 - Breakfast options were limited and the quality was disappointing.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - While there were several issues, the positive aspects, like location and service, create a balanced experience.
