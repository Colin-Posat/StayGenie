HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience luxury and comfort at the Hilton Chicago O'Hare Airport, where convenience meets elegance.Recreational Facilities: Dive into relaxation with our 24-hour health club and invigorating steam room. Take time for yourself and indulge in a world of wellness right at your doorstep.Culinary Delights: Savor the best of American cuisine at our on-site restaurant, Andiamo, or unwind with room service for a cozy night in. Our coffee shop offers delightful snacks to keep you satisfied throughout your stay.Business &amp; Event Spaces: Whether you’re working or hosting an event, our comprehensive business center and meeting rooms are designed for your success. Enjoy express check-in and check-out for seamless planning.Don’t miss out on the perfect getaway—book your stay today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Up to 2 children 18 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* A car is not required for transportation to and from this property.
* Contactless check-in and contactless check-out are available.
* This property welcomes guests of all sexual orientations and gender identities (LGBTQ+ friendly).
