HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Prince Gallery Tokyo Kioicho offers a sophisticated stay just minutes away from Akasaka-Mitsuke Station. Each room is elegantly furnished with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, minibar, and luxurious en suite bathroom, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing experience for every guest.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's sake bar, sushi restaurant, and teppanyaki restaurant. For more dining options, explore the variety of restaurants just a short walk away. Whether you're craving traditional Japanese cuisine or international flavors, there's something to satisfy every palate.Wellness and ConvenienceUnwind at the spa and sauna, stay active at the fitness centre, or take advantage of the 24-hour front desk and currency exchange services.
