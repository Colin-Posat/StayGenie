HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Grand Star hotel, located on Taksim Square, offers spacious rooms with modern amenities like free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and stunning sea views. Each room includes a built-in closet for convenience.Dining with a ViewIndulge in regional delicacies at Dion Bar and Restaurant while enjoying panoramic views of the Bosphorus. From lamb stews to local spirits, the restaurant offers a culinary experience like no other.Convenient LocationSituated just a stone's throw away from Taksim Metro Station, the hotel provides easy access to Istanbul's popular attractions. Whether you're visiting the Blue Mosque or heading to the airport, Grand Star Hotel's location makes travel a breeze.Experience luxury, fine dining, and convenience at Grand Star Hotel. Book your stay now to elevate your Istanbul experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests mentioned the cleanliness positively, but some minor maintenance issues were noted.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with some guests highlighting helpful staff, but also complaints about staff rudeness and unhelpfulness.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and beautiful views were well-received, although some guests had complaints about noise and specific room views.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - While rooms were generally comfortable with some great views, there were complaints about maintenance issues like broken shower doors and non-working air conditioning.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were generally satisfactory, but some guests highlighted the lack of variety in breakfast options and poor quality of sound insulation.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some feeling it was worth it for the location while others found it expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6.3/10 - Breakfast and food quality received mixed reviews, with some finding it boring and low quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, though there were complaints about noisy corridors and inconsistent service quality.
