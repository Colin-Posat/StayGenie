HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Major AttractionsLocated just 200 metres from Barcelona’s Monumental Bull Ring and a short distance from iconic landmarks like Sagrada Familia, Hotel Glòries offers a prime location for sightseeing and exploring the city.Convenient Transportation OptionsWith Monumental Metro Station a mere 3 minutes’ walk away, guests can easily access Plaza Catalunya in just 5 minutes. This makes traveling around the city a breeze for our guests.Excellent Services and AmenitiesHotel Glòries boasts a 24-hour front desk that provides services like car and bike hire, currency exchange, and ticket reservations, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay for all guests.Book your stay at Hotel Glòries now to experience the perfect blend of convenience and comfort during your Barcelona visit.
