HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel Emion Kyoto, ideally located in Kyoto, offers a delightful buffet breakfast and complimentary property-wide WiFi. The rooms feature modern amenities including air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and free toiletries. Some rooms even boast scenic mountain views for a truly relaxing stay.Relaxation and DiningIndulge in the various facilities Hotel Emion Kyoto has to offer, including a restaurant, terrace, sauna, and hot tub. Unwind in the serene atmosphere of the non-smoking property and make the most of your stay with these luxurious amenities.Convenient Location and Exceptional ServiceConveniently set just 1.7 km from Kyoto Station, this hotel provides easy access to nearby attractions. The multilingual staff, available 24/7, ensures a seamless experience for guests. Book your stay at Hotel Emion Kyoto for a perfect blend of comfort and convenience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
