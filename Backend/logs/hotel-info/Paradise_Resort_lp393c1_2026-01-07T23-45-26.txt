HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Spa Services and Outdoor ActivitiesPamper yourself with a visit to the spa for massages and facials. Enjoy the seasonal outdoor pool and rent bicycles for leisure activities.Delectable Dining Options and EntertainmentSavor Greek cuisine at Paradise restaurant with a view. Unwind with a drink at the bar/lounge or poolside bar. Explore the arcade/game room for entertainment.Comfortable Accommodations and Convenient AmenitiesRelax in one of the 36 guestrooms with private balconies. Take advantage of complimentary Wi-Fi, room service, and buffet breakfast. Featured amenities include limo service and airport shuttle options.Immerse yourself in luxury and relaxation at Paradise Resort, your ideal getaway in Santorini.
