HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient AmenitiesThis Days Inn offers a range of convenient amenities such as free WiFi, flat-screen cable TV, and a small refrigerator in all rooms. Guests can also enjoy a coffee maker, work desk, and ironing facilities for their comfort.Entertainment and RecreationGuests can unwind and have fun at the outdoor pool and games room with billiards. For those looking to stay active, a fitness centre is available on-site. Additionally, concierge services are provided to cater to the guests' needs.Prime LocationLocated just 7 minutes' walk from the Miami Airport Convention Center, this hotel is also in close proximity to Miami city centre and the Miami Seaquarium. With easy access to key attractions, Days Inn by Wyndham Miami International Airport is the perfect choice for your stay.
