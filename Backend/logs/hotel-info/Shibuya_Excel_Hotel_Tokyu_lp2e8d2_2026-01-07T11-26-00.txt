HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location at Shibuya Train StationDirectly connected to Shibuya Train Station, Shibuya Excel Hotel Tokyu offers unparalleled convenience for travelers seeking easy transportation links. Overlooking the iconic Shibuya Scramble intersection, guests are just steps away from popular landmarks like the Shibuya 109 Building and a short walk to Harajuku and Yoyogi Park.Comfortable and Stylish AccommodationsRelax in the calm and sophisticated rooms at Hotel Shibuya Excel, equipped with modern amenities like free Wi-Fi, satellite flat-screen TV, and a minibar. Enjoy city views while sipping tea in your cozy room, designed for a comfortable stay.Fine Dining and Relaxation OptionsIndulge in exquisite dining at Shunsai Japanese Restaurant or French Restaurant A Bientot, both offering breathtaking views of Tokyo from the 25th floor. For a more casual experience, Estacion Cafe serves delightful coffee and cake in a bright and spacious setting.Ready to experience the best of Tokyo? Book your stay at Shibuya Excel Hotel Tokyu now!
