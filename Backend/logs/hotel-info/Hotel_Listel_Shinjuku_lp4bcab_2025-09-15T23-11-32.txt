HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Cosy Rooms with Modern AmenitiesHotel Listel Shinjuku offers cosy rooms equipped with a fridge, electric kettle, and free internet. The en suite bathrooms and video-on-demand movies ensure a comfortable stay.Convenient LocationSituated just a 10-minute walk from Shinjuku Sanchome Subway Station, the hotel is also close to Shinjuku Train Station, Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden, and Kabukicho area, making it a convenient choice for travelers looking to explore the city.Dining and Relaxation OptionsIndulge in a culinary experience at Salon et Restaurant Femmenette, offering a mix of local and international cuisine. Unwind at the bar or opt for room service for a cozy night in.Book your stay at Hotel Listel Shinjuku for a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and culinary delights.
