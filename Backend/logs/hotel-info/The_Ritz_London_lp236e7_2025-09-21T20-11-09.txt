IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
•	Dress Code: Gentlemen are required to wear a jacket and tie (jeans and sportswear are not permitted for either ladies or gentlemen) for Afternoon Tea and lunch and dinner in The Ritz Restaurant and The Ritz Room. In all other areas of the hotel (The Ritz Restaurant breakfast service, The Rivoli Bar, The Long Gallery, and the Cigar Lounge), smart casual attire is required. Please note that shorts, ripped jeans, trainers and sportswear are not permitted in any of the hotel’s restaurants or bars.
•	Please be aware that The Ritz London requires guests to provide photographic identification during check-in. Non-UK residents are requested to provide a valid passport, while UK residents can present either a valid passport or a full UK driving licence.
•	Please note that an additional bed/sofa bed can be provided in the Deluxe King Room, Junior Suite, and Deluxe Suite for a charge of GBP 115 for a guest aged 16 years and older.
•	The Ritz prides itself on delivering the high service standards. Therefore, a discretionary accommodation service charge of 5% will be added to your bill.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  GBP 65 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 14 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 16 years old and over stay for £115 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
