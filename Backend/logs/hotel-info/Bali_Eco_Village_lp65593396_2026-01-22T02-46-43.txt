HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spectacular Views and Tranquil SettingNestled in the mountainous area of Belok/Sidan, Bali Eco Village offers breathtaking views of the surrounding forest and valleys, providing a peaceful retreat for guests.Comfort and ConvenienceFeaturing traditional Balinese-inspired rooms with modern amenities, including private bathrooms and complimentary WiFi, the hotel ensures a comfortable stay. Services like spa treatments, luggage storage, and tour assistance add to the convenience.Culinary Delights and Leisure ActivitiesGuests can indulge in Mediterranean and Asian cuisine at Bali Eco Restaurant and Bar, known for its fresh organic dishes. For leisure, activities like hiking, cycling, and pool games are available, promising a delightful stay.Experience serenity and luxury at Bali Eco Village – book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 200,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
