HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Complimentary BreakfastHomewood Suites by Hilton Miami Downtown/Brickell offers spacious suites with separate living and sleeping areas, fully-equipped kitchenettes, and two flat-screen TVs. Enjoy a complimentary breakfast to start your day right.Convenient Amenities and ServicesIndulge in a free evening reception with beer, wine, and light dinner fare, and take advantage of the 24-hour on-site mini-market. Stay connected with free WiFi, and make use of valet parking for added convenience.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by the 2nd-floor pool overlooking Miami's city centre, or stay active at the 24-hour fitness and business centres. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.3/10 - Guests expressed mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some reporting issues like stained pillow cushions and dirty sofas.
• Service: 6.9/10 - Service experiences varied, with some guests mentioning long wait times and unavailability of maintenance, while others appreciated the helpfulness of staff.
• Location: 7.8/10 - The hotel's location received positive feedback for its proximity to key landmarks and availability of nearby amenities, although some guests mentioned the inconvenience of discovering free services late into their stay.
• Room Quality: 8/10 - Rooms were generally described as spacious and comfortable, with minor maintenance issues reported by some guests.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Amenities were satisfactory overall, with highlights on the gym and pool, but some guests noted the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.2/10 - Guests had mixed feelings about the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location and amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Feedback on breakfast and restaurant services was generally positive, with minor suggestions for more variety in offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
