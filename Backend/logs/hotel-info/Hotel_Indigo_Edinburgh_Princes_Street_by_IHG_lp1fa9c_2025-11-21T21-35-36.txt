HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel Indigo - Edinburgh - Princes Street offers elegantly designed rooms with high ceilings and original windows, providing a charming ambiance. Enjoy stunning views of Edinburgh Castle from select rooms, along with modern amenities like free WiFi and tea/coffee facilities.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a culinary journey at Twenty Princes Street restaurant, showcasing Scotland's finest ingredients in delectable dishes. Pair your meal with a wide selection of drinks and cocktails at the Juniper bar for a complete dining experience.Prime LocationConveniently located in the heart of Edinburgh, our hotel is opposite Waverley Railway Station and within walking distance of major attractions like Holyrood Palace and St James Shopping Centre. Immerse yourself in the rich history and vibrant culture of the city during your stay.Experience luxury, gastronomy, and convenience at Hotel Indigo - Edinburgh - Princes Street. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
