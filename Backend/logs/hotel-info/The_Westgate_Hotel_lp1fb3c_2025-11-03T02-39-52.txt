HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spectacular Views and Luxurious AmenitiesLocated just 3 blocks from the 5th Avenue Light Rail Station, The Westgate Hotel offers stunning views of downtown San Diego. The hotel features a rooftop outdoor pool, a full-service spa, a gym, and a restaurant.Fine Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in Continental-California cuisine at The Westgate Room, or enjoy Afternoon Tea with live harp music on select days. Live piano entertainment awaits you at the Plaza Bar for a relaxing evening experience.Convenience and ComfortGuests can enjoy hand-crafted European furniture, a 49-inch LED TV, and free WiFi in all rooms. The hotel offers a business center, laundry services, ticket assistance, and unique amenities like an outdoor heated lap pool, hot tub, and a running track.Book your stay at The Westgate Hotel for a luxurious and unforgettable experience.
