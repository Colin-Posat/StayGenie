HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Homely Apartments with Free Wi-FiThe European Place, located just a 3-minute walk from Dhurakij Pundit University, offers comfortable apartments with free Wi-Fi and private balconies.Modern Amenities and Convenient LocationThese spacious apartments are fully air-conditioned with cable TV, fridge, and hot-water showers. Situated close to Kasetsart University and Impact Convention Centre, the hotel is also within a short drive to Central Plaza Chaengwattana Shopping Mall and Don Muang Airport.Fitness Centre and Business ServicesGuests can enjoy the convenience of a fitness centre on-site and avail of fax and photocopying services. With a golf club just a 5-minute walk away and free parking available, The European Place ensures a pleasant stay for both leisure and business travelers.Book now for a delightful experience at The European Place!
