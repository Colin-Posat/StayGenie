HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Hotel in Exclusive Rome LocationExquisite Dining Options: Indulge in a culinary journey with 3 diverse restaurants and a stylish piano bar offering discounts for guests. The exclusive rooftop restaurant, Hi-Res, provides breathtaking views of Rome while the wine cellar boasts an impressive collection of over 400 labels.Elegant Accommodations: Experience classic decor in rooms equipped with modern amenities like free WiFi and flat-screen TVs. Select rooms feature charming wood-beamed ceilings or luxurious marble bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay.Convenient Facilities: Stay active at the fitness centre or unwind on the rooftop terrace with panoramic city views. Located a short walk from Flaminio Metro Station and Villa Borghese gardens, the Valadier offers a prime location for exploring Rome.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at The Valadier!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 40 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for € 75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
