HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Haussman Building and Prime LocationSituated in an elegant Haussman building, Millennium Hotel is a luxurious retreat just steps away from Opéra Garnier and Galeries Lafayette. The perfect blend of sophistication and convenience.Charming French Brasserie and Elegant AccommodationIndulge in classic French cuisine at the hotel's glass-domed brasserie or savor a drink in the cozy La Bibliothéque lounge bar. The air-conditioned guest rooms feature exquisite period decor and modern amenities for a comfortable stay.Convenient Access to Paris AttractionsExplore Paris with ease as the hotel is within walking distance of the Musée du Louvre and well-connected to other attractions via the nearby Richelieu-Drouet Metro station. Your Parisian adventure awaits at Millennium Hotel Paris Opera.
