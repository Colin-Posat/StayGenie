HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsJust steps away from the Magnificent Mile, Viceroy Chicago offers modern guest rooms with flat-screen TVs, plush seating, and complimentary WiFi. The fully-stocked minibar and coffee machine ensure a comfortable stay.Culinary DelightsIndulge in dishes inspired by Midwest flavors at Somerset, the on-site restaurant. Enjoy crafted cocktails and small plates at the rooftop bar with stunning skyline views. In-room dining services are also available for convenience.Top-Notch AmenitiesRelax at the rooftop pool and bar with panoramic city views, or stay active at the state-of-the-art fitness center. With 14012 square feet of event space, the hotel is perfect for meetings and gatherings.Book your stay at Viceroy Chicago for a memorable experience in the heart of the Gold Coast neighborhood.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 91 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests consistently mentioned the cleanliness of the hotel as a positive aspect, with only a few minor complaints.
• Service: 8.7/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests, with warm and welcoming staff mentioned frequently. Some guests highlighted exceptional individual service experiences.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and attractions received glowing reviews, with many guests emphasizing its convenience and accessibility.
• Room Quality: 8/10 - Guests found the rooms spacious, stylish, and comfortable overall, although some minor maintenance issues were mentioned by a few.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Satisfactory amenities provided, with some guests praising the rooftop pool while others expressed disappointment over the lack of certain spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the prime location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, though some guests wished for more variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel and expressed a high likelihood of recommending it to others.
