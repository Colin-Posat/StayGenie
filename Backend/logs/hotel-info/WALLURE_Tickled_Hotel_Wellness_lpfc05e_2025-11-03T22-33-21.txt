HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 4-Star AccommodationLocated in Olbia, WALLURE - Tickled Hotel &amp; Wellness offers a luxurious 4-star experience just a short distance from Isola di Tavolara. Guests can enjoy the convenience of room service and unwind on the terrace, making their stay truly relaxing.Modern Amenities and ComfortEach room at the hotel is equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and free WiFi. Guests can indulge in a delicious Italian breakfast every morning and take in the city views from selected rooms. The private bathrooms feature a shower, complimentary toiletries, and cozy bathrobes.Convenient Location and ServicesSituated close to Olbia Harbour and the city centre, WALLURE - Tickled Hotel &amp; Wellness offers easy access to nearby attractions like San Simplicio Church and the Archeological Museum of Olbia. With a paid airport shuttle service, guests can travel stress-free to and from Olbia Costa Smeralda Airport.Book your stay at WALLURE - Tickled Hotel &amp; Wellness for a memorable and luxurious experience.
