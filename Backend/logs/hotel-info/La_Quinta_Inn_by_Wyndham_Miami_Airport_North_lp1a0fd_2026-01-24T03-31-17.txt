HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Airport Transfer ServiceThe Miami Airport North La Quinta Inn provides a seamless transfer service to the nearby Miami International Airport, ensuring a stress-free start or end to your journey.Relaxing Outdoor Pool and Breakfast OptionsUnwind by taking a dip in the hotel's refreshing outdoor pool or kickstart your day with a convenient Grab and Go breakfast, perfect for guests on the move.Comfortable Rooms and Complimentary AmenitiesEach guest room at this Miami hotel boasts a flat-screen TV with cable channels and coffee-making facilities. With added perks like a launderette on site and complimentary parking, your stay is made even more enjoyable.Book now to experience the convenience and comfort of the Miami Airport North La Quinta Inn.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Miami International Airport Shuttle is upon guest request and operates from 6:00 am to 10:00 pm, departing on the hour.

Pool access is temporarily suspended.

For Breakfast option we are only providing a 'grab n go' bag.

Please note that we take a pre-authorization on day of arrival to guarantee the reservation as well as a line of credit during the stay. The pre-authorization is the first night fee (room and taxes).

When traveling with pets, please note that an extra charge of USD 25 per pet, per night applies. The maximum charge is USD 75 per stay and maximum 2 pets.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
