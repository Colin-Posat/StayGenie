HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover your urban oasis at Novotel Miami Brickell, where modern comfort meets unparalleled convenience.Recreational Amenities: Dive into our outdoor pool or break a sweat in the 24-hour fitness center. For those looking to explore the vibrant Miami area, bicycles are available for rent, ensuring you enjoy every moment of your stay.Dining Delights: Savor delightful cuisine at Uva Lobby Bar or unwind poolside with refreshing cocktails. Our room service offers convenience during limited hours, and a buffet breakfast awaits each morning to fuel your adventures.Business &amp; Event Facilities: With 5,000 square feet of versatile conference space and 8 meeting rooms, we provide the ideal setting for your next event. Our 24-hour business center ensures you stay productive while enjoying your visit.Book your unforgettable stay at Novotel Miami Brickell today and experience the best of Miami!
