HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant charm of Barcelona at Hotel Indigo Barcelona - Plaza Catalunya by IHG, where contemporary luxury meets unmatched convenience.Recreational Amenities: Dive into our inviting outdoor pool or break a sweat at our fully equipped fitness center. Feel energized as you unwind and soak up the sun.Dining and Drinks: Savor delectable meals in our on-site restaurant or with 24-hour room service. Our poolside bar and three additional lounges are perfect for enjoying craft cocktails and refreshing drinks.Convenience and Comfort: Stay connected with complimentary wired and wireless internet, and enjoy the comfort of our well-appointed guest rooms featuring minibars and espresso makers. Let our concierge services cater to your every need.Don’t miss the chance to indulge in a uniquely Barcelona experience—book your stay with us today!
