HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience a luxurious retreat at Molitor Hôtel &amp; Spa Paris – MGallery Collection, where comfort meets elegance in the heart of the city.Spa and Wellness ParadiseIndulge in ultimate relaxation at our full-service spa, offering a variety of massages, body treatments, and facials, complemented by indoor and outdoor pools for rejuvenation and leisure.Culinary DelightsSavor exquisite French cuisine at Brasserie Molitor. Enjoy delightful meals in the restaurant or order 24-hour room service. Don't forget to unwind with a refreshing drink at our vibrant bar or poolside lounge.Convenient ComfortsOur 124 air-conditioned rooms are equipped with espresso makers, complimentary minibar items, and high-speed internet. Enjoy effortless access to business services and stay connected while exploring the best of Paris.Escape to sophistication—book your escape today!
