GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.2/10 - Guests had mixed opinions about cleanliness, with some mentioning cleanliness as a positive aspect and others highlighting issues with old and smelly surroundings.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated with friendly and helpful staff mentioned by several guests.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks, beaches, and restaurants was widely praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were described as spacious, comfortable, and well-equipped with kitchenettes, although some guests noted dated decor and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities with guests appreciating free coffee, kitchen facilities, and laundry service but some mentioning the lack of breakfast and outdated facilities.
• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - Opinions varied on value for money, with some feeling the price was reasonable for the location while others found it too high considering the hotel's condition.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast and dining services received positive feedback overall, although some guests mentioned a lack of variety or the absence of certain options like spa services.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
