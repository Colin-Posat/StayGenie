HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Sisli DistrictThe 22 Hotel is ideally situated in the heart of Sisli district, just a short stroll away from the trendy Nisantasi district and Cevahir Shopping Centre. Guests can enjoy the convenience of exploring the vibrant surroundings with ease.Modern Rooms with City ViewsExperience comfort and style in our modern rooms equipped with essential amenities such as flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and minibars. Some rooms even boast a private terrace with stunning city views, providing a perfect setting for relaxation.Convenient Access to AttractionsWith Osmanbey Metro Station nearby, guests have direct access to Taksim Square and Istiklal Avenue, making sightseeing and exploring the city hassle-free. Additionally, Ataturk Airport is just a short distance away for added convenience.Book your stay at The 22 Hotel now and indulge in a memorable experience in Istanbul.
