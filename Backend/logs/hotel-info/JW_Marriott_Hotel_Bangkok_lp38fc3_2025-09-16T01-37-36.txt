HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for THB 1,765 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests praised the cleanliness of the rooms, although there were a few mentions of cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Staff were commended for being helpful and friendly, with a few guests noting instances of outstanding service.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and public transportation was a highlight for many guests.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Guests enjoyed spacious and comfortable rooms, but some mentioned issues with outdated decor.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - The hotel's amenities were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the gym and bar, but some guests desired more diverse offerings.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
