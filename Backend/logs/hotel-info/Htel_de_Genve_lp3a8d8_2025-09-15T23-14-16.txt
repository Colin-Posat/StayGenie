HOTEL DESCRIPTION:

Explore the city's iconic landmarks with convenience.Comfortable AccommodationRelax in one of the hotel's 30 fully-equipped rooms after a day of sightseeing. The cozy and comfortable rooms offer a perfect retreat in the bustling city.Helpful Multilingual StaffOur friendly staff, fluent in English, French, and Italian, are dedicated to ensuring you have a memorable stay.
