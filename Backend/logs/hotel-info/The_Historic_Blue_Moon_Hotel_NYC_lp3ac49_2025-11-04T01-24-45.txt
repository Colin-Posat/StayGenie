HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern Comfort:Step into a piece of history at The Historic Blue Moon Hotel - NYC, a charming establishment built in 1879. Each of the 22 guestrooms is individually decorated, featuring kitchen amenities and plush bedding for a cozy stay.Delectable Dining and Convenient Amenities:Indulge in Italian cuisine at Sweet Dreams Cafe or grab a quick bite at the coffee shop. The hotel offers a business center, express check-in, and express check-out for a seamless stay.Prime Location and Nearby Attractions:Located in Manhattan, explore nearby attractions like New York University and One World Trade Center. With easy access to airports, your adventures in the city that never sleeps await you at The Historic Blue Moon Hotel - NYC.
