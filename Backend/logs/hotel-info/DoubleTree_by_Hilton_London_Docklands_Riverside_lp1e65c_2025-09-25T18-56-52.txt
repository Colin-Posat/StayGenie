HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Wharf BuildingThe DoubleTree by Hilton London - Docklands Riverside is housed in a charming 17th-century Wharf Building, featuring exposed brickwork and London's last dry dock for shipbuilding. Enjoy a blend of history and modern comfort during your stay.Scenic Views and Luxury AmenitiesIndulge in the stunning views of Canary Wharf and the River Thames from your en suite room, equipped with a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and tea/coffee facilities. With most rooms offering city or river views, relaxation and luxury are guaranteed.Dining and TransportationExperience culinary delights at the Columbia Restaurant with seasonal produce and outdoor terrace barbecues. Take advantage of the complimentary river taxi to Canary Wharf or explore London via the River Thames fleet services. Conveniently located near transportation hubs, this hotel offers both luxury and accessibility.Book your stay at DoubleTree by Hilton London - Docklands Riverside for a memorable experience by the River Thames.
