HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsHotel Firenze, located next to Saint Mark's Square in Venice, offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern comforts. Dating back to the 16th century, the elegant property features rooms with classic furnishings, including Murano glass lamps. Some rooms even boast a private balcony for a touch of exclusivity.Rooftop Breakfast with a ViewStart your day right with breakfast served on the rooftop terrace, overlooking the tower bell. The perfect way to immerse yourself in the beauty of Venice while enjoying a delicious meal.Convenience at Your FingertipsWith free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a prime location near the S. Marco Vaporetto Water Bus Stop, Hotel Firenze ensures convenience during your stay. Plus, take advantage of the reception services to book concert tickets and guided tours effortlessly.Experience the perfect blend of history and modernity at Hotel Firenze. Book now to secure your stay in this enchanting Venice hotel.
