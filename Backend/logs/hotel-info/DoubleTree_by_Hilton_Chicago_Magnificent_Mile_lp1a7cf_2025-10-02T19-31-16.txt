HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Stay in Downtown ChicagoOutdoor Pool and Dining Experience: Enjoy a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool at this hotel located just minutes away from the famous Magnificent Mile. Indulge in delectable dishes at the on-site restaurant, HotHouse, offering a vibrant dining experience.Modern Amenities: Unwind in rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs, iPod docking stations, and coffeemakers. Pamper yourself with complimentary toiletries and enjoy entertainment on a 50-inch flat-screen TV.Fitness and Culinary Delights: Stay active at the well-equipped fitness center or savor a warm chocolate chip cookie upon arrival. Hot House restaurant offers a socializing space to relish breakfast and dinner.Experience the best of Chicago at DoubleTree by Hilton Chicago Magnificent Mile. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
