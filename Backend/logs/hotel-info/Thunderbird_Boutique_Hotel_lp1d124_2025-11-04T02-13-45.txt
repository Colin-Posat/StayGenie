HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Unique AmenitiesLocated conveniently on the Strip at Boulevard South, Thunderbird Boutique Hotel offers a memorable stay just blocks away from the Stratosphere Hotel and Casino. The hotel boasts a bar, restaurant, and even a wedding chapel onsite, providing guests with a one-of-a-kind experience.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsEach guest room is thoughtfully designed with a 43-inch LED TV, a coffee machine, ironing facilities, and a spacious private bathroom. The air-conditioned rooms with openable windows ensure a comfortable and relaxing stay for every guest.Vibrant Entertainment and Dining OptionsGuests can unwind at the Thunderbird Lounge with live music, comedy shows, and cocktail karaoke. The nightly happy hour with drink specials adds to the lively atmosphere, making it a perfect spot to socialize and have a great time.Experience the excitement and comfort Thunderbird Boutique Hotel has to offer – book your stay now for an unforgettable Las Vegas getaway!

In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), wearing a face mask is mandatory in all indoor common areas.
A damage deposit of USD 150 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Upon check-in, presentation of photo identification and credit card are required. The hotel will authorize room and tax up-front on the card provided.

Smoking marijuana is not allowed in hotel rooms, lounge, or public spaces.

In addition the hotel requires a USD 150 credit card pre-authorization upon check-in to cover any incidental charges per room.

A nearby road construction by City of Las Vegas is taking place during this time, the property is fully accessible but might experience some traffic changes

The hotel does not accept any sort of pre-paid cards or gift cards.

All special requests are subject to availability upon check-in. Special requests cannot be guaranteed and may incur additional charges.

Please note: for the Pool Suite the credit card pre-authorization upon check-in will be 250 USD.
