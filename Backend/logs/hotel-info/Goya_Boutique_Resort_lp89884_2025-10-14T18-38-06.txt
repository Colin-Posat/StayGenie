HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 5 years old stay for Rp 275,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 10 years old stay for Rp 1,500,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for Rp 907,500 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location was a highlight, with guests enjoying its proximity to main shopping streets and attractions in Ubud.
• Room Quality: 8.6/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and spacious, with some minor maintenance issues reported in a few reviews.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with positive mentions of the pool and restaurant but some guests noting the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 8/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 8.3/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, although some guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 9.8/10 - Most guests had a highly positive overall experience, praising the staff, location, and amenities.
