CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for € 35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 45 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 7/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some feeling it was reasonable while others found certain services overpriced.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast was generally well-received, but there were suggestions for improvement in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, citing staff friendliness and convenient location.
