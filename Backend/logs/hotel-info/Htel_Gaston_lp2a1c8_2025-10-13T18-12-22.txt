HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Contemporary Design by Renowned DecoratorHôtel Gaston, located in Paris’ 17th arrondissement, boasts a contemporary design by Anne Lise Dees, a former collaborator of Philippe Stark. The modern rooms, soundproofed and air-conditioned, offer a comfortable stay with amenities like a TV, private safe, and stylish private bathrooms.Delicious Breakfast Options and Artistic DecorStart your day with a buffet or express breakfast in the convivial lobby, or opt for room service for a more private dining experience. Admire the artistic flair of the Splendens Factory community's creations adorning the corridors and lobby, adding a unique touch to your stay.Convenient Location and Additional ServicesWith proximity to Avenue des Champs-Elysées and the Grands Magasins area, and a short walk to Wagram Metro Station, explore Paris with ease. Enjoy services like dry cleaning, 24-hour reception, and an airport shuttle for a seamless stay at Hôtel Gaston.Book now to experience the perfect blend of style, comfort, and convenience at Hôtel Gaston in Paris!
