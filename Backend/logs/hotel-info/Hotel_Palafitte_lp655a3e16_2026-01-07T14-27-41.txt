HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Pavilions with Stunning ViewsThe Hotel Palafitte presents individual luxury pavilions overlooking Lake Neuchâtel and the Alps, with half of them standing on piles right in the lake.Exquisite AmenitiesEach 68 m² pavilion features a spa bath, Nespresso coffee machine, air-conditioning, free internet access, and complimentary wireless internet for your comfort.Direct Lake AccessEnjoy direct access to the lake from the private terrace of the water-based pavilions, enhancing your tranquil experience.Indulge in refined Swiss and local cuisine at the elegant La Table de Palafitte restaurant. Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hotel Palafitte.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for CHF 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
