HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Garden and Free Wi-Fi1920's and 1930's Photography DecorComplimentary Refreshments and In-House RestaurantLocated in the vibrant NoMad neighborhood, Hotel Giraffe by Library Hotel Collection offers a unique blend of luxury and convenience. With a charming rooftop garden and complimentary Wi-Fi, guests can unwind in style. The rooms, adorned with captivating photography from the 1920s and 1930s, provide a nostalgic yet elegant ambiance. Indulge in complimentary refreshments and dine at the in-house restaurant, Bread &amp; Tulips. Just steps away from Union Square Park and Chelsea, and with easy access to transportation, Hotel Giraffe is the perfect choice for your New York City adventure. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
