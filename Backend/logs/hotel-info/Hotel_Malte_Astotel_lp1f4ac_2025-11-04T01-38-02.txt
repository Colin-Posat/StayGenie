HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationIndulge in the elegant 4-star Hotel Malte - Astotel, nestled in central Paris just a stone's throw away from the iconic Louvre Museum. Each soundproofed room is equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and a cozy minibar.Charming AmenitiesRelax in the serene interior courtyard or unwind in the inviting lounge featuring a crackling fireplace. Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast served in the picturesque breakfast room or on the tranquil patio.Convenient LocationWith Pyramides Metro Station a mere 450 meters away, explore the enchanting streets of Paris with ease. Don't miss the opportunity to book your stay at Hotel Malte - Astotel and experience the epitome of luxury and convenience in the heart of the city.
