HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Free WiFiThe Bedrooms, located just a 5-minute walk from On-Nut BTS skytrain station, offers air-conditioned rooms with modern darkwood interiors and free WiFi. Each room features a cable TV, DVD player, and tea/coffee maker, providing a comfortable stay for guests.Convenient Location and AmenitiesConveniently situated, the hotel is a short drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport and Bangkok International Trade &amp; Exhibition Centre. Guests can enjoy daily buffet breakfast, fitness room, meeting facilities, and free parking. Additionally, the 24-hour reception, taxi services, and laundry service add to the convenience.Relaxation and Dining OptionsGuests can unwind with beauty treatments at the salon or indulge in Thai &amp; International meals at the on-site restaurant. With a range of amenities and services, The Bedrooms Boutique Hotel Bangkok offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience.Book your stay at The Bedrooms for a memorable experience!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 14 years old stay for THB 795 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for THB 495 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
