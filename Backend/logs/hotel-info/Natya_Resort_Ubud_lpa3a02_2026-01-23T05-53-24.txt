HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsNatya Resort Ubud offers a range of accommodations, from rooms with private pools to units with spacious terraces or balconies. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and personal safes. Guests can enjoy complimentary toiletries, slippers, and hairdryers for added comfort.Exceptional FacilitiesIndulge in the outdoor pool, sun terrace, and BBQ facilities at Natya Resort Ubud. The on-site restaurant, Natys Ubud, serves delectable American and Asian dishes. For special dietary needs, a customized menu is available. Additionally, guests can benefit from free WiFi, 24-hour front desk service, and options for bike or car rentals.Convenient LocationConveniently situated just 4 km from Ubud Market, Natya Resort Ubud provides easy access to nearby attractions like Ubud Palace and Ubud Monkey Forest. With airport transfer services and free private parking, your stay at Natya Resort Ubud promises both luxury and convenience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 750,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for Rp 750,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
