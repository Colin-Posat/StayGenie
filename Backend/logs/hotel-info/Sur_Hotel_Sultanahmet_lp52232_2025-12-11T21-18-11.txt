HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Ottoman House Overlooking Marmara SeaUnique Room Designs:Individually decorated in vibrant colors, the air-conditioned rooms at Sur Hotel &amp; Apartments offer cable TV, a mini fridge, and some with balconies showcasing stunning Marmara Sea views.Cozy Amenities:Guests can savor a delicious buffet breakfast on the terrace or unwind at the lobby bar with a drink. For a more hands-on experience, barbecue facilities are provided for self-cooked meals.Convenient Location and Services:Benefit from the 24-hour front desk for tour arrangements, a library for local insights, and free internet access. With the Blue Mosque, Topkapi Palace, and Grand Bazaar within walking distance, explore Istanbul effortlessly.Book now to experience the perfect blend of history and comfort at Sur Hotel &amp; Apartments!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 7 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
