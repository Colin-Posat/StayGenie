HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Paris:Hotel Des Mines is perfectly situated in central Paris, just a stone's throw away from iconic landmarks such as the Jardin du Luxembourg and Montparnasse Train Station.Comfortable and Convenient Amenities:Enjoy a relaxing stay in our air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi access, satellite TV, and a private bathroom. The soundproofed rooms ensure a peaceful night's sleep, while the safety deposit box adds an extra layer of security to your stay.Convenience at Your Fingertips:Start your day with a delicious breakfast served in the dining room or in the comfort of your own room. Take advantage of our 24-hour front desk, express check-in/check-out services, and proximity to key attractions like St-Germain des Prés and the Latin Quarter.Experience the best of Paris at Hotel Des Mines - book your stay now for an unforgettable experience!
