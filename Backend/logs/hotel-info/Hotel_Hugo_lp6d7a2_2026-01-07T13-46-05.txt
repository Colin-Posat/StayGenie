HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsDiscover a blend of comfort and style at Hotel Hugo, where each room features Italian glass-tiled bathrooms, complimentary WiFi, and stunning views of the Hudson River.Rooftop Bar and DiningIndulge in a cocktail at the chic rooftop bar or treat your taste buds to farm-to-table American cuisine at Café Hugo, all while enjoying panoramic views of the city skyline.Convenient LocationLocated in the trendy Hudson Square neighborhood, Hotel Hugo offers easy access to popular attractions like Times Square, Central Park, and Broadway, making it the perfect choice for your Manhattan getaway.Experience the allure of Hotel Hugo and book your stay now for an unforgettable New York City experience.
