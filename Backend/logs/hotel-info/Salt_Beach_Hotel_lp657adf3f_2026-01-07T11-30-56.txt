HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Seafront Location and Family-Friendly AtmosphereSalt Beach Hotel in Maafushi offers a picturesque 3-star accommodation by the sea. With a garden, shared lounge, and 24-hour front desk, guests can enjoy a cozy stay. The hotel caters to families with spacious rooms, some offering sea views and balconies.Convenient Amenities and Dining OptionsEach unit is equipped with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, kettle, and free WiFi. Guests can dine at the on-site restaurant serving delicious meals for all times of the day. The property also provides outdoor play equipment for children.Exciting Activities and ServicesSalt Beach Hotel offers a bicycle rental service for exploring the area, along with opportunities for canoeing and walking tours. Don't miss the chance to relax at Bikini Beach just a stone's throw away.Book your stay at Salt Beach Hotel for a memorable seaside retreat.
