HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AccommodationHotel Petit Lafayette offers modern accommodation in the heart of Paris, located between boulevard Montmartre and rue Lafayette. The rooms feature en suite facilities, satellite TV, work desk, and free Wi-Fi access.Convenient LocationSituated near Cadet Metro Station, the hotel is a short walk from the famous Garnier Opera, Le Printemps, and Galeries Lafayette department stores. Gare du Nord and Gare de l'Est are also just 1 km away.Delicious Breakfast and Nearby AttractionsGuests can enjoy a full breakfast served daily at the hotel. With its prime location and comfortable amenities, Hotel Petit Lafayette is the perfect choice for your Parisian getaway.
