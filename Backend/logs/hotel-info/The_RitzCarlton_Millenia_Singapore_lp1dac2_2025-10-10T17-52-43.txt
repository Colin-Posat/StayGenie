HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  SGD 15 per  day.

• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding the price high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
