HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience comfort and style at Canopy By Hilton Philadelphia Center City. Our hotel offers modern and air-conditioned rooms equipped with all the amenities you need for a relaxing stay, including a flat-screen TV, a coffee machine, and a private bathroom. Upgrade to a room with a kitchenette for added convenience during your visit to Philadelphia.Convenient FacilitiesEnjoy the convenience of our on-site restaurant, fitness centre, and business centre during your stay. With free WiFi throughout the property, a 24-hour front desk, and a shared lounge, we ensure all your needs are met. Start your day right with an à la carte breakfast before exploring the nearby attractions.Prime LocationSituated just steps away from Pennsylvania Convention Center and minutes from Liberty Bell, our hotel is the perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers. Explore the city with ease, as popular museums and landmarks are within walking distance. Book your stay at Canopy By Hilton Philadelphia Center City now for an unforgettable experience.
