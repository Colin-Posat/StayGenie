IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note the property cannot accommodate group bookings of more than 5 rooms.

Please note that children under the age of 18 are not permitted to stay in the pods. They can stay in the rooms when accompanied by a parent or guardian over the age of 18.

Rooms are cleaned every third day during your stay. For additional cleaning, this can be easily arranged for a small fee when making a booking, or simply ask one of the staff at the hotel. They can also help with fresh towels, if needed.

The bed linen in the Pods are changed every fifth day during your stay. For extra towels, you can ask the staff at the hotel.
