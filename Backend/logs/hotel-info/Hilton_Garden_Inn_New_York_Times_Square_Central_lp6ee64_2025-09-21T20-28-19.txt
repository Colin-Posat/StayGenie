HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in New York CityLuxurious Rooms and AmenitiesConvenient Facilities and ServicesBook your stay at Hilton Garden Inn New York - Times Square Central for a prime location in the heart of the city, just steps away from Times Square and Broadway. Indulge in our well-appointed rooms equipped with modern conveniences like flat-screen TVs, refrigerators, and free WiFi.
