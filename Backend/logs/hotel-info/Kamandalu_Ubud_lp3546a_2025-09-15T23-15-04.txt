HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Standalone Accommodation:Nestled on Ubud’s hillside, Kamandalu Ubud offers luxurious standalone accommodation with stunning views of the Petanu River. Inspired by traditional Balinese villages, each room and villa features picturesque views of hills or rice paddies, thatched roofs, and spacious interiors with 5-star amenities.Exquisite Spa and Activities:Guests can indulge in a variety of activities at Kamandalu Ubud, from bicycle tours through rice paddies to Balinese dance lessons. The Chaya Spa offers a range of pampering services including a beauty salon, sauna, and spa bath for ultimate relaxation. Additionally, the Andalu Shop on-site showcases and sells authentic Balinese crafts.Culinary Delights and Scenic Dining:Savor Asian specialties at Petulu Restaurant or enjoy breathtaking valley views while dining on Asian and Western cuisine at the poolside Aira Cafe. With a 10-minute drive to Ubud’s town centre and an hour's drive to Ngurah Rai International Airport, Kamandalu Ubud offers a perfect blend of tranquility and convenience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 2 years old to 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 2 years old to 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 525,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 975,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to town and surrounded by a quiet forest, providing a serene environment for guests.
• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, with some guests mentioning minor maintenance issues in a few cases.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with positive highlights on the gym, pool, and spa facilities, while there were some mentions of lacking spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally positive, although some guests desired more variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, praising the friendly staff, beautiful villa, and enjoyable facilities.
