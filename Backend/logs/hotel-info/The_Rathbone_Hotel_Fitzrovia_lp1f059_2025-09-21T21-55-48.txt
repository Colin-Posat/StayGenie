HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in the West EndLocated in the heart of the vibrant West End, The Rathbone Hotel, Fitzrovia is a boutique style hotel just minutes away from popular attractions like Oxford Street, Soho, and Theatreland.Luxurious Room AmenitiesEach room at The Rathbone Hotel, Fitzrovia is equipped with a TV with satellite channels, tea/coffee facilities, and complimentary bottled water.
