IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), please ensure that you are only booking this property following the local government guidelines of the destination, including but not limited to the purpose of travel, and maximum allowed group size.
A damage deposit of USD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note extended cruise port parking is available for 21 days upon request.

Please note that the cruise port transfer service has limited hours of operation. Charges are applicable. contact the property for details.

There is a daily per package fee for all packages delivered to the hotel. The name on the package must match the name of the registered guest.

Guests must present the same credit card used for making the reservation at the time of check in.

Please note the airport transfer service is only available from 7 am to 8 pm.

The property will charge a daily parking fee of US$50 for truck over 18 feet up to 24 feet.  Over this size is not allow in the property.
