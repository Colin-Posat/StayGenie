HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Hotel Near Gare de Lyon Train StationConvenient Location: Situated a mere 10-minute walk from Gare de Lyon Train Station, our hotel offers easy access to transportation links and nearby attractions.Comfortable Accommodations: Each guest room features satellite TV, free WiFi, and picturesque views of either the garden or the bustling street. The en suite bathrooms are equipped with all necessary amenities, including a hairdryer.Explore the Area: Discover the vibrant Bastille district just a short stroll away, or visit the green oasis of Bercy Park and the fascinating Cinémathèque Museum, both within 300 meters of the hotel.Book your stay now and experience the perfect blend of convenience and comfort!
