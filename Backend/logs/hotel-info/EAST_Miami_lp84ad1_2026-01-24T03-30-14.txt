HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesEAST Miami offers a lavish experience with 4 pools, a 24-hour fitness centre, and an on-site restaurant. The 20,000-square-foot outdoor pool deck boasts 4 unique pool experiences, including a lap pool and hot tub, providing ultimate relaxation for guests.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in a variety of culinary delights at EAST Miami. From authentic Uruguayan fare at Quinto to light snacks at Domain lounge, there's something to satisfy every palate. For breathtaking city views, head to the 40th-level rooftop bar, Sugar, and enjoy a refreshing drink while taking in the sights of Brickell.Prime LocationConveniently situated in Brickell City Centre, guests have access to premium retail shops, a movie theatre, and an array of dining options. Explore nearby attractions like the Perez Art Museum Miami and American Airlines Arena, or take a short drive to the vibrant South Beach for a day of fun in the sun.Experience luxury and convenience at EAST Miami - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for US$40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 55 per  day.
