HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Sun Terrace and Comfortable AccommodationBcnSportHostels, located in Barcelona’s Gracia Neighbourhood, offers a sun terrace and cozy accommodation with air conditioning and free WiFi. The property features female and mixed dormitories with bunk beds and individual reading lamps, as well as extra-large beds for added comfort.Fitness and Wellness FacilitiesGuests at BcnSportHostels enjoy discounted rates at the adjacent gym and access to a spa with a hot tub, saunas, and a fully equipped fitness area. Stay active and rejuvenate during your stay at this vibrant hostel.Convenient Amenities and Nearby AttractionsWith a small kitchenette, bar, and vending machines on-site, guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay. Explore the trendy Paseo de Gracia Avenue and visit iconic landmarks like La Sagrada Familia, all within easy reach from the hostel.Book now for an unforgettable stay at BcnSportHostels!
