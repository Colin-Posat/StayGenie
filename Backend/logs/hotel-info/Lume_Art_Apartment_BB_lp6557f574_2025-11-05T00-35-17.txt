HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Sustainable Luxury: Nestled in Sorrento near picturesque beaches, Lume Art Apartment B&amp;B offers a sustainable retreat with stunning city views and modern amenities. Guests can enjoy a sun terrace and complimentary WiFi throughout the property.Home Away from Home: Each air-conditioned unit is thoughtfully furnished with a fully equipped kitchen, private bathroom, and cozy living space.
