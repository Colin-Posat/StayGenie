HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Tropical RetreatThe Udaya Resorts and Spa in Ubud is a tranquil oasis nestled in lush greenery, offering a serene escape with a spa centre. Enjoy modern comforts in beautifully decorated rooms with private balconies or terraces overlooking the pool or garden.Indulgent Spa and Culinary DelightsUnwind with a rejuvenating spa treatment or join yoga classes at the on-site spa. Explore the rich Balinese and Indonesian flavors at Deeva Organic Restaurant, serving dishes made with fresh local ingredients. Don't miss the daily afternoon tea for a delightful treat.Convenient Amenities and ActivitiesStay connected with free WiFi, take part in biking and trekking tours, or simply relax in the private pool available in some units. The tour desk can assist with arranging trips and transportation for a seamless vacation experience.Book now for a luxurious stay at The Udaya Resorts and Spa, where nature and comfort harmoniously blend.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, with occasional mentions of minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service received high praise for being friendly, helpful, and responsive, although some guests experienced delays.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and public transport was lauded, although some guests found accessibility challenging.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but some guests reported outdated decor and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, particularly highlighting the pool and breakfast offerings, but some desired additional services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the perceived value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, though some guests desired more variety and a better buffet selection.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
