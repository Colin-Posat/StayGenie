HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Outdoor Pool and BBQ FacilitiesEarly American Motel offers a delightful experience with its seasonal outdoor swimming pool and barbecue facilities. Guests can unwind and enjoy a relaxing day under the sun while savoring delicious grilled meals.Convenient AmenitiesWith air-conditioned rooms featuring free WiFi, a shared kitchen, and a 24-hour front desk, Early American Motel ensures a comfortable stay for all guests. The rooms are equipped with modern conveniences like flat-screen TVs, free toiletries, and some even come with kitchenettes for added convenience.Exciting Nearby ActivitiesLocated near various attractions such as Jonathan Creek Cabin Area and Ruff Creek Cabin Area, guests can indulge in thrilling activities like diving, fishing, and snorkeling. For a memorable stay filled with adventure, book your stay at Early American Motel now.
