HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Opposite Artis Zoo LocationHotel Plantage offers a tranquil stay opposite the vibrant Artis Zoo. Enjoy the convenience of free Wi-Fi and soundproof rooms with double glazing and light-proof curtains for a restful night's sleep.Comfortable Rooms and AmenitiesIndulge in the comfort of box-spring beds, a private bathroom, and a TV in each guest room. For dining options, explore the array of restaurants and cafés nearby. Additionally, relish 24-hour access to fresh coffee and cold drinks in the lobby.Convenient Transportation and Nearby AttractionsBenefit from easy access to public transportation with the Plantage Kerklaan tram stop just across the hotel. Explore Amsterdam's popular spots like the Red Light District, located within a 15-minute walk from the hotel.Book your stay at Hotel Plantage for a delightful Amsterdam experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hotel Plantage in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of EUR 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that the hotel does not have a lift. The rooms are accessible by steep stairs and reception is located on the first floor.

Please note that the hotel has the right to take an authorization of the first night.

Please note that children's cots and cribs are not available.

Please note that the holder of the credit card used has to be present in case of a non refundable rate booked. If not, please contact the property prior to arrival.

Please note that different policies and charges may apply when booking 5 rooms or more.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests had mixed impressions on cleanliness, with some mentioning dirt and stains on towels.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with mentions of slow responses and unhelpful receptionists.
• Location: 9/10 - Location was highly praised for its convenience and proximity to key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and newly renovated, although some guests faced maintenance issues like broken shower heads.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Amenities were described as basic, with complaints about lack of mugs for coffee and poor room signposting.
• Value for Money: 5.5/10 - Value for money had mixed feedback, with some feeling the hotel was overpriced for the experience received.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services were decent, with guests appreciating breakfast options but desiring more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - Overall experience varied, with some guests loving the location and modern amenities, while others were disappointed with cleanliness and service levels.
