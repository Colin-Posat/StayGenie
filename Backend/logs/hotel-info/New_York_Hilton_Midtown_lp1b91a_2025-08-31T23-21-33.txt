Luxurious AccommodationsThe New York Hilton Midtown offers elegant rooms styled with maple wood furniture and marble bathrooms, providing a comfortable and upscale stay. Each room includes a flat-screen TV for entertainment.Gourmet DiningIndulge in delicious cuisine at Herb N’ Kitchen, offering a variety of seasonal salads, sandwiches, and brick oven pizza. HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 80 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Clean rooms
• Friendly staff

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• No spa services

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Overall, guests had mixed experiences with cleanliness, with some mentioning issues like unkempt hallways and lack of cleanliness in the room.
• Service: 7.4/10 - While many guests appreciated the service provided, there were complaints about noise levels, inadequate staffing, and lack of promptness in handling requests.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's location received high praise from guests for being in a convenient spot close to key landmarks and public transportation.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Opinions on room quality varied, with some guests mentioning issues such as uncomfortable mattresses and the need for room renovations.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - Guests expressed disappointment with the amenities, citing overpriced extras, lack of coffee machines and kettles, and outdated furnishings.
• Value for Money: 5.6/10 - Several guests felt that the hotel was overpriced for the services offered, especially in terms of amenities and room quality.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - While breakfast and food service were generally well-received, some guests noted issues with the variety and quality of offerings in the lounge.
• Overall Experience: 5.5/10 - Guests had mixed experiences overall, with many expressing dissatisfaction with the value for money, cleanliness, and service levels.
