HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and AmenitiesThe Paramount Hotel, a 605-room boutique hotel in New York's Theater District, offers a lavish stay with flat-screen TVs, iPod docking stations, and premium bedding including pillow-top mattresses and Egyptian cotton sheets.Convenient FacilitiesGuests can enjoy a 24-hour front desk, fitness center, and meeting rooms during their stay at The Paramount Hotel, ensuring a comfortable and seamless experience.Prime Location and Personalized ServiceLocated just steps away from Times Square, with attractions like the Museum of Modern Art and Radio City Music Hall nearby, The Paramount Hotel also provides personalized service through their concierge for dining, transportation, and entertainment arrangements.Book your stay at The Paramount Hotel for an unforgettable New York experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

We would welcome a dog on property but only when staying in a private room and subject to a fee per dog per Stay. Only Dog under 75lbs./ 34kg.

Luggage can be store for a small fee: $2 per bag for early arrival and $3 per bag after the check out.

AGE RESTRICTIONS: Individuals under the age of 18 can only stay at the Hostel when accompanied by a parent or legal guardian in the same room.
The room must be private.
Unaccompanied under 18's are not allowed to stay in shared rooms, unless they are part of a group that has booked out the entire shared room. The group leader must provide the required documentation to Generator. Failure to comply with these policies will result in automatic cancellation of the booking with no refund. Guests violating these rules will not be permitted to check into the Hostel.
