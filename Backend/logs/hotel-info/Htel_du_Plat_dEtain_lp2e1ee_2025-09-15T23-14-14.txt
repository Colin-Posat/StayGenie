Guest rooms at Hôtel du Plat d'Etain are equipped with QLED 43" 4K TV (with Chromecast, satellite and Premium channels : Canal+ and beIN Sports) and a minibar. All rooms are accessible by lift and have a private bathroom with a bath or shower.
