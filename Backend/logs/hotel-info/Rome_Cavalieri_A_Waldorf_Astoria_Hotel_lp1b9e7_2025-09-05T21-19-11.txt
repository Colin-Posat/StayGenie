HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Stunning ViewsRome Cavalieri, A Waldorf Astoria Hotel offers spacious rooms with balconies overlooking Rome and the Vatican. Enjoy marble bathrooms and access to the Imperial Club Lounge for ultimate luxury.Gourmet Dining and RelaxationIndulge in exquisite dining at the 3-Michelin star rooftop restaurant, La Pergola, or savor classic Italian dishes at L'Uliveto by the poolside terrace. Unwind at the 2500 m² wellness center with Turkish bath, saunas, and indoor swimming pool.Exclusive Amenities and ActivitiesExperience top-notch facilities including red-clay tennis courts, a fitness trail, and a Grand Spa with Technogym equipment. Explore the hotel's extensive art collection and take advantage of the free shuttle to popular attractions.Book your stay at Rome Cavalieri for a truly luxurious Roman getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Rome Cavalieri, A Waldorf Astoria Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
The wellness centre, indoor swimming pool and relaxation area are available at an extra cost.

Please note the sun loungers and parasols by the pool are subject to availability.

Parking is subject to availability and pets are allowed at extra charge.

Please note that occupancy of the accommodation cannot be exceeded.

Please note that when booking a breakfast included rate you can only have breakfast at the L'Uliveto restaurant.

Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), wearing a face mask is advisable in all indoor common areas.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 35 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 17 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Food services received positive feedback overall, with some suggestions for improvement in variety.
