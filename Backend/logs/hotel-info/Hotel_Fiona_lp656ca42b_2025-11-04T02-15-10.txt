HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Individually Designed Guest RoomsHotel Fiona offers uniquely designed guest rooms that pay homage to a famous figure in San Francisco history. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, iPod docking station, guest robes, coffee machine, and an in-room safe.Convenient AmenitiesStart your day with complimentary coffee and tea in the hotel's lobby. Guests also have the privilege of accessing a fitness center and a heated outdoor pool at the hotel's sister property just across the street.Prime LocationSituated a short 10-minute walk from San Francisco's Civic Center Plaza, Hotel Fiona is in close proximity to The Strand Theater and AT&amp;T Park. Additionally, the Civic Center/UN Plaza Station BART station is a mere 4 minutes away, providing easy access to the San Francisco International Airport.Book your stay at Hotel Fiona for a delightful and convenient San Francisco experience.
