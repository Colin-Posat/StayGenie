Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.3/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests mentioning cleanliness as a positive aspect while others reported issues like dirty bathrooms and roaches in the room.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Service received mixed feedback, with some guests appreciating the staff's helpfulness and cooperation, while others had complaints about delayed check-out charges and ineffective service.
• Location: 7.5/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and public transport was generally well-received, although some guests mentioned the distance from the main areas of the city.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Rooms were described as spacy and comfortable by some guests, but others reported issues like noisy AC, dark walls, and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on amenities, with mentions of a pool, gym, and free water daily, but also complaints about a dirty swimming pool and lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Reviews varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was reasonable given the location, while others found it too high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services received mixed feedback, with some guests appreciating them while others desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, highlighting aspects like friendly staff and a pleasant stay.
