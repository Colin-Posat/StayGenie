HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Themed RoomsZe Hotel in central Paris offers stylish guest rooms, each with a distinct theme and decor, providing a one-of-a-kind experience for every guest. From modern chic to classic elegance, there's a room to suit every taste.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy modern amenities such as free WiFi, flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, and minibars in the soundproofed rooms. Some rooms even feature iPod docking stations for your entertainment needs.Ideal LocationSituated between the vibrant Opera and artistic Montmartre districts, Ze Hotel is a perfect base for exploring Paris. With major attractions like Sacré-Coeur Basilica and Gare du Nord train station just a short walk away, and renowned department stores like Galeries Lafayettes nearby, convenience is at your doorstep.Ready to immerse yourself in the charm of Ze Hotel? Book now to secure your unforgettable Parisian getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 27 per  day.
