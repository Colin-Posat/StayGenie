HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Spectacular ViewsThe 4-star hotel Pullman Paris Tour Eiffel offers contemporary guest rooms with stunning panoramic views of Paris, including some with views of the iconic Eiffel Tower. Enjoy modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, iPod dock, and free WiFi access, ensuring a comfortable stay.Dining Experience and Fitness FacilitiesIndulge in Californian-style cuisine with French influences at the hotel's restaurant, Frame, which features an open-plan kitchen and a chic wine bar. Start your day with a diverse breakfast selection, including Asian, vegetarian, and gluten-free options. Stay active at the 24-hour fitness room equipped with cardio machines.Convenient Location and ConnectivitySituated just a stone's throw away from the Eiffel Tower and the Trocadero, Pullman Paris provides easy access to Parisian landmarks. With a connectivity lounge, guests can stay connected with complimentary access to computers and printers. Book now for an unforgettable Parisian getaway at Pullman Paris Tour Eiffel.
