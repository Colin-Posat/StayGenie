HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Exclusive Wellness FacilitiesEnjoy a rejuvenating experience at the Matthiol Boutique Hotel's wellness area, featuring a herbal sauna, steam bath, and Finnish sauna - all complimentary for guests. Stay fit with the free access to the fitness room and unwind in style.Luxurious Rooms with Stunning ViewsIndulge in comfort and style in the hotel's rooms equipped with free WiFi, life TV streaming, and a coffee machine. Most rooms offer a balcony with breathtaking views of the Matterhorn or Gornergrat, providing a truly memorable stay.Gourmet Dining ExperienceSavor Swiss and international cuisine at the trendy on-site restaurant, showcasing locally sourced ingredients. During winter, relish in the unique experience of indulging in cheese fondue in outside huts. Start your day right with a delicious buffet breakfast.Experience luxury and comfort at Matthiol Boutique Hotel - book now for an unforgettable stay!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Zermatt is a car-free resort. Please park your car in Täsch and proceed by train (leaving every 20 minutes). The hotel offers free transfers by electric taxi from the Zermatt Train Station to the hotel between 8:00 and 21:00. Please inform the hotel in advance about your arrival time at the train station.

If you are traveling with children, please inform the property in advance of their age. You can use the special request box when booking or contact the property directly. Contact details are stated in the booking confirmation.
