GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Good location
• Friendly staff
• Clean rooms
• Convenient amenities

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• No spa services

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, although there were occasional mentions of minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service received positive feedback overall, with some guests mentioning fast and friendly staff, while others complained about slow response times.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and public transport was highly praised by guests, particularly those looking to stay away from crowded areas.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were generally described as comfortable and spacious, although there were occasional complaints about the size of beds and sofa beds.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities such as the pool and restaurant received positive feedback, however, some guests noted the absence of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was too high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and dining options, although there were suggestions for more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
