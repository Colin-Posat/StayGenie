What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:
• Cleanliness issues
• Inconsistent housekeeping

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3/10 - Multiple guests criticized the cleanliness of the hotel, citing issues with bugs, pests, soap scum, and mildew.
• Service: 7.4/10 - While some guests praised the staff for their warmth and hospitality, inconsistency in housekeeping and unresponsive management brought down the overall rating.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location was highlighted as a positive aspect by several guests, contributing to the high rating in this category.
• Room Quality: 6.2/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with some guests mentioning small room size, design mismatch, and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Satisfactory amenities overall, with compliments on the lobby area, meeting rooms, and balcony in rooms, but criticism on the lack of stylish design and door safety issues.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Several guests mentioned that the price paid did not align with the quality of services received, impacting the rating in this category.
• Food and Beverage: 6.3/10 - Reviews on food and beverage were mixed, with complaints about fried food, breakfast quality, and pricing.
• Overall Experience: 7.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, largely influenced by the helpful and friendly staff, despite some shortcomings in cleanliness and room quality.
