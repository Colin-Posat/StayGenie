HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Parisian Hotel with Convenient Metro AccessElegant French-Inspired Rooms: Experience the essence of French tradition in our beautifully styled guest rooms, complete with modern amenities such as satellite TV and Wi-Fi.Prime Location: Situated just 2.5 km from Montmartre and the Sacre Coeur, our non-smoking hotel offers easy access to the city's iconic landmarks and attractions.
