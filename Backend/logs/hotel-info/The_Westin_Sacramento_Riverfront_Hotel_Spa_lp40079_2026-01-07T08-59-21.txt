HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Westin Sacramento boasts elegantly designed rooms with vintage Italian furnishings, claw foot bathtubs, and plush bathrobes. Enjoy river views from select balconies and unwind in luxury with provided coffee makers and premium linens.Fine Dining ExperienceIndulge in exquisite cuisine at Scott’s Seafood restaurant located on-site, offering a picturesque setting with patio seating and riverfront views. Delight your taste buds with four-star quality dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, or opt for convenient room service for a relaxing dining experience in your room.Convenient AmenitiesEnhance your stay with access to a year-round outdoor heated pool, hot tub, and a 24-hour gym. Explore nearby attractions such as William Land Park Golf Course and the California State Capitol Building, just a short drive away.Book your stay at The Westin Sacramento for a luxurious and memorable experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
