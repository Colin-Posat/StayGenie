HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationJust steps away from Kyoto Shiyakusho-mae Subway Station, Hotel Gran Ms Kyoto is perfectly situated for exploring the city. Within walking distance to the vibrant Gion district and two major train stations, the hotel offers easy access to Kyoto's top attractions.Comfortable AccommodationChoose between Western-style rooms or traditional Japanese rooms with tatami flooring and futon bedding. All rooms are equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, fridge, and electric kettle. Relax in the well-appointed rooms after a day of sightseeing in Kyoto.Convenient FacilitiesEnjoy the convenience of a 24-hour reception, free WiFi, and complimentary luggage storage at Gran Ms Kyoto Hotel. Additionally, guests can take advantage of the on-site vending machines, coin-operated laundry facilities, and more for a hassle-free stay.Ready to experience the best of Kyoto? Book your stay at Hotel Gran Ms Kyoto now!
