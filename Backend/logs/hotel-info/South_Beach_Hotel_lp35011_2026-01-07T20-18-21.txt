HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Chic Boutique Hotel in the Heart of South BeachModern Guest Rooms: Enjoy a comfortable stay in our modern guest rooms equipped with a 37-inch flat-screen TV, work desk, and an over-sized closet. Select rooms even offer extra seating space for your relaxation.Convenient Amenities: Take advantage of free WiFi, access to a nearby outdoor pool, and a fitness centre at the adjacent sister property. Stay connected and refreshed throughout your stay with our convenient amenities.Prime Location: Located just minutes away from Bass Museum of Art and steps from Collins Park and the Atlantic Ocean, our hotel offers the perfect blend of serenity and excitement. Explore nearby attractions like Miami Beach Botanical Garden and Bayshore Municipal Golf Course within walking distance.Book your stay at SBH South Beach Hotel for a memorable experience in South Beach!
