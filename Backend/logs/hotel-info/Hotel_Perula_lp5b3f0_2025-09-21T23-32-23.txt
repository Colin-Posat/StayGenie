HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Prime LocationSituated in the heart of Istanbul’s Old City, Sultanahmet, Hotel Perula offers a luxurious stay just steps away from iconic landmarks like the Grand Bazaar, Topkapi Palace, and Hagia Sophia. The air-conditioned rooms feature floor-to-ceiling windows and elegant wooden furniture, providing a comfortable and stylish retreat.Delicious Dining Options and Relaxing AmenitiesStart your day with a Turkish buffet breakfast at the terrace restaurant, serving homemade pastries. For other meals, enjoy the a-la-carte menu or unwind with a drink at the lobby bar. Guests can also indulge in a Turkish hammam experience or massage services for ultimate relaxation.Convenience and Hospitality at Your ServiceWith a 24/7 reception, dry cleaning facilities, currency exchange, and a tour desk, Hotel Perula ensures a convenient stay. Don't miss the complimentary Turkish delight upon check-in and the proximity to the Blue Mosque, making it a perfect choice for your Istanbul getaway. Book now for an unforgettable experience!
