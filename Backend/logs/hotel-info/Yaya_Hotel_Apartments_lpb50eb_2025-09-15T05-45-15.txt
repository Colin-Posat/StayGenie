HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationYaya Hotel &amp; Apartments in Nairobi offers a luxurious stay with fully serviced apartments featuring a kitchen, dining area, and modern amenities like satellite TV and free WiFi. The private bathrooms and comfortable seating areas ensure a relaxing experience for guests.Exciting Leisure FacilitiesGuests can enjoy a range of leisure facilities including an outdoor pool, tennis and basketball courts, fitness center, and a games room with a pool table and Playstation. The BBQ facilities, children's playground, and shared lounge add to the entertainment options.Convenient LocationConveniently situated near the Yaya Centre and just a short distance from Nairobi's city center, the hotel provides easy access to key attractions like the Kenyatta International Conference Centre. With free parking and a 24-hour front desk, Yaya Hotel &amp; Apartments ensures a hassle-free stay.Book your stay at Yaya Hotel &amp; Apartments for a memorable and comfortable experience in Nairobi.
