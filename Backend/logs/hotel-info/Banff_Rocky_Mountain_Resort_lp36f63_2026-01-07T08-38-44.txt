HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Suite Accommodation and Scenic LocationExperience spacious all-suite accommodation at our resort located along the beautiful Banff Rocky Mountain National Park. Enjoy breathtaking views and easy access to top-rated ski trails, scenic hiking paths, and golfing opportunities just moments away.Health and Fitness ClubIndulge in our health and fitness club offering a full gym, squash courts, outdoor tennis courts, a 15-meter indoor swimming pool, and both indoor and outdoor hot tubs. Stay active and rejuvenate during your stay with us.Alpha Bistro and EntertainmentSavor Canadian cuisine at Alpha Bistro located in the main building, providing a cozy dining atmosphere. Unwind and socialize in the presence of large screen TVs and make the most of the spacious outdoor patio for a relaxing time.Book your stay now to experience the perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and adventure at our resort!
