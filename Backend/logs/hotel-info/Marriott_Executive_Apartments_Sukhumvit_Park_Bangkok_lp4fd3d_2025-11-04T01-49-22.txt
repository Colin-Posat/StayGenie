HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesMarriott Sukhumvit Park offers a range of luxurious amenities, from the outdoor swimming pool and kids play room to the golf practicing area. The hotel's free Tuk Tuk shuttle service ensures convenient access to nearby attractions, making your stay both comfortable and enjoyable.Spa and Fitness FacilitiesIndulge in relaxation at the spacious 24-hour fitness centre and rejuvenating spa. The hotel also boasts a tennis court, squash and badminton courts, catering to guests looking to stay active during their visit.Delightful Dining ExperienceEnjoy a culinary journey at Bistro M, serving delectable Thai and international dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Unwind with a refreshing drink at the poolside bar, offering a perfect setting to relax after a day of exploring the vibrant city.Book your stay at Marriott Sukhumvit Park now to experience luxury and convenience in the heart of Bangkok!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
People 6 years old and over stay for THB 1,200 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
