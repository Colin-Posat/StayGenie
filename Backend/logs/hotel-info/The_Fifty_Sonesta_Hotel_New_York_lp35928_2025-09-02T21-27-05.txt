HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant heartbeat of New York City at The Fifty Sonesta Hotel, where comfort meets convenience just steps away from iconic landmarks.Modern AccommodationsEach of our 252 elegantly designed rooms features a refrigerator and Smart television for your entertainment. Enjoy the luxury of designer toiletries in private bathrooms and stay connected with wireless internet access.Exceptional AmenitiesUnwind after a day of exploring with a range of amenities, including a 24-hour fitness center, a cozy lobby fireplace, and banquet halls perfect for any occasion.
