HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationNovotel London Waterloo offers spacious, air-conditioned rooms with queen-size beds and stunning views of the London skyline. Enjoy internet access, flat-screen TVs, and on-demand movies in the comfort of your room.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in tasty international cuisine at Elements Restaurant or grab a coffee or cocktail at Elements Bar, open until 01:00. 24-hour room service is also available for your convenience.Wellness and Family-FriendlyUnwind at the InBalance Fitness and Wellbeing centre with complimentary access to a sauna and steam room. Families are welcome, with children under 16 staying for free and enjoying perks like Xbox consoles, baby equipment, and a special kids menu.Experience luxury and convenience at Novotel London Waterloo - Book your stay now!

• Location: 8.1/10 - Consistently praised for its prime location near attractions, metro, and bus stations.
• Room Quality: 6.7/10 - Room quality varied, with some guests satisfied with basic rooms and others disappointed with the size and cleanliness.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests appreciating the staff and basic facilities, while others mentioned issues like clogged toilets.
• Value for Money: 5.5/10 - Guests had differing opinions on value for money, with some feeling it was not worth the cost while others found it reasonable for the location.
• Food and Beverage: 5.9/10 - Breakfast received mixed reviews, with some enjoying the continental options while others found the cooked breakfast disappointing.
• Overall Experience: 5.6/10 - Feedback on the overall experience was varied, with some guests having positive experiences, while others faced challenges with cleanliness and service.
