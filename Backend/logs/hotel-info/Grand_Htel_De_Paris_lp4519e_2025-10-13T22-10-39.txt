HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ParisSituated just 100 metres from Gare de l’Est Train Station, the Grand Hôtel De Paris offers convenience and accessibility to explore the vibrant city of Paris.Charming and Unique Guest RoomsIndividually decorated guest rooms, some with balconies, provide a cozy and personalized stay for guests looking for a touch of charm during their travels.Convenient Amenities and ServicesWith Wi-Fi access throughout the hotel, a 24-hour front desk, and rooms serviced by a lift, guests can enjoy a comfortable and hassle-free stay. Additionally, start your day right with a delicious buffet breakfast served in the breakfast room.Book your stay at the Grand Hôtel De Paris for a memorable and convenient experience in the heart of Paris.
