Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, with most reviews praising the hotel's cleanliness.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with multiple mentions of friendly and accommodating staff members. Some guests experienced delays or issues with specific services.
• Location: 9.3/10 - The hotel is located in a perfect central location near key landmarks, with beach access and activities nearby, making it an ideal spot for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and clean by most guests, although a few reviews highlighted minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with mentions of enjoyable poolside yoga, beautiful pool area, and good restaurant services. Some guests noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location and facilities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with positive comments on the food quality. Some guests suggested more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hotel's atmosphere, beachside service, and friendly staff. The location and amenities were key highlights.
