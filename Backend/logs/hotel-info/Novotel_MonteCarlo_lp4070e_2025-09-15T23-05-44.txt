HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationLocated in the heart of the Principality of Monaco, Novotel Monte-Carlo offers elegantly renovated rooms and suites with Riviera-inspired décor. All accommodations feature air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and modern amenities for a comfortable stay.Exceptional FacilitiesIndulge in the hotel's seasonal outdoor pool, fitness room, hammam, and sauna for relaxation and rejuvenation. Enjoy a variety of breakfast options, Mediterranean cuisine at Azzurra, and signature cocktails at the on-site bar. The Azzurra Kitchen restaurant offers a delightful culinary experience inspired by the famous Rivieras.Prime LocationConveniently situated within walking distance of iconic attractions like Place du Casino and Casino de Monte-Carlo, Novotel Monte-Carlo is the perfect choice for a luxurious stay in Monaco. Book now for an unforgettable experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Novotel Monte-Carlo in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please be advised by Monegasque law, the maximum occupancy per room is 4 people (maximum 3 adults or 2 adults and 2 children). Children under 1 year old are considered as a person. A 16 year old's child is considered an adult.

Please note that children under 16 years old can enjoy breakfast for free if their parents have it.

The hotel has a parking called "The Roqueville" located in 2 Avenue de Roqueville, level D reserved for Novotel's Guests:
-Height: 2.1 meters .
-Charging station for electric cars.
-Space for disabled.

Cost: 29 EUR per day all year long no reservation needed.

Check in: Please note that guests will be asked to show the credit card used to make the reservation at check-in. If you do not have the credit card please ensure you contact the property in advance to let them know.

Rates and Sales Conditions: As soon the conditions become non-refundable, they are also non-changeable.
