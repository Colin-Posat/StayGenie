HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and FacilitiesShangri-La Bangkok offers a 10,000-sqf Fitness Centre, Chi The Spa, and a variety of dining options including an award-winning restaurant. Guests can enjoy daily aerobics classes, a spa pool, sauna, and shopping arcade.Exquisite Room FeaturesRooms at the Shangri-La boast Thai décor with silk and wood furnishings, along with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs, safety deposit boxes, and minibars. Choose between city or river views for a truly immersive experience.Culinary DelightsIndulge in authentic Thai cuisine at Salathip Restaurant, savor world-class meats at 'Volti Tuscan Grill &amp; Bar,' and treat yourself at The Chocolate Boutique. Enjoy live music and a luxurious dining atmosphere during your stay.Experience luxury and comfort at Shangri-La Bangkok - book your stay now to create unforgettable memories.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 12 years old and over stay for THB 2,590 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
