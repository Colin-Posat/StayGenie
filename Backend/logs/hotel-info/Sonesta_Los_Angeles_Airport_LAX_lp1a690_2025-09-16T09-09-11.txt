IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
* Up to 3 children 17 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests praising the cleanliness of the rooms while others complained about the presence of hairs on the floor.
• Service: 8/10 - Staff received positive feedback for their friendliness and helpfulness, although there were isolated complaints about front desk service.
• Location: 8.3/10 - Guests appreciated the hotel's proximity to the airport and public transportation, making it convenient for travelers.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as spacious, modern, and comfortable, though some guests mentioned issues with noise from thin walls.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with guests enjoying the comfortable beds but expressing disappointment with the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt the hotel was overpriced, especially regarding breakfast costs, while others found the price reasonable for the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.3/10 - Breakfast pricing was a common complaint among guests, who found it expensive for the quality and portion size offered.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - Overall, guests had a positive experience, praising the staff and location, but highlighting cleanliness and noise issues as areas for improvement.
