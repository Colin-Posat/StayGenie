HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Indulge in luxury at Sianji Well-Being Resort, a serene beach haven.Spa RetreatUnwind with our rejuvenating spa services, featuring an array of massages, body treatments, and facials designed to pamper you after a long day.Culinary DelightsSavor exquisite flavors at any of our 4 restaurants or enjoy the convenience of 24-hour room service. Don’t miss the complimentary breakfast and refreshing drinks from our beach bar and poolside lounges.Modern ComfortWith 117 beautifully designed rooms, our accommodations offer minibars, LCD televisions, and rainfall showers, ensuring your stay is as comfortable as it is luxurious.Escape to paradise and make your reservation today at Sianji Well-Being Resort for the ultimate getaway!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* The seasonal pool will be open from May to October.
* Hot springs access available from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* Contactless check-in and contactless check-out are available.
