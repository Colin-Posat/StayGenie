HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Overlooking UNESCO World Heritage Site: Hotel Sant Pau offers a picturesque setting with comfortable rooms and free Wi-Fi access.Spacious and Well-equipped Rooms: Amrey's rooms are designed for comfort, featuring amenities like satellite TV and hairdryers. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, although some mentioned issues with noise.
• Service: 7.1/10 - Feedback on service was mixed, with some guests praising the helpfulness of staff while others reported rude or unprofessional behavior.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's central location near main attractions and public transport was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and well-equipped, although there were occasional complaints about noise levels.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with particular praise for the views, balcony rooms, and cleanliness.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the hotel was overpriced while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Guests had varying opinions on the quality of the food and breakfast service, with some highlighting the lack of variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
