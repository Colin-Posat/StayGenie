HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 4 years old and over stay for THB 1,800 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Helpful staff
• Clean rooms
• Convenient transportation access

Areas for Improvement:
• Maintenance issues in rooms
• Mixed reviews on value for money
• Lack of spa services

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with exceptional staff mentioned in multiple reviews.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to key landmarks and public transport, with many guests praising the convenient location.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were comfortable, although some maintenance issues were reported by a few guests.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested more variety was needed in the food offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
