HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with AmenitiesHotel Odessa offers modern rooms equipped with satellite television, double-glazing, a wardrobe, and Wi-Fi access. The en suite facilities include a bathtub or shower, washbasin, and toilet, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests.Convenient FacilitiesGuests can benefit from the hotel's lift, providing easy access to its six floors. Additionally, a spacious luggage room is available for storing belongings on the day of departure, allowing guests to make the most of their last day in Paris hassle-free.Ideal LocationLocated near the Montparnasse Tower, Hotel Odessa is situated in a vibrant district with renowned brasseries and theatres. With Vavin Metro Station just 450 meters away, guests have quick access to popular attractions such as Saint Germain-des-Prés and the Luxembourg gardens.Book your stay at Hotel Odessa for a memorable Parisian experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The hotel does not have a breakfast room. Breakfast can only be served in guests' rooms.

Please note that the credit card used for the prepayment and the corresponding photo identification will be requested upon check-in. If it will not be possible, please contact the property before arrival.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
