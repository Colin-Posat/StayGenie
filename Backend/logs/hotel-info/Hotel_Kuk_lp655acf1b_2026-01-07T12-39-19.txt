HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesHotel Kuk in Istanbul boasts a garden, shared lounge, terrace, and bar, providing a serene and relaxing atmosphere for guests. With a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, and free WiFi, we ensure a seamless and enjoyable stay for all visitors.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsEach guest room at Hotel Kuk features modern amenities such as air conditioning, a fridge, minibar, and a flat-screen TV. The rooms also offer stunning sea views, ensuring a memorable and pleasant stay for our guests.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated just 11 km from both the Blue Mosque and Suleymaniye Mosque, Hotel Kuk offers easy access to Istanbul's popular landmarks. With the Istanbul Airport only 42 km away, guests can conveniently reach our hotel and start exploring the city.Experience luxury and comfort at Hotel Kuk in Istanbul. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
