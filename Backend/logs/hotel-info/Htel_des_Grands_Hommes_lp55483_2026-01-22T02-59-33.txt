HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortLocated in an 18th-century building opposite the Panthéon, the Hôtel des Grands Hommes Panthéon offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern comfort.Luxurious AmenitiesEach air-conditioned guest room features cable TV, free Wi-Fi, and a minibar. The soundproofed rooms boast Empire-style décor, with some offering a balcony for a delightful view.Convenient ServicesWith a 24-hour reception, tour desk, and ticket service, guests can easily explore the city. Enjoy a breakfast buffet each morning and unwind with a drink at the hotel bar. Meeting facilities are also available on site.Book your stay today at the Hôtel des Grands Hommes Panthéon and experience the perfect combination of luxury and convenience.
