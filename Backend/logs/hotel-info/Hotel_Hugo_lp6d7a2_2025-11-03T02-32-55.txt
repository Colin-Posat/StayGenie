HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsDiscover a blend of comfort and style at Hotel Hugo, where each room features Italian glass-tiled bathrooms, complimentary WiFi, and stunning views of the Hudson River.Rooftop Bar and DiningIndulge in a cocktail at the chic rooftop bar or treat your taste buds to farm-to-table American cuisine at Café Hugo, all while enjoying panoramic views of the city skyline.Convenient LocationLocated in the trendy Hudson Square neighborhood, Hotel Hugo offers easy access to popular attractions like Times Square, Central Park, and Broadway, making it the perfect choice for your Manhattan getaway.Experience the allure of Hotel Hugo and book your stay now for an unforgettable New York City experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Hotel Hugo in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Facility Fee which is charged per room per night includes:

- WiFi throughout the hotel and guestrooms on unlimited devices
- Water bottle per guest per day
- Coffee and tea with Keurig
- 24-hour access to gym
- 24-hour access to business centre
- Access to rooftop bar Bar Hugo
- 24-hour luggage storage

When travelling with pets, please note that an extra charge of 50USD per pet, per stay applies. Please note that a maximum of 1 pet is allowed per room .Please note that the property can only allow pets with a maximum weight of 75lbs and only dogs are allowed.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but some reviews also highlighted cleanliness issues in certain rooms.
• Service: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on service, with some guests appreciating helpful staff while others experienced slow service and lack of hospitality.
• Location: 8.2/10 - High praise for the central location and proximity to key attractions, although some guests noted noise and crowding issues.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Comfortable beds and quirky room designs were well-received, with minor complaints about room maintenance and amenities.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities, with guests enjoying the rooftop bar and coffee shop, but some mentioned noise and overcrowding in common areas.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feelings on pricing, with some guests feeling the value was adequate for the location, while others found it too high for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and dining options, with some guests suggesting room for improvement in variety and service speed.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Overall, guests had a mostly positive experience, praising the location and comfortable beds, but noting service, noise, and safety concerns.
