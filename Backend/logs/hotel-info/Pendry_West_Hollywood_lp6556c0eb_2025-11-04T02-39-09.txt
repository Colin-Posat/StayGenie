HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationPendry West Hollywood, a 5-star hotel in Los Angeles, offers elegant rooms with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and private bathrooms. Some rooms even feature balconies with stunning mountain views.Gourmet DiningIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's restaurant, serving a diverse menu of American, Mediterranean, and Asian cuisine. Special dietary requests are accommodated with vegetarian, dairy-free, and vegan options available.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by the outdoor swimming pool or on the sun terrace, stay active at the fitness centre, and enjoy the convenience of private parking and free WiFi. With a prime location near top attractions like Dolby Theater and Capitol Records Building, Pendry West Hollywood is the perfect choice for a luxurious stay in LA.To experience luxury and comfort at Pendry West Hollywood, book your stay now!
