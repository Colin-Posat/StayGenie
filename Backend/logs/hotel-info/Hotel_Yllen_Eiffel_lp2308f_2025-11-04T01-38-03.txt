HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated a short stroll from the iconic Eiffel Tower, Yllen Eiffel offers a prime location in Paris' vibrant 15th arrondissement. The nearby Volontaires Metro Station ensures easy access to the Porte de Versailles Exhibition Centre, making it ideal for both leisure and business travelers.Modern ComfortsEach air-conditioned room at Yllen Eiffel boasts modern amenities such as an LCD TV, minibar, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Guests can start their day with a continental breakfast and enjoy the convenience of in-room tea/coffee facilities. The 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and dry cleaning service add to the overall comfort of the stay.Exploration Made EasyExperience the best of Paris with local sights like Sacré Coeur and Champs de Mars within easy reach from the hotel. Convenient access to transportation options right outside the hotel allows for seamless exploration of the city's iconic landmarks. Book your stay at Yllen Eiffel now for a memorable Parisian getaway.
