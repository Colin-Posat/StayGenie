Discover your perfect getaway at Hotel Borgovico, where stunning waterfront views and modern amenities await.Convenient LocationNestled just minutes from top attractions like Piazza Cavour and the Como-Brunate Funicular, you’ll find yourself in the heart of Como’s vibrant culture and scenic landscapes.Comfort &amp; EntertainmentEnjoy a restful night in one of our 11 well-appointed guestrooms featuring Smart TVs and complimentary Wi-Fi. Our bathrooms come equipped with stylish showers and premium toiletries for your comfort.Delicious Dining OptionsSavor delectable dishes at our on-site coffee shop/café and unwind with a refreshing drink at the bar/lounge.
