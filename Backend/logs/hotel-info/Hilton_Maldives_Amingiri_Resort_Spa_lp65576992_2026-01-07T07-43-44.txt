HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Beachfront AccommodationLocated in the serene North Malé Atoll, Hilton Maldives Amingiri Resort &amp; Spa offers a 5-star experience with a private beach area, kids' club, and stunning sea views. The resort boasts spacious rooms equipped with modern amenities, including a private terrace with pool view and a safety deposit box for your convenience.Diverse Dining Options and Recreational ActivitiesIndulge in a continental or buffet breakfast at the resort and savor exquisite meals at the on-site restaurants. Unwind at the sauna, let your kids have fun at the playground, or explore the underwater world through snorkeling. With an outdoor swimming pool and various activities, there's something for everyone to enjoy.Convenient Premium Speedboat Transfers and Special EventsEasily accessible via a 20-minute speedboat ride from Velana International Airport, the resort offers seamless transfers for guests. Plan your stay around special events like the Christmas Eve Dinner and New Year’s Eve Gala Dinner to add a touch of celebration to your holiday experience. Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hilton Maldives Amingiri Resort &amp; Spa.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The property can be reached by a private and a shared speedboat.

Private Speedboat Transfers: 
From August 1, 2025 to December 31, 2026 the charges are as follows:
- Adult: USD 3860 per boat per way, with a maximum of 8 people.
- Child: USD 3860 per boat per way, with a maximum of 8 people. This rate is not included in the booking price and will be charged additionally.

Shared Speedboat Transfers: 
From August 1, 2025 to December 31, 2026 the charges are as follows:
- Adult (13 years and above): USD 386.
- Child (6–12 years): USD 193 per child. This rate is not included in the booking price and will be charged additionally.

The resort is accessible by a 20-minute speedboat ride from Velana International Airport. A shared premium speedboat transfer is arranged for all our guests according to their international flight schedule (arrival/departure time) and is available 24/7. Third-party transport arrangements are not permitted.

Compulsory Supplements:

- Christmas Eve Dinner: USD 400 per adult and USD 200 per child for stays passing over December 24, 2025.

- New Year’s Eve Gala Dinner: USD 600 per adult and USD 300 per child for stays passing over December 31, 2025.

All prices are exclusive of applicable taxes and service charges.

For any further details, please contact the hotel team at MLEAI_RES@hilton.com
