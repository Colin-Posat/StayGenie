HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Stay in the Heart of ParisPrime Location: Nestled in the Latin Quarter, a stone's throw away from Notre Dame Cathedral, Hotel Parc Saint Severin offers a convenient and picturesque setting for your Parisian getaway.Elegant Accommodations: Immerse yourself in luxury in our soundproofed, non-smoking rooms featuring modern amenities such as a minibar, flat-screen TV, and hot drink facilities. The tasteful decor with period furnishings adds a touch of sophistication to your stay, with some rooms even boasting private terraces.Convenient Amenities: Start your day with a delightful buffet breakfast and catch up on the latest news with complimentary newspapers. With a 24-hour reception and free Wi-Fi, we ensure a seamless and comfortable stay for all our guests.Book now for an unforgettable experience at Hotel Parc Saint Severin.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The photo identification and the credit card used to make the reservation are required upon check-in. The name on the photo ID and the credit card must match. Otherwise, the credit card will be re-credited and payment will be required directly at the reception desk.

Please note that the hotel may proceed to a pre-authorization on your credit card to ensure sufficient funds are available to cover the charges for your stay at the time of booking.

Please note that any reservation of more than 3 rooms for the same dates will not be accepted with individual conditions. Special reservation and cancelations policies will apply.

Children aged below 18 years must be accompanied by an adult at all times.
