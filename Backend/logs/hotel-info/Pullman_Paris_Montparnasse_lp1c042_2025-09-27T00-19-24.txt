Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.9/10 - Cleanliness was frequently praised, indicating well-maintained rooms and facilities.
• Service: 7.6/10 - Service received mixed feedback. While many guests found the staff friendly and helpful, a few had negative experiences with service.
• Location: 7.3/10 - The location was appreciated for its proximity to attractions and public transport but was marked down due to being far from the city center for some guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests enjoyed the modern and well-furnished rooms, although there were comments about a lack of privacy in family bookings.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Amenities were generally seen as adequate, with special mention of breakfast and the rooftop bar.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Opinions on value were divided, with some considering the hotel expensive, especially the breakfast and food options.
• Food and Beverage: 8.1/10 - Breakfast was often highlighted as excellent, despite some opinions of it being overpriced.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - The consensus on overall experience was very positive, with many guests willing to recommend the hotel.
