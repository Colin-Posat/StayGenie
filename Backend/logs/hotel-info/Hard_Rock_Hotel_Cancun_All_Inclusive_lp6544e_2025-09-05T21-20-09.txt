GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Helpful restaurant and pool staff
• Good ocean view from the pool area
• Comfortable beds

Areas for Improvement:
• Cleanliness issues including mold and dirty towels
• Poor service and lack of professionalism, especially during the wedding
• Inconsistent room quality
• Mistreatment of guests and lack of responsiveness to complaints

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3.7/10 - Guests expressed significant dissatisfaction with the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas, citing issues such as mold, dirty towels, and neglect.
• Service: 3.7/10 - Service was a major point of complaint, with guests feeling unwelcome and inconvenienced by staff interactions. Wedding services were particularly disappointing, with multiple failures in planning and professionalism.
• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's location was positively reviewed, being close to the beach and offering a great ocean view from the pool area.
• Room Quality: 5/10 - Rooms were a mixed bag, with some guests praising comfort and others mentioning issues like mold and stains in towels.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with highlights on restaurant and pool staff, but low marks for cleanliness and maintenance.
• Value for Money: 5/10 - Guests felt divided on the value for money, with some considering the experience far below expectations given the high cost of the wedding, while others found positives in the food, fun, and staff interactions.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - The food and beverage services received positive feedback, with standout staff members mentioned for their exceptional service.
• Overall Experience: 3.7/10 - The overall sentiment was negative, with guests expressing disappointment, feeling mistreated, and vowing not to return to the property.
