HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsGallery Hotel, nestled near Barcelona’s Passeig de Gracia, offers elegantly designed rooms equipped with modern amenities, including free Wi-Fi and complimentary water. The air-conditioned rooms feature flat-screen TVs and lavish bathrooms with premium toiletries.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind at the free sauna and gym, or bask in the sun at the seasonal rooftop terrace boasting a refreshing pool and stunning city vistas. The perfect retreat after a day of exploring the vibrant city.Dining DelightsIndulge in Mediterranean cuisine at the hotel's charming restaurant with a garden terrace, sip on cocktails at the bar, or grab a coffee at the café. A culinary journey awaits at Gallery Hotel.Book your stay now at Gallery Hotel for a truly unforgettable Barcelona experience.
