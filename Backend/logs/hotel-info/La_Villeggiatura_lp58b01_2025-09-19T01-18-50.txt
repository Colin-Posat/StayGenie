HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building in the Heart of VeniceLa Villeggiatura, a charming hotel nestled in a historic building, offers elegant rooms with classic furnishings and free Wi-Fi. Enjoy stunning views of Venice from your air-conditioned room equipped with modern amenities. Indulge in a delicious continental breakfast served daily and unwind in the shared lounge.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsJust a short walk from Grand Canal and Rialto Bridge, La Villeggiatura is ideally situated for exploring Venice. Visit the iconic Piazza San Marco located only 1 km away, or take a leisurely stroll to the Santa Lucia Train Station. With the Rialto Mercato water taxi stop just 300 meters from the hotel, transportation is a breeze.Book Your Stay at La Villeggiatura TodayImmerse yourself in the beauty and history of Venice by booking your stay at La Villeggiatura. Experience comfort, convenience, and exceptional service during your visit to this enchanting city.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 11 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
