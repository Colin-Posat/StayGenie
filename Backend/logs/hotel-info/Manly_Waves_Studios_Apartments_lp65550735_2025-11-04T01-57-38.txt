HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  AUD 20 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for AUD 30 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for AUD 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 100 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Location: 8.4/10 - Highly praised location close to key amenities, beach, restaurants, and public transport, making it convenient for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on room quality, with feedback on issues like outdated decor, uneven floors, and uncomfortable bedding.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Satisfactory amenities including air conditioning, heating, and basic essentials, although reports of limited services like daily housekeeping and spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Mixed reviews on value, with some guests feeling the price was not justified by the services provided, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, although some guests desired more variety and better communication on included meals.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing friendly staff, great location, and good value for money, despite some room quality and cleanliness concerns.
