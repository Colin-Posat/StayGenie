Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, although there were isolated reports of minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.6/10 - Service was praised for being friendly and accommodating, with only a few instances of less satisfactory interactions reported.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location received positive feedback for its proximity to public transportation and nearby amenities, although some guests noted a slightly longer walk to the metro station.
• Room Quality: 8.4/10 - Guests were satisfied with the room quality, highlighting spaciousness, comfortable beds, and modern bathrooms. Some remarks about minor furniture wear and tear were noted.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - The amenities were generally well-received, with positive mentions of the breakfast, self-check-in process, and cleanliness. Some guests noted the absence of certain amenities, such as a gym or sufficient dining options around the hotel.
• Value for Money: 8/10 - Overall, guests felt they received good value for money, especially appreciating the competitive rates, comfortable accommodations, and convenient location. Some guests, however, commented on the price relative to the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Guests enjoyed the breakfast and overall dining experience, although suggestions for more variety were mentioned. Some guests noted limitations in dining options around the hotel.
• Overall Experience: 8.8/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience, praising various aspects of the hotel such as friendly staff, comfortable rooms, convenient location, and good value for money. Few negative experiences were reported.
