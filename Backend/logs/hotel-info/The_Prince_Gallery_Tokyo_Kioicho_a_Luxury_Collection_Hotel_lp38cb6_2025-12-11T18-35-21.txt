HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Prince Gallery Tokyo Kioicho offers a sophisticated stay just minutes away from Akasaka-Mitsuke Station. Each room is elegantly furnished with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, minibar, and luxurious en suite bathroom, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing experience for every guest.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's sake bar, sushi restaurant, and teppanyaki restaurant. For more dining options, explore the variety of restaurants just a short walk away. Whether you're craving traditional Japanese cuisine or international flavors, there's something to satisfy every palate.Wellness and ConvenienceUnwind at the spa and sauna, stay active at the fitness centre, or take advantage of the 24-hour front desk and currency exchange services. With free WiFi throughout the property and laundry facilities available, The Prince Gallery Tokyo Kioicho ensures a seamless and enjoyable stay for all guests.Book your luxurious stay at The Prince Gallery Tokyo Kioicho now and experience the epitome of hospitality and comfort in the heart of Tokyo.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
For the use of spa facilities (lockers, bath, and sauna), an additional fee of JPY 3,300 per person per night will apply. Please note that access is free for the guests staying in Club, Grand Deluxe, and Suite rooms.

Children aged 13 and above can only use the swimming pool, and Children aged 16 and above can only use the fitness gym, the bath and the locker. Please note that children aged 18 and below can only use the mentioned facilities under adult supervision.

Please note that the hotel will undergo an annual inspection on the following dates and times: 
- 8 January 2026, 02:00 to 06:00 
- 19 January 2026, 01:00 to 05:00
During this period, In-room electrical and lighting equipment, elevators, etc., will not be available for use. Please contact the hotel for more details.Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
