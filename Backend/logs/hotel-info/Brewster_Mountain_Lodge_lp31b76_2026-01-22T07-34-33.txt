HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Western DecorExperience the rustic charm of Brewster Mountain Lodge with its log furnishings, western decor, and historic Brewster Family photos. Each room offers modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, coffee machine, and free WiFi.Convenient FacilitiesEnjoy the convenience of a 24-hour front desk, tour desk, and on-site shops at Brewster Mountain Lodge. Ski storage is available for winter enthusiasts, while the daily continental breakfast ensures a great start to your day.Prime LocationLocated in downtown Banff, our hotel is within walking distance to popular attractions like The Whyte Museum and Banff Park Museum. Explore the beauty of the Canadian Rockies with ease during your stay.Book your stay at Brewster Mountain Lodge now to enjoy a perfect blend of comfort and adventure!
