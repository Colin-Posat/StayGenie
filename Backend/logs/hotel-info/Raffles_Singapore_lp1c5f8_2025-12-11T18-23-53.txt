HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Oriental TouchesExperience the elegance of spacious suites at Raffles Singapore, featuring fine oriental carpets and teak wood flooring. Enjoy modern amenities like free WiFi, verandah, and a separate dining area for a comfortable stay.Wellness and Butler ServiceIndulge in a range of wellness activities including a spa, fitness center, and swimming pool. Benefit from 24-hour butler service ensuring all your needs are met promptly and efficiently by our friendly and multilingual staff.Culinary Delights and Historic CharmSip on the iconic Singapore Sling at the historic Long Bar or savor North Indian cuisine at Tiffin Room. Immerse yourself in the hotel's rich history with a guided historical tour and don't forget to explore The Raffles Gift Shop for souvenirs.Book your stay at Raffles Singapore for an unforgettable blend of luxury, history, and impeccable service.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
