HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated in the heart of Anaheim-Orange business and entertainment districts, Best Western Plus Meridian Inn &amp; Suites Anaheim-Orange offers easy access to popular attractions like Honda Center, Disneyland Park, and Angel Stadium.Amenities GaloreGuests can enjoy the outdoor pool, sun terrace, fitness centre, courtyard, pool and spa, and business centre. The 24-hour front desk with a ticket desk and laundry facilities ensure a comfortable stay for all visitors.Traveler-Friendly ServicesStart your day right with a complimentary breakfast bag including a granola bar and water. With the Anaheim Resort Transit stopping at the hotel and John Wayne Airport nearby, convenience is key for travelers.Book your stay at Best Western Plus Meridian Inn &amp; Suites Anaheim-Orange for a memorable and hassle-free experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
For all pre-paid reservations: The credit card used for initial payment must be presented at check-in. The hotel only accepts credit cards with a chip but does not accept credit card authorization forms. Cash is not accepted.

Debit Cards: When using a debit card for the deposit/incidental, the card presented will be authorized for a minimum of 250 USD.

Please note that pool and spa are currently closed for maintenance. We expect that these facilities will be available again by March 4, 2022.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
