HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 19th-Century BuildingExperience the charm of the 19th-century building at The Artemide located on the bustling Via Nazionale. Indulge in complimentary minibar treats and stay connected with free Wi-Fi in each elegant room.Artemis Spa RetreatRelax and rejuvenate at the Artemis Spa with a sauna, hot tub, Turkish bath, and a range of pampering treatments available for booking during your stay.Gourmet Dining and Rooftop ViewsStart your day with a lavish buffet breakfast at Artemide and unwind in the lounge bar with plush leather armchairs. For a breathtaking dining experience, head to the Ambrosia Rooftop Restaurant &amp; Bar for panoramic views of Rome.Book now to immerse yourself in luxury at Hotel Artemide, conveniently located near Repubblica Metro Station for easy access to Rome's iconic landmarks and airport shuttle services.
