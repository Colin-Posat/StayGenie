HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesIstanbul Marriott Hotel Asia, a 5-star gem in Atasehir District, boasts an array of luxurious amenities. From outdoor and indoor pools to a spa with a hot tub and sauna, guests are pampered throughout their stay.Family-Friendly FeaturesFamilies are well taken care of with a kid's pool, playground, and kids' club service. Parents can unwind on sun loungers by the pool while the little ones are entertained, creating a perfect balance of relaxation and fun for all.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's diverse dining options. From authentic Turkish cuisine at Orange Southern Mediterranean Grill to light bites at 49 East Lounge, there's something to satisfy every palate.Experience luxury, family fun, and exquisite dining at Istanbul Marriott Hotel Asia. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 12 years old to 16 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
