GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Clean and comfortable
• Helpful and friendly staff
• Convenient airport location
• Great airport shuttle service

Areas for Improvement:
• Overpriced or miscommunicated breakfast costs
• Lack of nearby restaurant options
• Room maintenance issues
• Dated decor
• Unpleasant area
• Slow refund process

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Rooms were frequently described as clean, though some reviews mentioned concerns over older buildings and bathroom conditions.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service received praise for being friendly and helpful, although some negative experiences with staff and slow refund processes were noted.
• Location: 7.6/10 - Many guests found the location convenient for airport access; however, some had concerns regarding the surrounding area and the desire for more nearby dining options.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Guests were generally pleased with room comfort but mentioned dated decor and some maintenance issues like broken air conditioners.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Guests appreciated the airport shuttle service but were disappointed by non-working ice machines and the lack of included breakfast.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Mixed feedback on value, with some guests citing overpriced breakfast and issues that made the stay less cost-effective.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - The quality of food received praise, but being overpriced and not included as expected was a recurring complaint.
• Overall Experience: 7.3/10 - While there were overarching concerns about certain aspects, many guests had a pleasant stay and found the hotel satisfactory.
