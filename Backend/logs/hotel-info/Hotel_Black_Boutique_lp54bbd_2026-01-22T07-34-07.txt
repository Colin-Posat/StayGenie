HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with City ViewsExperience the vibrant city of Mexico City from the comfort of your room at Hotel Black Mexico City. Enjoy modern amenities including free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and air-conditioning. Each room offers stunning views of the city, providing the perfect backdrop for your stay.Dining and Business FacilitiesIndulge in delicious Mexican cuisine at the hotel's restaurant or unwind with a cocktail at the snack bar. For business travelers, take advantage of the on-site business center, fax machine, and photocopier. The attentive staff is also available to provide local area tips and assistance.Convenient LocationLocated just 1.1 km from the United States Embassy, Hotel Black Mexico City is ideally situated for both leisure and business travelers. Explore nearby attractions such as The Angel of Independence and Museo de Memoria y Tolerancia. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Mexico City!
