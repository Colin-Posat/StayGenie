HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the perfect blend of comfort and modern elegance at Mercure Olbia, just a stone's throw from the stunning port of Olbia.Stylish AccommodationOur modern rooms are designed for your relaxation, featuring air conditioning, satellite flat-screen TVs, and well-stocked minibars. Each room offers a private bathroom equipped with a shower and hairdryer, ensuring a comfortable stay.Wellness and FitnessIndulge in our spa, complete with an indoor pool to unwind after a day of exploring. Keep active in our complimentary fitness area, or enhance your experience with a wellness session purchased on-site.Culinary DelightsSavor a delightful buffet breakfast featuring both sweet and savory options, including local cold cuts and cheeses. Unwind at the Café Bistrot offering a variety of drinks and snacks to complement your stay.Book your escape to Mercure Olbia today and experience unparalleled hospitality.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Overall, guests appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, although some reviews mentioned issues like stale bread and unclean towels.
• Service: 7.9/10 - The majority of guests found the service to be friendly and accommodating, but there were occasional complaints about front desk service and wait times.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and amenities was praised by guests, despite a few negative comments about the surrounding area.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the spacious and comfortable rooms, with some highlighting maintenance issues and old furnishings.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - The amenities provided, including the pool and breakfast, were positively received, though the lack of spa services was mentioned as a drawback.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others deemed it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The quality of breakfast and restaurant services received generally positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety and options like gluten-free or vegetable drinks.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some specific complaints and areas for improvement.
