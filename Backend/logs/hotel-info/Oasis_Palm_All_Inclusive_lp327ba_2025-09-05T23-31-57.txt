Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 4.3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness issues as a major concern, with multiple complaints about mold and dirty common areas.
• Service: 6.3/10 - Mixed reviews on service, with mentions of rude staff, slow check-in process, and lack of responsiveness from management to guest concerns.
• Location: 7.6/10 - The location was generally well-received for its proximity to the beach and nice view of Cancun, but some guests found it too crowded.
• Room Quality: 5/10 - Rooms were criticized for being dirty, old, and having maintenance issues such as stained bedding and moldy smells.
• Amenities: 5.5/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests mentioning the lack of air conditioning in certain areas and limited food options.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Opinions varied on value for money, with some guests feeling it was a decent option for the price, while others felt disappointed with the quality of services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 5.3/10 - Guests had concerns about the quality of food, cleanliness of buffet areas, and reported instances of getting sick after consuming meals.
• Overall Experience: 5.5/10 - Many guests had negative experiences related to cleanliness, service, and room quality, leading to early checkouts and dissatisfaction with customer care.
