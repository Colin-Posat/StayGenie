HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Ottoman MansionHotel Tulip House, a historic Ottoman mansion in the heart of old Istanbul, offers a unique stay experience. Each room boasts original architectural features and ebru artworks, reflecting the city's rich heritage.Charming AmenitiesGuests can start their day with a delightful buffet breakfast on the shaded terrace and unwind in the evening at the cozy backyard with a cup of tea. The hotel's proximity to various dining options ensures a culinary adventure just steps away.Convenient ServicesFrom dry cleaning and ironing services to a helpful tour desk for local insights, Hotel Tulip House caters to every guest's needs. The 24-hour front desk and easy access to transportation hubs make exploring Istanbul a breeze.Experience the allure of old Istanbul at Hotel Tulip House. Book your stay now for an unforgettable journey.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests mentioning issues like noise coming from ventilation and broken shower wall mount.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally praised for being friendly and helpful, although a few guests had complaints about rude receptionists.
• Location: 8.6/10 - Highly praised location near popular landmarks, although some guests found the area congested and lacking parking space.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated comfortable beds and clean rooms, but there were mentions of maintenance issues like broken parts in showers.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - Satisfactory amenities, with mentions of free tea and coffee, but some guests highlighted the lack of certain facilities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 5.5/10 - Mixed feedback on pricing, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the services offered and poor value for money.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Generally positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some suggesting more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff and location despite some minor issues in other categories.
