HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at The Newbury Boston, your ideal escape in the heart of the city.Unmatched ComfortRelax in one of our 286 exquisitely designed guestrooms, featuring down comforters, Frette Italian sheets, and minibars for your indulgence. Experience leisure like never before with designer toiletries in luxurious private bathrooms.Exceptional AmenitiesStay active at our 24-hour fitness center or unwind in our picturesque garden. Enjoy a delightful dining experience at our on-site restaurant, followed by a refreshing drink at the lively bar/lounge.Prime LocationSituated just steps from Newbury Street and a short walk from Boston Common, embrace the vibrancy of the city with easy access to shopping, dining, and iconic landmarks.Elevate your Boston experience—book your stay at The Newbury Boston today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Up to 2 children 12 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* Guests can arrange to bring pets by contacting the property directly, using the contact information on the booking confirmation (surcharges apply and can be found in the Fees section).
* Contactless check-in and contactless check-out are available.
