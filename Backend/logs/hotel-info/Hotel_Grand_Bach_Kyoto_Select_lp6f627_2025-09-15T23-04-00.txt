HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodations and Convenient LocationHotel Grand Bach Kyoto Select, open since April 2014, offers contemporary rooms just a short stroll from Kawaramachi Train Station. With free Wi-Fi throughout the property and spacious public baths, guests can unwind effortlessly. The air-conditioned rooms are well-equipped with a flat-screen TV, fridge, and complimentary green tea.Convenience and ServicesGuests can enjoy the convenience of laundry services, luggage storage, and a restaurant serving Japanese and Western set menus. The hotel's proximity to Shijo Station and various attractions like Kiyomizu Temple and Nijo Castle makes it an ideal choice for travelers.Relaxation and Culinary DelightsIndulge in a relaxing stay with provided slippers and the option to book rooms with a shower booth. Don't miss out on the delicious breakfast available for an additional cost. Book now for a delightful stay at Hotel Grand Bach Kyoto Select.
