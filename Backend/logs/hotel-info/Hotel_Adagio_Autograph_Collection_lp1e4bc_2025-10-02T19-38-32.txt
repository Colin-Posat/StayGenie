CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast

• Safety concerns in the surrounding area at night

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally clean, but some guests reported issues with dirt and lack of cleanliness.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Guests appreciated the helpful and friendly staff, although some mentioned issues with quick response times and lack of instructions.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and transportation options was well-received, but some guests were concerned about the surrounding area and safety at night.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally spacious and comfortable, with some complaints about maintenance issues and lack of amenities like ventilation.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While guests appreciated amenities like breakfast and the restaurant, some were disappointed by additional charges for WiFi and the lack of in-room tea and coffee facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions were mixed regarding the value for money, with some feeling the prices were somewhat high for the services offered while others found it reasonable considering the included benefits.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, although some felt there could be more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing the helpful staff, comfortable rooms, and convenient location as highlights.
