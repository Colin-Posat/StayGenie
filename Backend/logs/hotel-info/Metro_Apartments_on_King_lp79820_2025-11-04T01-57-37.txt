Please inform Metro Apartments on King in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Check in for this property is found across the road at the Metro Apartments on Darling Harbour, 132-136 Sussex St Sydney.

Please note that there are approximately 12 steep stairs leading into the property. If you require assistance with your luggage, please do not hesitate to ask at reception.

If you expect to arrive outside reception opening hours, please inform Metro Apartments on King in advance, using the contact details found on the booking confirmation.

Please note that there is a 2.5% charge when you pay with an American Express or Diners Club credit card and a 1.5% charge when you pay with Visa and Mastercard credit cards.

Metro Hotels complies with all Government restrictions and public health orders. Metro Hotels reserves the right to refuse entry to its properties in accordance with current Government-mandated public health orders at the time of guest check-in, which may include (but not limited to) the wearing of face masks, entry screening, and contact tracing. For the latest, please visit the NSW Government website.

Upon check-in, a $100 credit card authorisation or $200 cash security bond is needed for potential incidental expenses. At check-out, if no charges are outstanding, the credit card authorisation will be reversed (which may take up to 14 days for the bank to process), or the $200 cash security deposit will be fully refunded.
