HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Boutique Hotel with 18th Century CharmUniquely Decorated Rooms: The Morris House Hotel offers charming rooms with views of a private garden or the city, accessible by stairs only. Free WiFi is included for guests' convenience.Complimentary Dining Experience: Guests can start their day with a delicious continental breakfast and indulge in afternoon tea served with homemade cookies, all included during their stay.Prime Location: Situated near Philadelphia's main attractions, such as Independence Hall and the Liberty Bell, guests can easily explore the city's iconic landmarks and enjoy nearby restaurants, shops, and theaters.Book your stay at the Morris House Hotel for a historic and luxurious experience in the heart of Philadelphia.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Morris House Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note the main entrance is located off St. James Street. Once guests check in they will receive a key to the 8th Street entrance.

Please note, this property does not have an elevator, and rooms are only accessible via stairs.

People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
