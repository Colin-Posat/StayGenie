GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Clean rooms
• Spacious accommodations
• Friendly staff

Areas for Improvement:
• Quality of some staff members
• Breakfast variety
• Resort fees and parking charges

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests complimented the overall cleanliness of the hotel, with some mentioning the need for carpet maintenance.
• Service: 7.3/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests praising the promptness of housekeeping and accommodating staff, while others noted issues with inexperienced front desk staff.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's proximity to Disneyland, shopping areas, and restaurants was highly appreciated by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests found the rooms spacious and comfortable, although there were mentions of thin mattresses on sofa beds.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with praises for the water park, pool, and hot tub, but complaints about malfunctioning waffle machines and food quality at the bar.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Conflicting opinions on the value for money, with some guests feeling the resort fees and parking charges were excessive, while others appreciated the proximity to attractions.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed reviews, with complaints about breakfast variety, long wait times at the bar, and issues with ordered food quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - While there were some issues reported, most guests had a positive overall experience and would consider staying at the hotel again.
