HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at Grand Prince Hotel Takanawa, nestled in the heart of Tokyo.Recreational Amenities: Dive into relaxation with our seasonal outdoor pool or energize yourself at our 24-hour fitness center. Whatever your fitness needs, we’ve got you covered!Dining Delights: Savor delectable French cuisine at Le Trianon, along with two other outstanding restaurants and a cozy coffee shop. Whether you're looking to indulge or just grab a quick bite, our dining options are sure to please.Business and Event Facilities: Planning a corporate event? With 25,801 square feet of versatile conference space, our hotel is equipped to host meetings and gatherings of all sizes with top-notch amenities.Unlock the gateway to an unforgettable stay in Tokyo—book your room at Grand Prince Hotel Takanawa today!

* The seasonal pool will be open from July 20 to September 01.

Additionally, lodging proprietors are required to photocopy passports for all registering guests and keep the photocopy on file.
