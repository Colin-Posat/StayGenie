HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Heated Indoor Pool and Hot Tubs:Enjoy a relaxing stay at our Lake Louise hotel with a heated indoor pool and hot tubs, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventure in the mountains.On-Site Dining Options:Indulge in delicious meals at our two on-site restaurants - Legends Restaurant for all-day dining and Timberwolf Pizza and Pasta Café for a cozy family experience. Don't forget to visit Explorers Lounge for après ski delights.Convenient Amenities:With free shuttle service to Lake Louise Ski Resort, free WiFi, and ski storage/tune-up center, we ensure a seamless stay. Our suites and apartments with balconies and fireplaces offer stunning mountain views.Book your stay at Lake Louise Inn now for a memorable mountain getaway.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.1/10 - Guests mentioned the hotel is well-kept and tidy, but some rooms were slightly dated.
• Service: 5.9/10 - Mixed feedback on service; staff were helpful and friendly, but some guests experienced delays and unhelpful behavior.
• Location: 8.2/10 - Most guests appreciated the convenient location close to key landmarks and transport options.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Rooms were described as basic with minimal amenities, some experiencing maintenance issues like noisy aspirateur and small kitchens.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - The hotel offered average amenities with highlights on free bike rentals, pool, and hot tub, but lacked certain services like air conditioning and spa.
• Value for Money: 5.6/10 - Several guests found the accommodation overpriced for the basic offerings, especially considering additional charges like resort fees.
• Food and Beverage: 6.85/10 - Food options on site received mixed feedback, with some praising breakfast while others criticized the quality of the restaurants.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the location, staff kindness, and the convenience of onsite facilities.
