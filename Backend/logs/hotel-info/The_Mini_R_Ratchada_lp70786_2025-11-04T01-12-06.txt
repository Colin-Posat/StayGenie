HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Free Wi-FiThe Mini R Ratchada offers modern air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, perfect for staying connected during your visit to Bangkok. Each room features a flat-screen cable TV, a minibar, and a safety deposit box, providing all the comforts of home.Convenient LocationLocated just a 10-minute walk from Huaykwang MRT Subway Station, The Mini R Ratchada provides easy access to explore the city. Additionally, being a short drive away from Makkasan Airport Rail Link Station and Central Rama 9 Department Store makes traveling around the city a breeze.Dining Experience at Maxi's PizzeriaGuests can indulge in a culinary experience at Maxi's Pizzeria, offering a delicious selection of Thai and American dishes. Enjoy a satisfying meal without having to leave the comfort of the hotel.Book your stay at The Mini R Ratchada for a memorable and convenient experience in Bangkok.
