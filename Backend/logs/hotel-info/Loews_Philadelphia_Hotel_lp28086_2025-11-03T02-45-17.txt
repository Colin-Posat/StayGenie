HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThis Downtown Historic Philadelphia hotel offers a blend of modern comfort and historic charm. The contemporary rooms with city views, flat-screen TV, and minibar ensure a comfortable stay. The high ceilings and spacious accommodations add a touch of luxury to your experience.Relaxation and DiningIndulge in relaxation at the full-service spa offering massages and body treatments. After a rejuvenating session, savor American cuisine at Bank &amp; Bourbon restaurant and bar. Enjoy unique house-aged cocktails, craft beers, and exceptional wines while taking in the stunning city views.Ideal LocationWith the Philadelphia Convention Centre, City Hall, and iconic landmarks just steps away, Loews Philadelphia Hotel provides the perfect base for exploring the city. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
