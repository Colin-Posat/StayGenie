HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsThe 5-star Hyatt Regency Istanbul Atakoy offers rooms with panoramic views of the city and the Marmara Sea. Each room features modern amenities such as complimentary internet access, flat-panel screen HDTV, and a safe deposit box. The full separate bathtub and walk-in shower provide a touch of luxury for guests.Diverse Dining Options and Relaxation FacilitiesIndulge in the diverse dining options at Brizo Restaurant with Eastern Mediterranean cuisine or VUE Lounge &amp; Bar with ceviche and Latin American tapas. After a delicious meal, guests can unwind at the spa with a fitness center, indoor and outdoor pools, and modern spa treatments at Levana Spa &amp; Fitness.Convenient Location and Access to EntertainmentLocated in the residential area of Atakoy, the hotel is close to the harbour, exhibition centers, and shopping complexes. With Ataturk Airport just 7 km away, guests have easy access to transportation. Book your stay at Hyatt Regency Istanbul Atakoy for a luxurious and convenient experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 43.20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
