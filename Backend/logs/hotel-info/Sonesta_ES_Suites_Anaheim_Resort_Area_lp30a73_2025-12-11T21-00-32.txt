Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.8/10 - Guests had mixed experiences with cleanliness, with some mentioning dirty curtains and bugs in the bed.
• Service: 8/10 - Guests appreciated the friendly and attentive staff, although there were some complaints about parking and language barriers.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks like Disneyland was a highlight, despite some concerns about parking availability.
• Room Quality: 6.5/10 - Guests found the room sizes adequate but mentioned issues with dated appearance, maintenance, and cleanliness.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with mentions of breakfast quality, room service, and the lack of daily maid service.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Some guests felt additional charges for parking and early/late check-in/out impacted the perceived value of the stay.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Breakfast received positive feedback overall, with some suggestions for improvement in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and appreciated specific aspects like the staff, location, and amenities.
