HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsThe George Hotel, overlooking the Dubai Creek, offers spacious rooms with breathtaking views of the city skyline and the historic Deira Heritage areas.Exceptional Dining and Entertainment OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at our all-day dining restaurant and savor delicious dishes at the bar &amp; grill.Convenient Location with Easy Access to AttractionsJust a short walk from the Gold Souk and the Al Ras Metro Station, our hotel provides easy access to Dubai's business and entertainment hubs.Experience luxury and convenience at The George Hotel - book your stay now for an unforgettable Dubai experience!

Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Children breakfast will be subject to extra charges.

Please note that there is an additional 10 AED tourism fee per bedroom per unit per night payable at the hotel directly.

Dear guest, St George Hotel shall call you closer to your check-in date to confirm arrival status and keep the room ready for pay at hotel/ non-prepaid bookings. In case of non-contactability, your booking may be put on hold or cancelled. St George Hotel shall try to reinstate the booking, however, pay at hotel guests are requested to provide St George Hotel an arrival confirmation in case you shall check-in after 6 PM local time.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  AED 50 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for AED 75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
