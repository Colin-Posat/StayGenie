HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Porta de Gallecs is strategically positioned off the AP7 Motorway, just a short drive from the Montmeló Formula 1 Race Track. Ideal for both leisure and business travelers, the hotel's proximity to Barcelona, local dining options, and nearby nature reserves offers a perfect blend of convenience and relaxation.Comfortable AccommodationThe hotel boasts air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities including free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and minibars. Guests can unwind in the outdoor pool or explore the vibrant streets of Mollet town center, all within walking distance from the hotel.Scenic SurroundingsSituated between the Montseny Nature Reserve and the Costa Maresme beaches, Hotel Porta de Gallecs provides a picturesque backdrop for a memorable stay. Whether you seek adventure or tranquility, this hotel offers the best of both worlds.Experience comfort and convenience at Hotel Porta de Gallecs. Book your stay now!
