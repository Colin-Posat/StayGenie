HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Lofts with Fully Equipped KitchenCitadines Fusionopolis Singapore offers stylish lofts with fully equipped kitchens, perfect for guests looking for a comfortable and convenient stay. The modern lofts feature a living room, dining area, and a well-equipped kitchen with a coffee machine and stove, providing the feeling of a home away from home.Convenient Location and AmenitiesConveniently located at the doorstep of one-north MRT Station, guests have easy access to explore the city. The residence is also close to Rochester Park and Holland Village, offering a variety of dining and entertainment options. Additionally, with a supermarket, food court, bistros, and restaurants available in the adjoining complex, guests can enjoy everything they need within reach.Balcony Overlooking the CitySituated from the 17th floor, each loft at Citadines Fusionopolis offers a balcony overlooking the city, providing stunning views and a relaxing atmosphere. Whether guests are traveling for business or leisure, the scenic city views add an extra touch to their stay.Experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Citadines Fusionopolis Singapore. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 129.60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, some guests found it to be well maintained, while others mentioned cleanliness could be improved.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally praised for being friendly and helpful, with a few mentions of exceptional housekeeping.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location above the MRT station was a highlight for many guests, providing convenience and easy access to public transport and eateries.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the comfortable beds, spacious units, and modern decor, although some mentioned that rooms looked tired and in need of refurbishment.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities provided, with some guests praising the convenient meals availability, while others mentioned the lack of breakfast and spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Guests had varied opinions on the value for money, with some finding the price reasonable considering the location, while others felt it was expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant options were generally well-received, with some guests wishing for more variety in the food choices.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some areas for improvement highlighted in the reviews.
