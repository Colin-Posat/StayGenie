HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Decor and Stunning ViewsCa Maria Adele offers uniquely decorated rooms with a blend of Arab, African, and Venetian decor, providing a luxurious and exotic ambiance. Guests can enjoy breathtaking views of Santa Maria della Salute Basilica from the quiet neighborhood in central Venice.Modern Amenities and Relaxing FeaturesEach room at Ca Maria's is equipped with a king-size bed, LCD TV, spa bath or waterfall shower, and free Wi-Fi, ensuring a comfortable stay. The rooftop terrace serves as a perfect spot for breakfast with panoramic views, creating a tranquil setting for guests.Prime Location and Nearby AttractionsConveniently located near Santa Maria della Salute church and the Grand Canal, Ca Maria Adele offers easy access to must-see attractions like Peggy Guggenheim Museum and Accademia Bridge, making it an ideal choice for exploring Venice.Ready to experience luxury and charm in Venice?
