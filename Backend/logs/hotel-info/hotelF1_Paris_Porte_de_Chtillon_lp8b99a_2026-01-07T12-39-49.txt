HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
The hotelF1 Paris Porte de Châtillon offers convenient amenities such as complimentary wireless internet access, a banquet hall, and a vending machine. Continental breakfast is served for a fee. This property has received an official star rating from ATOUT France. Featured amenities include express check-in, express check-out, and a 24-hour front desk. Make yourself at home in one of the 321 air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi. Bathrooms feature showers and hair dryers. Explore nearby attractions like Parc Montsouris and Paris Catacombs. Stay at our hotel for easy access to Paris Expo and Notre-Dame. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Paris!
