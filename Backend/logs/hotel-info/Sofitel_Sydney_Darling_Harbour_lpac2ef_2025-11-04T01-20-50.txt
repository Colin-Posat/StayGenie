HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Iconic Views and World-Class FacilitiesExperience iconic views of the Sydney skyline from the rooftop infinity pool at Sofitel Sydney Darling Harbour. The hotel boasts state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring a truly unique stay for every guest.Convenient Location and Elegant RoomsLocated just a 1-minute walk from the International Convention Centre, the hotel offers easy access to Sydney CBD. Each elegant room features a flat-screen Smart LED TV, free WiFi, and luxurious bathrooms with high-quality finishes.Fine Dining and Wellness ExperienceIndulge in fine dining at the hotel's restaurant or unwind at one of the 3 bars, including the rooftop pool bar. For ultimate relaxation, visit the Sofitel SPA Darling Harbour offering a range of treatments, infrared sauna, and a guest relaxation lounge.Book your stay at Sofitel Sydney Darling Harbour for a luxurious and unforgettable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note, there is a 1.4% credit card surcharge when using a credit card.
Photo ID provided must match personal details on Credit Card. Credit card used at time of reservation must be presented upon check in at the hotel, or check-in will be denied. The accommodation requires a prepayment of the full accommodation and a valid Credit Card on file for incidentals, for a guest to be able to check in to the hotel. The views from the room when booking a Darling Harbour view room vary facing east, north east and north, all with views of the water. Floor allocations will depend on the room type booked. Requests for high floor and bathtubs cannot be guaranteed. Food and beverage operating hours for Atelier Restaurant and Champagne Bar may vary. We recommend booking and checking opening times via our website. Guests booking with breakfast-inclusive rates will receive breakfast for 2 adults and up to 1 child per room. Additional children receive 50% off the adult breakfast price. We are a non-smoking hotel and associated cleaning fee starting from $500 will be applied should the rule not be adhered by. Please note entry to the fitness centre is for registered guests only over the age of 16. Pool water is heated and as the pool is located outdoors the ambient temperature may impact the water temperature during cold days. Pool deck has limited number of sun beds/ seatings and these cannot be held or reserved. Club Millesime dress code during the evening drinks and canapés service is smart casual with appropriate footwear. Thongs/flip-flops and hotel slippers are not permitted. To ensure this standard is upheld, guests may be denied entry if not adhering to dress code. Persons under 18 years old are not permitted in Club Millesime after 5:30pm.
