HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Indonesian InfluenceHotel Jakarta Amsterdam offers 200 luxurious rooms with an Indonesian twist, providing a unique and cultural experience for guests. Most rooms feature a balcony with stunning views over the river IJ and the central station, blending comfort with picturesque scenery.Culinary Delights at Café Jakarta and Sky Bar MalabarIndulge in a culinary journey at Café Jakarta, offering a delightful selection of breakfast, lunch, and dinner options. For a more elevated experience, visit the sky bar Malabar on the 8th and 9th floor, where you can enjoy a refreshing drink while taking in panoramic views of the city.Wellness and Relaxation by the River IJUnwind and rejuvenate at the hotel's wellness center and swimming pool, boasting scenic views of the river IJ. Whether you're looking to relax after a day of exploring or simply pamper yourself, this tranquil setting provides the perfect escape.Experience the perfect blend of luxury, culture, and relaxation at Hotel Jakarta Amsterdam. Book your stay now and immerse yourself in the charm of this sustainable riverside retreat.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of EUR 100 is required. The host charges this 7 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  EUR 40 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for € 50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for € 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
