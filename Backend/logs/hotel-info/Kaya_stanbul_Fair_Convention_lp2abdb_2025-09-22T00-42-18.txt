HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 11 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and spacious, although some guests mentioned issues with ventilation and room amenities.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews about amenities, with some guests enjoying the gym and pool while others noted the absence of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 7.1/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests finding the prices high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, though some guests suggested improvements in variety and value.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
