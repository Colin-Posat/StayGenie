HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location with Easy Access to Rome's AttractionsHotel Antico Acquedotto offers affordable accommodation in a green, residential area near an ancient Roman aqueduct. With excellent transport links just steps away, guests can easily explore Rome's iconic landmarks such as the Basilica of Saint John Lateran and Roma Termini Train Station.Comfortable Rooms with Free Wi-Fi and BalconiesGuests can enjoy a comfortable stay in rooms equipped with free Wi-Fi and some featuring private balconies. The hotel's 3 common terraces provide stunning panoramic views of the city and its historic buildings, perfect for relaxation.Blend of Tranquility and VibrancyExperience the peaceful surroundings of the hotel while being a short walk from the lively Pigneto area, known for its trendy bars and restaurants. Hotel Antico Acquedotto offers the best of both worlds for a memorable stay in Rome.Book your stay at Hotel Antico Acquedotto for a delightful Roman experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

Free public parking is outside the property. Please note that parking spaces cannot be reserved and are subject to availability.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 15 per  stay.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 6.5/10 - The amenities were satisfactory, though there were some mentions of limited services like the absence of spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on the value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was high for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining options were generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
