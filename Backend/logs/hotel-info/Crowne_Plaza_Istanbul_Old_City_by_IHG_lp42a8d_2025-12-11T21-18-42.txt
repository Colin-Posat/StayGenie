HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at Crowne Plaza Istanbul - Old City by IHG, nestled in the heart of Istanbul.Spa and Wellness RetreatRejuvenate your senses with indulgent treatments at our tranquil spa, offering a range of massages, body treatments, and facials. Stay active with our health club, take a refreshing dip in the indoor pool, or unwind in the sauna for a truly relaxing stay.Culinary DelightsSavor exquisite dishes at Casa Mia Cuisine, our on-site restaurant, or grab a quick bite at the coffee shop. Enjoy 24-hour room service and mingle with fellow guests during our complimentary daily receptions.Convenient AmenitiesStay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and indulge in the comfort of our 257 air-conditioned rooms equipped with minibars and LCD TVs. Whether you're here for business or pleasure, our hotel features versatile meeting spaces and a complimentary shuttle service for easy exploration.Book your unforgettable stay today!
