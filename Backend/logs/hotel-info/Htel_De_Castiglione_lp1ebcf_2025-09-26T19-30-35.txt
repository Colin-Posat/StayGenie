HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel De Castiglione, nestled on Rue Faubourg Saint-Honoré, offers elegant rooms and suites with modern amenities and stunning views of Paris. Each room comes with a minibar, work desk, and a private bathroom, some boasting views of the iconic Eiffel Tower.Convenient ServicesEnjoy a delightful breakfast every morning and benefit from the hotel's 24-hour reception. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned issues with cleanliness, including mold in showers and lack of room cleanliness.
• Service: 4.3/10 - Several guests experienced poor service, including delays in room availability, nonexistent room service, and noisy construction work.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location was highlighted as a positive aspect by multiple guests, with convenient access to shopping areas and public transport.
• Room Quality: 4.6/10 - Room quality was generally rated low due to issues such as lack of soundproofing, uncomfortable beds, and poor layout.
• Amenities: 4.3/10 - Amenities were below expectations, with guests mentioning the lack of extra services, poor breakfast variety, and inadequate room facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the hotel did not meet the price paid due to various issues.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Food and beverage options received mixed feedback, with some guests appreciating the restaurant services and others noting the lack of variety in breakfast.
• Overall Experience: 5.6/10 - Overall guest experiences were varied, with several guests expressing disappointment and frustration with their stay.
