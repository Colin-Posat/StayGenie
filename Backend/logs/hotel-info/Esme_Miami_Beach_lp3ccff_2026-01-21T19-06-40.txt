HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the epitome of comfort and style at Esme Hotel in Miami Beach. Each air-conditioned room is tastefully furnished with a desk, free WiFi, a seating area, and a flat-screen TV for your entertainment. The private bathrooms are equipped with modern amenities, ensuring a relaxing stay.Premium AmenitiesUnwind and rejuvenate by taking a dip in our refreshing outdoor swimming pool or enjoy a delicious meal at the on-site restaurant. With a bar and shared lounge, there are plenty of spaces to socialize and relax during your stay. Our 24-hour front desk and room service are always available to cater to your needs.Prime LocationConveniently located near popular attractions like New World Center and Versace Mansion, Esme Hotel offers easy access to the best of Miami Beach. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our hotel is the perfect choice for your stay.Book your stay at Esme Hotel now and indulge in a luxurious experience like no other.
