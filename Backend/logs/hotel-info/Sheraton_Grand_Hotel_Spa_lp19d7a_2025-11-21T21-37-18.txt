HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and AmenitiesThe Sheraton Grand Hotel &amp; Spa offers elegant rooms with free wireless Internet, 24-hour room service, and interactive satellite flat-screen TV. The spacious rooms feature beautiful furnishings, mood lighting, and luxurious bathrooms with a separate bath and walk-in shower.One Spa Wellness RetreatGuests can indulge in the complimentary use of the 19-metre swimming pool, gym, and fitness studios at One Spa. Additionally, they can book various beauty and spa treatments in advance. The Thermal Suite and rooftop Hydropool experiences at Escape at One are must-try features for a relaxing stay.Dining Experience at One Square Bar + BrasserieExperience a modern Scottish menu and a unique Gin Tasting experience at the vibrant One Square Bar + Brasserie. Enjoy a culinary journey in a lively setting overlooking Edinburgh Castle.Book your stay at the Sheraton Grand Hotel &amp; Spa for a luxurious and unforgettable experience.
