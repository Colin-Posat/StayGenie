HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Impressive Interiors and Central LocationThe JW Marriott El Convento Cusco features stunning antique exposed bricks and vaulted ceilings, creating a designer ambiance. Just 3 blocks from Cusco’s main square, the location is ideal for exploring the city's attractions.Charming Colonial Courtyard and On-Site AmenitiesStep into the hotel's Colonial courtyard with a sculpted stone arch and a decorative fountain at its center. Enjoy a massage at the on-site room or browse the souvenir gift shop. Free WiFi is available in common areas and rooms.Elegant Rooms and Dining OptionsIndulge in elegantly styled rooms with parquet floors, cable TV, and minibars. The hotel offers two dining options - QESPI restaurant for Peruvian gourmet dishes and bar QESPI for international cuisine. Plus, don't miss the benefits of the destination fee, including spa vouchers and daily buffet breakfasts.Book your stay at JW Marriott El Convento Cusco for a luxurious and memorable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Destination fee is per night and is not included in the room rate.
Rates include the following:
- 2 daily Buffet Breakfast
- 2 Pisco Sours
- Daily Oxygen in room
 - Daily High Speed Internet access(up to 3 devices)
- USD 25 Spa voucher
- Daily Coca tea
- Access to Press Reader
- Walk-In hotel tour (daily at 6:00pm)
- Pisco Sour Class at Qespi Bar (daily at 6:40pm)

Foreign business travelers who require a printed invoice, will also be charged the additional 18% regardless of the length of their stay in Peru.
