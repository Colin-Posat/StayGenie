HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Junior Suites and Deluxe RoomHotel Al Ponte Dei Sospiri, set on a canal with stunning views of the Bridge of Sighs, offers luxurious junior suites and deluxe rooms, all featuring free WiFi. Each accommodation boasts an 18th-century Venetian décor and unique views, ensuring a truly memorable stay.Hydromassage Baths and ShowerIndulge in luxury with our bathrooms complete with a hydromassage bath or a refreshing shower. Relax and rejuvenate after a day of exploring the charming streets of Venice.Exclusive Water Taxi LandingConveniently located around the corner from Saint Mark’s Square, our hotel provides its own landing for water taxis, offering easy access to explore the city by water. Book your stay at Hotel Al Ponte Dei Sospiri for an unforgettable Venetian experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for € 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
