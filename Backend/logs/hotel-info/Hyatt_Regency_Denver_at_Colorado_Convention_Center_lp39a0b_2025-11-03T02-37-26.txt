HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 10 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  USD 54 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

What Guests Love:
• Great location

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• No spa services

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Most guests mentioned the cleanliness of the hotel as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, although some isolated incidents were reported.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and public transport was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were generally described as spacious and comfortable, with some minor maintenance issues reported.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with particular praise for the gym and pool facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed feedback on the perceived value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some guests suggesting more variety would be appreciated.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
