• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests finding the price high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, though a few guests suggested more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 8.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
