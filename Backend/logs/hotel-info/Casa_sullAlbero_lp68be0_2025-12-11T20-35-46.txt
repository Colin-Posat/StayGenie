HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover a tranquil oasis at Casa sull'Albero, where comfort meets convenience in the heart of Malgrate.Relax and UnwindPamper yourself with soothing onsite massages or take a refreshing dip in our seasonal outdoor pool. The perfect place to recharge after a day of exploration!Culinary DelightsSavor authentic Italian cuisine at Ristorante Da Giovannino, complete with stunning garden views. Enjoy room service or unwind at the bar/lounge with your favorite drink.Convenience at Your FingertipsStay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and enjoy modern comforts in one of our 12 well-appointed guestrooms. We also offer shuttle services to nearby beaches and shopping destinations, ensuring a hassle-free stay.Experience the best of Malgrate and create unforgettable memories—book your stay with us today at Casa sull'Albero!
