HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Modern DesignThe Times Square EDITION offers a cutting-edge take on luxury with 452 stylish guest rooms, a state-of-the-art fitness centre, and a dynamic performance venue.Culinary ExcellenceIndulge in a gastronomic journey with six distinctive dining options curated by renowned Michelin-starred Chef John Fraser. From innovative cuisine to exquisite flavors, every meal is a true culinary experience.Prime LocationSituated just steps away from Times Square and Restaurant Row, this hotel places you at the heart of the vibrant city life. Explore the best of New York City with ease, with LaGuardia Airport conveniently located just 13 km away.Book your stay at The Times Square EDITION now for an unforgettable blend of luxury, dining, and convenience.
