HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated in Paris’ 17th arrondissement, Hôtel des Batignolles is just a short stroll away from Montmartre and The Moulin Rouge, making it the perfect base for exploring the historic district.Comfortable AccommodationThe en suite guestrooms are tastefully furnished and come with modern amenities such as free WiFi, satellite TV, and a cozy work desk. Some rooms even offer charming courtyard views, adding a touch of tranquility to your stay.Relaxing AmenitiesStart your day with a delicious continental breakfast in the cozy breakfast room, unwind in the evening at the lounge, and take advantage of the 24-hour reception.
