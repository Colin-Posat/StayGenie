HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Modern AmenitiesThe Ibis Barcelona Molins de Rei hotel, located just off the A-2 motorway, offers a convenient stay about 13 km from Barcelona. The modern rooms feature flat-screen satellite TVs and wooden floors, with air conditioning or heating as per the season.Dining and ServicesGuests can enjoy the on-site restaurant with a 24-hour snack bar, while the hotel provides 24-hour reception, free WiFi, and meeting rooms. Additionally, complimentary safes are offered at reception for your convenience.Easy Access to City Center and AirportWith Central Barcelona a short 15-minute drive away, and El Prat Airport within 15 km, the hotel's proximity is ideal for both leisure and business travelers. The nearby Molins de Rei Railway Station offers regular services to Barcelona, ensuring hassle-free travel.Book your stay at Ibis Barcelona Molins de Rei for a comfortable and convenient experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note, rooms cannot accommodate both an extra bed and a cot.

Please note that free safes are not available inside the room. Please ask at reception for more information.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 7.50 per  day.
