HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Breathtaking Views of Sydney Harbour: Nestled overlooking the stunning Sydney Harbour and the iconic Harbour Bridge, View Sydney offers a picturesque backdrop for your stay.Luxurious Rooms with Modern Amenities: Relax in air-conditioned rooms equipped with a flat-screen TV, work desk, and tea/coffee making facilities. Upgrade to a room with a view of the Sydney Harbour Bridge or indulge in a bath in the private bathroom.Delicious Dining and Convenient Location: Enjoy bistro-style dishes and Australian wines at LB’s Restaurant. Explore nearby attractions like Luna Park and easily access Sydney's key sights with the hotel's proximity to North Sydney train station.Experience the beauty of Sydney from View Sydney - Book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  AUD 40 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.
