HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Victoria StationThe Chester Hotel, a charming Georgian townhouse, is just a 3-minute walk from Victoria Station. Enjoy the convenience of being close to Buckingham Palace and excellent transport links.Modern Amenities and ServicesRelax in comfortable en-suite rooms equipped with free high-speed WiFi, digital TV, and tea/coffee facilities. Benefit from daily room service, 24-hour reception, and complimentary parking on weekends.Explore the SurroundingsDiscover the vibrant neighborhood with shops, pubs, and restaurants within walking distance. Visit nearby attractions like Victoria Palace Shopping Centre and Westminster Cathedral for a memorable stay.Ready to experience the best of London? Book your stay at The Chester Hotel now!

This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Please inform Chester Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests wishing to check-in prior to 14:00 can use the luggage storage room at the hotel for free, or check in to the room for a GBP 5 per hour surcharge, subject to availability. Check in time for hotel rooms is from 14:00 and apartments from 16:00.
