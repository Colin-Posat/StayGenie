HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
European Ambiance and Central Park ViewsExperience the deluxe Park Lane New York hotel with its European ambiance and stunning views over Central Park and the New York skyline. Just a stone's throw away from the upscale shops of 5th Avenue, this hotel offers a prime location for your city getaway.Luxurious Rooms and On-Site DiningIndulge in spacious and bright rooms featuring oversized windows and premium linens at Park Lane New York. Enjoy modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs and access to the on-site fitness centre. Treat your taste buds to a delightful culinary experience at The Park Room Restaurant with a seasonal menu by Joseph Fontanals, or unwind with a selection of cocktails at Harry's New York Bar.Proximity to Top Attractions and Recreational ActivitiesExplore iconic landmarks like the Museum of Modern Art, Radio City Music Hall, and Rockefeller Center, all within a brief 10-minute walk from the hotel. With Central Park right at your doorstep, take advantage of leisurely strolls, jogging paths, and recreational activities during your stay.Book your stay at Park Lane New York now for an unforgettable experience in the heart of the city.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Park Lane New York in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
A damage deposit of USD 150 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note the resort fee includes the following:

- High-speed wireless Internet (WiFi) for up to 6 devices
- All local, long distance and international phone calls
- Unlimited use of 24/7 Fitness Centre (may not be available due to safety guidelines)
- Unlimited use of the Business Centre
- Luggage Storage until 23:00
-$1000 for deep cleaning fee

Please note that there is a package handling charge of USD 10 per package per stay for packages weighing under 5 pounds. Additional restrictions apply. Contact property for details.

Valet parking is only available at the 59th Street entrance.

Park Lane New York is a 100% non-smoking hotel and any violation will be subject to a $500 deep cleaning fee. If found in violation during a guest stay, Park Lane New York reserves the right to ask a guest to depart the hotel room without refund. Park Lane New York reserves the right to visually inspect all guest rooms after 24 hours of refusal of service to ensure the well-being of the guest and the condition of the room.

Upon check-out, any unused funds will be credited back to your account in 72 hours or more per your bank business rules.
