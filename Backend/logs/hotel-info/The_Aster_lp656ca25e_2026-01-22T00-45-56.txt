HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the allure of Los Angeles at The Aster, your perfect getaway steps away from Hollywood's iconic attractions.Recreational AmenitiesDive into relaxation with our outdoor pool or stay active in our state-of-the-art 24-hour fitness center, providing guests with everything needed for a rejuvenating stay.Dining and BeveragesSavor delectable Californian cuisine at our restaurant, Lemon Grove, or unwind with a cocktail at one of our chic bars. Room service and snacks from our coffee shop café are always available for your convenience.Luxurious AccommodationsOur 35 uniquely designed guest rooms are equipped with minibars, Smart TVs, and private bathrooms featuring designer toiletries, ensuring your comfort and a touch of luxury throughout your stay.Book your unforgettable experience at The Aster today!
