CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for € 8 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for € 8 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
