HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Hotel Polamar offers a beachfront escape with air-conditioned rooms and stunning sea views. Comfort and Convenience: Enjoy 24-hour reception, free Wi-Fi, and sea-view rooms. Dining Delights: Indulge in traditional Spanish cuisine and breakfast with a view. Ideal Location: Just 17 minutes from Alicante Airport with easy access to nearby attractions. Book your stay at Hotel Polamar for a memorable beach getaway.
