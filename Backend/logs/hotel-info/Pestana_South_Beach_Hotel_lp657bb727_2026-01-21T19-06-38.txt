HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Miami Hotel near Lincoln Road MallModern Amenities: All rooms feature an iPod docking station, cable TV, and coffee-making facilities. Some rooms offer scenic views of the garden, city, or pool.Convenient Facilities: Enjoy free Wi-Fi, an on-site restaurant, a central courtyard for relaxation, a business center, bicycle rental, and an on-site launderette.Prime Location: Just a short walk from Lincoln Road Mall and the Miami Art Deco District, and minutes away from the Bass Museum of Art and the Miami Beach Convention Center. Adjacent to the beach.Book your stay at Pestana South Beach Hotel for an unforgettable Miami experience!

Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for US$30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
