HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Landmark PropertyKimpton Fitzroy London, a Grade II listed landmark building, offers a lavish stay with its terracotta exterior and contemporary designer interiors. Located near Russell Square, this hotel is a stone's throw away from Covent Garden and The British Museum.Modern Luxury RoomsIndulge in modern luxury in redesigned rooms with free WiFi, Smart TVs, and en-suite bathrooms with upscale toiletries. Enjoy a minibar for added convenience during your stay.Dining and EventsExperience a variety of dining options from casual coffeehouse to upscale bar settings. With a large ballroom and multiple event spaces, the hotel is perfect for hosting events and gatherings.Book your stay at Kimpton Fitzroy London for a luxurious experience in the heart of Bloomsbury.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  GBP 30 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for £50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
