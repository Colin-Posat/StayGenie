HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tranquil Garden and Lounge: Guests at Madi Grand Maldives can relax in the serene garden or socialize in the inviting shared lounge.Refreshing Outdoor Pool: Take a dip in the year-round outdoor pool or soak up the sun on the lovely sun terrace while enjoying views of the garden and inner courtyard.Culinary Delights: Indulge in buffet and à la carte breakfast options featuring local specialties, pancakes, and fruits. The on-site restaurant offers a variety of Chinese dishes, as well as vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options.To experience the tranquility and culinary delights at Madi Grand Maldives, book your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Advance reservation is required for speedboat transfers and should be indicated under special request during hotel booking.

Please note that payments are accepted in USD. Credit & Debit Card payment facility is available at the hotel.

Please note that selling and consumption of alcohol is illegal in the local islands.

Please note wearing a bikini in the public areas of the island is illegal by law. However, there is a bikini beach a 5-minute walk from the guest house.This property does not accommodate bachelor(ette) or similar parties.

Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

• Room Quality: 8.45/10 - Rooms were described as spacious and well-equipped, although some complained about small balcony sizes.
• Amenities: 8.48/10 - The hotel offers a swimming pool and snorkeling gear, but guests noted a need for more umbrellas and chairs.
• Value for Money: 9.44/10 - Guests found the prices reasonable for the quality of service and facilities offered.
• Food and Beverage: 8.99/10 - Food was consistently described as delicious, although some noted breakfast could be warmer.
• Overall Experience: 9.46/10 - Guests had very positive experiences due to the care provided by the staff.
