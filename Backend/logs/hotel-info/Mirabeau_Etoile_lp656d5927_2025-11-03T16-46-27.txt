HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationMirabeau Etoile in Zermatt offers elegant rooms with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, private bathroom, and stunning mountain views from the balcony. Some rooms even feature a fully equipped kitchen for added convenience.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind at the hotel's hammam after a day of exploring Zermatt. For outdoor enthusiasts, activities such as hiking, skiing, and cycling are easily accessible, ensuring a memorable stay filled with adventure.Convenient ServicesFrom a 24-hour front desk to a concierge service, Mirabeau Etoile caters to every guest's needs. Enjoy a delicious buffet breakfast each morning before heading out to explore nearby attractions like Zermatt Railway Station and Matterhorn Golf Club.Experience luxury and comfort at Mirabeau Etoile - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway in Zermatt.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Please inform Mirabeau Etoile in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
