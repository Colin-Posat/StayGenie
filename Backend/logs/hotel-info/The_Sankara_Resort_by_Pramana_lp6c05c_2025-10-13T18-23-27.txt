HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Modern AmenitiesSankara Ubud Resort combines modern decor with traditional elements to offer a luxurious stay. Each air-conditioned unit features a seating area, flat-screen TV, and a personal safe. The en suite bathrooms are equipped with a bathtub, double sink, and complimentary bath amenities.Convenient Facilities and ServicesGuests can enjoy the convenience of a 24-hour front desk, free WiFi, and complimentary parking. The resort also offers airport transfers, car rental services, and laundry service for a hassle-free stay.Culinary Delights at The Kumbuh RestaurantIndulge in a variety of Indonesian and Western dishes at The Kumbuh Restaurant, which also provides room service for guests to dine in the comfort of their rooms.Experience luxury and comfort at Sankara Ubud Resort - Book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 5 years old and over stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel.
• Service: 8.4/10 - The staff received high praise for being friendly, helpful, and professional.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was described as great, with proximity to attractions and public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but some guests noted the need for renovations and mentioned issues like dampness and noisy air conditioning.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, particularly the pool and gym, but some noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some finding the prices high for the services provided while others thought it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The variety of breakfast and restaurant options received positive feedback, although some guests desired more options.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel to others, praising the atmosphere and friendly staff.
