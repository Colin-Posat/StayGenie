IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
If you inform the property of your arrival time 1 day in advance, pick-up from any location in Zermatt can be arranged.

Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.

HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 9 years old to 16 years old stay for CHF 55 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 9 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 55 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
