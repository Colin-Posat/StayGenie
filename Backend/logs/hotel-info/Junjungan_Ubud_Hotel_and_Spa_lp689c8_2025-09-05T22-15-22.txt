HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsAmenities for Relaxation and EntertainmentConvenient Location and Exceptional ServiceExperience the ultimate stay at Junjungan Ubud Hotel and Spa, where luxury meets tranquility. Indulge in the comfort of air-conditioned rooms with four-poster beds, offering modern amenities and breathtaking views of the rice fields from your private balcony. Unwind by the outdoor pool, enjoy a rejuvenating massage, or explore the area with cycling tours. With a restaurant serving delectable dishes, 24-hour front desk assistance, and tour arrangements, your every need is catered to. Conveniently located near top attractions, including the Ubud Art Market and Monkey Forest, this hotel offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Book now for an unforgettable experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for Rp 125,000 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for Rp 565,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for Rp 300,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
