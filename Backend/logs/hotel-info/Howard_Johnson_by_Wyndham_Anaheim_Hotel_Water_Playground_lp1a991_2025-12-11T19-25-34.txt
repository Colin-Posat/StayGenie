HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Private Themed Waterpark and Outdoor PoolsEscape to fun and relaxation at Howard Johnson by Wyndham Anaheim Hotel &amp; Water Playground. Enjoy our private themed waterpark with a 30-foot pirate ship, drench buckets, slides, and water cannons. Cool off in the outdoor pools or unwind in the poolside hot tub.Convenient LocationJust an 8-minute walk to Disneyland Resort and 10 minutes to Downtown Disney, our Anaheim resort is the perfect choice for your magical getaway. Explore nearby attractions like Newport Beach or catch a flight easily from Ontario International Airport, located just 50 km away.Comfortable Rooms with Free WiFiRelax in our cozy rooms featuring modern amenities such as free WiFi, flat-screen cable TVs, and coffee machines. Select rooms even offer balconies for a breath of fresh air. Category Ratings:

• Value for Money: 7.2/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, though some mentioned a desire for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Overall, most guests had a positive experience and recommended the hotel to others.
