HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationGrand Hyatt Tokyo offers stylish and modern rooms with warm wood furnishings and Japanese aesthetics. Each room features a rain shower, deep soaking limestone bathtub, flat-screen TV, and complimentary yukata robes for ultimate relaxation.Dining ExcellenceIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's 10 restaurants and bars offering a diverse range of international cuisine. From French delicacies to Japanese sushi and seasonal kaiseki dishes, guests can savor a variety of flavors in unique settings like open-air terraces or a jazz lounge with live music.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind with spa treatments at Nagomi Spa, take a dip in the indoor swimming pool with natural stone designs, or enjoy massages at the Fitness Center. The hotel's central location in Roppongi Hills allows for easy access to shopping, dining, and entertainment options.Book your stay at Grand Hyatt Tokyo for a luxurious and unforgettable experience.
