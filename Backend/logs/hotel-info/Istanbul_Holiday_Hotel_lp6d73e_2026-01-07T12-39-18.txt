HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in Historic PeninsulaLocated in the heart of Istanbul's Historic Peninsula, Istanbul Holiday Hotel offers easy access to iconic landmarks such as Topkapi Palace and Hagia Sophia Museum. Immerse yourself in the rich history of the city just steps away.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesExperience comfort and convenience in our well-equipped rooms featuring a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a minibar. Relax in the private bathrooms with complimentary toiletries and enjoy garden views in select rooms.Convenient Services and Dining OptionsBenefit from our 24-hour front desk, laundry services, and daily breakfast offerings. Explore nearby dining options for lunch and dinner, or take a leisurely stroll to the tram station for seamless city exploration.Book your stay at Istanbul Holiday Hotel for a memorable experience in Istanbul's vibrant Historic Peninsula.
