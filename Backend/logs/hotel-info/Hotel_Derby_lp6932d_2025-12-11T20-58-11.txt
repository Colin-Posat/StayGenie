HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and ConvenienceThe Hotel Derby in Zermatt offers a prime location on the main street, just 50 meters from the train station and the Gornergrat Railway. With the Sunnegga Cable Car a mere 5-minute walk away, convenience is at your doorstep for exploring the stunning surroundings.Cozy Chalet-Style Rooms and Modern ApartmentsExperience the charm of chalet-style rooms in the main building, while the Residence Bellevue boasts modern renovations. Each unit includes essential amenities like a minibar, safety deposit box, and private bathroom. Additionally, apartments with fully equipped kitchens are available for a comfortable stay.Dining Excellence and DiscountsIndulge in Italian and international cuisine at the on-site restaurant with a terrace, offering delectable flambé dishes. As a bonus, hotel guests enjoy a generous 15% discount at the restaurant, enhancing your dining experience.Ready to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Hotel Derby? Book now for an unforgettable stay in Zermatt!

Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in.
