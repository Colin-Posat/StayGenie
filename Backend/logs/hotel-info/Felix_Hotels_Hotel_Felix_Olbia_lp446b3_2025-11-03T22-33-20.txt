• Amenities: 7.4/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with highlights on the pools, spa facilities, while some guests mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding the price reasonable given the location, while others thought it was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested more variety could be added.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, praising the staff, cleanliness, and location.
