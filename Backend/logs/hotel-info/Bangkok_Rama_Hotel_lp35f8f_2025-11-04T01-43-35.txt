HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near AirportLocated just a short 10 minutes' drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport, Bangkok Rama Hotel offers air-conditioned rooms and cottages for a comfortable stay before or after your flight.Relaxing Outdoor Pool and Spacious AccommodationEnjoy a refreshing dip in the 100 square metre outdoor swimming pool or unwind in the spacious rooms and cottages equipped with modern amenities like satellite TV, minibar, and personal safe. Cottages even feature a kitchenette and balcony with garden views.Exciting Activities and Dining OptionsGuests can indulge in dance classes at the Dance Studio or savor delicious Thai and Chinese dishes at Baansiri Restaurant. Don't miss the daily buffet breakfast offering a variety of options to start your day right.Book your stay at Bangkok Rama Hotel now for a memorable and convenient experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 7.4/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport. However, some guests found the location inconvenient for city access.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
