HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Panoramic ViewsRooftop Panoramic Bar and Spa&amp;Fitness CentreIndulge in the breathtaking views of Princes’ Islands while sipping unique cocktails at our rooftop bar. Rejuvenate your senses at our 1300 m² spa&amp;fitness centre equipped with indoor and outdoor swimming pools, Turkish bath, sauna, and more.Elegant Rooms with Modern AmenitiesExperience comfort and style in our contemporary rooms featuring elegant wooden furniture, flat-screen TVs, and free internet access. Enjoy a fine dining experience at our Main Restaurant and health bar, offering a variety of Turkish and international dishes as well as fresh-squeezed juices.Convenient Location and Wellness FacilitiesLocated near Pendik Marina, our hotel provides easy access to ferry services. Unwind at Nerolie Spa&amp;Fitness Centre with a range of massages, professional trainers, and a large rain room. Book your stay now and enjoy luxury and convenience in Istanbul.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Spa, steam room, rain room, hot tub, pool and fitness are free of charge for adults. Children under 14 years old are not allowed to enter spa and fitness area. For swimming pool, children between 2-14 years old are subjected to an extra fee and should be supervised by an adult.

The room rate will be converted into Turkish Lira based on Turkish Central Bank's exchange rate of the check-in day.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
