HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location and Convenience: Only a 2-minute walk from Kyoto Station, Kyoto Century Hotel offers easy access to transportation and various attractions in the city.Comfortable Rooms and Quality Amenities: The air-conditioned rooms at Century Kyoto are well-equipped with modern amenities including a TV, refrigerator, and free internet. Guests can also enjoy complimentary toiletries and access to massage treatments for relaxation.Diverse Dining Options: With 3 dining choices including the popular All Day Dining La Jyho, guests can indulge in a Japanese and Western breakfast buffet and other delicious meals throughout the day.Book your stay at Kyoto Century Hotel now to experience convenience, comfort, and delicious dining options during your Kyoto adventure.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
When booking for 8 guests or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Please note this is strictly a non-smoking property. Any violation of this policy will result in eviction from the property and additional cleaning fees will be charged.

Breakfast for children sleeping in an existing bed who are 4-6 years old can be provided for an additional fee.
