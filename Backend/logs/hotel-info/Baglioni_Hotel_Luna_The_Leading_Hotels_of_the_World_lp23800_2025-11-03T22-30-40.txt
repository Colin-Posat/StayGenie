HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Impressive Interiors and Stunning ViewsBaglioni Hotel Luna offers a captivating experience with original frescoes and breathtaking lagoon views, just steps away from Saint Mark's Square. The spacious rooms feature elegant antique furniture, Murano glass chandeliers, and marble bathrooms, ensuring a luxurious stay.Gourmet Dining and Elegant Venetian ExperienceIndulge in a lavish buffet breakfast in the grand Salone Marco Polo hall, and savor Venetian specialties and fine wines at the award-winning restaurant Canova. Caffè Baglioni offers drinks and snacks by a charming canal throughout the day, adding to the hotel's sophisticated dining options.Exclusive Amenities and Convenient LocationWith a private dock for stylish arrivals by motorboat or gondola, Baglioni Hotel Luna provides a unique Venetian experience. Additionally, the proximity to the San Marco-Vallaresso Vaporetto water bus stop offers easy access to explore Venice's enchanting attractions.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Baglioni Hotel Luna in central Venice.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 16 years old and over stay for € 77 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
