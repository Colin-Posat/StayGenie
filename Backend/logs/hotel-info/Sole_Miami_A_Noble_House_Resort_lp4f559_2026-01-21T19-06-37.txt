Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.78/10 - Multiple reviews highlighted issues such as dirty rooms, mould in showers, and neglected areas.
• Service: 9.25/10 - Some reviews praised the kindness and helpfulness of staff, although there were criticisms regarding responsiveness.
• Location: 9.79/10 - Many reviewers emphasized the great location with easy access to local amenities.
• Room Quality: 7.24/10 - Critiques included old and neglected furnishings, such as beds without headboards.
• Amenities: 7.77/10 - Some amenities were appreciated, but issues like non-working elevators reduced the overall experience.
• Value for Money: 6.73/10 - Guests felt the hotel was overpriced considering the quality of stay and services.
• Food and Beverage: 8.78/10 - Food was mentioned positively despite some limitations on menu options.
• Overall Experience: 9.25/10 - Mixed but generally leaning towards positive with some highlights overshadowed by cleanliness and maintenance issues.
