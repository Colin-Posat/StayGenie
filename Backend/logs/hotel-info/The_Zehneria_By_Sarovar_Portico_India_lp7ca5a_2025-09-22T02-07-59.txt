HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Zehneria Portico offers elegantly furnished rooms and suites, each equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, fully stocked minibar, and air-conditioning. The spacious suites even feature kitchenettes for added convenience.Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's restaurant, Seasons, which offers a diverse range of Continental, Asian, African, and Oceanic cuisine. After a delicious meal, unwind with cocktails and drinks at the poolside bar.Convenient LocationLocated in the suburb of Westlands, The Zehneria Portico is just 4 km away from Nairobi Central, making it a perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers. Explore nearby attractions like Nairobi Arboretum Park or easily reach Jomo Kenyatta International Airport within 30 minutes by car.Experience luxury and convenience at The Zehneria Portico - book your stay now!

Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for US$30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 16 years old and over stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests commented positively on the cleanliness of the rooms, although some mentioned issues with bathroom cleanliness.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Staff were generally friendly and welcoming, with efficient check-in processes. A few guests highlighted specific staff members for their excellent service.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location near major shopping malls and business centers was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were described as spacious and comfortable, though some guests noted maintenance issues in the bathrooms.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests praising the pool and gym while others mentioned limitations like a small gym and poorly maintained pool.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Some guests felt the hotel was overpriced for the services provided, especially regarding amenities like the gym and pool.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the food, especially breakfast, although some mentioned a lack of variety and delayed room service.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to revisit the hotel.
