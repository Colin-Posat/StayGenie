HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Waikiki BeachLocated just two blocks from Waikiki Beach, Ewa Hotel Waikiki offers comfortable accommodations in a prime location near key attractions.Well-Equipped RoomsEach room at Ewa Hotel Waikiki comes with modern amenities such as air conditioning, TV with cable channels, and a kitchenette in select rooms. The bathrooms are stocked with complimentary toiletries and feature a shower and bathtub for relaxation.Stunning 8th Floor SundeckGuests can unwind and take in breathtaking ocean views from the hotel's 8th-floor sundeck, perfect for enjoying the Hawaiian sunsets.Book your stay at Ewa Hotel Waikiki now and experience the perfect blend of convenience and relaxation just moments away from the beach and popular local attractions.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 200 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Guests must use the stairs and assistance can be provided upon request.

Please note that car parking is limited and subject to availability. All parking is first come, first served. Oversized vehicles are not permitted. Oversized vehicles are considered any vehicle over 6 ft tall and/or exceeding the width or length of a standard parking stall.

Daily amenity fee of $21/night includes:
- Daily local newspaper (in lobby),
- In-room coffee packets replenished on request,
- In-room safe,
- Local and toll-free calls,
- Shell lei greeting upon arrival,
- WiFi access throughout the property,
- Bishop Museum admission (1 adult and children under 17),
- Honolulu Museum of Art admission (1 adult and children under 18).

Pet policy: Pet fee is $39 USD. A maximum of 2 dog under 30 lbs is permitted per room. Please contact property in advance for restrictions. A refundable security deposit and taxable nightly fee applies.
