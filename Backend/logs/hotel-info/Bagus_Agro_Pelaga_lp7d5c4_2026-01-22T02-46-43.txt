HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Mountain Retreat with Private Villas and Dining OptionsNestled in the serene Pucak Mangu Mountain Range, Bagus Agro Pelaga offers a tranquil escape 950 meters above sea level. Private Villas with Stunning Views feature balconies and private plunge pools, perfect for relaxation. Dining Delights await at Lotus Restaurant and Tunjung Restaurant, offering a fusion of local and western cuisine.Indulge in the comfort of two-bedroom villas with kitchenettes and picturesque views of Mount Agung. Relaxing Amenities include en-suite bathrooms with both a bathtub and shower. Explore nearby attractions with the help of the tour desk or simply unwind with agricultural products from the on-site shop.Experience a rejuvenating mountain getaway at Bagus Agro Pelaga - book your stay now!

PETS:

INTERNET:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for Rp 84,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 560,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
