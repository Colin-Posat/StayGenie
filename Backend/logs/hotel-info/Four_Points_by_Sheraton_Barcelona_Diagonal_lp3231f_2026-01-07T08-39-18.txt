HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern &amp; Convenient LocationLocated in the trendy 22@ district, Four Points by Sheraton Barcelona Diagonal is just steps away from Glòries Shopping Centre, offering a modern and convenient stay in Barcelona.Elegant AccommodationsExperience comfort in soundproofed rooms with free Wi-Fi and TV, featuring stylish bathrooms and wooden flooring. Opt for rooms on higher floors for stunning views, including sights of the iconic Sagrada Familia.Delicious Dining OptionsIndulge in traditional Spanish cuisine at La Avenida Restaurant, savor a daily buffet breakfast, and unwind at 4Taps Bar with local crafted beers and sports projections. Free coffee is available in the early hours for a perfect start to your day.Book now for a memorable stay at Four Points by Sheraton Barcelona Diagonal, where modern amenities and a prime location await you.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not possible) and costs  EUR 22 per  day.
