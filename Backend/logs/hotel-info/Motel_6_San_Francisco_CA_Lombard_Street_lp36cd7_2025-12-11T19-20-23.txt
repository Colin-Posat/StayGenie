GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Close to shops and dining options

Areas for Improvement:
• Poor cleanliness standards
• Unhelpful staff
• Lack of basic amenities
• Issues with room quality

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3.6/10 - Guests raised numerous cleanliness concerns, including stained sheets, dirty rooms, holes in walls, and unclean bathrooms.
• Service: 2.9/10 - Service feedback was overwhelmingly negative, with guests highlighting unhelpful staff, difficulties reaching reception, and refusal for refunds in unpleasant circumstances.
• Location: 6.7/10 - The hotel's proximity to key amenities and food places was mentioned positively by guests.
• Room Quality: 3.1/10 - Guests reported poor room conditions such as stained mattresses, cockroaches, urine smell, and malfunctioning amenities like hairdryers and alarm clocks.
• Amenities: 1.3/10 - Several complaints were made regarding the lack of basic amenities like fridges and microwaves, as well as the unpleasant odor and uncleanliness within the rooms.
• Value for Money: 1.8/10 - Guests overwhelmingly felt that the hotel did not offer value for money due to the poor quality of rooms, unhygienic conditions, and inadequate services.
• Food and Beverage: 5/10 - Feedback on food and beverage was mixed, with some guests appreciating the nearby dining options and others noting issues with cleanliness and functionality of in-room amenities.
• Overall Experience: 2.1/10 - Guests had extremely negative overall experiences, mentioning dirty rooms, unpleasant odors, unhelpful staff, and lack of basic amenities.
