IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Zermatt is a car-free resort. Guests can only drive as far as Täsch and take the shuttle train from there. Car parks for a fee are available in Täsch.

Please note that additional guests who are not using an extra bed need to pay the additional breakfast directly at the hotel.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for CHF 30 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 11 years old stay for CHF 80 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 15 years old stay for CHF 120 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for CHF 120 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for CHF 160 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
