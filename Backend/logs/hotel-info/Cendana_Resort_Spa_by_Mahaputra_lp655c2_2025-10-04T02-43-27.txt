HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Traditional Balinese DecorExperience the charm of Bali at Cendana Resort &amp; Spa by Mahaputra, offering air-conditioned rooms adorned with traditional Balinese décor and modern amenities. Relax on a four-poster wooden bed in a spacious room that opens to a private balcony, creating a serene atmosphere.Relaxing Spa Treatments and Outdoor PoolsIndulge in pampering spa treatments and take a refreshing dip in one of the two outdoor pools, surrounded by lush greenery. Unwind and rejuvenate your senses in this tranquil oasis overlooking the picturesque rice fields of Bali.Convenient Location and Culinary DelightsIdeally situated just a short walk from Monkey Forest Ubud, Cendana Resort &amp; Spa provides easy access to Ubud Centre and Puri Lukisan Museum. Enjoy delectable Indonesian and international cuisines at Sun Rise Restaurant, complemented by a daily buffet breakfast. Book your stay now for a memorable Bali getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 7 years old and over stay for Rp 200,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews about cleanliness with some guests mentioning issues like moldy walls and dirty areas.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Service was generally praised for being friendly and helpful, but some guests raised concerns about noise and slow responses.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and its peaceful atmosphere received positive feedback.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Room quality had mixed reviews, with mentions of old, dark rooms and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities received mixed feedback, with positive mentions of pools and negative comments about spa services and cleanliness.
• Value for Money: 7.1/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location and others finding it expensive for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Mixed feedback on food and beverage, with positive remarks about breakfast but some guests desiring more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the staff, pool, and location despite some shortcomings in cleanliness and room quality.
