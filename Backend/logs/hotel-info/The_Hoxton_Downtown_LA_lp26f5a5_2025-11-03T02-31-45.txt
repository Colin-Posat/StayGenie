HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Hoxton, Downtown LA offers a 4-star experience with modern amenities and stylish rooms, including city views and complimentary WiFi. Each room is equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay, from air conditioning to a flat-screen TV.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's restaurant, offering a mix of French, Peruvian, and Spanish cuisine. Special dietary needs are catered to with vegetarian, dairy-free, and kosher options available upon request. Start your day with a delicious breakfast before exploring the vibrant city of Los Angeles.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by the outdoor swimming pool or explore the city with the free bike rentals provided by the hotel. The terrace offers a serene spot to enjoy the California sun. With a prime location near Microsoft Theater and Staples Center, The Hoxton, Downtown LA is the perfect choice for your next stay in LA.
