HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Japanese ExperienceAt Hotel Edoya, immerse yourself in a traditional Japanese stay with tatami-mat rooms and futon bedding. Relax in the public hot bath or enjoy a soothing massage to unwind after a day of exploring Tokyo.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a delicious breakfast buffet at Kosetsu Soba Restaurant, offering a mix of local and Western dishes. From simple snacks to elaborate traditional meals, satisfy your taste buds without leaving the hotel.Convenient LocationLocated centrally in Tokyo, Hotel Edoya is a short walk from Yushima Subway Station and Ochanomizu Station, with easy access to popular spots like Akihabara for shopping and entertainment.Experience the best of Japanese hospitality at Hotel Edoya - book your stay now for a memorable Tokyo getaway.

Children of any age are allowed.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel rooms, although some mentioned minor dust issues in certain areas.
• Service: 8.2/10 - The staff were friendly and helpful, although there were complaints about limited after-hours access and the need for improved front door security.
• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its proximity to key landmarks and public transportation, despite being situated uphill.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Guests enjoyed the traditional Japanese style rooms, but some found them lacking in comfort and with limited views.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The hotel's amenities such as the onsen and breakfast were generally well-received, although some desired more variety and storage space.
• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - Opinions on the value for the price paid were mixed, with some feeling it was reasonable and others wanting more for the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - The quality of the breakfast and meals was generally positive, although some guests preferred dining out for more options.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the traditional atmosphere, friendly staff, and convenient location.
