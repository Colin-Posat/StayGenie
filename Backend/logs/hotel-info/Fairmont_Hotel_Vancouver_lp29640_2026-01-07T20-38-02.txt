HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Fairmont Hotel Vancouver, nestled in the heart of Vancouver city centre, offers elegantly appointed rooms with flat-screen TVs, minibars, and stunning city views. Enjoy the convenience of tea and coffee-making facilities in every room, ensuring a comfortable stay.Exceptional Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at Notch8 Restaurant &amp; Bar, located in the lobby. Delight in internationally inspired dishes made with local produce, promising a gastronomic adventure for your taste buds.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind at The Health Club, open 24/7, offering spa, sauna, and an indoor swimming pool. Whether you're here for leisure or business, take advantage of the on-site business centre for your convenience. With attractions like BC Place, Rogers Arena, and Orpheum Theatre nearby, your stay at The Fairmont Hotel Vancouver will be truly unforgettable.

GROUPS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for CAD 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  CAD 62 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and costs  CAD  14.95 per  24 hours.
