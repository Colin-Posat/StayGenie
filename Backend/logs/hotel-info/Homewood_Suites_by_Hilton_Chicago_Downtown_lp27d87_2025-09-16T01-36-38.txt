Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Most guests appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, but some mentioned issues like sticky carpets and dirty rooms.
• Service: 8.5/10 - The staff's friendliness and attentiveness were highlighted by many guests, although some had negative experiences with valet parking and front desk responsiveness.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's central location was praised for its proximity to shops, eateries, landmarks, and public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests generally found the rooms comfortable and well-equipped, but there were complaints about dated decor, noise insulation, and cleanliness.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The hotel's amenities were considered satisfactory, with some guests appreciating the breakfast, pool, and kitchen facilities, while others mentioned the lack of a hot tub and inconvenient parking.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding the price reasonable given the location, while others felt it was too high for the overall services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast availability was appreciated, but there were suggestions for improvement in variety and quality. Some guests also mentioned issues with restaurant service.
• Overall Experience: 8.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the location, service, and amenities, although some suggested improvements in cleanliness and value for money.
