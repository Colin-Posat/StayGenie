HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation in Bangkok's Shopping DistrictAffordable Luxury: Citin offers modern rooms with a 32-inch flat-screen TV, free internet, and stylish decor in neutral tones with vibrant accents.Convenient Amenities: Enjoy air conditioning, private bathrooms with bathtubs or rainshowers, tea/coffee facilities, and a minibar in every room.Explore with Ease: Take advantage of the tour desk for travel arrangements and a complimentary tuk-tuk shuttle to nearby shops.Indulge in a variety of local and international dishes at Citin Café, including vegetarian options. With popular shopping malls within walking distance, make Citin your home in Bangkok.
