HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in luxury at Hôtel Recamier with rooms furnished in opulent fabrics and featuring spacious canopy beds. Enjoy modern amenities like iPod docking stations, with some rooms offering stunning views of the square and Saint Sulpice.Charming Dining OptionsStart your day with a delightful breakfast in the pastel-coloured lounge or opt for in-room dining. For a unique experience, dine on the terrace or unwind under the twinkling lanterns in the evening. Don't forget to savor complimentary afternoon tea in the elegant lounge.Convenient LocationNestled in the heart of Saint Germain des Prés, our hotel offers easy access to nearby attractions like Jardin du Luxembourg and Saint-Sulpice Metro Station. With free Wi-Fi and parking nearby, your stay at Hôtel Recamier is both luxurious and convenient.Experience elegance and comfort at Hôtel Recamier - book your stay now!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
