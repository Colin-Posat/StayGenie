HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Bosphorus CoastlineLocated just a stone's throw away from the Bosphorus coastline, Nidya Hotel Galataport offers easy access to popular attractions and transportation hubs in Istanbul.Modern Accommodations with Sea ViewsExperience contemporary rooms and suites adorned with modern decor and equipped with tea/coffee making facilities. Some units even boast stunning sea views for a truly memorable stay.Panoramic Dining and Spa ExperienceEnjoy a delightful buffet breakfast and savor local and international cuisine at the restaurant while taking in panoramic views. Unwind at the spa with sauna, Turkish bath, fitness room, and massage services available for a relaxing retreat.Ready to immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Istanbul? Book your stay at Nidya Hotel Galataport for a memorable experience!
