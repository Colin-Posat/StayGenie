HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Alpine RetreatHistoric Charm: Dating back to 1903, our hotel exudes traditional charm and offers a serene escape in the heart of Zermatt. Experience a piece of history during your stay.Cozy Accommodations: Each room at ALPINA budget rooms is tastefully decorated in a classic Alpine style, complete with modern amenities like a safe, cable TV, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Relax in comfort and style after a day of exploring.Convenient Location: Situated just a 2-minute walk from the main street and 500 meters from the Gornergratbahn Railway Station, our hotel provides easy access to the best of Zermatt. Explore the town's attractions with ease during your stay at ALPINA budget rooms.
