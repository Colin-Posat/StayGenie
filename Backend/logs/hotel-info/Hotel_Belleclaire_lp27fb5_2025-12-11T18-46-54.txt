HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the charm of Hotel Belleclaire, your ideal retreat in the heart of New York City.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy seamless connectivity with complimentary wireless internet access, and take advantage of our concierge services and gift shops/newsstands for all your needs.
