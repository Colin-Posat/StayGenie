HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and Peaceful Terrace GardenExperience luxury in the heart of Cihangir at Witt Istanbul Suites. Indulge in our open-plan rooms featuring a retro-modern design, parquet floors, and organic wooden furniture. Relax in our peaceful terrace garden and enjoy the serene surroundings.Local and Organic BreakfastStart your day with a delicious home-cooked breakfast made from the finest local and organic ingredients. Delight in traditional Turkish breakfast delicacies like marinated olives and white cheeses. Our 24/7 room service ensures you're always well taken care of.Cosy Lobby Bar and Convenient LocationUnwind in our cosy lobby bar, choose a book or DVD, or catch up on emails using the Apple iMac. With Taksim Square just 1 km away and Istanbul's Old City within 3 km, explore nearby attractions like Dolmabahçe Palace, Topkapi Palace, Hagia Sophia, and the Blue Mosque.Book your stay at Witt Istanbul Suites for a luxurious and unforgettable experience.
