HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Memphis City CentreThis hotel in Memphis is ideally situated, just a short 15-minute walk from the vibrant Beale Street with its array of bars and restaurants.Modern Amenities and Dining OptionsEnjoy contemporary guest rooms equipped with a flat-screen TV, work desk, and coffee-making facilities. Indulge in delicious meals at the on-site Notes &amp; Provisions Restaurant and Bar, offering a full-service bar and a variety of dishes.Convenience and ComfortTake advantage of the outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi, fitness centre, and business centre during your stay. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests praising the cleanliness of the rooms while others mentioned issues with cleanliness.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service received positive feedback overall, with guests appreciating the friendly staff and prompt assistance. Some complaints about service quality were also noted.
• Location: 8.3/10 - Guests highly valued the convenient location of the hotel, citing its proximity to attractions and transportation options.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but some guests reported maintenance issues and concerns about noise levels.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Feedback on amenities was mixed, with some guests praising the pool and gym facilities while others expressed disappointment over the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Opinions on the value for money were divided, with some guests feeling that the price was too high for the services provided and others considering it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Food and beverage offerings received mixed reviews, with some guests enjoying the breakfast and restaurant services, while others found them limited and overpriced.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
