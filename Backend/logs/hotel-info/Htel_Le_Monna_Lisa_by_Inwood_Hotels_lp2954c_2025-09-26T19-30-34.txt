HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Design and Prime LocationLocated in Paris’s 8th district, just steps away from the iconic Champs Elysees, Hotel Monna Lisa Champs Elysées offers chic air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi. The contemporary design, Slovenian oak flooring, and American walnut furniture provide a luxurious ambiance for guests to enjoy.Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in the comfort of plush bathrobes and slippers provided in each room, along with a flat-screen satellite TV, telephone, and a private bathroom. Accessibility is made easy with a lift available to all rooms.Convenient ServicesStart your day right with a delicious buffet breakfast served every morning. With nearby metro stations such as Saint-Philippe-du-Roule and Franklin D. Roosevelt, exploring Paris's attractions like the Arc de Triomphe and The Tuileries Garden is a breeze.Book your stay at Hotel Monna Lisa Champs Elysées for a memorable Parisian experience!
