HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Ryder Hotel in Charleston, South Carolina offers a blend of elegance and comfort. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen LCD TV, in-room safe, and free Wi-Fi, ensuring a relaxing stay for every guest.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy the convenience of an outdoor pool, 24-hour front desk, and access to an off-site fitness center. The Little Palm daytime cafe and cocktail bar on-site provides a perfect spot for breakfast and evening drinks.Prime LocationSituated just a block away from the College of Charleston, and within walking distance to King and Market Street, the Ryder Hotel is the ideal base for exploring the city. Don't miss out on the city tours available nearby.Book your stay at the Ryder Hotel now and indulge in a memorable Charleston experience!
