HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation Near Graceland MansionConvenient Amenities: The Guest House at Graceland offers 2 on-site restaurants, a theatre, outdoor pool with pool bar, and complimentary airport shuttle service. Guests can also indulge in the evening Peanut Butter and Jelly Bar.Comfortable Rooms: Each room is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, work desk, coffee maker, seating area, and compact refrigerator. Select rooms provide views of the courtyard, and room service is available for added convenience.Entertainment and Dining: Enjoy the fitness centre, business centre, gift shop, and various lounge areas for relaxation. Indulge in Southern comfort dishes at Delta’s Kitchen and grab a drink at EP’s Bar &amp; Grill for a delightful dining experience.Book now for a memorable stay at The Guest House at Graceland!

HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
