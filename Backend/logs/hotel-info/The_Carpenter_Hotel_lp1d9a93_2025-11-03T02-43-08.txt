Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned minor cleanliness issues in specific areas.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service received positive feedback, with guests highlighting the helpful and friendly staff, although a few complaints were noted about slow responses and lack of communication.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its proximity to key attractions and public transportation options, making it convenient for guests to explore the area.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and well-designed, but some guests mentioned issues with room amenities and maintenance.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - While some guests enjoyed the amenities like the gym and pool, others noted the absence of certain services like spa facilities which impacted their experience.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was high compared to the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Guests generally appreciated the breakfast and restaurant services, although there were suggestions for improvement, particularly in terms of variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel and would recommend it to others, praising the overall vibe and design elements.
