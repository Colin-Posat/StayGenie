HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Complimentary BreakfastHomewood Suites by Hilton Miami Downtown/Brickell offers spacious suites with separate living and sleeping areas, fully-equipped kitchenettes, and two flat-screen TVs. Enjoy a complimentary breakfast to start your day right.Convenient Amenities and ServicesIndulge in a free evening reception with beer, wine, and light dinner fare, and take advantage of the 24-hour on-site mini-market. Stay connected with free WiFi, and make use of valet parking for added convenience.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by the 2nd-floor pool overlooking Miami's city centre, or stay active at the 24-hour fitness and business centres. With grocery delivery services and meeting space available, everything you need is at your fingertips.Book your stay at Homewood Suites by Hilton Miami Downtown/Brickell for a perfect blend of comfort and convenience.
