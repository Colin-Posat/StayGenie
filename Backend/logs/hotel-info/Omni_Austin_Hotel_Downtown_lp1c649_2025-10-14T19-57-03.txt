HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThe Omni Austin Hotel Downtown offers a roof-top heated pool and fitness centre, providing guests with the perfect way to unwind and stay active during their stay.Delightful Dining OptionsGuests can indulge in delicious meals at the on-site Capital A restaurant, known for its communal tables and unique private dining room. For a quick pick-me-up, President’s House Coffee offers a variety of coffee, tea, pastries, and light bites.Convenient ServicesWith a 24-hour front desk, laundry service, dry cleaning, and daily maid service, guests can enjoy a hassle-free stay. Meeting facilities and a gift shop are also available on site, catering to all their needs.Book your stay at Omni Austin Hotel Downtown now for a memorable and enjoyable experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were fresh and modern, but could use a little more lighting. Some guests found the rooms a bit dark.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory. Some guests enjoyed the pool, while others mentioned the need for room renovations.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews regarding value for money. Some guests felt prices were high, while others found the location justified the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with a few guests mentioning the need for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
