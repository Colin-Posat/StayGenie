• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with some guests mentioning a blood stain on the carpet.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, although some mentioned noise disruptions and lack of sleep.
