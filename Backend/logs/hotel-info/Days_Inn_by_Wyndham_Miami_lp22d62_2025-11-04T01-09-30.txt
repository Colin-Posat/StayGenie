Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.6/10 - While most guests praised the cleanliness of the rooms, some reviews mentioned issues with ants, dirty shower curtains, and dirty stair floors.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Friendly staff were a common highlight, although a few guests mentioned slow service and unhelpful morning clerk on personal calls.
• Location: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the hotel's proximity to key landmarks and easy highway access, although some found the lack of nearby attractions disappointing.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Clean and comfortable rooms were frequently mentioned, but some reviews highlighted maintenance issues, small pillows, and lack of elevator in older properties.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities including limited breakfast selection in some properties, no spa services, and some guests expecting better supplies and TV options.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions on pricing varied, with some guests feeling the rates were high for the offered services while others found them reasonable for location and room quality.
• Food and Beverage: 6.6/10 - Breakfast choices were a common concern with mentions of lacking variety, no fruit or juice, and unattractive presentation.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had positive overall experiences and highlighted friendly staff, comfortable beds, and clean rooms as key positives.
