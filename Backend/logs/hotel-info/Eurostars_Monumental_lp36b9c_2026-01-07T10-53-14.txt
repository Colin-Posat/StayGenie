HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Design and Convenient LocationThis design hotel offers free WiFi and is just 400 metres from Sagrada Família Church. Decorated in elegant neutral tones, rooms include a plasma screen TV with satellite channels.Comfortable Rooms and Stylish BathroomsRooms at the Eurostars Monumental have wooden floors and modern lighting. Toiletries and a hairdryer are included in the stylish marble bathrooms.Delicious Dining Options and Easy TransportationA breakfast buffet is served in the hotel’s restaurant. You can also find various restaurants and cafés within a 5-minute walk, in the area around the Sagrada Familia. Eurostars Monumental is conveniently located near Monumental Metro Station and Estació del Nord Bus Station, providing easy access to explore the city.Book now to experience comfort and convenience at Eurostars Monumental!
