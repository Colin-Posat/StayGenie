HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and DesignStep into a world of sophistication and style at Downtown Los Angeles Proper Hotel, where 148 guest rooms and suites await you. Designed by the renowned Kelly Wearstler, the interiors are sure to captivate your senses with their unique charm and elegance.Culinary ExcellenceSavor the culinary delights crafted by James Beard Award-winning Chef Suzanne Goin and Restaurateur Caroline Styne at the two destination restaurants on-site. From innovative dishes to classic favorites, your taste buds are in for a treat during your stay.Unparalleled AmenitiesIndulge in the state-of-the-art fitness center to stay active and energized, or unwind at the rooftop pool while enjoying breathtaking views of the city. With easy access to key attractions like the Staples Center and Walt Disney Concert Hall, your stay at Downtown Los Angeles Proper Hotel promises to be unforgettable.Experience luxury and comfort like never before - book your stay now at Downtown Los Angeles Proper Hotel!
