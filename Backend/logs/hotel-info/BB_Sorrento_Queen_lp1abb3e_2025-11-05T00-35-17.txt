HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Beachfront AccommodationLocated in Sorrento, B&amp;B Sorrento Queen offers a stunning beachfront experience with breathtaking mountain and garden views. Guests can enjoy a range of amenities including a garden, bar, and shared lounge. The air-conditioned units are well-equipped with modern facilities such as a flat-screen TV, minibar, and private terrace. HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:

PETS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
