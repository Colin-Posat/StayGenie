• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While some guests appreciated the gym and pool facilities, others expressed a desire for additional amenities such as spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied regarding the price relative to the services offered, with some guests feeling it was on the higher side.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews were received for the hotel's restaurant, with some guests praising the breakfast options while others found the overall dining experience lacking.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
