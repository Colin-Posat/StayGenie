Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.7/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but some reviews highlighted issues with housekeeping.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly staff noted in many reviews, despite some instances of delayed housekeeping and maintenance response.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and public transport was praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms were described as cozy but with various issues such as small size, noisy air conditioning, and cleanliness concerns.
• Amenities: 6.3/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests appreciating the lobby offerings while others criticized paid mini-fridge and kettle, and lack of room service.
• Value for Money: 5.3/10 - Several guests felt that the hotel did not provide value for the price paid, especially in terms of room quality and service levels.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, with some suggestions for improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and appreciated the ambiance, staff friendliness, and location.
