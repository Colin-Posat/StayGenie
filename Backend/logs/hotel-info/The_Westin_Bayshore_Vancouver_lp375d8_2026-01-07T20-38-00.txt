HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Waterfront Property: The Westin Bayshore, Vancouver offers a harmonious blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty, providing an ideal retreat to experience the best of Vancouver.Luxurious Accommodations and Dining: Newly renovated guestrooms with panoramic views, along with revamped dining venues like H2 Kitchen &amp; Bar, H Tasting Lounge, and T &amp; Co Cafe, offer a culinary journey that complements the stunning surroundings.Wellness and Fitness Oasis: From outdoor and indoor pools to a rejuvenating spa, WestinWORKOUT fitness centre, yoga studio, and complimentary bike rentals, every aspect is tailored to enhance your well-being during your stay.Book now to elevate your Vancouver experience at The Westin Bayshore, where luxury and wellness converge seamlessly.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  CAD 45 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for CAD 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.
