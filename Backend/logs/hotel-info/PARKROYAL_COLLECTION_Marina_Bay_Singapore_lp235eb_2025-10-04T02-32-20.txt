Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, although a few minor cleanliness issues were reported.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally well-received, with efficient staff and helpful services. Some guests mentioned room for improvement in terms of staff going the extra mile.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's central location near key attractions like Marina Bay and easy access to public transport were highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, but some guests noted aging issues and minor maintenance concerns.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - The hotel's facilities, including the pool and gym, were generally well-received. Some guests mentioned the need for improvement in amenities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the offered services, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Guests enjoyed the breakfast selection and restaurant services, though some suggested improvements in variety and quality, especially with beverages.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting service, location, and facilities as key factors.
