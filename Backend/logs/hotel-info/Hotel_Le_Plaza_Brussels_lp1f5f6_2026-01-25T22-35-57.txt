HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Heritage SettingStep into the opulent world of Le Plaza with its French façades, high ceilings, and exquisite tapestries. Situated in front of the Rue Neuve Shopping District, this hotel is a blend of history and elegance, just moments away from the vibrant Grand Place.Elegant Rooms and DiningIndulge in comfort in the guest rooms adorned with flat-screen TVs, minibars, and stylish air conditioning. The Brasserie Estére beckons with its painted dome, marble accents, and gourmet cuisine, providing a refined dining experience.Convenient Location and AmenitiesExplore Brussels easily with Rogier Metro Station nearby and iconic attractions like the Manneken Pis Statue within walking distance. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, a fitness center, and the charm of a heritage-listed Moorish-style theatre during your stay at Hotel Le Plaza.Book now to experience the allure of Le Plaza and create unforgettable memories in Brussels.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that lunch is a 3-course menu, excluding beverages.

Please note that the restaurant is closed for lunch and dinner every Saturday and Sunday.

Please note that guests will be charged total amount of their stay upon check-in.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 35 per  day.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available cot.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
