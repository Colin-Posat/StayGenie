HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in UskudarSituated in Uskudar on the Asian side, The Bosphorus Houses is a mere 10-minute stroll from the seafront and the iconic Maiden’s Tower, offering convenience and stunning views.Comfortable Self-Catering StudiosThe Bosphorus Houses boasts self-catering studios featuring free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and well-equipped kitchens with modern amenities. Some studios even come with a cozy sofa bed and a private terrace for a touch of luxury.Convenient Amenities and ServicesWith a 24/7 front desk and security service, guests can enjoy peace of mind and easy access to the studios. Additionally, the proximity to Uskudar Ferry Port and Marmaray Metro Station makes exploring the city a breeze.Book your stay at The Bosphorus Houses today and experience a perfect blend of comfort and convenience.
