HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and SurroundingsThe Hotel Royal Reforma is ideally situated amidst shopping centers, business districts, and entertainment hubs. With its close proximity to Chapultepec Avenue, Avenida Insurgentes, and Paseo de la Reforma, guests can easily explore the vibrant surroundings.Modern Amenities and Meeting SpaceEach room at the hotel is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including free WiFi, cable TV, and a work desk. Additionally, guests can utilize the meeting space that accommodates up to 130 people, making it perfect for business gatherings or special events.Dining Options and City ViewsIndulge in a culinary experience at the on-site restaurant, Spring, offering a delightful selection of Mexican and international cuisine. Unwind at one of the two bars, each offering a unique ambiance - one in the lobby and the other on the terrace with stunning city views.Experience the best of Mexico City's attractions with ease while staying at Hotel Royal Reforma. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
