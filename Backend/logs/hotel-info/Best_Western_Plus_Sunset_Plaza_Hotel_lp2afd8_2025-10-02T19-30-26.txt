IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note: The property has a 30 day cancellation and payment policy for groups booking 5 or more rooms. If the group book less than 30 days in advanced, then payment is due at the time of booking. A rooming list is due 14 days prior to arrival date.

Room location is not guaranteed when booking any room type. Please contact the hotel directly with any room location requests.

There is a capacity for one rollaway bed/cot upon request, please contact property for more details.

Due to installation of new solar panels on the property there could be periods in the day where there could be construction noise from March 16-April 30, 2023.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Guests had mixed feedback on cleanliness, with some mentioning clean rooms while others found old facilities and low-quality bedding.
• Service: 8/10 - Staff was generally friendly and helpful, with specific mentions of Carlos being extremely helpful.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's central location near key landmarks was highlighted as a positive aspect by many guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable for many guests, although some mentioned issues with natural light and basement rooms.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Feedback on amenities was mixed, with mentions of a nice swimming pool but also complaints about the lack of basic amenities and old facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.4/10 - Guests had varying opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the experience offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast was generally well-received, with positive feedback on the quality and variety of the breakfast offerings.
• Overall Experience: 6.7/10 - Overall experiences ranged from highly positive with recommendations to extremely negative due to incidents like room explosions and poor cleanliness.
