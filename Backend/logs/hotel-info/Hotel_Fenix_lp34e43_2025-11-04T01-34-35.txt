HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Quiet Garden and Elegant RoomsThe Fenix boasts a tranquil garden and stylish rooms with modern amenities like air conditioning and satellite TV. Many rooms even offer a private balcony for a touch of luxury.Delicious Buffet BreakfastIndulge in a scrumptious buffet breakfast at Hotel Fenix, featuring a variety of Italian coffee, cakes, and pastries. A perfect way to start your day of exploring the city.Convenient Location and TransportationLocated near Luiss University and Villa Torlonia park, the hotel offers easy access to bus links to Roma Termini Train Station and the historic center. Explore the charming Paganini Gardens in the vicinity.Ready to experience comfort and convenience at Hotel Fenix? Book your stay now for a memorable trip!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were comfortable and well-appointed, although a few guests noted the need for renovations in some areas.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While some guests enjoyed the available amenities like the garden and breakfast, others wished for more comprehensive services such as a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was justified and others finding it expensive for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and food options were generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing factors like comfort, cleanliness, and service.
