IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that breakfast is available every day of the week.

Upon check-in a physical government-issued photo identification and a matching credit card are required and all credit and debit transactions are charged a 3% transaction fee.

All special requests are subject to availability upon check-in. Special requests cannot be guaranteed and may incur additional charges.

Property allows early check-ins before 3:00pm at a surcharge of 30 USD/hour, it does not allow either late check-in after 0:00. If arrival is past 0:00, please confirm with property, otherwise reservation is subject to cancelation. Late check-out is possible up to 12:00 noon upon request and at a surcharge of 30 USD/hour. If you need to check-in after 18:00, you must contact Hollywood Liberty Hotel a week prior to arrival with your arrival time. have to notify the hotel in advance, otherwise the reservation will be canceled. Housekeeping during stay is available upon request.

Parking is first come first serve, so if there are no parking spots available for you to park your vehicle despite already paying for parking for a 30 USD per night, the front desk will refund your parking charge and advise you on alternative parking. If parking is paid for by card, it would be refunded in the morning.

Please note: The property has designated rooms for guests with service animals. Guests that travel with service animals need to contact the property in advance before booking to make sure that these rooms are available.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in.
