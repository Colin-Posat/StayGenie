CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for US$30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
