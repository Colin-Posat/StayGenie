HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation - Kimpton Charlotte Square Hotel offers elegantly designed rooms with modern amenities such as flat-screen Smart TVs, premium bed linen, and complimentary treats, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests.Culinary Delights - Indulge in a gastronomic experience at The Garden, serving a variety of dishes and drinks all day, and BABA, a restaurant and cocktail bar offering delectable Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisine.Leisure and Relaxation - Guests can enjoy complimentary access to the hotel's leisure facilities, including a gym, swimming pool, and spa where they can unwind with a range of rejuvenating treatments, making their stay both relaxing and enjoyable.Experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at Kimpton Charlotte Square Hotel in Edinburgh. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note the property does not accept cash payments due to COVID-19.

The credit or debit card used to book the Advance Purchase rate must be presented to reception upon arrival at the hotel. Please note that failure to do so will result in an alternative payment method being required.

Parking is limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Guests are asked to contact the property directly in order to guarantee a space, using the details on the Booking Confirmation. If you would like to reserve a car parking space please contact the Concierge department at the property, once you have made your reservation. Prices of parking are GBP 35 per night.

Smoking is not permitted at the property.

When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Children's swimming times are between 07:00 - 09:00 and 16:00 - 20:00 daily.Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
