HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with City ViewsJupiter NEXT, located just 1.3 km from downtown Portland, offers a stylish stay with city views and unique chalkboard doors. Each room boasts a 60-inch flat-screen TV and essential amenities for a comfortable stay.Convenient On-Site AmenitiesIndulge in the lobby restaurant and bar, or unwind in the private garden retreat with city views. With free WiFi, keyless entry, and a digital concierge, your stay at Jupiter NEXT is both convenient and luxurious.Central Location near Popular AttractionsExplore Portland's top attractions like the International Rose Test Garden and Powell's City of Books, all within a few kilometers of the hotel. Don't miss out on the nearby Oregon Convention Center and Moda Center for events and entertainment.Experience modern comfort and convenience at Jupiter NEXT.
