HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Victorian Townhouse in KensingtonSituated in a tranquil Kensington street, My Place Hotel offers elegant Victorian charm just steps away from Earl's Court Underground Station.Comfortable Rooms and Inclusive BreakfastAll rooms feature en suite facilities, a fridge, and a flat-screen TV. Enjoy a separate seating area in some rooms, along with an inclusive continental breakfast each morning. Guests can also indulge in complimentary tea and coffee daily.Charming Lounge and Walled GardenRelax in the spacious lounge with an all-day bar, or unwind in the attractive walled garden's seating area.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsExplore vibrant bars, restaurants, and shops in Earls Court, with Kensington High Street a short walk away. Discover Kensington Palace, the Royal Albert Hall, and Holland Park within a leisurely stroll from the hotel.Experience luxury and convenience at My Place Hotel - book your stay now!
