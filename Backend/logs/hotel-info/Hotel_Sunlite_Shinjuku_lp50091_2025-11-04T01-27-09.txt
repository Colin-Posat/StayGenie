HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Comfort in the Heart of ShinjukuConvenient Location: Situated just 800 meters from Shinjuku Station, Hotel Sunlite is the perfect base for exploring Tokyo. With Shinjuku Sanchome Subway Station a short walk away, popular attractions like Shinjuku Gyoen Garden and Kabukicho are easily accessible.Relaxing Amenities: Unwind in our air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs and free wired internet. Enjoy a soak in the bath, with slippers and a fridge provided for your convenience.Culinary Delights: Indulge in a delicious breakfast buffet at Restaurant Floret, offering a mix of Japanese and Western cuisine. For lunch and dinner, savor Western-style dishes in a cozy setting.Book your stay at Hotel Sunlite now and experience comfort and convenience in the heart of Shinjuku.
