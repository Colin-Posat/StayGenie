HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
French-Inspired Architecture and Ideal City Centre LocationThe Marker San Francisco offers guests a unique experience with its French-inspired architecture and convenient city centre location, just minutes away from Union Square.Luxurious Amenities and Upscale AccommodationsGuests can indulge in the upscale amenities provided in the guest rooms and suites, including LCD flat-screen TVs, iHome alarm clocks, and designer bath products, ensuring a comfortable and luxurious stay.Dining Excellence and Elegant AmbianceExperience culinary delights at Tratto, the hotel's modern Italian trattoria, serving up delectable dishes and crafted cocktails in a sophisticated setting, perfect for breakfast, happy hour, or dinner.Book your stay at The Marker San Francisco for a memorable and luxurious experience in the heart of the city.
