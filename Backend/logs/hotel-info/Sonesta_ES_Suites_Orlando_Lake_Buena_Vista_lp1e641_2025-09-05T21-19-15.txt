IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Sonesta ES Suites Orlando - Lake Buena Vista in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
The property will be going through renovation works from Mon 02 Dec 2024 until Mon 10 Mar 2025. During this period, guests may experience some noise or light disturbances, and some hotel facilities and services may not be available.
A damage deposit of USD 150 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
This property charges additional fees for receiving guests' packages. Contact property for details.

Please note that all guests who book with prepaid reservations are required to show the credit card used at booking upon check-in. Name on credit card must match on valid ID and on reservation in order to check in.

Guests booking more than 3 rooms: Different group policies apply with a different deposit amount. Contact the property for details.

Special requests cannot be guaranteed and may incur additional charges.

Incidental Hold is $150/ per stay. Return to your account can take up to 7 business days if there are no incidental charges.

When travelling with pets, please note that an extra charge of $75 fee per stay for stays up to 7 nights; $150 per stay thereafter.
