HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 4-Star Hotel in Historic Bloomsbury DistrictStylish and Contemporary Rooms:Experience comfort in our air-conditioned rooms with plasma flat-screen TVs and free WiFi. Each room is equipped with an electronic safe and individual climate controls for your convenience.Convenient Location:Located near major attractions like the British Museum, Covent Garden, and Oxford Street, our hotel is within walking distance of Holborn and Russell Square Underground stations.
