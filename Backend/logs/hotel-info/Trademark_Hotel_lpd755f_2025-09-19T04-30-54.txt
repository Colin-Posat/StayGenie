HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience unparalleled comfort and luxury at Trademark Hotel, your oasis in the heart of Nairobi.Relaxation and Wellness: Indulge in rejuvenation at our spa, offering an array of massages, body treatments, and facials designed to refresh your mind and body.Dining Delights: Satisfy your culinary cravings at Harvest, our on-site restaurant featuring a bar/lounge, or grab a coffee while enjoying our buffet breakfast served daily.Business and Leisure: With complimentary Wi-Fi and a 24-hour business center, stay connected while impressing clients in our spacious conference facilities. When you’re ready to unwind, take a dip in the outdoor pool or challenge your friends in the arcade/game room.Make unforgettable memories at Trademark Hotel – your luxurious getaway awaits! Book now to secure your relaxing stay.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 5 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* A car is required for transportation to and from this property.
* Parking height restrictions apply.
* Contactless check-in is available.
