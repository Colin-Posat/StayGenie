HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Escape to paradise at Pueblo Bonito Sunset Beach Golf &amp; Spa Resort, an all-inclusive gem nestled in Cabo San Lucas.Unwind in LuxuryRelax at our full-service spa offering rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials. Dive into one of our 6 stunning outdoor pools or bask on the pristine private beach for the ultimate relaxation experience.Culinary DelightsSavor international cuisine at La Nao, one of our 5 exquisite restaurants, or enjoy the convenience of 24-hour room service. Quench your thirst at our bar/lounge, swim-up bar, or one of the two lively poolside bars.Comfort and ConnectivityRetreat to one of our 756 spacious guestrooms featuring kitchenettes, premium bedding, and private balconies. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi, while a roundtrip airport shuttle ensures your travel needs are met.Your dream getaway awaits—book your stay today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Reservations are required for golf tee times, massage services, and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the property prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* A car is recommended for transportation to and from this property.
* Cashless payment methods are available for all transactions.
* Contactless check-out is available.
