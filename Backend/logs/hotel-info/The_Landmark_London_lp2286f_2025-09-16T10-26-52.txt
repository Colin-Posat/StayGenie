HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe deluxe hotel in London’s Marylebone offers spacious air-conditioned rooms adorned with fine fabrics and elegant furniture. Guests can enjoy modern amenities like a flat-screen satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, and luxurious marble bathrooms with designer toiletries.Spa &amp; Health ClubIndulge in a pampering experience at the Spa &amp; Health Club, offering a range of relaxing treatments such as massages, reflexology, and hot stone therapies. Unwind in the steam room, sauna, relaxation room, or stay active in the fully equipped gym.Dining ExcellenceExperience culinary delights at The Winter Garden serving modern cuisine and the Great Central Bar &amp; Restaurant offering European dishes. Guests can also unwind at the garden terrace and Mirror Bar. Don't miss the opportunity to book your stay and enjoy complimentary access to the Spa at The Landmark London.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for £60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.3/10 - Most guests praised the cleanliness of the hotel, mentioning it as one of the positive aspects.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service received high appreciation, with special mention of staff going above and beyond. However, there were some complaints regarding delayed room availability.
• Location: 7.6/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and public transport was highlighted positively, but some guests found the location far from major tourist attractions.
• Room Quality: 8.5/10 - Guests generally found the rooms spacious and clean, with minor maintenance issues mentioned by a few.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities with mixed reviews, some guests appreciated the gym and pool while others desired additional services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Mixed opinions on pricing, with some guests feeling it was not justified for the services provided, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, though some guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
