HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near St. Peter's SquareModern Amenities and ServicesConvenient Sightseeing Tours AvailableJust 100 metres from St. Peter’s Square, the Emmaus offers free Wi-Fi, and is located in the historic centre of Rome. Bus 64 stops nearby and provides quick access to Termini Train Station.The hotel Emmaus has a lobby with a small bar, and free internet terminals with printer.
