HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tranquil Accommodation with Outdoor Pool:Nestled in Ubud, just a short stroll from Ayung River, Ubud Raya Shala offers a serene retreat with an inviting outdoor swimming pool.Convenient Services and Amenities:Enjoy a range of services including massage treatments, bicycle rentals, and complimentary Wi-Fi. The air-conditioned rooms are elegantly decorated and feature modern amenities for a comfortable stay.Dining and Accessibility:Indulge in Indonesian and Western delicacies at Sayan Restaurant, open all day. With a 24-hour front desk and proximity to Ubud attractions and the airport, Ubud Raya Shala ensures a seamless and enjoyable stay.Book now to experience a peaceful getaway at Ubud Raya Shala!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 250,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 5 years old and over stay for Rp 400,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
