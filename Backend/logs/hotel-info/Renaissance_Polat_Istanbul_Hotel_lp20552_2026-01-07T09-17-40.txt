HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsThe 5-star Renaissance Polat Istanbul Hotel, situated just steps away from the beach in Yesilyurt, offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the Marmara Sea. The hotel boasts uniquely large swimming pools and a 2600 m² sports area featuring a renovated gym, massage facilities, and a Turkish bath.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentIndulge in delicious seafood dishes at the Marmara Fish Restaurant or savor Mediterranean and Turkish cuisine at Daphe Restaurant &amp; Grill with views of the sea and Princes' Islands. Enjoy live music performances at Noblesse Bar and unwind with aromatic tea and pastries at Polat Patisserie.Convenient Location and Leisure ActivitiesExplore the nearby wooden Art Nouveau style houses, visit Yesilkoy Marina for outdoor activities, and easily access CNR EXPO, IDTM, and Istanbul city center. End your day by relaxing in the club lounge with complimentary snacks and drinks.Experience luxury and comfort at Renaissance Polat Istanbul Hotel - Book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 8 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 9/10 - Amenities were generally well received, especially the pool and gym facilities.
• Value for Money: 8.5/10 - Mixed feelings about pricing in relation to hotel offerings; some aspects deemed not worth the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - Food quality noted as average with some items needing improvement, though breakfast was praised.
• Overall Experience: 9/10 - Despite some drawbacks, many guests expressed a positive overall experience, highlighting specific enjoyable aspects.
