HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in AmsterdamLocated in the heart of Amsterdam on the bustling shopping street Nieuwendijk, The Flying Pig Downtown offers convenience and excitement. Just a 3-minute walk from Amsterdam Central Station, our hostel is the perfect starting point for your city adventures.Comfortable Accommodation OptionsWhether you prefer a dormitory room with bunk beds or a private room with modern amenities like a TV for streaming and a refrigerator, we have you covered. Enjoy the convenience of shared or private shower and toilet facilities tailored to your needs.Vibrant Atmosphere and Dining OptionsStart your day with a delicious continental buffet breakfast before exploring the city. Unwind at our hotel bar or venture out to discover the diverse range of cafes and restaurants nearby. With Dam Square and the Anne Frank House within walking distance, your Amsterdam experience awaits.Ready to immerse yourself in the vibrant city life of Amsterdam?
