HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesWaikiki Beach Marriott Resort &amp; Spa, located across from the famous Waikiki beach, offers guests a truly luxurious experience. With 5 restaurants to choose from and the Royal Kaila Spa on-site, relaxation and indulgence are always at your fingertips.Entertainment and RecreationGuests at Marriott Waikiki Beach Resort and Spa can stay active and entertained with access to a gym, 2 outdoor swimming pools, and nightly live entertainment by the pool. Whether you prefer a workout or a refreshing dip, there's something for everyone to enjoy.Convenient LocationConveniently situated just a short walk from popular attractions like the Honolulu Zoo and Waikiki Aquarium, as well as being a stone's throw away from the beach, the Waikiki Beach Resort and Spa is the perfect choice for a memorable Hawaiian getaway.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Waikiki Beach Marriott Resort &amp; Spa!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  USD 55 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Comfortable beds
• Friendly staff
• Free beach accessories

Areas for Improvement:
• Expensive parking
• Noise disturbances at night
• Limited pool safety for kids
• Mixed reviews on value for money

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel is well located near key landmarks and public transport, with beach access and convenient proximity to tourist attractions.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues and noise disturbances.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with guests appreciating beach accessories but mentioning noise issues and limited pool safety for kids.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some finding the price high for services offered and others considering the amenities worth it.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests desired more variety and found breakfast expensive.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
