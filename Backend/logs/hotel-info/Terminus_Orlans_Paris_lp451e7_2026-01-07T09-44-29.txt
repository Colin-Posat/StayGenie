HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Terminus Orléans Paris, situated in the 14th district, offers easy access to Paris' renowned attractions with a metro and tram stop right at its doorstep.Charming AccommodationsEach room at the hotel features a simple yet cozy decor, equipped with modern amenities such as a safe, double-glazed windows, TV, and a private bathroom for a comfortable stay.Delightful Dining ExperienceIndulge in traditional French delicacies at the hotel's restaurant, complemented by a continental breakfast served in a dining area with a unique car theme, while also enjoying refreshing drinks at the on-site bar.Experience the best of Paris with Hotel Terminus Orléans Paris as your base.
