HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location &amp; AccessibilityHotel Chatelet Inn Kyoto is ideally situated just a 4-minute walk from Karasuma-Oike Subway Station, making it a perfect choice for travelers looking for convenience and easy access to transportation options.Comfortable Accommodation OptionsGuests at Kyoto Hotel Chatelet Inn can enjoy a comfortable stay in rooms located on the 4th to 10th floors, featuring modern furnishings and a choice between Japanese futon or Western-style bedding. Each room is equipped with essential amenities such as a TV, refrigerator, and tea/coffee making facilities, ensuring a relaxing and enjoyable stay.Additional Services &amp; FacilitiesIn addition to its prime location and cozy rooms, the hotel offers a range of services including bicycle rental, massage treatments, internet facilities, and laundry services. These extra conveniences cater to the diverse needs of guests, enhancing their overall experience during their stay.Book your stay at Hotel Chatelet Inn Kyoto now and experience comfort and convenience at an affordable price.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Spa and gym facilities at this property are unavailable due to Coronavirus (COVID-19).
Please note that all Japanese-style rooms come with enough futon mats for the number of guests staying in each room. Guests prepare their own futon mats.

Please note that on-site cafe will be temporarily closed for renovation from 21 March 2020 until further notice.
