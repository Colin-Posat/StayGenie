HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationJazz Hotel, conveniently located near Olbia Costa Smeralda Airport and the city centre, offers modern and air-conditioned rooms with LCD TVs and free Wi-Fi. The contemporary design, private terraces, and spa baths in suites provide a touch of luxury.Relaxation and DiningGuests can unwind in the exclusive swimming pool with a hot tub, emotional shower, and Turkish bath. The on-site restaurant serves a diverse selection of international cuisine for lunch and dinner. Enjoy a herbal tea in the relaxation area after a soothing spa session.Convenience and ConnectivityWith modern conference rooms and easy access to the area's attractions and main roads, Jazz Hotel is the perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers. Book now to experience a blend of comfort and sophistication.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although there were complaints about staff rudeness and unhelpfulness in some cases.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel is conveniently located close to key landmarks and public transport, although some guests found it impractical for walking to the city center.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were described as modern and comfortable, with a few mentions of maintenance issues impacting the overall experience.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with a mix of positive comments about the pool and negative feedback regarding the lack of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value proposition, with some guests feeling the price was high relative to the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received generally positive feedback, although some guests suggested improvements in variety and pricing.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
