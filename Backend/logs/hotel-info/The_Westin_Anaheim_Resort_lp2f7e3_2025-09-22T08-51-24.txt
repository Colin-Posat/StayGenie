GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Most guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, but a few mentioned issues with cleanliness such as dirty floors in some rooms.
• Service: 8.9/10 - Service was a highlight for most guests, with many praising the staff's friendliness and helpfulness. Specific mentions of exceptional service from staff like Soomin, William, and Gerardo.
• Location: 9.2/10 - The hotel's proximity to Disneyland and other local attractions was a major plus for guests, making it convenient for both leisure and business travelers.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests generally found the rooms to be spacious, comfortable, and well-maintained, although there were a few reports of minor issues like the absence of toiletries.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Guests were satisfied with the amenities offered, with positive mentions of the club lounge and rooftop lounge. Some guests expressed disappointment over missing toiletries and cleanliness of floors.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions were divided on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location and services, while others thought it was slightly high considering the offered amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Overall, guests enjoyed the food and beverage options at the hotel, with positive comments on breakfast, room service, and the variety of offerings. However, some guests suggested more variety could be added.
• Overall Experience: 8.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the staff's efforts to make their stay memorable. Many expressed a desire to return to the hotel for future visits.
