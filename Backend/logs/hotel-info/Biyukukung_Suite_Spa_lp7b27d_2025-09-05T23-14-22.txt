IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Biyukukung Suite & Spa in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that the property requires a deposit payment. Staff will contact guests directly for payment instructions.

In celebration of Nyepi Day (Day of Silence), Ngurah Rai International Airport will be closed, motor vehicles are allowed on the streets only for emergencies, guests are confined to the grounds of the resort.

Room service is available with the last order taken at 22:30. The property has 2 restaurants on-site that serve lunch and dinner, as well as afternoon tea from 15:00 to 17:00.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:
• Maintenance issues in rooms
• Variability in amenities satisfaction
• Mixed opinions on value for money

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Overall, guests were satisfied with the cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned specific areas that were not up to standard.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was a highlight for most guests, with many praising the friendly and accommodating staff. However, a few guests had negative experiences with slow response times.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's central yet quiet location near key attractions was widely appreciated by guests, making it convenient for exploring the area.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - While many guests enjoyed the comfort and design of the rooms, there were complaints about maintenance issues such as weak showers and old mattresses.
• Amenities: 7/10 - The hotel's amenities, including pools and spa services, received mixed reviews. Some guests appreciated them, while others felt there was room for improvement.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money offered by the hotel. Some guests found it to be worth the cost, while others thought it was overpriced considering the facilities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - The quality of food and beverages, particularly breakfast, received positive feedback from most guests, although some suggested more variety would enhance the experience.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and were likely to recommend the hotel to others, despite some minor issues.
