HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Enchanting Accommodation in Paris' 10th DistrictComfortable Rooms with Modern Amenities: Each guest room at Hôtel Liege Strasbourg features a flat-screen TV, a desk, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Accessibility is key, with an elevator reaching all floors except the 6th.Delicious Breakfast and Relaxing Lounge: Start your day with a delightful breakfast spread of French breads and pastries in the breakfast salon. Unwind later at the hotel's lounge bar with a refreshing drink, or grab a cold beverage from the vending machines.Convenient Location for Exploring: Situated near Gare de L’Est and Gare du Nord, the hotel is a stone's throw away from Canal St Martin's charming cafés and bars. Easy access to the metro at Chateau-d’Eau Station makes exploring the city a breeze.Experience the charm of Paris at Hôtel Liege Strasbourg - Book your stay now!
