Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for THB 120 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for THB 600 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.2/10 - Guests had mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some mentioning issues like dirty fridge and low water pressure in the basin.
• Service: 7.3/10 - Overall, service was appreciated by guests, although some mentioned issues with arranging shuttles and lack of follow-through.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised, being close to markets, eateries, and key landmarks.
• Room Quality: 6.7/10 - Rooms were generally well-received for their size and location, although some guests suggested updates to air conditioning and toilet facilities.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Guests had mixed feedback on amenities, with some appreciating daily refills and assists, while others mentioned minimal breakfast and low shower pressure.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some guests finding the price fair for the location, while others felt it was not justified due to issues like minimal breakfast and hard beds.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - The food and beverage services received generally positive feedback, with tasty breakfast mentioned by some despite issues like low variety and low water pressure.
• Overall Experience: 6.2/10 - Guests had a varied overall experience, with positive aspects like location and service offset by issues with cleanliness, room quality, and amenities.
