HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern and Convenient AccommodationCampanile Barcelona South offers spacious rooms with free internet access and flat-screen TVs, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests. Each room is tastefully decorated with contemporary designs, equipped with air conditioning and tea/coffee facilities for added convenience.Delicious Dining OptionsGuests can enjoy a varied buffet breakfast with continental and English choices at the hotel's restaurant. For dinner, Mediterranean dishes are served, providing a delightful culinary experience during your stay.Ideal Location for Business and LeisureConveniently located outside central Barcelona, near El Prat Airport, and in close proximity to Fira Cornellà Conference Centre, Campanile is the perfect choice for business travelers. With easy access to transportation links, exploring the vibrant city of Barcelona is effortless.Book your stay at Campanile Barcelona South for a memorable experience!

Please note that a valid photo ID and a credit card corresponding to the name on the booking are required at check-in.
