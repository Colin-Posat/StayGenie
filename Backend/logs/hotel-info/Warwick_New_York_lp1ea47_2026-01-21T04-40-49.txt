HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Hotel in Midtown ManhattanWarwick New York, a historic hotel in midtown Manhattan, offers a unique blend of luxury and convenience. Located just steps away from Central Park, this elegant hotel boasts 2 restaurants, a fitness centre, and concierge services to cater to all your needs.Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in the lavish accommodations at Warwick New York, where every room features marble bathrooms, desks with international plug-ins, flat-screen TVs, and fully stocked minibars. Enjoy a comfortable and stylish stay in the heart of the city.Dining ExperiencesExperience culinary delights at Mural’s on 54th, the hotel's onsite restaurant named after the renowned Dean Cornwell. For a taste of traditional American cuisine, visit Randolph’s Bar and Lounge. Delight your taste buds without leaving the hotel premises.Ready to experience the charm of Warwick New York? Book your stay now and create unforgettable memories in the heart of Manhattan.
