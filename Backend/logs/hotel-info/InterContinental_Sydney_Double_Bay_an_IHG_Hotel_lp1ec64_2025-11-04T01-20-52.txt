HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Seaside AccommodationIndulge in the luxurious seaside accommodation at InterContinental Sydney Double Bay, just a short drive from the bustling Sydney City Centre. Relax by the stunning outdoor swimming pool, surrounded by limestone rock walls and chic day beds, or sip a cocktail at the rooftop bar while enjoying a picturesque sunset.Convenient Location and AmenitiesLocated a stone's throw away from Sydney Opera House, with a variety of shopping and dining options nearby, InterContinental Double Bay offers the perfect blend of convenience and luxury. The hotel also provides air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV, minibar, and complimentary toiletries.Exceptional ServicesExperience top-notch services such as room service, concierge assistance, and valet parking during your stay. Whether you're exploring the city or unwinding at the hotel, InterContinental Sydney Double Bay ensures a memorable and comfortable experience.Ready to elevate your stay? Book now at InterContinental Sydney Double Bay for a luxurious seaside retreat!

Please inform InterContinental Sydney Double Bay, an IHG Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.

Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
