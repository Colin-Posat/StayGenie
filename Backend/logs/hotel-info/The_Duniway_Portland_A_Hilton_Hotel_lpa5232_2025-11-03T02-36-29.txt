Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews regarding cleanliness, with some guests mentioning rooms not being the cleanest.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with courteous and helpful staff mentioned in multiple reviews.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Great location in the city center, close to attractions and with easy access to public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were comfortable with some mentioning great views, although a few reviews highlighted maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests appreciating convenient parking while others mentioned the lack of included breakfast.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Varied opinions on the value for money, with some guests feeling the parking costs were high.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on the restaurant service and food quality, although some guests wished for more inclusive breakfast options.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and praised the staff, location, and comfortable rooms.
