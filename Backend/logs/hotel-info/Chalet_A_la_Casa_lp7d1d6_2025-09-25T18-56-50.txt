HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Panoramic Views and Ski-In AccessChalet A la Casa offers stunning panoramic views of Zermatt and the Matterhorn, making it a picturesque retreat for guests. With ski-in access in the winter, you can hit the slopes and return right to the front door, ensuring a convenient and enjoyable stay.Modern Amenities and Free Wi-FiEach unit at Chalet A la Casa is equipped with a kitchen, allowing guests the flexibility to prepare their meals. Additionally, the property provides free Wi-Fi, keeping you connected throughout your stay and making it easy to share your memorable experiences with loved ones.Convenient Location and Easy AccessSituated just 150 meters from the ski bus stop and a short 10-minute walk from the center of Zermatt, Chalet A la Casa offers the perfect blend of convenience and tranquility. Connected by a lift to the riverside, guests can easily explore the surrounding area and enjoy all that Zermatt has to offer.Experience the beauty and convenience of Chalet A la Casa - book your stay now for an unforgettable mountain getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Chalet A la Casa in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.

Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for CHF 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children from 2 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for CHF 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
