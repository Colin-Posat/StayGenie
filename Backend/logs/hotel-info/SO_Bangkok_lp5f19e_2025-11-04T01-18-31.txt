HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Urban Oasis with Elemental InspirationThis stylish urban oasis, SO/ Bangkok, rises 30 stories above the city, offering themed rooms inspired by the 5 natural elements. The elegant and modern rooms feature full-length windows with views of the city or Lumpini Park, equipped with smart TVs, free WiFi, and complimentary minibars.Convenient Location and Access to LandmarksLocated just 200 meters from Lumphini MRT Subway Station, SO/ Bangkok provides easy access to the city's landmarks and attractions. Within walking distance to Silom Road and Sala Daeng BTS Skytrain Station, guests can explore the city effortlessly.Luxurious Amenities and Dining ExperiencesIndulge in world-class treatments at the mythological forest-inspired SoSPA, relax by the outdoor pool, or stay active at the fitness center. Enjoy a culinary journey at Red Oven, HI-SO Rooftop Bar, or Chocolab Café for a delightful dining experience.Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at SO/ Bangkok!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for THB 3,531 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
