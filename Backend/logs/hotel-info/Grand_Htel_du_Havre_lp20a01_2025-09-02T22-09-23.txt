HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ParisLocated in the chic 9th district beside Saint-Lazare Train Station, this hotel offers convenience and style for your Parisian getaway.Comfortable Rooms and AmenitiesFeaturing classic décor, satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, and private bathrooms with hairdryers, each room ensures a cozy stay. Enjoy the ease of access with a lift available for all rooms.Convenient Services and Nearby AttractionsWith a 24-hour reception, the hotel offers assistance in reserving taxis and show tickets. Explore the city with Galeries Lafayette and Madeleine Church within walking distance.Book now for an unforgettable experience at Hôtel du Havre!

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children from 2 years old to 12 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.8/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with mentions of old carpet, stained carpets, and dirty sheets.
• Service: 6.9/10 - Service received mixed feedback, with complaints about rude reception staff and slow responses to issues.
• Location: 8.2/10 - Location near train stations and central Paris attractions was widely praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Room quality varied, with comments on outdated decor, small room sizes, and noise disturbances.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Lack of amenities like microwaves, refrigerators, and tea/coffee facilities mentioned as drawbacks.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was fair for the location while others found it overpriced for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6.1/10 - Average ratings for food and beverage options, with some guests highlighting the mid-level breakfast offerings.
• Overall Experience: 5.8/10 - Overall experience received varied feedback, with reports of rude staff, noisy rooms, and lack of basic amenities.
