• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but there were complaints about room size, maintenance issues, and cleanliness.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests had mixed reviews about the amenities, with some praising the location and service, while others mentioned issues like lack of proper ventilation and bathroom hooks.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Opinions on value for money were split, with some guests feeling the hotel was overpriced for the quality provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services received average feedback, with guests mentioning the adequacy of breakfast and the lack of complimentary water bottles.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience with the hotel, citing factors like location, cleanliness, and comfortable beds.
