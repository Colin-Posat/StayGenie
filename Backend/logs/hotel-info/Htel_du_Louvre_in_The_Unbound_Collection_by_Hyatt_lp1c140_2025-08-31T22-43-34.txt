HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Hotel du Louvre in the Unbound Collection by Hyatt offers elegant guest rooms in a Hausmannian-style building, renovated in 2019. Each air-conditioned room features satellite TV, a minibar, and free WiFi access. Some rooms provide stunning views of Parisian landmarks, and all rooms include a private bathroom with marble accents.Exquisite Dining ExperienceIndulge in culinary delights at La Brasserie du Louvre by Bocuse, showcasing iconic dishes by Bocuse in a traditional setting. Enjoy meals on the terrace overlooking the Louvre museum. The Bar offers cocktails and jazz evenings under an authentic glass roof. Start your day with a buffet breakfast.Unique Activities and LocationDiscover hidden gems of Paris with complimentary activities such as boules at Tuileries Garden and guided tours. Picnic baskets are available for a fee to enjoy near the hotel. Conveniently located near Palais Royal Metro Station, explore the city with ease.Experience luxury and charm at Hotel du Louvre. Book your stay now to immerse yourself in Parisian elegance.
