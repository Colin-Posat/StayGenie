HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Eiffel Tower ViewsExperience Paris in style at Duquesne Eiffel, where soundproofed rooms with free Wi-Fi and Eiffel Tower views await you. Each air-conditioned room features a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom for your comfort.Convenient AmenitiesStart your day with a delicious breakfast buffet and unwind at the hotel bar. With a 24-hour reception offering local tourist information, your stay will be hassle-free. Elevator access is available to all rooms for your convenience.Prime LocationSituated just minutes away from Parc du Champs de Mars and Ecole Militaire Metro station, our hotel offers easy access to Paris's top attractions. Explore the city with direct metro routes to Champs-Elysees, Opera Garnier, and Place de la Bastille.Book your stay at Duquesne Eiffel now for an unforgettable Parisian experience!
