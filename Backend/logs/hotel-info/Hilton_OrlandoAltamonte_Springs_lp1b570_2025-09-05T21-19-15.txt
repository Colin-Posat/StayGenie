HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Altamonte MallThis Altamonte Springs hotel is just a 5 minutes' drive from the popular Altamonte Mall, offering guests easy access to shopping and entertainment options.Modern Amenities and FacilitiesGuests can enjoy free Wi-Fi, an on-site fitness centre, restaurant, and outdoor pool during their stay. The rooms are equipped with flat-screen TVs, small refrigerators, and coffee makers. Some rooms even have separate living areas for added comfort.Dining Options and ServicesThe U Restaurant offers American fusion cuisine for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, while the Burger Bar allows guests to customize their own hamburgers. The hotel also provides transfer services, a business centre, meeting and conference facilities, and a second fitness centre for added convenience.Book your stay at Hilton Orlando Altamonte Springs now for a memorable experience!
