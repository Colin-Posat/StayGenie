IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests must check in by 19:30 to eat dinner at this property. Guests who check in after this time may not be served dinner, and no refund will be given.
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
A Japanese dinner featuring seasonal dishes and a Japanese breakfast are served in the dining area (advance reservations required).
If you require additional meals, please inform the property at least 3 days in advance.
If you have any allergies or food preferences, please inform the property at least 5 days in advance.
Please note that guests with a dinner inclusive reservation who arrive after 20:00 will not be able to receive dinner.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Clean rooms
• Friendly staff
• Scenic and tranquil location
• Delicious kaiseki meals
• Comfortable futons

Areas for Improvement:
• Language barriers
• Questionable authenticity of onsen water
• Expensive pricing

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.4/10 - Guests consistently mentioned clean rooms and well-maintained amenities.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Staff were praised for being friendly and helpful, although some guests experienced language barriers.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's tranquil and scenic location in Kyoto's foothills was highly appreciated by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Spacious traditional rooms with comfortable futons, although some guests found the tatami mats lacking in cushioning.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Good variety of amenities and beautiful communal bath, but some guests questioned the authenticity of the onsen water.
• Value for Money: 7.7/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling it was worth the cost for the experience, while others found it expensive.
• Food and Beverage: 8.1/10 - High praise for the delicious kaiseki meals served, with some guests mentioning a lack of variety for picky eaters.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - The majority of guests had exceptional and unforgettable experiences, praising the beautiful surroundings and attentive service.
