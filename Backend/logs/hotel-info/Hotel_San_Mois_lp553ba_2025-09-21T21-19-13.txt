HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsLocated just a short stroll from Saint Mark's Square, Hotel San Moisè offers a unique stay in a 16th-century building overlooking a picturesque canal. The classic Venetian rooms seamlessly blend historic charm with modern conveniences like satellite TV and tea/coffee makers.Exceptional Services and AmenitiesGuests at San Moisè can enjoy complimentary tickets to the Venice Casino, free international newspapers, and commission-free opera reservations, all available at the reception. In the summer, relax in the tranquil courtyard with a refreshing drink, and start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast in the dining room with stunning canal views.Prime Location and Easy AccessConveniently situated just 200 meters from La Fenice Theatre, and with the Vaporetto stop for Venice Santa Lucia Train Station a mere 500 meters away, Hotel San Moisè offers the perfect base for exploring Venice.Book your stay at Hotel San Moisè now for an unforgettable Venetian experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 7 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Location: 8.8/10 - The central location close to key attractions and convenient transport options was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were described as adequate, with some complaints about size and inaccurate descriptions.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Amenities like breakfast and mini-bar were mentioned positively, but room size and description were areas of concern.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price didn't match the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast options were satisfactory, but guests desired more variety and on-site dining options.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests reported positive overall experiences, citing friendly staff and cleanliness.
