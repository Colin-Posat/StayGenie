HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 15 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 17 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3.5/10 - Guests expressed significant dissatisfaction with cleanliness, citing issues such as hair, pubic hairs on beds, dead moth, mold, dust, and unclean bathroom.
• Service: 6.8/10 - Opinions on service were mixed. While some guests found the staff normal and affable, others reported problems with housekeeping and cleanliness.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its convenience and proximity to key areas, despite the reported incidents with taxi drivers.
• Room Quality: 6.3/10 - The room quality received mixed reviews, with some mentioning comfortable beds and good value, while others complained about cleanliness and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Guests mentioned the lack of an elevator as a downside affecting convenience, while appreciating the affordable pricing.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - There were differing opinions on value for money, with some guests finding it reasonable for the services provided, while others mentioned cleanliness and maintenance concerns.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Breakfast and location were highlighted as positives, while cleanliness and room maintenance issues impacted the overall experience.
• Overall Experience: 5/10 - The overall sentiment was mixed, with a significant number of negative points affecting the average rating.
