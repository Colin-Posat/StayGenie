Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.3/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on cleanliness, with some mentioning cleanliness as a positive aspect, while others highlighted issues like uncleanliness or outdated facilities.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Overall, service was appreciated by guests. Some guests mentioned helpful staff at the front desk, while others had complaints about slow response times and unapologetic management.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location received praise, being close to public transport and key landmarks. However, some guests mentioned inconveniences related to nearby construction and film crew disturbances.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable by some guests, but others mentioned issues such as small room size, uncomfortable beds, and maintenance problems.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found amenities satisfactory, with some highlighting the rooftop bar and friendly staff. However, complaints were also raised about limited amenities like no in-room coffee services or unavailable spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions on value for money varied among guests. Some felt the price was justified given the location, while others considered it too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested improvements like more variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 7/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience, citing factors such as great location, helpful staff, and enjoyable amenities. However, there were also mentions of disappointments like comfort issues and unmet expectations.
