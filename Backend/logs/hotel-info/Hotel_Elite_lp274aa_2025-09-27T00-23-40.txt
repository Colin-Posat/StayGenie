HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in RomeHotel Elite, nestled in the heart of Rome's Spagna district, offers a prime location just steps away from iconic landmarks such as Barberini Metro Station, Piazza Barberini, and Quirinal Hill.Modern AmenitiesExperience comfort and convenience in our air-conditioned rooms featuring free WiFi, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a safety deposit box. Enjoy a cup of tea anytime with the in-room kettle provided for your relaxation.Near Major AttractionsExplore Rome effortlessly with popular attractions like Spagna Metro Station, Via Condotti, and Repubblica - Teatro dell'Opera Metro Station within walking distance. Don't miss out on the vibrant city center and the famous Spanish Steps just 800 meters away.To immerse yourself in the beauty of Rome, book your stay at Hotel Elite today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please inform Hotel Elite in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note airport shuttle service and nearby private parking are at extra costs.

If you require an invoice, please include your company details in the Special Requests box when booking.

When booking a Non-Refundable Rate, please make sure that the name on the credit card used for the booking corresponds to the guest staying at the property. Otherwise, a third-party authorisation by the cardholder must be submitted when booking. Please note that the credit card used for the booking must be shown at check-in.

Check-in and check-out is at sister hotel White, in via in Arcione 77, at 4 minutes walking distance.
