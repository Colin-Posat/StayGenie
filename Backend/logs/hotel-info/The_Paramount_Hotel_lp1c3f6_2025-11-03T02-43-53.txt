HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in modern comfort at The Paramount Hotel, where each room offers free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and stylish furnishings. Relax in a cozy atmosphere adorned with dark wood furniture and plush linens, ensuring a restful stay.Convenient LocationLocated just minutes away from top attractions like Pike Place Fish Market and the Seattle Convention Center, our hotel is the perfect hub for exploring the vibrant city. With the Space Needle a mere mile away, you'll be at the heart of all the action.Exceptional AmenitiesStay active at our fitness centre, savor delicious meals at the onsite restaurant, and take advantage of our business centre for any work needs. With Seattle Tacoma International Airport a short drive away, your journey to relaxation begins the moment you arrive at The Paramount Hotel.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
