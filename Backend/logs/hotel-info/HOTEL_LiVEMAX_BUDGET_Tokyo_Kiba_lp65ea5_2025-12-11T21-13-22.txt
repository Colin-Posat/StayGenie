IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Credit card will be used for guarantee purposes only. When settling the bill, the hotel will accept cash only (in Japanese Yen).

Guests must check in by 22:00.

The hotel is nearest to Exit 1 of Kiba Subway Station.

Transfer at Otemachi Subway Station to reach Tokyo Station. Tokyo Station is on the Marunouchi Subway Line, and Kiba Station is on the Tozai Subway Line.

To reach Tokyo Sky Tree, transfer at Nihonbashi Station to the Asakusa Line, and get off the subway at Oshiage Station.
To reach Tsukiji Fish Market, transfer at Kayabacho Station to the Hibiya subway line and get off at Tsukiji Station.

Guests can send their luggage to the hotel the day before or on the day of arrival. They must notify the hotel in advance, and the delivery fee must be paid by the sender at time of shipping. The hotel may not be able to accept the luggage without prior notification.
