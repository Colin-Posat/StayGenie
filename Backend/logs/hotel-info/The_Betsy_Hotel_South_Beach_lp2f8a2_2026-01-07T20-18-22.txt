HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and AmenitiesThe Betsy Hotel, South Beach offers a unique blend of luxury and comfort, with each room adorned with fresh orchids, original art, hardwood floors, and marble bathrooms. Enjoy modern conveniences like flat-screen TVs and minibars, ensuring a relaxing stay.Rooftop Oasis with Stunning ViewsRelax and unwind at Betsy's rooftop pool with 360-degree views, perfect for soaking up the sun or enjoying a cocktail at the Ocean Deck. Guests can also take advantage of the fitness center with Peloton bikes, private beach service, and ocean views for a truly rejuvenating experience.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentIndulge in a culinary journey at LT Steak &amp; Seafood, a contemporary American steakhouse, or sip on cocktails at the Lobby Bar. Don't miss the Carlton Room Café for your daily coffee fix. With entertainment options like the Carlton Room Lounge and proximity to Miami Beach Convention Centre, The Betsy Hotel offers a complete experience for your stay.Book now for an unforgettable stay at The Betsy Hotel, South Beach.
