Hotel parking policy guarantees 1 car per room. Additional parking permits are available for 10 USD per day per vehicle. Parking is only available for guests at the hotel.

All reservations are subject to an authorization 48 hours prior to arrival up to or equal to the first night, excluding prepaid reservations.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 17 years old and over stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
