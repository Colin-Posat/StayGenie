HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Décor and Comfortable RoomsThe Hotel des Beaux-Arts offers modern décor in a warm setting, providing guests with a comfortable stay. Each room is equipped with Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, and a work desk, ensuring convenience and entertainment during your visit.Charming Inner Courtyard and Delicious BreakfastRelax in the hotel's inner courtyard, surrounded by lush greenery, and enjoy a delightful homemade continental breakfast. Indulge in fresh pastries, breads, hot drinks, and freshly squeezed orange juice while starting your day in a tranquil outdoor setting.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated just minutes away from multiple metro stations, including Tolbiac, Place d'Italie, and Olympiades, the hotel provides easy access to popular landmarks like the Louvre Museum, Eiffel Tower, and Gare de Lyon. Explore the vibrant Rue de la Butte-aux-Cailles, known for its lively bars and restaurants, a short walk from the hotel.Book your stay at the Hotel des Beaux-Arts for a memorable and convenient Parisian experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hôtel des Beaux Arts in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that when booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:

PARKING:

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
