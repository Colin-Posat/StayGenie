HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 16 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.13/10 - Several reviews mentioned issues with cleanliness, including an unpleasant arrival experience.
• Service: 9.6/10 - Multiple positive mentions of staff's friendliness and responsiveness, especially Eduardo's exceptional service.
• Location: 9.64/10 - The location received consistent praise for being convenient and close to attractions.
• Room Quality: 8.59/10 - Reviews highlighted spacious rooms but noted some issues with linens and towels.
• Amenities: 8.62/10 - Amenities were generally rated positively, although some noted the pool and restaurant were dated.
• Value for Money: 8.58/10 - The cost was considered high for the service and cleanliness provided, indicating room for improvement in value.
• Food and Beverage: 8.13/10 - Breakfast options were seen as overpriced, and there were some issues with service in the restaurant.
• Overall Experience: 9.1/10 - Overall stay was pleasant for most guests, with some notable issues.
