HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated just a 5-minute walk from the historic Old Town of Biel and Biel Train Station, Hotel La Balance self check-in offers easy access to the city's key attractions. A bus stop conveniently located right in front ensures hassle-free transportation.Comfortable RoomsThe Artus Hotel boasts well-equipped rooms featuring amenities such as cable TV, a safe, and a work desk for your convenience. With all rooms accessible by lift, comfort and accessibility are guaranteed.Nearby AttractionsExplore the beauty of Lake Biel with the shipping pier just 1 km away, and unwind at the Strandbad Nidau, a public beach located 2 km from the hotel. Additionally, a variety of dining options, shops, and bars are within a short 200-meter distance.Book your stay now at Hotel La Balance for a memorable experience in Biel.
