HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Direct Beach Access and Outdoor PoolHotel Almirante is the only hotel with direct access to Alicante's San Juan Beach, allowing guests to enjoy the Costa Blanca's longest beach right at their doorstep. The large outdoor pool provides a perfect spot to relax and soak up the sun.Convenient Location and TransportationSituated just a 10-minute drive from Alicante city centre and 20 minutes from the airport, Hotel Almirante offers a prime location for both leisure and business travelers. With free on-site parking and a tram stop only 100 meters away, getting around is easy and stress-free.Modern Rooms and Dining ExperienceEach modern, air-conditioned room at the Almirante features a flat-screen satellite TV and a private bathroom with amenities. The on-site Pocardy restaurant serves a delicious buffet breakfast and Mediterranean cuisine for lunch and dinner, providing a delightful dining experience for guests.To experience beachfront luxury and exceptional service, book your stay at Hotel Almirante today.
