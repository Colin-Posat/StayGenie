HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the enchanting 3100 Kulmhotel Gornergrat, where breathtaking alpine views meet ultimate comfort.Ski-In/Ski-Out Access: Enjoy the thrill of skiing right from your doorstep with direct access to Zermatt-Matterhorn Ski Paradise. Unwind after an exhilarating day on the slopes in the cozy ambiance of our welcoming hotel.Inviting Accommodations: Choose from our 22 well-appointed guestrooms, each featuring flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms with complimentary toiletries, and daily housekeeping to ensure a relaxed and refreshing stay.Culinary Delights: Start each day with our complimentary buffet breakfast, and savor delightful meals at Vis a Vis, where you'll experience local flavors in a captivating setting.Elevate your mountain escape to the next level—book your stay at 3100 Kulmhotel Gornergrat today!
