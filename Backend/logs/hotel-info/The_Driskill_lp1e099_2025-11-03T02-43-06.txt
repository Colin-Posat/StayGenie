HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 16 years old stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 55 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Overall, guests found the hotel to be clean and well-maintained, although some reviews mentioned concerns about mold and datedness.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was a standout aspect with many guests praising the staff's friendliness and attentiveness.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's central location near key attractions and venues was highly appreciated by guests.
• Room Quality: 7/10 - While some guests enjoyed the character and comfort of the rooms, others raised concerns about outdated decor and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews were received regarding amenities, with some guests praising the historic charm while others criticized the lack of modern facilities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Several guests felt that the hotel was expensive for the value provided, especially considering the need for renovations and updates.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Guests generally appreciated the food offerings, although some mentioned limited options or high prices.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing the historical charm, comfortable beds, and friendly staff as highlights.
