HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views and Luxurious AmenitiesOffering breathtaking views of the Caribbean Sea and Nichupté Lagoon, Secrets The Vine Cancun is a luxurious AAA Four Diamond resort located on Kukulkan Boulevard. The modern and stylish hotel boasts 6 restaurants, 3 outdoor swimming pools, and a rejuvenating spa.Unparalleled Spa ExperienceIndulge in relaxation at the spa which features a sauna, steam room, hydrotherapy, beauty treatments, a hair salon, and a fully-equipped gym. Pamper yourself with a range of wellness options and leave feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.Cutting-Edge Technology and Culinary DelightsExperience convenience with the free connectivity app offered exclusively to guests for unlimited calls and messages worldwide. Delight your taste buds with international cuisine at the buffet and à la carte restaurants offering fusion Asian, Italian, Mexican, Peruvian, and Mediterranean flavors.Book now to experience luxury and serenity at Secrets The Vine Cancun!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
A damage deposit of USD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that when reserving more than 10 rooms group policies will apply. Please contact the property with the details provided in your confirmation for instructions.

This property enforces a dress code in the restaurants.

Blue Water Grill, Dragons & Nebbiolo:

Casual Elegance: Sneakers, tennis shoes or dress sandals (no flip-flops). Dress shirts with sleeves. Long pants required for men. No sleeveless shirts for men.

Market Cafe, Olio & Sea Salt Grill:

Casual: Bermudas, long shorts or capri pants. Sneakers, tennis shoes or sandals. No sleeveless shirts for men. No bathing suits. Ladies must wear a bathing suit wrap or cover-up.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Overall, cleanliness was mentioned positively, but a guest found a plastic wrap in their drink.
• Service: 8/10 - Staff were generally friendly and attentive, with the exception of slow room service and issues with restocking water bottles.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its proximity to key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were described as beautiful with great showers, but some mentioned problems with room temperature food and cleanliness.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Guests highlighted amenities like the coffee shop and karaoke night, but mentioned issues with the spa and main pool cleanliness.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some finding the resort not worth the price tag.
• Food and Beverage: 8.5/10 - Food and drinks were greatly enjoyed, with a standout mention for Asian and Italian restaurants.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Despite some shortcomings, most guests had an amazing overall experience at the hotel.
