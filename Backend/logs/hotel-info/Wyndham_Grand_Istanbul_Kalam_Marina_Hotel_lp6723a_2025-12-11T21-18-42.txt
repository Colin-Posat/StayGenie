HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but some reported maintenance issues in the rooms.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with staff being friendly and helpful. However, some guests faced issues during check-in and room changes.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel is well-located near the beach and parks, with good access to transport links. Some rooms offer picturesque views of the marina and sea.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, but some guests complained about unpleasant smells and outdated amenities.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Guests enjoyed the pools, spa, and gym facilities. However, there were complaints about broken sunbeds and lack of transparency in pricing.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money. Some guests felt the services offered justified the price, while others found it expensive.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally positive, with some complaints about food quality and variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, especially appreciating the staff's dedication and helpfulness.
