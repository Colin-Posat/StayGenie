HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover a delightful stay at Ibis Paris Gare Montparnasse Catalogne, where comfort meets convenience in the heart of Paris.Unbeatable LocationEnjoy easy access to iconic Parisian landmarks, with Montparnasse Tower and the Paris Catacombs just a short stroll away. The vibrant atmosphere of the city is right at your doorstep!Modern AmenitiesRelax in our 146 stylish guest rooms, complete with LED televisions, rainfall showers, and blackout curtains to ensure a restful night. Enjoy seamless connectivity with complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.Delicious Dining OptionsSavor a delectable buffet breakfast each morning or grab a quick bite at our snack bar/deli. Wind down with your favorite drink at the bar/lounge, making it a perfect spot to relax after a day of exploring.Book your stay now and immerse yourself in a Parisian experience like no other!
