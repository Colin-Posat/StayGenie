HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Beachfront Accommodation:Edgewood Tahoe Resort in Stateline offers a 5-star experience with an outdoor pool, fitness center, and terrace. Enjoy a private beach area and a bar just steps away from Lakeside Beach.Culinary Delights:Indulge in a variety of dining options at the resort's restaurant, from American to French cuisine. Special dietary needs are catered to with vegetarian, dairy-free, and vegan choices available upon request.Recreation and Relaxation:Unwind in the hot tub, enjoy evening entertainment, or stay active with table tennis, hiking, and skiing nearby. The resort also offers a helpful reception providing area information for guests.Book now for a luxurious stay at Edgewood Tahoe Resort, where relaxation and adventure await!
