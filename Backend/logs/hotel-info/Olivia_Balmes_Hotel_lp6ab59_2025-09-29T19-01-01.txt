HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Stay in the Heart of BarcelonaThe Olivia Balmes Hotel offers a luxurious stay in the heart of Barcelona's elegant Eixample neighborhood. With stylish air-conditioned rooms, free Wi-Fi, and flat-screen TVs, guests can unwind in comfort.Relaxation and Dining OptionsGuests can enjoy the outdoor swimming pool, sun terrace, and sun loungers, perfect for relaxation. The hotel's restaurant serves delicious meals throughout the day, while the bar offers cocktails and snacks.Convenience and AccessibilityWith private parking and proximity to Barcelona Airport, convenience is key at Olivia Balmes Hotel. Explore nearby attractions like Plaza Catalunya and Gaudi's iconic architecture within minutes.Book your stay at Olivia Balmes Hotel for a blend of luxury, convenience, and relaxation in Barcelona.

• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
