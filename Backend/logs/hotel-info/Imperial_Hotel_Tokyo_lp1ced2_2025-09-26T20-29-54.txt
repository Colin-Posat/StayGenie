HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsImperial Hotel Tokyo, a prestigious establishment since 1890, offers elegantly decorated rooms with modern amenities, including free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and en suite bathrooms. A perfect blend of comfort and sophistication for a memorable stay.Top-Notch FacilitiesEnjoy access to a fitness center, indoor pool, and beauty massage salon during your stay. Indulge in a workout session, relax with a massage, or take a refreshing dip in the pool - all within the hotel premises.Diverse Dining OptionsSatisfy your culinary cravings with a choice of 13 restaurants and 3 bars at Imperial Hotel. From French cuisine to Teppanyaki, lavish buffets to classic cocktails, there's something to please every palate.Book now for an unforgettable experience at Imperial Hotel Tokyo!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Imperial Hotel Tokyo in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
To use the property's airport limousine buses, please make a reservation at least 1 day in advance. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

When booked for 3-person use, a Twin Room with an extra bed will automatically be assigned.

Please note that the swimming pool, sauna facilities and reception on 20th floor of the Tower building will be closed from January 6 to 8, 2025 due to statutory maintenance.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  JPY 12000 per  day.
