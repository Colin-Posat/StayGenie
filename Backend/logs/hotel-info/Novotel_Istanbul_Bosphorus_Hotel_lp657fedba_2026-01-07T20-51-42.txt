HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Bosphorus ViewsThe Novotel Istanbul Bosphorus Hotel offers contemporary rooms with stunning views of the Bosphorus and the city. Each room is elegantly designed and equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV and air conditioning.Relaxation and Wellness FacilitiesGuests can start their day with a gourmet buffet breakfast and then unwind at the indoor pool or the spa center, which features a variety of relaxing options such as massages, saunas, steam rooms, Turkish baths, and fitness facilities.Convenient Location for Exploring IstanbulConveniently situated in Karakoy, guests can easily explore Istanbul's top attractions such as the Istanbul Modern Art Museum, Galata Tower, and the Spice Bazaar. With nearby access to transportation options like the funicular and ferry ports, as well as free on-site parking, exploring the city is a breeze.Book your stay at Novotel Istanbul Bosphorus Hotel for a luxurious and convenient Istanbul experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 10 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
