HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Architecture and Gourmet DiningExperience the charm of historic architecture at our 5-star hotel in Zocalo, Mexico City. Indulge in authentic Mexican cuisine at our gourmet restaurant with stunning views of Zócalo Plaza and the National Palace.Luxurious Amenities and ServicesRelax in comfort with bathrobes, slippers, and a minibar in each guest room. Stay connected with free WiFi and unwind at the gym. Our non-smoking hotel also offers laundry services and 12 meeting rooms for your convenience.Prime Location and Nearby AttractionsImmerse yourself in the cultural heart of the city with a stay at Gran Hotel Ciudad de Mexico. Explore the Metropolitan Cathedral, the Presidential Palace with art by Diego Rivera, and the lush gardens of Castillo de Chapultepec, all just steps away.Book your stay now and elevate your Mexico City experience at Gran Hotel Ciudad de Mexico.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
