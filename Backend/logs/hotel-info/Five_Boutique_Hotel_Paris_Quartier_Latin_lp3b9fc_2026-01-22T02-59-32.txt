HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Unique Features - Each contemporary room in our boutique hotel is equipped with a flat-screen TV offering over 100 multi-lingual channels. The star-studded ceiling and glass desks, featuring optic fibres, add a touch of elegance and modernity to your stay.Delicious Breakfast and Convenient Amenities - Start your day with a delightful buffet breakfast of tea, coffee, and French pastries in our modern dining room. Throughout the day, enjoy the convenience of our honesty bar stocked with a variety of snacks and beverages for you to indulge in at any time.Prime Location with Easy Access to Attractions - Situated in the vibrant Latin Quarter, just 300 metres from Rue Mouffetard, our hotel offers easy access to Les Gobelins Metro Station and is a short 15-minute walk to the beautiful Luxembourg Gardens. Book your stay now for a memorable experience!
