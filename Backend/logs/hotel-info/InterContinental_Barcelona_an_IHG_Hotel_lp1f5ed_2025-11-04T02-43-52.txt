IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of EUR 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that only guests aged 16 or older can access the spa. Guests under 18 must be accompanied by an adult to access the spa

Please note: Spa access has an extra cost of 40 EUR per adult and access (45minutes).

Please note that the rates offered with dinner include three course dinner from set menu in a designated hotel restaurant for one or two resident adults sharing a room. A la carte dinner is not available for those reservations.

What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Comfortable beds
• Spacious rooms
• Rooftop bar with great views

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited breakfast variety
• Mixed reviews on cleanliness and maintenance issues
• Distance to main attractions

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests generally mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, with a few minor complaints about maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests, with specific mentions of friendly and helpful staff.
• Location: 7.8/10 - The hotel is situated in a quieter area, which some guests found peaceful, while others noted the distance to main attractions as a drawback.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were described as spacious and comfortable, with some guests highlighting the comfortable beds.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, although some improvement was suggested, particularly in terms of food quality.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was reasonable given the quality, while others mentioned high prices for limited services.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Reviews on breakfast and food services were mixed, with some positive feedback on the rooftop bar and dinner experiences.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience, particularly highlighting the friendly staff and comfortable rooms, with some minor issues mentioned.
