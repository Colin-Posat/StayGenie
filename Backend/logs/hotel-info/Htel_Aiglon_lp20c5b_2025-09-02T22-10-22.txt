HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Design in Montparnasse DistrictThis stylish hotel in the heart of Montparnasse offers modern guest rooms just steps away from the Raspail Metro stop. Enjoy soundproofed and air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a mini-bar.Relaxing Amenities and ServicesStart your day with a continental breakfast in the dining room or your room, then unwind with a drink at the bar or in the library lounge. Benefit from a fully renovated hotel in 2023, a 24-hour reception with express check-in, car hire, and a tour desk.Convenience and ComfortAccessible via a lift, the Aiglon ensures a comfortable stay with its convenient amenities. Whether you're here for leisure or business, this hotel caters to all your needs. Book now for a memorable stay in Paris.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that the Cosy Double Room cannot accommodate a baby cot.

The photo identification and the credit card used to make the reservation are required upon check-in. The name on the photo ID and the credit card must match. Otherwise, the credit card will be re-credited and payment will be required directly at the reception desk.

Guests paying in cash will be requested to pay a EUR 100 deposit.

The Hôtel Aiglon was completely redecorated in 2023 in a revisited art deco spirit.
