IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
From Ginza Metro Station, Exit A3 is nearest to the hotel. Upon leaving the exit, continue straight out of the exit and turn left between GINZA SIX and the Ginza Lion Building, and the hotel is on your left.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.5/10 - While some guests appreciated the cleanliness, others reported issues with dirty toilets, walls, and furniture.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Service was generally positive, with polite staff and efficient luggage assistance. However, some guests found self-check-in confusing.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's central location in Ginza was highly praised for its proximity to key landmarks, shopping areas, and transportation options.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Guests mentioned comfortable beds and spacious rooms, but some noted issues with room size and maintenance.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests appreciated amenities like laundry facilities and complimentary beverages, but some mentioned a lack of daily cleaning services and limited breakfast variety.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some feeling the price was justified by the location while others found it expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, although some guests wished for more variety in the food offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, with location, service, and cleanliness being key highlights.
