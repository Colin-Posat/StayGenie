HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated in the heart of Montparnasse, Hôtel Renoir Montparnasse is within walking distance of popular attractions like Saint-Germain-des-Près and the Latin Quarter. With Montparnasse Train Station and the iconic Montparnasse Tower just a stone's throw away, exploring Paris is effortless.Comfortable AccommodationThe hotel offers well-appointed air-conditioned rooms featuring modern amenities such as an LCD TV and a minibar. Guests can unwind in the comfort of their rooms with an en suite bathroom and complimentary toiletries for a relaxing stay.Delightful Dining and ConnectivityStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast served daily. Stay connected with Wi-Fi available in rooms. Explore Paris with ease, thanks to the convenient access to transport links to popular landmarks like the Champs Elysées and Châtelet.Experience Paris in comfort and style at Hôtel Renoir Montparnasse.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and charges may be applicable.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
