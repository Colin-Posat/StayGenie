HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views of Coal HarbourLocated in Vancouver, the Pinnacle Vancouver Harbourfront Hotel offers captivating views of Coal Harbour. With juliette balconies in every room, guests can enjoy the scenery right from their accommodation.Luxurious AmenitiesGuests can unwind in the indoor pool, stay active in the fitness centre, and indulge in delicious dishes at the on-site P2B Bistro Bar. The hotel also offers Keurig coffee-making facilities in each room for added convenience.Convenient LocationSituated just a short walk from the Vancouver Convention and Exhibition Centre, this hotel is the perfect choice for both business and leisure travelers. Explore nearby attractions like Stanley Park and the Vancouver Aquarium, all within walking distance.Experience comfort and luxury at Pinnacle Vancouver Harbourfront Hotel. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of CAD 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note, the minimum age for check-in is 19 years of age with valid identification. Please note, all vehicles must be register at time of check-in for parking. Parking costs CAD $7.50 hourly, CAD $43 per night with in-out privileges. All applicable taxes are not included in this price. Parking rates are subject to change. There is a maximum height clearance of 6’0 feet.
