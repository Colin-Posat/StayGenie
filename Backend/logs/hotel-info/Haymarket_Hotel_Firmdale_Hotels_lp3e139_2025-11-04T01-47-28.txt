CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for £78 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable, beautifully decorated, and well-designed, although some guests mentioned the lack of tea and coffee in rooms.
• Amenities: 7.9/10 - While the swimming pool was highlighted as great, some guests noted the lack of spa services as a downside.
• Value for Money: 8.4/10 - Guests generally felt that the hotel offered good value for the quality of services provided, although a few mentioned minor issues with pricing.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were positively received, with some suggestions for improvement in coffee taste.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - The majority of guests had a highly positive overall experience, praising the staff, location, design, and amenities of the hotel.
