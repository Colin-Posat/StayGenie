HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Thai RetreatThai-Inspired Rooms: Experience Thai luxury in every detail, from teak decor to Thai silk accents. Enjoy modern amenities like a flat-screen TV and DVD player.Relaxation and Wellness: Indulge in the spa's private plunge pools and herbal tea. Stay active with fitness classes and unwind in the steam room. Families are catered to with special amenities.Culinary Delights: Savor Italian, Japanese, and local Thai cuisine in the hotel's 9 dining options. From Spice Market's Thai specialties to Aqua Lounge's casual dining, there's something for every palate.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Anantara Siam Bangkok Hotel, where luxury meets convenience.

- With breakfast: THB 2590 per bed, per person, per night

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 4 years old and over stay for THB 2,354 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
