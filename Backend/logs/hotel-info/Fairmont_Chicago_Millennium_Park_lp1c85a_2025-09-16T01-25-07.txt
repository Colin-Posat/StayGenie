HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience modern rooms at Fairmont Chicago Millennium Park featuring 42-inch flat-screen TVs, marble bathrooms with rain showers, and Le Labo bath amenities. Enjoy minibar beverages and Starbucks coffee in-room for ultimate comfort.Gourmet DiningIndulge in the tap house experience at Columbus Tap with a Midwest-focused menu. Pair signature craft cocktails and a wide wine selection with small plates at THE BAR for a delightful dining experience.Prime LocationSituated just 1 block from Millennium Park and Navy Pier, and 3 blocks from Chicago’s Magnificent Mile, Fairmont Chicago offers easy access to the city's top attractions. The Field Museum and Shedd Aquarium are also just 2 miles away.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at Fairmont Chicago Millennium Park.
