Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was a highlight for many guests, with staff being friendly and helpful. Some reviews mentioned the staff going out of their way to ensure a pleasant stay.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's proximity to Ubud city center and its tranquil surroundings near a creek were highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and well-furnished, but some guests noted maintenance issues and room size limitations.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While some guests appreciated the spa and common areas, others mentioned the need for maintenance and updates in the facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified for the experience, while others mentioned maintenance issues impacting perceived value.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and dining options were generally well-received, with some guests appreciating the quality and variety offered.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the tranquil ambiance, friendly staff, and convenient location.
