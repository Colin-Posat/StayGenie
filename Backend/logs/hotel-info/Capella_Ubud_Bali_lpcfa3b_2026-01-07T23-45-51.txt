HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Tented Accommodation: Capella Ubud, Bali offers a unique blend of luxury and nature, with tented accommodation featuring salt water pools amidst lush greenery.Fine Dining Experience: Indulge in exquisite Balinese and international cuisine at the on-site restaurant, complemented by large terraces and outdoor decks overlooking rice fields or the rainforest.Personalized Service and Activities: Enjoy personalized service with modern amenities in rooms adorned with Balinese-style design. Guests can also partake in activities like cycling with complimentary bikes and relish Asian cuisine at various dining options.Experience the perfect harmony of luxury and nature at Capella Ubud, Bali. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 1,420,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for Rp 1,420,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 2,502,500 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
