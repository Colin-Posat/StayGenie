IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), this property is taking steps to help protect the safety of guests and staff. Certain services and amenities may be reduced or unavailable as a result.
Please inform ClinkNOORD Hostel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note that children under 18 years old must be accompanied by an adult.

Please note that guests who bring children under 16 years old have to book a whole dorm or a private room.

Please note that children of 16 and 17 years old can stay in a shared dorm, but must be accompanied by an adult.

Please note there is a disabled access and a lift at the rear end of the building. Quadruple rooms are wheelchair accessible.

Failure to return the key card on check out will incur a EUR 2 fee, charged to the card provided.

If the booking name is different to the name on the payment card, the hostel may request a copy of the photo ID or card.

Please note that cash payments are not accepted at the property.

 At Clink Noord guests will only be able to access their room from 3pm. A luggage room is available for guest use if they arrive earlier than 3pm. Check out time is 10am.

Guests are advised that all dormitories are mixed, unless a female dormitory is booked.

The hostel cannot guarantee that guests travelling together will be placed in the same room when booking a bed in a dorm room. Guests are advised to book a private or whole room to guarantee this.

Please note, there are no parking spaces directly at the hostel. Surcharge parking is available nearby.

Please note that when booking for more than 20 persons, different policies and supplements may apply. Please contact the property for this information.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Reviews mentioned mixed feedback on cleanliness, with some guests finding the rooms clean and others noting instances of uncleanliness.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests praising the staff's friendliness and organization, while others mentioned instances of unhelpful or unfriendly reception.
• Location: 9.1/10 - The location was highly praised by guests for its convenience and accessibility to key areas.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were described as generally comfortable, but some guests noted issues with room design and amenities.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Mixed opinions on amenities, with some guests appreciating the communal areas and facilities, while others mentioned lack of sufficient charging points, storage space, and shower design issues.
• Value for Money: 6.2/10 - Guests had varied opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price was reasonable for what was offered and others expressing dissatisfaction with additional charges and lack of included amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Food and beverage services received positive feedback overall, with guests enjoying the breakfast options but noting occasional quality issues.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Despite some shortcomings, most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
