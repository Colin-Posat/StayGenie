HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Luxury AccommodationHotel Palazzo Giovanelli offers a unique experience with its 16th-century building, original paintings in elegant rooms, and stunning views of the Grand Canal. Many rooms boast historic wooden beams and balconies overlooking Venice.Elegant Venetian Decor and AmenitiesGuests can enjoy air-conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and a delicious breakfast buffet. The spacious lounges adorned with Murano-glass chandeliers and Italian furniture add to the luxurious ambiance.Convenient Location and Exceptional ServiceLocated just 2 stops away from Saint Mark's Square, the hotel provides easy access to Venice's top attractions. The helpful multilingual staff can assist with booking water taxis, ensuring a seamless stay.Experience the allure of historic Venice at Hotel Palazzo Giovanelli e Gran Canal - book your stay today for an unforgettable getaway.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for € 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
