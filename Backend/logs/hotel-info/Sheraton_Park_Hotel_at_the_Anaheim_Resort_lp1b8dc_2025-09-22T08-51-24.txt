Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Rooms were consistently mentioned as clean and well-maintained by guests.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Staff friendliness and helpfulness were appreciated overall, with quick resolution of most issues.
• Location: 8.6/10 - Convenient proximity to Disneyland and other attractions made the hotel a popular choice for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and modern, with occasional noise disturbances from connecting doors.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Satisfactory amenities overall, with mentions of pool, smoking area, and convenience store, but some guests desired better kitchen facilities.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling it was reasonable, while others thought it was slightly overpriced.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, but some guests wished for more variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience, highlighting the staff, location, and comfort of the hotel.
