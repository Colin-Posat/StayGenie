HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in a stay at The Standard - East Village, where each room is equipped with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, Bluetooth speakers, and designer toiletries. Relax in style with plush bedding and sleek furnishings, all while enjoying a drink from the minibar.Delicious Dining OptionsSatisfy your culinary cravings at Café Standard or Narcissa, both offering mouthwatering dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
