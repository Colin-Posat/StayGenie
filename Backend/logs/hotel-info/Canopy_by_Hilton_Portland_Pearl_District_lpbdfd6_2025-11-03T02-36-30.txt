• Limited parking

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness positively overall, but some mentioned issues like carpet stains.
• Service: 6.9/10 - While some guests appreciated the service, there were complaints about delayed check-in and understaffing.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The majority of guests praised the location for its convenience and safety.
• Room Quality: 5.8/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with some guests mentioning comfortable beds while others complained about old mattresses.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with positive mentions of toiletries and valet service overshadowed by complaints about extra charges and lack of slippers.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Many guests felt the hotel was overpriced for the services provided, especially considering additional charges and room quality.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast and food services received mixed reviews, with suggestions for improvement in variety and service.
• Overall Experience: 6.8/10 - While some guests had a positive experience, there were significant concerns raised about room quality, service, and value for money.
