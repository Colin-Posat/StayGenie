HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsElysian Resort in Nairobi is a 4-star hotel located just 10 km from Nairobi National Museum. Guests can indulge in the beauty of garden views from every room and enjoy access to a terrace and bar. The hotel offers a range of amenities including an outdoor pool, fitness center, and free WiFi throughout the property.Diverse Dining OptionsSatisfy your taste buds at the on-site restaurant offering a variety of cuisines including African, American, and Asian dishes. Special dietary needs are catered to with vegetarian, halal, and vegan options available upon request.Family-Friendly EnvironmentFamilies will appreciate the children's playground and spacious rooms equipped with modern comforts. With a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, and a shared lounge, Elysian Resort ensures a convenient and enjoyable stay for all guests.Experience luxury and comfort at Elysian Resort - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway!
