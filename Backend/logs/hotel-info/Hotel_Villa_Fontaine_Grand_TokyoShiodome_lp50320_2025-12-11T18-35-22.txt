HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AccommodationsHotel Villa Fontaine Grand Tokyo-Shiodome offers sleek and contemporary rooms with convenient amenities including a flat-screen TV and free WiFi. The en suite bathrooms provide a relaxing retreat with a bathtub and complimentary toiletries.Convenient FacilitiesEnjoy access to a business center with internet PCs, photocopying services, and free luggage storage. The 24-hour front desk offers laptop rentals and a handy trouser press for your convenience. Additionally, a coin-launderette is available on-site.Prime LocationSituated near major train stations and attractions like Hamarikyu Garden, this hotel provides easy access to Tokyo's key destinations. With Haneda International Airport just a short monorail ride away, Hotel Villa Fontaine Grand Tokyo-Shiodome is the perfect choice for your stay in Tokyo.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
- An accommodations tax per person per night is included in the rate.

- Breakfast buffet opens from 06:30

- To use the on-site parking, please make a reservation in advance. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

- Early check in is subject to availability, and charges apply. Guests who wish to check in early are requested to contact the property in advance.

- Guests who wish to send luggage to the property in advance are required to fill out the full name, confirmation number and check-in date on each item. Items can be stored for 3 days prior to guests’ arrival. For more information, please contact the property directly.

- Please note baby cots are not available.
