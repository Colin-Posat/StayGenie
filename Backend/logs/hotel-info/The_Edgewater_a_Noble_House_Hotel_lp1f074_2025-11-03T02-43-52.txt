HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Waterfront Location:Situated at Pier 67 on Seattle's waterfront, The Edgewater hotel offers stunning views of Elliott Bay and the Olympic Mountains from its guest rooms.Cozy and Well-Equipped Rooms:Guest rooms and suites feature gas fireplaces, knotty pine furniture, leather chairs, and European-style spa bathrooms. Enjoy complimentary bath amenities, in-room coffee machines, minibars, and picturesque views.Convenient Amenities and Dining Options:Stay fit at the on-site fitness center, shop for souvenirs at 'Shop...on The Edge,' and indulge in Pacific Northwest cuisine at Six Seven restaurant with breathtaking views of the Puget Sound.Ready to experience luxury by the water in Seattle?
