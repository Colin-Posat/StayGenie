• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was well-received by most guests, with friendly and helpful staff. Some reviews highlighted minor service delays.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and easy access to public transport received positive feedback from guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests enjoyed spacious and comfortable rooms, with occasional mentions of maintenance issues that detracted from the experience.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with positive comments on the gym and pool, although some guests missed the availability of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some guests finding the price reasonable for the services offered, while others felt it was on the higher side.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, with some suggestions for increased variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel and would recommend it to others.
