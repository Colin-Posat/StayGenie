• Cleanliness: 5.5/10 - Guests had mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some mentioning dirty rooms and others finding it satisfactory.

• Room Quality: 6.4/10 - Rooms were described as spacious and comfortable by some, but others mentioned issues like dead bugs and cleanliness concerns.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the lobby, gym, and pool. However, some guests noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions were divided on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the historical charm of the hotel, cleanliness, friendly staff, and convenient location.
