HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Victorian Town House SettingNestled in the heart of stylish Paddington, White House Hotel offers a warm welcome with a 24-hour front desk and comfortable bedrooms. Each room features a private bathroom, tea/coffee facilities, TV, and telephone for your convenience. Start your day with a traditional continental breakfast in the spacious dining room or explore nearby international dining options. Just a short stroll from Paddington Rail Station, enjoy easy access to Heathrow Airport and explore the vibrant Oxford Street within minutes. Experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience during your stay at White House Hotel.To book your stay and experience Victorian elegance, contact us now!

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for £50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
