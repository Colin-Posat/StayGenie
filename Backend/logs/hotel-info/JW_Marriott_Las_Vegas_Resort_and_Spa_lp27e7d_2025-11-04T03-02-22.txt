Each room features a 2-person spa bath for ultimate relaxation.Outdoor OasisEnjoy the 11,000-square-foot outdoor pool with a tranquil waterfall, perfect for a refreshing dip under the Las Vegas sun. Stay active with the gym and volleyball courts available on-site.Culinary DelightsSatisfy your taste buds at the 8 fine dining and casual restaurants offering American, Italian, Japanese, and Mediterranean cuisines. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note:

- There is a daily resort fee at the property. Please contact for details.

- Room rates that include breakfast provide a buffet breakfast at the Hawthorn Grill for up to 2 adults and 2 children under the age of 12 staying in the same room.

The daily resort fee is taxable and includes the following:
-High speed internet and wireless internet in guest rooms
-Complimentary access to fitness centre in Spa Aquae
-Complimentary shuttle service to Fashion Show Mall on the strip
-Offer booklet with spa specials and food and beverage offers
-A credit for free slot play at Rampart Casino with Players Club sign up. Contact the property for more details

HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited dining options on-site
• Mixed feelings on value for money
• Some minor service complaints

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned the cleanliness of the hotel as a positive aspect.
• Service: 7.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with some guests having minor complaints about front desk staff.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location is highly praised for being away from the strip yet close to key attractions like Red Rock, perfect for exploring.
• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Rooms, particularly the newly renovated suites, were spacious and well-loved by guests.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, but noted limited dining options on-site.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money with some feeling the services offered justify the price while others found it expensive.
• Food and Beverage: 6.5/10 - Breakfast and dining services were generally well-received with some mentioning limited variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
