HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience a luxurious retreat at Levni Hotel &amp; Spa, located in the vibrant heart of Istanbul.Indulge in RelaxationPamper yourself with rejuvenating onsite massages or unwind in our serene spa facilities. Our fitness center invites you to maintain your workout regime, ensuring a restful and revitalizing stay.Gourmet Dining OptionsSavor delectable dishes at our Levni restaurant, complete with a chic bar/lounge. Enjoy the convenience of 24-hour room service, and don't miss our sumptuous buffet breakfast served daily.Modern ComfortsStay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi in our elegantly appointed rooms, featuring plush pillowtop beds, LCD televisions, and deep soaking bathtubs for the ultimate comfort.Book your unforgettable stay at Levni Hotel &amp; Spa today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* Children 6 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* Contactless check-out is available.
