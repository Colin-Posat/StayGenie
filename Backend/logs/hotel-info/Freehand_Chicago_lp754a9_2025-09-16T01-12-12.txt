HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Boutique Hotel ExperienceLocated in Chicago's vibrant River North neighborhood, Freehand Chicago offers a blend of comfort and style. The property features a restaurant and free WiFi, ensuring a convenient stay.Versatile Room OptionsChoose between private and shared rooms, each equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and cozy seating areas. The provision of linen and towels adds to the convenience and comfort of your stay.Exceptional Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in signature cocktails and small bites at The Broken Shaker, or savor gourmet coffee and creative breakfast dishes at Cafe Integral. The hotel's proximity to popular attractions like the Magnificent Mile and John Hancock Observatory makes it an ideal choice for travelers.Book your stay at Freehand Chicago for a memorable and enjoyable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please be informed that, any reservation of 10 people or more per night will be considered a group with different terms and conditions. Groups reservations can only be made through our reservations team, and Generator reserves the right to cancel it otherwise.

Additionally, we would welcome a dog on property but only when staying in a private room and subject to a fee per dog/per Stay.  Please note that the property can only allow pets with a maximum weight of 34 kilos / 75 pounds.


PAYMENT:  For Advance Purchase rates/Non Refundable rates, we require the payment card used for booking to be presented upon check in.
