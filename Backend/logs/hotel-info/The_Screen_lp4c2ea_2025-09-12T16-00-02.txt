HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Guest Rooms and Unique Design:Indulge in the lavish experience of staying in uniquely designed guest rooms at The Screen. Each room is a masterpiece on its own, offering a blend of luxury and comfort that is unmatched.Concierge Services and Rejuvenating Massages:Experience top-notch service with our 24-hour concierge desk, ready to cater to your every need. Treat yourself to a rejuvenating massage to unwind and relax after a day of exploring the beautiful city of Kyoto.Gourmet Dining and Scenic Views:Savor the exquisite fusion dishes at Bron Ronnery restaurant, showcasing the best of local ingredients. Enjoy a picturesque dining experience at Sorayuka Shoki's open-air terrace, or grab a coffee at Lounge Sunsun. Book your stay at The Screen now for an unforgettable Kyoto getaway.

The Executive Suite Rooms can accommodate up to 2 extra beds at a surcharge.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for ¥3,000 per person per night when using an available cot.
People 7 years old and over stay for ¥9,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
