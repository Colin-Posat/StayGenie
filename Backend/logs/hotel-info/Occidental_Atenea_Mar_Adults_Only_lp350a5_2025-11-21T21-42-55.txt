HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Beachfront LocationSituated just 150 meters from Barcelona’s Nova Mar Bella Beach, Occidental Atenea Mar - Adults Only offers a luxurious retreat with stunning sea views.Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in the hotel's sauna, gym, and free WiFi zone. Relax on the terrace overlooking the sea, take a dip in the seasonal infinity pool with hydromassage beds, or rent Balinese beds and sun loungers for the ultimate relaxation experience.Culinary DelightsSavor Mediterranean cuisine at the Canela restaurant or enjoy a varied breakfast buffet each morning. With bars and restaurants on the nearby promenade, culinary adventures await just steps away.Experience the epitome of relaxation and luxury at Occidental Atenea Mar - Adults Only. Book your stay now for an unforgettable beachfront getaway.
