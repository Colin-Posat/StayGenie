HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Magnificent MileLocated just 700 meters from the iconic Magnificent Mile, Moxy Chicago Downtown offers a vibrant and convenient stay in the heart of Chicago. With popular attractions like Water Tower Chicago, 360 Chicago, and CIBC Theatre within walking distance, guests can immerse themselves in the city's charm effortlessly.Modern Amenities and City ViewsExperience comfort and style in the well-equipped rooms featuring flat-screen Smart TVs with streaming capabilities. Upgrade to rooms with stunning city views for a truly memorable stay. Stay connected with complimentary WiFi and unwind in the fitness centre or socialize at the trendy on-site bar.Culinary Delights and Exceptional ServiceIndulge in flavorful Mexican cuisine at the hotel's restaurant or explore the diverse dining options in the bustling River North neighborhood. The attentive staff is always ready to assist, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable stay for every guest.Book your stay at Moxy Chicago Downtown now for an unforgettable Chicago experience!
