IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hôtel Maxim Opéra in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
When booking half board, please note that drinks are not included.
Please note that the credit card used to make the reservation and valid photo ID will be requested upon arrival. If you wish to pay on behalf of a guest, please contact the hotel in advance to fill out an authorisation form.

Please note that children's cots can only be accommodated in the Superior and Prestige room types.

Please note that one Classic Double Room is adapted to guests with reduced mobility. If you require this room, please contact the hotel straight after booking to verify the availability.
