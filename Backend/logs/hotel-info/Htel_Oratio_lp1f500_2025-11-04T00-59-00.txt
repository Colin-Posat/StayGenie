HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in the Latin QuarterLocated between Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Pantheon, Hôtel Oratio offers a prime spot in the heart of Paris. Guests can easily explore the historic charm of the city with convenient access to major attractions.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsExperience a relaxing stay in air-conditioned and soundproofed rooms featuring classic décor. Enjoy modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries, and free WiFi access. The convenience of a lift adds to the comfort of your stay.Delicious Buffet Breakfast and 24-Hour Front DeskStart your day with a delightful buffet breakfast made from fresh ingredients served in the dining room. The hotel's 24-hour front desk ensures that assistance is always at hand for any needs during your stay.For a memorable stay in Paris, book your room at Hôtel Oratio now!
