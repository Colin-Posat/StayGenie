HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for THB 667 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for THB 400 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for THB 667 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The hotel provided satisfactory amenities, but some guests noted the noise disturbances from neighboring establishments.

• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Breakfast offerings were generally well-received, but some guests felt there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience despite some noise disturbances, and would recommend the hotel to others.
