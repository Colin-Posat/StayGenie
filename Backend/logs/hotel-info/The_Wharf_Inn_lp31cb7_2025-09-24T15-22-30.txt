HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Fisherman's WharfThe Wharf Inn, just a short stroll from the vibrant shops and restaurants of Fisherman's Wharf, provides comfortable accommodations with free WiFi.Modern Amenities for a Comfortable StayEach room at The Wharf Inn features a 40-inch LCD TV with premium channels and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Guests can also enjoy complimentary coffee and newspapers to start their day right.Access to 24-Hour Fitness Center and Nearby AttractionsGuests have the convenience of a 24-hour reception and can access a fitness center just steps away. Explore nearby attractions like Pier 33 for Alcatraz Island cruises and the picturesque Golden Gate Bridge. Book your stay at The Wharf Inn for a memorable San Francisco experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Wharf Inn in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
A damage deposit of USD 150 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Guests must be 18 years of age or older to register.

At the time of booking, guests will be charged a pre-payment equal to the first night's stay. Parking is limited to one car per guestroom, contact the property for more details. For over-sized vehicle parking, please contact the hotel.

An early check-in fee applies to all check-ins before 15:00; an early parking fee applies to all parking prior to check-in at 15:00. No parking before 11:00.

Early check-in and parking are subject to availability.

When booking a non-refundable reservation, no modifications to the reservation are allowed.

Pets are only allowed in specific room types. Please contact the propery prior to arrival to secure a pet friendly room. Only 1 dog allowed. A non-refundable fee of $25 per night applies.

Parking spots are limited and available on a first come first serve basis. If the parking garage is full upon arrival, parking is available for a fee at a nearby garage.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 10 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
