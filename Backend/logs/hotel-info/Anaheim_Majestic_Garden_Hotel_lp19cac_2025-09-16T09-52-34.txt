HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Disneyland:Enjoy the convenience of a free shuttle to Disneyland and California Adventure, just a short distance away from Anaheim Majestic Garden Hotel.Comfortable and Modern Accommodations:Relax in well-equipped rooms featuring flat-screen TVs, refrigerators, and coffee makers. Accessible rooms with special amenities are also available for those who need them.Fantastic Amenities for All Guests:Take advantage of free access to the gym, outdoor whirlpool, and pool. Have fun in the games room or stay productive in the business center. Indulge in delicious dining options at Garden Court Bistro and Bar or California Deli.Book your stay at Anaheim Majestic Garden Hotel for a magical and comfortable experience near Disneyland!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 200 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note, cats and dogs are permitted in specific and designated 2 queen bed traditional rooms only. Rooms are limited in number. If traveling with a pet please contact the hotel in advance to arrange for a pet room assignment. A non-refundable deposit plus tax will apply per stay. The pet must be accompanied by an owner at all times - on a leash or in a crate - and may not be left in the room unattended.

• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were comfortable and spacious, although there were occasional complaints about outdated decor and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests highlighting the pool and shuttle service, while others complained about the lack of updates and spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value varied, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location while others thought it was too high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with only a few mentions of overpricing and limited variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, with particular praise for the staff and convenient location.
