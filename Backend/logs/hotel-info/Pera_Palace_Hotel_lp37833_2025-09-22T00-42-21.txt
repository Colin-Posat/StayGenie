HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with City ViewsThe Pera Palace Hotel offers a unique blend of European and Ottoman influences in its classical-style rooms and suites. Featuring hardwood floors, floor-to-ceiling windows, and marble bathrooms inspired by traditional hammams, each room provides a luxurious stay with stunning city views from balconies overlooking Pera and the Golden Horn.Dining and Entertainment OptionsIndulge in contemporary Turkish cuisine at Agatha Restaurant, named after the renowned mystery writer, or enjoy a sophisticated atmosphere at Orient Bar &amp; Terrace, a favorite among Istanbul's elite. For a relaxing experience, visit Kubbeli Saloon for tea accompanied by live piano music. And don't miss the delectable treats at Patisserie de Pera, offering a selection of Turkish and French-inspired delicacies.Relaxation and Wellness FacilitiesUnwind at Pera SPA by Spa Soul, featuring a traditional hammam, steam bath, hot tub, sauna, and a fully equipped gym. The indoor jet-streamed swimming pool provides a rejuvenating experience year-round. After exploring the vibrant Istiklal Avenue just steps away, return to the hotel for a pampering session at the spa.To experience the charm and luxury of Pera Palace Hotel in the heart of Istanbul, book your stay now and immerse yourself in a world of elegance and comfort.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 60 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 8 per  stay.
