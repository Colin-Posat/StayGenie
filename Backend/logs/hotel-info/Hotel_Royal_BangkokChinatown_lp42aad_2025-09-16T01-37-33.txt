HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesHotel Royal Bangkok@Chinatown offers an outdoor salt-water pool, a bar, and a restaurant, providing guests with the perfect blend of relaxation and convenience.Modern ComfortsEach air-conditioned room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and ironing facilities. Guests can also enjoy free WiFi, ensuring they stay connected throughout their stay.Convenient LocationLocated just meters away from Wat Mangkon MRT Subway Station, exploring Bangkok is a breeze. With a fitness centre, tour desk, and airport shuttle services, Hotel Royal Bangkok@Chinatown caters to all your travel needs.Book now and indulge in a luxurious stay at Hotel Royal Bangkok@Chinatown!

Parking is subject to availability due to limited spaces.
Guests need to meet one or more requirements to stay in this property: proof of full Coronavirus (Covid-19) vaccination, a recent valid negative Coronavirus PCR test, or recent proof of Coronavirus recovery.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 10 years old and over stay for THB 1,200 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
