HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Property with Modern AmenitiesCorte Grimani offers spacious self-catering apartments in a historic building, blending old-world charm with modern conveniences. Each apartment boasts a fully equipped kitchen, including a washing machine and dryer, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests.Central Location with Scenic ViewsSituated between the iconic Rialto Bridge and St. Mark's Square, Residence Corte Grimani provides a picturesque backdrop overlooking a charming canal. Some apartments offer stunning views of the surrounding area, immersing guests in the beauty of Venice.Convenient Services and SurroundingsGuests can enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi, daily cleaning services, and the assistance of friendly English-speaking staff available 24/7. With a vibrant neighborhood filled with shops, bars, and restaurants, Corte Grimani ensures a memorable and convenient stay in the heart of Venice.Book your stay at Residence Corte Grimani for an unforgettable Venetian experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 5 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
