HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsYear-Round Outdoor Pool and Spa TreatmentsThe Jannata Resort and Spa boasts a picturesque year-round outdoor pool with lush greenery views, offering a serene retreat for guests. Indulge in relaxing massages and treatments at the spa center while enjoying the tranquil surroundings.Diverse Activities and Dining OptionsWith a helpful multilingual staff, guests can easily arrange exciting activities like cycling and hiking to explore the area. After a day of adventures, savor Western and Asian cuisine at the Jannata Resort Restaurant, offering both continental and à la carte options for a delightful dining experience.Experience luxury and relaxation at Jannata Resort and Spa - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
