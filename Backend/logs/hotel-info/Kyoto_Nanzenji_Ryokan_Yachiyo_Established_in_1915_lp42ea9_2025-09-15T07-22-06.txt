HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Chic Japanese-style Rooms and Public BathsExperience the charm of Kyoto at Kyoto Garden Ryokan Yachiyo, located next to the historic Nanzen-ji Temple. The chic Japanese-style rooms with tatami flooring and futon bedding offer a serene retreat. Unwind in the large public baths or enjoy views of the beautiful Japanese gardens.Relaxing Lounge and AmenitiesIndulge in relaxation at the lounge with free internet access and unwind in the public baths. Treat yourself to a rejuvenating massage or explore the gift shop on site. With free Wi-Fi throughout the property, stay connected during your stay.Delicately-Prepared Japanese MealsSavor traditional Japanese meals at the on-site restaurant overlooking the gardens. From vegetarian multi-course options to accommodating special requests, delight in a culinary experience that complements your stay.Book now to immerse yourself in the tranquility of Kyoto Garden Ryokan Yachiyo.

• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally described as beautiful and charming, with a special mention of rooms with a private onsen.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities with a variety in breakfast options, but no specific mentions of additional standout amenities.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Mixed reviews regarding value for money, with some guests comparing the experience to higher-end accommodations.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Most guests enjoyed the food and beverage offerings, particularly the restaurant meals and daily breakfast variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - While some guests had specific preferences that influenced their overall experience, many praised the authentic Japanese atmosphere and friendly staff.
