HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Hotel with Rooftop Pool and GymLuxurious Rooms with Terrace ViewsThe rooms at Ilunion Barcelona are equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, a desk, and a TV. Superior rooms offer a touch of luxury with a furnished terrace boasting stunning sea or city views.Accessibility and Specialized ServicesIlunion Barcelona is committed to inclusivity, offering rooms adapted for disabled guests with reduced mobility or hearing/visual impairment. Specialized services are available upon request to ensure a comfortable stay for all guests.Convenient Location and Dining OptionsSituated near Bogatell Beach and Rambla de Poblenou, the hotel provides easy access to shopping centers and a variety of dining options. Guests can enjoy a delicious breakfast at the on-site restaurant or explore the à la carte menu.Experience the best of Barcelona at Ilunion Barcelona - book your stay now for a memorable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different payment and cancellation policies and additional supplements may apply.

Please note that the outdoor swimming pool is open from mid March until October 30th, subject to weather conditions.

Please note that guests must present the credit card used to make the reservation on arrival. If you are not the owner of the credit card used to make the reservation, please contact the property in advance.

This is a pet friendly property. Pets have an extra cost of 30 EUR per pet per night. Pets can only be accommodated in "Double Room Pet Friendly". Guests are required to confirm the pet reservation and check the hotel availability before the arrival to also make sure that the room has everything needed. At the property, guests will be given the admission regulations for reading and approval.

License number HB-4313

This hotel is officially adapted for disabled guests. It has rooms adapted for people with reduced mobility or hearing/visual impairment, available upon request.

Ilunion Barcelona's restaurant serves breakfast and also offers an à la carte menu. The hotel has a bar and provides room service. A menu for guests with specific dietary requirements is also available.
