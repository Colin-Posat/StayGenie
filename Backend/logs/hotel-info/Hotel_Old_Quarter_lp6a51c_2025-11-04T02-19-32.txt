HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building and Prime LocationLocated just 300 metres from Amsterdam Central Station, Hotel Old Quarter is housed in a historic building, offering a charming and convenient stay in the heart of the city.Cozy Rooms and Dining OptionsIndividually decorated basic rooms, some with cable TV, provide a comfortable retreat. Guests can enjoy breakfast in the restaurant and savor traditional Dutch snacks in the evening, complemented by a relaxing drink at the bar.Exploration and ConvenienceWith the Red Light District a stone's throw away and main attractions like Dam Square and Kalverstraat within walking distance, the hotel's prime location ensures easy access to Amsterdam's vibrant sights and sounds.Book your stay at Hotel Old Quarter for an unforgettable Amsterdam experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Hotel Old Quarter in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of EUR 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that the hotel buildings do not have a lift. Please note that the street the hotel is located on is not accessible by car. Guests who expect to arrive outside of the stated check-in times must contact the property directly as soon as possible. Contact details appear on the Booking Confirmation issued by this site. Credit card details are only required to guarantee your booking. Payment will be done at the hotel. Please note that this is not applicable for non-refundable reservations, since full payment will be taken directly after booking. Please note that the credit card used for booking must be presented upon check-in and the main booker must be able to show his/her ID. When booking 5 or more rooms group policies apply. If cancelled or modified up to 4 weeks in advance no fee will be charged. If cancelled or modified later full amount will be charged. Please note that the street is not accessible by any means of transport except bicycle. Please note that Twin or Double bed preference is subject to availability. Please note that each floor contains stairs to get to the rooms. Please note that the property cannot process any requests to have rooms close to each other.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 5 years old stay for € 55 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for € 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
