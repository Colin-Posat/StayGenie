HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Oasis in MarrakechUnwind in Style: The Semiramis hotel offers a serene escape in a 4-hectare park, boasting a refreshing outdoor pool and a rejuvenating spa for ultimate relaxation.Culinary Delights: Indulge in a variety of dining experiences, from traditional Moroccan cuisine at Le Chellah to Mediterranean dishes at La Strada and L’Oasis, all while enjoying the lush surroundings.Endless Entertainment: Stay active with tennis and volleyball, then pamper yourself at the spa with a steam bath and massages. As night falls, head to the on-site nightclub for a memorable evening.Book your stay at Semiramis now and experience luxury and comfort in Marrakech's exotic setting.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 4 years old to 11 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, but some mentioned minor issues like dusty surfaces.
• Service: 7.3/10 - While some guests praised the friendly staff and service, others reported slow response times and a lack of proper communication.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and availability of taxis received consistent praise from guests.
• Room Quality: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with some guests mentioning old furniture and uncomfortable beds while others appreciated the spacious rooms.
• Amenities: 7/10 - The hotel's amenities were satisfactory, but there were complaints about the lack of certain services like a working WiFi and spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the prices were justified by the location while others found the services lacking compared to the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 6.6/10 - While some guests appreciated the food quality, others reported issues like food poisoning, limited variety, and miscommunication regarding dietary restrictions.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Despite some drawbacks, most guests had a positive overall experience and appreciated the staff's friendliness and the convenient location.
