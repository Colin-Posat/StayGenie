HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Eiffel TowerLocated in the heart of Paris, the Novotel Paris Centre Tour Eiffel is just a short stroll away from the iconic Eiffel Tower, offering a prime spot for exploring the city.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's two restaurants, Greem and Benkay, offering a blend of French and Japanese cuisine with picturesque views of the Seine.Wellness and SustainabilityRelax and rejuvenate at the fitness center with an indoor heated pool or explore the city easily with convenient access to public transport. Plus, feel good about your stay with our commitment to sustainability by eliminating plastic bottles.Experience Paris in style at Novotel Paris Centre Tour Eiffel.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that the credit card used to make the reservation will be requested upon arrival and must be valid at the time of check-in.

Please note that children under 16 years old can enjoy breakfast for free.

Breakfast buffet is available at the restaurant, bar open all day, with lunch and dinner menu. Our room service remains available.

Our swimming pool and gym classes are accessible upon reservation only. Access to the pool is free.
