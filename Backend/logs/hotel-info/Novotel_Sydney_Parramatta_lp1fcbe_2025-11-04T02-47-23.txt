HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  AUD 35 per  day.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Overall, guests mentioned cleanliness positively, but some reviews highlighted issues with bathroom mold and outdated rooms.
• Service: 7.3/10 - Service received mixed feedback, with some guests praising front desk service and quick issue resolution, while others mentioned initial disinterest and unhelpfulness.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was widely praised for being convenient for business meetings and close to key landmarks like Commbank Stadium and public transport.
• Room Quality: 6.4/10 - Rooms were comfortable and provided necessary amenities, but some guests complained about outdated bathrooms, lack of natural light, and room odor issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities were satisfactory, with mentions of a gym and refillable toiletries; however, some guests noted limited amenities like no minibar or basic gym equipment.
• Value for Money: 5.3/10 - Opinions on value for money were varied, with some guests feeling the price was too high for the services offered and others finding it reasonable based on the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Restaurant food and breakfast had a good variety, but some guests complained about expensive meals and limited options.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with a highlight on the location and some concerns about service inconsistencies and room quality.
