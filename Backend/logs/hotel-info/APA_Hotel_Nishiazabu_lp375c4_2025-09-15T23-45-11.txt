HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation in Vibrant LocationConvenient Location: Situated just 10 minutes from the bustling Roppongi area, APA Hotel Nishi-Azabu offers easy access to popular spots like Tsukiji and Ginza. With Hiroo Subway Station a mere 5-minute walk away, exploring Tokyo is a breeze.Comfortable Rooms: The renovated rooms are thoughtfully designed with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, fridge, and work desk. The unique mattress design ensures a restful sleep, while the en suite bathroom with a bathtub and provided Yukata adds a touch of luxury.On-Site Delights: Indulge in freshly baked goods at bakery ecru or grab a drink from the vending machine. Free WiFi in the lobby and massage services further enhance your stay at this stylish hotel.Experience Tokyo in comfort at APA Hotel Nishi-Azabu.
