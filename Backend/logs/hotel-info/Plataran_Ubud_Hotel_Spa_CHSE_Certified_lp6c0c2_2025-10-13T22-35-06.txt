HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsExperience the tranquility of Plataran Ubud Hotel &amp; Resort, where you can unwind in beautifully designed rooms overlooking lush rice fields. Enjoy modern amenities including flat-screen TVs, minibars, and en suite bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay.Relaxation and RecreationIndulge in soothing massages, stay active at the fitness center with daily complimentary activities, or simply lounge by one of the two infinity-edged swimming pools. With free WiFi throughout the property, you can stay connected while immersing yourself in the serene surroundings.Culinary Delights and Cultural ExperiencesSavor authentic Indonesian cuisine at the on-site Teras Restaurant, offering a variety of delectable dishes. Immerse yourself in Balinese culture with activities like morning rice paddy walks and dancing classes, creating unforgettable memories during your stay.Book now and elevate your Ubud experience at Plataran Ubud Hotel &amp; Resort.

Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 600,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
