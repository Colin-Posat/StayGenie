HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with AmenitiesThe Hotel Zags Portland offers modern guest rooms with a work desk, small fridge, and coffee-making facilities. Relax in front of a flat-screen HD cable TV in the comfort of your room.Local Art and 24-Hour ConciergeImmerse yourself in the vibrant local art scene with over 500 pieces displayed throughout the hotel. Need assistance? The 24-hour concierge service is there to help you make the most of your stay.Onsite Dining and Nearby AttractionsIndulge in a culinary journey at Zags Restaurant &amp; Patio, serving delectable dishes inspired by French and Italian Rivera cuisine. Explore the nearby Waterfront Park or visit the Rock Bottom Restaurant and Brewery just a short walk away.Book your stay at The Hotel Zags Portland for a memorable experience in downtown Portland.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please Note: There is a USD 30 daily amenity fee to be collected at the property, which will include the following

- A food and beverage credit good for use in the in-room minibar and/or hotel sundry shop.
- Access to The Hotel Zags Portland’s exclusive multi-use space, The Colosseum (based on availability).
- Full use of 24-Hour Fitness, including Indoor Lap Pool, Sauna, Steam Room, and Whirlpool.
- Access to a fleet of bikes for complimentary day use
- Unlimited phone calls within the U.S.
- 24/7 concierge services
- In-room gourmet coffee.
- Business center kiosks on lobby level open 24 hours with complimentary printing at the Front Desk.
- Complimentary Continental Breakfast included for all hotel guests.

Please contact property for further details.
