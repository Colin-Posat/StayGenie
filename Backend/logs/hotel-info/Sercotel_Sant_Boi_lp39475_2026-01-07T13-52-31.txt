HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Free Sauna and Gym AccessExperience comfort and convenience at Sercotel Sant Boi, just a short drive from Barcelona Airport. Enjoy modern rooms equipped with satellite TV, air conditioning, and a pillow menu. Take advantage of complimentary access to the sauna and gym, perfect for relaxation after a day of travel.Convenient Location and Easy AccessLocated near Barcelona's Fira II trade fair site and with easy access to the C-32 motorway, our hotel is ideal for both business and leisure travelers. Explore the nearby beaches at Castelldefels and Gava, all within a short distance from the property. A bus stop outside the hotel provides direct access to central Barcelona.Onsite Parking and Car Hire ServicesFor added convenience, onsite parking is available upon request. Need to explore the city? Our hotel offers car hire services to help you make the most of your stay. Book now for a memorable experience at Sercotel Sant Boi.
