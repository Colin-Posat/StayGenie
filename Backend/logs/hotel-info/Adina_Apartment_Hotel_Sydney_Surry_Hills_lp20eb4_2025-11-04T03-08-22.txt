CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for AUD 80 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:
• Inconsistent cleanliness

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Overall, guests had mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some mentioning issues like dirty floors, greasy windows, and lack of thorough cleaning.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally positive, with guests appreciating helpful staff and warm greetings, although some mentioned staff availability as a concern.
• Location: 9.2/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised by guests for its convenience, close proximity to key landmarks, and easy access to restaurants and cafes.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but there were complaints about maintenance issues, lack of cleanliness, and amenities like cutlery.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Guests had mixed feedback on amenities, with some enjoying the pool and location, while others mentioned the lack of proper cleaning, cutlery, and baby-friendly offerings.
• Value for Money: 7.7/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some feeling the services matched the price, while others found the hotel overpriced for the quality offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Guests appreciated the breakfast and restaurant services, but some suggested improving variety and cleanliness standards.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, praising factors like location, friendly staff, and comfortable rooms.
