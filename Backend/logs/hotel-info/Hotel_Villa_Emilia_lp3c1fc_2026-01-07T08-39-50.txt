HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Trendy Rooftop Bar and Stylish DesignThe Hotel Villa Emilia in Barcelona stands out with its trendy rooftop bar, offering a stylish setting to unwind after a day of exploration. The modern and chic design of the hotel provides a unique ambiance for guests to enjoy during their stay.Creative Catalan Cuisine and Live MusicIndulge in the creative Catalan dishes served at the hotel's restaurant, promising a culinary experience that captures the essence of the region. Additionally, the Villa Emilia Bistrot's live music adds a vibrant touch to the evenings, creating a lively atmosphere for guests to revel in.Convenient Services and Prime LocationBenefit from the 24-hour reception staff's local expertise, ensuring you make the most of your Barcelona visit. With car and bicycle rental services available on-site, exploring the city is made easy. Located near key attractions like Plaza Espanya and Sants Station, Hotel Villa Emilia offers a prime location for your stay in Barcelona.To experience the stylish design, culinary delights, and convenient services of Hotel Villa Emilia, book your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that this is a nonsmoking hotel, but there is a roof terrace where it is possible to smoke.

Please note, when booking more 5 rooms or more, a nonrefundable deposit is required.
