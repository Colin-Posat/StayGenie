HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern LuxuryExperience the allure of a Scottish Baronial castle at the National Historic Site, Fairmont Banff Springs. Built in 1888, this resort in Banff National Park boasts 11 restaurants, 14 shops, and a captivating blend of old-world charm and contemporary amenities.Luxurious Spa and RecreationIndulge in relaxation at the 27-treatment area Fairmont Spa, offering a range of massages and beauty treatments. Enjoy leisure activities like bowling, tennis, horseback riding, and golf. Take a dip in the 32-metre lap pool or the 20-metre heated outdoor pool for a refreshing experience.Skiing ParadiseConveniently located near various ski locations, the resort provides ski passes, storage, and rental services. Don't miss the shuttle service to popular ski resorts like Mount Norquay, Sunshine Village, and Lake Louise for a seamless skiing adventure.Book your stay at Fairmont Banff Springs for an unforgettable blend of history, luxury, and adventure.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Upon check-in photo identification and credit card is required. All special requests are subject to availability upon check-in. Special requests cannot be guaranteed and may incur additional charges.

Breakfast is included in Fairmont Gold Room's rate.

Please note: Parking is $35 per night for self parking and $49 per night for valet parking.  This does not include the park fees. Guests do have in-and-out privileges when parking at the hotel. Parking spots are not reserved.

Please note a maximum of 2 pets are allowed in each guest room.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  CAD 35 per  day.

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in all areas and costs  CAD  14.65 per  24 hours.
