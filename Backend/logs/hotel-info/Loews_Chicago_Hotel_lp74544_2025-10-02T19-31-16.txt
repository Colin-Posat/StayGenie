HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 78 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel, with a few mentions of minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff. Some guests had complaints about delayed responses and communication.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its convenience and proximity to key landmarks, restaurants, and entertainment options.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests enjoyed spacious and comfortable rooms, although there were occasional complaints about maintenance issues and room temperature.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating the gym and pool while others noted the lack of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 7.1/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with a few suggestions for improvement in variety and menu options.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
