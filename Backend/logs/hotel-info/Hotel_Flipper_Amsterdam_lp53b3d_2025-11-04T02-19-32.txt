HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Flipper Amsterdam, nestled in Amsterdam's Berlage Quarter, offers budget-friendly rooms near the Amstel River. With easy access to the Amsterdam RAI Congress Centre, it's an ideal choice for business travelers and tourists alike.Comfortable AccommodationsChoose from rooms with shared facilities or a private bathroom, each equipped with an LED TV and complimentary Wi-Fi. Unwind in the lobby lounge by the fireplace, perfect for relaxation after a day of exploring the city.Amenities GaloreEnjoy round-the-clock reception services, free internet on the public computer, and the option to rent bicycles to explore Amsterdam conveniently. The nearby Amstelkade tram stop provides a quick link to popular attractions, making your stay at Hotel Flipper Amsterdam both convenient and memorable.Book your stay now at Hotel Flipper Amsterdam for a delightful and budget-friendly experience in the heart of Amsterdam!
