HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AccommodationsStaycity Aparthotels Gare de l'Est, just a 5-minute walk from Gare de l’Est, offers contemporary apartments and studios with LCD TVs and private bathrooms. Some even include a separate bedroom for added comfort.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy the convenience of kitchenettes with hotplates and a microwave to prepare meals. The 24-hour reception is there to assist with drink and snack purchases, as well as help with dinner reservations and show tickets.Prime LocationSituated near Canal Saint-Martin and République Square, guests can easily explore the waterside cafés, restaurants, and lively attractions in the area. Book your stay at Staycity Aparthotels Paris Gare de l'Est for a safe and modern travel experience.
