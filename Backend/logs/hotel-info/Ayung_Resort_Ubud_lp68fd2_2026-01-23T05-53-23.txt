HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tropical Retreat with Free Yoga Classes and Outdoor PoolAyung Resort Ubud offers a serene tropical escape with complimentary yoga classes and a refreshing outdoor pool nestled amidst lush greenery.Balinese-Inspired Rooms with Private BalconiesEach room at Ayung Resort Ubud is thoughtfully designed in Balinese style, featuring a private balcony with stunning garden views. The air-conditioned rooms are elegantly furnished with wooden fittings and marble flooring, complemented by modern amenities like a flat-screen satellite TV.Convenient Amenities and ServicesGuests can indulge in daily afternoon tea, enjoy free WiFi throughout the property, and savor delicious meals at Wijaya Kusuma Restaurant offering local and international cuisine. The resort also provides a free shuttle service to central Ubud and has a 24-hour front desk for any assistance needed.Experience tranquility and luxury at Ayung Resort Ubud - Book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 650,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 5 years old and over stay for Rp 650,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
