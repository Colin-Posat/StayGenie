HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesFairmont Century Plaza Los Angeles offers a range of upscale amenities including free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre, and a terrace. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of a restaurant, room service, and a 24-hour front desk, as well as free WiFi throughout the property.Scenic ViewsCertain rooms at the property feature a balcony with stunning city views, providing guests with a picturesque backdrop to their stay. Whether relaxing in the room or enjoying a morning coffee, the cityscape vista adds an extra touch of charm to the accommodation.Convenient LocationLocated just a short distance from popular attractions such as Petersen Automotive Museum and Los Angeles County Museum Of Art, Fairmont Century Plaza Los Angeles is the ideal choice for travelers looking to explore the city. With car hire available and close proximity to the airport, guests can easily navigate their way around Los Angeles.Experience luxury and convenience at Fairmont Century Plaza Los Angeles.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Fairmont Century Plaza Los Angeles in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note: Hotel has only private valet parking. This includes in and out privileges and can be charged to the room. There is no self parking or public parking at the hotel.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 65 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 8.2/10 - Amenities were well-received, especially the fitness center and thoughtful touches; lack of spa services was occasionally noted.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Guests were divided on value for money, with some considering it pricey.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - The hotel's restaurant was appreciated; however, issues with variety and one complaint about the restaurant experience were observed.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Despite some specific grievances, overall experience remained high with guests likely to recommend the hotel.
