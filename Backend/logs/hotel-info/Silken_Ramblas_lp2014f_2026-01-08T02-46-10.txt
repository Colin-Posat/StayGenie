HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Rooftop PoolLocated just steps away from Las Ramblas and Plaza Catalunya, the Silken Ramblas offers a prime spot for exploring Barcelona. Guests can relax and soak up the sun at the rooftop pool while enjoying stunning city views.Stylish Rooms and AmenitiesEach room at the Silken Ramblas is tastefully decorated and equipped with modern conveniences such as a flat-screen TV, minibar, and air conditioning. The hotel also features a restaurant and bar, as well as a terrace for guests to unwind after a day of sightseeing.Convenience and ServicesWith a tour desk and easy access to the sightseeing bus at Plaza Catalunya, the Silken Ramblas ensures that guests make the most of their Barcelona experience. Plus, with free Wi-Fi and room service available, every need is taken care of during your stay.Book now to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at the Silken Ramblas.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 30 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 1 year old and over stay for € 65 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
