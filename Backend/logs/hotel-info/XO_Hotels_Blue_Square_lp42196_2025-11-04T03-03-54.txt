HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern and Convenient AccommodationThe XO Hotels Blue Square offers a modern and convenient stay just a short drive away from Amsterdam city centre and Schiphol Airport. The hotel provides free Wi-Fi and a 24-hour reception for the comfort of its guests.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsEach air-conditioned room at XO Hotels Blue Square features box-spring beds, a flat-screen TV, and tea/coffee making facilities. With some rooms offering scenic views of the Erasmus canal, guests can relax in comfort during their stay.Accessible Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated just 160 metres from Lodewijk van Deysselstraat tram stop, guests have easy access to explore the area, including a short drive to historic Haarlem and the RAI Conference Centre.Book now to experience a convenient stay with modern amenities at XO Hotels Blue Square.
