HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated in Sydney’s Eastern Suburbs, Edgecliff Lodge Motel is just a short 10-minute drive from the bustling Sydney CBD, offering the perfect blend of tranquility and accessibility.Comfortable AccommodationEach air-conditioned room at Edgecliff Lodge Motel is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, private bathroom with complimentary toiletries, and a cozy sofa in select rooms. Guests can also make use of the convenient kitchenette for added comfort.Nearby AttractionsWith Allianz Stadium and Capitol Theatre in close proximity, guests can easily immerse themselves in the vibrant entertainment scene of Sydney. And with Kingsford Smith Airport just 8 km away, traveling to and from the property is a breeze.Book your stay at Edgecliff Lodge Motel now for a delightful Sydney experience!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
