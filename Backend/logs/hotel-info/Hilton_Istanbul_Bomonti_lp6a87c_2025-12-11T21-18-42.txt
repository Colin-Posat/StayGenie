HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Panoramic ViewsExperience the elegance of Hilton Istanbul Bomonti with spacious rooms offering breathtaking views of the city or Bosphorus. Enjoy the luxurious furnishings, air conditioning, and floor-to-ceiling windows in every room. Upgrade to an Executive room for exclusive access to the Executive Lounge and indulge in a residential feel with top-notch amenities.Culinary Delights and Dining ExperiencesSavor a variety of international cuisines and Turkish specialties at The Globe restaurant. Treat yourself to hot and cold mezzes made from high-quality ingredients. Unwind at the Executive Lounge on the 34th floor with a stunning view while enjoying continental breakfast and evening buffets.Relaxation and WellnessIndulge in relaxation at Eforea Spa with an indoor pool, sauna, Turkish hammam, and fitness center. Pamper yourself with spa treatments and private facilities for couples. Don't miss out on the outdoor pool and hairdresser services for a complete rejuvenating experience.Book your stay at Hilton Istanbul Bomonti now for an unforgettable blend of luxury, culinary delights, and relaxation.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that smoking is only allowed on certain floors. Smoking in a forbidden area is subjected to a legal fine. Pets are allowed up to 34 kg at an additional fee of EUR 45. Service pets are allowed in the hotel. Please note that early check-in and late check-out is possible for an extra charge. Please contact the property after booking for more details. Please note that external guests are not accepted in the rooms.

Relax in our spacious family-friendly home suites, featuring panoramic Bosphorus view, a master bedroom with king-sized Hilton Serenity Bed, plus a second bedroom with twin beds, and a business work area in the living room. Each suite also includes a washing machine, tumble dryer, dishwasher, fridge, microwave, cutlery, and crockery. Bomonti Home Suites offer long term accommodations with all the comforts and amenities of a luxurious 5-star hotel property.

You will also enjoy exclusive access to the hotel's Executive Lounge, serving complimentary continental breakfast, drinks, and snacks throughout the day, plus private check-in and check-out. Wi-Fi is complimentary. The luxury marble bathroom features a walk-in shower and a bathtub with shower. Each Residence comes fully equipped with a kitchenette, dining table, and a spacious living area.

For those traveling as a family, a baby bottle warmer, monitor, baby tub, and baby toilet are also available on request. We provide amenities for kids such as kid slippers, toothbrush, soap, and shampoo upon request.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 10 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.
