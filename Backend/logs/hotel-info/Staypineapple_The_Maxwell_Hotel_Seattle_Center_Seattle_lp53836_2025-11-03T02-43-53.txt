HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Vibrant Interiors and Artwork:Staypineapple, The Maxwell Hotel, Seattle Center Seattle offers a unique experience with its original artwork and colorful interiors, creating a vibrant and lively atmosphere for guests to enjoy.Luxurious Amenities:Guests can indulge in the luxurious amenities provided in each room, including a gourmet coffee maker, designer toiletries, and plush bathrobes. The hotel also offers complimentary coffee and pineapple treats in the afternoon, adding a touch of sweetness to your stay.Convenient Location and Services:Conveniently located within walking distance of Seattle’s Space Needle, the hotel provides easy access to popular attractions such as the Seattle Aquarium and the Washington State Convention Center. Guests can also take advantage of the onsite fitness room, indoor pool, and business center for a well-rounded and comfortable stay.Experience the vibrant charm of Staypineapple, The Maxwell Hotel, Seattle Center Seattle - book your stay now for a delightful getaway!
