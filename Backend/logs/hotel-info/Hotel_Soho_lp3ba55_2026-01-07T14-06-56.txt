HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Pool and Sun Terrace with Panoramic ViewsHotel Soho, centrally located on Gran Via in Barcelona, offers a stylish stay with a rooftop seasonal pool and sun terrace providing breathtaking views of the city. The chic, soundproof rooms are equipped with modern amenities like flat-screen satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, and ambient lighting, ensuring a comfortable stay.Rich Breakfast on Spacious TerraceGuests can start their day with a delicious breakfast on the terrace, weather permitting, and benefit from the 24-hour front desk's ticket service. The convenient location, just a short walk from Plaza Catalunya and Passeig de Gracia Boulevard, makes exploring Barcelona easy for visitors.Close Proximity to Transportation HubsWith the airport bus stop at Plaza Universitat a mere 5-minute walk away and Urgell Metro Station nearby, Hotel Soho offers easy access to transportation for guests to explore the vibrant city of Barcelona. Book your stay now for a memorable experience at Hotel Soho!
