HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in the Gothic QuarterLocated in the heart of the Gothic Quarter and adjacent to Urquinaona Metro Station, HCC Montblanc provides easy access to Barcelona's iconic attractions and vibrant neighborhoods.Comfortable Rooms and Dining OptionsThe hotel offers modern air-conditioned rooms equipped with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a minibar. Guests can indulge in a delicious buffet breakfast and Mediterranean dishes at El Racó del Montblanc restaurant.Proximity to Major LandmarksSituated near the Born district, the hotel is within walking distance to Barcelona Cathedral and Plaza de Catalunya. The renowned Palau de la Musica Concert Hall, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is just a short stroll away.Book your stay at HCC Montblanc for a memorable Barcelona experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for € 70 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
