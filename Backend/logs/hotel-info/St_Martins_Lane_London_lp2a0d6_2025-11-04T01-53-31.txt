HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Innovative Design and Stylish Rooms - St Martins Lane London offers a unique experience with Philippe Starck-designed public areas and guest rooms featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and interactive light installations.Complimentary Gym Access and Bicycle Rental - Enjoy access to the gym and complimentary gym kit laundry services during your stay. Explore London on wheels with free bicycle rental provided by the hotel.Dining and Entertainment Options - Indulge in All-Day Dining at The Den and The Restaurant, savor a coffee at EllaMia London, and discover creative cocktails at Blind Spot London. With top attractions just minutes away, your London adventure awaits.Book your stay at St Martins Lane London for a memorable and luxurious experience in the heart of Covent Garden.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for £50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for £50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
