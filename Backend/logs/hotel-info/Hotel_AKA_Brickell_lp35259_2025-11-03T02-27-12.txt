HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience unparalleled luxury at Hotel AKA Brickell, situated in the vibrant heart of Miami.Relaxation &amp; WellnessIndulge in our serene spa, offering a range of soothing massages, rejuvenating body treatments, and refreshing facials. Unwind in our outdoor pool or soak away your worries in the hot tub, all while keeping fit at our 24-hour fitness center.Culinary DelightsSavor gourmet meals at Adrift Mare, one of our two exceptional restaurants, or take advantage of our room service for a comfortable in-room dining experience. Enjoy a cocktail at the poolside bar or one of our stylish lounges.Exclusive ComfortOur 215 elegantly designed guestrooms feature pillowtop beds adorned with Egyptian cotton sheets, spacious bathrooms with deep soaking tubs, and 55-inch LCD TVs for your entertainment. Complimentary Wi-Fi ensures you stay connected throughout your stay.Book your unforgettable escape at Hotel AKA Brickell today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* Children 17 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* Guests can arrange to bring pets by contacting the property directly, using the contact information on the booking confirmation (surcharges apply and can be found in the Fees section).
* A car is recommended for transportation to and from this property.
* This property welcomes guests of all sexual orientations and gender identities (LGBTQ+ friendly).

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 9/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with specific praise for friendly and helpful staff members such as Elijah, Mario, Rodrigo, Eduard, and Dieago.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks, restaurants, bars, and convenient stores.
• Room Quality: 9/10 - Rooms were spacious, clean, and well-equipped, with guests particularly enjoying the views and comfortable beds.
• Amenities: 7/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the pool, jacuzzi, and outdoor workout area.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money: some felt the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location and quality.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praised the friendly staff, clean rooms, great location, and comfortable beds.
