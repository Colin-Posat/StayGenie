HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Chicago's Finest Boutique Hotel in River NorthHistorical Inspiration: Immerse yourself in the elegance of the Chicago World’s Fair with our intricately designed rooms and chic décor. Enjoy entertainment on the 37-inch flat-screen TV or pamper yourself in the large designer bathrooms with plush bathrobes.Wellness and Dining: Take a dip in the indoor pool, soak up the sun on the sundeck, or stay active in the 24-hour fitness center. Indulge in complimentary morning coffee and tea before savoring regional cuisine at CBR. Don't miss the delectable brunch menu on weekends.Prime Location: Just steps from the CTA Red Line, our eco-friendly hotel is a stone's throw away from Navy Pier and Millennium Park. With both airports within easy reach, your journey to luxury begins as soon as you land.Book now to experience the epitome of Chicago luxury at The Royal Sonesta Chicago River North.

Please note: The property's cancellation policy includes any applicable taxes.

The hotel considers 10 or more rooms a group. The hotel reserves the right to cancel 10 or more rooms booked online. Please contact the hotel directly if you are booking 10 or more rooms.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but some guests mentioned maintenance issues and outdated decor.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests disappointed by closed pools and lack of clarity on included services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location while others found it overpriced.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, despite some complaints about variety and pricing.
• Overall Experience: 7.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, with highlights on the location and friendly staff.
