HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Canoe and Suites offers 4-star air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each featuring a private bathroom and stunning mountain views from a balcony. Enjoy the convenience of a kettle, flat-screen TV, and free toiletries in every room.Convenient LocationLocated near popular attractions like The Whyte Museum and Banff Park Museum, this non-smoking property provides easy access to explore the beauty of Banff. The attentive staff is ready to assist you at any time, ensuring a memorable stay.Exceptional ServiceWith a restaurant on-site and guidance available at the reception, Hotel Canoe and Suites prioritizes your comfort and satisfaction throughout your visit. Book now to experience a blend of luxury and convenience in Banff.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Bookings of 3 rooms or more will be classed as a group reservation and charged a 1 night plus tax as a deposit. A group cancelation policy of 30 days prior arrival will be applicable. Changes or cancelations will need to be made at least 30 days in advance or the 1 night deposit will be forfeited. There must be a name attached to each room.This property does not accommodate bachelor(ette) or similar parties.
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
