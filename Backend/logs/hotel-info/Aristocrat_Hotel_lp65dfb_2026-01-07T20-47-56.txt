HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationSituated in Sultanahmet district, Aristocrat Hotel offers modern, soundproofed rooms with elegant wooden furniture, minibar, and flat-screen TVs. The air-conditioned rooms also provide free WiFi, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests.Spectacular Rooftop TerraceIndulge in breathtaking views of historical landmarks such as the Blue Mosque and Hagia Sophia from the hotel's rooftop terrace. Guests can savor a delightful breakfast buffet while taking in the panoramic scenes or unwind with a cup of Turkish tea at the terrace cafe.Prime LocationConveniently located just a short stroll from the Blue Mosque and Hagia Sophia, Aristocrat Hotel provides easy access to other attractions like Basilica Cistern and Topkapi Palace. With Sultanahmet Bus Station nearby, exploring Istanbul is a breeze.Experience luxury and convenience at Aristocrat Hotel. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Istanbul getaway.
