HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Accommodation OptionsLocated just a 5-minute walk from JR Ikebukuro Station, Sakura Hotel offers a variety of rooms including Western, Japanese-style, and dormitory rooms.Amenities and ServicesEnjoy free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and en suite bathrooms in private rooms. The 24-hour front desk provides luggage storage, and there's a coin launderette on-site.Dining and Nearby AttractionsSakura Cafe &amp; Restaurant serves Asian and Western cuisine, while popular districts like Shinjuku and Shibuya are a short train ride away.Book your stay at Sakura Hotel for a comfortable and convenient experience in Tokyo!
