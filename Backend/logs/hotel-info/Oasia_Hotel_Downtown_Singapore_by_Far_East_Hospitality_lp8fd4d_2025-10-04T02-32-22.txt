HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Green Façade and Urban OasisLocated in the heart of Singapore, Oasia Hotel Downtown offers a unique escape from the city's hustle and bustle with its lush green façade, resembling a tropical resort. The hotel's strategic location near Tanjong Pagar MRT station provides easy access to popular attractions like the Singapore City Gallery and Red Dot Design Museum, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers.Exclusive Club Room Amenities and Wellness ExperienceGuests staying in the Club Rooms at Oasia Hotel Downtown can indulge in an all-inclusive experience, complete with access to exclusive facilities and services. From a private Club Lounge offering superfoods to complimentary wellness activities, Club Room guests can fully rejuvenate and recharge during their stay. The rooms are equipped with luxurious amenities, including a bathtub, minibar, and personalized wellness items, ensuring a comfortable and revitalizing experience.Rooftop Pools and Culinary DelightsUnwind and relax at the hotel's outdoor rooftop pools on Level 27, offering stunning views of the city skyline. For a more exclusive experience, Club Room guests can enjoy the indoor infinity pool on Level 21. Indulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's restaurants and bars, where guests can savor a variety of delectable dishes and beverages. With complimentary Wi-Fi, top-notch fitness facilities, and personalized services, Oasia Hotel Downtown promises a memorable and rejuvenating stay for all guests.Book your stay at Oasia Hotel Downtown now for an unforgettable urban oasis experience in Singapore!
