HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Designer Hostel in Former Zoological BuildingDesigner Lounge and Bar, Dining Space, and Secret BarComfortable Rooms with Air Conditioning and Private BathroomsExperience the vibrant atmosphere of Generator Amsterdam, a designer hostel set in a former zoological building near De Pijp. Unwind in the designer lounge and bar, dine in the unique spaces, and discover the secret bar in the former boiler room. Enjoy the comfort of air-conditioned rooms with private bathrooms. Start your day with breakfast at the onsite café and take advantage of the 24-hour front desk. Explore the city with bike hire and visit nearby attractions like Rembrandtplein and Heineken Experience. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Amsterdam.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
GROUP POLICY: Any reservation of 10 people or more per night will be considered a group with different terms and conditions. Groups reservations can only be made through our reservations team, and Generator reserves the right to cancel it otherwise.

PARKING: Please note that parking is subject to availability due to limited spaces. Please contact the hotel directly to make a reservation for the parking space. Parking costs EUR 35 per day.

ACCESSIBILITY: Please note that some Family Quadruple Rooms (Quadruple Room with 2 Single Beds and 1 Bunk Bed with Private Bathroom) are wheelchair accessible. Please contact the hostel for more information. Please note that not all rooms are wheelchair accessible. Please inform the property via the Special Requests box during reservation.

AGE RESTRICTIONS: Please note that guest between 16 and 18 years old can stay at the hostel, only private dorm or room,  if parental consent is provided.

AMENITIES: Towels are only included in the Twin rooms, Premium double rooms and SkySuite, for other rooms towels can be rented on site for a supplement of EUR 6. Alternatively, guests can bring their own.

PET POLICY: We would welcome a dog on property but only when staying in a private room, and subject to a fee per dog per Stay. Only Dog under 75lbs./ 34kg.
