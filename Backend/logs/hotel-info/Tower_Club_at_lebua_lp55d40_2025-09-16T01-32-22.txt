HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Stunning Views:Towering over downtown Bangkok, Tower Club at lebua offers luxurious suites with breathtaking views of the Bangkok skyline and Chao Phraya River. From Michelin-starred dining to premium amenities, immerse yourself in luxury at the world's first vertical destination.Exquisite Dining Experiences:Indulge in a culinary journey at the 2-Michelin-starred Mezzaluna, Sirocco &amp; Sky Bar, and the award-winning pan Asian restaurant, Breeze. With 6 dining options and exclusive bars offering a fine selection of beverages, your taste buds are in for a treat.Unparalleled Facilities and Services:Enjoy access to fitness facilities, an outdoor pool with spa jets, and Tower Club Lounge serving complimentary drinks. With sauna, steam rooms, and free property-wide WiFi, every aspect of your stay at Tower Club At lebua is designed for ultimate comfort and relaxation.Experience luxury like never before at Tower Club At Lebua - Book now to elevate your Bangkok getaway!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
For payments made by credit or debit card, the card holder must be present upon check-in and the card must be presented with original passport for cardholder’s protection.

Please note that this property requires a refundable credit card pre-authorisation or cash deposit upon check in to cover incidental charges.

Please note that early check-in is subject to availability and an extra cost. Guests are kindly advised to contact the property directly for more information.

For the safety of accompanying children, balcony doors of all suites will be unlocked upon personal request provided all guests are at least 14 years old. A Balcony Usage Form must be signed upon check-in.

Please check the minimum age requirement ​for​ restaurants / bars at the Dome with the hotel.

Smart Casual Dress Code for Restaurants / bars at the Dome and Tower Club Lounge : Male Guests - Collared shirts, such as polos or button-downs, as well as t-shirts, tailored shorts, dress sandals, and loafers are welcome. Female Guests - Elegant tops, blouses, t-shirts, dresses, skirts, and tailored shorts of a respectable length, paired with dress sandals, are appropriate.

Please note that athletic wear, swimwear, sleeveless shirts for male guests, flip-flops and beach slippers are strictly prohibited.

Additional Requirements for Chef’s Table & Mezzaluna - Smart Elegant Attire: Men - A higher standard of dress is required, including collared shirts and long trousers. T-shirts, sleeveless clothing, shorts, and caps are not permitted. Long jeans may be worn if paired with fully-covered shoes.

Please note that our smart casual dress code applies to children as well.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 17 years old stay for THB 2,877 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 4 years old to 11 years old stay for THB 1,295 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for THB 2,590 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
