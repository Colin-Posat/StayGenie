HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Spectacular ViewsAt Embassy Suites by Hilton Waikiki Beach Walk, experience the perfect blend of comfort and luxury just moments away from the vibrant Waikiki Beach. Indulge in spacious suites with kitchenettes, city or ocean views, and a private balcony for a truly unforgettable stay.Relaxation and EntertainmentUnwind at the open-air Grand Lanai featuring a heated pool, hot tub, and children's pool. Sip on cocktails from the Pakini Bar while enjoying live Hawaiian entertainment. The ideal spot to bask in the sun and soak in the tropical paradise.Convenience at Your FingertipsEnjoy a complimentary full cook-to-order breakfast, fitness center, and business facilities. Explore the 8-acre Waikiki Beach Walk with shopping, dining, and cultural activities just steps away. Your dream Hawaiian getaway awaits!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally clean, with a few complaints about sticky spots on the carpet.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was highly praised for being friendly and responsive, with only a few minor issues reported.
• Location: 8.8/10 - Excellent location near the beach and local amenities, ideal for families and tourists.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with some mentioning a need for renovations and maintenance.
• Amenities: 7.9/10 - Great amenities, including free breakfast, pool, and evening reception, with some mentions of overcrowding.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money, with some feeling it was worth the price and others expecting more.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining services received positive feedback, although some desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and praised the hotel for its hospitality and services.
