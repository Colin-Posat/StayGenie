HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Luxury in South BeachLuxurious Accommodations: Hotel Via offers modern, custom-made furniture and stunning views of Oracle Park and the city skyline. Relax in style with flat-screen TVs, free toiletries, and high-speed WiFi.Rooftop Oasis: Indulge in the rooftop bar's 360-degree views of Oracle Park, the Bay, and San Francisco. Unwind with cocktails and small plates made from seasonal ingredients at the onsite bar and restaurant.Convenience at Your Fingertips: With a 24-hour front desk, breakfast café, meeting room, event space, communal workspace, and fitness center, Hotel Via ensures a seamless stay. Explore nearby attractions like Union Square and Moscone Center, all just a short distance away.Book your luxurious stay at Hotel Via for an unforgettable San Francisco experience.
