HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Miami Convention Center and BeachesAdjacent to the Miami Convention Center, Comfort Inn &amp; Suites Downtown Brickell-Port of Miami offers a prime location just minutes away from the stunning beaches of Miami.Comfortable Accommodations with City ViewsThe hotel provides spacious and air-conditioned rooms with cable TV, ironing facilities, and picturesque views of the city or Miami River, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Convenient Amenities and ServicesGuests can enjoy a daily breakfast, relax by the outdoor pool with a sundeck, and access weekday newspapers in the 24-hour lobby. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 150 is required. The host charges this 7 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note the property does not accept cash.

For reservations made by a third party, you will need to complete an authorisation form and present a copy of the person's ID and credit card.

Please note that rooms with river view may have limited availability.

• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Satisfactory amenities but mixed feedback; some guests enjoyed the breakfast and pool, while others noted limited options and noise issues.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money; some guests felt the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some minor inconveniences.
