Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Guests had mixed experiences with cleanliness. Some mentioned the rooms were clean, while others highlighted outdated rooms and old furniture.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although some guests had complaints about the slow elevator response and hotel layout.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was well-received for its proximity to transport links and the train station.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were described as modern with a great shower, but there were comments about uncomfortable beds and noise issues.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities. Some guests praised the roof terrace and storage options, while others mentioned issues with air conditioning and soundproofing.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions were split on the value for money, with some guests feeling it was overpriced for the quality offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast was a common point of feedback, with some finding it disappointing and others praising it. Some guests also mentioned noise issues affecting sleep quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing the great location and cleanliness as highlights. However, there were mentions of noise disturbances and comfort issues.
