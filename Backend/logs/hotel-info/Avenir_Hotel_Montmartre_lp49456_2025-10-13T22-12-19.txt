HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in MontmartreThe Avenir Hotel Montmartre is perfectly situated in the heart of Montmartre, just a short stroll away from iconic landmarks like the Sacré-Cœur Basilica and the Place du Tertre.Comfortable and Convenient AccommodationEnjoy a peaceful stay in soundproofed rooms equipped with modern amenities including free WiFi, satellite TV, and a private bathroom with a shower. The convenience of a lift servicing all rooms adds to the comfort of your stay.Convenient Services and AccessIndulge in daily breakfast offerings, served in-room for a relaxing start to your day. Benefit from the 24-hour front desk and the hotel's prime location opposite Anvers Metro station, providing easy access to Paris' famous attractions.Experience the charm of Montmartre at the Avenir Hotel Montmartre - Book your stay now!
