HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Elegance with Modern LuxuryNestled in the heart of Barcelona's Gothic Quarter, Hotel Neri offers a unique blend of 18th-century charm and contemporary sophistication.Sophisticated Dining and RelaxationIndulge in delectable cuisine at the hotel restaurant, unwind with a drink at the terrace bar, or escape to the tranquil library or rooftop garden terrace for a peaceful retreat.Preserved Heritage and Exclusive AccommodationsWith its preserved historic façade, 17th-century stone wall, and Specialty Monument status, Hotel Neri provides a truly exclusive experience. Additionally, guests can opt for luxurious stays in the meticulously restored apartments nearby.Experience the allure of ancient Barcelona at Hotel Neri Relais &amp; Châteaux. Book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 33 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly and helpful staff
• Convenient location in the Gothic Quarter
• Beautiful presentation of breakfast
• Historical charm and modern luxury

Areas for Improvement:
• Noise disturbances in the location
• Mixed reviews on amenities and room quality
• Varied opinions on the value for money

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, with no major complaints mentioned.
• Service: 8.6/10 - Exceptional customer service was consistently highlighted as a positive aspect of the hotel, with guests praising the helpful and attentive staff.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location in the Gothic Quarter was highly praised for its convenience and proximity to attractions, although some noise disturbances were noted in a few reviews.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests generally found the rooms to be comfortable and well-maintained, although a few mentioned minor issues like noisy air conditioning.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews regarding amenities, with some guests enjoying the rooftop areas while others mentioned limitations such as uncomfortable bathrooms.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Varied opinions on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the service and location, while others found it expensive for the experience provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services received positive feedback overall, with compliments on the breakfast presentation and staff recommendations, although minor issues like limited variety were mentioned.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, highlighting the combination of historical charm, modern luxury, and exceptional customer service.
