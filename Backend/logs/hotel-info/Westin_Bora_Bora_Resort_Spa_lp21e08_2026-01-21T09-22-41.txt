HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Overwater Accommodations: Experience the best of Bora Bora in our incredible overwater bungalows and villas, offering stunning views and ultimate comfort.Wellness and Eco-Friendly Features: Indulge in a variety of well-being and spa offerings, while also supporting our beloved Eco Center and Turtle Sanctuary without leaving the resort.Spectacular Dining and Sunset Views: Enjoy diverse culinary options, relax at our Spa and Wellness center, and end your day with a sunset drink at our spectacular bar overlooking the beautiful scenery.To unveil the newest luxury destination in Bora Bora, book your stay at Westin Bora Bora Resort &amp; Spa now!
