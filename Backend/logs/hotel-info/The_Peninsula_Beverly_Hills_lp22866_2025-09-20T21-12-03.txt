HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in the cream-colored rooms at Beverly Hills The Peninsula, each equipped with an iPod docking station, refrigerator, and minibar for your comfort.Gourmet Dining ExperienceSavor world-class cuisine at the Belvedere restaurant, offering award-winning meals in a lively European Brasserie setting. Enjoy al fresco dining on the outdoor terrace under the Beverly Hills evening sky.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind at the full-service spa, take a dip in the large rooftop outdoor pool, or stay active at the modern gym with personal coaches. Free WiFi, fresh fruit at check-in, and shoe shining services add to the luxurious experience.Book your stay at Beverly Hills The Peninsula for a truly unforgettable getaway.

• Cleanliness: 3/10 - Guests mentioned poor cleanliness and visible neglect in the hotel.
• Service: 3.5/10 - Staff were described as rude, unhelpful, and dismissive, leading to an unwelcoming experience.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was praised as a peaceful oasis with a lovely garden and the ability to see fireworks.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests enjoyed the comfortable, quiet, and old-world elegant rooms but noted issues like a non-working iPad and no in-room coffee.
• Amenities: 7/10 - High tea was exquisite with excellent service and ambiance, though minor issues like a non-working iPad were mentioned.
• Value for Money: 5.6/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with negative experiences impacting the overall perception of cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - High tea was praised for its quality and service, with no significant cons mentioned.
• Overall Experience: 6.3/10 - Mixed reviews led to an average overall experience rating, with positives like high tea and staff kindness balanced by negatives in service and cleanliness.
