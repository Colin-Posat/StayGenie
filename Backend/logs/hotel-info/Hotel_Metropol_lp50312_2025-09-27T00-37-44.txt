HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Mexico CityLocated on the tree-lined avenue of Luis Moya, Hotel Metropol is just a block away from the iconic Palace of Fine Arts, offering guests a vibrant and convenient stay in the heart of Mexico City.On-Site Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey with delicious authentic Mexican and international cuisine at our on-site restaurant. Enjoy a refreshing drink and live music at the Lobby Bar in the evenings, creating the perfect ambiance for relaxation.Comfortable Accommodation and Room ServiceUnwind in our cozy guestrooms after a busy day exploring the city and take advantage of our convenient room service. Wake up refreshed to explore the nearby cathedrals and colonial architecture, just steps away from the hotel.Book your stay at Hotel Metropol today for an unforgettable experience in Mexico City.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 8 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 6 years old and over stay for MXN 150 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
