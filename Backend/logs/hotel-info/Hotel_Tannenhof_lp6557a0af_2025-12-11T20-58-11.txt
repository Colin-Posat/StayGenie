HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ZermattLocated in the heart of Zermatt, Hotel Tannenhof offers a convenient base for your stay. Just 600 meters from Zermatt Railway Station, this 3-star hotel is perfect for exploring the city on foot.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesEach room at Hotel Tannenhof is thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen TV, coffee machine, and free WiFi. The property also offers allergy-free rooms and a ski pass sales point, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay.Dining Options and Nearby AttractionsIndulge in a variety of cuisines at the hotel's restaurant, including Italian and Mediterranean dishes. With the Matterhorn Museum just 200 meters away and skiing and cycling opportunities nearby, there's plenty to explore during your stay at Hotel Tannenhof.Ready to experience comfort and convenience in Zermatt?
