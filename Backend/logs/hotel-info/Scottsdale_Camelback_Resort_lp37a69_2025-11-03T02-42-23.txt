GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Close to landmarks
• Comfortable beds
• Family-friendly amenities
• Extensive recreation opportunities

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited pool hours
• Cleanliness issues
• Expensive breakfast
• Diverse service quality

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.5/10 - Opinions about cleanliness were mixed. While some guests praised the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas, others reported instances of cleanliness issues like musty smells, dirty pool, and stains.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Service received positive feedback overall, with guests appreciating friendly staff, fast check-in, and helpful assistance. However, there were isolated incidents of rude behavior by staff members.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location near Camelback and Old Scottsdale was highly valued by guests for its convenience and proximity to shopping and dining options.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Guests generally found the rooms comfortable, spacious, and well-maintained. Some mentioned specific issues like eerie feelings, maintenance problems, and lack of views.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Mixed reviews were received for amenities, with some guests enjoying the recreation opportunities and pool facilities, while others expressed disappointment over limited offerings like lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Opinions on value for money were divergent, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location and amenities, while others thought it was too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Most guests were satisfied with the breakfast and restaurant services offered, though some desired more variety in the food selection.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing factors like comfort, security, and family-friendly amenities.
