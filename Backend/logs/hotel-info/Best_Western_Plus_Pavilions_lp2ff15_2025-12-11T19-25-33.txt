HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location Near Disneyland and Convention CenterBest Western Plus Pavilions, located just a short walk from Disneyland Resort and Anaheim Convention Center, offers comfortable rooms with convenient amenities like a microwave and refrigerator.Relaxing Outdoor Pool and Complimentary BreakfastGuests can unwind in the outdoor pool and hot tub or start their day with a complimentary breakfast at the adjacent Denny's Restaurant.Convenient Services and Tickets to Disneyland® ResortEnjoy free Wi-Fi, cable TV, and access to vending machines for snacks.
