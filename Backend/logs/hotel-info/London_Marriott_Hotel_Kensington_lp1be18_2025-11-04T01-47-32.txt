Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.

Category Ratings:

• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's location near public transport and key landmarks was highly appreciated by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were comfortable, but some guests mentioned smaller room sizes and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.4/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests enjoying the gym and pool while others noted the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Views on pricing varied, with some guests feeling it was high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
