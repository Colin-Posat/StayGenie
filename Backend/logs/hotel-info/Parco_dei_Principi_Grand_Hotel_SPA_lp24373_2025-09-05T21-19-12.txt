HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and Stunning ViewsExperience unparalleled luxury at the Parco dei Principi Grand Hotel &amp; SPA, where each room offers breathtaking views of Rome's iconic landmarks. Relax in style in our spacious rooms equipped with state-of-the-art amenities and enjoy the lush garden vistas from your window.Gourmet Dining and RelaxationTreat your taste buds at the Pauline Borghese gourmet restaurant, offering a delightful culinary experience overlooking the summer pool. Unwind at the Prince Spa with a range of wellness facilities including indoor pool, sauna, and Turkish bath. Indulge in beauty treatments and savor herbal teas in the serene relaxation area.Convenience and AccessibilityConveniently located next to Villa Borghese park, our hotel is a short walk from the renowned Galleria Borghese museum and easily accessible from Roma Termini Train Station. Book now for an unforgettable stay at Parco dei Principi Grand Hotel &amp; SPA.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 35 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 17 years old stay for € 120 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
