HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesTamarind Tree Hotel boasts an outdoor heated swimming pool, fitness centre, and a beautiful garden for guests to relax and unwind. The terrace offers stunning views and is perfect for enjoying a drink or meal.Elegant Rooms with ViewsEach room at Tamarind Tree Hotel is equipped with a 43-inch flat-screen TV, private bathroom, and picturesque garden or pool views. The rooms are designed for comfort and convenience, with a desk, chair, and tea &amp; coffee making facilities provided.Convenient LocationSituated just 5 km from the Kenyatta International Conference Centre, Tamarind Tree Hotel is centrally located near key attractions such as the Nairobi National Museum and Karen Blixen Museum. With easy access to Wilson Airport and Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, guests can travel effortlessly.Experience luxury and comfort at Tamarind Tree Hotel - book your stay now for a memorable experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 11 years old stay for US$25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 11 years old stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for US$75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
