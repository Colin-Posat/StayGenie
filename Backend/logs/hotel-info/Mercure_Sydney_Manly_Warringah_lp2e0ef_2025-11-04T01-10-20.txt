HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Newly Refurbished Mercure Sydney Manly Warringah is your perfect springboard for Northern Beaches adventures.Luxurious Rooms with Modern Amenities: Enjoy a comfortable stay in one of our 120 rooms equipped with a private kitchenette, TV, air conditioning, ironing equipment, and a safe.Convenient Services: Benefit from on-site parking, free Wi-Fi, a laundry, a 24-hour reception, and a self-check-in desk ensuring ultimate comfort and convenience.Dining Options and Nearby Attractions: Indulge in a buffet breakfast at our restaurant or order from the all-day menu for in-room dining. Explore Warringah Mall, just a short walk away, or take a ferry to Sydney CBD for more adventures.Book your stay at Mercure Sydney Manly Warringah now for an unforgettable experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Mercure Sydney Manly Warringah in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of AUD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note, there is a 1.4% credit card surcharge when using a credit card.
Please note guests receive free WiFi at 512Kbps for up to 2 devices per room.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 50 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  AUD 20 per  day.
