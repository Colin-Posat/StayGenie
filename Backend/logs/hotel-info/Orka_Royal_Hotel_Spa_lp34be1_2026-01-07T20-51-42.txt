HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Spa and Recreation FacilitiesIndulge in massages, body treatments, or a refreshing dip in the outdoor or indoor pool at our Art Deco hotel. A health club is also available for your recreational pleasure.Culinary DelightsSavor international cuisine at Eugenius Restaurant or grab a coffee at the cafe. Enjoy drinks at the bar/lounge or poolside bars. 24-hour room service ensures you can dine at your convenience.Convenient Amenities and LocationBenefit from complimentary WiFi, a business center, and event facilities. Our hotel offers express check-in/out and a hair salon. Located centrally in Istanbul, near iconic landmarks and the airport, a stay at Orka Royal Hotel &amp; Spa promises both relaxation and convenience.
