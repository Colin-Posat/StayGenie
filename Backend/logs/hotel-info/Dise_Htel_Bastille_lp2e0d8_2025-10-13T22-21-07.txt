HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesLocated just 150 meters from Charonne Metro Station, the Classics Hotel Bastille offers on-site parking, free Wi-Fi, and a charming interior garden. The guest rooms are well-equipped with amenities such as hot drink facilities, flat-screen TVs, and en suite bathrooms.Delicious Breakfast and TerraceStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast served every morning at the hotel. Enjoy the meal on the terrace, especially when the weather is pleasant.Nearby AttractionsExplore the iconic Père Lachaise cemetery, located 850 meters away, where you can visit the graves of famous personalities like Jim Morrison and Edith Piaf. The bustling Place de la Bastille is also just 1.4 km from the hotel.Book your stay at Classics Hotel Bastille for a comfortable and memorable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that extra beds can only be added (on request) to the Triple Room.

Please note that the parking can not accommodate large vehicles (180 cm high, 470 cm long, 190 cm wide).
You are kindly requested to book your parking space before arrival.

The hotel does not have air conditioning but does provide fans in the rooms.
