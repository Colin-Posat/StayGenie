HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated just a 2-minute walk from Inaricho Station with easy access to Ginza and Shibuya, Hotel Mystays Ueno East is a prime choice for travelers seeking convenience. The hotel is also an 8-minute walk from the Asakusa exit of Ueno Train Station, making exploring Tokyo a breeze.Comfortable RoomsEach air-conditioned room at Mystays Ueno East is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, fridge, and electric kettle. Guests can enjoy a restful night's sleep on memory foam pillows and make use of the provided sleepwear and slippers. The private bathrooms come stocked with complimentary toiletries for added convenience.Relaxing ServicesGuests can unwind with the hotel's massage services or enjoy a meal at the casual Western restaurant. With free WiFi throughout the property and an array of services like laptop rentals and in-room massage, Hotel Mystays Ueno East ensures a relaxing and enjoyable stay.To experience comfort and convenience in the heart of Tokyo, book your stay at Hotel Mystays Ueno East today.
