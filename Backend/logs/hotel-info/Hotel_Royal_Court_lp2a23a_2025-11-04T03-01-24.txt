HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Boutique Hotel in RomeArt-Nouveau Design: Step into a world of elegance and style with our beautifully designed town house turned 4-star hotel. Enjoy the luxury of wood flooring, fine fabrics, and period furniture in your room.Convenient Location: Located just 400 meters from Termini Station, Hotel Royal Court offers easy access to public transport, making it a breeze to explore the city. With connections to international airports and iconic landmarks like the Coliseum and Roman Forum nearby, your Roman adventure awaits.Tranquil Garden Oasis: Escape the hustle and bustle of Rome in our small garden, a peaceful retreat after a day of sightseeing. Unwind and relax in this serene setting, exclusive to our guests.Book your stay at Hotel Royal Court now for a perfect blend of luxury, convenience, and tranquility.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that the hotel does not accept Visa Electron to guarantee reservations.

For reservations of 3 or more rooms, a deposit is required and the standard cancellation policy does not apply.

All reservations have to be guaranteed with a credit card held by the guest. If the credit card used to guarantee the reservation is in a different person's name the reservation will be not accepted.

The hotel can be considered a spread hotel, with 3 different locations: the main building, the Dependance located in via Marghera 29  and the Royal Appartment located in via Vicenza 38.

• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but some guests experienced issues like noisy surroundings and high bathtubs.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating the cleanliness and comfort, while others mentioned noise issues.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Several guests felt that the hotel was not a good value for the price paid due to noise and room quality issues.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and overall food services received positive feedback, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7/10 - While many guests had positive experiences, some were disappointed with aspects like noise levels and room quality.
