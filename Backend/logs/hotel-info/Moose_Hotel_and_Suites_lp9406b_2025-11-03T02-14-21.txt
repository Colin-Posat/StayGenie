HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Moose Hotel &amp; Suites, just a stone's throw away from Banff's bustling center, offers a tranquil escape with its spa center and cozy suites featuring private balconies.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a delightful a la carte breakfast each morning and savor the gourmet Italian cuisine at Pacini Italian Restaurant, promising a culinary experience like no other.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind at the Meadow Spa &amp; Pools with its 10 treatment rooms and outdoor hot pool. Take a dip in the indoor swimming pool, stay active at the exercise room, or soak in the breathtaking views from the rooftop hot pools.Book your stay at Moose Hotel &amp; Suites now for an unforgettable retreat in the heart of Banff!
