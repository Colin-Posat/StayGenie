IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests who anticipate arriving after 23:00 should contact the hotel directly prior to travel.

Please note that the restaurant is closed on weekends and bank holidays.

The snack bar is open from 7:00 to midnight.

Reservations of more than 9 rooms may be subject to special conditions and may incur additional supplements

Please note that free Wi-Fi is available in public areas of the hotel.
Early check-in and late check-out are available for an additional fee of EUR 10.
The hotel reserves the right to pre-authorize credit cards prior to arrival.
Guests must present the same credit card used to make the booking upon check-in. If this is not the case, they must pay for their stay with a new card that they can physically present and prove through physical identification that it belongs to them.
A damage deposit of € 150 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. It will be refunded to you upon check-out. The deposit will be refunded in full by credit card, subject to an inspection of the accommodations.
Reservations for more than 5 rooms may be subject to different policies and additional supplements.
Reservations for more than 11 people are subject to different policies and additional supplements.
Transfer: The hotel does not offer free transfers. The hotel works with a trusted company to offer a quality private transfer service to and from major transportation points. Guests can book this service if they wish.
Pets: The hotel is Pet Friendly. The fee is €20 per pet, per week. Extras and amenities for the animal are not included.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
