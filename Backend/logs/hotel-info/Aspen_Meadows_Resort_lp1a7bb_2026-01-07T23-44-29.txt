HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Aspen Meadows Resort offers spacious all-suite accommodations with modern amenities like wireless internet access, mini-refrigerators, and flat-screen TVs. The rooms also feature sofa beds and iPod docking stations for ultimate comfort.Recreational ActivitiesNestled in the Colorado Rocky Mountains, guests can enjoy a plethora of activities year-round, from top-rated ski trails in the winter to scenic hiking paths and white water rafting in the summer. The resort's outdoor year-round swimming pool and state-of-the-art fitness facilities provide additional entertainment options.Gourmet Dining and Local TransportIndulge in gourmet on-site dining options and unwind at the resort's bar after a day of adventure. Take advantage of the free 24-hour local transport service to explore the charming town of Aspen, with its array of shops and restaurants just a ride away.Book your stay at Aspen Meadows Resort for a luxurious mountain getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
