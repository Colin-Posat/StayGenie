HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Spectacular ViewsAstro Palace Hotel &amp; Suites offers elegantly designed rooms with modern amenities and breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. Each room features a large balcony or terrace, satellite LCD TV, and free Wi-Fi, providing guests with a comfortable and luxurious stay.Wellness and RelaxationGuests can unwind at the hotel's wellness centre, complete with a gym, hot tub, sauna, and a variety of spa treatments at Cassiopeia Spa. The sun beds by the swimming pool offer a perfect spot for relaxation, while the serene ambiance adds to the overall rejuvenating experience.Culinary Delights and Wine TastingIndulge in a gastronomic journey at Pleiades restaurant, where guests can savor a range of menus paired with a wine tasting experience. With stunning views of the Aegean Sea as the backdrop, dining at the hotel becomes a memorable and delightful experience.Experience luxury, relaxation, and culinary excellence at Astro Palace Hotel &amp; Suites. Book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.
