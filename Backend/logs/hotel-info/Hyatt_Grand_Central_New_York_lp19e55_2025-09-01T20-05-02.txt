• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally described as comfortable and spacious, although some guests encountered issues like malfunctioning showers and lack of amenities.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed opinions on the amenities, with some guests enjoying the hotel's offerings such as the lobby and chic atmosphere, while others mentioned long check-in lines and lack of facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Guests had differing views on the value for money, with some feeling the prices were justified for the location, while others found them excessive for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Feedback on food and beverage services was generally positive, with guests appreciating the breakfast and restaurant offerings, though some suggested a wider variety.
• Overall Experience: 6.6/10 - Overall experience varied greatly among guests, with mixed sentiments ranging from positive highlights to major disappointments.
