CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 10 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 11 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.9/10 - Guests had mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some mentioning clean rooms and others pointing out outdated or underwhelming cleanliness.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, with mentions of polite and friendly staff. However, some guests reported issues with unreliable or unresponsive staff.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel's location was a major highlight, with guests praising its central and convenient proximity to key landmarks, train stations, and harbors.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and basic by most guests, with some complaints about outdated amenities and maintenance issues like non-functioning air conditioning.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - The amenities were rated satisfactory by guests, with some highlighting clean rooms and comfortable beds, while others mentioned underwhelming breakfast options and lack of desired amenities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Guests had mixed feelings about the value for money, with some considering it overpriced for the services provided, while others found it reasonable based on the location and comfort.
• Food and Beverage: 6.5/10 - Food and beverage services received varied feedback, with some guests appreciating breakfast and restaurant options, while others found them lacking in variety or quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, highlighting the location, staff friendliness, and comfortable rooms.
