HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and Traditional ArchitectureRiyad El Cadi offers a unique experience in Marrakech's medina with its 7 traditional Moroccan houses, each showcasing intricate courtyards and arched terraces. The air-conditioned rooms and suites boast minibars, while the hammam adds a touch of relaxation to your stay.Culinary Delights and Rooftop DiningIndulge in a daily continental breakfast served on the rooftop terrace or in the elegant salons. Savor traditional Moroccan and international cuisines for lunch and dinner, or opt for a packed lunch for your adventures in the city.Relaxation and Leisure FacilitiesUnwind in the plunge pool, lounge on the terraces with tents and deckchairs, or explore the 3 salons, alcoves, and library. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi in the main courtyard and let the staff assist you with local tours and excursions.Book your stay at Riyad El Cadi for a blend of luxury, culture, and comfort!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 17 years old stay for € 50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
