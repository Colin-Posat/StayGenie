HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in Parioli District: Hotel Villa Glori is ideally situated next to the Tiber River, offering easy access to Flaminio Metro Station and Piazza del Popolo.Convenient Amenities and Services: Enjoy free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, assistance with tourist information, transfers, and tours, as well as comfortable rooms with air conditioning, satellite TV, and minibar.Relaxing Atmosphere and Nearby Attractions: Unwind at the Toccata e Fuga bar, explore MAXXI Museum and Flaminio Stadium within walking distance, and visit the Olympic Stadium and Auditorium Parco della Musica just a short drive away.Book your stay at Hotel Villa Glori for a memorable experience in Rome!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
