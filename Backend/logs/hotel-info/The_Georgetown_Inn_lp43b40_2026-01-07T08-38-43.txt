HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Old English Décor and Mountain ViewsExperience a touch of Old English charm in our spacious rooms with stunning mountain and valley views. Some rooms even boast a cozy seating area and fireplace for ultimate relaxation.Delightful Amenities and ServicesIndulge in complimentary tea and freshly baked shortbread while enjoying modern amenities like a DVD player, radio, and free Wi-Fi. Our pet-friendly hotel also offers elevators for convenience.On-Site Dining and Nearby AttractionsSavor unique dishes like stuffed French toast or fish and chips at our on-site pub, or unwind with appetizers by the wood-burning fireplace. With easy access to Banff National Park and Canmore's center, adventure awaits just minutes away.Book now to enjoy a memorable stay at the Inn Georgetown, where comfort meets adventure in the heart of the Canadian Rockies.

Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for CAD 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
