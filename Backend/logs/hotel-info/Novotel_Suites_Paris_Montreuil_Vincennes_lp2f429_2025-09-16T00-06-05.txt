HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Free Wi-FiIndulge in the comfort of our 4-star hotel located in the vibrant east of Paris. Our suites offer free Wi-Fi, Canal +, and BeIN Sport channels for your entertainment. Just an 8-minute walk from Porte de Montreuil Metro Station, explore Paris effortlessly from our convenient location.Modern Amenities and ServicesExperience a seamless stay with satellite TV, unlimited calls within France, air-conditioning, microwave, and refrigerator in all suites. Start your day right with our delicious buffet breakfast and enjoy the convenience of a 24-hour reception for all your needs.Convenient Location with Easy AccessWith private parking available on-site and proximity to the Peripherique Ring Road, access Charles de Gaulle and Orly Airports with ease. Explore Paris with direct connections to iconic landmarks and major train stations within 20 minutes.Book your stay at Novotel Suites Paris Montreuil Vincennes for a memorable Parisian experience!
