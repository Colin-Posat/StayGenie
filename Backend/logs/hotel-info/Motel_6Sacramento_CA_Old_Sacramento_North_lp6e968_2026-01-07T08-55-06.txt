Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Rooms were described as clean by multiple guests, although some mentioned instances of bugs and dust.

• Location: 7.7/10 - The hotel's location was praised for being close to attractions and downtown, but some guests expressed concerns about safety and nearby loiterers.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and well-furnished, with positive comments on recent remodeling. However, some guests reported issues like lack of shampoo and bright lighting.
• Amenities: 6.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with mentions of protected parking lot but complaints about out-of-order vending machines, dirty pool, and lack of breakfast service.

• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Food and beverage services were decently received, with positive comments on cleanliness and variety but also some mentions of limited options.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing cleanliness, location, and comfortable rooms as highlights. However, concerns about safety, service quality, and value for money were also evident.
