HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation ChoicesLe Maitai Polynesia offers a variety of rooms and bungalows with stunning garden or lagoon views. The overwater bungalows provide a unique experience with a glass table for underwater viewing.Exquisite Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at “Haere Mai” restaurant serving delectable French and Polynesian dishes amidst a tropical garden ambiance. Enjoy traditional Polynesian performances while dining.Exciting Activities and AdventuresGuests can explore the crystal-clear waters with complimentary fishing, snorkelling, and kayaking gear. For more adventures, the tour desk assists in arranging island cruises and scuba diving trips.Experience luxury, dining, and adventure at Le Maitai Polynesia - book your stay now!
