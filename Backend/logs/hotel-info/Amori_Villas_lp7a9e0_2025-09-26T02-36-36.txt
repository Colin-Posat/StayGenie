HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for Rp 200,000 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for Rp 250,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 16 years old stay for Rp 650,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 16 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 17 years old and over stay for Rp 600,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.8/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with attentive staff.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's proximity to Ubud and serene surroundings were highly valued.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms, especially the 'cabanes,' were praised for their comfort and design.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests enjoyed the pool and restaurant facilities, though some noise issues were noted.
• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - Mixed opinions on pricing, with some guests feeling it was justified for the overall experience.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - The restaurant's cuisine and breakfast were well-received, though noise from the waterfall was a downside.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and recommended the hotel for relaxation and easy access to Ubud.
