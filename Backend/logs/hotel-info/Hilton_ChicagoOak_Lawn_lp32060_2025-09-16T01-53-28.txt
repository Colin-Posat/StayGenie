HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesLocated just 4 miles from Chicago Midway International Airport, Hilton Chicago/Oak Lawn offers free airport shuttles, making travel a breeze. The indoor pool provides relaxation, while spacious rooms with flat-screen TVs and free WiFi ensure a comfortable stay.Diverse FacilitiesGuests can enjoy a fitness center, business center, and two on-site restaurants - South Side Social Neighborhood Kitchen and Tap, and Starbucks. With 17,000 sq ft of meeting spaces, this hotel caters to both leisure and business travelers.Nearby AttractionsConveniently situated near Oak Lawn subway station and Children’s Museum, the hotel is also in close proximity to Brookfield Zoo, University of Chicago, and Adler Planetarium.
