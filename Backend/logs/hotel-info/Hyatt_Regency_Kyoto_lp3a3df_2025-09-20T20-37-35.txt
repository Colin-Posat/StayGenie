HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience the stylish Hyatt Regency with spacious rooms adorned in light natural tones, offering modern amenities such as a 32-inch plasma TV and a DVD player. Enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi in the lobby and unwind in comfort with provided nightwear and slippers.Exceptional FacilitiesIndulge in a range of services including a fitness centre, spa treatments at Riraku Spa, and the convenience of rental bicycles. Benefit from a 24-hour concierge, baby-sitting services, and private parking on-site.Culinary DelightsSavor a variety of cuisines at the hotel's 3 restaurants - from Japanese delicacies at Touzan to Italian fare at Trattoria Sette. Don't miss out on the cafe cuisine at Restaurant cafe 33 or enjoy a drink at the bar.Book your stay at Hyatt Regency Kyoto for a luxurious and convenient experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Guests are required to enter their full name in English when making a booking.

Please note that construction work is going on nearby from July 1, 2024 all the way to May 31, 2025 (Monday - Friday, between 10:00 am and 5:00 pm) and some rooms may be affected by noise.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  JPY 2000 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for ¥7,458 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
