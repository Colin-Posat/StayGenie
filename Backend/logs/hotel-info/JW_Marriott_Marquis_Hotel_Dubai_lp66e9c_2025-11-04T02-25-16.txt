HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience unparalleled luxury at JW Marriott Marquis Hotel Dubai, the crown jewel of hospitality in the vibrant heart of the city.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind at our full-service spa, offering an array of rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials. For fitness enthusiasts, our fully equipped health club, outdoor pool, and sauna provide the perfect retreat to relax and refresh.Culinary DelightsIndulge your senses in international cuisine at Kitchen6, one of our 12 exceptional dining options. Whether you choose to dine in or savor 24-hour room service, our culinary team ensures a memorable food experience, complemented by a vibrant poolside bar.Convenience and ConnectivityWith extensive conference facilities and express check-in/out, your business needs are met with ease. Boasting complimentary Wi-Fi and roundtrip airport shuttle service, we make every journey seamless.Book your stay with us today for an unforgettable experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* Up to 2 children 18 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* Some facilities may have restricted access. Guests can contact the property for details using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
