HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building and Prime LocationHotel del Mar, housed in a listed building, offers a unique stay just 800 meters from the seafront and Barceloneta Metro Station. Situated a short stroll from the vibrant Born neighborhood and Ciutadella Park, immerse yourself in the heart of Barcelona's culture and beauty.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesRelax in air-conditioned rooms featuring flat-screen satellite TV, a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries, and some with a private balcony for stunning views. For added peace of mind, a safe is available for use.Convenient Services and Nearby AttractionsBenefit from a 24-hour front desk, proximity to França Train Station, Barcelona Cathedral, and Maremagnum Shopping Centre, all within walking distance. Parking is also conveniently located just 5 minutes away. Experience the best of Barcelona at Hotel del Mar.
