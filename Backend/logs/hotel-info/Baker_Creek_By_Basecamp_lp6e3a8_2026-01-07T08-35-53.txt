HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rocky Mountain ResortSpectacular Views of the Rockies: Enjoy stunning views of the Rocky Mountains from our spacious chalets and suites, each designed with wood panelling and a cozy log cabin feel.Year-Round Recreation: Stay active with access to our fitness centre, steam room, and sauna in the winter. In the evenings, gather around campfires along the creek or hit the skating rink for some fun.Convenient Location: Nestled in Banff National Park, our resort offers easy access to Bow Valley Parkway and is just a short drive from Lake Louise and Yoho National Park.Ready to experience the beauty of the Rockies?
