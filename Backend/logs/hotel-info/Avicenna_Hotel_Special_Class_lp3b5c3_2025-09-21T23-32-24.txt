HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the charm of Avicenna Hotel - Special Class, where modern elegance meets historical splendor in the heart of Istanbul.Elegant AccommodationsRelax in one of our 48 beautifully appointed guestrooms, featuring minibars and LED televisions. With complimentary wireless internet access, satellite programming, and private bathrooms equipped with premium toiletries, you'll feel right at home.Gourmet Dining ExperienceSavor exquisite Turkish cuisine at our TERRACE bar/lounge, where you can enjoy alfresco dining amidst stunning garden views. Indulge in our complimentary buffet breakfast each morning and take advantage of 24-hour room service for a truly convenient stay.Prime Location &amp; Exceptional ServicesLocated just a short stroll from historical landmarks like the Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque, our hotel offers concierge services, a hair salon, and shuttle options to nearby beaches and shopping centers, ensuring an unforgettable Istanbul experience.Book your stay now to explore the wonders of Istanbul at Avicenna Hotel!
