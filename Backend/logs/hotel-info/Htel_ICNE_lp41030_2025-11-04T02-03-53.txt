HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Cinema-Inspired RoomsHotel Icône offers individually decorated rooms that pay homage to cinema icons, providing guests with a unique and stylish stay. Each room is soundproofed and equipped with a flat-screen TV for entertainment.Convenient Services and AmenitiesGuests can enjoy breakfast delivered to their rooms or opt for the buffet in the charming dining room with an open fireplace. With 24-hour front desk assistance, car and bicycle rental services, and a ticket service for local attractions, guests' needs are well taken care of.Prime Location in ParisLocated in the heart of Paris' 2nd district, Hotel Icône is within walking distance of iconic landmarks like Opera Garnier and the Richelieu-Drouot Metro Station. Explore the city easily with attractions like Galeries Lafayette and the Louvre Museum nearby.Book your stay at Hotel Icône now for a memorable Parisian experience!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children 1 year old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 10 years old stay for € 45 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for € 45 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
