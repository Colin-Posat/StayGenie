HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Free WiFiHoliday Inn Express Sydney Macquarie Park offers stylish rooms with free WiFi, perfect for both business and leisure travelers. Each room features modern amenities and a comfortable work space for your convenience.Convenient LocationLocated in the bustling Macquarie Park Business Hub, our hotel is just a short walk from Macquarie University and public transport. With Macquarie Centre shopping mall nearby, guests can easily explore dining options and fashion stores. Easy access to Sydney City Centre by car or train makes it an ideal choice for travelers.Functional Room FeaturesOur rooms are designed to cater to your needs with a modern work space, 40-inch LCD TV, and tea/coffee facilities. The private bathrooms are equipped with walk-in showers and quality hairdryers for a comfortable stay.Book your stay at Holiday Inn Express Sydney Macquarie Park for a seamless and enjoyable experience.
