HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Beachside Luxury AccommodationRefurbished in 2019, Adina Apartment Hotel Coogee offers stylish studio, one, and two-bedroom apartments just steps away from Coogee Beach. Enjoy modern amenities like an indoor heated pool, free WiFi, and covered parking.Relaxed Beachside LivingEach apartment features a soothing beach-inspired color scheme, LCD TV with cable channels, and a private balcony. Larger units come with a fully equipped kitchen and separate living area. Unwind in the garden courtyard or head to the beach for relaxation.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsExplore coastal walks, visit Gordon's Bay, or take a short bus ride to Sydney city center. Parking is subject to availability due to limited spaces.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Adina Apartment Hotel Coogee Sydney in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.

Please note that there is a 2.5% charge when you pay with Diners Card and JCB.

This will be done during the booking process and on check in. The hotel team is required to check vaccination certificates of guests for both vaccination doses or medical exemption forms at the hotel and in the restaurant and bars. For more information, please contact the property using the contact details found on the booking confirmation.

Please note that bookings over 7 nights will only receive a weekly housekeeping service.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  AUD 25 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for AUD 50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.
