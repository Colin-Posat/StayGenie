HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesElk + Avenue Hotel offers a sauna, hot tub, and modern guest rooms with flat-screen TVs and iPod docking stations. Enjoy free WiFi and stunning mountain views from select rooms.Convenient ServicesExperience top-notch service with a ticket desk, tour assistance, and luggage storage available on-site. Guests can partake in various activities such as skiing, hiking, and sightseeing depending on the season.Ideal LocationSituated just 4 km from Banff Gondola, and a short drive to Lake Minnewanka and Lake Louise, this hotel provides the perfect base for exploring the scenic beauty of the area.Book your stay at Elk + Avenue Hotel now to indulge in luxury and convenience during your Banff getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The property offers paid underground parking with a limited number of spaces on a first-come, first-served basis and a vehicle height limit for entry of 6.1 feet. There is a $10/night fee for stays from October to May, and is included in stays from June to September. Our team is available to assist with any questions or concerns. Please note that when booking 10 or more rooms, group policies apply.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
