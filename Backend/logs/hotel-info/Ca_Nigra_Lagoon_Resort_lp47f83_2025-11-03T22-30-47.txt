HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsCa’ Nigra Lagoon Resort, dating back to the 17th century, offers a unique Venetian experience with frescoed walls and a historic ambiance. The air-conditioned rooms blend historic charm with modern amenities like satellite TVs and minibars. Some rooms even feature a luxurious spa bath for ultimate relaxation.Scenic Canal Views and Tranquil GardenSituated directly on the Grand Canal, the hotel provides picturesque views and a serene atmosphere. Guests can unwind in the spacious garden overlooking the canal, offering a peaceful escape from the bustling city.Delightful Breakfast and Convenient LocationStart your day with a delicious breakfast spread including hot drinks, fresh pastries, fruits, eggs, and more. With Saint Mark's Square and Fenice Theatre nearby, and Rialto Bridge just a short walk away, Ca’ Nigra Lagoon Resort is the perfect choice for a memorable stay in Venice.Book your stay now and experience the charm of Ca’ Nigra Lagoon Resort!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for € 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
