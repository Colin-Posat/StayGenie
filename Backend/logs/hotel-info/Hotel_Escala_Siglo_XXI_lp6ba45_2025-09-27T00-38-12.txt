HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Decor and Convenient LocationHotel Escala Siglo XXI offers stylish décor and is conveniently located just 1 km from the Graphic Arts Park and 700 meters from the Chabacano Metro Station. The modern design and free Wi-Fi make it a perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers.Comfortable and Spacious RoomsThe spacious rooms at Hotel Escala Siglo XXI are equipped with a sofa, cable TV, and a private bathroom with a shower. Guests can unwind and relax in the cozy ambiance after a day of exploring the city.Proximity to Popular AttractionsSituated just a 10-minute drive from the vibrant La Condesa district with its nightlife, restaurants, and bars, this hotel is also within reach of the iconic Mexico City Zocalo Square, located 8 km away. Moreover, the Benito Juárez International Airport is only a 25-minute drive, ensuring convenience for travelers.Book your stay at Hotel Escala Siglo XXI for a memorable experience!
