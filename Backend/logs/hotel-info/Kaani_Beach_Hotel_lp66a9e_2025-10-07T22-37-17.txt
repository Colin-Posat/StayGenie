CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 5 years old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 17 years old stay for US$25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 17 years old stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests enjoying the offerings while others noted the lack of facilities and nearby noise.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Opinions varied regarding the value for money, with some finding it expensive for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Guests were generally satisfied with the breakfast and dining options, with some wishing for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the friendly staff, great location, and comfortable beds.
