Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the rooms and facilities, with some minor complaints about room size and bathroom cleanliness.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Staff were described as friendly, accommodating, and helpful. Some guests noted delays in services such as bed making.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's location received high praise for its proximity to key attractions, convenience for travelers, and easy access to public transportation.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were described as clean, comfortable, and well-appointed, although some guests mentioned small room sizes and lack of sunlight in some rooms.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Overall satisfaction with amenities, including mentions of rooftop views, comfortable beds, and luggage storage facilities. Some guests noted limitations such as small room size and lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests considering it expensive for the services provided and others finding it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast options and quality of pastries, with some guests wishing for more variety. Mixed reviews on healthy food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff, cleanliness, and location, while some mentioned room size and breakfast options as areas for improvement.
