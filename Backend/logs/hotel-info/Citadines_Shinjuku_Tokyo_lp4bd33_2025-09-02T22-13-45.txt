HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Studio Apartments with Free WiFiCitadines Shinjuku Tokyo offers stylish studio apartments with free WiFi and wired internet access. The sleek, modern design provides a comfortable and convenient stay for guests looking for a home away from home.Convenient Amenities and ServicesGuests can take advantage of the 24-hour fitness room, coin-operated laundry facilities, and drink vending machines. The 24-hour front desk with luggage storage service ensures a hassle-free experience. Paid parking is also available on site for those arriving by car.Prime Location near Popular AttractionsLocated just minutes away from Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden and the Isetan Department Store, as well as direct subway access to the Ginza and Shinjuku areas, Citadines Shinjuku Tokyo offers a prime location for exploring the vibrant city of Tokyo.Book your stay at Citadines Shinjuku Tokyo now for a modern and convenient experience in the heart of Tokyo.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for ¥550 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for ¥4,840 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
