HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Japanese-Balinese RetreatExquisite Accommodations: Immerse yourself in Japanese design and Balinese charm with air-conditioned rooms featuring Japanese-style beds, private balconies with pool views, and pampering amenities like a minibar and free toiletries.Tranquil Oasis: Nestled amidst lush tropical greenery, indulge in the outdoor pool, spa massages, and complimentary daily yoga. Explore the area with bicycle rentals or savor Indonesian, Japanese, and Western cuisine at the on-site restaurant.Convenient Amenities: Enjoy free WiFi, parking, and a 24-hour front desk. With a shuttle to Ubud center and proximity to top attractions like Ubud Market and Ubud Palace, HOSHINOYA Bali offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.Book your luxurious retreat at HOSHINOYA Bali now for an unforgettable experience!
