Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3.8/10 - Cleanliness was a major issue in several reviews, with guests mentioning dead bugs, blood stains, black mold, cockroaches, and overall uncleanliness.
• Service: 3.9/10 - Service quality varied widely, with complaints about rude staff, slow room service, unhelpful front desk, and incidents of staff being pushy or scolding guests.
• Location: 6.6/10 - While some guests appreciated the central location, others disliked the distance from the city center, noisy surroundings, and the need for further improvements.
• Room Quality: 3.5/10 - Room quality was highly criticized, with reports of broken beds, low water pressure, missing amenities, unclean rooms, and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests mentioning the lack of advertised appliances, poor water pressure, and limited access to Wi-Fi in rooms.
• Value for Money: 5.6/10 - Value for money was a concern for many guests, with reports of high prices for subpar services, theft incidents, and overall dissatisfaction with what was provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, although some guests wanted more variety and better quality.
• Overall Experience: 4.2/10 - Overall experience was overwhelmingly negative, with reports of stolen belongings, unclean rooms, rude staff, and a lack of basic amenities.
