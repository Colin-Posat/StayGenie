HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and charges are applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 59 per  day.

• Amenities: 7/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with some highlighting the gym and pool, while others noted the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.2/10 - Opinions about the value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although there were suggestions for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
