HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and charges may be applicable.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 10 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for £35 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for £70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests praising the cleanliness while others mentioned cleanliness issues, particularly in bathrooms.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was a strong point, with many guests appreciating the attentive and friendly staff. However, some incidents of slow service were reported.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its convenience and proximity to attractions, making it a standout feature for many guests.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the spacious and well-equipped rooms. However, a few complaints were made regarding room maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating the offerings like the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location, while others found it expensive for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, particularly breakfast and restaurant offerings. Some guests suggested a wider variety of options.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the luxurious ambiance, exceptional staff, and the hotel's central London location.
