HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views and Luxury AmenitiesDiscover Hotel Arya, BW Premier Collection, just a short distance from Miami International Airport. Enjoy breathtaking bay or city views from your room while staying connected with complimentary WiFi. Unwind in the outdoor pool, hot tub, and sauna, or explore nearby CocoWalk and a variety of restaurants within walking distance.Modern Comforts and ConvenienceRelax in modern guest rooms featuring a 42-inch flat-screen TV, alarm clock radio, safe, and coffee maker.Art, Fitness, and EntertainmentImmerse yourself in over 8,000 works of art throughout the hotel. Stay active in the well-equipped gym and squash courts. Explore nearby attractions like Vizcaya Museum and Gardens, Dolphin Harbor Miami Seaquarium, Jungle Island, and Miami Metro Zoo.Experience luxury and convenience at Hotel Arya, BW Premier Collection.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Food & beverage services at this property may be limited or unavailable due to Coronavirus (COVID-19).
A damage deposit of USD 60 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Guests with a non-refundable reservation must provide the same credit card upon check-in that was used during booking. Contact hotel for further details.

If you have purchased a rate that includes breakfast, breakfast is provided for up to 2 adults per day. Additional guests must pay for breakfast directly at the property. Breakfast only applies at the onsite restaurant.

Guests must be 21 years of age or older to check in without a parent or official guardian.

Please note the resort fee is valid for up to 5 guests and includes the following:

• Swimming pull access
• Fitness centre access
• Squash court access
• Business centre access
• Package handling
• Concierge services

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 30 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
