HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Comfort and ConvenienceHotel Fine Harmony, just steps away from Amsterdam Central Station, offers modern amenities including free WiFi, Nespresso coffee machines, and a 24-hour front desk.Central LocationConveniently situated near popular attractions like Dam Square and the Royal Palace Amsterdam, this hotel provides easy access to must-see spots in the city.Safety and SecurityWith features like a safety deposit box and a laptop safe in each room, Hotel Fine Harmony ensures a worry-free stay for all guests.Book now to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Hotel Fine Harmony in Amsterdam.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
When booking more than 5 rooms different policies apply.

The name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property. For reservations made by a third party, you will need to complete an authorisation form and present a copy of the person's ID and credit card.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location, while others found it higher than expected for the services provided.
