HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationPension Ciudadela guest house is conveniently located opposite Barcelona’s França Train Station and just a stone's throw away from Ciudadela Park.Comfortable RoomsAll rooms at Ciudadela are equipped with basic amenities including air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and private bathrooms. Most rooms also offer a work desk and a private balcony for added comfort.Nearby AttractionsSituated in the heart of Barcelona, guests can easily explore the vibrant Born district and enjoy a leisurely stroll to the picturesque Barceloneta Beach.Book now to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Pension Ciudadela!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
