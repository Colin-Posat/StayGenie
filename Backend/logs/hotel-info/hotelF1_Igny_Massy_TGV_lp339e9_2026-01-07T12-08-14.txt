HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near ParisHotel F1 Igny, located just 7 kilometers from the Chateau de Versailles, offers affordable accommodation with easy access to Paris.Modern AmenitiesEnjoy comfortable rooms equipped with Satellite TV and free WiFi. The hotel also provides free parking and is easily reachable from the A10 and N444 highways.24/7 Accessibility and ServicesGuests can access their rooms anytime with the onsite credit card-payment machine. The hotel operates all year, welcoming guests in both French and English, and offers a convenient buffet breakfast daily.Book your stay at Hotel F1 Igny now for a budget-friendly and hassle-free experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The reception is open from 06:30 to 21:00 Monday to Friday, and from 07:30 to 12:00 and 17:00 to 21:00 on weekends and public holidays.

If you plan on arriving outside the opening hours, please contact the hotel directly. The telephone number is indicated on the confirmation of reservation.

To be more eco-friendly, this property does not provide standard towels. Guests can purchase a large microfibre towel at the hotel at an extra cost of EUR 3.

A meal tray with a main course, a yogurt and a bottle of water is available for EUR 10.

Please note that the breakfast will be offered at a rate of 2.50 € only.Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
