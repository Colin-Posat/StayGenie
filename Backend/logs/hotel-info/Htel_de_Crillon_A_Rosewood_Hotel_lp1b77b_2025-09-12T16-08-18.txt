HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Spa and Recreation Options Indulge in massages, treatments, and facials at the spa, or take a dip in the indoor pool. Stay active at the fitness center for a rejuvenating experience.Fine Dining and Elegant Accommodations Savor French cuisine at L'Ecrin or enjoy 24-hour room service. Unwind with a drink at the bar/lounge or by the pool. Experience comfort in uniquely decorated guestrooms equipped with modern amenities.Convenient Location and Exclusive Services Centrally located near iconic landmarks like Champs-Élysées and Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honore, the hotel offers easy access to attractions. Benefit from services like limo/town car service, area shuttle, and roundtrip airport shuttle for a seamless stay.Immerse yourself in luxury at Hôtel de Crillon A Rosewood Hotel. A stay here promises a blend of relaxation, sophistication, and top-notch services.
