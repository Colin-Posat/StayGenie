HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and English CharacterLocated just off Piccadilly and next to Green Park, The Stafford London exudes elegance and charm, boasting 5 AA Red Stars for excellence. With a prime location ideal for West End theatres, dining, and shopping, this property is surrounded by art galleries, museums, and royal landmarks.Fine Dining and Luxury AmenitiesIndulge in fine contemporary British cuisine at The Game Bird, sip cocktails at the historic American Bar, or enjoy 24-hour room service. Relax with afternoon tea in The Gamebird Lounge. Each room features modern amenities including a Bose Wave CD player, Nespresso machine, and luxury bathroom products.Historic Architecture and Exceptional ServiceComprising three 17th-century townhouses, The Stafford London offers a unique blend of history and luxury. Guests can access a gym in the Mews and benefit from an award-winning concierge service. Experience traditional English hospitality with a touch of modern luxury at The Stafford London.
