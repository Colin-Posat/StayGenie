HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodations with Free WiFiINNSiDE by Meliá Amsterdam, situated in the vibrant Zuidas business district, offers stylish accommodations with complimentary WiFi. Each room features a flat-screen TV with Netflix, air conditioning, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of in-room breakfast service.Sky Bar with Panoramic ViewsIndulge in a unique experience at the 19th-floor sky bar 'Hasta la vista, Baby!' boasting breathtaking city views. Savor modern Spanish cuisine and handcrafted cocktails while admiring the skyline. Additionally, La Tierra restaurant serves delectable à la carte dishes with special diet menus available upon request.Convenient Location and AccessPerfectly located just 2 km from the Van Gogh Museum and 4 km from the Anne Frank House, the hotel offers easy access to Amsterdam's top attractions. With Amsterdam Zuid station a mere 50 meters away, and Schiphol Airport just 8 km from the hotel, travel convenience is guaranteed.
