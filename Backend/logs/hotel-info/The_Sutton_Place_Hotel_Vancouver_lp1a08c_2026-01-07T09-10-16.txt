HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  CAD 55 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for CAD 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned issues with spots on sheets and a bad smell in rooms.
• Service: 8.8/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests, with many mentioning the helpfulness and friendliness of the staff.
• Location: 9.1/10 - The hotel's location was a standout feature, being close to key attractions, restaurants, and shopping areas.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but some guests reported maintenance issues such as broken AC units and lack of proper maintenance.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Amenities offered were satisfactory, but some guests noted shortcomings such as an outdated pool and lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 5.1/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money, with some finding the prices high compared to the services provided and issues with billing and minibar charges.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and commended the hotel for its service and location.
