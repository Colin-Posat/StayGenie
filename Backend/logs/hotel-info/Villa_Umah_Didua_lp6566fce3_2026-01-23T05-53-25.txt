HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Private PoolVilla Umah Didua, located just 1 km from Lovina Beach, offers a luxurious stay with air-conditioned rooms featuring balconies and pool views. The private pool, sun terrace, and lush garden provide a serene atmosphere for relaxation. Enjoy the convenience of a 24-hour front desk, free WiFi, and well-equipped kitchens in some units.Family-Friendly Amenities and ActivitiesWith a kids' pool, bicycle and car rental services, and exciting cycling and hiking opportunities nearby, Villa Umah Didua caters to families and adventure enthusiasts alike. Packed lunches for outings and various wellness packages ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for all guests.Convenient Location and ServicesExplore Ganesha Beach and Agung Beach within a few kilometers and take advantage of the paid airport shuttle service for easy transportation. Indulge in the tranquility and top-notch services at Villa Umah Didua for a memorable getaway.
