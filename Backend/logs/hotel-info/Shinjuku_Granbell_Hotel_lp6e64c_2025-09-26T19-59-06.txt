HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern and Stylish AccommodationGranbell Hotel, located in the vibrant Kabuki-cho area, offers uniquely designed guest rooms with a touch of urban sophistication. Each room is equipped with modern amenities including an iPod dock speaker and Simmons beds, ensuring a comfortable stay.Convenient Facilities and ServicesGuests can enjoy free WiFi, a terrace bar with a stunning view, and a 24-hour front desk for any assistance. The hotel also provides a coin launderette, luggage storage, and on-site parking for added convenience.Dining with a ViewIndulge in casual French and Italian cuisine at the modern-style restaurant or savor drinks at the rooftop bar while overlooking the bustling city of Shinjuku. The ground floor cafe offers a relaxing atmosphere surrounded by greenery.Book your stay at Granbell Hotel for a memorable experience in Shinjuku!
