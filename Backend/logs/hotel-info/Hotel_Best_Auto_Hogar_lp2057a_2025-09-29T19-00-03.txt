HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated opposite Parallel Metro Station and a short walk from the Ramblas, Hotel Auto Hogar offers easy access to Barcelona's top attractions.Comfortable AccommodationEnjoy air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities including flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms with hairdryers, and vanity mirrors for your convenience.Dining and ServicesIndulge in Mediterranean cuisine at the hotel's restaurant open daily, or grab a coffee at the on-site café. Take advantage of free newspapers at the reception and explore the city with bicycle or car hire services.Book your stay at Hotel Auto Hogar for a memorable Barcelona experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
For reservations for more than 10 persons, or more than 7 nights, a prepayment is required. Once the reservation has been made a representative will contact the guest to arrange this prepayment.

Please note, when booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.﻿

Extra beds for guests over 12 years of age are available upon request and carry a charge of EUR 30.

Please note that air conditioning is available only from April to October.

Please note that when booking a rate where payment is due before arrival, the property will send detailed payment instructions and a link to a secure payment platform.

Keep in mind if the reservation is for more than 10 people, special conditions and extra charges may apply

Payment is requested upon arrival, in pay at the property reservations.

Other services that can be requested and are subject to availability include early check-in, late check-out, gluten-free breakfast, breakfast bag (for early check out), and cribs. These services may have an additional cost.
