HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm in Central Barcelona Hotel España, located in a 19th-century building, offers a unique stay in the heart of Barcelona. Culinary Delights by Martín Berasategui Enjoy exquisite Mediterranean cuisine at the renowned chef's restaurant. Relaxation and Convenience Unwind at the rooftop terrace bar with a drink, take a dip in the small swimming pool, and explore the city with ease from the nearby metro station. Book your stay at Hotel España for a memorable Barcelona experience.
