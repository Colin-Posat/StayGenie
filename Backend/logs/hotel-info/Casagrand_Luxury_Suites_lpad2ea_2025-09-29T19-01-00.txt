HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Apartments with Rooftop Pool and SaunaSoundproofed Comfort and AmenitiesThe Casagrand Luxury Suites offers soundproofed apartments with a pillow menu, turndown service, flat-screen TVs, and luxurious Molton Brown toiletries. Some apartments even feature a private terrace for a more exclusive experience.Convenient Facilities and ServicesGuests can enjoy free WiFi, access to a rooftop terrace with a pool and sauna, and a 24-hour gym on-site. The attentive staff at the reception is always ready to assist with services and provide recommendations to enhance your stay.Prime Location for ExploringLocated just a 5-minute walk from Paseo de Gracia and near Gaudi’s La Pedrera, the hotel is surrounded by restaurants, cafes, and bars on Rambla de Catalunya Boulevard. With easy access to Diagonal Metro Station, exploring Sagrada Familia and other attractions is a breeze.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at Casagrand Luxury Suites.
