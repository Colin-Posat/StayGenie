HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Private Garden and En Suite RoomsFrance Eiffel, just a short stroll from the iconic Eiffel Tower, boasts a private garden and cozy en suite rooms. Enjoy the comfort of individually decorated rooms with private patios and modern amenities like an LCD TV and tea/coffee facilities.Terrace Breakfast and Asian-themed LoungeStart your day with a delightful breakfast on the terrace during summer or have it brought to your room. The hotel's lounge, adorned with elegant Asian furnishings, offers a relaxing spot to unwind. Plus, benefit from 24-hour reception service and daily newspapers.Convenient Location for SightseeingWith Dupleix Metro Station nearby and Champs de Mars RER Station a stone's throw away, exploring Paris is a breeze. Direct access to famous landmarks like the Arc de Triomphe and the Sorbonne University make France Eiffel the perfect base for your Parisian adventure.
