HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Villas Surrounded by NatureImmerse yourself in the serenity of The Samaya Ubud, nestled amidst rice fields and hillsides. Unwind in the spa bathtub of your en suite bathroom, designed for relaxation and rejuvenation.Exquisite Dining and Relaxation OptionsIndulge in spa treatments at the Ayung Riverside Spa Centre or savor seafood delicacies at the wine cellar restaurant. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
People 11 years old and over stay for Rp 1,500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
