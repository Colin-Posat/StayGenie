HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience luxury and comfort at Sura Design Hotel and Suites - Boutique Class, where every moment is tailored for your pleasure.Wellness Retreat: Indulge in a range of rejuvenating onsite massages, body treatments, and facials, complemented by a soothing sauna and a seasonal outdoor pool for the ultimate relaxation experience.Gastronomic Delights: Savor authentic Turkish cuisine at Sura Kebab House or enjoy 24-hour room service for your convenience. Our buffet breakfasts and inviting bar/lounge ensure your culinary cravings are fully satisfied throughout your stay.Modern Amenities: Stay connected with complimentary wired and wireless internet access in our stylishly designed rooms, complete with minibars, LED televisions, and luxurious bathrooms featuring designer toiletries.Don't miss out on your dream getaway—book your stay at Sura Design Hotel and Suites today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* The seasonal pool will be open from May to November.
* One child 1 year old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* Contactless check-out is available.
