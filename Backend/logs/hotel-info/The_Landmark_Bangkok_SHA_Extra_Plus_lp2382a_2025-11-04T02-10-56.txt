HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location and Stunning ViewsLocated right next to Nana BTS Skytrain Station, The Landmark offers breathtaking views of Bangkok City. With a 4-storey shopping arcade and 9 dining options, convenience and luxury are at your fingertips.Luxurious Rooms and Dining ExperiencesThe modern air-conditioned rooms feature internet access, large windows, and a TV with satellite channels. Indulge in gourmet steaks at Rib Room &amp; Bar Steakhouse or savor Asian delicacies at Sui Sian. With a variety of international cuisines and 24-hour dining options, your culinary desires are sure to be satisfied.Fitness and Relaxation AmenitiesStay active in the fitness center, take a dip in the outdoor pool, or enjoy a game of squash. Afterward, unwind with a drink at Rendezvous Bar or the Huntsman Pub. The Landmark Bangkok offers the perfect blend of leisure and business facilities. Book your stay now at The Landmark Bangkok for an unforgettable experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for THB 1,300 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
