HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Stay in the Heart of ParisPrime Location: Situated between the Sorbonne and Luxembourg Gardens, The Saint-Paul Rive-Gauche offers a prime location on Paris's Left Bank, perfect for exploring the city's cultural gems.Charming Amenities: Enjoy air-conditioned, soundproofed rooms with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and free WiFi. Unwind in the shared living area by the open fireplace, adding a touch of coziness to your stay.Culinary Delights: Indulge in a buffet breakfast served in a 17th-century arched cellar, adding a historical charm to your mornings.
