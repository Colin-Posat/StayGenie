HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in the Heart of New YorkWorld-Class Amenities: Enjoy air-conditioned rooms, a fitness centre, free WiFi, and a restaurant at the 5-star Hard Rock Hotel New York.
