HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Boutique Hotel in the Heart of ParisLuxurious Rooms with a ViewThe rooms at Hôtel Delavigne offer a unique blend of modern decor and classic furnishings, providing a cozy and elegant atmosphere for guests. With soundproofing for a peaceful stay, guests can enjoy stunning views of central Paris from the upper floors, adding a touch of magic to their experience.Delicious Breakfast and Room ServiceStart your day right with a delicious breakfast served under beautiful stone arches in the breakfast area. For those who prefer to relax in their rooms, room service is available throughout the day, ensuring a convenient and indulgent stay.Prime Location for ExploringConveniently located in the Latin Quarter, Hôtel Delavigne is within walking distance of iconic landmarks such as Notre Dame and the Louvre. With Odeon Metro station nearby, guests have easy access to the city's treasures. Book now for an unforgettable Parisian getaway at Hôtel Delavigne.
