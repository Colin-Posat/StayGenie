HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the charm of a typical Haussmannian building with modern, air-conditioned rooms featuring free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and complimentary minibar. Enjoy daily room service and soundproofed comfort for a restful stay.Gourmet Dining OptionsIndulge in a daily buffet breakfast in a charming stone cellar or opt for a personalized à la carte breakfast delivered to your room. Unwind in the evening with complimentary non-alcoholic beverages and snacks in the hotel lobby, creating a memorable dining experience.Convenient LocationLocated just steps away from Saint-Georges Metro Station, explore Paris with ease and visit iconic landmarks like Montmartre and the Champs Elysées. With a 24-hour reception and concierge service, your Parisian adventure awaits at Lorette Opéra.
