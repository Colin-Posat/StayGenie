IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hôtel Métropole Monte-Carlo in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please note that the extra bed rate does not include the breakfast. It will be charged on top of the extra bed cost if requested. The health and safety of our guests and employees is our top priority.

We have obtained the MONACO SAFE certification, a true mark of safety and confidence in the Principality with the implementation of the necessary health protection measures to make the most of your stay with us.

Please note that the credit card used for reservation must be presented upon check-in. Lunch or dinner at the bar costs on average 100 € per person. Please note that your credit card will be pre-authorized at time of booking as a guarantee.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 8 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 42 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 207 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 13 years old to 16 years old stay for € 150 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
