Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Clean rooms were appreciated by guests, but the issue of poisonous gases in the pool affected the overall perception.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Staff hospitality and check-in process received positive feedback, but lack of room service was a concern for guests.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its convenience and proximity to key areas, with some noise complaints from freeway-facing rooms.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Comfortable beds and spacious rooms were highlighted, although issues with thermostat and surprise deposit impacted guest experience.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests enjoying the breakfast and others expressing dissatisfaction with pool and hot tub conditions.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Perceived value varied among guests, with concerns raised about the high price point for some services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast quality, but suggestions for more variety were noted by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - A majority of guests had a positive overall experience, despite specific issues with amenities and room quality.
