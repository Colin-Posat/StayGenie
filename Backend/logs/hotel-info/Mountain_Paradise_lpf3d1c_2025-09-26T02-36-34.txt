WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for CHF 70 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for CHF 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for CHF 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Guests enjoyed spacious rooms with great views, although some mentioned maintenance issues like loose toilet seats.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the spa and sauna, but some guests noted areas for improvement.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some finding it reasonable for the location, while others felt it was overpriced for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant offerings, though some felt there could be more variety and freshness.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing friendly staff and stunning views.
