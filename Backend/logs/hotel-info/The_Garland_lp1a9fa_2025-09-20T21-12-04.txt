CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 9/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a highlight, describing the rooms as spotless clean.
• Service: 8.8/10 - Service was highly praised by guests, with staff being friendly and knowledgeable, and the concierge being exceptional.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location was appreciated for its proximity to key landmarks like Universal Studios and Hollywood, although some mentioned homeless people around the area.
• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Rooms were described as nice, comfortable, and rustic in some ways, with very comfortable beds.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While the pool was praised for being clean and family-friendly, some guests noted the lack of certain amenities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some finding the price reasonable for the location and services, while others felt it was a bit high.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast was highly rated, but some guests had negative experiences with dinner at the restaurant.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Overall, guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel, highlighting the shuttle to Universal Studios and nighttime movie by the pool.
