HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 11 years old stay for US$30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel is conveniently located near the airport, making it easy for travelers. Close to key places and has a good shuttle service.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Rooms were comfortable and clean, with some guests mentioning the need for better noise insulation.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests appreciating the shuttle service and pool bar.
• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - Mixed feelings among guests about the value for money, with some finding it expensive while others considered it reasonable for the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Breakfast and dinner services were well-received, though some guests desired more variety and quicker service.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others. Friendly and helpful staff stood out.
