HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesBoasting a boomerang-shaped pool and an on-site restaurant, the Avalon Hotel Beverly Hills offers a unique and upscale experience for guests. Each spacious room is elegantly decorated and equipped with modern amenities such as a minibar, flat-screen TV, and an iPod docking station. The hotel also provides tablets for guests' convenience, ensuring a comfortable stay in a smoke-free environment.Exquisite Dining ExperienceViviane, the hotel's elegant eatery, offers a sophisticated dining experience by the pool. Guests can enjoy indoor or outdoor seating surrounded by vintage furniture from the 1950s &amp; 1960s. The menu features a blend of European and American cuisine, complemented by a curated wine list and seasonal cocktails inspired by the 1950s.Fitness and Wellness OptionsFor health-conscious travelers, the Avalon Hotel provides a fitness center with top-of-the-line equipment and the option for personalized training sessions. Guests can also rent bikes to explore the area or participate in poolside yoga sessions by appointment. Whether you seek relaxation or activity, this boutique hotel caters to all your needs.To experience the epitome of luxury and relaxation in Beverly Hills, book your stay at the Avalon Hotel Beverly Hills today.

INTERNET:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
