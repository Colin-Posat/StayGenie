HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationMagic Village Views Trademark Collection by Wyndham offers air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities like a coffee machine, dishwasher, and flat-screen TV. Some units even boast garden views and a private patio for a relaxing stay.Fantastic FacilitiesGuests can enjoy a fitness center, hot tub, and tennis courts during their stay. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.

Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Opinions were divided on the value for money offered by the hotel, with some guests feeling the prices were high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant offerings, although some suggested improvements in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others, highlighting the enjoyable stay.
