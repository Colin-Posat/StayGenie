HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic CharmOnce the residence of an aristocratic Venetian family, Hotel Pausania offers a unique and charming experience. Dating back to the 14th century, this property seamlessly blends history with modern comfort.Elegant InteriorsThe bright and spacious sitting room upstairs invites you to unwind in style. Connect to the internet for free on the ground floor, ensuring you stay connected during your stay.Unique ExperiencesIndulge in the complimentary offerings such as free WiFi, a TV, and air conditioning in each room. Explore the garden with its original well, a perfect spot to unwind after a day exploring Venice. Your stay also includes a free entrance to the Venice Casino and a tour of a glass factory in Murano.Experience the allure of Venetian history at Hotel Pausania. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Venice.
