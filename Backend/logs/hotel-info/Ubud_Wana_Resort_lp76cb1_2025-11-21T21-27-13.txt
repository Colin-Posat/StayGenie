HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 1 and older are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 1 year old to 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 400,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
