HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Modern AmenitiesThe Grande Centre Point Hotel Ratchadamri Bangkok offers luxurious rooms and suites with modern décor and top-notch facilities. From flat-screen TVs to free WiFi access, guests can enjoy a comfortable stay with all the amenities they need.Convenient Location for Exploring BangkokSituated just a 5-minute walk from Ratchadamri BTS Skytrain Station, this hotel provides easy access to key attractions in central Bangkok. Whether it's shopping at nearby malls or exploring cultural sites, guests can conveniently navigate the city from this prime location.Relaxation and Recreation OptionsGuests can unwind at the outdoor pool, spa, or fitness centre, offering a perfect balance between relaxation and recreation. The sauna provides additional relaxation, while the on-site tour desk helps in planning exciting day trips.Experience luxury and convenience at Grande Centre Point Hotel Ratchadamri Bangkok. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
A damage deposit of THB 2000 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that any type of extra bed or baby cot is upon request and needs to be confirmed by the hotel.

The refundable deposit THB 1,000 per room per night will be charged upon check-in.

Please present the same credit card used to guarantee your booking when checking-in at the hotel.
Cardholder name must also match with guest’s identification document or guest may be asked to pay with alternative method.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 3 years old and over stay for THB 2,100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
