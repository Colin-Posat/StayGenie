HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Safari Lodge StyleOverlooking Nairobi National Park, Ole-Sereni boasts a unique safari lodge ambiance with vibrant colors, wooden accents, and indigenous artwork, creating an unforgettable stay.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's restaurants, offering a range of cuisines from grills to Italian, Indian, and Oriental dishes. Enjoy wildlife sightings from the main restaurant's deck.Relaxation and ConvenienceUnwind in the outdoor pool, health club with massage facilities, sauna, and steam rooms. The contemporary rooms feature modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and free WiFi, ensuring a comfortable stay.Ready to experience luxury amidst nature? Book your stay at Ole-Sereni Hotel today!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
