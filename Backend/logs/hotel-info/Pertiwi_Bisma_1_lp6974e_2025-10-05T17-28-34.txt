HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Serene Oasis with Stunning ViewsNestled amidst lush rice fields and swaying palm trees, Pertiwi Bisma 1 offers a tranquil escape overlooking the picturesque Campuhan Valley.Luxurious Accommodations and RelaxationIndulge in spacious rooms with private terraces, modern amenities, and en suite bathrooms with shower and bath facilities. Unwind by the 2 outdoor pools, enjoy massage services, and stay connected with free WiFi.Exceptional Dining and Convenient ServicesSavor Indonesian and Western cuisines at Bisma Resto or opt for in-room dining. Let the 24-hour front desk arrange day trips, cycling activities, car rentals, and laundry services. Complimentary shuttle service to Ubud Market adds to the convenience.Book your stay at Pertiwi Bisma 1 for a perfect blend of luxury and nature retreat.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for Rp 350,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
