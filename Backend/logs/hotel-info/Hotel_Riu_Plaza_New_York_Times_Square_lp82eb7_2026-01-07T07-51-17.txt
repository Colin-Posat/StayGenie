• Value for Money: 7.92/10 - Value considered poor due to high prices and the quality perceived by guests.
• Food and Beverage: 9.88/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast variety and quality, though busy at peak times.
• Overall Experience: 8.93/10 - Overall experience was mixed due to cleanliness and service issues, but location and breakfast positively noted.
