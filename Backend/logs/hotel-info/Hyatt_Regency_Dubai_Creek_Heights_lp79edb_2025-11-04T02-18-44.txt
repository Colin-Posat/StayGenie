HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and AmenitiesHyatt Regency Dubai Creek Heights offers sleek interiors, an outdoor swimming pool, 24-hour gym, a luxurious spa, and a kids’ club. Each guestroom provides stunning views, individually-controlled air conditioning, and modern facilities like a rain shower and a safety deposit box.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a wide range of dining experiences at Hyatt Regency Dubai Creek Heights. From gourmet coffee and pastries at market24 to quirky Mexican cuisine at La Tablita, there's something for every palate. Enjoy all-day dining at Sufra or sip on cocktails at Eve Penthouse &amp; Lounge with breathtaking city views.Convenient Location and Additional AmenitiesSituated near Dubai Creek, the hotel is a short drive from iconic landmarks like Burj Khalifa and Dubai Mall. With free WiFi, a beauty salon, free parking, and easy access to nearby attractions, your stay at Hyatt Regency Dubai Creek Heights promises both luxury and convenience. Book now for an unforgettable experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for AED 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
