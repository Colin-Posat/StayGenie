HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unforgettable Views of Ancient RomeHotel Colosseum, nestled in a quiet city center street, boasts a captivating roof terrace overlooking ancient Rome. The proximity of the Coliseum and Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica adds to the allure of this historic location.Elegant Rooms with Modern AmenitiesEach air-conditioned room at Colosseum Hotel features a satellite TV, stylish parquet floors, and a private bathroom equipped with bath or shower, hairdryer, and toiletries. The blend of comfort and sophistication ensures a delightful stay for all guests.Varied Buffet BreakfastStart your day with a delicious and diverse breakfast buffet, offering a range of options to suit every palate. The perfect way to fuel up before exploring the wonders of Rome.Book now to experience the charm and comfort of Hotel Colosseum.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 3 years old to 5 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
