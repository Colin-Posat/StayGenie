HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation Along the Amstel RiverDe L'Europe Amsterdam, built on a medieval fortress, offers a blend of classic design and modern amenities. The rooms and suites feature Carrara marble bathrooms, Apple TV, Bose sound systems, and motion detecting floor lighting. Some rooms offer balconies with stunning views of the Amstel River.Culinary Excellence and Iconic BarIndulge in fine dining at Flore with 2 Michelin Stars, savor French cuisine at brasserie Marie, and enjoy cocktails at the iconic Freddy's Bar with live piano music. All venues offer picturesque views of the Amstel River and Amsterdam's city center.Luxurious Wellness FacilitiesRelax at Le Spa by Skins Institute with organic body treatments and skincare. Enjoy the saunas, swimming pool, and 'technogym' fitness studio. Valet parking, Dutch Masters replicas, and proximity to Dam Square and the Flowermarket add to the allure of De L'Europe Amsterdam.Experience luxury at De L'Europe Amsterdam - Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 17 years old stay for € 65 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 65 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 75 per  day.

• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on pricing, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the level of service, while others found it reasonable given the prime location.
• Food and Beverage: 8.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with a few guests suggesting more variety in the food offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
