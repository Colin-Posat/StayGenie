HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Secluded Beachfront Retreat:Kelapa Lovina Beach Villa offers a tranquil escape with a stunning beachfront location surrounded by lush gardens. Guests can unwind by the infinity pool or soak in the hot tub while enjoying panoramic ocean views.Luxurious Villas with Modern Amenities:The tastefully decorated villas at Kelapa Lovina Beach Villa feature a range of modern amenities including flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and minibars. Guests can also request a DVD player with access to an extensive collection, and enjoy a marble-tiled bathroom with premium toiletries.Exceptional Service and Activities:The friendly 24-hour front desk staff can assist with organizing various activities such as sightseeing tours, diving trips, and dolphin watching tours. Additionally, guests can dine at the on-site restaurant serving a variety of cuisines or opt for room service for added convenience.Experience a beachfront paradise at Kelapa Lovina Beach Villa - book now for an unforgettable stay!
