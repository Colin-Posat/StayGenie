HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  USD 15 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness. While some guests found the rooms tidy, others reported issues like mold, dust, and unpleasant smells.
• Service: 7.6/10 - Staff friendliness was generally praised, but some guests mentioned issues with quickly vacating the room and slow WiFi.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The central location was highlighted positively by most guests, although some felt disappointed by the lack of additional services like free parking or breakfast.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the comfortable rooms and well-thought-out amenities, but there were complaints about maintenance issues and outdated facilities.
• Amenities: 6/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities. While some guests enjoyed the accommodations, others were dissatisfied with the lack of breakfast and outdated facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Varied opinions on value for money, with some feeling the price was justified by the location, while others found it too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed feedback. While breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others despite some shortcomings.
