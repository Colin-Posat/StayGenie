• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with complaints about limited variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the hotel's location and friendly staff, but concerns about room comfort and value for money were noted.
