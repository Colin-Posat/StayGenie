HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated in the heart of Esplugues de Llobregat, Hostal Lami provides easy access to both Barcelona El Prat Airport and the city center. With nearby bus and tram connections, exploring iconic sights like Plaça Espanya and Montjuïc Feria is a breeze.Comfortable AccommodationEnjoy a cozy stay in the simple yet comfortable rooms at Hostal Lami. Each room is equipped with air conditioning and a private bathroom, ensuring a relaxing retreat after a day of sightseeing.Convenient AmenitiesBenefit from the 24-hour reception and additional services such as laundry, dry cleaning, and fax facilities. Indulge in snacks from the vending machines, adding an extra touch of convenience to your stay.Book your stay at Hostal Lami now for a seamless blend of comfort and accessibility during your Barcelona adventures.
