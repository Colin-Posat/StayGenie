HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Prime LocationFairmont Singapore offers top-notch accommodation in the heart of Singapore's bustling arts and shopping district. With stunning views of the skyline and Marina Bay waterfront from private balconies, the spacious air-conditioned rooms provide a comfortable retreat for guests.Dining and Leisure Options GaloreIndulge in a culinary journey with 12 dining options to choose from, including an international buffet breakfast and a wide array of Asian favorites at Asian Market Café. Unwind at the Anti:dote bar with refreshing cocktails and tapas. For relaxation, Willow Stream Spa offers rejuvenating body treatments, gym facilities, and even afternoon tea.Convenient Amenities and ServicesConnected to Raffles City Shopping Centre and equipped with 2 outdoor pools, Fairmont Singapore ensures a convenient and enjoyable stay. With free WiFi, express laundry services, and a multilingual concierge, guests can expect nothing but the best during their visit.Book now to experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at Fairmont Singapore!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The name on the credit card used and passport provided for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property.

For bookings of more than 10 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

For children below 12 years old, a rollaway bed will be free of charge, subject to availability.

To guarantee the rollaway bed, additional charges of SGD 120++ per night will apply.

Please contact the Reservations Team for the rollaway bed arrangement. All rooms can accommodate only 1 baby cot or 1 rollaway bed due to space constraints and guest safety.

Children above 12 years old will be charged for the extra person charge.

Extra person charge is inclusive of a rollaway bed for room only rate and a rollaway bed plus daily breakfast for room with breakfast rate.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
