HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation: Hotel Gracery Kyoto Sanjo offers elegant rooms with sophisticated decor and modern amenities including air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and free WiFi access.Convenient Location: Situated just a short walk from Sanjo Train Station and near popular attractions such as Shijo Dori and Gion area, guests can easily explore the city and immerse themselves in the local culture.Exceptional Services: With 24-hour front desk luggage storage, a business corner with a PC, and coin-operated laundry facilities, guests can enjoy a comfortable and hassle-free stay at Kyoto Gracery Sanjo Hotel.Experience luxury and convenience at Hotel Gracery Kyoto Sanjo.
