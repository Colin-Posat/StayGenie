HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Escape to The Dominick, a luxurious oasis nestled in the heart of Manhattan, where comfort meets sophistication.Relaxation &amp; WellnessIndulge in the ultimate pampering experience at our full-service spa, featuring invigorating massages, rejuvenating body treatments, and refreshing facials. Unwind in our sauna or take a dip in the seasonal outdoor pool before keeping up with your fitness routine in our state-of-the-art 24-hour fitness center.Fine Dining ExperienceSavor delightful culinary creations at our on-site restaurant or enjoy the convenience of room service. Unwind with a refreshing drink from one of our three bars, including the poolside bar, perfect for leisurely afternoons.Comfort &amp; StyleStay in one of our 391 elegantly designed rooms, equipped with air conditioning, minibars, and espresso makers. Enjoy complimentary high-speed internet and indulge in luxurious baths featuring deep soaking tubs and premium toiletries.Book your stay at The Dominick today for an unforgettable New York experience!
