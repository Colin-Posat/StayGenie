HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesThis Miami hotel offers free shuttle services to the Miami International Airport, making travel a breeze for guests. Each suite comes equipped with a full kitchen for added convenience, and the outdoor pool provides a refreshing escape.Modern Comforts and EntertainmentGuests at Homewood Suites by Hilton Miami – Airport West can enjoy complimentary WiFi in every suite, along with cable TV featuring HBO® for entertainment. The on-site convenience store and snack shop cater to any cravings, while laundry facilities and a business center add to the overall comfort of your stay.Special Touches and Nearby AttractionsExperience a complimentary evening reception on select nights, complete with a light meal and beverages. Plus, with Sun Life Stadium and Zoo Miami just a short drive away, there is no shortage of entertainment options near the hotel.Book your stay at Homewood Suites by Hilton Miami – Airport West today for a perfect blend of convenience and comfort.
