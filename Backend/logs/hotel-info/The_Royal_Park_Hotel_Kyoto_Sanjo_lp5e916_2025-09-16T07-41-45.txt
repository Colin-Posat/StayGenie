HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Modern AccommodationsThe Royal Park Hotel Kyoto Sanjo offers sleek and modern rooms with free wired internet access, flat-screen TVs, and elegant decor. The private bathrooms feature spacious shower areas and bathtubs, providing the perfect retreat after a day of exploring Kyoto.Convenient LocationSituated just a 3-minute walk from Kyoto Shiyakusho-mae Subway Station and Sanjo Keihan Station, our hotel provides easy access to popular attractions such as Kyoto Imperial Palace, Nijo Castle, Gion area, and Yasaka Shrine. Whether you're here for sightseeing or business, our central location ensures you can make the most of your time in Kyoto.Exceptional Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary delight at Monsieur Ito, where our award-winning chef creates Western dishes using the finest local ingredients. After a day of sightseeing, unwind at our stylish bar with a selection of late-night drinks. For those looking to explore further, a variety of restaurants surround the hotel, offering a taste of Kyoto's vibrant food scene.Book your stay at The Royal Park Hotel Kyoto Sanjo now for a memorable experience in the heart of Kyoto.
