HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Contemporary Décor and Free WiFiHôtel Soft, located just steps away from Place de la République and Gare de l'Est Train Station, offers a modern and stylish setting with complimentary WiFi access for all guests.Convenient AmenitiesThe hotel boasts a 24-hour front desk, ensuring assistance is always available whenever needed during your stay.Prime LocationSituated close to iconic landmarks such as Pompidou Centre and La Cigale Concert Hall, Hôtel Soft provides easy access to Paris's vibrant attractions. With Paris - Orly Airport just a short distance away, your travels are made convenient.Book your stay at Hôtel Soft now for a memorable and hassle-free experience.

In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.

Payment before arrival via bank transfer is required. The property will contact you after you book to provide instructions.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
