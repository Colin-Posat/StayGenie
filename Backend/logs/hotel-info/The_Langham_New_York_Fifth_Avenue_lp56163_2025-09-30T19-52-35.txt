HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the elegance of The Langham, New York, Fifth Avenue, ideally situated in the heart of Manhattan.Luxurious AccommodationsUnwind in one of our 234 stylish guestrooms featuring plush pillowtop beds, contemporary decor, and cutting-edge amenities including LED televisions and iPod docking stations. Each room is a sanctuary, complete with designer toiletries and down comforters for your comfort.State-of-the-Art AmenitiesStay connected with complimentary wired and wireless internet access throughout the hotel. Our fitness center allows you to maintain your routine, while our business center provides convenience for professionals on the go.Culinary DelightsIndulge in delicious meals at our on-site restaurant or unwind with a drink at the bar/lounge. Don't miss our continental breakfasts, available daily to fuel your adventures in the city.Book your stay today and immerse yourself in the luxe lifestyle of New York City!
