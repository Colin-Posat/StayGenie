HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Santa Marta, a stone's throw away from Barcelona's trendy Born area, offers a convenient stay just 100 metres from França Railway Station. Barceloneta Metro station and the vibrant bars of Barceloneta are within walking distance, while the Gothic Quarter is a mere 500 metres away.Comfortable AccommodationEnjoy a comfortable stay in the hotel's simple, air-conditioned rooms equipped with TV, tiled floors, and private bathrooms for your convenience.AmenitiesIndulge in the convenience of vending machines offering a variety of drinks and snacks on-site. Additionally, a car park is located just a short 5-minute walk from the property.Book your stay at Hotel Santa Marta now for a memorable Barcelona experience.
