CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Adults  stay for € 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Service: 8.6/10 - Staff was very accommodating, friendly, and helpful, going above and beyond to make guests feel welcome.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Conveniently close to top attractions, restaurants, and public transport, making it easy for guests to explore Paris.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were comfortable, well-designed, and provided necessary amenities for a relaxing stay.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with positive mentions of the gym and pool, but some guests noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Mixed reviews regarding value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Good diversity and quality of food, but some guests suggested more variety and additional TV channels in English.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Guests had a highly positive overall experience, with many expressing a desire to return and recommending the hotel to others.
