HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated just 100 meters from Kayabacho Subway Station, Smile Hotel Nihonbashi provides easy access to various attractions in Tokyo. Tokyo Train Station and Tsukiji Fish Market are just a short drive away, while Kayabacho Station offers direct subway links to popular destinations like Roppongi and Akihabara.Comfortable AccommodationThe air-conditioned rooms at Smile Hotel Tokyo Nihonbashi are equipped with modern amenities such as free wired internet, a tea maker, and a private bathroom. Guests can relax with a cup of green tea from the electric kettle provided in the room and enjoy additional comforts like slippers and a bathtub.Relaxation and ConvenienceAfter a day of exploring the city, guests can unwind with a massage or take advantage of the coin launderette on site. For added convenience, laptops can be rented at the 24-hour front desk, ensuring a hassle-free stay at Smile Hotel Nihonbashi.Book your stay now at Smile Hotel Nihonbashi for a memorable Tokyo experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that the buffet will not be served until further notice. The property will serve a breakfast meal set alternatively.

Directions to hotel: From Exit 1 of Kayabacho Subway Station, turn around and walk straight for 197 feet. The hotel is on the right.
