Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Overall, cleanliness was rated positively, though some guests raised concerns about room cleanliness in specific reviews.
• Service: 8.1/10 - The service received positive feedback from guests, with helpful and friendly staff noted, although a few reviews mentioned waiting times.
• Location: 8.3/10 - Guests appreciated the hotel's central location near public transportation and key landmarks, making it convenient for exploring the area.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were generally described as comfortable, but some reviews highlighted small room sizes and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory, though some guests voiced concerns about limited laundry facilities and the absence of a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and dining services generally received positive feedback, although some guests wished for more variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel and expressed willingness to recommend it to others.
