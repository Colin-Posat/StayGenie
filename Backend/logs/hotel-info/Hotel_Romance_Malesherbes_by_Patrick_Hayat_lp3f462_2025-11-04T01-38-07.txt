HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsHotel Romance Malsherbes, housed in a 19th-century building, offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern comforts. Each floor's distinct pastel color scheme adds a touch of elegance to the ambiance.Luxurious AmenitiesThe spacious guest rooms are adorned with antique-style furnishings and equipped with flat-screen LCD TVs and free Wi-Fi. Select rooms feature a convenient kitchenette with a microwave and fridge. Guests can indulge in Nespresso coffee and tea available at the reception.Delightful Dining and Convenient ServicesStart your day with a delicious continental breakfast served in the vaulted cellar. Enjoy freshly-squeezed orange juice, pastries, and cold cuts. The hotel also boasts a 24-hour front desk and offers an airport shuttle service for added convenience.Ready to experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience?
