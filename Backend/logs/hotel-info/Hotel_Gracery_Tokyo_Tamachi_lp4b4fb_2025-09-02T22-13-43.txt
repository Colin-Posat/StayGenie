HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Convenient LocationHotel Gracery Tamachi offers modern accommodation just minutes away from major train stations. The hotel provides a seamless blend of comfort and convenience for all guests.Comfortable Guestrooms with Thoughtful AmenitiesEach guestroom is well-equipped with air-conditioning, heating, and a selection of beverages. The rooms also feature flat-screen TVs and en suite bathrooms, ensuring a relaxing stay for all visitors.Convenient Facilities and ServicesGuests can enjoy complimentary newspapers and beverages in the cozy lounge area. The hotel also provides free computer access and laundry services, catering to all the needs of the guests.Book your stay at Hotel Gracery Tamachi for a delightful and hassle-free experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
<Parking Information>
- An affiliated car park is available at an additional charge, and is situated an 8-minute walk away from the hotel.
- The car park is situated a 6-minute drive from the Shibaura Exit of the Route 1 Shuto Expressway.
- Reservation is not required.
- Guests must park their vehicles by 23:00, and are able to exit the car park from 07:00
- Please note that you cannot move your vehicle in and out of the car park once it is parked.
- The car park is closed on certain dates due to annual inspections. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Please note the Ladies Room is for female guests only and is situated on a ladies only floor.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, although some carpets were perceived as old.
• Service: 6.6/10 - Service received mixed reviews, ranging from helpful and attentive staff to reports of unfriendliness and disorganization.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location close to public transport and local amenities was praised for its convenience.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable with good facilities, although some guests mentioned limited space and minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Guests appreciated the provided amenities like matching pajamas and room cleanliness, though some mentioned the need for room upgrading.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was fair, while others found it expensive for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally satisfactory, though some guests desired more variety and lower prices for meals.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and were likely to recommend the hotel for its convenient location, comfortable rooms, and friendly staff.
