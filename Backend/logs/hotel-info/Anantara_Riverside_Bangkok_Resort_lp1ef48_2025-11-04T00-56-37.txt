HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover your oasis at Anantara Riverside Bangkok Resort, where luxury meets convenience in the heart of vibrant Bangkok.Relaxing Spa RetreatIndulge in rejuvenation at our full-service spa, offering a range of massages, body treatments, and facials designed to soothe your senses.Endless Recreation OptionsEnjoy outdoor tennis courts, a fully-equipped health club, and a sparkling outdoor pool, perfect for both relaxation and activity.Gourmet Dining and ServicesSavor delectable meals at The Market or delight in light bites from our coffee shop. With 24-hour room service and poolside drinks, every craving is met.Elevate your travel experience and make unforgettable memories—book your stay with us today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 4 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* Contactless check-out is available.
