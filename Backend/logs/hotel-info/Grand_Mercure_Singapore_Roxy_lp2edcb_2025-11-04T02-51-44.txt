HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Convenient LocationSituated in Katong, Grand Mercure Singapore Roxy provides a luxurious stay just 2 km from East Coast Park. The hotel boasts spacious rooms with a wide-screen LCD TV, safety deposit box, and elegant bathrooms with bathrobes.Dining Options and Leisure FacilitiesWith 4 dining options including Jia Wei Chinese Restaurant and Feast Roxy Restaurant, guests can indulge in Cantonese and local cuisine. Relax by the outdoor pool or stay active at the fitness centre.Convenient Access to Major AttractionsConveniently located near Parkway Parade Shopping Centre, the hotel offers easy access to Changi International Airport and popular attractions like the Singapore Flyer and Orchard Road.Book your stay at Grand Mercure Singapore Roxy for a perfect blend of luxury and convenience.
