IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
SLS Hotel at Beverly Hills will pre-authorize credit cards USD 300 prior to arrival for incidental charges.

The rollaway bed amenity is purely a request and cannot be guaranteed until the day of arrival. The Superior Double, Superior King , Premier Double , and Premier King will not be able to accommodate any roll-away beds.

Please note: The property offers valet parking only. They do not have a self-park option.

***Please note: The breakfast included rate offers breakfast for 2 guests only***

All outlet checks are subject to a 20% staff charge and 9.75% sales tax (per State of California) that is not included with the breakfast.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
