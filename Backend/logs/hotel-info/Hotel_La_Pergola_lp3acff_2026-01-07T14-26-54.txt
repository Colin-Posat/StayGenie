HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location in BernLocated in the heart of Bern, Hotel La Pergola offers easy access to popular attractions such as the House of Parliament Bern and Bern railway station.Comfortable AccommodationThe hotel provides cozy non-smoking rooms with modern amenities including free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and picturesque city views.
