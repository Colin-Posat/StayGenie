HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the perfect blend of traditional Japanese design and modern amenities in the spacious rooms at Capitol Hotel Tokyu. Enjoy stunning city views, a minibar, and a large private bathroom with a bathtub.Dining ExcellenceIndulge your taste buds at the hotel's three restaurants offering a variety of cuisines. From stylish Chinese dishes to authentic Japanese teppanyaki and sushi, there's something for every palate. Don't miss the elegantly served cocktails at The Capitol Bar.Convenient FacilitiesUnwind in the 14th-floor indoor pool overlooking Tokyo or pamper yourself with a massage at Carju Rajah TIADO Spa. The hotel also features a fitness centre, beauty salon, and business centre for all your needs.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at Capitol Hotel Tokyu!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note extra beds cannot be accommodated in the following room types: Deluxe King Room, Deluxe Hollywood Twin Room and Club Deluxe King Room.

Guests under 18 years old are not allowed in the swimming pool or fitness club.

Children aged 13 - 17 years and below can only use the pool area under adult supervision.

Please note that in accordance with local regulations, the hotel will be completely replacing the pool water on the following dates: December 16th and 17th, 2025.
During this period, the pool, jacuzzi, warming room, and relaxation room will be unavailable.

Please note that in accordance with the Electricity Business Act, the hotel will be conducting regular electrical equipment inspections on the following dates /times: 7th January, 2026, from 9:15 AM to 3:15 PM.
During this period, facilities and guest rooms on the 14th floor and above will be without power. In-room electricity, toilets, hot water, and other services will be unavailable.
Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
An accommodation tax per person, per night is not included in the price and must be paid at the property.
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  JPY 4000 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for ¥18,975 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
