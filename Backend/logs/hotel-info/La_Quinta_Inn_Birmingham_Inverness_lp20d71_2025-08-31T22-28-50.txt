• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Food quality received positive feedback, but there were complaints about the lack of variety and noise disruptions during dining.
• Overall Experience: 7/10 - While some guests had issues with specific aspects, many still had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel.
