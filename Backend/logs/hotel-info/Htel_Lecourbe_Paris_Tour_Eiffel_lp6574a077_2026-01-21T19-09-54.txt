HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated just a 2-minute walk from the Sevres-Lecourbe Metro station, The Originals City, Hôtel Lecourbe, Paris Tour Eiffel (Inter-Hotel) offers easy access to Paris' iconic attractions, including a quick 10-minute bus ride to the Eiffel Tower.Comfortable AccommodationGuests can relax in spacious rooms featuring modern amenities such as a radio, flat-screen TV, and en suite facilities. The hotel also provides a 24-hour reception for any assistance needed during your stay.Dining and RelaxationStart your day with a delicious breakfast served daily and savor regional cuisine at the hotel's restaurant. Unwind in the tranquil atmosphere of the flowered patio, adding a touch of serenity to your Parisian getaway.Book your stay at Hotel Lecourbe now for a memorable experience in the heart of Paris!
