HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsUnparalleled Dining ExperienceRelaxation and Wellness at Its BestExperience luxury at its finest at The Phoenician Resort, nestled at the base of Camelback Mountain. Indulge in spacious guest rooms with premium amenities and breathtaking views from private patios or balconies. Treat your taste buds to a culinary journey at 8 unique restaurants, offering everything from upscale Italian cuisine to modern American comfort food. Unwind and rejuvenate at the 22,000 square foot Centre for Well-Being Spa, or take a dip in one of the 9 swimming pools. With a championship golf course, family activities, and proximity to Old Town Scottsdale, your stay at The Phoenician Resort will be unforgettable. Book now for a truly exceptional experience.
