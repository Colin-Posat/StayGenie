HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationMonument Hotel, situated on Paseo de Gracia, offers elegantly decorated soundproofed rooms with high-tech amenities and luxurious bathrooms. Most rooms feature a balcony with stunning city views.Culinary ExcellenceIndulge in a gastronomic journey at Lasarte, the 3 Michelin-starred restaurant by chef Martin Berasategui, or savor fresh Mediterranean cuisine at Oria, the on-site bistro-style restaurant.Prime Location and HistoryConveniently located near iconic landmarks like Casa Battló and La Pedrera, the hotel is a short walk from Plaza Catalunya and Las Ramblas. Housed in Enric Batllo’s architectural gem, Monument Hotel holds Specialty Monument status.Experience luxury, fine dining, and history at Monument Hotel. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
