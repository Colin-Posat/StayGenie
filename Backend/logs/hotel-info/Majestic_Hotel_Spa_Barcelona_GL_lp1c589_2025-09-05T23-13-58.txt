HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in a Neoclassical BuildingExperience luxury at Majestic Hotel &amp; Spa Barcelona GL, housed in a neoclassical building on Passeig de Gràcia. The stylish rooms boast classic décor and modern amenities, maintaining the hotel's original façade.Gastronomic Delights and Rooftop ViewsIndulge in Catalan cuisine at SOLC Restaurant and enjoy live music at Bar del Majestic. During summer, savor cocktails at Dolce Vitae Cocktail Bar by the rooftop plunge-pool with stunning city views.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind at the spa with steam baths, a sauna, and a variety of treatments, or stay active at the fitness centre. Gaudí’s Casa Batlló and other iconic landmarks are a stone's throw away, making it a perfect choice for your Barcelona stay.To book your luxurious stay at Majestic Hotel &amp; Spa Barcelona GL, reserve now for an unforgettable experience.
