Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with specific mentions of attentive staff members like Jorge, Jonny, Victor, and Griselda.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location in close proximity to the beach and landmarks received high praise from guests for its convenience.
• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Guests enjoyed spacious rooms with comfortable beds and well-appointed bathrooms, with some minor issues reported in the oceanfront pool area.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While the amenities were satisfactory overall, some guests expressed disappointment over limited hours at the rooftop bar and issues with the oceanfront pool.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the hotel was priced too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - The food and beverage offerings received mixed reviews, with highlights such as great service at Agave but negative feedback on the pricing and quality of coffee and hot chocolate.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, particularly praising the staff's friendliness and customer service.
