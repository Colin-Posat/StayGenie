HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Great ViewsLocated just 3 blocks from the iconic cable cars and Civic Center, San Francisco European Hostel offers a convenient and picturesque stay. Enjoy stunning views of downtown from the rooftop balcony.Variety of Room Options and Common RoomChoose between dorm rooms with 4 or 6 beds or opt for a private room for more privacy. The hostel also provides a full-service kitchen and a common room equipped with a large TV and free Wi-Fi for guests to unwind and socialize.Easy Access to Popular AttractionsHop on the nearby cable car to explore Chinatown, Union Square, and the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art.
