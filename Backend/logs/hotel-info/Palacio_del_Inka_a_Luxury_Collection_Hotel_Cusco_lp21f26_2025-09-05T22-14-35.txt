HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and AmenitiesPalacio del Inka, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Cusco, offers stylish and modern rooms with heating, cable TV, and quality linen. Suites can provide oxygen and coca tea for high altitude comfort.Culinary Delights and LeisureIndulge in a buffet breakfast, local and international cuisine at the restaurant, and unwind at the bar with stunning views. Enjoy complimentary Pisco lessons and unlimited local calls during your stay.Wellness and ActivitiesTake advantage of the on-site gym, indoor swimming pool, and partnership with Venturia travel agency for city tours. Explore the hotel facilities with a guided tour and visit the nearby Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort &amp; Spa.Book now for an unforgettable experience at Palacio del Inka, Cusco!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
All minors must present their ID and be in the company of one or both parents. The hotel must comply with the LAW 30802: The purpose of this law is to establish conditions for the entry of children and adolescents in order to guarantee their protection and integrity.

When booking 9 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Please Note: Destination Fee is per night, mandatory, and is not included in the room rate.

-Daily buffet breakfast
-Unlimited local calls
-Daily coca infusion
-Pisco sour making lessons (Includes 02 pisco sours)
-Spa voucher (25 USD)
-Access to Press Reader

-Audio guide (Plaza de Armas and Cusco Cathedral)
-Walking Hotel Tour (daily at 5:00pm)
