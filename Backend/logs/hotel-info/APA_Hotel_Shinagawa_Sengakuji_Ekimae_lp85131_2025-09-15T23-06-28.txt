HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsAPA Hotel Shinagawa Sengakuji Eki-Mae offers comfortable rooms with modern amenities including free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms with complimentary toiletries.Relaxation and WellnessGuests can unwind in the public bath and open-air bath, or indulge in a massage service at the property. The hotel also provides a 24-hour front desk, ensuring convenience and assistance at any time.Dining ExperienceEnjoy a delicious Japanese and Western-style breakfast buffet at the hotel restaurant, or venture out to explore the diverse culinary options in the vibrant Azabu-Juban area. Whether it's lunch buffets or dinner courses, the dining choices are plentiful.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at APA Hotel Shinagawa Sengakuji Eki-Mae.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
Please inform APA Hotel Shinagawa Sengakuji Ekimae in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
When booking for 15 guests or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Please note, breakfast can be purchased on site for an additional fee. Please contact the property directly for details.

• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its convenience, accessibility to city attractions, and proximity to transportation hubs.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Guests generally found the rooms comfortable and well-maintained, with occasional complaints about room size and lack of storage space.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory, with mentions of the public bath (onsen) as a positive feature, but some guests noted limitations in laundry facilities.
• Value for Money: 7.9/10 - Perceived value for money varied among guests, with some considering the hotel a good deal, while others questioned the pricing in relation to the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast options were generally appreciated, while some guests desired more variety and better cleaning standards in the rooms.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, noting the hotel's convenient location, cleanliness, and helpful staff as key highlights.
