HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to our vibrant hostel in the heart of Amsterdam's Red Light District, just minutes away from Dam Square and Amsterdam Central Station.Convenient Amenities: Enjoy free Wi-Fi in every room and the convenience of a 24-hour reception for all your needs.Explore Nearby Attractions: Take a leisurely 15-minute walk to the iconic Anne Frank House or hop on the Dam Square tram stop for easy access to Museumplein and the charming South of Amsterdam.Book your stay now and immerse yourself in the bustling energy of Amsterdam!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of EUR 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
The breakfast room is closed in November and December due to renovation works. For this reason, the breakfast services can temporary not be booked in this period. For guest who have already booked breakfast, a solution will be provided.

Renovation work is done daily in the accommodation and some rooms may be affected by noise. Please note that there is no elevator in the hostel and that the floors are accessible via stairs. A pick-up/delivery parking service is provided by a corporate company in their private parking garage.

No reservation is needed and the cost is 45 EUR per day (5 EUR additional fee for only 1 day parking request).

Please note that for non-refundable reservations we do not accept payments on arrival. The total amount of the booking (including vat/tax/city tax) will be charged directly on the credit card. In case of cancellation/modification, this charge will not be refunded.

When booking for 20 people or more or 4 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. The group booking policies will be communicated separately. If the group booking terms are not accepted and confirmed by guest, the booking may be cancelled by us.

For private rooms you may be asked a €50 euro deposit on arrival.

For rooms consisting of 6 or more beds, you may be asked a €100 euro deposit on arrival. For beds in shared dorms, a €20 deposit to have towel, room key and locker key is required.
