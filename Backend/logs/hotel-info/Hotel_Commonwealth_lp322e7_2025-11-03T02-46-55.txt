HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel Commonwealth, conveniently located near Fenway Park and Boston University, offers guest rooms with modern amenities including a '48-inch' flat-screen smart TV and in-room spa services. Each room features a small refrigerator, a cozy sitting area, and a work desk for your convenience.Culinary DelightsIndulge in fine dining at Blue Ribbon Sushi or savor fresh seafood at Pescador Coastal Grill. For a late-night treat, visit TsuruTonTan Udon Noodle Brasserie. The hotel offers a diverse culinary experience to satisfy every palate.Convenience and ComfortWith a 24-hour fitness center, award-winning concierge service, and rooms with stunning views of Fenway Park or Kenmore Square, Hotel Commonwealth ensures a comfortable and convenient stay. Explore nearby attractions like Massachusetts Institute of Technology and the Hynes Convention Center.Experience luxury and convenience at Hotel Commonwealth. Book your stay now!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
