HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Alpine RetreatLocated in Zermatt, The Alpenhotel Fleurs de Zermatt is a haven of tranquility. The hotel, renovated in 2013, boasts a wellness area with a hot tub and sauna, perfect for relaxation.Elegant Rooms with Stunning ViewsThe bright Alpine-style rooms are furnished with modern amenities like cable TV and Nespresso machines. Guests can enjoy breathtaking Matterhorn views from the south-facing rooms with balconies.Convenient Location and Exceptional ServiceWith a short walk to the train and funicular stations, exploring Zermatt is a breeze. Indulge in a delicious buffet breakfast each morning before heading out.Experience the beauty of Zermatt at The Alpenhotel Fleurs de Zermatt and book your stay today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Alpenhotel Fleurs de Zermatt in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Payment before arrival via bank transfer is required. The property will contact you after you book to provide instructions.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Zermatt is a car-free resort. You have to park your car in Täsch and proceed by train or taxi. During winter it is recommended to reserve a parking space in Täsch well in advance.

Please note that the submitted credit card will be used for the deposit and has to be presented upon check-in.

Please contact the property if you require any extra beds.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for CHF 40 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for CHF 40 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 10 years old stay for CHF 60 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 11 years old to 15 years old stay for CHF 80 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 10 years old stay for CHF 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Children from 11 years old to 15 years old stay for CHF 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 16 years old and over stay for CHF 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.
