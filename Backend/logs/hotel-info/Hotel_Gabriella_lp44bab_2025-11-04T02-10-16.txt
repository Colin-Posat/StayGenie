HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Newly Renovated RoomsHotel Gabriella offers newly renovated rooms that are not only comfortable but also well-appointed, ensuring a relaxing stay for all guests.Convenient LocationLocated in a central district within walking distance of Termini Metro Station, Hotel Gabriella provides easy access to famous landmarks like the Colosseum and the Spanish Steps. Additionally, Termini Train Station, with links to Fiumicino Airport, is just a 10-minute walk away.Additional ServicesGuests can conveniently book tours of the town and surrounding areas directly at the reception. For added convenience, the hotel offers a shuttle service to and from the city airports at an extra cost.Experience the charm and convenience of Hotel Gabriella - book your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Full payment is due on check-in. Please be aware that reservations cannot be modified at check-in. In addition, debit cards cannot be accepted to guarantee the reservation.

A surcharge of EUR 30 applies for arrivals after 00:00. All requests for late arrival are subject to confirmation by the property.

If you are booking a prepaid rate and require an invoice, please include your company details in the Special Requests box when booking.

Please note, air conditioning in the guest rooms is only available from June to September.

• Value for Money: 6/10 - Mixed opinions on the price point, with some feeling the basic facilities did not justify the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 4/10 - A lack of variety and issues with cleanliness in the breakfast area led to a lower rating.
• Overall Experience: 4/10 - The negative aspects of cleanliness, room quality, and amenities heavily impacted guests' overall experience, resulting in a low rating.
