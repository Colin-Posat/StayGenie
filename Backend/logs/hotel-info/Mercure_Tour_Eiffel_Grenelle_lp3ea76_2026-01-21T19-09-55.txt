HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Eiffel TowerSituated in Paris’ 15th district, Mercure Tour Eiffel Grenelle is a mere 10 minutes’ walk from the iconic Eiffel Tower. Enjoy stunning views and convenient access to Paris' most famous landmark.Modern Amenities for a Comfortable StayRelax in air-conditioned rooms equipped with a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and free WiFi. Unwind in the fitness room or sauna, and indulge in a delicious buffet breakfast each morning.Convenient Access to Paris AttractionsWith Dupleix Metro station only 30 meters away, explore Paris effortlessly. Visit nearby attractions like the Montparnasse Tower, Galeries Lafayette, and the Arc de Triomphe. Book your stay at Mercure Tour Eiffel Grenelle for an unforgettable Parisian experience.
