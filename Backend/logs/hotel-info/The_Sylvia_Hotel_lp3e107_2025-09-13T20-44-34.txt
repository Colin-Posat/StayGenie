HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Ocean Views and Convenient LocationOceanfront Dining and Comfortable AccommodationsThe Sylvia Hotel offers a unique blend of comfort and convenience with its on-site restaurant and lounge overlooking Vancouver's English Bay. Guests can enjoy delicious meals while taking in the stunning ocean views. The bright and cozy guestrooms, some with full kitchens and sofa beds, provide a comfortable stay for visitors.Meeting Facilities and Outdoor ActivitiesFor business travelers, the hotel offers a meeting room with hosting facilities. Additionally, guests can take advantage of the hotel's prime location by engaging in outdoor activities like cycling, walking, or rollerblading along the nearby seawall. With Stanley Park just a short walk away, guests have easy access to nature and recreation.Book your stay at The Sylvia Hotel now to experience the perfect combination of relaxation and adventure by the ocean.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  CAD 25 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for CAD 5 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for CAD 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.
