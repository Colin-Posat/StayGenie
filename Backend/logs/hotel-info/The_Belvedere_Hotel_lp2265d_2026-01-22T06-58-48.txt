HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location in the Theater District and Times SquareSituated in the bustling Theater District and Times Square, The Belvedere Hotel offers a prime location less than 1 km from Radio City Music Hall and a short 10-minute stroll from Broadway Theatre. With free WiFi available throughout the property, stay connected during your visit.Modern Amenities for a Comfortable StayEach unit at the hotel features a flat-screen TV, work desk, microwave, and refrigerator, ensuring a convenient and comfortable stay. Indulge in Brazilian cuisine at the on-site restaurant or enjoy daily morning coffee in the Hudson Lobby from 7am to 10am.Fantastic Facilities and ServicesTake advantage of the hotel's fitness centre, business centre, and complimentary unlimited local calls. Additionally, benefit from package receiving services and 24-hour tips and assistance from the front desk staff.Ready to experience the best of New York City? Book your stay at The Belvedere Hotel now for an unforgettable trip!
