HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the ultimate retreat at DoubleTree by Hilton Dubai - Jumeirah Beach, where luxury meets beachfront relaxation.Indulge in RelaxationPamper yourself at our exquisite spa, offering rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials. Dive into bliss with not one, but two stunning outdoor pools, and enjoy exclusive access to a pristine private beach.Culinary DelightsSavor delectable dishes at Gastro Kitchen, one of our two gourmet restaurants. Whether you opt for 24-hour room service or a quick snack at our café, you can unwind at our vibrant bar or beach bar, perfect for enjoying the sunset with your favorite drink.Business &amp; ComfortOur hotel features 10 versatile meeting rooms, alongside a fully-equipped business center. Kick back in one of our 109 air-conditioned rooms, each with a private balcony, LCD TV, and complimentary Wi-Fi, ensuring a comfortable stay.Elevate your stay and immerse yourself in luxury—book your escape now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.

* Contactless check-out is available.
