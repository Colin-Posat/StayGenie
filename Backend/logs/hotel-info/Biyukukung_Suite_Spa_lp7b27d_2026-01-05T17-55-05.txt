HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in Ubud EstateExperience the serene beauty of Ubud at Biyukukung Suites and Spa, just a short walk from Monkey Forest. Enjoy stunning views of Balinese rice fields from our cosy rooms featuring modern amenities.Relaxation and RecreationIndulge in pampering spa treatments, take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool, or explore the area on a rented bicycle. Free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and tea/coffee facilities ensure a comfortable stay.Culinary DelightsSavor a variety of American, Continental, and Indonesian dishes at our two restaurants. Don't miss the daily breakfast and afternoon tea offerings.Book now to experience luxury and tranquility at Biyukukung Suites and Spa.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that the property requires a deposit payment. Staff will contact guests directly for payment instructions.

In celebration of Nyepi Day (Day of Silence), Ngurah Rai International Airport will be closed, motor vehicles are allowed on the streets only for emergencies, guests are confined to the grounds of the resort.

Room service is available with the last order taken at 22:30. The property has 2 restaurants on-site that serve lunch and dinner, as well as afternoon tea from 15:00 to 17:00.Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
