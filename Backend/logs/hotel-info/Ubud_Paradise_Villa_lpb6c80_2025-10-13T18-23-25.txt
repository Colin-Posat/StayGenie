HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Private Pool Villa in UbudLocated in the heart of Ubud, Ubud Paradise Villa offers a serene escape with a private pool surrounded by lush tropical gardens. Enjoy free WiFi, secured parking, and a complimentary shuttle to central Ubud.Elegant Accommodation with Modern AmenitiesIndulge in spacious rooms with a seating area, flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen. Relax in the semi-open private bathrooms with free toiletries and stunning views. Daily breakfast is included for a perfect start to your day.Relaxing Garden Oasis with Convenient ServicesUnwind in the garden, on the terrace, or in the shared kitchen. Take advantage of car hire, bike rental, and activities like cycling and hiking. Let our staff assist you with massages, grocery deliveries, and more.Experience luxury and tranquility at Ubud Paradise Villa - Book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 10 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 13 years old stay for Rp 250,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
