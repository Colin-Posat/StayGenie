Category Ratings:

• Service: 9.33/10 - Service was praised highly with friendly staff and good attentiveness in many situations, though certain delays at the bar were noted.
• Location: 9.37/10 - The hotel location is excellent, near the beach and attractions, contributing positively to the overall experience.
• Room Quality: 7.82/10 - Rooms are generally clean but mentioned as small and somewhat dated in decor, impacting overall expectations of luxury.
• Amenities: 8.85/10 - Amenities such as the pool and on-site cafe were well received, but some limitations on pool heating were noted.
• Value for Money: 8.31/10 - Some reviews suggest that for the price, expectations weren't fully met, particularly regarding food and extra charges.
• Food and Beverage: 7.86/10 - Mixed feelings about restaurant quality and high prices; some meals were not enjoyable compared to value.
• Overall Experience: 8.93/10 - While there were several issues, the overall sentiment remained positive primarily due to staff and location.
