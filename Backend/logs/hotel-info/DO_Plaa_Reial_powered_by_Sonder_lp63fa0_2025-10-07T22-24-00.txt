• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests appreciated the spacious and well-decorated rooms, although there were occasional complaints about noise levels.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests enjoying the offerings like the gym and pool, while others noted shortcomings such as the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location, while others found it to be on the higher side.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Reviews on food and beverage varied, with positive comments on breakfast and restaurant services, but also suggestions for improvement in variety and included services.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
