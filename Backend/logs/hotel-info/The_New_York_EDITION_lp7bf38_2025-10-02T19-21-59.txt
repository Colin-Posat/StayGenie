HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spectacular City Views and Luxury AmenitiesAt The New York EDITION, enjoy breathtaking city views from oversized windows in every room, complemented by photography by Melvin Sokolsky. Relax in style with a 48” flat-screen TV and indulge in custom Le Labo bath amenities in your private bathroom.Award-Winning Dining ExperienceTreat your taste buds at The Clocktower restaurant, known for its updated classics and elegant ambiance. Explore three uniquely-designed dining rooms, a billiards room, and a bar adorned with gold leaf, all located on the hotel’s landmarked second floor.Chic Bar and Convenient LocationUnwind with bespoke cocktails at the Lobby Bar, where you can also savor breakfast, lunch, and light dinner options. With iconic attractions like Eataly and Dover Street Market just steps away, The New York EDITION offers the perfect blend of luxury and convenience.Experience luxury living at The New York EDITION - Book now for an unforgettable stay!
