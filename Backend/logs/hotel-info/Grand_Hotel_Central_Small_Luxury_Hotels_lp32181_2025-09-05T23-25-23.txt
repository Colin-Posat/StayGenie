GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Comfortable rooms
• Friendly staff
• Lovely rooftop bar and pool

Areas for Improvement:
• Slow service at times
• Limited amenities like spa services
• Mixed perceptions on value for money

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms and facilities, with some minor complaints about pool area cleanliness.
• Service: 7.7/10 - While most staff were friendly and accommodating, there were a few incidents of slow service and lack of proactive problem-solving.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's central location and proximity to key landmarks were highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Guests found the rooms to be comfortable and well-equipped, with a few mentions of room size and outdated decor as minor issues.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The hotel's amenities, including the rooftop bar and pool, were generally appreciated, but some guests expressed a desire for more facilities like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on the perceived value for money, with some guests feeling the prices were high relative to the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Guests enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, but there were suggestions for more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - The overall experience was positive for most guests, with high ratings for location, rooms, and service.
