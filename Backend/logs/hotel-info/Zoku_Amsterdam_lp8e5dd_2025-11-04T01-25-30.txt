HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Loft Living ExperienceZoku Amsterdam offers 133 Lofts designed for professionals and remote workers, featuring a lofted bed, living room, kitchen, and a 4-person table. The spacious Zoku Loft is perfect for living and working, allowing you to host guests and collaborate seamlessly.Vibrant Social SpacesExperience the vibrant rooftop Social Spaces including a Living Room, Kindred Spirits Bar, and a Living Kitchen restaurant. Whether you need a coworking space, meeting rooms, or just want to relax on the terrace, Zoku has everything to cater to your social and practical needs.Convenient Location and AmenitiesLocated near Amsterdam Central Station, Zoku provides easy access to business districts and historical sites. Enjoy personalized artwork in your Loft, high-speed Wi-Fi, and 24/7 access to shared spaces with a dedicated team to assist you.Book your stay at Zoku Amsterdam for a unique blend of work, leisure, and socializing in the heart of the city.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Please note that there is a 24-hour front desk and check-in is possible from 15:00. It is not needed to inform Zoku of your estimated arrival time.

Due to the purpose of clean rooms, Zoku Amsterdam can not provide an early check-in. Guests who arrive before 15:00 can leave their luggage in the luggage room on-site.

Please note that reservations of 5 or more rooms are only possible upon request. Different policies and supplements may apply for these reservations.

This property is cashless, valid credit card details are asked to hold on file during your stay. A valid physical credit card needs to be presented upon check in - no third party authorisation is possible.

Please note that breakfast will be served from 07:00 to 10:00 during weekdays, and will be served from 08:00 to 10:00 on weekends. After that there is brunch on weekends from 11:00 to 14:00.

The city tax will be charged upon arrival.

Cleaning service is offered every third day. Additional cleaning can be arranged at an extra fee when desired.
