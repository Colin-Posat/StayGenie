Luxurious Handmade Furniture and Natural EssencesExperience a stay like no other at Hotel Parque México Boutique, where every room is adorned with completely handmade furniture and scented with natural essences. Enjoy the comfort of a coffee machine, a kitchenette with kitchenware, and a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries.Cultural Dining Experience with Park ViewsIndulge in a culinary journey at the Centro Cultural Parque Mexico restaurant gallery and bar located on-site. Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere with live music four times a week.Exclusive Services and ToursBenefit from 24-hour front desk and concierge services that offer access to exclusive spots and organize culinary and historical tours.
