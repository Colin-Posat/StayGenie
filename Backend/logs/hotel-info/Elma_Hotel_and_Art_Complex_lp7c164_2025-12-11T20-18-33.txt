HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Hotel with Dual PoolsEscape to Elma Arts Complex Luxury Hotel in Zikhron Ya‘aqov, where you can enjoy both an indoor and outdoor pool. The indoor pool, exclusively for guests 16 and older, offers year-round relaxation, while the outdoor pool is perfect for all ages during the summer months.Upscale AmenitiesIndulge in the comfort of rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and coffee machines. Unwind at the spa and wellness center, stay active at the fitness center, and savor delicious meals at the on-site restaurant.Convenient LocationConveniently located just 7 km from a sandy beach and 65 km from Ben Gurion Airport, Elma Arts Complex Luxury Hotel offers free WiFi, parking, and luggage storage for a stress-free stay.Book now to experience luxury and relaxation at Elma Arts Complex Luxury Hotel!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Based on local tax laws, guests with a tourist visa to Israel are exempted from paying VAT for their reservation. Israeli citizens and residents ("non tourists" as defined in the VAT law) must pay VAT. This tax is automatically calculated in the total cost of the reservation for domestic customers. For guests booking from outside of Israel, the tax is not included in the total price.

Please note that children can be accommodated only in some room types. Children are not allowed in the indoor pool nor the spa. Sunday through Thursday, our indoor pool will be open for guests aged 16 and over. Please note that on Saturdays, check-in is only possible after 18:00.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
