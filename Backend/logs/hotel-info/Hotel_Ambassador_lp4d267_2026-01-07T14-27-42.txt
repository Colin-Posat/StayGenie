HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and Convenient LocationHotel Ambassador, renovated by star architect Ali Tayar, offers free underground parking, spa facilities, and air-conditioned rooms with stunning views. Located just 7 minutes from Bern's city centre, with free public transport right outside, guests can easily explore the area.Culinary Delights and Meeting SpacesIndulge in the American breakfast buffet or opt for a quick breakfast. With two unique restaurants on-site, including a Japanese Teppan-Yaki option and a panoramic international cuisine restaurant, guests can savor a variety of flavors. The hotel also boasts a bar, bistro, and 8 meeting rooms accommodating up to 250 people.Exceptional Service and Local GuidanceThe friendly staff at Hotel Ambassador ensures a warm welcome and is ready to assist with recommendations for activities and sightseeing. Immerse yourself in luxury and convenience at Hotel Ambassador for an unforgettable stay.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 4 years old to 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for CHF 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
