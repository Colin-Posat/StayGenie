HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Amenities and Renovated SpacesNovotel Istanbul Zeytinburnu, conveniently situated near the city centre, boasts recently-renovated modern meeting rooms, a stylish restaurant, and a welcoming lobby. The chic contemporary décor of the spacious rooms, all with full sea views, flat-screen TVs, and ergonomic work areas, offer a comfortable stay.Relaxation and Leisure FacilitiesGuests can unwind at the compact fitness centre, sauna, steam bath, or outdoor swimming pool at Novotel Istanbul Zeytinburnu. Whether it's staying active or indulging in relaxation, the hotel provides a variety of options for a rejuvenating experience.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentIndulge in a daily rich breakfast, Turkish and international cuisine at the elegant restaurant, and enjoy live music on select evenings. The cosy bar is perfect for snacks and drinks, offering a delightful setting for unwinding after a day of exploration.Book your stay at Novotel Istanbul Zeytinburnu for a memorable experience in Istanbul!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 0 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 16 years old stay for € 0 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
