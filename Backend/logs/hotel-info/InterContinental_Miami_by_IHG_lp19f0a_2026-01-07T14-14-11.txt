HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience luxury at its finest with accommodations featuring marble bathrooms, in-room entertainment, and modern amenities like a safety deposit box and minibar.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind at mySpa Miami with a variety of spa treatments, stay active at the 24-hour fitness center, or bask in the sun at the outdoor pool overlooking Biscayne Bay.Dining and ConvenienceWith five dining options on-site, including a Starbucks® and a Pan Latin restaurant, guests can indulge in a range of culinary delights. Plus, take advantage of the full-service business center and electric car charging station.Book now for an unforgettable stay at InterContinental Miami, where luxury meets convenience in the heart of Miami's business district.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that the guest name and credit card used at time of booking, and valid ID must match and be presented at time of check in.

Please note that charges may apply for storing guests' packages.

Please note that breakfast-included rates are for a maximum of 2 guests. Breakfast for any additional guests will incur a surcharge.

The service charge per night includes the following amenities:
- High-speed internet
- 24-hour fitness center access and complimentary attire
- Sauna, steam, drench shower, and relaxation room access
- 2 bottles of water in-room daily
- Daily access on your personal electronic to 'PressReader,' featuring more than 2,000 newspapers and magazines published in 56 languages
- Poolside refreshments and suncare
- Turndown service, upon request
- Black-and-white photocopying and printing
- In-room coffee and tea
- Luggage storage for day of check-in and check-out

Please note that there is an additional charge of USD 30.00 for any guest above 2 adults.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
