IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Please inform Mandarin Oriental, Tokyo in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A penalty fee of JPY 100,000 (including taxes) will apply if smoking is detected in the room

When travelling with pets (only dogs) advance reservations are required, an extra charge of 12,650 yen per night (tax and service charge included) 1 dog per room (15 kg or less)

Should send the pet (dog) vaccination certificate to the Accommodation Reservation Section

No pet (dog) is allowed in the lounge, bar, spa, fitness center, meeting room

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  JPY 6000 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and costs  JPY  1800 per  24 hours.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Adults  stay for ¥12,650 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

What Guests Love:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was a standout aspect, with many guests appreciating the helpful and courteous staff.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The central location near Tokyo Station was highly praised for its convenience and accessibility to key areas.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests enjoyed the spacious and well-kept rooms, although some mentioned minor improvements needed.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests highlighting positive aspects like the gym and pool, while others noted areas for enhancement such as spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some finding it slightly expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, though some felt there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - The majority of guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel, appreciating the luxury and exceptional service.
