IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform The Samara Villas & Restaurant in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), this property has reduced reception and service operating hours.
Please note that no cars are allowed within 300 metres of The Samara. Guests will have to walk in.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 2 years old to 15 years old stay for Rp 300,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Peaceful location
• Spacious rooms
• Excellent service

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited accessibility to facilities
• Issues with room setup and aging property
• Mixed reviews on value for money

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the property, although a few mentioned minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests for being friendly, helpful, and efficient.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The peaceful location in the middle of rice fields was frequently mentioned as a positive aspect, although some found it slightly inconvenient for access to facilities.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests generally found the rooms spacious and comfortable, but a few mentioned issues such as separate bedroom setups and aging property.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The amenities were generally satisfactory, with some positive comments about the jacuzzi and food quality.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling it was worth the cost and others expecting higher standards for the price.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Food and beverage services received positive feedback for quality and variety, although some guests desired improvements.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had an exceptional overall experience, appreciating the serene environment, friendly staff, and delicious food.
