HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationWalker Hotel Tribeca offers 4-star air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and city views. Each room includes modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and plush bed linens.Top-Notch FacilitiesGuests can enjoy the convenience of a fitness centre, free bikes, and multilingual staff at the reception. The property is non-smoking, ensuring a comfortable stay for all visitors.Prime LocationLocated just 600 metres from Bloomingdales and 1.5 km from the National September 11 Memorial &amp; Museum, Walker Hotel Tribeca is surrounded by popular attractions including Brooklyn Bridge and One World Trade Center.Experience luxury and convenience in the heart of New York City at Walker Hotel Tribeca.
