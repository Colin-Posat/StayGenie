HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location:Located in the historic heart of Barcelona, Hostal Girona is just a short walk from iconic landmarks such as Passeig de Gràcia and Plaça Catalunya.Modern Amenities:This small guest house offers free Wi-Fi, air-conditioned rooms with TVs, and the convenience of a lift. Whether you prefer an en suite or shared bathroom, Hostal Girona caters to your needs.Historic Charm:Housed in a 19th-century building that played a part in the design of the Eixample neighborhood, Hostal Girona seamlessly blends history with modern comfort.Experience the perfect combination of history and modernity at Hostal Girona. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Barcelona!
