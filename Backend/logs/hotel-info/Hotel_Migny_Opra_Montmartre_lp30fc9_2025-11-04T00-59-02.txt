HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Parisian CharmLocated in the heart of Paris, Migny Opéra Montmartre offers a unique experience in a typically Parisian building from the 19th century. The hotel is just a short distance from iconic landmarks such as Sacré-Coeur and the vibrant Montmartre district.Comfortable AccommodationEach air-conditioned room at Migny Opéra Montmartre is designed for comfort, featuring an en-suite bathroom, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and charming wooden flooring. Some rooms even boast a balcony or terrace, providing a perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploring the city.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can start their day with a delicious buffet breakfast and enjoy French cuisine in the comfort of their room. The hotel's 24-hour lounge bar is the ideal place to unwind in the evening, adding a touch of relaxation to your stay.Ready to experience the historic charm and convenience of Migny Opéra Montmartre? Book your stay now for an unforgettable Parisian getaway.

• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding the pricing high for the services offered, while others considered it reasonable due to the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally positive, though some guests suggested improvements in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, highlighting the hotel's charm and amenities.
