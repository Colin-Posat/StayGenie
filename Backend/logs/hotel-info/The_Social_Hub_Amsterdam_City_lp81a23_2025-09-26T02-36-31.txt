HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Design Accommodation and Convenient LocationThe Social Hub Hotel Amsterdam City offers stylish design accommodation just a short walk or subway ride away from the city centre. With the metro station right outside, exploring the city's hotspots is a breeze.Modern Amenities and Comfortable RoomsEach room at The Social Hub Hotel Amsterdam City is equipped with an en suite bathroom and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Guests can also enjoy free WiFi and complimentary use of the on-site gym.Unique Communal Spaces and Nearby AttractionsIn addition to the cozy rooms, guests can take advantage of communal areas like a library, workspaces, and a café. The hotel's proximity to Waterlooplein vintage market and the vibrant Oost district ensures there's always something exciting to do nearby.Ready to experience the vibrant city of Amsterdam?
