HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationUrban Hotel Kyoto is conveniently situated just a short walk from Fushimi Inari Shrine and Ryukokudaimae-Fukakusa Train Station, making it an ideal base for exploring Kyoto's cultural gems.Modern AmenitiesThe hotel offers renovated non-smoking rooms with free WiFi, air conditioning, heating, and essential facilities like a fridge, kettle, and TV with satellite channels. The en suite bathrooms are stocked with toiletries and a hairdryer for your convenience.Dining and FacilitiesIndulge in a delicious buffet breakfast at Cafe Restaurant Lavender, showcasing a mix of Japanese, Western, and Chinese cuisine. For dinner, savor local specialties and drinks. Additionally, guests can rent bicycles to roam the area, shop at the souvenir store, or use the on-site launderette.Book your stay at Urban Hotel Kyoto for a memorable and comfortable experience in Kyoto!
