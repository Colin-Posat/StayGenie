SPECIAL CONDITIONS FOR GROUPS:

Reservations of 6 or more rooms are subject to our group policies:
- An advance payment of 50% of the total reservation is required at the time of booking, otherwise the reservation will be canceled.
- For reservations with a flexible policy, the maximum cancellation period without charge is 20 days before arrival.
- Tips apply to bell-boys and waitresses.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
