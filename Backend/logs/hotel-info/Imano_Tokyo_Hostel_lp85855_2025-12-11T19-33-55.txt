HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Imano Tokyo Hostel: Your Comfortable Stay in ShinjukuConvenient Location: Situated in the vibrant Shinjuku area, Imano Tokyo Hostel provides dormitories, private, and family rooms. Enjoy free WiFi across the property and unwind at the Cafe and Bar.Comfortable Accommodations: Air-conditioned rooms offer privacy with curtains, bed lights, and plug outlets in dormitories. A 24-hour front desk, safety deposit box, and common bathing area with a hair dryer ensure a hassle-free stay.Additional Amenities: Benefit from a shared kitchen, laundry service, and optional amenities like toothbrushes and earplugs for a fee. Explore nearby attractions such as Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden and Meiji Shrine.Book your stay at Imano Tokyo Hostel for a memorable experience in Tokyo!
