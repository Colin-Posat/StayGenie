HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Modern AmenitiesThe Residence Inn New York Downtown Manhattan/World Trade Center Area offers contemporary suites with free WiFi access. Each suite features a flat-screen satellite TV and kitchen facilities. Many rooms provide stunning views of Downtown Manhattan.Convenient LocationSituated in the heart of the Downtown District, the hotel is steps away from iconic landmarks such as One World Observatory and the 9/11 Memorial. Guests can easily explore the vibrant neighborhood filled with bars, cafés, and restaurants.Fitness Center and Nearby AttractionsIn addition to the well-equipped suites, the hotel boasts a fitness center for guests to stay active. With its proximity to Fulton Street/Broadway-Nassau Street and Trinity Church, as well as being just a short distance from JFK International Airport, this hotel is the perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers.Book your stay now at Residence Inn New York Downtown Manhattan/World Trade Center Area for a memorable experience!
