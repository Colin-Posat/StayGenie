HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Thai Decor RoomsVabua Asotel Bangkok offers air-conditioned rooms with modern Thai decor, warm lighting, and a range of amenities including cable TV, minibar, and fridge. Some rooms even feature a DVD player and sofa for added comfort.Convenient Dining OptionsGuests can indulge in delicious Thai and international cuisine at the 24-hour Vaboir Restaurant or enjoy al fresco dining and live entertainment at the Beer Garden. In-room dining is also available with 24-hour room service for ultimate convenience.Excellent Services and FacilitiesFrom arranging day trips to currency exchange and laundry services, the hotel caters to all guest needs. With a 24-hour front desk and an airport shuttle service, Vabua Asotel Bangkok ensures a seamless and enjoyable stay for all visitors.Book your stay at Vabua Asotel Bangkok for a memorable experience in Bangkok!
