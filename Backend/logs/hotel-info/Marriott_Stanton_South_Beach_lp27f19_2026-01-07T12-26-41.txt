HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Oceanfront Miami Hotel:Experience the ultimate beachfront getaway at our luxurious Miami hotel in the heart of the vibrant South Beach Art Deco District.Infinity Edge Pool and Spa Retreat:Indulge in relaxation at our infinity edge pool or escape to the spa for a rejuvenating treatment after a day of exploration in South Beach. Unwind in the hot tub with stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean.Culinary Delights and Convenient Amenities:Savor Mexican-inspired dishes at Lolo's Surf Cantina, enjoy Japanese cuisine at Azabu Miami Beach, and grab a coffee from Starbucks. With amenities like a fitness centre, concierge desk, and launderette, we ensure a comfortable stay for all our guests.Book now for a luxurious beachfront experience at Marriott Stanton South Beach!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note a WiFi upgrade is available for an additional cost upon check-in.

Please note that breakfast is for 2 guests when booking the breakfast-included rate. Additional guests on the reservation must pay separately.

The daily resort fee of USD 42 per room per night includes the following:
- 2 beach chairs
- Bike rental
- 2 welcome drinks
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

• Value for Money: 7.65/10 - The $45 resort fee does not provide satisfactory value considering the conditions reported.
• Food and Beverage: 9.65/10 - Free gelatos and nearby restaurant options enhance the experience.
• Overall Experience: 9.15/10 - Enjoyable overall for beach access, but complaints about cleanliness and service issues.
