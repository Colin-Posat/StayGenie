HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury meets Adventure at Our Las Vegas LodgeThis newly reopened hotel, where boutique comfort meets the rustic charm of a modern lodge, is located less than 5 miles from the Las Vegas Strip and features its own 90,000-square-foot casino. Guests can enjoy thoughtfully renovated rooms with in-room Starbucks coffee service and mini-refrigerators, providing a boutique hotel ambiance for a relaxing stay.Award-Winning Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at our award-winning restaurants, including Twin Creeks Steakhouse, Sundance Grill, Mi Casa grill cantina, Su Casa sushi bar, WuHu Noodles, Mermaid Lounge, and Johnny Rockets. Don't miss the nightly early-evening wine-tasting experience at Wine Time.Unique Shopping ExperienceExplore our lodge's flagship Bass Pro Shops, located within the same building, offering a unique shopping experience for outdoor enthusiasts.Book now for an unforgettable stay at our Las Vegas lodge!
