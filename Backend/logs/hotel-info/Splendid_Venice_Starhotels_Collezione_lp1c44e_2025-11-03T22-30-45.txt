HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Splendid Venice - Starhotels Collezione offers elegant air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities, including satellite TV, a minibar, and free WiFi. Some rooms even boast stunning canal views, providing a true Venetian experience.Gourmet DiningIndulge in a daily buffet breakfast and savor contemporary and traditional Venetian dishes at the Le Maschere restaurant. For a special experience, head to the Altana rooftop terrace for drinks while enjoying panoramic views of Venice.Convenient LocationSituated just a short walk from St. Mark's Square and with a private pier, this hotel provides easy access to Venice's top attractions. With the Rialto Bridge and water bus stop nearby, exploring the city is effortless.Experience luxury and convenience at the Splendid Venice - Starhotels Collezione.
