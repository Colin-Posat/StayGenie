HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Nautical-Themed RoomsHotel Zephyr San Francisco offers nautical-themed rooms with fishnet-covered light fixtures and porthole-style windows. Each room is equipped with modern amenities like satellite TV, WiFi, and en suite bathrooms with complimentary toiletries. Some rooms even provide breathtaking views of San Francisco Bay.Convenient Facilities and ActivitiesEnjoy 24-hour reception, a mini-market, shared lounge, games room, and ticket service at Hotel Zephyr San Francisco. Guests can partake in various activities including cycling, ensuring a memorable stay.Prime LocationSituated just 300 meters from Pier 39 Fisherman's Wharf, the hotel is also within walking distance of Lombard Street and Ghirardelli Square. With San Francisco International Airport only 21 km away, Hotel Zephyr San Francisco is the perfect choice for your next stay in the city.
