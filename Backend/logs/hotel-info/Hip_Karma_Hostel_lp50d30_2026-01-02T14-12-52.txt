HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Modernist Building:Experience a stay like no other at Hip Karma Hostel, located in a traditional Modernist building just 300 metres from Urquinaona Metro Station.Comfortable Accommodation Options:Choose between private rooms with smart TVs and private bathrooms or dormitory style rooms with bunk beds, each equipped with curtains, reading lights, and security lockers with electrical sockets.Convenient Amenities:Enjoy complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, lockers, bag storage, and kitchen facilities. Additionally, guests have access to a shared kitchen with a large fridge and satellite TV.Ready to elevate your travel experience?
