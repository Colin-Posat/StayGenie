HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to METT Barcelona, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of Sarrià-Sant Gervasi!Unwind and RejuvenateIndulge in our full-service spa, offering an array of massages, body treatments, and facials designed to relax and revitalize you.Dining DelightsSavor exquisite Mediterranean cuisine at Barcelonas, or enjoy 24-hour room service in the comfort of your room. Don’t forget to explore our café for delightful snacks or unwind with a drink by the pool.Convenience at Your FingertipsStay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and make the most of our concierge services, area shuttles, and well-equipped meeting rooms, ensuring your visit is seamless and enjoyable.Embark on an unforgettable escape at METT Barcelona – your perfect getaway awaits!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Cash transactions at this property cannot exceed EUR 1000, due to national regulations. For further details, please contact the property using information in the booking confirmation.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* The property allows pets in specific rooms only and has other pet restrictions (surcharges apply and can be found in the Fees section). Guests can arrange to bring pets by contacting the property directly, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* A car is recommended for transportation to and from this property.
