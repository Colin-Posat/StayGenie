HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique Hotel in the Heart of Union SquarePrime Location: Situated on the famous Powell Cable Car Line, the Chancellor Hotel on Union Square is a mere 2 minutes' walk from elite shopping destinations like Tiffany's and Macy's, offering guests the perfect blend of convenience and luxury.Comfort and Style: Each guest room is thoughtfully designed with modern decor and features amenities such as a flat-screen TV, a menu of 12 specialty pillows, and room windows that can be opened for fresh air. The inclusion of a compact refrigerator and a ceiling fan ensures a comfortable stay for all visitors.Relaxation and Indulgence: Unwind at the on-site Clipper Ship Bar during the daily Happy Hour, or enjoy complimentary coffee, tea, and snacks in the cozy hotel lobby. With its proximity to Union Square Park and other major attractions, Chancellor Hotel on Union Square is the ideal choice for discerning travelers.Book now to experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at Chancellor Hotel on Union Square.
