HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and Convenient AmenitiesThe Inn Off Capitol Park, an Ascend Collection Hotel, offers spacious rooms with seating areas, work desks, and modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs, microwaves, and refrigerators. Guests can enjoy the convenience of coffee makers and ironing facilities, with some rooms featuring cozy sofas in addition to the seating areas.Business-Friendly Services and Relaxing Atrium LobbyBusiness travelers will appreciate the hotel's business center, free Wi-Fi, and wired Internet access. The atrium lobby provides a welcoming atmosphere with comfortable seating and casual conversation areas, perfect for unwinding after a busy day.Prime Location near Key AttractionsConveniently located across from the California state capitol complex, the hotel is within walking distance of the State Capitol, Museum, and the Sacramento Convention Center. Explore nearby attractions like Old Sacramento State Historic Park and the Sacramento Zoo for a memorable stay.Book your stay at the Inn Off Capitol Park for a perfect blend of comfort and convenience.
