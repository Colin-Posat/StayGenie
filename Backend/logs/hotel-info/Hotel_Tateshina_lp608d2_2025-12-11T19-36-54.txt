HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Tateshina, just a 3-minute walk from Shinjuku Sanchome Subway Station, offers easy access to the vibrant heart of Shinjuku. Explore the nearby Shinjuku Gyoen Garden and Isetan Department Store within minutes.Comfortable AccommodationEnjoy a comfortable stay in our air-conditioned rooms featuring modern amenities such as free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a bathtub. Relax with a cup of green tea prepared in your room.Explore the CityImmerse yourself in the bustling atmosphere of Kabukicho, a short 10-minute walk from the hotel. With Shinjuku Train Station also nearby, Hotel Tateshina is the perfect base for your Tokyo adventures.Book your stay at Hotel Tateshina now for a memorable experience in Shinjuku!
