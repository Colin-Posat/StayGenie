HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Fairmont Waterfront offers contemporary rooms with top-notch amenities like flat-screen TVs, minibars, and plush bathrobes. Enjoy a comfortable stay with stunning views of the harbor and mountains.Gourmet Dining ExperienceIndulge in fresh and handcrafted cuisine at ARC Food &amp; Drink throughout the day. From breakfast to dinner, savor delicious meals made from local ingredients in a sophisticated setting.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind at the outdoor heated pool or rejuvenate at the modern gym and hot tub in the on-site health club. Take in the breathtaking scenery from the pool patio or explore nearby attractions like FlyOver Canada and Gastown.Experience luxury and convenience at The Fairmont Waterfront - book your stay now for an unforgettable Vancouver getaway.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  CAD 58 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Amenities like the pool and food court were appreciated, but some guests desired more services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, though some guests wanted more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
