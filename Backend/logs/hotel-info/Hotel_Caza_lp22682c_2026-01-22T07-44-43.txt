HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Caza Fisherman's Wharf, located in San Francisco, offers a premium stay just 400 meters from Ghirardelli Square. The hotel boasts free bikes, private parking, an outdoor pool, and a fitness center for guests to enjoy.Top-Notch FacilitiesGuests can indulge in the hotel's restaurant, relax in the shared lounge, or seek assistance from the 24-hour front desk. With free WiFi throughout the property, a garden, and a sun terrace, every need is catered to with utmost care.Exciting ActivitiesFor leisure, guests can engage in a game of billiards at this 4-star establishment. Moreover, popular attractions like Alcatraz, Pier 39, and Coit Tower are just a stone's throw away, ensuring a memorable stay.Ready to experience luxury and convenience at Hotel Caza Fisherman's Wharf?

• Parking until 6pm on the day of check-out (with paid parking)

• Access to the Game Zone – indoor and outdoor games

• Yoga mats

• Two collapsible water pouches and access to Flo water Stations

• Printing services

• Local and toll-free calls

• San Francisco postcards – postage is on us!

• Evening milk and cookies hour

• Discounts at local eateries and attractions

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 2 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 73.52 per  day.
