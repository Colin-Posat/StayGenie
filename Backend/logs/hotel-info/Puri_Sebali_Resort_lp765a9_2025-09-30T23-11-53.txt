HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 750,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 9/10 - Service was exceptional, with guests praising the professionalism, politeness, and kindness of the staff.
• Location: 9.3/10 - The hotel's serene and remote location surrounded by nature, rice terraces, and a peaceful river was highly appreciated by guests.
• Room Quality: 8.4/10 - Guests found the rooms surprisingly large and comfortable, with some minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.9/10 - Satisfactory amenities with mentions of a beautiful pool and helpful staff, but some guests wished for more diverse options.
• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the price, with some guests feeling it was a bit high for the services offered while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - Both the food quality and restaurant services were praised, though a few guests suggested more variety.
• Overall Experience: 9.4/10 - Most guests had a superb experience, praising the staff, serene atmosphere, and beautiful views.
