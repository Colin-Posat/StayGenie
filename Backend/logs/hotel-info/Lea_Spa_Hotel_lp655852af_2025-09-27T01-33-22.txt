HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Beachfront 3-Star AccommodationLea Spa Hotel in Maafushi offers a beachfront 3-star experience with a shared lounge, terrace, and bar. Enjoy the convenience of a concierge service, tour desk, and free WiFi.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesRelax in rooms equipped with a kettle, flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and private bathroom with free toiletries. Some rooms even feature a balcony for a picturesque view.Dining Variety and Proximity to Bikini BeachIndulge in Italian, Mediterranean, and Pizza cuisine at the on-site restaurant. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for US$17.50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
