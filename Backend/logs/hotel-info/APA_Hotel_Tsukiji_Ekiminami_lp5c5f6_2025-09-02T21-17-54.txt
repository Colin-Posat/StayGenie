HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Fine BeddingsAPA Hotel Tsukiji-eki Minami offers modern rooms with fine beddings, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, Video on Demand system, electric kettle, refrigerator, and an original mattress designed for optimal weight distribution. Guests can also enjoy complimentary nightwear, a tea/coffee set, and free toiletries, along with a special origami crane in each room.Convenient LocationLocated just a 5-minute walk from Tsukiji Station and a short distance from Tsukiji Fish Market, our hotel provides easy access to popular attractions such as the Ginza shopping area and Akihabara Station. With Tokyo Station a quick drive away and Tokyo Disney Resort within a 30-minute train ride, guests can explore the city with ease.On-Site AmenitiesGuests can dine at the hotel's restaurant, take advantage of laundry facilities, and indulge in relaxing massage services. Additionally, property-wide free Wi-Fi ensures seamless connectivity throughout the stay. With various dining options and convenience stores nearby, guests can enjoy a hassle-free and enjoyable stay at APA Hotel Tsukiji-eki Minami.
