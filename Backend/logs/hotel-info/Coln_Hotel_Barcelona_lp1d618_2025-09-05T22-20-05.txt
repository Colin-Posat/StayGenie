HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Spectacular ViewsExperience a stay of grandeur at the Colón Hotel Barcelona, located just opposite the stunning Barcelona Cathedral. Indulge in stylish rooms with modern amenities including free WiFi, satellite TV, and Nespresso coffee machines. Some rooms even offer breathtaking views of the cathedral façade.Culinary Delights and Rooftop RelaxationSavor Catalan and international cuisine at the elegant hotel restaurant or unwind at the outdoor terrace café overlooking the cathedral. For a truly special experience, head to the roof-top terrace to enjoy panoramic views of the city and the cathedral.Convenience and HospitalityWith a 24-hour front desk and car rental services, your comfort is our priority. Take advantage of the prime location just a 5-minute walk from Plaza Catalunya. Book now to elevate your Barcelona experience at the Colón Hotel.
