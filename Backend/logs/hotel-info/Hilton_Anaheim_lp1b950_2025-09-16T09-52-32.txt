HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Disneyland and Convention CenterLuxurious Amenities and Dining OptionsThe Hilton Anaheim offers modern rooms with flat-screen TVs and a variety of dining options including Mix Lounge and a food court with five restaurants. Guests can also enjoy a Starbucks and outdoor restaurant on-site.Health Club, Spa, and Meeting SpacesGuests can unwind at the outdoor pool, play area, and health club with fitness classes, basketball court, whirlpools, and sauna. The hotel also features an adults-only swimming pool and 140,000 square feet of meeting spaces.Book now to experience the ultimate blend of luxury and convenience at the Hilton Anaheim.
