HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Pelham, nestled in South Kensington, offers boutique stylish rooms with a blend of antique and modern furnishings, air-conditioning, and flat-screen TVs. Each room is uniquely decorated, ensuring a one-of-a-kind stay for every guest.Culinary DelightsIndulge in exquisite dining at La Trattoria by Alfredo Russo, a Michelin-starred chef's restaurant within the hotel. Enjoy freshly sourced Italian cuisine paired with organic wines and champagnes. The hotel's wood-panelled drawing room and library are perfect for relaxing with afternoon tea.Prime LocationConveniently located near South Kensington Tube Station, The Pelham is a short walk from iconic attractions like the Royal Albert Hall and the Natural History Museum. Explore Hyde Park, Kensington Gardens, and Notting Hill, all within easy reach. Experience luxury and convenience at The Pelham - book now for an unforgettable stay.

Please note that guests are required to show the credit card used to book the nonrefundable reservation upon check-in.
