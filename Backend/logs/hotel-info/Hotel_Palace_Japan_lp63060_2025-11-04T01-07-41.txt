HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesHotel Palace, just a short walk from JR Minami-Senju Station, offers free Wi-Fi and a smoke-free environment. Guests can enjoy the shared kitchen and internet station during their stay.Comfortable Rooms and Shared FacilitiesAll air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV and fridge. With shared bathrooms and toilets, guests can also borrow hair dryers and alarm clocks from the front desk at no extra cost.Explore Tokyo with EaseLocated near Sensoji Temple and Asakusa, and with easy access to Ueno Park, Hotel Palace Japan is the perfect base for exploring Tokyo. The nearby subway station provides direct links to popular districts like Ueno, Akihabara, and Roppongi.Book your stay at Hotel Palace now for a convenient and comfortable Tokyo experience!
