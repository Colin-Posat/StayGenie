HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the elegance and charm of The Kimberly Hotel, perfectly situated in the heart of New York City.Luxury AccommodationsMake yourself at home in one of our 194 uniquely designed guest rooms, featuring pillowtop beds with down comforters and Frette Italian sheets, ensuring a restful retreat after a day of exploration.Delectable DiningSavor American cuisine at our renowned restaurant, Upstairs, or unwind with a drink at one of our two vibrant bars/lounges.
