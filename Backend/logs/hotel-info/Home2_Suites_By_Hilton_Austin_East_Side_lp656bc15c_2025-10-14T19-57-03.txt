HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationModern AmenitiesOutdoor Pool and Fitness CentreBook your stay at Home2 Suites By Hilton Austin East Side for a perfect blend of comfort and convenience. Enjoy a relaxing time by the outdoor swimming pool, stay active at the fitness centre, and make use of modern amenities such as free WiFi and a kitchenette in every room. Guests can start their day with a delicious breakfast buffet and later indulge in a barbecue. With a prime location near Festival Beach and the Austin Convention Center, this hotel is the ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers. Experience the best of Austin during your stay at Home2 Suites!
