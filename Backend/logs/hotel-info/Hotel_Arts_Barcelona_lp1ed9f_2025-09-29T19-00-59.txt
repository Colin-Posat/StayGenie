HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Design and AmenitiesLocated overlooking the beautiful Barceloneta Beach and the city center, Hotel Arts boasts 2 outdoor pools, a luxury spa with stunning city views, and an impressive art collection. The rooms feature modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and sound systems, with elegant bathrooms offering both a bath and shower for ultimate relaxation.Culinary ExcellenceIndulge in a gastronomic experience at the hotel's Enoteca restaurant, holding 2 Michelin stars and serving exquisite Mediterranean cuisine. Additionally, enjoy a variety of bars and restaurants on-site, including a pool-side bar for refreshing summer meals.Convenience and ServicesWith a prime location near Barcelona's Olympic Port, guests have easy access to shops, bars, Barceloneta Beach, and Ciutadella Park. The hotel also offers a hair salon, luxury boutiques, a multilingual concierge team, and a fully equipped business center for a seamless stay.Experience luxury and convenience at Hotel Arts - book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for € 0 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 120 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 60.50 per  day.
