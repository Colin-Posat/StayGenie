HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for THB 0 per person per night when using an available cot.
People 3 years old and over stay for THB 1,500 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, although a few mentioned some minor issues with bathroom amenities.

• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's prime location near key attractions and convenient access to public transport received high praise from guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests enjoyed the spacious and modern rooms, with positive feedback on comfort and maintenance.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities were noted by guests, with some mentions of the rooftop pool, fitness center, and free tuktuk service, while others desired additional spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests finding the price reasonable, while others expressed that it was slightly high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast quality and restaurant services, although some guests suggested more variety in offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
