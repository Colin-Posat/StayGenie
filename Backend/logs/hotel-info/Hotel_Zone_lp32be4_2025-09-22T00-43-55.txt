HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Clean rooms

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited amenities

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although some guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 7.5/10 - The hotel is located in a quiet residential area, away from shops and supermarkets.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms were basic with a comfortable bed, but some guests mentioned a lack of plugs and USB ports for charging.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Limited amenities with no separate bar facility, and drinks served in the restaurant only.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the rooms were overpriced.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests felt the variety could be improved.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Most guests had a pleasant overall experience, but some were disappointed with certain aspects.
