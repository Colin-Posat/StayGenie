HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Venetian Style RoomsIndulge in the charm of Venetian decor with blue carpets, yellow wallpaper, and custom-made white furniture. Relax in marble bathrooms with modern amenities and enjoy stunning lagoon views from select rooms.Convenient AmenitiesExperience comfort with air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and free Wi-Fi available in all rooms. Start your day with a delicious breakfast buffet served at the hotel or enjoy it outside with picturesque views of Venice during the summer.Prime LocationLocated near Venice Biennale and St. Mark’s Square, Hotel Ca' Formenta offers easy access to top attractions. With a private wharf and proximity to key landmarks, your Venetian getaway awaits. Book now for an unforgettable stay.
