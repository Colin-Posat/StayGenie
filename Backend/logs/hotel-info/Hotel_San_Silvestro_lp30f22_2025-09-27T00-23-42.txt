HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Rome's Historical CentreHotel San Silvestro, nestled next to the Church of San Silvestro in Capite, offers a prime location in the heart of Rome's historical center. Just a 5-minute stroll from the renowned Spanish Steps, this small hotel provides air-conditioned rooms and complimentary WiFi for a comfortable stay.Convenient Access to Rome's AttractionsSituated in the bustling shopping district around Via del Corso, Hotel San Silvestro is within walking distance of iconic landmarks like the Trevi Fountain and the Pantheon. Explore Rome's treasures with ease from this centrally located hotel.Comfortable Rooms and AmenitiesEach room at San Silvestro Hotel boasts a classic design and is equipped with modern conveniences such as a TV, tea/coffee making facilities, and a minibar. Start your day with a delicious continental buffet-style breakfast before setting out to discover the city's charms.To experience the charm of Rome from a convenient and cozy base, book your stay at Hotel San Silvestro today.
