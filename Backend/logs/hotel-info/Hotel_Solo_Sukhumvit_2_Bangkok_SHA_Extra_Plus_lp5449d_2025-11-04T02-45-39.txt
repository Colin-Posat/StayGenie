HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Accommodation with Free Wi-FiHotel Solo, Sukhumvit 2, Bangkok - SHA Extra Plus is a boutique hotel that offers stylish accommodation with free Wi-Fi. The modern decor and wooden flooring in each room create a chic and comfortable atmosphere for guests. Enjoy the convenience of a flat-screen cable TV, an espresso machine, and a private bathroom with a rainshower and electronic scale.Outdoor Pool and Dining OptionsRelax by the outdoor pool or indulge in delicious dining options at Hotel Solo. Sala Di Solo, the all-day restaurant, serves Asian, Western, and fusion dishes for a culinary adventure. For a unique experience, head to the Cloud 9 Bar to savor coffee and cocktails under the stars.Convenient Location and ServicesLocated just a 10-minute walk from Ploenchit BTS Skytrain Station, Hotel Solo offers easy access to transportation. Take advantage of the free shuttle service to Sukhumvit Road or arrange day trips at the tour desk. With laundry services, free parking, and free laptops in the lobby, your stay will be both convenient and enjoyable.Book your stay at Hotel Solo now for a memorable experience in Bangkok!
