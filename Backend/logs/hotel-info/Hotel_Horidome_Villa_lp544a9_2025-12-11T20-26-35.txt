IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Full payment is required upon check-in.

From Tokyo Station, the Nihonbashi Exit or Exit B8a (from connected Otemachi subway station) are nearest to the hotel. From here it is either a 20-minute walk or a 10-minute taxi ride.
