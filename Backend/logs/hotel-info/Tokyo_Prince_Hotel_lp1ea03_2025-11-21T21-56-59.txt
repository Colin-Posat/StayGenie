HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views of Tokyo TowerNewly renovated in April 2017, Tokyo Prince Hotel offers modern accommodation with breathtaking views of Tokyo Tower just a 3-minute walk away. The spacious rooms feature large windows, modern decor, and essential amenities like an electric kettle and personal safe.Luxurious AmenitiesGuests can indulge in a seasonal outdoor pool, spa treatments, and a shopping arcade all within the hotel premises. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, private bathroom, hairdryer, and toiletries for a comfortable stay.Culinary DelightsSavor a variety of cuisines at the hotel's restaurants - from Western dishes at Restaurant Porto to sushi at Shimizu Japanese Restaurant. Unwind with drinks at Windsor Main Bar or enjoy a relaxing cup of tea at Pikake Tea Salon.Experience luxury and convenience at Tokyo Prince Hotel - Book now for an unforgettable stay!
