HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Hotel with Prime LocationThe Ibis Barcelona Mollet, located in Mollet del Valles, offers a contemporary stay just a short drive from Circuit de Catalunya Race Track. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, soundproofed rooms with flat-screen TVs, and a 24-hour snack bar.Comfortable and Functional RoomsExperience a peaceful night's sleep in the completely soundproofed rooms featuring a modern design, wooden floors, and air conditioning for utmost comfort during your stay at Ibis Mollet.Convenient Amenities and Easy AccessIndulge in drinks and light meals at the hotel's bar, take advantage of the 24-hour reception and tour desk, and benefit from the hotel's proximity to the AP7 Motorway for easy travel to Barcelona. With private covered parking and direct transport links to the city, your stay at Ibis Barcelona Mollet is both convenient and enjoyable.To book your stay at the Ibis Barcelona Mollet and experience comfort and convenience, make your reservation now!
