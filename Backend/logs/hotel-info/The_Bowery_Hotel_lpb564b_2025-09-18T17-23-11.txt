HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique Hotel in the Heart of Lower East Side and East VillageChic Rooms with City Views and Elegant AmenitiesIndulge in the boutique charm of The Bowery Hotel with its stylish rooms boasting floor-to-ceiling windows offering stunning city views. The industrial design elements blend seamlessly with residential loft-style amenities, including hardwood floors, Oushak rugs, and premium bedding. Pamper yourself in the marble bathroom featuring rain showers, plush towels, and luxury C.O. Bigelow bath products.Exceptional Services and Complimentary OfferingsExperience top-notch hospitality with 24-hour concierge service and complimentary bicycle rentals to explore the vibrant neighborhood. Enjoy access to newspapers, laptops, and iPads upon request for your convenience.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentSavor delectable Italian cuisine at Gemma, the on-site restaurant offering a diverse menu for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, along with refreshing cocktails. Don't miss the weekend brunch or the option for 24-hour room service. Plus, immerse yourself in the vibrant music scene with popular venues like Bowery Ballroom and Mercury Lounge just a short walk away.Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at The Bowery Hotel.
