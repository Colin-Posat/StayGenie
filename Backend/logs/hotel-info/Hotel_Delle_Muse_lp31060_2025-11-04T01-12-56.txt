HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Family-Run Hotel in Rome's Quiet Parioli DistrictComfortable Accommodation: Hotel Delle Muse features rooms with 21-inch flat-screen TVs and air conditioning, ensuring a cozy stay for guests.Delicious Dining Options: Guests can enjoy a daily breakfast buffet in the garden during summer and savor traditional Italian meals for lunch and dinner on the large, covered terrace.Convenient Location: Situated just 500 meters from Villa Ada park and a short drive from Auditorium Parco della Musica, Hotel Delle Muse offers a peaceful retreat with easy access to Rome's attractions.Experience the charm and convenience of Hotel Delle Muse - book your stay now!
