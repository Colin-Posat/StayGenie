HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated just 5 kilometres from the Château de Versailles, Premiere Classe Versailles offers easy access to popular attractions like the French miniature village. The hotel's proximity to the A12 and A86 motorways makes it a convenient choice for travelers.Comfortable AccommodationGuests can enjoy affordable yet comfortable en suite rooms equipped with free Wi-Fi and air conditioning. The hotel also provides free parking for added convenience.Access to AmenitiesStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast and explore nearby dining options and supermarkets. With Saint-Cyr RER station just a short walk away, guests have direct access to central Paris and can easily reach Orly Airport and Thoiry Zoo.Book your stay at Premiere Classe Versailles for a convenient and comfortable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
How to check-in/out: The opening hours are 06.30 to 11:00 and 17:00 to 21:00 on weekdays and 07:00 to 11:00 and 17:00 to 21:00 on weekends and public holidays. If you plan to arrive outside those hours please check that you have brought the credit card used to make the reservation.

Guests arriving after 21:00 are kindly requested to contact the hotel using the details on the booking confirmation in order to obtain access to their room.
