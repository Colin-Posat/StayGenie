HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Garden SettingNestled among ancient trees in a lush garden, TH Roma - Carpegna Palace offers a tranquil escape just steps away from Cornelia Metro Station.Modern ComfortsIndulge in contemporary rooms featuring flat-screen TVs, free WiFi, and minibars. Some rooms even boast tea/coffee facilities for added convenience.Convenient LocationLocated near Villa Carpegna Park and with easy access to St. Peter’s Basilica, this historic hotel is the perfect base for exploring Rome. Plus, with free parking and proximity to the A90 road, getting around the city is a breeze.Ready to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience? IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform TH Roma - Carpegna Palace in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that small and medium-sized dogs only are allowed. Dog weight must not exceed 10 kg (guide dogs are excluded). The supplement applied is 20€ per night per pet.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
