HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Style AccommodationHotel Mademoiselle offers stylish and modern accommodations located conveniently between Gare du Nord and Gare de l'Est train stations. The rooms are air-conditioned and equipped with satellite TV, a telephone, and a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries.Relaxation and Dining OptionsGuests can unwind in the hammam and sauna, available free of charge when shared or for private use upon request. Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast and enjoy a glass of wine at the on-site snack bar. Additionally, there are 3 cozy living rooms where you can relax, read newspapers, and access free Wi-Fi.Prime LocationSituated just a short stroll from the Grands Boulevards, Hotel Mademoiselle is a perfect base for exploring Paris. With the Montmartre district and the Sacré-Coeur Basilica only a 10-minute Metro ride away, the hotel offers convenience and comfort for your stay in the City of Light.Book your stay at Hotel Mademoiselle now for a memorable Parisian experience.
