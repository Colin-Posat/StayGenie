HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in Tropical ParadiseExquisite Villas: Experience wooden interiors, private pools, and stunning views at Black Penny Villas Ubud. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 6 years old and over stay for Rp 550,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 9/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel, highlighting the well-maintained rooms and common areas.
• Service: 9.2/10 - Service was a standout aspect of the hotel, with guests appreciating the attentive and helpful staff.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and with easy access to public transport garnered positive feedback from guests.
• Room Quality: 8.5/10 - Rooms were spacious, comfortable, and offered stunning views, though some guests noted minor inconveniences like open bathrooms.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with special mentions for the gym and pool, and some concerns raised about the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was high relative to services provided, while others found it reasonable given the prime location.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant offerings, though a few suggested improvements in variety and selection.
• Overall Experience: 8.8/10 - Most guests had an excellent overall experience, expressing willingness to recommend the hotel to others and praising various aspects like staff, views, and service.
