HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location Across from SeaWorld OrlandoSituated just opposite SeaWorld Orlando, the Hilton Garden Inn Orlando at SeaWorld offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience for your stay in Orlando.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesEach room at the Hilton Garden Inn Orlando at SeaWorld is equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. Enjoy complimentary toiletries and the convenience of in-room appliances.Dining Options and On-Site FacilitiesIndulge in delicious meals at the Garden Grill and Bar, grab drinks at Sting Ray’s Lounge, or opt for take-away meals from the 24-hour Pavilion Pantry. With a pool, hot tub, business center, and shuttle service to nearby attractions, your stay is bound to be memorable.Book your stay at the Hilton Garden Inn Orlando at SeaWorld for a delightful Orlando experience!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
