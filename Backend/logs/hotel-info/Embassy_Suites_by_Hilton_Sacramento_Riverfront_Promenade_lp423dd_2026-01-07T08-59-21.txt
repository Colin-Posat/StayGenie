HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Riverfront Location with Spacious Suites:Located on the Sacramento River, Embassy Suites by Hilton Sacramento Riverfront Promenade offers spacious 2-room suites with separate living areas, perfect for a comfortable stay.Entertainment and Dining Options:Within walking distance to the Golden 1 Center, guests can enjoy various events and shows. The hotel also offers a cooked-to-order breakfast and a Marketplace Café for light meals, salads, and pizza.Nearby Cultural Attractions:Explore the vibrant culture of Sacramento with the Crocker Art Museum and California State Capitol Museum just minutes away from the hotel.Experience comfort, convenience, and entertainment at Embassy Suites by Hilton Sacramento Riverfront Promenade.
