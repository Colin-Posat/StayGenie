HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe 5-star Hôtel Barrière Fouquet's Paris, situated on the prestigious Champs-Élysées and Avenue George V, offers opulent rooms designed by renowned decorators. Enjoy modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, complimentary minibar, and elegant bathrooms with luxurious bathrobes and slippers.Gourmet Dining and BarsIndulge in exquisite culinary experiences at the hotel's 2 restaurants, including the iconic Fouquet's brasserie, and sip on creative cocktails at the 3 stylish bars. Whether dining at Fouquet's brasserie or Le Joy restaurant, guests are treated to a delightful gastronomic journey.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind at the spa's swimming pool and sauna, stay active at the gym, or pamper yourself with a rejuvenating hammam session. With free access to the spa facilities, guests can truly unwind and rejuvenate during their stay.Experience unparalleled luxury and convenience at Hôtel Barrière Fouquet's Paris. Book your stay now to indulge in a world of elegance and sophistication.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 55 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
