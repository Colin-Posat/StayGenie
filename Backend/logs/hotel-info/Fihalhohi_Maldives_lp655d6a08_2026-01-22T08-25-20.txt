HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Island GetawaySerene Accommodations: The air-conditioned rooms at Fihalhohi Island Resort offer a comfortable stay with modern amenities including a terrace, tiled flooring, and free WiFi.Exciting Activities: Guests can enjoy water sports like fishing and diving at the PADI-certified dive school on-site. The tour desk also helps with sightseeing and travel arrangements for a memorable vacation experience.Delicious Dining Options: Indulge in mouthwatering Maldivian and international cuisines at the Palm Grove restaurant, or unwind with a drink at the Fisherman’s Bar and Blue Lagoon Bar.Experience paradise at Fihalhohi Island Resort - Book now for an unforgettable island retreat!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The property can be reached by speedboat only.

The charges for speedboats are as follows:

1.  Shared Speedboat Transfers: Round-trip from Malé International Airport
- Adult (12 years and above): USD 170 per person.
- Child (2–15 years): USD 85 per child. 

2. Private Speedboat Transfers: Round-trip from Malé International Airport
- Maximum 4 guests: USD 1,100.

Please share your flight details with the property at least 72 hours before your arrival to secure your seat(s) for the transfer.

Please note that additional guests will incur an additional charge.

The following complimentary facility will be provided for guests booking honeymoon packages:
- One bottle of sparkling wine.
- Basket of tropical fruits.
- Honeymoon cake.

Honeymoon package rates are only available within 6 months of the guests' wedding date.

Guests are responsible for providing proof of marriage, if requested by the property.

Please note that a Green Tax of USD 12 per person, per night is applicable additionally. This tax is already calculated and included in the booking price for adults.

Due to safety and privacy concerns, the operation of unmanned aerial systems or drones by any of the guests, including model aircraft by recreational users and hobbyists, is prohibited.Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$82 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
