HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Green Park Pendik Hotel &amp; Convention Center, located in the heart of Pendik district, offers lavish rooms with modern amenities including LCD TVs and rain showers. Some rooms even boast private spa baths with stunning views of the Marmara Sea.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can enjoy a range of facilities such as indoor and outdoor swimming pools, a well-equipped gym, and a luxurious spa featuring steam rooms, snow rooms, and private massage rooms. Whether you seek relaxation or fitness, this hotel has you covered.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in culinary delights at the hotel's 7 bars and restaurants, offering a variety of cuisines and settings. From a rich buffet breakfast with sea views to fine dining experiences and casual pub nights, there's something for every palate.Experience luxury and convenience at the Green Park Pendik Hotel &amp; Convention Center. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.38/10 - Most reviews highlighted the cleanliness of the hotel, mentioning that the rooms were clean and well-maintained.
• Service: 7.85/10 - The staff were frequently praised for being helpful and accommodating, leading to a higher score in this area.
• Location: 7.89/10 - The location was consistently mentioned as a positive aspect, being near the center and other attractions.
• Room Quality: 6.34/10 - Some reviews noted that although rooms were spacious and clean, there were mentions of old furniture and the need for refurbishment.
• Amenities: 5.37/10 - Several reviews indicated that while some amenities were appreciated, others, like the spa, were in need of maintenance and improvement.
• Value for Money: 5.83/10 - Overall satisfaction leads to a decent value for money rating, though some may feel it's priced at a higher tier due to certain issues.
• Food and Beverage: 6.88/10 - Breakfast was described positively with variety; however, not enough detail was provided for a higher score.
• Overall Experience: 6.05/10 - General satisfaction with the hotel stays, but acknowledged areas for improvement.
