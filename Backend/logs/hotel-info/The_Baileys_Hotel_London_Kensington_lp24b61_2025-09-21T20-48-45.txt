HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Victorian CharmThe Bailey’s Hotel London Kensington offers a taste of 19th-century elegance in the heart of Kensington. Retaining its original Victorian features, this townhouse provides a stylish and historic setting for your stay.Prime Location for ExploringSituated just steps away from Gloucester Road Tube Station, the hotel is a perfect base for exploring top attractions like Kensington Gardens, Hyde Park, and world-renowned museums within minutes. The central location allows for easy access to London's iconic landmarks.Modern Comforts and DiningIndulge in spacious rooms equipped with modern amenities, including flat-screen TVs and complimentary refreshments. Unwind in the fitness room or savor delectable Asian cuisine at Bugis Street Brasserie, offering a fusion of Singaporean, Chinese, and Malaysian flavors.Experience luxury, history, and convenience at The Bailey’s Hotel London Kensington. Book your stay now to immerse yourself in Victorian grandeur and contemporary comforts.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 8 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  GBP 50 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for £36 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
