HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near St Mark’s SquareHotel Antigo Trovatore, just 150 metres behind St Mark’s Square, offers a convenient and central location for exploring Venice. With great water bus connections and proximity to the Zaccaria Vaporetto stop, guests can easily access Santa Lucia Train Station.Comfortable Rooms with ViewsThe air-conditioned rooms at Antigo Trovatore are simply decorated and equipped with modern amenities including free WiFi and satellite TV. Some rooms offer terraces while others boast stunning views of the 16th-century Doges Palace and Saint Mark's bell tower.Convenient Services and SurroundingsGuests appreciate the 24-hour reception service for assistance and luggage storage on check-out day. Situated amidst Venice's popular shops, cafés, and restaurants, the hotel provides a perfect base for immersing in the city's vibrant atmosphere.Plan your stay at Hotel Antigo Trovatore for an unforgettable Venice experience.
