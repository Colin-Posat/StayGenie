HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with KitchenetteHotel MyStays Higashi-Ikebukuro offers modern air-conditioned rooms with a kitchenette, equipped with essential appliances for a comfortable stay. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a stovetop, microwave, sink, and fridge, perfect for preparing meals during their visit.Convenient AmenitiesGuests at MyStays Higashi-Ikebukuro can relax and unwind with amenities such as a bathtub-equipped bathroom with complimentary toiletries, a flat-screen TV with video-on-demand, and an air purifier. The memory foam pillows ensure a restful night's sleep after a day of exploring Tokyo.Prime LocationLocated just a minute's walk from Mukohara Station, the hotel provides easy access to popular destinations like Shinjuku and Shibuya. With nearby transportation options like JR Otsuka Train Station and Shin-Otsuka Subway Station, guests can explore the city with ease.Book your stay at Hotel MyStays Higashi-Ikebukuro for a convenient and comfortable Tokyo experience.
