Discover the charm of Hôtel des Saints-Pères, nestled in the heart of Saint-Germain-des-Prés, Paris.Contemporary ComfortEach of our 38 air-conditioned rooms is designed for your comfort, featuring flat-screen televisions and complimentary wireless internet access.
