HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated next to Fabra i Puig Station, Ibis Barcelona Meridiana provides easy access to Plaza Catalunya in just a 10-minute Metro ride.Modern AmenitiesEnjoy air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen satellite TV, ensuring a comfortable stay. The 24-hour bar offers a variety of snacks and drinks for your convenience.Entertainment and ShoppingAdjacent to the Heron City Shopping Centre, our hotel allows guests to explore various dining options, cinemas, and even a gym. Additionally, indoor public parking is available at an extra cost.Ready to experience Barcelona with us? Book your stay at Hotel Ibis Barcelona Meridiana now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

Please note that parking in the public car park can not be pre-paid.

Children up to 12 years old will stay for free but the meal (breakfast, dinner, lunch) is not included.

• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were described as basic but clean, with some complaints about air conditioning effectiveness and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - While some guests appreciated the calm bar and nearby park, others mentioned issues like small towels and slow service.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests finding it comfortable and quiet for the price, while others thought it was not worth recommending.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast and bar services received mixed feedback, with some guests enjoying the selection while others faced issues with cocktails and slow service.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had positive experiences, praising aspects like a friendly receptionist, quiet location, and clean rooms.
