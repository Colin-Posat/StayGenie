HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 55 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, with a few minor mentions of maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was generally praised for being friendly and helpful, although some guests had complaints about wait times and check-in issues.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and convenient location in downtown Portland was well-received by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and spacious, though some guests mentioned the need for updates in bathrooms.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with positive mentions of the fitness center and free bikes, but some noted the lack of room service and dining options.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed opinions on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with some guests suggesting more variety would be appreciated.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some issues raised.
