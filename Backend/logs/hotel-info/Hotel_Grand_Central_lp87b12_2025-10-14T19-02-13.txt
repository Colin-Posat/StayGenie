HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Centrally Located Luxury HotelStylishly-Decorated Rooms with Modern AmenitiesEach room at Hotel Grand Central features energy-saving technology, a 39-inch LED TV, filtered water dispenser, and LED lighting. Guests can enjoy coffee/tea making facilities, a safety deposit box, and en suite bathrooms with rain showers and complimentary toiletries.Convenient Facilities and ServicesGuests can benefit from free WiFi, meeting rooms, and business facilities. The hotel also offers a rooftop swimming pool, a fitness room, and a 24-hour front desk for luggage storage. The property's proximity to major MRT stations and popular attractions makes it an ideal choice for travelers.Book your stay at Hotel Grand Central now to experience luxury and convenience in the heart of Singapore's shopping district.

Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
1. Maximum additional 1 child 6 years old and below per room can share the existing bed with parents. Take note that no free extra bed is given. 7 years old and above is required to pay for extra bed. Please check the extra bed charges with hotel directly.

2. Please check with hotel directly for maximum permissible group size per room. We seek your kind understanding that it is subject to the latest Safe Management Measures.

3. Guests and visitors must pre-register at the Front Desk before presenting themselves in the room. All visitors are not allowed to stay overnight and must leave the premises by 11pm. Hotel reserves right to refuse entry for guests' visitors if they are not pre-registered.

4. No booking is required for use of swimming pool and gym. However due to the situation continues to evolve, we seek your kind understanding that there is a capacity limit and we may not be able to fit your preferred time. Please check the status with our front desk directly upon your arrival.

5. Due to the current situation, buffet breakfast at the hotel is not available. If you book room with breakfast, you can proceed to our sister hotel (Hotel Chancellor@Orchard) just located besides us to have the mini buffet breakfast at level 2. We seek your kind understanding that it is subject to the latest Safe Management Measures. Please check the breakfast arrangement with our front desk directly upon your arrival.

6. Children 5 years old and above will be charged for breakfast. Please check the breakfast charges with hotel directly.

7. Parking at the hotel is not available. You may park at Hotel Chancellor@Orchard just located besides us and charges apply. Please check the parking charges with hotel directly.

8. Local calls and International calls are chargeable. Please check the charges with hotel directly.
