HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Amsterdam’s Canal DistrictThe Townhouse Hotel, located in the heart of Amsterdam’s canal district, offers basic accommodation just a short walk from the popular Nine Streets shopping area. With free Wi-Fi available, guests can stay connected throughout their stay.Comfortable Rooms with Convenient AmenitiesThe hotel features rooms with shared facilities, while some offer the added privacy of a private shower. Each room is equipped with a sink and coffee and tea facilities, ensuring guests have everything they need for a comfortable stay.Exploration and Convenience at Your DoorstepSituated within walking distance of the renowned Anne Frank House and with a tram stop just a 2-minute walk away, guests have easy access to explore the city. Additionally, the vibrant Utrechtsestraat nearby provides a plethora of dining and entertainment options for guests to enjoy during their stay.Book your stay at The Townhouse Hotel now for a memorable Amsterdam experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Townhouse Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of EUR 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that there is no elevator and there are steep stairs, making it less suitable for elderly or disabled guests.

Some rooms are located in an annex building, 15 metres from the main building.
