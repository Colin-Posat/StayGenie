IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 200 is required on arrival. This will be collected as a cash payment. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full in cash, subject to an inspection of the property.
The pre-authorisation charge will appear as a withdrawal for debit cards. The first night’s room charges and taxes will be authorized on the credit card provided at the time of reservation.

The resort fee includes hi speed Wi Fi, 3 daily bottled Poland Spring waters, Nespresso coffee makers, an in room welcome snack (first night only) and all local and long distance (in the continental USA) phone calls.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel, although some noise issues were reported.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Friendly and approachable staff with helpful service, although some guests experienced noise disturbances.
• Location: 8.8/10 - Prime location near key attractions, restaurants, and public transport, offering a central and safe experience.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were described as quiet, clean, and comfortable, with notable views of the Empire State Building, but noise levels in some rooms were a concern.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities provided, such as Nespresso coffee machines and free bottled water, but noise disturbances affected overall experience.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests noting unexpected fees and noise disturbances impacting the overall perceived value.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, though noise disturbances and limited variety were mentioned.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff, location, and room quality, despite some noise issues.
