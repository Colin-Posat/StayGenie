HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Residence Inn Marriott Chicago Midway, your ideal home away from home!Recreational AmenitiesTake advantage of our inviting indoor pool and a state-of-the-art 24-hour fitness center, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring the city. The cozy lobby, featuring a fireplace, is ideal for relaxing with friends or family.Dining OptionsStart your day with our complimentary continental breakfast, and when hunger strikes, grab a snack from our grocery/convenience store. Enjoy al fresco dining with barbecue grills available for guest use!Convenient LocationWith a complimentary area shuttle service, getting to nearby attractions like the Chicago Ridge Shopping Mall and United Center is a breeze. Additionally, our business center and event space make us the perfect choice for both leisure and corporate travels.Your Chicago adventure awaits—book your stay with us today!
