HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for THB 118 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 11 years old and over stay for THB 706 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the clean rooms, with a few minor complaints about corridors and humidity.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Overall, the service was friendly and helpful, but there were some mixed reviews about staff and pool hours.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's prime riverfront location near key landmarks was a highlight for guests.
• Room Quality: 7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but there were complaints about room size, thin walls, and lack of plug sockets.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - The hotel offered basic amenities, but some guests missed having a spa and better gym facilities.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Opinions varied on whether the hotel provided good value for the price, with some appreciating the affordability and others expecting more for the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - While breakfast and dining services were generally satisfactory, there were suggestions for improvement in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the convenient location, friendly staff, and pleasant stay.
