IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
Please note:

- Not all rates include breakfast. If you book a breakfast-inclusive rate, breakfast is offered for 2 adults and up to 2 children under 12 years old.

- Select rooms feature Concierge Lounge access. Access is limited to two (2) adults and two (2) children under 12 years old per room. Additional guests are permitted for a fee of USD $60 per day.

- Bed type for all rooms is on a request only basis and cannot be guaranteed. Please contact Los Angeles Airport Marriott with further information.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.2/10 - Guests mentioned mixed opinions about cleanliness, with some highlighting cleanliness as a positive aspect and others noting concerns about cleanliness, including old carpets and unclean rooms.
• Service: 6.6/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests praising the welcoming and amazing staff, while others cited issues with rude staff, slow check-in, and poor communication.
• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's prime location near key landmarks and airport, as well as its shuttle service, were well-received by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were generally described as spacious, clean, and comfortable, though some guests mentioned maintenance issues and noise disturbances.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - The amenities provided received mixed feedback, with mentions of a lovely pool, spa, and Starbucks onsite, alongside complaints about limited restaurant options and issues with TV and coffee.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Guest opinions varied regarding value for money, with some feeling the price was reasonable for the location and services offered, while others found it overpriced for the experience.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Feedback on food and beverage services was mixed, with guests appreciating breakfast options and Starbucks but noting issues like expensive breakfast, limited food variety, and slow service.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some minor issues mentioned in other categories.
