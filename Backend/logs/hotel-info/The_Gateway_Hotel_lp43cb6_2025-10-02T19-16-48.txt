HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique En Suite Rooms and Free Wi-FiGateway Hotel, located just 150 meters from Clapham South Tube Station, offers en suite rooms with free Wi-Fi. Each room is individually designed and includes a flat-screen TV/DVD player with Freeview channels.Convenient Location in Lively ClaphamSituated in the vibrant area of Clapham, guests can easily explore numerous bars, shops, restaurants, and trendy gastro pubs. With excellent access to the London Underground, popular destinations like central London, the West End, and Wimbledon are within reach.Parking and AmenitiesThe hotel provides private parking on-site for an additional fee. Please reserve in advance. Note that there is no lift in the hotel, but rooms on lower floors can be requested. Smoking is strictly prohibited on the premises.Book your stay at Gateway Hotel for a memorable experience in Clapham.
