HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic LocationHotel Porta Pia, named after the nearby ancient Roman gate, offers a modern stay in a 19th-century building. Guests can enjoy the historic charm while experiencing top-notch service from the multilingual staff.Modern AmenitiesThe completely renovated guest rooms at Porta Pia Hotel feature satellite TV and elegant wood floors, providing a comfortable and contemporary setting for travelers. The complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the property ensures guests stay connected during their stay.Convenient AccessibilitySituated conveniently on the second floor, this 2-star hotel provides easy access to public transport, allowing guests to explore Rome with ease. With Termini Station just a short bus ride away and Villa Borghese within walking distance, guests can make the most of their Roman holiday at Hotel Porta Pia.Experience the perfect blend of history and modern comfort at Hotel Porta Pia. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Roman getaway.
