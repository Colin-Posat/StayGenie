HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience elegance at its finest at The St Regis Bangkok, located in the heart of Bangkok's commercial district. Each spacious room is adorned with warm colors, stylish rugs, and Thai artwork, offering a truly luxurious stay.World-Class AmenitiesIndulge in pampering at Clinique La Prairie Aesthetics &amp; Medical Spa or enjoy personalized services from your own butler. Take a dip in the outdoor pool, work out with personal trainers in the well-equipped gym, or unwind at the full-service business center.Exquisite Dining OptionsSavor unique fine dining experiences at IGNIV Bangkok or delight in International cuisine at VIU restaurant with stunning views of the Bangkok skyline. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for THB 2,500 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
