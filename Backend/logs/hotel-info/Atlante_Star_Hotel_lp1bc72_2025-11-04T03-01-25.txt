Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests praising it while others complained about issues like old bathrooms and black mould.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Overall positive feedback on service, with some guests mentioning helpful staff while others had complaints about unhelpful staff members.
• Location: 8.6/10 - Consistently commended for its great location near key landmarks and beautiful views from rooftop areas.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Mixed feedback on room quality, with some guests appreciating comfortable beds and old-world style, while others mentioned the need for renovations and small rooms.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Satisfactory amenities mentioned, with positive comments on breakfast, rooftop bar, and cleanliness, but some complaints about outdated facilities and lack of certain amenities like air conditioning.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location and views, while others thought it was too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Generally positive feedback on food and beverage offerings, with breakfast and rooftop bar highlighted positively, but some guests felt the food was not exceptional for the price.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, especially praising the location, views, and friendly staff, though some mentioned room quality and cleanliness issues.
