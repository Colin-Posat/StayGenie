HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationCatalonia Castellnou is conveniently situated just 250 meters from Tres Torres Underground Station, providing easy access to various attractions and medical centers in Barcelona.Comfortable AccommodationThe hotel offers bright, air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests after a day of exploring the city.Nearby AttractionsWithin walking distance, guests can find a variety of dining options, shopping centers like El Corte Ingles, and even the renowned Camp Nou Stadium for football enthusiasts.Experience the convenience and comfort Catalonia Castellnou offers - book your stay now!
