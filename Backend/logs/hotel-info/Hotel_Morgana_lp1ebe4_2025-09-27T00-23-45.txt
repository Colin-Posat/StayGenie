HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Antique Charm and Modern ComfortsHotel Morgana, located just 200 metres from Termini Railway Station, seamlessly blends antique furniture with modern amenities. The rooms offer a unique mix of old-world charm and contemporary facilities, all while providing free WiFi throughout.Convenient Location and Easy AccessSituated near Termini Railway Station, Hotel Morgana offers easy access to Rome's public transport system, making it a breeze to explore the city and reach the international airports. The Colosseum is also just 2 metro stops away, perfect for sightseeing enthusiasts.Delightful Dining and Fitness OptionsStart your day with a generous buffet breakfast in the hotel's dining room, featuring a variety of sweet and savoury options. Guests can also enjoy free access to a small gym located conveniently nearby.Experience the perfect blend of history and modernity at Hotel Morgana. Book your stay now to enjoy a memorable Roman getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that the hotel does not accept Visa Electron to guarantee reservations.

For reservations of 4 or more rooms, a deposit is required and the standart cancellation policy does not applies.

All reservations have to be guaranteed with a credit card in the guest's name. If the credit card used to guarantee the reservation is in a different person's name the reservation will be not accepted and you will be asked to provide a different credit card.

Please note that the total amount of the original booked stay will be charged in the case of early departure.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Adults  stay for € 45 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 15 per  hour.

• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's proximity to transportation hubs and key landmarks was a major highlight for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but there were mentions of maintenance issues and some rooms being smaller than expected.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests appreciating the gym and pool while others found the lack of spa services disappointing.
• Value for Money: 6.2/10 - Guests had varied opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high relative to services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the hotel's strengths in service and location.
