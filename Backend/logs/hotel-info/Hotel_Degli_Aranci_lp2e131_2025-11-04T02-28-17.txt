HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Degli Aranci, nestled in Rome's prestigious Parioli neighborhood, offers elegant rooms with modern amenities. The 19th-century building's charm is accentuated by a garden filled with fragrant orange trees, providing a serene escape.Gastronomic DelightsIndulge in a culinary journey at Il Fiore d'Arancio restaurant, where guests can savor delectable dishes in a picturesque outdoor setting. From gourmet meals to light bites, the dining experience here is sure to tantalize your taste buds.Convenient LocationSituated just a short stroll from Euclide Train Station, exploring Rome's iconic landmarks like Piazza del Popolo is effortless. With easy access to transportation hubs, including the nearby 223 bus to Termini Train Station, discovering the city's wonders is convenient.Experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at Hotel Degli Aranci - book your stay now for an unforgettable Roman getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that all reservations have to be guaranteed with a credit card held by the guest. If the credit card used to guarantee the reservation is in a different person's name, then written authorisation to use this card must be provided before check-in. If not, your reservation cannot be guaranteed.

Parking is 200 metres from the property and should be booked in advance.
