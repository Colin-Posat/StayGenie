HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Well-Equipped Rooms with Traditional TouchesAPA Hotel Tokyo Kiba offers rooms with a bathtub and yukata, just a short walk from Kiba Station. The modern rooms boast original mattresses for a comfortable stay and come with amenities like a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and a fridge.Convenient Location for SightseeingSituated only 11 minutes from Tokyo Big Sight, this hotel provides easy access to popular areas like Nihombashi, Tsukiji Fish Market, and Ginza. Whether you prefer a subway or taxi, exploring the city from this central location is a breeze.Delicious Dining OptionsStart your day with a Japanese and Western-style Gozen breakfast at Public Bar Clover or grab a snack from the vending machines on site. After a day of sightseeing, unwind with a meal at the hotel's restaurant.Book your stay at APA Hotel Tokyo Kiba for a comfortable and convenient experience.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Overall, guests had mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some mentioning clean rooms and others noting maintenance issues.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, although some mentioned slow response times.
• Location: 7.8/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and public transport was praised, but some guests found the area quiet or lacking amenities.
• Room Quality: 5.7/10 - Rooms received mixed feedback, with some guests mentioning spaciousness and comfort, while others complained about small or smoky rooms.
• Amenities: 5.9/10 - The amenities offered were satisfactory to some guests, but others felt the hotel lacked certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.3/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was reasonable given the location, while others found it too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services received mixed reviews, with some guests praising the offerings and others wishing for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 6.6/10 - While most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, there were also concerns about room quality, amenities, and value for money.
