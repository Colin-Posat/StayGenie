HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with City Views and Free WiFiHoliday Inn Bangkok Sukhumvit offers modern rooms with stunning city views, ensuring a memorable stay for guests. Enjoy the convenience of free WiFi throughout the property, perfect for staying connected during your visit.Convenient Location for Shopping and TransportationSituated just a 5-minute walk from Prompong BTS Skytrain Station and Sukhumvit MRT Subway Station, the hotel provides easy access to popular shopping centers like The Emporium and The EmQuartier. Additionally, reaching Suvarnabhumi International Airport or Don Muang International Airport is a breeze with a 30-minute taxi ride.Dining Options and Leisure FacilitiesIndulge in a variety of dining experiences at Holiday Inn Bangkok Sukhumvit, from local Thai delights to international dishes at Zeta Cafe and authentic Indian cuisine at Maya rooftop bar. Unwind at the 24-hour fitness center, outdoor lap pool, or enjoy a drink at the bar. Book your stay now for a delightful blend of comfort and convenience at Holiday Inn Bangkok Sukhumvit.

HOTEL POLICIES:

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for THB 1,770 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for THB 1,770 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
