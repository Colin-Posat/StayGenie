Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests consistently mentioned clean rooms and bathrooms as a positive aspect, but some reviews highlighted minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally praised for being friendly, responsive, and helpful. However, some guests experienced delays in services.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's proximity to restaurants, stores, and major landmarks was well-received by guests, despite being neutral for the area.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests appreciated the spacious and comfortable rooms, but reported some maintenance issues like faulty shower lights and water pressure.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The provided amenities were generally satisfactory, with guests enjoying the gym and pool facilities. However, some mentioned a lack of additional spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered while others considered it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast services were mostly positive, with guests enjoying the varied options. Some guests suggested improvements in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff, cleanliness, and location. They would recommend the hotel to others.
