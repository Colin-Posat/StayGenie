HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Spectacular ViewsThe Shangri-La Tokyo offers a prime location next to JR Tokyo Station, boasting elegant rooms with stunning views of the Imperial Palace, Tokyo Sky Tree, and Mount Fuji. Enjoy top-notch amenities including a 20-meter indoor pool, CHI spa, and exclusive Horizon Club lounge access.Convenient Transportation and Exceptional ServiceWith easy access to major subway stations and airports, the hotel ensures a seamless travel experience for guests. Take advantage of the Narita Express Train for airport transfers and enjoy the convenience of staff pick-up from Tokyo Station platforms.Relaxation and Wellness FacilitiesIndulge in rejuvenating spa treatments at CHI, The Spa, or stay active at the 24-hour Health Club with a heated pool and modern gym equipment. Unwind in style and comfort at Shangri-La Tokyo, where luxury meets convenience.To experience luxury and convenience at its finest, book your stay at Shangri-La Tokyo today.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests who require the free train station escort must provide their train arrival schedules directly to the hotel.

Please contact the property directly prior to your stay in order to request an extra bed, as different rates are applied.

As of 01 January 2019, the property will be entirely nonsmoking.

"Children under 4 years old are not allowed in the pool facility. Children of ages 4~11 years old must be accompanied by their parents/guardian at all times when using the pool facilities"

Please contact the property in advance of your stay to check the availability of dog-friendly rooms. Additional charges may apply.

Please note that the property can only accommodate pets with a maximum weight of 15 kg or less.

Please note that the hotel will undergo a power outage due to a mandatory inspection of the electrical facilities on the following dates/times: Saturday 23 08 2025, 23:00 - 06:00 on Sunday 24 08 2025. Please note that it is the subject to change based on work progress.An accommodation tax per person, per night is not included in the price and must be paid at the property.
