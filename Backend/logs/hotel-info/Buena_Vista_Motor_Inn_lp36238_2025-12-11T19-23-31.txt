HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and ParkingLocated in San Francisco’s Marina District, Buena Vista Motor Inn is just 1.6 km from Fisherman’s Wharf, offering easy access to popular attractions. Enjoy the convenience of free on-site parking during your stay.Comfortable Rooms with ViewsEach traditional-style room at the inn is equipped with cable TV, a seating area, coffee maker, and hairdryer. Some rooms even offer stunning views of the iconic Golden Gate Bridge. Upgrade to a room with a microwave and fridge for added convenience.Nearby AttractionsExplore the city with ease as the famous Lombard Street is a short 9-minute walk away. Visit the Aquarium of the Bay and embark on an adventure with Alcatraz Cruises, both just 1.6 km from the hotel.Book your stay at Buena Vista Motor Inn now for a memorable San Francisco experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.
