HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and Prime LocationLocated in one of Rome's most exclusive neighborhoods near Villa Borghese and Via Veneto, Hotel Priscilla offers a luxurious stay with attentive staff and top-notch service.Delightful Dining and Refreshing BarEnjoy a complimentary continental breakfast in the newly refurbished breakfast room and bar, as well as dinner options every evening.Convenient Services and Nearby AttractionsBook sightseeing tours, museum visits, and airport transfers effortlessly with the English-speaking staff available 24/7.
