HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AccommodationsRenovated in July 2015, E Hotel Higashi Shinjuku offers modern rooms with free WiFi and flat-screen TVs. Each room features both air conditioning and heating for ultimate comfort, along with convenient amenities like an electric kettle and en suite bathrooms with toiletries.Convenient FacilitiesGuests can take advantage of the 24-hour convenience store and self-service laundry machines provided by the property. Additionally, the hotel's proximity to Higashi-Shinjuku Subway Station (Exit A1) and various attractions such as Kabukicho make it a convenient choice for travelers.Prime LocationSituated just a 10-minute walk from the bustling Kabukicho area, E Hotel Higashi Shinjuku is also near Shinjuku Train Station and Waseda University. With direct subway access to popular areas like Ikebukuro and Shibuya, guests can easily explore the city from this central location.Book your stay at E Hotel Higashi Shinjuku for a modern and convenient experience in Tokyo!
