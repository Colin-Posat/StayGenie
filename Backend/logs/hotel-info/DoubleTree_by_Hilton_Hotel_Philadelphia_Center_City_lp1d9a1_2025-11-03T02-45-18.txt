• Limited parking

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.1/10 - Guests provided mixed feedback on cleanliness. Some mentioned clean facilities, while others reported issues like dirty rooms and bathrooms.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service received positive comments overall for being friendly and helpful. However, there were complaints about slow response times and unhelpful staff at times.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's location was a highlight for many guests, with easy access to key landmarks and public transportation.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but some guests reported maintenance issues and small room sizes.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Amenities were satisfactory, with guests appreciating certain features like fitness centers, but also noting the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Feedback on value for money was mixed, with some guests feeling the prices were high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Food and beverage offerings generally received positive feedback, though some guests suggested more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some shortcomings in specific areas.
