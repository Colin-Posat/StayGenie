HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Convenient LocationComfortable Rooms with Thoughtful AmenitiesThe rooms at APA Hotel Shinjuku Gyoenmae feature original mattresses for optimal comfort, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms with free toiletries. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of having a refrigerator, hairdryer, and electric kettle in their rooms.Convenient Facilities for a Relaxing StayThe hotel offers a range of facilities including luggage storage, massage services, vending machines, laundry facilities, and a public bath. With a 24-hour front desk and a communal microwave, guests' needs are well taken care of during their stay.Book now to experience modern comfort and convenience at APA Hotel Shinjuku Gyoenmae.
