The modern rooms feature comfortable amenities and stunning city and park views, ensuring a relaxing stay.Abundance of Entertainment and Relaxation OptionsGuests can enjoy a variety of entertainment options including a bowling alley and karaoke, as well as relaxation services such as a sauna and massage facilities. Access to the fitness center, hot-spring spa, indoor swimming pool, and exclusive 33rd-floor lounge adds to the luxurious experience.Diverse Dining Experiences with Breathtaking ViewsIndulge in a culinary journey with panoramic city views at the Brise Verte French Restaurant and Stellar Garden Sky Lounge. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Full payment is required at check-in.

Guests must be over 20 years old to use the 33rd-floor lounge as well as the spa and fitness center which includes the swimming pool and hot-spring baths. Extra charges apply.

Breakfast is served at the Club Lounge located on the 32nd floor of the property for guests who book a rate plan with Club Lounge access during 7 AM-11 AM (last order at 10:30 AM).

Please note that only Deluxe Twin Room can accommodate an extra bed.

One child under 1 year old stays free of charge when using a child cot or crib.

Children up to and including 5 years old are free of charge when using existing beds. Meal-inclusive rate does not include meals for children sleeping in existing beds. Additional fees apply for children's meals if adults book a meal-inclusive rate.

When booking 5 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Please note that The Prince Park Tower Tokyo Spa & Fitness (B2) will undergo annual equipment inspections on the following dates/times: January 13, 2026 (Tuesday) - January 22, 2026 (Thursday) (10 days). During this period, Hot Spring Area (including Dry Sauna)  will be closed.

Children aged 12 years and below are not allowed to use the The Premium Club Lounge cocktail hour and nightcap hours.

Power outage by annual maintenance work of the electrical equipment will be carried out from 07/01/26 01:00 to 05:00, 19/01/26 02:00 to 05:00.
Please note that the following facilities are unavailable: the lighting in the guest rooms and elevators. 

Fitness center is closed from 2026-01-22 until 2026-01-13
