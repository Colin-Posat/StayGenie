HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover a charming retreat in the heart of Anaheim at DoubleTree Suites by Hilton Anaheim Resort - Convention Center, where comfort meets convenience.Recreational AmenitiesUnwind and rejuvenate in our luxurious outdoor pool, soak in the hot tub, or stay fit in our fully-equipped fitness center. Whatever your leisure style, we have the perfect space for relaxation.Dining OptionsSavor delicious meals at our on-site restaurant or grab a quick snack at the coffee shop. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Overall, guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but there were also reports of bed bugs in one room.
• Service: 7.7/10 - While most guests found the staff friendly and helpful, there were some instances of rude behavior and unresponsiveness.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's proximity to Disneyland and key areas was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but there were complaints about outdated furnishings and appliances.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - While the hotel provided basic amenities, some guests felt that additional services like a spa were lacking.
• Value for Money: 6.2/10 - Mixed reviews about the pricing with some guests feeling it was expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services received mixed feedback, with some guests wanting more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to return in the future.
