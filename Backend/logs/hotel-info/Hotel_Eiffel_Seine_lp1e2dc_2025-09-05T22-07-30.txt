HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location Along the River SeineLocated just a stone's throw away from the iconic Eiffel Tower, the Art Nouveau styled Hotel Eiffel Seine offers a prime location along the scenic River Seine. With Bir-Hakeim Metro Station conveniently opposite, exploring Paris's renowned attractions like the Arc de Triomphe is a breeze.Modern Comforts and AmenitiesThe contemporary air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Eiffel Seine provide a comfortable retreat, complete with free WiFi, double glazed windows, and a flat-screen TV. Guests can enjoy a relaxing stay with complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer in the sleek bathrooms.Charming Bar and Breakfast OptionsUnwind at the hotel's bar and lounge area or savor a delightful buffet breakfast in the dining room, terrace, or even in the comfort of your own room. The 24-hour front desk and laundry facilities ensure a seamless stay for all guests.Ready to experience Parisian elegance by the River Seine? Book your stay at Hotel Eiffel Seine now.
