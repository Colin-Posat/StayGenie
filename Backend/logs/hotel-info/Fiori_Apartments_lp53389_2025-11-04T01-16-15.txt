HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsLocated just 4 minutes’ walk from Parramatta Railway Station, Fiori Apartments offer modern self-contained apartments with private balconies. The air-conditioned apartments feature laundry facilities, kitchenettes with dishwashers, and spacious seating areas with flat-screen TVs.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind in the indoor lap pool, hot tub, and sauna, or enjoy a meal in the barbecue area. The complex also boasts covered golf facilities for leisure activities.Convenient LocationSituated in the heart of Parramatta, a short walk from Westfield Shopping Mall, Fiori Apartments provide easy access to Wet'n'Wild Sydney and Sydney’s city centre. With a convenience store on-site, everything you need is at your fingertips.Experience comfort and convenience at Fiori Apartments Parramatta. Book your stay now for a memorable getaway.
