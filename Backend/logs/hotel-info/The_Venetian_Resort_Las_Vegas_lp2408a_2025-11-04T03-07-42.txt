HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites and AmenitiesThe Venetian Resort offers spacious suites with modern amenities, including LCD HDTVs, a private bathroom with deluxe toiletries, and a sunken sitting area. Guests can indulge in the over-sized soaking tub and glass-enclosed shower for a truly luxurious experience.Diverse Dining OptionsWith over 40 international restaurants, guests can explore a wide array of culinary delights, from bistro fare at Thomas Keller’s Bouchon to American, Italian, Asian, and fusion cuisine. In-suite dining is also available for a more intimate experience.Relaxation and RecreationEnjoy the 1.2 acre pool deck with 10 outdoor pools, poolside dining, and private cabana rentals. Guests can also stay active with complimentary access to the fitness gym at Canyon Ranch spa + fitness. Madame Tussauds Las Vegas Wax Museum on-site adds to the entertainment options.Experience luxury and convenience at The Venetian Resort in Las Vegas. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 150 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Guests under the age of 21 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

Extra beds are available upon request.

Additional charges may apply (see policies).

The daily resort fee includes:
- En-suite internet access
- Unlimited local and toll free phone calls
- Fitness centre access for 2 adults
-Boarding pass printing
- Access to thousands of top newspapers and magazines using PressReader app while connected.

Upon check-in, the guest must be present with a valid ID and provide the booking credit card. If the guest, the proof of ID, the booking credit card, or the booking credit card holder is unavailable, the guest's deposit will be refunded to the booking credit card, and another form of payment will be required.

The Venetian Resort Las Vegas allows personal sized coolers. Please be advised that wheeled or large two-person coolers are not permitted. Oversized coolers may be stored at the Bell Desk during the stay.
