HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Monumental Building in Central AmsterdamDe Roode Leeuw provides a unique stay in a historic building, just steps away from Dam Square. Enjoy the charm of Amsterdam right at your doorstep.Convenient Location and Easy AccessOpposite the hotel is De Bijenkorf, Amsterdam's renowned department store. With public transport nearby and regular trains to Schiphol Airport, exploring the city is a breeze.Modern Comforts and AmenitiesExperience a comfortable stay with climate control, free Wi-Fi, and soundproof windows. Each room is equipped with coffee/tea facilities, wired internet, and a flat-screen TV.Book your stay at De Roode Leeuw for a memorable Amsterdam experience.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.9/10 - Despite some complaints about smelly rooms and cleanliness issues in a few reviews, overall cleanliness was mentioned in a neutral light.
• Service: 7.6/10 - The majority of guests appreciated the friendly and helpful staff, but there were some isolated complaints about rude or dismissive behavior.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Location was consistently praised across multiple reviews for being central and convenient for exploring the city.
• Room Quality: 7/10 - Mixed reviews about room quality, with highlights on spaciousness and clean communal areas, but complaints about small rooms, smelly conditions, and old amenities.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - Amenities received mixed feedback, with some guests mentioning the lack of certain items in rooms and ongoing construction work affecting the overall experience.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the hotel was overpriced for the service provided, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and food services, with occasional suggestions for improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Guests had a mix of experiences, with some praising the hotel for a great weekend and others disappointed by damp smells, ongoing construction, and unhygienic conditions.
