HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Private Pool VillasAyuterra Resort offers a luxurious experience with private pools in each unit, providing stunning views of Ayung River and the lush greenery.Spa Treatments and DiningIndulge in relaxation with massage treatments, a spa center, and a hot tub. HOTEL POLICIES:

Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 12 and older are allowed.
Children from 12 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 1,500,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 1,500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
