GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Contemporary design
• Friendly staff

Areas for Improvement:
• Disrespectful service
• Unclean rooms
• Poor room quality
• Complicated parking system
• Unresponsive housekeeping

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness with some guests praising the cleanliness while others reported issues with wetness in the air and stained carpets.
• Service: 4.8/10 - Service received predominantly negative feedback about disrespectful front desk staff, unresponsive housekeeping, delayed towel requests, and unhelpful response to noise complaints.
• Location: 7.4/10 - Positive comments about the location, but negative remarks about the complicated parking system and limited usability of balconies.
• Room Quality: 4.6/10 - Negative feedback on room quality, including no mattresses on pull-out sofas, dirty and sticky carpets, uncomfortable beds with stains, and broken or missing room amenities.
• Amenities: 5.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with positive comments on the lobby design and bar drinks, but negative remarks on the pool cleanliness, lack of doggy bags, and charging for valet services not used.
• Value for Money: 4.4/10 - Low rating for value for money due to high prices for services and amenities that did not meet guest expectations.
• Food and Beverage: 5.8/10 - Average rating for food and beverage with positive comments on the bar drinks and negative feedback about expensive and limited breakfast options.
• Overall Experience: 5.6/10 - Mixed reviews for the overall experience with guests highlighting some positives like contemporary design and friendly staff, but overall dissatisfaction with the service quality and room conditions.
