Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.8/10 - Guests had mixed experiences with cleanliness, with some reporting sticky floors and dirt in the spa tub filter.
• Service: 8.9/10 - Guests appreciated the 24-hour customer service, quick response to concerns, and maintenance issues. However, clear communication about housekeeping and unexpected deposits was lacking.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location was praised for being close to shopping malls and casinos, but some guests were surprised by additional fees and lack of access to certain amenities.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms had highlights such as beautiful views, but were marred by issues like malfunctioning jacuzzi, locked balcony, and delayed pool openings.
• Amenities: 6.3/10 - Some guests praised valet parking, while others were disappointed by the surprise deposit, lack of cleaning supplies, and additional fees for basic items.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Guests felt the value was impacted by unexpected fees, lack of disclosure about additional costs, and issues with amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Comments were generally positive about breakfast and restaurants, with some guests noting issues with paid Wi-Fi.
• Overall Experience: 6/10 - While some guests appreciated the location and staff, others had significant complaints about cleanliness, maintenance issues, and lack of communication, leading to mixed overall experiences.
