HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:

GROUPS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

• Value for Money: 6/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some feeling it was expensive given the additional costs, while others considered it reasonable for the overall experience.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback for the quality of coffee and calm lobby atmosphere, with some guests suggesting improvements in noise insulation.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would consider revisiting for certain services like the spa and dining options.
