HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Private Villas with Swimming PoolsNestled in the heart of Ubud, Dedary Kriyamaha Ubud offers a luxurious escape with private villas featuring individual swimming pools. Relax in your own private oasis and enjoy the stunning views of the garden or valley from the comfort of your villa.Spa Centre and Dining ExperienceIndulge in ultimate relaxation at the on-site spa centre or treat your taste buds to a delightful dining experience at the all-day restaurant. Unwind in the lobby lounge while taking in the breathtaking sunset views over the picturesque rice fields.Convenient Amenities and ServicesFrom car hire with a driver to bike rentals, Dedary Kriyamaha Ubud ensures a convenient stay for all guests. Explore the nearby attractions such as Ubud Palace or simply enjoy the tranquility of the property. Book your stay now and experience luxury in the heart of Bali.

If the card owner is not the one staying at the hotel, the guest is required to present clear photocopies of the front and back of the credit card used, a valid government issued photo ID of the card owner along with an authorization letter stating that the credit card holder has allowed the guest to use his/her card for the reservation. The hotel may cancel a confirmed reservation if a booking is suspected to be made using a fraudulent credit card.

People 11 years old and over stay for Rp 1,400,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
