IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that the property requires a credit card pre-authorisation upon check in. Cash is not an acceptable form of payment at this hotel, and you are kindly requested to present a valid credit card upon arrival.

For groups of 5 rooms or more, please note that special policies and fees apply.

As part of ongoing improvements to the Shoreline Hotel Waikiki, we will be painting the building's exterior and enhancing our lanais/balconies starting August 19, 2024 thru April 30, 2025. Lanais/balconies will be inaccessible during this time.

There will also be construction-related noise Monday - Friday from 8:00am to 5:00pm, with the potential for occasional Saturday work. Rest assured that every effort will be made to reduce disruptions during this period. The scope and schedule of work is subject to change without notice.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the hotel, with minor mentions of room maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.1/10 - The friendly and attentive staff received positive feedback, with a few minor complaints about noise levels and service timing.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's prime location near key landmarks, beaches, and public transport was highly appreciated by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests enjoyed spacious and comfortable rooms but noted issues like noisy entryways and saggy beds.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - Mixed feedback regarding amenities, with mentions of limited complimentary beach supplies and insufficient parking options.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some noting the excellent price and quality balance, while others found issues with valet parking and room size.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast services, with some mentions of limited variety and small room refrigerators.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the hotel's central location, friendly staff, and good value for money.
