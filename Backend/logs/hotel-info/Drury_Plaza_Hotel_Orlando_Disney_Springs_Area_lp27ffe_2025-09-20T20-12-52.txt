HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationLocated just 2.2 km from Disney Springs, Drury Plaza Hotel Orlando - Disney Springs Area offers a premium stay with modern amenities. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and free WiFi, providing a comfortable retreat after a day of adventure.Convenient FacilitiesGuests can unwind in the fitness centre or relax in the hot tub, ensuring a stress-free stay. The on-site business centre caters to corporate travelers, while the 24-hour front desk offers assistance in English and Spanish, prioritizing guest satisfaction.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in American and Italian cuisine at the hotel's restaurant, followed by cocktails at the bar. With proximity to popular attractions like Walt Disney World and Epcot, Drury Plaza Hotel is the perfect choice for a magical Orlando getaway.Book your stay now at Drury Plaza Hotel Orlando - Disney Springs Area for an unforgettable experience!
