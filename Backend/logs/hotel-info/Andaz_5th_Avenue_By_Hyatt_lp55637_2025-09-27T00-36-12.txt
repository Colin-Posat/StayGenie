HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Accommodation in the Heart of ManhattanPrime Location: Situated opposite Bryant Park and the New York Public Library, Andaz 5th Avenue offers a convenient stay in the bustling city of New York. Explore the vibrant surroundings and iconic landmarks just steps away from the hotel.Chic &amp; Modern Rooms: Experience a stylish stay in rooms adorned with modern décor, featuring amenities like a flat-screen TV, kitchenette, and luxurious en suite bathrooms with complimentary robes and toiletries. Upgrade to a suite with a balcony for a breathtaking view of the city.Exceptional Facilities: Indulge in the complimentary minibar with snacks and non-alcoholic beverages, stay active at the 24-hour fitness centre, conduct business seamlessly at the onsite business centre, and unwind at the speakeasy-style bar after a day of exploration.Book your stay at Andaz 5th Avenue-a concept by Hyatt for a luxurious and memorable experience in New York City.
