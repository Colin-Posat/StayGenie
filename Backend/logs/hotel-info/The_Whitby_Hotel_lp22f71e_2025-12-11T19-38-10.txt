HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in the individually designed rooms at The Whitby Hotel, featuring floor to ceiling windows and some with private terraces overlooking the iconic Manhattan skyline. Designed by Kit Kemp, the interiors offer a unique and stylish experience for guests.Dining and LeisureExperience the vibrant atmosphere of The Whitby Bar and Restaurant, relax in the Orangery or unwind in the book-lined drawing room. Stay active during your stay with access to the fully equipped gym, catering to all your fitness needs.Prime LocationLocated just steps away from Central Park and prestigious shopping destinations like Saks Fifth Avenue and Bergdorf Goodman, The Whitby Hotel offers a convenient and luxurious stay in upper midtown Manhattan. Explore nearby attractions such as the Museum of Modern Art, Rockefeller Centre, and St. Patrick’s Cathedral, all within walking distance.Book your stay at The Whitby Hotel for an unforgettable New York City experience!

GROUPS:

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for US$75 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for US$75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
