• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Rooms received mixed reviews, with some guests mentioning outdated facilities, dirty curtains, and malfunctioning air conditioning.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - While some guests appreciated the location and room size, others mentioned the lack of essential amenities like a microwave and fridge.
• Value for Money: 5/10 - Several guests felt that the hotel did not offer value for money due to outdated facilities, poor room conditions, and disappointments during the stay.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 5.8/10 - The overall guest experience was polarized, with some having positive experiences while others expressed major disappointments with the hotel's condition.
