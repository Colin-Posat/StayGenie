HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated a mere 20 metres from Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station, Hotel Orazia offers easy access to Rome's top attractions. Just a 10-minute walk from the Coliseum, guests can explore the city with ease.Comfortable AccommodationThe air-conditioned rooms with marble floors and modern amenities provide a comfortable stay. Enjoy the luxury of a satellite flat-screen TV, minibar, and a private bathroom. Some rooms even offer picturesque views of the square's gardens.Delicious BreakfastStart your day right with a delightful buffet breakfast at the hotel. Indulge in a spread of yoghurt, freshly baked croissants, cold cuts, and cheese, catering to both sweet and savoury preferences.Experience the perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and culinary delights at Hotel Orazia. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Roman getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:

PETS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 6 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
