The villa also features a charming terrace where you can unwind with a drink in hand, taking in the serene surroundings.Convenient LocationConveniently situated just 3.1 km from Elephant Cave and 4.4 km from Ubud Market, Villa Candi Kecil Tujuh offers easy access to popular attractions. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Villa Candi Kecil Tujuh in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Payment before arrival via bank transfer is required. The property will contact you after you book to provide instructions.
A damage deposit of IDR 6400000 is required. The host charges this 7 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for Rp 280,000 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for Rp 730,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
