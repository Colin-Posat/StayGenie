HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AccommodationsFresa Inn Kyobashi, established in 2011, offers contemporary rooms with Sealy beds, LCD TVs, and free Wi-Fi. The air-conditioned rooms boast a sleek design in neutral tones, complete with nightwear, a cooler box, and an electric kettle for convenience.Convenient LocationSituated just minutes away from subway stations, guests have easy access to popular areas like Ginza, Asakusa, Ueno, and Shibuya. JR Tokyo Station is also within walking distance, providing seamless travel connections.On-Site AmenitiesThe hotel provides a range of amenities including a lobby with complimentary coffee &amp; tea, a microwave, a coin-operated laundry, and a 24-hour front desk for guests' convenience. Indulge in a traditional Japanese breakfast at SUSHI BAR Kyobashi Gensei for a delightful start to your day.Book your stay at Fresa Inn Kyobashi now for a memorable and comfortable experience.
