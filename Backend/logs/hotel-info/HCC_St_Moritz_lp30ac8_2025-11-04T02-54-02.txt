HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Listed 19th-Century Building and Stylish AccommodationLocated just a short stroll from Gaudí’s Casa Batlló, the St. Moritz hotel offers a unique stay in a listed 19th-century building. The stylish and modern air-conditioned rooms provide a comfortable retreat, complete with free Wi-Fi and LCD satellite TV.Classic Décor and Mediterranean DiningThe Hotel HCC St. Moritz boasts a blend of classic and modern design, featuring its original central staircase and marble reception. Each room is elegantly decorated with a minibar, private bathroom, and complimentary toiletries. Guests can indulge in a varied buffet breakfast and savor Mediterranean cuisine at the St Gallen Restaurant.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsSituated just a few minutes' walk from Passeig de Gràcia and its Metro Station, the hotel offers easy access to Plaza de Catalunya and Las Ramblas. Don't miss the chance to explore Barcelona Port, located just over 1 km from the St. Moritz.Book your stay at the St. Moritz for a delightful blend of history, style, and convenience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for 100% of the adult rate per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
