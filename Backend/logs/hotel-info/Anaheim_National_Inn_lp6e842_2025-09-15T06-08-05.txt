Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3.2/10 - Guests had numerous complaints about cleanliness, including soiled bed sheets, dirty furniture, and pests like roaches.
• Service: 3.9/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with guests mentioning unhelpful front desk staff and lack of room readiness.
• Location: 7.5/10 - Despite other issues, guests appreciated the hotel's location in some reviews. It is a key positive aspect.
• Room Quality: 4.8/10 - Guests expressed dissatisfaction with room quality, mentioning old furniture, broken amenities, and cleanliness issues.
• Amenities: 4.7/10 - Amenities were lacking, with guests mentioning the absence of basics like hand towels and ice being charged.
• Value for Money: 4.5/10 - Many guests felt that the hotel did not offer good value for money, highlighting issues with cleanliness, amenities, and service.
• Food and Beverage: 5/10 - Feedback on food and beverage services was limited, with some guests mentioning dissatisfaction with the quality and range.
• Overall Experience: 3.9/10 - The overall guest experience was predominantly negative, with several guests expressing disappointment and frustration.
