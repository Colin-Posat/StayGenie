HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious All-Suite Hotel in ScottsdaleSpacious Suites with Separate Living Areas: Each suite at Embassy Suites by Hilton Scottsdale Resort offers a separate living area with cable TV and video games, along with convenient amenities like a refrigerator, microwave, and coffee-making facilities.Dining at Granada Bar &amp; Grill: Enjoy a delicious meal at Granada Bar &amp; Grill, conveniently located between the east and west complexes of the hotel.Fantastic Amenities: Guests can take advantage of the 2 outdoor heated pools with hot tubs, a gym, tennis court, and a 24-hour business centre. HOTEL POLICIES:

Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

INTERNET:

• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, although some maintenance issues were mentioned.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Amenities were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the gym and pool, but some guests missed spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
