HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Sea ViewsSustainable 3-star iCom Marina Sea View in Maafushi offers a serene escape with a garden, shared lounge, and private parking. Enjoy sea views from air-conditioned units equipped with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV and private bathroom. Indulge in delicious meals at the on-site restaurant and unwind with spa facilities and yoga classes.Family-Friendly Amenities and ActivitiesFamilies are catered to with indoor and outdoor play areas, baby safety gates, and a variety of wellness options. Parents can relax while children play, or enjoy activities like canoeing and bike rentals.Convenient Location near Bikini BeachLocated just 500 meters from Bikini Beach, iCom Marina Sea View is the perfect choice for a beach getaway. Book now for a memorable stay in Maafushi!
