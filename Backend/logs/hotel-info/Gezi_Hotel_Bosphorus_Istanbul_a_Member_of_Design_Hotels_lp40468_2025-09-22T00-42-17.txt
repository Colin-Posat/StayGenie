HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Stunning ViewsLocated in downtown Istanbul, the Gezi Hotel Bosphorus offers a luxurious stay with stunning views of the Bosphorus or the park from every room. The rooms feature wooden floors, marble bathrooms, mood lighting, and modern amenities including satellite LCD TVs and free WiFi. Some rooms even boast Bosphorus views from the shower, providing a unique and unforgettable experience.Relaxation and WellnessGuests can unwind at the hotel's spa, equipped with a fitness centre, Turkish bath, hot tub, sauna, aromatherapy showers, therapeutic water beds, and an ice fountain. Indulge in a relaxing massage or rejuvenate your senses in this tranquil oasis in the heart of the bustling city.Dining with a ViewExperience culinary delights at the Blu Restaurant &amp; Bar, offering Mediterranean cuisine and a friendly atmosphere with breathtaking views over the Bosphorus. Start your day with a rich breakfast buffet and savor delicious meals and trendy drinks while enjoying the panoramic scenery from the terrace.Book your luxurious stay at Gezi Hotel Bosphorus and immerse yourself in the beauty and comfort of Istanbul.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
