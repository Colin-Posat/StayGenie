HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Water Sports and ExcursionsSummer Villa is the perfect destination for water sports enthusiasts, offering activities such as diving, jet skiing, island hopping, and dolphin watching.Comfortable AccommodationThe basic rooms at Summer Villa ensure a comfortable stay with amenities like air conditioning, a fan, a dressing table, and a minibar. The attached bathrooms feature hot/cold shower facilities for your convenience.Convenient Facilities and DiningEnjoy free Wi-Fi access throughout the property and take advantage of services like airport transfers, a 24-hour reception, tour desk, and packed lunches. Indulge in a culinary experience at Summer Villa Garden Restaurant, serving fresh seafood, Western dishes, and Maldivian specialties.Book your stay at Summer Villa now for a memorable island getaway filled with adventure and relaxation.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please share your flight details with the property at least 72 hours before your arrival to secure your seat(s) for the transfer.

The property can be reached by a ferry and a speedboat.

Transfer is also available by speedboat. Hiring a speedboat takes only 30 minutes travel time. A 24-hour advanced request is required if guests wish to rent a speedboat.

The guesthouse is located on the local island of Maafushi. Government regulations require that guests do not wear bikinis or similar swimwear while in Maafushi. However, the guesthouse will arrange trips to picnic islands for guests where these restrictions do not apply.
