HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Clan Hotel Singapore by Far East Hospitality offers 5-star luxury with air-conditioned rooms featuring modern amenities like a coffee machine, minibar, and flat-screen TV. Guests can enjoy a comfortable stay with room service and a 24-hour front desk.Convenient LocationLocated just 700 meters from Singapore City Gallery and within walking distance of the city centre, guests can easily explore nearby attractions like Sri Mariamman Temple and Marina Bay Sands Casino. The hotel's proximity to Seletar Airport makes travel hassle-free.Dining and WellnessIndulge in a buffet or continental breakfast at the hotel's restaurant and bar, or unwind at the fitness center and terrace. Free WiFi, a safety deposit box, and quality bed linen ensure a relaxing stay at The Clan Hotel Singapore by Far East Hospitality.Book your stay now for a luxurious experience in Singapore!
