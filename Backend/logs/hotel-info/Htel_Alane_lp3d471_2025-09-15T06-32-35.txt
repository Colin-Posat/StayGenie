HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location:Hotel Alane is ideally situated just minutes away from Gare de l'Est and Gare du Nord Train Stations, offering easy access to Paris' top attractions and shopping centers.Comfortable Accommodation:Enjoy a peaceful stay in our soundproofed rooms equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries.Delicious Breakfast Options:Start your day right with a delicious continental breakfast served daily in our breakfast room, ensuring you have the energy to explore the nearby Opera Garnier and Pompidou Centre.Experience the perfect blend of convenience and comfort at Hotel Alane.
