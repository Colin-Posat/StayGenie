Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Mixed reviews regarding cleanliness, with some guests mentioning cleanliness issues while others found the property nice and clean.
• Service: 6.8/10 - Service received mixed feedback, with some guests experiencing excellent service from staff members and others mentioning rude or unhelpful behaviors.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Highly praised for its amazing location close to key attractions and public transport, offering convenience to guests.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Guests had varied experiences with room quality, with some finding the rooms fine while others reported issues like cigarette smoke smell and maintenance problems.
• Amenities: 5.8/10 - Received lower ratings due to complaints about the lack of amenities such as spa services and inconsistencies in the quality of facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.4/10 - Guests had mixed views on the value for money, with some feeling the services offered did not justify the price paid.
• Food and Beverage: 5.2/10 - Comments on the food and beverage services were negative overall, with complaints about breakfast quality, availability, and pricing.
• Overall Experience: 6.7/10 - While some guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel, others encountered multiple issues affecting their stay, leading to lower overall satisfaction.
