HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities:Unwind in style at Erboy Hotel with a terrace boasting stunning views and concierge services at your disposal. Make the most of complimentary Wi-Fi and gift shops/newsstands during your stay in this Mediterranean gem.Culinary Delights:Savor delectable meals at the hotel's 2 restaurants or opt for intimate dining with room service. Join daily complimentary receptions and treat yourself to a favorite drink at the bar/lounge. Don't miss out on the buffet breakfasts available daily for a fee.Convenient Location:Ideally situated in Istanbul, this upscale hotel is a short walk from iconic landmarks such as Basilica Cistern and Hagia Sophia. With a 24-hour airport shuttle available, exploring the city is a breeze. Book your stay at Erboy Hotel for an unforgettable experience.
