HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Warm and Intimate AtmosphereJust renovated, Hotel Le Daum offers a warm and intimate stay in the heart of Paris. The retro chic design and soft colors create a cozy ambiance for guests to relax in.Convenient LocationLocated in the 12th district, near Gare de Lyon and Bercy Village, the hotel provides easy access to key attractions. With bus stations and the Daumesnil Metro Station just a short walk away, exploring the city is effortless.Modern AmenitiesEnjoy modern comforts in every room, including a smart TV for entertainment, free wifi for staying connected, and air conditioning for a pleasant stay. The 24/7 reception and continental breakfast add convenience to your visit.Book your stay at Hotel Le Daum for a memorable Parisian experience!
