HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Ueno Okachimachi is perfectly situated just minutes away from both Shin-Okachimachi Subway Station and JR Okachimachi Train Station, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking for easy access to transportation options.Comfortable AccommodationsThe air-conditioned rooms at the hotel are equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, fridge, and electric kettle. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of nightwear, slippers, and free toiletries provided in each en suite bathroom, ensuring a comfortable stay.Convenient FacilitiesGuests can take advantage of the coin-launderette on site, borrow a trouser press or iron for in-room use, and make use of the free-use microwave in the lobby. Additionally, a simple breakfast buffet is served daily, offering a variety of warm bread and coffee to start the day off right.Book your stay at Hotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Ueno Okachimachi now for a seamless and comfortable travel experience.
