HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Art-Deco Building:Step into a piece of history at Hôtel Mercedes, housed in a charming 1930s Art-Deco building near the iconic Champs-Elysées and Arc de Triomphe.Elegant Rooms with Modern Amenities:Indulge in comfort in our air-conditioned rooms equipped with free WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and convenient tea/coffee facilities. Enjoy a relaxing stay with a private bathroom stocked with complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer.Convenient Services and Proximity to Attractions:Experience top-notch services including 24-hour reception and room service, along with the option of an airport shuttle. With Wagram Metro Station a stone's throw away and Parc Monceau within walking distance, Hôtel Mercedes offers the perfect blend of convenience and luxury.Book your stay at Hôtel Mercedes now for an unforgettable Parisian experience!
