HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Courtyards and Unique FeaturesThe Libertel Canal Saint Martin boasts 3 buildings interconnected by delightful courtyards, each with its own unique charm and features, creating a truly enchanting atmosphere for guests to enjoy.Delightful Views and Classic DecorThe air-conditioned rooms offer a classical décor and stunning views of either the ivy-clad buildings, Parisian facades, or the serene inner courtyard. Guests can relax in comfort while taking in the picturesque surroundings of the hotel.Convenient Location and Easy AccessSituated near Canal Saint-Martin and Park des Buttes Chaumont, with Metro Jaures just steps away, the hotel provides easy access to Paris' top attractions. Whether guests are exploring Belleville or Parc de la Villette, they are just a short distance from all the city has to offer.Book your stay at Libertel Canal Saint Martin now and experience the perfect blend of charm, comfort, and convenience during your Parisian getaway.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness, with multiple reviewers highlighting the peace, quiet, and overall cleanliness of the hotel.
• Service: 8.6/10 - The staff received high praise for being friendly, kind, and always available. A few negative reviews mentioned disrespectful attitudes at the front desk.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The central location near major train and metro stations was widely appreciated, with some exceptions citing noise issues.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and clean, but some negative comments included small room sizes and noisy environment.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - While some guests mentioned the convenient location and storage options as pros, others noted limitations like no safe, fridge, or proper soundproofing.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money, with some feeling it was great for the location, while others mentioned high prices for small rooms.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining services were generally positively mentioned, with a few suggestions for improvements in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience but there were also negative experiences involving noise issues, small rooms, and lack of apologies for service mishaps.
