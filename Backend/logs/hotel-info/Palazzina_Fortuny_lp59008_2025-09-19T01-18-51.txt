HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationPalazzina Fortuny, nestled in a quiet Venice street, presents elegant rooms featuring free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a minibar. Indulge in the marble bathrooms and a selection of tea varieties for a relaxing stay.Convenient LocationJust a short 5-minute stroll from the iconic Rialto Bridge, this hotel is a stone's throw away from the Fortuny Museum and the Sant’Angelo Vaporetto water bus stop. Explore Saint Mark's Square and the Rialto Bridge, both within a 5-minute walk.Charming SurroundingsImmerse yourself in the charm of Venice with a stay at Palazzina Fortuny. Book now to experience luxury and convenience in the heart of this mesmerizing city.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Palazzina Fortuny in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
From the Rialto Bridge, simply follow the signs for the Fortuny Museum.

Breakfast is not included in the room rate and must be requested separately. Breakfast will be served from 8.00 to 11.00.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
