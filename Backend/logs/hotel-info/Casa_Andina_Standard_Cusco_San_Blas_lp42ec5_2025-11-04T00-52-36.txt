This property does not admit children under 18 years of age without the company of their parents or duly accredited guardian, in accordance with Law No 30802. All children under the age of 18 must have ID or passport to be identified.

Dogs with a maximum weight of 18 kilos are allowed with an additional cost of US$ 49.00 per night per dog.

Only two dogs per room will be admitted. During your stay, your pet can make use, on loan, of: dog bed, food plate and mat. Restrictions and queries to consult directly at the hotel.

According to Peruvian tax regulations, Peruvian citizens (and foreigners who stay more than 59 days in Peru) must pay 18% sales tax. To be exempt from paying 18% sales tax, guests must present a copy of their immigration card and passport.

Both documents must be submitted in order to qualify for the exemption from payment of sales tax. Otherwise, guests must pay the indicated tax.

This tax applies in the case of rooms shared by guests who must pay sales tax and guests exempt from the payment of sales tax.

Foreign business travelers requesting a printed invoice will also be charged 18% sales tax regardless of the length of their stay in Peru. This tax is not automatically included in the total amount of the reservation.
