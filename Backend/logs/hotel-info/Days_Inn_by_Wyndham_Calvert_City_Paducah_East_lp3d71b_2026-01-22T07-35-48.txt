HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesThe Days Inn of Calvert City, conveniently situated off Interstate 24 and Interstate 69, offers a comfortable stay with an outdoor pool and complimentary WiFi access. The proximity to Kentucky Lake recreational areas adds to the allure of this property.Cozy Rooms with Modern FeaturesEach room at the hotel is equipped with plush bedding, a 39-inch flat-screen TV, microwave, refrigerator, and other essential amenities like an iron, hairdryer, and coffee maker. The inclusion of HBO and local HD channels ensures entertainment for guests.Relaxing Outdoor Spaces and Additional ServicesGuests can unwind in the barbecue facilities and picnic area, taking advantage of the 24-hour front desk and free truck parking. With nearby attractions like Kentucky Lake Motor Speedway and Venture River Water Park, there's something for everyone at Calvert City Days Inn.
