HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Apartments and Spa CentreLocated just 2 minutes from Sorbonne University, Résidence &amp; Spa Le Prince Régent offers spacious apartments and a luxury spa centre for an indulgent stay in Paris. Each air-conditioned unit features a flat-screen TV, fully equipped kitchen, and Nespresso coffee machine. Enjoy access to the wellness spa with an indoor pool, hammam, massages, and beauty treatments.Convenient Amenities and ServicesBenefit from a 24-hour front desk, daily room cleaning, and a continental breakfast served in-room. The property is surrounded by a vibrant selection of bars, restaurants, and entertainment options, ensuring a lively Parisian experience.Ideal Location near Prominent LandmarksWithin a 5-minute walk from Jardin du Luxembourg and Notre-Dame Cathedral, and with the Odeon Metro stop just 300 meters away, Résidence &amp; Spa Le Prince Régent provides a prime location for exploring the cultural treasures of Paris.Book now for an unforgettable Parisian retreat at Résidence &amp; Spa Le Prince Régent!
