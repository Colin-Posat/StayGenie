HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the epitome of luxury at The Sukhothai Bangkok with spacious rooms adorned with teakwood furnishing and Thai silks. Enjoy modern amenities like flat-screen TVs, iPod docks, and Nespresso coffee makers for a truly indulgent stay.Pampering Spa ServicesIndulge in relaxation at Spa Botanica with a variety of massage and facial treatments to rejuvenate your body and mind. Keep fit at the 24-hour fitness centre, or enjoy a friendly game of tennis and squash during your stay.Culinary DelightsSavor award-winning Thai cuisine at Celadon Restaurant, known as the “Best Restaurant in Bangkok”. Don't miss the champagne Sunday brunch and signature chocolate buffet. For a taste of Italy, head to La Scala Restaurant for an exquisite dining experience.Book your stay at The Sukhothai Bangkok for an unforgettable blend of luxury, relaxation, and culinary excellence.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform The Sukhothai Bangkok in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for THB 3,414 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 6/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 8.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
