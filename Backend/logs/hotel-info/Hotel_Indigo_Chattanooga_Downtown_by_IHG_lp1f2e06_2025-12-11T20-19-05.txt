• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions on the value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was high relative to the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Overall, breakfast and restaurant services were positively reviewed, though some guests desired more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
