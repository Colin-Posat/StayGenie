HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsInfinity Pool and Rooftop BarThe hotel boasts an infinity pool where guests can relax and enjoy breathtaking views of Singapore's skyline. Additionally, the rooftop bar offers a perfect setting to unwind with a refreshing drink while taking in the city's beauty.Modern Amenities and ServicesEach air-conditioned room is equipped with a 49-inch HD TV, floor-to-ceiling windows, and a luxurious en suite bathroom. Guests can also take advantage of free WiFi, 24-hour front desk services, a fitness centre, and delectable dining options at the in-house restaurant and rooftop bar.Convenient LocationSituated in the vibrant Novena District, the hotel is within walking distance to shopping malls and the Novena MRT Station, providing easy access to explore the city. With Orchard Road just a short drive away, Courtyard by Marriott Singapore Novena is the perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at Courtyard by Marriott Singapore Novena!
