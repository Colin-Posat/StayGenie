HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Villas with Stunning ViewsNestled in an 18-hectare valley landscape overlooking the Ayung River, Royal Pita Maha offers a serene retreat celebrating Bali's rich art and culture. Each tastefully decorated villa features a seating area, dressing room, satellite TV, and a luxurious bathroom.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by the outdoor pool, pamper yourself with a spa treatment, or enjoy a BBQ session amidst the lush surroundings. Guests can also book a soothing massage or explore the area with assistance from the tour desk.Convenience and ComfortWith free Wi-Fi, in-room entertainment, and amenities like a minibar and coffee/tea maker, Royal Pita Maha ensures a comfortable stay. Take advantage of services like vehicle rentals, currency exchange, and in-room dining for a hassle-free experience.Book now for an unforgettable Bali getaway at Royal Pita Maha!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Royal Pita Maha in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note that the hotel's staff will contact guests directly regarding payment instructions.

Upon check-in, customers are required to present the confirmation page, and the credit card used to pay for the booking. The holder of the credit card used to pay for the booking should be part of the traveling party.

If you are making payment using another cardholder's credit card, kindly provide the following to the hotel prior to your arrival:
- Authorization letter with cardholder's signature
- Copy of the cardholder's card (front and back of card with cardholder's signature)
- Copy of the credit card holder's passport.

The information may be sent to the hotel via e-mail or facsimile. Please note that hotel may contact the cardholder for verification purposes. The hotel will only accept credit card for deposit payment to secure the booking instead of debit card.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 1,100,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 1,100,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
