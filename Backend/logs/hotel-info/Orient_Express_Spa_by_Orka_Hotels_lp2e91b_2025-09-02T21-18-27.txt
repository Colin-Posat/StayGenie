Enjoy the convenience of room service and air conditioning during your stay.Convenient Services and Nearby AttractionsTake advantage of car rental services, a tour desk, and a 24-hour reception for all your travel needs. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
