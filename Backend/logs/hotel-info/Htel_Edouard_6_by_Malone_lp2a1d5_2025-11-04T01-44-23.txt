HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious and Convenient AccommodationAt Hotel Hôtel Edouard 6 by Malone in the vibrant Montparnasse district of Paris, experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience. The air-conditioned rooms are designed for your comfort, featuring soundproofing, an LCD TV with satellite channels, and complimentary WiFi access.Delicious Dining OptionsStart your day right with a delightful continental breakfast served in the charming dining room or opt for the convenience of in-room dining for a small additional fee. Indulge in a variety of delectable options to kickstart your Parisian adventures.Prime Location for ExploringConveniently situated just a short stroll away from the Montparnasse-Bienvenüe Metro and train station, Hotel Edouard 6 by Malone offers easy access to iconic attractions like Boulevard Saint Germain des Prés, the Luxembourg Garden, and the Montparnasse Tower, all within 1.5 km.
