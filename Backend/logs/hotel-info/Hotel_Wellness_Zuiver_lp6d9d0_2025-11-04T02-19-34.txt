IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), this property is taking steps to help protect the safety of guests and staff. Certain services and amenities may be reduced or unavailable as a result.
A damage deposit of EUR 300 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that payment for this property is made via a secure online link. The property will get in contact shortly after booking with further instructions on how to access this link and make the payment for your booking.

Please note that due to safety reasons, access to the fitness facilities and spa is only granted to people older than 18 years.

Please note that from September 01, 2020 the property will organize extra swimwear days at Zuiver. From this date there will be special swimwear days on Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays. Swimwear is mandatory on these days.

Please note that a bathrobe, towels and slippers are mandatory in the spa. Guests have the possibility to bring their own, or rent them on site against a surcharge.

Please note that the spa will be closed on:
- January 1st (New Year's Day)
- King's Day (27 April)
- Christmas Day (25 December)
- Second Day of Christmas (26 December)

Please note that use of the wellness facilities will incur an additional charge of €29.95 EUR per person, per day.
