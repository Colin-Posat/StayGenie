HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesRiviera Marriott Hotel La Porte De Monaco offers a range of upscale amenities including an outdoor swimming pool, fitness center, and access to private beaches. The air-conditioned rooms are elegantly furnished with modern comforts such as flat-screen TVs, minibars, and tea/coffee facilities.Gourmet DiningIndulge in regional delicacies at the onsite restaurant Bolinas or sip on cocktails at the lounge and poolside bar. The hotel provides a culinary experience that caters to every palate.Convenient LocationSituated just 10 meters from Monaco's border, the hotel is a stone's throw away from the Marina of Cap d'Ail. With Monte-Carlo Casino a short drive away and a free shuttle to Monaco's center, Riviera Marriott Hotel La Porte De Monaco ensures a seamless travel experience.Ready to experience luxury by the French Riviera? Book your stay now at Riviera Marriott Hotel La Porte De Monaco.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and charges are applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  EUR 25 per  day.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 45 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
