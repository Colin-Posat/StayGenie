Notice of Extensive Luxury Room Renovation from 2nd October 2023

Renovation period:
From 2nd October 2023 to 14th December 2024, for 15 months, a full comprehensive refurbishment of guest rooms and corridors, spanning from 7 to 31 floors.

Renovation work:
The noisiest work will occur from Monday to Saturday, specifically between 11:00 AM and 2:30 PM.
On Sundays and during extended public holidays like Golden Week (GW), New Years Eve and Obon, there will be no construction work.
Restaurants and bars will remain fully open, but we anticipate some noise in the lobby, restaurants and bars between 11:00 and 14:30 from April onwards.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for ¥9,322 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  JPY 3900 per  day.
