HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Balinese-Style Villas with Private PoolThe Lovina offers a stunning beachfront location with Balinese-style villas, each equipped with a private pool and breathtaking ocean views. The spacious villas feature an open concept design, with select units offering full kitchen facilities. Guests can enjoy modern amenities like flat-screen TVs, DVD players, and eco-friendly water bottles for unlimited refills.Exciting Activities and RelaxationGuests can indulge in complimentary snorkelling gear, canoeing, and stand-up paddleboarding. Daily Yoga sessions, Balinese dancing classes, and pampering massages at the spa ensure a rejuvenating stay. The resort also boasts a gym, laundry services, and the option to arrange area shuttles or day trips.Delicious Dining Options and EntertainmentSavour delectable meals at Heliconia Restaurant, Pizzeria, or Beach Bar while enjoying pool and beach views. For a special experience, request a barbecue party. With its convenient location near Krisna Water Park and Banjar Hot Spring, The Lovina is the perfect tropical getaway.To book your stay at The Lovina and experience the ultimate beachfront retreat, visit our website now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for Rp 150,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 450,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
