HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the perfect retreat at The Juniper Hotel, an exquisite Art Deco gem nestled in the heart of Banff National Park.Recreational OasisUnwind in our hot tub or take in breathtaking views from the serene terrace and lush garden. Our hotel offers a range of recreational opportunities designed to help you relax and rejuvenate.Culinary DelightsSavor delightful meals at Juniper Bistro and Lounge, where our chefs create mouthwatering dishes using fresh, local ingredients. End your day with a refreshing cocktail at our stylish bar/lounge.Comfort and ConvenienceOur 52 spacious guestrooms feature luxurious pillowtop beds, premium bedding, and modern amenities such as complimentary Wi-Fi and satellite programming. Enjoy elegant private bathrooms complete with deep soaking tubs and quality toiletries.Discover the enchantment of The Juniper Hotel and book your unforgettable stay today!

• Food and Beverage: 8.1/10 - The on-site restaurant was praised for its quality and ambiance, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Guests had a positive overall experience, with friendly staff, clean rooms, and beautiful mountain views being highlights.
