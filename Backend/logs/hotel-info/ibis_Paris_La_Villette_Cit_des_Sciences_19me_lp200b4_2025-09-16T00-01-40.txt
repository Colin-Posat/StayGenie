Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.6/10 - Guests frequently commended the hotel for its cleanliness and tidy rooms.
• Service: 6.2/10 - Service was a mix of experiences, with attentive staff in some cases and language barriers or unfriendly staff in others.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its proximity to public transport and attractions.
• Room Quality: 4.9/10 - Numerous guests complained about the small size of the rooms and soundproofing issues, although a few reported big rooms.
• Amenities: 5.5/10 - The available amenities received mixed reviews, with some guests mentioning inadequacies like the lack of water or an iron.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was too high for the offered facilities.
• Food and Beverage: 6.5/10 - Guests generally found the breakfast good, but some had issues with dinner variety and drink quality.
• Overall Experience: 6.3/10 - Guests had a wide range of experiences, but many would recommend the hotel for work-related stays rather than personal trips.
