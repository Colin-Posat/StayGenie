HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Fulfilling StayDesigned for comfort and convenience, Arthouse Hotel in New York offers a range of enticing amenities. Stay active at the fitness center or unwind on the terrace, while staying connected with complimentary Wi-Fi. Enjoy delicious Italian cuisine at Serafina, and sip your favorite drink at the bar.Convenient ServicesFrom a 24-hour business center to dry cleaning services, our hotel ensures all your needs are met efficiently. Let our 24-hour front desk assist you throughout your stay, making it hassle-free and memorable.Prime LocationSituated in Manhattan, our hotel is a short walk from iconic landmarks like Central Park and the American Museum of Natural History. Easy access to popular attractions like Lincoln Center and Columbus Circle makes exploring the city a breeze.Book your stay at Arthouse Hotel New York City for an unforgettable experience in the heart of the Big Apple.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.

* Contactless check-in and contactless check-out are available.
