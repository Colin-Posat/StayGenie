HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Spa and Recreational Amenities:Indulge in massages, body treatments, and facials at the spa, or relax in the outdoor pool and sauna for a rejuvenating experience.Exceptional Dining and Entertainment Options:Dine at Legong Terrace, enjoy snacks at the cafe, and unwind at the bar or poolside bar with your favorite drink, all while experiencing impeccable service.Convenience and Comfort:Experience comfort in individually decorated guestrooms with private pools and fireplaces. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and enjoy satellite programming and DVD players for entertainment.Don't miss the chance to experience luxury by the river at Puri Wulandari a Boutique Resort and Spa. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* This property's policy is to refuse certain bookings for the purpose of group events or parties, including pre-wedding stag/bachelor and hen/bachelorette parties.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 5 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
