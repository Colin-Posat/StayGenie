HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the charm and comfort of the Argonaut Hotel, where San Francisco's picturesque Fisherman’s Wharf meets modern elegance.Recreational FacilitiesUnleash your adventurous spirit with our 24-hour fitness center and bicycles available for rent, making it easy to explore the beautiful surroundings.Dining ExcellenceSavor delectable seafood at our on-site restaurant, Blue Mermaid, where you can enjoy alfresco dining and unwind with drinks at the bar/lounge. Room service is also available for those cozy nights in.Premium ComfortRelax in one of our 252 well-appointed rooms featuring premium bedding, iPod docking stations, and high-speed internet access, ensuring a restful stay filled with entertainment and comfort.Don't miss out on the opportunity to create unforgettable memories—book your stay at the Argonaut Hotel today!
