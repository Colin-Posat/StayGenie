HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations in the Heart of SingaporePrime Location: Situated in Singapore's civic district, The Capitol Kempinski Hotel offers easy access to major attractions like Raffles City Shopping Mall and City Hall MRT Station.Top-Notch Amenities: From the outdoor saltwater swimming pool to the fitness centre, guests can relax and rejuvenate during their stay. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 6 years old and over stay for S$ 59.95 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
