HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooftop Terrace with Stunning ViewsFairmont Monte Carlo offers a 4-star experience with a rooftop terrace overlooking the Circuit de Monaco. Guests can enjoy the Mediterranean Sea and the Casino of Monte Carlo from this prime location.Diverse Dining Options and Onsite ActivitiesWith two outdoor pools, 4 restaurants and bars, a shopping mall, hairdressers, fitness centre, and spa, there's something for everyone. Kids can have a blast at the July-August kids club while parents indulge in traditional Japanese cuisine at Nobu Restaurant or French dishes at Lobby Lounge Bar &amp; Restaurant.Unwind in Style with Exclusive AmenitiesEach air-conditioned room features a private marble bathroom and balcony with sea, Circuit de Monaco, or garden views. Guests can relax at La Vallée Spa, work out at the fitness centre, or take a dip in the rooftop pool with panoramic Riviera views.Book now to experience the epitome of luxury at Fairmont Monte Carlo.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Fairmont Monte Carlo in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that when booking 5 rooms or more, different policies may apply. Please contact the property for further details.

During the Monaco Grand Prix, guests can enjoy free access to the terrace where they can see the races.

Please note that Nikki Beach is open from April to the end of September or mid-October. The Horizon Deck Restaurant and Champagne Bar are open annually from April to the end of September or mid-October for breakfast and dinner. From October to April it offers breakfast every day and the dinner service is available from Monday to Saturday.

Guests are required to show the credit card used to make the reservation at check-in. If you do not have the credit card, please ensure you contact the property in advance to let them know.

Please note that only people aged 18 and older can check in.

Please note that the following fees will apply on top of the total amount of the reservation for early check-in and late check-out:
- check-in between 08:00 and 11:00: an amount equivalent to 50% of the total amount of the booking will be charged
- check-in between 11:00 and 15:00: EUR 100
- check-out between 12:00 and 16:00 EUR 200
- check-out after 16:00: an amount equivalent to 100% of the total amount booking will be charged

Sunbeds are only for hotel’s guests, upon availability, and can be booked at EUR 35 per person, per day.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 85 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 75 per  day.
