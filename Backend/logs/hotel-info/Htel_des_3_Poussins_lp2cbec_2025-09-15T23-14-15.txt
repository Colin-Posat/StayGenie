HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Hotel in the Heart of ParisConvenient Location: Situated in the 9th district, Hôtel 3 Poussins is a stone's throw away from Montmartre, Opera Garnier, and major department stores, making it the perfect base for exploring Paris.Comfortable Accommodation: The spacious guest rooms, each uniquely decorated, come equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, satellite TV, and lift access, offering views of the serene patio or courtyard.Exceptional Services: Enjoy a daily continental breakfast in the charming attic breakfast room, take advantage of the 24-hour front desk for all your needs, relax in the shared living room with a library, and opt for the convenient laundry service.Book now for an unforgettable Parisian experience at Hôtel 3 Poussins!
