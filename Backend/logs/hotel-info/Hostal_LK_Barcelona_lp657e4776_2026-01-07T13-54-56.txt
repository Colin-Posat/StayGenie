HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and ConvenienceLocated just 500 meters from Barcelona's AVE Train Station, Sants Estació, Hostal LK Barcelona offers easy access to public transportation with several Metro Stations within a short walk.Modern and Comfortable RoomsEnjoy chic and modern rooms at Hostal LK Barcelona featuring wooden floors, air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and work desks. Stay comfortable with heating and make use of the private bathroom with complimentary toiletries.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can benefit from the property's fridge, microwaves, and kettle. Additionally, luggage storage is available for convenience. Explore nearby attractions like Plaza Espanya, Las Arenas Shopping Centre, and the Magic Fountain just minutes away.Book your stay at Hostal LK Barcelona for a comfortable and convenient experience.
