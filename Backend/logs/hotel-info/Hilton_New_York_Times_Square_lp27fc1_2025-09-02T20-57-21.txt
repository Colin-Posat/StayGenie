HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsLocated in the heart of New York City, Hilton New York Times Square offers air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities. Each room features a TV with cable channels, a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries, and some even boast stunning city views.Exceptional Dining OptionsGuests can indulge in a delicious American or à la carte breakfast at the hotel's restaurant, which also caters to vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free diets. Additionally, the bar and room service provide convenient dining choices throughout the day.Convenient LocationSituated near iconic landmarks like Times Square and Bryant Park, this 4-star hotel is perfect for travelers wanting to explore the best of the city. With a fitness center, shared lounge, and 24-hour front desk, Hilton New York Times Square ensures a comfortable and memorable stay.Book now for an unforgettable experience at Hilton New York Times Square!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and costs  USD  15 per  24 hours.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 95 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for US$50 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
