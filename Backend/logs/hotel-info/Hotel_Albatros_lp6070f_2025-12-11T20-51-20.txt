HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Albatros, nestled in a serene location just a short stroll from Zermatt's center, offers a range of elegant rooms and suites. From spacious deluxe rooms to charming attic suites boasting stunning Matterhorn views, each accommodation comes with a balcony and plush bathrobes for ultimate comfort.Relaxation &amp; WellnessUnwind at the hotel's inviting bar after a day of mountain adventures, or pamper yourself at the on-site spa center. Indulge in a rejuvenating sauna session, steam bath, or soak in the hot tub. Complete your relaxation in the tranquil relaxation room, ensuring a peaceful stay.Modern AmenitiesStay connected with complimentary wireless internet throughout the property, and enjoy the convenience of slippers provided in every room. The family ambiance and attention to detail make Hotel Albatros a perfect choice for your Zermatt getaway.Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and comfort at Hotel Albatros!

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for CHF 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Adults  stay for CHF 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
