HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Rooms with Free InternetHotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Shinjuku, just a 7-minute walk from JR Shinjuku Station, offers chic guest rooms with free wired internet. The modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and fridges ensure a comfortable stay.Vibrant Location with Dining OptionsLocated in the bustling Kabuki-cho area, guests can explore numerous dining and entertainment venues nearby. Shinjuku Gyoen Park and Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building are also a short walk away, perfect for sightseeing.Convenient Services and FacilitiesGuests can enjoy free WiFi in public areas, leave luggage at the front desk post-check-out, and access copy/fax services for a fee. Additionally, the hotel provides drink vending machines for added convenience.Book your stay at Hotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Shinjuku for a stylish and convenient experience.
