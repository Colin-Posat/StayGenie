HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsExperience the blend of history and elegance at Hotel Villa San Lorenzo Maria, a former noble residence turned into a cozy hotel. With rooms offering air conditioning and private bathrooms, enjoy a comfortable stay in the heart of Rome.Garden Oasis and Buffet BreakfastRelax in the garden oasis or savor a delicious buffet breakfast in the serene surroundings. Start your day right with a variety of options to choose from, setting the perfect tone for exploring the city.Convenient Location and Helpful StaffLocated in the bustling area of San Lorenzo, surrounded by charming eateries, our hotel offers easy access to Termini station and the historic center.
