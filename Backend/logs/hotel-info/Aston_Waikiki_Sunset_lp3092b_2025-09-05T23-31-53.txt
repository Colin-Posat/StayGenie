CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness positively, although some reviews indicated the need for better cleanliness in certain areas.
• Service: 7.6/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although a few guests mentioned rudeness among staff members.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Guests praised the hotel's location for its proximity to key landmarks and ease of access to public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally well-received for their comfort and amenities, although some maintenance issues were reported.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities, particularly the pool and gym, but mentioned a lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Food services received mixed feedback, with some guests suggesting improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
