HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Swim, Sunbathe, and Enjoy the ViewHotel Gardenia offers a perfect blend of relaxation and convenience. With a swimming pool and a rooftop terrace overlooking the Bay of Naples, guests can unwind and soak up the stunning views.Eco-Friendly ComfortAs an Ecoworldhotel, Gardenia Hotel ensures a stay that's not only comfortable but also environmentally conscious. The refurbished property boasts private balconies in each room and eco-friendly policies, providing a guilt-free getaway.Delightful Dining and LeisureIndulge in a delicious American-style buffet breakfast each morning, featuring homemade pastries. After a day of exploration, unwind in the garden, TV lounge, or nearby fitness club.Experience the charm of Sorrento at Hotel Gardenia. Book your stay now for a memorable vacation.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:

• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
