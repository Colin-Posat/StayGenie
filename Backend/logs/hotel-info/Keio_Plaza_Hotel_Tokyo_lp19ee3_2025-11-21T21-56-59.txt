HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spectacular Views and Convenient LocationExperience panoramic views of the Shinjuku skyline at Keio Plaza Hotel Tokyo, just minutes away from Shinjuku Train Station. Enjoy free WiFi and diverse dining options during your stay.Luxurious AmenitiesRelax in comfort with flat-screen TVs, minibars, and en suite bathrooms with complimentary toiletries. Japanese-style yukata robes and slippers are provided for a touch of elegance.Diverse Dining and Leisure OptionsIndulge in Japanese, Italian, or Chinese cuisine at the hotel's restaurants. Unwind at Sky Lounge Aurora with breathtaking city views or savor premium sake at Amanogawa Sake Bar. Take advantage of the fitness center, outdoor pool, convenience store, and coworking space for a well-rounded stay.Book your stay at Keio Plaza Hotel Tokyo now for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Shinjuku.
