HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortConvenient Location: Hotel Al Gazzettino, a 17th-century building, is just a short walk from St Mark’s Square, Rialto Bridge, and the Doge’s Palace.Elegant Rooms: Featuring Baroque furniture, Murano-glass lamps, free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and satellite TV, the rooms blend historic charm with modern amenities.Personalized Service: Enjoy an Italian breakfast and dine in the in-house restaurant where reservations are welcomed. Let the staff assist you with city tours, public transport tickets, and event bookings.Experience Venice at its finest at Hotel Al Gazzettino. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Reception is closed after 18:00. Guests requesting to check-in after 18:00 are kindly requested to contact the property in order to receive instructions for self check-in prior to arrival, including the access code.

Late check-in from 18:00 until 24:00 costs extra EUR 30.

After Midnight until 8 am costs EUR 50.

Please note that breakfast is served from 08:30 until 10:00.

The property has no elevator.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for € 100 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for € 100 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
