HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Colonial-Style Hotel in the Heart of ParisConvenient Location: Situated just 1 km from Montparnasse Tower and Jardin de Luxembourg, Istria Montparnasse offers easy access to the Latin Quarter and is a mere 100 meters from Raspail Metro station.Comfortable Accommodation: Enjoy modern amenities in every room such as free WiFi, a minibar, and tea/coffee facilities. Unwind with satellite TV and refresh in the private bathroom stocked with complimentary toiletries.Exceptional Facilities: Benefit from a 24-hour reception, a lift for convenience, and express check-in services. Indulge in a daily breakfast spread before exploring nearby attractions like Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Louvre Museum.Book your stay at Istria Montparnasse for an unforgettable Parisian experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Late check-out carries an extra charge of EUR 20 per hour.

A security deposit of EUR 70 is required upon arrival. It must be paid by credit card. The refund should be made on the day of your departure. The security deposit will be fully refunded to your credit card, if no damage has been found by the establishment.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
