HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Private Beach Area and Rustic CharmImmerse yourself in the serene beauty of Playa Canek Boutique Eco Hotel, nestled along the pristine beaches of Tulum. Experience the perfect blend of rustic elegance and modern comfort in our bright rooms, some offering breathtaking sea and garden views. Relax on your private balcony or sway in a hammock while enjoying the sea breeze.Fresh Seafood Dining and Exciting ActivitiesIndulge in delectable fresh seafood prepared over an open fire by our skilled staff. Explore a variety of dining options in Tulum town or savor international cuisine. Unwind in our garden, try water sports, or embark on cycling and snorkeling adventures. The possibilities are endless!Convenient Amenities and Nearby AttractionsBenefit from our airport shuttle service, shared lounge, and tour desk for a seamless stay. With proximity to Tulum Archaeological Site and easy access to Playa del Carmen, your Mexican getaway is bound to be unforgettable. Book your stay at Playa Canek Boutique Eco Hotel today for a truly memorable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Playa Canek Beachfront Eco Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of USD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected as a cash payment. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full in cash, subject to an inspection of the property.
A 50% deposit is required to secure your reservation, payment can be made with card through our Paypal account. The hotel does not accept American Express credit cards and payments by credit card (Visa /Mastercard) will aplly 5% fee.

Parking subject to availability.

Car is not needed, prime location near bars, shops and restaurants. Parking not available but possible, bicycle available at the hotel. Beer, wine and any kind of alcoholic beverage is forbidden at Playa Canek Boutique Eco Hotel, the property has a restaurant and a beach bar on site.

To help the planet and take care of the environment, solar panels are used in the Hotel.  For that reason, air conditioning is available from 8:00 PM until 08: 00 AM.

We are eco friendly: towels and blankets will change every 3 days.

Transportation to the hotel can be booked for an extra charge.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 4.8/10 - Guests were highly dissatisfied with the cleanliness, citing stained linens, sandy floors, clogged drains, and lack of proper cleaning in rooms.
• Service: 7.6/10 - While some guests appreciated the friendly and attentive staff, there were complaints about air conditioning timings, unhelpful front desk responses, and delayed room readiness.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location was considered a major strength, with close proximity to shops, restaurants, beach clubs, and key attractions in the area.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with positives like spacious penthouse accommodations and a bohemian feel. However, issues such as broken amenities and dirty pool were mentioned.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Limited information provided in reviews about specific amenities, with some references to beach clubs and good vibes, but also complaints about restricted air conditioning times.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Opinions varied on value for money, with mentions of reasonable pricing, good service at beach clubs, but also concerns about false expectations from website images.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and beach bar experiences, but critiques on kelp presence, adjacent construction noise, and limited variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the location, friendly staff, and vibrant atmosphere despite some minor setbacks.
