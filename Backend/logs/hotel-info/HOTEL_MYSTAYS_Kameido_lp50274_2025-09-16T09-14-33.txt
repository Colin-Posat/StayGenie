HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated just a 4-minute walk from JR Kameido Train Station, HOTEL MYSTAYS Kameido offers air-conditioned rooms with a kitchenette and free WiFi. Guests can easily access popular areas like Akihabara and Shinjuku from nearby Kameido Station.Well-Equipped RoomsIdeal for long-term stays, guestrooms feature a mini-refrigerator, microwave oven, and induction stove tops. Enjoy a private bathroom with free toiletries and the option to borrow kitchenware and tableware free of charge.Dining OptionsIndulge in Japanese and Western-style buffet dishes for breakfast or visit the Japanese and French restaurants on-site. For added convenience, there are also convenience stores and dining choices within a 5-minute walk.Book your stay at HOTEL MYSTAYS Kameido now to experience comfort and convenience in Tokyo!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Housekeeping service is not included. Guests can request this service in advance, which is available at an additional cost.

For guests staying 7 nights or longer, towels and amenities can be replaced only upon request. Additional charges apply.
