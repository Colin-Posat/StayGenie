HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Haussmannian Hotel in Central ParisLuxurious Accommodations: Enjoy fully air-conditioned guest rooms with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and soundproofing for a comfortable stay. Some rooms even offer a flat-screen TV and all come with private bathrooms equipped with a hairdryer.Convenient Location: Opposite Gare du Nord Train Station, with Gare de l’Est Metro Station just 600 meters away, explore Paris with ease. Direct access to iconic landmarks like the Louvre Museum and the Latin Quarter awaits.Delightful Dining: Start your day with a delicious continental breakfast served in the unique arched stone cellar. Don't miss the chance to experience the vibrant Montmartre district just a short 15-minute walk away.Book your stay at New Hôtel Gare Du Nord now to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience in the heart of Paris.
