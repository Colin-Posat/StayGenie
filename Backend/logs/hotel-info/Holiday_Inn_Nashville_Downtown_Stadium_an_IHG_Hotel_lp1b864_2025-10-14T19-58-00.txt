HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Nashville City CentreThis Nashville hotel is conveniently situated in the heart of the city, just 1.6 km from the popular Bridgestone Arena. Guests can easily access major sports and concert events while being surrounded by the vibrant downtown atmosphere.Luxurious Amenities and ServicesEnjoy a relaxing stay with top-notch amenities such as an indoor salt water pool, complimentary hot buffet-style breakfast, and free WiFi throughout the hotel. Each room is equipped with modern conveniences like a flat-screen TV, alarm clock, and work desk for a comfortable experience.Convenient Facilities and Nearby AttractionsMake the most of your stay with access to a business centre, fitness facility, and free hourly transfer service to the Music City Center. Explore nearby attractions including Nissan Stadium and Top Golf, all within walking distance from the hotel.Book your stay at the Clarion Hotel Downtown Nashville - Stadium for a memorable experience in Music City!
