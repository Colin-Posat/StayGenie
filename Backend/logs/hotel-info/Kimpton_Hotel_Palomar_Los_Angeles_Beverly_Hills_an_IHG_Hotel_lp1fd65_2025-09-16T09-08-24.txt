HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Spa Baths and City ViewsIndulge in the newly renovated suites at Kimpton Hotel Palomar, offering spa baths, stunning city views, and separate living rooms. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, minibar, and luxurious free toiletries. Enjoy in-room spa services and 24-hour room service for the ultimate relaxation experience.Outdoor Pool and Wine Tasting HourRelax by the outdoor swimming pool or unwind at the hosted daily wine tasting hour at this stylish 100% non-smoking hotel in Los Angeles. The perfect way to enjoy your stay and mingle with other guests.Convenient Location with Free Bicycle RentalsLocated just 2 miles from Beverly Hills and a short drive to UCLA, Kimpton Hotel Palomar offers a prime location for exploring the city. Take advantage of free bicycle rentals to visit nearby attractions like Santa Monica State Beach.Book your stay at Kimpton Hotel Palomar for a luxurious and unforgettable experience in Los Angeles.
