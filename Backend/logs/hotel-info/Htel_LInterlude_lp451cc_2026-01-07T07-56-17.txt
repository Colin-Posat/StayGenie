HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location in ParisLocated in the vibrant 12th arrondissement, Hôtel L'Interlude is nestled between Place Daumesnil and the serene Bois de Vincennes park. Enjoy easy access to the metro stop Michel Bizot, connecting you to the heart of Paris in minutes.Modern Rooms with Comfortable AmenitiesExperience renovated rooms featuring modern conveniences such as a flat-screen TV, telephone, and en suite facilities. Relax in comfort with double-glazed windows and some rooms offering views of the tranquil interior courtyard.Complimentary Wi-Fi and Buffet BreakfastStay connected with free unlimited Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.
