• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was a bit high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with positive comments on the quality of service and atmosphere, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff, cleanliness, and convenient location.
