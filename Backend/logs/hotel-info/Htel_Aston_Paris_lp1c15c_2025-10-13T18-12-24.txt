HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Paris’s Grands Boulevards AreaThis boutique hotel, nestled in a traditional Haussmanian building, offers a modern stay just 1 km from Opéra Garnier. The air-conditioned guestrooms at Hotel Aston provide a comfortable retreat with free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and coffee facilities. Some rooms even feature charming balconies.Delicious Breakfast and 24-Hour ReceptionStart your day with a buffet breakfast under an arched stone ceiling or enjoy it in the privacy of your room. The hotel's 24-hour reception is ready to assist with ticket reservations for shows and excursions, ensuring a seamless stay.Convenient Access and Nearby AttractionsWith Grands Boulevards Metro Station a short walk away, exploring Paris, including iconic sights like The Eiffel Tower, is effortless. Additionally, Galeries Lafayette department store is within easy reach for a delightful shopping experience.Book your stay at Hotel Aston now for a memorable Parisian getaway.
