HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Apartments with Bay ViewsPerched high above Ghirardelli Square, The Fairmont Heritage Place Ghirardelli Square offers luxurious apartments with stunning San Francisco Bay views. Each apartment boasts a fireplace, high-end entertainment system, and enclosed glass shower. The exposed brick walls provide natural soundproofing and a touch of historic charm. Select units even offer breathtaking bay views.Concierge Services and Gourmet DiningGuests can enjoy personalized grocery shopping, chef services, and 24-hour concierge assistance. Indulge in room service from top-notch restaurants like Palette and McCormick &amp; Kuleto's Seafood Restaurant. The hotel's elevators conveniently open to Ghirardelli's famous Ice Cream and Chocolate Shop, as well as local boutiques and restaurants.Prime Location for ExplorationSituated just minutes from Fisherman’s Wharf and the Golden Gate Bridge, guests can easily explore the city's top attractions. The dog-friendly Crissy Field is a short bike ride away, perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. Plus, all Residence Reservations include two complimentary valet parking spots.Experience luxury and convenience at The Fairmont Heritage Place Ghirardelli Square – book your stay now for an unforgettable San Francisco getaway.
