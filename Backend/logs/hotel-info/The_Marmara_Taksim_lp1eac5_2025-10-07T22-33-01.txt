HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsExperience luxury at its finest at the Marmara Hotel overlooking Taksim Square. Enjoy spacious rooms with city views, cable TV, and free WiFi. Relax in the outdoor pool deck or pamper yourself at the Turkish hammam spa.Culinary Delights and Dining OptionsIndulge in a variety of dining experiences at the hotel's restaurants and bars. From Tuti Restaurant's wide selection of dishes to Panorama Restaurant's panoramic city views, there's something for every palate. Don't miss out on the rich handmade chocolates at the Chocolate Shop.Relaxing Spa and Wellness FacilitiesUnwind and rejuvenate at the traditional Turkish hammam or treat yourself to a massage. The hotel's spa area offers a sauna, hot tub, solarium, and a gym with a view. Perfect for a relaxing getaway in the heart of Istanbul.To book your luxurious stay at the Marmara Taksim and enjoy all these exceptional amenities, reserve your room now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 4 years old to 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
