HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated just minutes away from Chicago's Midway Airport and a short distance from the city center, Hampton Inn Chicago-Midway Airport offers easy access to both the airport and downtown attractions.Complimentary AmenitiesEnjoy a range of complimentary amenities including free Wi-Fi, cable TV, ironing facilities in every room, and a daily continental breakfast. Guests can also take advantage of the free airport shuttle service and 24-hour transfer to the Orange Line, ensuring a hassle-free stay.Explore with EaseWith the nearby railway station providing convenient transportation, guests can effortlessly explore the vibrant city of Chicago and its surrounding areas.Book your stay at Hampton Inn Chicago-Midway Airport for a comfortable and convenient experience.
