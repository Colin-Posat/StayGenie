HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsLondon Lodge, nestled in the heart of Kensington, offers elegant rooms with satellite TV, private bathrooms, and a range of amenities including a writing desk, minibar, and individually controlled heating and air conditioning.Convenient LocationJust a short stroll from Earl’s Court Underground Station, our hotel provides easy access to top attractions like Hyde Park, Knightsbridge shops, The Natural History Museum, and Holland Park, making it an ideal choice for exploring the best of London.Charming Café BarUnwind at Stephanie’s Café Bar with its own patio, offering a delightful setting to enjoy a full English breakfast or choose from a selection of drinks. Don't miss the chance to experience comfort and convenience at London Lodge.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  GBP 45 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for £45 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for £45 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
