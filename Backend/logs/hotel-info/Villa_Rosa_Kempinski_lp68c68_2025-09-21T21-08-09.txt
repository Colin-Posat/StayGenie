HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with City ViewsThe Villa Rosa Kempinski, located just a short drive from Nairobi’s CBD, offers elegantly furnished rooms with stunning city views. Each room comes equipped with a minibar, desk, and flat-screen TV, with some even featuring balconies for a breath of fresh air.Culinary Delights and Dining OptionsIndulge in a variety of dining experiences at Villa Rosa Kempinski, from local delicacies to international cuisine. Enjoy the flavors of Italy at Lucca Restaurant or grab a quick bite at Deli at Cafe Villa Rosa.Relaxation and Wellness AmenitiesUnwind in style at the spa with 9 treatment rooms, sauna, and outdoor pool. Keep fit at the fully equipped fitness center. With Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and Nairobi National Park nearby, your stay is both convenient and luxurious.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Villa Rosa Kempinski!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for US$27 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
