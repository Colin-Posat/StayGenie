• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and public transport was highlighted positively by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were generally spacious and comfortable, with some complaints about noise and room quality.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the gym and pool, but some guests wished for more services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding it high for the services, while others thought it was reasonable for the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received mixed reviews, with some guests desiring more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the friendly staff, cleanliness, and location.
