HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the epitome of luxury at W New York Times Square, where each room features a plasma TV, terry cloth bathrobes, and Bliss bath amenities. Enjoy a stocked minibar, light snacks, and room service for added convenience.Gourmet DiningIndulge in a culinary journey at the on-site restaurants, including Dos Caminos, Izi, and Blue Fin sushi. Sip on signature cocktails at The Living Room Bar or savor Japanese izakaya-inspired dishes at Izi, paired with a selection of boutique Japanese and American whiskeys.Convenience and StyleFrom currency exchange to a tour desk and a high-end fashion and beauty shop, W New York - Times Square offers a seamless blend of convenience and style. Explore nearby attractions like the Empire State Building, Central Park, and the Metropolitan Museum of Art.Book now for an unforgettable stay at W New York - Times Square.
