HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Property with Modern AmenitiesHotel Peter and Paul, nestled in New Orleans, offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern comforts. The 4-star hotel boasts air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each featuring a private bathroom and a flat-screen TV. The shared lounge, terrace, and restaurant provide perfect spots to unwind after a day of exploring the city.Convenient Services and ActivitiesWith a 24-hour front desk, private parking, and tour organizing services, guests can expect a seamless stay. Additionally, the hotel offers an ATM for added convenience. For those looking to explore, cycling around the vibrant city is a must-do activity.Prime Location for ExploringLocated just a few kilometers from major attractions like Morial Convention Center and Mercedes-Benz Superdome, Hotel Peter and Paul is the ideal base for discovering the best of New Orleans.Ready to experience the perfect blend of history and luxury?
