HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Free WiFiHotel Sonya, located in central Rome in front of the Opera House, offers a quiet retreat with free WiFi throughout the property. Enjoy being just 50 meters from the Viminale Palace.Comfortable Rooms with AmenitiesRelax in air-conditioned rooms at the Sonya Hotel, each equipped with cable TV, a private bathroom, and a convenient tea/coffee maker for your comfort.Convenient Services and Nearby AttractionsStart your day with a variety of breakfast options and get recommendations from our friendly reception staff on the best nearby restaurants. Explore Rome easily with the hotel's close proximity to Termini Train Station and enjoy complimentary city maps and ticket-booking services.Book your stay at Hotel Sonya now and experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience in the heart of Rome.
