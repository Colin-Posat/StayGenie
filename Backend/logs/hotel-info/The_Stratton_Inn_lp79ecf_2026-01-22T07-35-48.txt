HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Comfortable StayThe Stratton Inn, nestled in Princeton, offers a convenient stay just a stone's throw away from Prizer Point Public Use Area and Lake Barkley State Park. Guests enjoy the ease of free private parking and a shared lounge, making it a perfect choice for those exploring the area by car.Modern Amenities and Cozy RoomsExperience comfort in the air-conditioned rooms equipped with free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. The allergy-free property ensures a pleasant stay, with attentive staff providing bed linen and towels for a hassle-free experience.Explore and UnwindEngage in outdoor activities like hiking and cycling in the picturesque surroundings of Princeton. After a day of adventure, return to The Stratton Inn and unwind in the serene garden or seek local insights from the helpful 24-hour front desk staff.Ready to experience comfort and convenience at The Stratton Inn?
