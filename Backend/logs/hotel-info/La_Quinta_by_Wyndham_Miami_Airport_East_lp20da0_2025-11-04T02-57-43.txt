Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Most guests praised the cleanliness of the rooms, although a few instances of bugs and dirty floors were reported.
• Service: 7.1/10 - Mixed feedback on service quality, with mentions of attentive staff, but also complaints about slow service and unfriendliness.
• Location: 8.3/10 - Highly appreciated location, especially for being close to the airport, downtown, and shopping centers.
• Room Quality: 6.3/10 - Rooms were comfortable but showed signs of age and maintenance issues, such as old tubs and chipped paint.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Limited amenities reported, with a general satisfaction level but some complaints about the lack of complementary water and spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed opinions on value for the price paid, with some guests feeling it was reasonable while others found it overpriced for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast services were generally positive, though some guests desired more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 6.5/10 - Guests' overall experiences varied, with significant mentions of issues with room quality, service, and value for money.
