HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Chic Accommodation in AmsterdamLuxurious AmenitiesHotel De Hallen features non-smoking rooms equipped with modern conveniences like a flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and free WiFi. Guests can unwind with complimentary tea and coffee facilities, along with a well-stocked minibar. The private bathrooms are stocked with premium toiletries and a hairdryer for added comfort.Convenient LocationSituated in the vibrant Oud-West district, the hotel is a stone's throw away from the bustling Dam Square and a short walk from the charming market at Ten Katestraat. With a tram stop just 500m away, exploring the city is a breeze, with direct connections to Central Station and popular attractions like Leidseplein.Book your stay at Hotel De Hallen for a sophisticated and memorable experience in Amsterdam.
