HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant heart of Miami at Courtyard by Marriott, where comfort meets convenience.Recreational Paradise Enjoy our refreshing outdoor pool and fully-equipped fitness center, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the hotel and relax in our stylish common area.Dining Delights Savor delightful meals at our Bistro, offering a vibrant atmosphere alongside a welcoming bar/lounge. For a quick pick-me-up, visit our cozy coffee shop/café, where full breakfasts are available to fuel your day.Business &amp; Events Our hotel features a 24-hour business center and flexible meeting spaces designed to cater to your business needs. Experience seamless check-in and check-out for your convenience, making it the ideal place for corporate gatherings.Your perfect Miami getaway awaits—book your stay today!
