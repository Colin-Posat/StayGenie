HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious White Glove ServiceThe Pierre, A Taj Hotel, stands majestically across from Central Park, offering guests a taste of white glove luxury service. With its iconic copper mansard roof gracing the New York City skyline, this hotel is a beacon of sophistication and elegance.Exquisite Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in the culinary delights at Perrine, the hotel's restaurant, serving up seasonal modern American cuisine and daily specials inspired by Pierre's culinary classics. For a more laid-back experience, head to Two E, the bar/lounge, for traditional afternoon tea and live jazz nights.Family-Friendly Amenities and Exclusive ExperiencesYoung travelers are in for a treat at The Pierre, with a range of child-friendly amenities including special treats, movies, toys, and museum passes on demand. The hotel's proximity to the Central Park Zoo and Museum Mile makes it an ideal choice for families seeking unforgettable experiences.Experience luxury and charm at The Pierre, A Taj Hotel – book your stay now to immerse yourself in unparalleled hospitality and sophistication.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
