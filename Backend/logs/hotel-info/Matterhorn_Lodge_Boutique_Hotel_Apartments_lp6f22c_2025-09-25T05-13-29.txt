HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Classic Alpine Style Decor:Hotel Matterhorn Lodge, opened in 2012, offers a charming Alpine ambiance in the heart of Zermatt. The classic Alpine decor creates a cozy and inviting atmosphere for guests to relax and unwind.Wellness Area with Saunas:Indulge in relaxation at the hotel's wellness area, boasting three different saunas. After a day of skiing or exploring the picturesque surroundings, guests can rejuvenate their senses and soothe their muscles in the tranquil sauna facilities.Convenient Amenities and Services:From free WiFi and flat-screen TVs to a delicious daily breakfast, the Matterhorn Lodge ensures a comfortable stay for every guest. Additionally, with a hotel bar, ski storage room, and complimentary shuttle service to the train station, convenience is at the forefront of the hotel's offerings.Experience the allure of Zermatt at Hotel Matterhorn Lodge. Book your stay now for a memorable Alpine getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continute to Zermatt by train or taxi.

If you are travelling with children, please inform the property in advance of how many are arriving and include their age.

Please also note that extra beds have to be requested before arrival. Contact details are stated in the booking confirmation.

Please note that the wellness centre is open from 14:3–21:00 daily.
