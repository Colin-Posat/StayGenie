IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Smoking is only permitted in the designated smoking area.

Please note that child rates are applicable to children 12 years and younger and adult rates are applicable to children 13 years and older. Please contact the property for more details.

Please note that the maximum occupancy of the room is 3 persons and this cannot be exceeded under any circumstances. An extra bed will be prepared for a 3rd guest at an additional charge, only when the booking is made for 3 people. The additional charge for the extra bed applies to all adults and children.

Please note, the following age restrictions apply to the on-site spa and wellness centre:
Swimming pool: Guest must be 4 years and older
Other pool and bath facilities: Guests must be 12 years and older
Fitness and locker rooms: Guests must be 16 years and older
Spa treatment: Guests must be 14 years and older
