IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Enjoy all of the amenities below for a daily charge of $25.00 per room. Daily Resort Charge includes: Scheduled shuttle service to SeaWorld, Aquatica, Discovery Cove, Universal Studios & Disney/Magic Kingdom theme parks; two rounds of mini-golf; two daily I-Drive trolley tickets to shopping, dining & entertainment; two daily bottled waters; 15% off at hotel gift shop.

Breakfast included rate plan is a Buffet Breakfast and is for each registered adult and child age 5 and under who share the same room.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 30 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 4/10 - Several guests reported cleanliness issues such as bed bugs, dirty rooms, and cockroaches.
• Service: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on service quality, with some guests praising staff friendliness while others mentioned issues with room cleaning and response to complaints.
• Location: 8.6/10 - Highly praised for its convenient location near major attractions and amenities.
• Room Quality: 5/10 - Numerous complaints about room conditions, including leaking AC, dirty rooms, and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - General satisfaction with amenities, but parking availability was a significant concern for many guests.
• Value for Money: 5.2/10 - Several guests felt the hotel did not meet the expected standards based on the price paid.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Positive feedback on the quality of breakfast and restaurant offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests enjoyed their stay and would consider returning, despite some specific issues mentioned in other categories.
