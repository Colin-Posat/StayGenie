HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Hotel in Bercy DistrictThis modern Campanile in Paris's lively Bercy district offers soundproofed rooms just minutes away from Bercy Village's attractions.Comfortable AccommodationThe Hotel Campanile Paris-Bercy Village provides air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV, tea/coffee facilities, and private bathrooms with a hairdryer and shower.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy an American breakfast buffet, evening bar service, and meals available for order. With a 24-hour front desk and free Wi-Fi, your stay is made hassle-free.Book your stay at Hotel Campanile Paris-Bercy Village for a comfortable and convenient experience in the heart of Paris!
