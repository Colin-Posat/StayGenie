HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience the charm of Chiang Mai at Maninarakorn Hotel, where locally handcrafted furniture meets modern amenities like free Wi-Fi and cable TV in every room.Relaxation and WellnessIndulge in traditional Thai massage at Kinnari Spa, unwind in the sauna and steam rooms, or stay active at the jogging track - all within the hotel premises.Culinary DelightsSavor a fusion of Asian and Western flavors at ZENZA Restaurant, where children under 8 enjoy a complimentary American breakfast. Don't forget to grab a freshly brewed coffee at the Coffee Shop before exploring Chiang Mai.Book now and elevate your stay at Maninarakorn Hotel, just minutes away from the vibrant Night Bazaar and Thapae Gate.
