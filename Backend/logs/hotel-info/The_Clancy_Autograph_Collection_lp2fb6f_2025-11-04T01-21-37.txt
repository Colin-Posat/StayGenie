HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests mostly praised the cleanliness of the rooms, although some mentioned issues with carpet cleanliness.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service was generally well-received, but some guests had complaints about communication and hidden fees.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was highlighted as a major positive aspect, with easy access to key landmarks and attractions.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the spacious rooms and comfortable beds, though some mentioned maintenance issues and unpleasant odors in certain rooms.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests praising the facilities while others expressed disappointment with the lack of inclusive services and clarity on additional fees.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Guests had varied opinions on value for money, with some feeling the prices were too high for the offering, particularly during events, while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Breakfast received mixed reviews, with some guests finding it limited and repetitive for the price, while others enjoyed the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hotel's cleanliness, location, and room quality, though issues with service and pricing were noted.
