HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience unparalleled luxury at Bangkok Marriott Marquis Queen’s Park, your oasis in the heart of Bangkok.Unwind in StylePamper yourself at our luxurious spa offering an array of rejuvenating treatments, including massages, body treatments, and facials. Dive into our stunning outdoor pool, relax in the hot tub, or enjoy a session in the sauna for the ultimate relaxation experience.Gastronomic DelightsSavor international cuisine at Goji Kitchen + Bar, among our four exquisite dining options. Whether you choose to indulge in a buffet breakfast or a delightful meal through our 24-hour room service, culinary satisfaction is always at your fingertips.Convenient AmenitiesWith 1,388 elegantly furnished guest rooms, complimentary Wi-Fi, and a range of amenities including a business center and event facilities, your comfort and convenience are our priorities. Free self-parking and a 24-hour airport shuttle make travelling a breeze.Book your stay today to enjoy the magic of Bangkok!

* Cashless payment methods are available for all transactions.
