HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesVibe Hotel, nestled in Sydney CBD, offers a luxurious experience with a rooftop pool, fitness centre, and free WiFi. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with satellite TV, tea/coffee facilities, and a private bathroom.Convenient LocationSituated just a short stroll from Oxford Street, guests can explore popular shopping and dining options. Hyde Park is a mere 3 minutes away, while Darling Harbor and the Sydney Opera House are within a 30-minute walk.Culinary DelightsIndulge in Mediterranean specialties at Storehouse, the hotel's on-site restaurant. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Vibe Hotel Sydney in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of AUD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that a 1.5% surcharge applies for payments made with credit card.

Please note bookings over 7 nights will only receive a weekly housekeeping service.
