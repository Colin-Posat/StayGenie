HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Conveniently Close to DisneylandLocated just 4 blocks from Disneyland, Residence Inn Anaheim Resort Area/Garden Grove offers a perfect retreat for families and travelers looking to experience the magic of Disney.Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in the outdoor pool, palm tree-rimmed courtyard, and free daily breakfast in full American buffet style. The rooms feature free WiFi, fully equipped kitchens, 27’’ TVs with HBO and Disney Channel, work desks, and coffee makers.Family-Friendly FeaturesWith a fitness center, outdoor wading pool for children, and Disneyland ticket sales on-site, guests of all ages can enjoy a comfortable and convenient stay.Book now to make your magical Disneyland vacation unforgettable at Residence Inn Anaheim Resort Area/Garden Grove!
