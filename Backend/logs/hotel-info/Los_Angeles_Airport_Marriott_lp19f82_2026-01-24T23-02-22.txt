IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note:

- Not all rates include breakfast. If you book a breakfast-inclusive rate, breakfast buffet is offered for 1 adult.

- Select rooms feature Concierge Lounge access. Access is limited to two (2) adults. Additional guests are permitted for a fee of USD $50 per day.

- The hotel will authorize an incidental hold of USD $100 per night to cover any additional charges incurred during the stay.

- Bed type for all rooms is on a request only basis and cannot be guaranteed. Please contact Los Angeles Airport Marriott with further information.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
