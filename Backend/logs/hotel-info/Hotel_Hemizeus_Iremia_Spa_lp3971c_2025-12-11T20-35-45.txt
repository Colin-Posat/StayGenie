IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.

Please call the hotel from the free phone at the hotel information board by the train station. The hotel can pick you up for free between 8 am and 6 pm (after 6 pm only if the porter is available.)Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.

PETS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for CHF 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 13 years old stay for CHF 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Children from 14 years old to 16 years old stay for CHF 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
