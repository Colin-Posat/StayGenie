HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 12 and older are allowed.
Children 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for US$100 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was highlighted as convenient and close to key landmarks, making it a favorable choice for travelers.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests generally found the rooms spacious and comfortable, but some reported issues like noise, outdated amenities, or maintenance problems.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests enjoying the gym and breakfast, while others mentioned the lack of modern facilities like fridges or quality of TV channels.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the offered services, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and food services received positive feedback overall, with some guests appreciating the variety and taste.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the friendly staff, great location, and comfortable beds.
