HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Ski-in Alpenlodge Superior in ZermattPanoramic Views and Wellness: Enjoy breathtaking views of the Matterhorn from the sunny slope location. Free access to the modern Zen spa with sauna and steam bath, as well as a relaxation room overlooking the mountains.Elegant Alpine Rooms: Relax in stylish rooms with free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and DVD players. Indulge in comfort with bathrobes and hairdryers provided for your convenience.Convenient Amenities and Services: Start your day with complimentary coffee, tea, and cakes. Unwind in the cosy lounge bar by the fireplace or on the terrace with stunning village and Matterhorn views. Easy access to ski facilities and transportation options.Book now for an unforgettable mountain getaway at Alpenlodge Superior!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please call the hotel as soon as you are in the train/taxi from Täsch to Zermatt, so that the hotel can organize the transportation to the hotel.

Please note that the spa area is open from 4 p.m. until 9 p.m.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 5 years old stay for CHF 20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
