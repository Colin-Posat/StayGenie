HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesAd Lib Hotel Bangkok offers a salted outdoor pool, a fitness centre, and free Wi-Fi throughout the property. Guests can also enjoy a free Tuk Tuk shuttle service to nearby attractions, making transportation a breeze.Convenient LocationSituated in the heart of Bangkok, Ad Lib Hotel Bangkok is adjacent to Bamrungrad Hospital and just a short drive from Central World. With BTS Nana and Nana Plaza within walking distance, guests have easy access to shopping, dining, and entertainment options.Exceptional ServicesThe hotel provides 24-hour front desk assistance, room service, a tour desk, and a laundry service for utmost convenience. Additionally, the on-site restaurant offers a variety of Thai and International dishes, available 24 hours a day.Book your stay at Ad Lib Hotel Bangkok for a seamless blend of luxury, convenience, and exceptional service.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Ad Lib Hotel Bangkok in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of THB 7000 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
When travelling with pets, please note that an extra charge of THB 1,000 per pet, per night applies and has to pay directly to Hotel on arrival date.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for THB 1,200 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
