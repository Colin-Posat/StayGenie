IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
A damage deposit of USD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note: Property offers Bi-weekly complimentary housekeeping for stays over 30-nights.
Please note: Daily housekeeping is available for an additional fee. Additional fees are as follows: 120 USD per day for a one-bedroom suite,150 USD a day for a two-bedroom suite and 200 USD a day for a three-bedroom suite.
Please note: Property will not accept cash as a form of payment. A one night deposit of room and tax guarantees each reservation.
Please note: A 100 USD per night incidental hold will be required upon arrival.
Please note: Guest must be over 21 years of age for check-in.
