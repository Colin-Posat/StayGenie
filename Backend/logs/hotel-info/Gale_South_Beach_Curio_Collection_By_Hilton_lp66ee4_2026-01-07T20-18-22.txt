HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Beachfront LuxuryLocated in Miami, the Gale South Beach hotel offers a prime beachfront location with a range of luxurious amenities for guests to enjoy.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in Italian-inspired cuisine at Dolce Italian or sip on classic cocktails at The Regent Cocktail Club. The hotel offers a vibrant dining and entertainment scene for guests to unwind and socialize.Convenience and ComfortWith a rooftop pool, fitness center, and 24-hour reception, Gale South Beach ensures convenience and comfort throughout your stay. The spacious guest rooms feature modern amenities like a 55-inch flat-screen TV and minibar for a relaxing retreat.Experience the ultimate Miami getaway at Gale South Beach - book your stay now for a memorable beachfront escape.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note the mandatory resort fee is for 2 guests and is due at check-in.

Daily Resort Charge will be added to the room rate and includes: Guest internet access, beach club access at Shelborne Resort with 2 lounge chairs and towels, beach cruiser bikes, 2 bottle waters daily, 24-hour book collection access, complimentary manager's toast daily, 15% discount at Pizza Bar and Bodega Taqueria.

Additional resort fees apply for each extra guest. Contact property for details.

Please note if paying in cash, the hotel must receive USD 500.00 for incidentals for the 1st night and USD 150.00 per night for any additional nights.

If paying with a credit card, the hotel requires a USD 100.00 hold per night for incidentals. Contact property for details.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
