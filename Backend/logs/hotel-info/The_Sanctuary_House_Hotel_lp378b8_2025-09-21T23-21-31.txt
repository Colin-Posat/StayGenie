HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Westminster DistrictSituated in the heart of Westminster, The Sanctuary House Hotel offers easy access to iconic landmarks like Westminster Abbey, Houses of Parliament, and Big Ben within minutes.Charming Victorian Architecture and Modern AmenitiesExperience the elegance of a grand Victorian hotel combined with modern comforts in each air-conditioned room. Enjoy a flat-screen TV, work desk, and complimentary tea and coffee facilities, all designed for a comfortable stay.Traditional Pub Dining ExperienceIndulge in classic British dishes at the Fuller’s traditional pub located on-site. From deep-filled pies to fish and chips, savor home-cooked meals paired with a selection of fine wines and award-winning ales.Book your stay at The Sanctuary House Hotel for a delightful blend of historic charm and contemporary convenience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for £35 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for £5.50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for £35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
