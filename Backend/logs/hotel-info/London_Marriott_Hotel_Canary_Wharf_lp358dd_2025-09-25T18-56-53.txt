IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
A damage deposit of GBP 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
For security purposes, guests are required to present the same credit card used to make their reservation at the time of check-in. Alternatively the hotel reserves the right to ask for an alternative method of payment.

Please note that guests can order room service via the Marriott app.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  GBP 24 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for £70 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, although a few mentioned minor issues.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Staff friendliness and helpfulness were highlighted as positive aspects of the service, with a few complaints about check-in delays.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel's proximity to key attractions and public transport was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and well-equipped, though some guests raised concerns about certain design elements.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with mixed reviews mentioning the gym and pool positively and the lack of spa services negatively.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions varied on the pricing, with some guests feeling it was too high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast offerings and restaurant services received generally positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
