HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views of Chao Phraya RiverLocated just a 5-minute walk from Saphan Taksin BTS Station, Centre Point Silom offers breathtaking views of the Chao Phraya River. The elegant suites with hardwood flooring and fully equipped kitchens provide a luxurious stay for guests.Convenient Amenities and ServicesGuests can enjoy the convenience of an on-site restaurant serving a variety of Thai and international dishes, as well as free Wi-Fi throughout the property. The hotel also features a sauna, outdoor saltwater swimming pool, and a well-equipped fitness centre for relaxation and recreation.Ideal Location for ExploringSituated near local restaurants, entertainment venues, and MBK Shopping Mall, Silom Centre Point is the perfect base for exploring the vibrant city of Bangkok. With easy access to Suvarnabhumi Airport, this hotel is a great choice for travelers.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at Centre Point Silom!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Centre Point Silom in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of THB 3000 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
The refundable deposit of THB 1,000 per room per night and THB 2,000 per room per night for Two-Bedroom Executive Suite will be collected upon check-in.

Please present the same credit card used to guarantee your booking when checking-in at the hotel.
Cardholder name must also match with guest’s identification document or guest may be asked to pay with alternative method.

Any type of extra bed or baby cot is upon request and needs to be confirmed by the hotel.

Please be informed that the pool hours are 06:00-20:00 hrs.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 3 years old and over stay for THB 1,413 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, although some minor maintenance issues were reported.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Staff were generally friendly and helpful, with a few instances of delayed room readiness and noise complaints.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's prime location near key landmarks, public transport, and shopping centers was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, with positive comments on bed quality, although some design flaws were pointed out.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Guests appreciated the gym and pool facilities, while minor complaints were made about noise and limited pool hours.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews were received on the pricing, with some guests feeling it was slightly high for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with a few suggestions for improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
