HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Connected to Florida Mall and Close to Theme ParksThis Orlando hotel is conveniently connected to the Florida Mall, offering easy access to over 270 stores. Situated just 17 km from Walt Disney World and Disney Springs, it's the perfect choice for both shopping and theme park enthusiasts.Dining Options and Outdoor PoolEnjoy the convenience of 3 on-site dining options, including a bar with a varied beverage menu and a bistro serving delicious American cuisine. Unwind by the outdoor pool with a hot tub after a day of shopping or exploring the nearby theme parks.Spacious Rooms and 24-Hour Fitness CenterRelax in spacious rooms equipped with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, refrigerator, and coffee machine. Stay active at the 24-hour fitness center, complete with free weights and cardio machines for your workout needs.Experience the best of Orlando with a stay at the Florida Hotel and Conference Center.
