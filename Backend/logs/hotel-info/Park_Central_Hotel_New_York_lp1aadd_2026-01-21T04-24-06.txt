HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience luxury and convenience at the Park Central Hotel New York, where a world of culture awaits just beyond your doorstep.Unmatched LocationNestled steps away from Carnegie Hall and Broadway, our hotel places you in the vibrant heart of New York City. Enjoy easy access to iconic landmarks like Central Park and Times Square, making your stay unforgettable.Exceptional AmenitiesStay fit at our 24-hour health club or rent a bicycle to explore the city. Enjoy relaxed dining at our on-site restaurant or grab a quick bite at our café, and unwind with your favorite drink at the bar/lounge.Comfort and StyleRetreat to one of our 761 elegantly designed guestrooms, all equipped with premium bedding, LCD televisions, and complimentary Wi-Fi.
