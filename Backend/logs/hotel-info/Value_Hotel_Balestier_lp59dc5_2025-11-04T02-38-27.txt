HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Free Wi-FiValue Hotel Balestier offers modern and cosy rooms with flat-screen Internet TV and complimentary Wi-Fi. Each room is equipped with convenient amenities like a tea/coffee maker and a hairdryer. The private bathroom features a hot shower and toiletries for a comfortable stay.Convenient Facilities and ServicesThe hotel provides 24-hour front desk luggage storage and laundry services upon request for guests' convenience. Start your day with a delicious breakfast at the dining area, with the option of outdoor seating available.Accessible Location with Shuttle ServicesConveniently located just 1 km from Novena MRT Station, Hotel Value Balestier is a short drive from Orchard Road and the Central Business District. Complimentary shuttle services to Novena MRT Station make exploring the city easy for guests.Book your stay at Value Hotel Balestier for a comfortable and convenient accommodation experience.
