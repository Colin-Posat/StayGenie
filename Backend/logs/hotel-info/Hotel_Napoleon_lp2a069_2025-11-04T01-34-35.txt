HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsLuxurious AccommodationsIndulge in air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen TVs, minibars, and private bathrooms stocked with free toiletries. Some rooms even feature a charming balcony for a delightful view.Culinary DelightsSavor Italian specialties paired with fine wines at the hotel's restaurant. Start your day with a sumptuous breakfast buffet offering a variety of sweet and savory options, all complemented by authentic Italian coffee.Prime LocationConveniently situated in front of Vittorio Emanuele Metro and near Termini Station, Hotel Napoleon provides easy access to explore nearby attractions like Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica and the iconic Coliseum.Book now to experience the perfect blend of history and luxury at Hotel Napoleon!

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 25 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 17 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 17 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
