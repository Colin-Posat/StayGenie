HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Getaway with Private PoolNestled just a short drive away from Ubud's main attractions, Jungle Retreat by Kupu Kupu Barong offers a luxurious escape with a private outdoor swimming pool, modern amenities, and complimentary shuttle service to the city center.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsIndulge in beautifully decorated rooms equipped with air conditioning, premium TV channels, and a minibar. Relax in the seating area or pamper yourself in the en suite bathroom with bath and shower facilities. The attentive staff ensures a comfortable stay with daily housekeeping and laundry services.Dining Excellence and Tour AssistanceSavor Indonesian delights at Tamiang Restaurant or Japanese delicacies at Oishi Tei Restaurant. Explore the island with the help of the tour desk or simply unwind with room service. Your unforgettable jungle retreat awaits at Jungle Retreat by Kupu Kupu Barong.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 700,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for Rp 700,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
