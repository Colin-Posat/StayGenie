HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the perfect blend of luxury and tranquility at Hotel Lomas del Volcán, nestled in the breathtaking mountains of La Fortuna.Spa BlissIndulge in our full-service spa, where soothing massages and rejuvenating facials await to pamper your senses and provide the ultimate relaxation.Culinary DelightsSavor international cuisine at Restaurante Hojarasca while enjoying garden views, or unwind with drinks at our poolside bar. Room service is also available for those who prefer to dine in style.Convenient AmenitiesOur hotel offers complimentary Wi-Fi, business facilities, and event spaces to cater to all your needs. Each of our 70 elegantly decorated guestrooms features private balconies, pillowtop beds, and modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay.Book your escape to paradise at Hotel Lomas del Volcán today!
