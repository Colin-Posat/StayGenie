HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Venetian ViewsHotel Alcyone offers a unique stay in an 18th-century building, providing picturesque views of Venetian rooftops. Enjoy breakfast in a charming dining room overlooking a typical Venetian canal.Elegant Venetian DecorExperience the perfect blend of tradition and modernity in our compact bedrooms. Adorned with Murano-glass lamps and Venetian-style decor, each room features modern amenities such as air conditioning and TV.Convenient LocationSituated just 2 km from Venezia Santa Lucia Train Station, our hotel offers easy access to transportation. Explore the city effortlessly with the Rialto water-bus station a 3-minute walk away and St. Mark's Square just a 5-minute stroll.Ready to immerse yourself in the beauty of Venice? Book your stay at Hotel Alcyone now.

PARKING:

INTERNET:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
