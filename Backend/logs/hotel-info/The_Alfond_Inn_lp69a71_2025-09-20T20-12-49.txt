HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsLocated in Winter Park, Florida, The Alfond Inn offers elegant rooms with modern amenities including free WiFi, a refrigerator, and a coffee maker. Each room also features a cable TV and iPod docking station for entertainment.Convenient FacilitiesGuests can enjoy a range of facilities such as an outdoor pool, fitness centre, and business centre. The hotel also provides laundry services, a daily maid service, and a 24-hour front desk for added convenience during your stay.Dining ExperienceIndulge in delicious southern-style cuisine at Hamilton’s Kitchen for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Don't miss the weekend brunch at the on-site restaurant, which also boasts a full-service bar for relaxing evenings.Experience luxury and comfort at The Alfond Inn in Winter Park.
