Convenient Location near Fira Exhibition CentreLocated just 500 meters from Barcelona’s Fira Exhibition Centre at Plaza España, Hostal Elkano offers heated rooms with free Wi-Fi.Diverse Dining and Shopping OptionsWithin a 5-minute walk, guests can explore numerous bars, restaurants, and shops. Sant Antoni Market and the Arenas Shopping Centre are also within walking distance, providing a variety of choices for dining and entertainment.Easy Access to Attractions and TransportationWith Poble Sec Metro Station just 200 meters away, exploring Barcelona's attractions like Montjuic Park, Las Ramblas, and Passeig de Gracias is convenient. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 15 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.
