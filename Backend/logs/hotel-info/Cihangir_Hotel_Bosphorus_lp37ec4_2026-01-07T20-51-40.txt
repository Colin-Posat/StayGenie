HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Breathtaking Bosporus ViewsCompletely renovated in 2013, Cihangir Hotel boasts a stunning terrace offering panoramic Bosporus views. The air-conditioned rooms feature complimentary WiFi and a restaurant serving authentic Turkish cuisine, from delightful mezze to the famous Raki.Elegant and Comfortable AccommodationsThe rooms at Cihangir Hotel are elegantly decorated in rich, dark tones and equipped with modern amenities such as blackout curtains, cable TV, and a fully stocked minibar. Guests can unwind in the full-size wardrobe and enjoy the convenience of an en suite bathroom.Exceptional Services and LocationThe dedicated staff at Cihangir Hotel can assist in arranging outdoor excursions and sightseeing tours, ensuring a memorable stay. With a prime location just 300 meters from Taksim Metro Station, exploring Istanbul's vibrant Taksim district and nearby attractions is effortless.Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience at Cihangir Hotel!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
