• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but some guests experienced maintenance issues and dark rooms.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests appreciating the gym and pool, while others mentioned missing spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Varied opinions on pricing, with some guests feeling it was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received mixed feedback, with some guests wanting more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
