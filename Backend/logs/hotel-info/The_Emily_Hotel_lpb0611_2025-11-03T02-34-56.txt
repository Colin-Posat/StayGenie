HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 73 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.8/10 - Service was widely appreciated with special mentions for the guest experience team's attentiveness.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel is situated in a vibrant area with easy access to shops, restaurants, and public transportation.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but some guests had concerns about dated decor and bathroom features.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - While some guests appreciated the facilities like the coffee bar, others found the amenities lacking compared to the price paid.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the perceived value for the cost of stay, with some feeling it was expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, though suggestions for improvement were noted.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the friendly staff, convenient location, and cleanliness of the hotel.
