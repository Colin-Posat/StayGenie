HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Grand City offers cosy air-conditioned guestrooms with a simple decor and pleasant neutral tones. Each room is equipped with a satellite flat-screen TV, free internet access, a fridge, and a full en suite bathroom for your comfort and convenience.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy the convenience of a restaurant on-site, a relaxing massage service, and a free internet terminal in the lobby. The hotel also provides coin-operated laundry machines on the 3rd floor and a Wi-Fi converter that can be borrowed for free from the reception.Prime LocationLocated just a 5-minute walk from JR Ikebukuro Train Station, Hotel Grand City offers easy access to popular attractions like the Sunshine 60 Building. With direct rail and subway links to Shinjuku and Shibuya, the hotel is the perfect choice for travelers looking to explore Tokyo.Book your stay at Hotel Grand City now for a memorable and comfortable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
From Ikebukuro Station, the East Exit (North) and Exit 32 are nearest to the hotel.
DIRECTIONS: From either of these exits, go down the street between the HSBC branch and Tokyo-Mitsubishi UFJ Bank (red signs, UFJ). Continue for about 300 metres, until you see a bowling alley. Turn left. The hotel is 50 metres ahead on the right.
