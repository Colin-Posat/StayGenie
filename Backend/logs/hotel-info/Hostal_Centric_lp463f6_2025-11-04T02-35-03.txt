HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Modern AmenitiesCentrally located, Hostal Cèntric offers free Wi-Fi, 24-hour reception, free luggage storage, and a free safe. The air-conditioned rooms feature flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms with free toiletries, and some even have balconies.Dining and Entertainment OptionsSurrounded by restaurants, shops, and trendy bars, guests can explore the vibrant Raval District and Sant Antoni Metro Station nearby. Enjoy a shared terrace with table and chairs or take a leisurely walk to Plaza Universitat and Rambla.Easy Access to Attractions and TransportationUniversitat Metro Station and MACBA Museum are within walking distance, while Sepulveda Bus Stop for airport buses is conveniently close. Benefit from discounted public parking and make the most of your stay at Hostal Cèntric.
