HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Free Wi-FiThe hotel, located just a short walk from Montparnasse Tower and Train Station, offers modern rooms with complimentary Wi-Fi. Each bright guest room features a desk, flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and an en suite bathroom with a hairdryer. With Pernety Metro Station nearby, guests have direct access to the iconic Champs-Elysées.Buffet Breakfast and 24-Hour ReceptionStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast provided each morning at the ibis Styles Paris Maine Montparnasse. Enjoy the convenience of additional services such as a 24-hour reception, ensuring a comfortable stay throughout.Nearby AttractionsExplore the charming surroundings with a leisurely 20-minute walk to Georges Brassens Park or visit the Paris Expo Porte de Versailles exhibition centre, located just 2.8 km away. Experience convenience and modern comfort at our hotel!
