HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Modern AmenitiesHotel Clé Reforma Insurgentes, nestled in Mexico City, offers a blend of convenience and modern amenities. With a fitness centre, terrace, and a restaurant and bar on-site, guests can unwind and dine with ease.Exceptional Services and Comfortable AccommodationThis 3-star hotel stands out with its concierge service, tour desk, and 24-hour front desk. The air-conditioned rooms are thoughtfully equipped with a desk, coffee machine, and flat-screen TV, ensuring a comfortable stay for every guest.Nearby Attractions and Easy AccessLocated just 1.2 km from The Angel of Independence and 1.1 km from the United States Embassy, Hotel Clé Reforma Insurgentes offers easy access to key landmarks. Plus, with airport transfers available, reaching the hotel from Benito Juarez International Airport is a breeze.Book your stay at Hotel Clé Reforma Insurgentes for a memorable and hassle-free experience.
