HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Hotel with Complimentary Shuttle ServiceConvenient Amenities for a Relaxing StayThis modern hotel offers a free 24-hour shuttle service to Los Angeles International Airport, making travel a breeze for guests. After a busy day of exploring the city, unwind in the outdoor heated pool or enjoy a delicious meal at the on-site dining options.Comfortable Rooms and Dining OptionsGuest rooms at Sheraton Gateway Los Angeles Hotel feature modern décor and essential amenities like a 50-inch LCD TV, work desk, and coffee machine. Indulge in a culinary experience at Costero California Bar and Bistro for lunch and dinner, or savor a breakfast buffet at Brasserie.Fitness Facilities with Scenic ViewsStay active during your stay with the on-site gym equipped with free weights and cardio machines. Enjoy panoramic views of the outdoor pool and hot tub while working out. Book your stay now for a memorable experience at Sheraton Gateway Los Angeles Hotel.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests under the age of 21 are only allowed to check in with a parent or official guardian.

Please note: The hotel requires a deposit for incidentals at check-in.

For nonrefundable bookings, the credit card used at the time of booking must be preset at the time of check-in.

Smoking is not permitted. If caught, there is a cleaning fee of USD 500.

Late check-out 11 AM — 6 PM available for 85 USD.

Availability and hours of operation in food outlets and public area amenities are subject to change.

Please note that the gym is accessible through 24 hours.

 Enjoy the convenience of checking in before Noon for a fee of 75 USD, or take advantage of our 40 USD fee for check-ins between 12:00 and 14:00.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.

HOTEL POLICIES:

WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  USD 75 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
