HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Renovated Classical Building: Experience the charm of a classical building with a modern twist at Express by gaiarooms in Barcelona's Gracia district.Elegant Design and Modern Amenities: Enjoy the minimalist and elegant room design with central air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and free Wi-Fi, ensuring a comfortable stay.Convenient Location: Explore the vibrant Gracia district with ease as the hotel is just minutes away from popular attractions, bars, and restaurants. With nearby train and metro stations, iconic spots like La Rambla are only a short ride away.Book your stay at Express by gaiarooms and immerse yourself in the heart of Barcelona's lively Gracia district.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests generally praised the cleanliness of the rooms and bathrooms, although there were a few mentions of cleanliness issues such as dust and dirt in some cases.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Staff responsiveness and availability were highlighted as positive aspects, with some guests mentioning quick and helpful interactions. However, a few guests had issues with keyless entries and check-in processes.
• Location: 8/10 - The central location of the hotel was widely appreciated, with guests mentioning easy access to key landmarks. However, some noise-related issues were reported.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Guests generally found the rooms to be clean and comfortable, but there were mentions of room appearance not matching online photos and suggestions for better lighting.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Amenities received mixed feedback, with some guests appreciating keyless entries and digital check-in processes, while others reported issues with functionality and accuracy.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - There were mixed opinions on the value for money, with some guests finding the hotel overpriced for the services offered, while others appreciated the convenience of digital processes.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Reviews on food and beverage services were generally positive, with guests enjoying the breakfast and restaurant options. However, some guests suggested a wider variety of choices.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a pleasant overall experience, praising the location, staff, and cleanliness. However, areas for improvement included room quality and amenities.
