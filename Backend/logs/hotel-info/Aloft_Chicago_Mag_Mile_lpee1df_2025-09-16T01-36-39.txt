Category Ratings:

• Service: 7/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests appreciating the friendliness of the staff while others had complaints about unresponsiveness.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's central location near key attractions and amenities was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable by some, while others mentioned issues with maintenance and amenities.
• Amenities: 5.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests enjoying the gym and pool, but others disappointed by the lack of variety and services like spa.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Several guests felt that the hotel was overpriced for the services provided, leading to dissatisfaction among some.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Breakfast options received mixed reviews, with some guests finding them limited, while others appreciated the restaurant services.
• Overall Experience: 6.1/10 - Both positive and negative feedback contributed to an average rating for the overall experience, with suggestions for improvements in various aspects.
