HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Central ParkLocated just 50 metres away from Central Park on Manhattan’s Upper West Side, West Side YMCA offers a prime location for exploring the city. Only a 5-minute walk from Columbus Circle, guests can easily access popular attractions and landmarks.State-of-the-Art Fitness Center and AmenitiesThe property boasts a 65000 square-feet fitness centre equipped with a sauna, steam room, weight-lifting and cardiovascular equipment, and 2 indoor swimming pools.
