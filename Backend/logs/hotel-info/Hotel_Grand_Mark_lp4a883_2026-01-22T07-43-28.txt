HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsHotel Grand Mark, nestled in Istanbul's Fatih district, is a mere stroll away from the iconic Grand Bazaar. The grand rooms boast modern furnishings, marble bathrooms, and balconies offering panoramic views of the city.Culinary Delights and Refreshing BeveragesIndulge in a delectable buffet breakfast each morning at the hotel's Turkish restaurant, complemented by hot tea and fresh fruit. In the evenings, unwind at the bar with a selection of soft drinks and authentic Turkish spirits. Additionally, numerous seafood restaurants are conveniently located just a short walk away.Convenient Location for ExploringExplore the cultural and historical gems of Istanbul with ease, as the hotel is in proximity to Armenian and Orthodox Churches. With public transportation nearby, guests can effortlessly navigate the city. Benefit from 24-hour front desk service and the convenience of on-site private parking.Book your stay at Hotel Grand Mark now and experience luxury in the heart of Istanbul!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
