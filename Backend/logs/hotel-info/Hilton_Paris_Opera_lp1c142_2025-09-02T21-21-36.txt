CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Varied opinions on pricing, with some feeling it was high for the services provided.

• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
