• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was reasonable, while others thought it was not justified by the quality of services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - The food offerings received positive feedback for variety and quality, though some guests desired more options and better timing for breakfast.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Overall, most guests had a positive experience and would consider returning, despite some issues with maintenance and customer service.
