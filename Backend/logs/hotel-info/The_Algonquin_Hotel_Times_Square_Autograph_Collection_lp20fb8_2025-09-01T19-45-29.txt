• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money, with some guests feeling the prices were high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Food and beverage services received positive feedback overall, with guests enjoying the breakfast and restaurant offerings.
