HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  USD 85.50 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 7.7/10 - While some guests felt the price was reasonable for the amenities and location, others considered it expensive, especially for additional services like breakfast.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Guests appreciated the dining options and breakfast buffet, with some mentioning the need for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive experience with the hotel and would recommend it, citing good service, location, and amenities.
