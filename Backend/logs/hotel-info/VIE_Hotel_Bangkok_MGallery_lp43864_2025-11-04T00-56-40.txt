HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 5-Star Stay at VIE Hotel BangkokPrime Location and Convenience: Experience 5-star luxury at VIE Hotel Bangkok, conveniently located just 160 meters from Ratchatewi BTS Skytrain Station. Explore nearby attractions like MBK, Siam Paragon, Chinatown, and the Temple of the Golden Mount.Elegant Design and Comfort: Admire the specially designed hotel adorned with works of art, exotic furniture, and vibrant colors. Relax in spacious rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows offering stunning city views. Enjoy modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, minibar, and 24-hour room service.Relaxation and Dining: Unwind at the outdoor pool on the rooftop, indulge in pampering massages at the spa, or stay active at the fitness center. Treat your taste buds at La VIE with a high-end dining experience featuring creative French cuisine and a premium wine list.Book your luxurious stay at VIE Hotel Bangkok now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
A damage deposit of THB 5000 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that the name of the credit card holder must be the same as the guest's name and credit card must be presented to the property upon check-in. If the same credit card is not provided, guests will be required to pay for the room charge.

Please note that shuttle service to nearby shopping centres, e.g., Siam Paragon, Siam Center, and MBK are provided free of charge. Reservation is required.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 6 years old to 11 years old stay for THB 1,118.15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for THB 2,236.30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
