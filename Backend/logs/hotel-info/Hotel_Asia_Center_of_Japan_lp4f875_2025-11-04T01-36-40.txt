HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesLocated just 500 metres from Aoyama-Itchome Subway Station, Hotel Asia Center offers easy access to popular areas like Shibuya and Ginza. The hotel provides free WiFi, LAN cable in rooms, and a massage service for guests to unwind.Comfortable AccommodationGuest rooms at Hotel Asia Center feature modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, private bathrooms with bathtubs, and wooden desks. Enjoy complimentary Japanese green tea and houjicha in your room for a relaxing stay.Dining and ServicesIndulge in a mix of Japanese and Western cuisine at the onsite restaurant, SHIKI. Additionally, the hotel offers a 24-hour reception, coin-operated internet stations, and laundry facilities for your convenience. Book your stay now and experience the best of Tokyo at Hotel Asia Center!

Guests are required to show a photo identification or passport upon check-in.

Children under 6 years old can stay free of charge when using existing beds. Adult rates are applicable to children 6 years and older.
