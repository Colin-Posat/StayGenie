HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Private Villas with Stunning ViewsVilla Semana in Ubud, Bali, offers luxurious villas with private pools and breathtaking views of rice padi fields and the Ayung River.Traditional Charm with Modern ComfortsExperience the perfect blend of traditional Indonesian charm and modern amenities at Villa Semana. Each villa features a well-stocked minibar, iPod docks, rustic outdoor showers, and spacious indoor bathrooms with sunken bathtubs.Relaxation and Culinary DelightsUnwind by the main infinity pool surrounded by tropical greenery or indulge in traditional Balinese massage treatments at the spa. Don't miss the Pondok Thai Fusion Restaurant overlooking the pool and river, offering a delectable selection of local fare and fine Asian cuisine.Book your stay at Villa Semana now to immerse yourself in luxury and tranquility in Ubud!

• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
