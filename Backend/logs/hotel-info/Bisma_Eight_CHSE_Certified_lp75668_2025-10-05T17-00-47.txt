HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsBisma Eight - CHSE Certified offers spacious contemporary rooms with traditional Japanese soaking tubs, artisan-appointed rooms, and modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and minibars. The lush green surroundings and Balinese-inspired villas provide a tranquil retreat for guests.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind in the outdoor temperature-controlled pool, private villa pools, or enjoy a free daily yoga class. The property also features a fitness center, garden, sun terrace, and library, catering to both relaxation and recreation.Exceptional Dining and ServicesIndulge in Asian cuisine at Copper Kitchen &amp; Bar, or savor pastries at The Library Cafe. The hotel's 24-hour front desk, concierge service, and additional offerings like laundry, airport transfers, and shuttle services ensure a seamless and enjoyable stay.Book now for a luxurious and rejuvenating experience at Bisma Eight!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 150,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 10 years old and over stay for Rp 700,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
