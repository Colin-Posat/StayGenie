HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Panoramic Views and Historic CharmLocated in the heart of Amalfi, Hotel Croce Di Amalfi offers breathtaking views of the town and the Mediterranean Sea from its 19th-century residence. The air-conditioned rooms feature modern amenities such as flat-screen satellite TV and free Wi-Fi, ensuring a comfortable stay.Convenient Beach Access and Nearby AttractionsJust a short 200-meter walk from the hotel, guests can reach the beach via a scenic flight of steps. The town's iconic cathedral is a mere 2-minute stroll away, offering a glimpse into Amalfi's rich history and culture.Relaxing Accommodations and Delicious BreakfastGuest rooms at Hotel Croce Di Amalfi are soundproofed for a peaceful stay and include a minibar and private bathroom. Many rooms boast balconies, with some even featuring a spa bath. Start your day with a continental breakfast in a charming room with traditional tiled floors, setting the perfect tone for your Amalfi adventure.Book now to experience the beauty and comfort of Hotel Croce Di Amalfi.
