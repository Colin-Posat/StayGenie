HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Pacific Coast HighwaySituated less than a mile from the Pacific Coast Highway, Hotel MDR - a DoubleTree by Hilton in Marina del Rey offers easy access to the stunning coastal views and attractions.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesEach guest room at the 100% non-smoking hotel features free WiFi, a small fridge, and coffee-making facilities. Guests can relax in the seating area with a flat-screen cable TV and work desk provided in every room.On-Site Dining and Recreational FacilitiesGuests can enjoy dining at Barbianca, which serves a breakfast buffet and California cuisine for lunch and dinner. The hotel also boasts a heated outdoor swimming pool, fitness and business center, and offers bicycle rentals for exploring the area.Book your stay at Hotel MDR - a DoubleTree by Hilton for a relaxing and convenient experience near popular LA destinations.
