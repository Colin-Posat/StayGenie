HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

What Guests Love:

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited guest services after 10 pm

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests consistently appreciated the cleanliness maintained throughout the hotel.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Staff service was acknowledged positively, although a few guests mentioned issues with responsiveness after 10 pm.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks was a highlight, although some guests found the immediate surroundings lacking.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were overall comfortable, with some complaints about bed comfort and room layout.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Guests appreciated the pool, breakfast spread, and children's amenities, but some felt that there could be improvements in other facilities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the services justified the cost while others found it slightly overpriced.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Strong positive feedback on the breakfast variety and quality, with occasional suggestions for improvement.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - The majority of guests had a positive experience at the hotel and expressed intentions of returning or recommending it.
