HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Sea ViewsHotel La Flore, nestled between Nice and Monaco, offers breathtaking panoramic views of the sea. The Provencal-style rooms blend traditional charm with modern amenities, providing a cozy and comfortable stay. Most rooms feature balconies with mesmerizing Mediterranean vistas, perfect for a relaxing evening.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy free Wi-Fi access throughout the hotel and avail of parking facilities for a fee. The private bar offers a relaxing atmosphere, with a complimentary computer for internet use. Whether for leisure or business, Hotel La Flore ensures a convenient and enjoyable stay for all visitors.Ideal LocationFacing south towards the bay of Villefranche, the hotel is conveniently situated on a bus route connecting major coastal cities of the French Riviera. Explore the beauty of the region with ease, starting your journey from the charming Hotel La Flore.Book your stay at Hotel La Flore now and indulge in a delightful coastal getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 10 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
