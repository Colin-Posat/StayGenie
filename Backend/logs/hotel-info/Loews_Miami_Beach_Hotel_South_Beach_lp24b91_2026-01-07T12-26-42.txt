Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 9/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.6/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with staff being friendly and accommodating, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport, making it convenient for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally clean and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues and uninspired design.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, especially mentioning the valet parking and lack of complimentary water replenishment.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with positive comments on staff interactions and customized dietary requests.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the location, cleanliness, and friendly staff, though some felt the value for money was lacking.
