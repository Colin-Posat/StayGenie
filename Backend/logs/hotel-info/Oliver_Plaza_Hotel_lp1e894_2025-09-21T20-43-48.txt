HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Top AttractionsLocated in the heart of Earl's Court, the Oliver Plaza Hotel offers convenient access to popular destinations like Kensington, Knightsbridge, and the Victoria and Albert museum. With Earl’s Court Underground Station just a stone's throw away, exploring London couldn't be easier.Modern Amenities for a Comfortable StayEnjoy a stay in our newly refurbished hotel featuring contemporary furniture, free Wi-Fi, and modern LCD TVs with satellite channels in every room.
