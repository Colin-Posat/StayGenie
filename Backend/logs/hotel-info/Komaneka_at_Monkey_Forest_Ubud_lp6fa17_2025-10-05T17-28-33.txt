HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Balinese AccommodationKomaneka at Monkey Forest offers a stunning retreat in the heart of Ubud, Bali. Indulge in elegantly designed rooms with private terraces overlooking picturesque rice fields or lush gardens.Relaxation and ConvenienceUnwind by the outdoor pool, pamper yourself at the spa, and stay connected with complimentary WiFi. The resort's spacious rooms feature traditional Balinese decor, modern amenities, and serene bathrooms with separate shower and bathtub.Culinary Delights and Cultural ExperiencesSavor local cuisine at Komaneka Bali Restaurant, enjoy breakfast with a view, or opt for in-room dining. Immerse yourself in Balinese culture with a visit to nearby attractions like Ubud Palace and Campuhan Ridge Walk.Experience the essence of Bali at Komaneka at Monkey Forest - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 1,250,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 900,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 8.6/10 - Rooms were well-appointed and comfortable, with occasional mentions of minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Guests appreciated the provided amenities, with some highlighting the pool and landscaped gardens, while others desired additional services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 8/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location, while others found it slightly high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 8.1/10 - The quality of food and beverage served was generally well-received, although some guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 9.2/10 - Most guests had an overwhelmingly positive experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
