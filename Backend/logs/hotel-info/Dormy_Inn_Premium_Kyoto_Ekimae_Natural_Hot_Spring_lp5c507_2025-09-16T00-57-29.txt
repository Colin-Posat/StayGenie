HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Kyoto Station and Kyoto TowerLocated just a 3-minute walk from Kyoto Station and Kyoto Tower, Dormy Inn Premium Kyoto Ekimae Natural Hot Spring offers convenient access to popular attractions and transportation hubs.Relaxing Natural Hot Spring Baths and AmenitiesEnjoy the large natural hot spring baths, sauna, and massages offered at the hotel. Guests can also unwind in the top-floor indoor and semi-outdoor hot tubs. Free laundry facilities and a convenience store add to the comfort of your stay.Delicious Breakfast Buffet and Nearby AttractionsStart your day with a breakfast buffet featuring Japanese and Western dishes at the hotel's restaurant. Explore nearby sites such as the Higashi Hongan-ji Temple and Shosei-en Garden, both just a 5-minute walk away.Book your stay at Dormy Inn Premium Kyoto Ekimae Natural Hot Spring for a relaxing and convenient experience.

When sending packages and luggage to the property, please write the booking number and guest name used to make the reservation on the packing slip. Mail without the above information may not be accepted by the property.

Meal-inclusive rate does not include meals for children sleeping in existing beds. Additional fees apply for children's meals if adults book a meal-inclusive rate. Please contact the property for details.
