HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Contemporary Guest RoomsIndulge in modern comfort at Hôtel Mistral with air-conditioned rooms equipped with essential amenities like a minibar, telephone, and satellite TV. Each room features a private bathroom with convenient facilities for a relaxing stay.Floral Patio and Buffet BreakfastStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast served in the charming ground-floor breakfast room with access to a serene floral patio. Enjoy a peaceful retreat or mingle with other guests in this lovely setting.Convenient LocationLocated in the vibrant Montparnasse district, Hôtel Mistral offers easy access to transportation with the Gaîté Metro stop nearby. Explore popular attractions like Montparnasse Tower, Fondation Cartier Museum, and Luxembourg Gardens within walking distance.Experience Parisian hospitality at its best - book your stay at Hôtel Mistral today!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.55/10 - Several reviews mentioned cleanliness issues, particularly in regards to hair found in bathrooms and a general need for better upkeep.
• Service: 9.99/10 - Most reviews praised the hotel staff for being friendly, helpful, and accommodating, indicating a high level of service.
• Location: 9.56/10 - Multiple reviews highlighted the hotel's convenient location near transport and attractions, making it a strong positive aspect.
• Room Quality: 8.51/10 - Some complaints about room size and amenities were noted, but overall, rooms were described as clean and comfortable.
• Amenities: 9.04/10 - General satisfaction was noted with amenities, especially breakfast, though some improvements could be made.
• Value for Money: 8.5/10 - Guests felt that some aspects, particularly room quality and amenities, did not fully match the expectations based on pricing.
• Food and Beverage: 9.05/10 - The breakfast received positive mentions, but a few guests suggested that offering breakfast with room rates would enhance value.
• Overall Experience: 9.62/10 - While there were some minor issues noted, guests generally had a positive experience and expressed satisfaction with their stay.
