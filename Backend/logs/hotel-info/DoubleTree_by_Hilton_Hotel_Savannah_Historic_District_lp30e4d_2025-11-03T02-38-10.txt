Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, with some minor complaints about substandard room cleaning.
• Service: 6.7/10 - Service was uneven, with both positive and negative experiences reported. Guests appreciated the welcoming staff at the front desk but had complaints about the inflexible late checkout policy.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location near the river and historic district was highly praised for its convenience and accessibility, although some guests had concerns about noise levels.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Rooms were generally clean and comfortable, but some guests experienced noise issues and maintenance concerns.
• Amenities: 7/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with a mix of positive feedback on the gym and cleanliness, and minor complaints about room noise and AC issues.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions were divided on the value for money offered by the hotel, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location but others finding it too high for the services received.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with some guests appreciating the quality and variety offered.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing friendly staff, great location, and clean rooms as highlights, despite some service and room quality issues.
