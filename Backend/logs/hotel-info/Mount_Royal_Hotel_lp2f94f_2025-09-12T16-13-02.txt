HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in BanffLocated in Banff, Mount Royal Hotel offers a convenient stay with a restaurant on-site. Enjoy modern amenities such as free WiFi and a flat-screen TV. Unwind in the sauna and hot tub after a day of exploring Banff Upper Hot Springs just 4 km away.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsRelax in air-conditioned rooms equipped with a coffee machine, desk, and seating area. Freshen up in the private bathroom with complimentary toiletries provided. Additional conveniences include a safety deposit box and ironing facilities.Convenient Services and ActivitiesBenefit from a 24-hour front desk, luggage and ski storage, as well as dry cleaning services. Engage in various activities like skiing both on-site and in the surrounding areas. Don't forget to visit Lake Minnewanka and Banff Gondola nearby.Book your stay at Mount Royal Hotel for a memorable experience in Banff!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of CAD 250 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note, different policies will apply for groups bookings of more than 10 rooms.

There is a $5/night fee, with a limited number of spaces on a first-come, first-served basis.
