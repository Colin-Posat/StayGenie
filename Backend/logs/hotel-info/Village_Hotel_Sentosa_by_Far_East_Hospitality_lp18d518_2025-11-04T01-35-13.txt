HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationVillage Hotel Sentosa by Far East Hospitality offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests. Each room is equipped with a private bathroom and a flat-screen TV with satellite channels.Convenient LocationLocated in Sentosa Island, the hotel is within walking distance to popular attractions like Wings Of Time, Adventure Cove Waterpark, and S.E.A. Aquarium. Universal Studios Singapore and VivoCity are also just a short distance away, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore the area.Family-Friendly ExperienceWith 606 rooms decorated in calming hues inspired by nature, Village Hotel Sentosa provides a unique experience for families and leisure groups. The hotel's proximity to retail and dining options at the Mess Hall, along with flexible meeting spaces at the Events Centre, caters to a variety of guests.Experience luxury and convenience at Village Hotel Sentosa - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway!
