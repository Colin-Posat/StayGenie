HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 560,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.8/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel, mentioning it as a positive aspect in multiple reviews.
• Service: 8.7/10 - The exceptional service provided by the staff was highlighted in various reviews, with guests appreciating their friendliness and attentiveness.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's prime location in the heart of Ubud was a key feature for guests, making it easy to access local attractions and amenities.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Guests found the rooms to be clean, quiet, and comfortable, although some noise disturbances were reported in a few reviews.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - While the amenities provided were generally satisfactory, some guests noted the absence of certain services like a lift or spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling it was worth it for the location and services, while others found it slightly expensive.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, although some guests desired more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a highly positive overall experience at the Alaya Resort and expressed their intention to recommend it to others.
