* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.6/10 - Service was highly appreciated and staff was courteous, with mentions of a private butler catering to guests' needs.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel is in a perfect location according to guests, making it convenient to explore the area.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as spacious and luxurious by most guests, although minor issues like a poorly stocked fridge were mentioned.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests appreciated the gym and spa facilities but some mentioned the lack of certain amenities like a spa service.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - There were mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests finding it reasonable given the quality and location, while others felt it was slightly overpriced.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, although some guests wished for more variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, feeling like royalty and being impressed by the hotel's beauty and service quality.
