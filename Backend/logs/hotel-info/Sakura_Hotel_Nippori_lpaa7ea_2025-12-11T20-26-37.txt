HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Japanese-Style Rooms with Traditional Futon BeddingExperience authentic Japanese hospitality at Hotel Sakura Nippori, just a short walk from Sendagi Station. Each room features tatami floors and futon bedding, providing a comfortable and cultural stay.Convenient Amenities and ServicesEnjoy the convenience of free luggage storage, WiFi, and access to a 24-hour front desk. Borrow a hairdryer, iron, or iron-board at no additional cost. The shared bathroom and towels provided add to the ease of your stay.Explore and DiscoverLocated near Sensoji Temple and Yasukuni Shrine, our hotel offers easy access to Tokyo's cultural landmarks. With Tokyo Haneda International Airport just a train ride away, your journey is made simple.Book your stay at Hotel Sakura Nippori for a memorable and hassle-free experience.
