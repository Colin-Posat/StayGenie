HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Private Villas and Serene EnvironmentExperience the ultimate relaxation at The Payogan Villa Resort and Spa, where private villas and an outdoor pool await in a lush green setting overlooking the Oos River Valley.Balinese-Style Villas and Spa RetreatIndulge in the spacious Balinese-style villas with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and DVD players. Unwind in the large semi-outdoor bathrooms featuring a sunken bathtub. Pamper yourself with rejuvenating spa treatments and meditation spaces within tropical gardens.Exquisite Dining and Convenient LocationSavor local and international cuisine at Lesung Restaurant while enjoying stunning views. With a free shuttle to Ubud's center, the resort offers easy access to nearby attractions. Book your stay now and immerse yourself in luxury at The Payogan Villa Resort and Spa.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 6 years old and over stay for Rp 750,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
