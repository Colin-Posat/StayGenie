HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm in Bloomsbury District:Arosfa Hotel London by Compass Hospitality offers a unique stay in a 200-year-old Georgian townhouse once belonging to the renowned artist Sir John Everett Millais.Cozy Rooms with Modern Amenities:Each room at the hotel features small en suite facilities, free Wi-Fi, and a flat-screen TV with Freeview channels. Guests can also enjoy tea/coffee making facilities and complimentary toiletries during their stay.Convenient Location for Sightseeing:Situated in the heart of London, the hotel is a short walk from popular attractions like Oxford Street, The British Museum, and Regent’s Park. With nearby tube stations, exploring the city, including Oxford Street and Leicester Square, is a breeze.Book now to experience a blend of history and comfort at Arosfa Hotel London!
