Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms, despite a few minor issues mentioned in some reviews.
• Service: 8.6/10 - Staff hospitality and service were highly praised by guests, with only a few isolated complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's proximity to the airport and key areas, as well as ample parking space, were positively highlighted by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests found the rooms comfortable and well-equipped, with some mentioning minor maintenance issues like noisy AC units.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The hotel's amenities were satisfactory, with positive features like free WiFi and streaming apps, but some guests noted issues like malfunctioning shower doors.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some feeling the price was justified by the location and service, while others found it a bit high for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Overall positive feedback on the breakfast and restaurant services, although some guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with high praises for staff members like Jose and Carlos.
