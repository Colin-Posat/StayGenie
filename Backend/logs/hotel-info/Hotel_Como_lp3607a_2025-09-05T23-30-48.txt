HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Como offers spacious rooms with parquet floors, air conditioning, and modern amenities like a flat-screen TV and minibar. The well-equipped bathrooms include complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer. Stay connected with free WiFi during your stay.Rooftop Terrace with a ViewGuests can bask in the summer sun on the hotel's rooftop terrace, complete with a refreshing swimming pool and breathtaking views of the hills surrounding Como. It's the perfect spot to unwind and take in the scenic beauty of the area.Complimentary Fitness FacilitiesFor health-conscious guests, the hotel provides complimentary access to fitness facilities. Stay active and energized during your stay with state-of-the-art equipment and workout spaces available at your convenience.Book your stay at Hotel Como now and experience the perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and convenience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please note, outdoor parking is free and subject to availability. Indoor garage parking comes at extra cost and is upon request.

The swimming pool is open from June until September.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 15 per  day.
