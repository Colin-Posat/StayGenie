HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$20 per person per stay when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$20 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, although there were a few mentions of maintenance issues in some rooms.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Staff members were commended for being friendly and helpful, providing excellent customer service. However, there were occasional complaints about response times and staffing levels.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and public transport was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Most guests found the rooms to be spacious and comfortable, with some pointing out maintenance issues like noisy refrigerators and bathroom cleanliness.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - A mixed sentiment was noted regarding amenities, with some guests appreciating the gym and pool while others desired additional services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests finding the price justified by the location, while others felt it was too high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, although there were suggestions for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would consider staying at the hotel again, despite some minor issues reported.
