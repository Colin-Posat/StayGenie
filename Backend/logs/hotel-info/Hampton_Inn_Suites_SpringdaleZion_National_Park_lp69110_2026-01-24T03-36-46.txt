HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Zion National ParkLocated just 1 mile from the entrance to Zion National Park, Hampton Inn &amp; Suites Springdale/Zion National Park offers a prime location for nature enthusiasts. The O.C. Tanner Amphitheater is also just a short 5-minute drive away, providing easy access to entertainment options.Comfortable AccommodationsEach spacious guest room features a 42-inch flat-screen TV, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring. With amenities like a microwave and small refrigerator, guests can enjoy added convenience during their stay.Relaxing AmenitiesGuests can take advantage of the outdoor pool and hot tub for a refreshing dip or relax at the fitness center. For those needing to catch up on work, the business center is available. Additionally, the convenience mart and gift shop on-site make souvenir shopping a breeze.Book your stay at Hampton Inn &amp; Suites Springdale/Zion National Park for a memorable and convenient getaway.
