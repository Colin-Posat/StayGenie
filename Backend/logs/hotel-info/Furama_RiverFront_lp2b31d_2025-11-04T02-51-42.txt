HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location &amp; Stunning ViewsFurama RiverFront, nestled between vibrant Chinatown and bustling Orchard Road, offers a picturesque setting along the Singapore River. Enjoy easy access to top attractions like Clarke Quay and Orchard Road, making it a perfect choice for exploring the city.Comfortable Rooms &amp; Modern AmenitiesIndulge in elegantly designed rooms equipped with cable TV, coffee/tea facilities, and 24-hour room service. The en suite bathrooms provide complimentary toiletries for your convenience, ensuring a pleasant stay.Dining Options &amp; Leisure FacilitiesSavor delicious Halal Indonesian cuisine at Kintamani Indonesian Restaurant or unwind at the Waterfall Lounge with refreshing drinks and live entertainment. Take a dip in the outdoor pool, work out at the gym, or utilize laundry services, all while experiencing exceptional hospitality.Experience the best of Singapore at Furama RiverFront - book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 10 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
