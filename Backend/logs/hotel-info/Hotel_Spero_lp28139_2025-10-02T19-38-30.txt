HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Eco-Friendly Landmark Hotel in Downtown San FranciscoChic and Comfortable RoomsIndulge in the natural light streaming through large windows in the colourful air-conditioned rooms at Hotel Spero. Relax in the rejuvenating glass enclosed shower or tub and enjoy modern amenities like a 50-inch TV, plush bathrobes, and a mini-refrigerator.Delicious Dining OptionsSavor American cuisine, handcrafted cocktails, and locally brewed beers at Jasper's Corner Tap &amp; Kitchen, open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Start your day with a delightful cup of coffee offered by the hotel.Convenient Amenities and ServicesStay productive with the 24-hour gym and business centre, equipped with fax and photocopying services. Explore the city easily with the hotel's proximity to Union Square, Chinatown, and the Powell Street BART Metro Station.Book your stay at Hotel Spero for an unforgettable San Francisco experience.

Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$0 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Overall, guests mentioned the hotel's cleanliness as a positive aspect, but some reviews highlighted cleanliness issues related to plumbing and maintenance.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, with compliments towards the friendly staff members, but a few reviews mentioned unfriendly reception staff and issues with breakfast vouchers.
• Location: 8.1/10 - Guests praised the hotel's prime location, proximity to key landmarks, and easy access to public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and had good features, but some reviews highlighted issues such as dirty windows, old furniture smell, and small room space.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - The hotel's amenities received mixed reviews, with some guests appreciating the gym and pool while others mentioned a lack of breakfast options and spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions on the value for money were varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Guests generally found the breakfast and restaurant services to be satisfactory, although some mentioned a desire for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, with highlights on the friendly staff, convenient location, and comfortable beds.
