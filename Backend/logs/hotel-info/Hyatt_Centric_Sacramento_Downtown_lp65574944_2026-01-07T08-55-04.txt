HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in the Heart of SacramentoModern Amenities: Enjoy air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, a fitness centre, and a shared lounge. Indulge in room service, dine at the on-site restaurant, or unwind on the terrace.Comfortable Rooms: Relax in well-equipped rooms with desks, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms. Upgrade to rooms with stunning city views for a truly memorable stay.Convenient Location: Explore nearby attractions such as the California Capitol Building and Crocker Art Museum. Benefit from easy access to Sacramento Valley Station and the airport.Book your stay at Hyatt Centric Sacramento Downtown for a perfect blend of comfort and convenience.
