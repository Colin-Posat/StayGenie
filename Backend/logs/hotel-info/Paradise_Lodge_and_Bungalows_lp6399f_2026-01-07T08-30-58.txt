HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation OptionsJust 1 km from Lake Louise, Paradise Lodge and Bungalows offers spacious lodge rooms and fully equipped cabins. A satellite TV and a seating area are provided in Paradise’s suites and wooden bungalows. All units are equipped with a coffee maker and a bar refrigerator. Select rooms offer kitchen facilities and balconies with mountain views.Beautiful Surroundings and AmenitiesThe property is surrounded by gardens which include a barbecue and a playground. Hiking trails start less than 2 km from Paradise Lodge and Bungalows. A sightseeing gondola tour is just 6.8 km away.Convenient Location for Outdoor ActivitiesWith its proximity to Lake Louise and various outdoor attractions, Paradise Lodge and Bungalows is the perfect base for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike. Book now to experience a memorable stay in the heart of nature.
