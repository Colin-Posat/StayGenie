HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Kyoto StationLocated right in front of Kyoto Station, Miyako Hotel Kyoto Hachijo offers convenience and easy access for travelers.Luxurious AmenitiesThe hotel features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, a beauty salon, a convenience shop, and 6 dining options. Guests can enjoy modern comforts and convenience during their stay.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a variety of cuisines at the hotel's buffet restaurant Le Plaisir, Chinese Sichuan cuisine at Shisen, and traditional Japanese dishes at Restaurant Kyoyamatoya. A treat for food enthusiasts.Experience the best of Kyoto at Miyako Hotel Kyoto Hachijo. Book your stay now to enjoy a memorable and comfortable stay.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

A baby cot is only available for babies up to 6 months old.
