HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThe Westin Las Vegas Hotel &amp; Spa boasts a state-of-the-art fitness centre and a tranquil spa, offering guests the ultimate relaxation experience. The heated outdoor swimming pool provides a perfect spot to unwind after a day of exploring the vibrant Las Vegas Strip.Gourmet DiningIndulge in delectable American cuisine at Jake &amp; Eli restaurant, open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. For a more intimate dining experience, guests can opt for the convenient 24-hour room service, ensuring a delightful meal at any time of the day.Prime LocationSituated just a block away from the bustling Las Vegas Strip, The Westin offers the perfect blend of excitement and serenity. Book your stay now to experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at The Westin Las Vegas Hotel &amp; Spa.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Westin Las Vegas Hotel & Spa in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note: The breakfast is available for each registered adult per room when applicable. Children are not included. Please contact the property for details as breakfast is not included with every reservation.

The resort fee is subject to a 13.38% tax that is not included in the quoted rate.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.

• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel is situated close to key landmarks, attractions, and public transport options.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were described as clean, comfy, and spacious, with some minor concerns about noise levels and lighting.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities offered, with some mentioning issues with spa hours and lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 5.5/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 8.2/10 - Positive feedback on the quality of food and beverage offerings, especially the breakfast and chocolate cake.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
