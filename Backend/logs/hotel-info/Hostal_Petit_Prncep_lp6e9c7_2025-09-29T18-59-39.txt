HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Free Wi-FiHostal Petit Príncep offers air-conditioned, modern rooms on Diagonal Avenue in Barcelona. Each room features free Wi-Fi, heating, and vibrant decor. Some rooms even boast stunning city views.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy the convenience of a private bathroom with a hairdryer, as well as access to a communal fridge and microwave. The property's location provides easy access to a variety of shops, bars, restaurants, and a 24-hour supermarket.Perfect Location for ExploringSituated just a 15-minute walk from iconic landmarks like Gaudi’s Pedrera and Casa Batlló, Hostal Petit Príncep is an ideal base for exploring Barcelona. With Plaza Catalunya, the old town, and Gaudi’s Sagrada Familia nearby, guests can immerse themselves in the city's vibrant culture.Book your stay at Hostal Petit Príncep for a memorable Barcelona experience!
