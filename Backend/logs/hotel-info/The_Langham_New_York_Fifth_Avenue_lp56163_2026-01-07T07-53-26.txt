Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 9.2/10 - The room was spacious, clean, and well maintained, but cleanliness in common areas and overall hotel quality was questioned.
• Service: 10/10 - The staff went above and beyond, especially during the anniversary celebration; however, some interactions were noted as rude.
• Location: 10/10 - The hotel's location is convenient and great for a winter trip, enhancing the overall experience.
• Room Quality: 9.7/10 - Rooms were spacious and clean, but issues with lighting were mentioned that affected comfort.
• Amenities: 8.7/10 - Amenities were minimal and below expectations for a 5-star hotel, with repeated closures of the business center.
• Value for Money: 9.2/10 - Given the overall experience, especially during a luxury stay, value for money is questionable despite some positives.
• Food and Beverage: 10/10 - Complimentary champagne and overall food quality during the anniversary were highlighted positively.
• Overall Experience: 10/10 - While there were significant issues noted, memorable moments during special occasions were emphasized, leading to an overall positive experience.
