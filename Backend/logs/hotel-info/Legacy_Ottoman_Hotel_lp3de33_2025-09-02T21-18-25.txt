HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical Charm and Modern ComfortLegacy Ottoman Hotel, housed in a historical Byzantine-style building, seamlessly blends historic charm with modern amenities. The indoor pool, hot tub, hammam, and spa treatments offer a luxurious experience, while the air-conditioned rooms maintain their grand style.Culinary Delights and Scenic ViewsIndulge in typical Turkish cuisine at the Teras Restaurant while enjoying breathtaking views of the Bosphorus. The set menu for lunch and dinner ensures a delightful dining experience. Additionally, the roof-top bar provides panoramic cityscapes and a variety of drinks to unwind.Convenient Location and Cultural ExplorationConveniently located in Istanbul’s Eminönü District, guests can easily explore iconic landmarks such as the Galata Bridge and the Egyptian Bazaar. With free Wi-Fi throughout the property, staying connected and immersing yourself in the city's vibrant culture is effortless.Experience the perfect blend of history and luxury at Legacy Ottoman Hotel. Book now to elevate your stay in Istanbul!

HOTEL POLICIES:

WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 15 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but some reviews noted issues like torn insulation on hairdryer cords and delays in room cleaning.
• Service: 6.3/10 - While some guests appreciated the service and staff, others mentioned poor service, long wait times for requests, and issues with housekeeping.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and public transport was highly appreciated by guests in most reviews.
• Room Quality: 6.2/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, but many reviews highlighted outdated equipment, damaged furniture, and issues with housekeeping.
• Amenities: 6/10 - Mixed reviews about amenities, with some guests mentioning issues like a cold spa pool, missing items in rooms, and delays in service.
• Value for Money: 5.5/10 - Guests had mixed feelings about the value for money, with some mentioning high prices for services offered and others appreciating the location despite shortcomings.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 6.8/10 - While most guests had a positive overall experience, there were notable complaints about service quality, room conditions, and amenities.
