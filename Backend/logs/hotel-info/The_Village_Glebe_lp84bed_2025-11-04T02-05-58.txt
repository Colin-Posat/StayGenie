Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.6/10 - Overall, cleanliness was mentioned in both positive and negative reviews. Some guests praised the clean rooms and beds, while others noted issues with bathroom cleanliness.
• Service: 6.9/10 - Service received mixed feedback with some guests appreciating the friendly staff and welcoming atmosphere, while others mentioned noisy nights and inconsiderate staff conversations as a downside.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Guests generally praised the excellent location with easy access to key landmarks and public transport, making it a highlight of their stay.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, with positive comments about the private room and bathroom option. Some guests mentioned maintenance issues and lack of cleanliness.
• Amenities: 6.3/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests appreciating the facilities like the rooftop and simple gym, while others mentioned issues with cleanliness in the kitchen and public areas.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the prices were affordable and others mentioning issues like expensive breakfast and lack of kitchen utensils.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Food and beverage services received average ratings, with guests appreciating the good vibes and reasonable prices, but some noted limited food options and lack of variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.4/10 - The overall experience was positive for most guests, with friendly staff and good location being major pros. However, issues like cleanliness, noise, and lack of amenities were mentioned as cons.
