HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated just 2 km from Times Square, Hilton Garden Inn New York Manhattan East offers easy access to popular attractions like Rockefeller Center and the United Nations Headquarters. Guests can also conveniently reach Times Square with nearby subway stations.Modern AmenitiesWith a 24-hour fitness centre, business centre, and front desk, guests can enjoy round-the-clock convenience and service. The rooms are equipped with free WiFi, work desk, and flat-screen cable TV. Select rooms even offer stunning city views.Dining OptionsGuests can dine at the on-site Garden Grille and Bar or explore nearby dining choices. Additionally, laundry facilities are available for added convenience during your stay.Book your stay at Hilton Garden Inn New York Manhattan East for a memorable and convenient experience!
