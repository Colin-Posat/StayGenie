HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in AmsterdamLocated in the heart of the Jordaan district, Hotel Calisto is a charming property housed in a small monumental building. With Central Station just a 12-minute walk away, guests can easily explore the vibrant city.Cozy Rooms and On-Site RestaurantThe hotel features 10 cozy rooms, each equipped with a private bathroom and essential amenities. Guests can also indulge in delicious meals at the on-site restaurant, open 7 days a week for dinner and offering lunch on weekends.Vibrant Surroundings and Nearby AttractionsSurrounded by boutique shops, cafés, and bars in the lively Haarlemmerdijk area, guests can immerse themselves in the local culture.
