HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Hotel in the Heart of ParisThis modern hotel in the 16th-district of Paris offers a convenient and luxurious stay for guests looking to explore the city of lights.Comfortable Rooms and AmenitiesThe rooms at Auteuil Tour Eiffel provide a cozy retreat with amenities such as satellite TV, free WiFi, and tea/coffee facilities. Guests can also enjoy private bathrooms with L'Occitane toiletries, and some rooms even feature balconies.Relaxation and ConvenienceGuests can unwind in the sauna, stay active in the 24-hour fitness room, and stay connected with wireless internet. The hotel's 24-hour reception ensures assistance at any time, while the shaded terrace and lounge offer perfect spots to unwind.Book your stay at Auteuil Tour Eiffel for a memorable Parisian experience.
