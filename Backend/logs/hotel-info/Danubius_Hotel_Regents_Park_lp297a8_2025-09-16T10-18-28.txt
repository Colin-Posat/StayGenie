Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests mentioned the hotel's cleanliness as satisfactory, but some highlighted minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with mentions of warm welcome and helpful staff. Some guests had concerns about the internet connection and service speed.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks like Abbey Road and Regent's Park was a major highlight, with easy access to public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as cozy, comfortable, and nicely appointed, although issues with room views and AC functionality were reported.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found breakfast amazing and staff very helpful, but some wished for closer external amenities and better TV signal.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the pricing was justifiable for the location, while others found it slightly high for the services received.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and food services were generally well-received, but a desire for more variety was noted by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience with the hotel, appreciating the location, comfort, and service received.
