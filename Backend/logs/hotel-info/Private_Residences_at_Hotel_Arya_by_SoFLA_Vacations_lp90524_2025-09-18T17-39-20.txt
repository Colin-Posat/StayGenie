HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThe Private Residences at Sonesta by SoFLA Vacations offer a plethora of luxurious amenities including a heated outdoor pool, hot tub, sauna, fully equipped fitness centre, and even 2 squash courts.Gourmet Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary experience with continental and Peruvian specialties at the two on-site restaurants, both offering stunning views of Coconut Grove and Sailboat Bay.Convenient LocationConveniently situated just 3 minutes from CocoWalk for shopping and 7 km from Miami city centre, the property also provides easy access to Miami Beach and Miami Airport.Book now to experience the ultimate blend of luxury and convenience at the Private Residences at Sonesta by SoFLA Vacations.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Guests will receive a rental agreement which must be signed and returned to the property prior to arrival. If guests do not receive the agreement on time, they should contact the property management company at the number on the booking confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please inform Private Residences at Hotel Arya by SoFLA Vacations in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of USD 250 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
If paying by credit card, guests must provide the full billing address associated with the credit card to the property at booking.

Guests may also pay via bank wire transfer by notifying the property in the Special Requests box when booking. The property will contact the guest with payment instructions.

Please note the cleaning fee is for a final cleaning after check out. No daily maid service is provided.

Note: Cleaning fees vary by room type.
