HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Private BalconiesExperience unparalleled luxury at Radisson Blu Hotel &amp; Residence Nairobi Arboretum with each room featuring a private balcony offering stunning views of the pool or the Arboretum Flora. Enjoy top-notch amenities including a flat screen TV, rain shower, and exclusive bathroom amenities for a truly indulgent stay.Relaxation and Wellness FacilitiesUnwind and rejuvenate at our hotel's treatment rooms, steam room, sauna, and heated swimming pool. Whether you prefer a pampering session or a refreshing dip, our facilities are designed to cater to your relaxation needs. Plus, enjoy complimentary WiFi and convenient on-site parking during your stay.Culinary Delights and 24-Hour FitnessIndulge in delectable continental and super breakfast options at The Fire Lake and Grill House, and savor a variety of teas at The Arborista Coffee Lounge. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 5 years old and over stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
