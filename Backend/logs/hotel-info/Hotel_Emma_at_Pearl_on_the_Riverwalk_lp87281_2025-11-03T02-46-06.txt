HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesFeaturing free WiFi and a year-round outdoor pool, our hotel offers a luxurious stay just 2.1 km from The Alamo. Relax on the sun terrace, stay active in the fitness centre, and unwind with a drink at our onsite bar. Private parking is available for your convenience.Elegant RoomsAll rooms boast a 48&quot; flat-screen TV, with some offering a private terrace overlooking the San Antonio River or the city. Enjoy the comfort of a private bathroom with a shower, plush bath robes, and slippers during your stay.Gourmet Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in a farm-to-table menu at Supper, explore local goods at Larder, and sip on craft beer and cocktails at Sternewirth. With golfing, cycling, a library, and free bicycle use, there's something for everyone at our hotel.Book now for an unforgettable experience at our riverside retreat.
