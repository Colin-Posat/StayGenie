Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on cleanliness, with some mentioning the rooms were clean while others complained about the unpleasant smell in certain areas of the hotel.
• Service: 9.2/10 - Service was exceptional and highly praised by guests for being attentive, helpful, and personalized.
• Location: 9.5/10 - The hotel's prime location was widely appreciated as it offered convenience and access to key landmarks and amenities in the area.
• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Rooms were generally well-received for their comfort and design, although some guests noted issues with lighting and limited space in certain areas.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with some mentioning the beautiful room, lobby atmosphere, and tasty breakfast, while others highlighted inconveniences like iron delivery and elevator wait times.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions were divided on the value for money, with some guests feeling the services justified the price, while others thought it was too high for the experience provided.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - The breakfast and dining services received positive feedback for quality and variety, though some guests suggested improvements for room service and menu readability.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff, location, and amenities, while also mentioning minor issues related to cleanliness and value for money.
