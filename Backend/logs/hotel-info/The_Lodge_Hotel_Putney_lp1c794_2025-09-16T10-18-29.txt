HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Boutique Hotel in PutneyConvenient Location: Situated in tree-lined Putney, The Lodge Hotel offers easy access to East Putney Tube Station and is a short drive from the River Thames and Wimbledon.Stylish Accommodations: Each room features a flat-screen TV, luxury bedding, work area, and complimentary tea/coffee. The luxurious bathrooms are equipped with a hairdryer for your convenience.Dining and Leisure: Enjoy breakfast at the on-site restaurant, Hide &amp; Seed, which also serves light meals and pastries. Unwind in the elegant bar, lounge, or library. Guests can also make use of the hotel's gym facilities.Ready to experience the charm of The Lodge Hotel in Putney? IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of GBP 50 is required. The host charges this 7 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that parking is subject to availability and is on a first-come, first-serve basis for £15.00 per day

Please note, extra beds can only be provided with prior notice and approval with the property.

The credit card used at the time of payment must be present at the time of check-in. Please note, if the card holders name is different to the guest name on the booking, the property will contact you after booking in order to obtain a third party authorisation form in order to take payemt.
