Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 2.7/10 - Multiple reviews highlighted cleanliness issues, including dirty rooms, unclean bathrooms, and sticky furniture.
• Service: 7.8/10 - While some guests praised the staff for being apologetic and helpful, others experienced delays and miscommunication at the front desk.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's prime location near key landmarks and its accessibility to public transport received positive feedback from guests.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but complaints about stained showers, uncomfortable beds, and remnants left by previous guests were mentioned.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the variety of amenities, but some mentioned limited internet access and the absence of complimentary water bottles in rooms.
• Value for Money: 7.6/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money, with some guests feeling it was good value and others mentioning limitations like limited free internet and no complimentary services.
• Food and Beverage: 8.5/10 - Positive reviews for the restaurant services and staff, while some mentioned a lack of tea options and slow check-in.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the friendly staff, convenient parking, and good value, despite some room cleanliness issues.
