HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Start your luxurious and relaxing stay at Capella Hotel, Singapore, nestled within the lush rainforests of Sentosa Island.Luxurious Amenities: Indulge in 3 outdoor pools, an award-winning spa, and free Wi-Fi in every room with stunning sea or garden views.Personalized Service: Enjoy complimentary newspaper delivery, in-room refreshments, and garment pressing services during your stay.Convenient Location: Explore Sentosa Island for free, with nearby attractions like Universal Studios and easy access to Changi International Airport. Book now for an unforgettable experience at Capella Hotel, Singapore.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Payment before arrival via bank transfer is required. The property will contact you after you book to provide instructions.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
The payable total price may be increased by 1% due to the GST increase in 2024.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 227.81 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
