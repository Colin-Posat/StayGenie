HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation Surrounded by NatureRice Paddy Views and Outdoor Pools: Immerse yourself in the beauty of Ubud with stunning rice paddy views and relax by one of the two outdoor pools.Convenient Location and Tour Desk: Explore Ubud with ease as we are centrally located near Monkey Forest, Ubud Market, and Ubud Palace. Our tour desk can help you plan your adventures.Balinese Hospitality and Services: Experience the warm Balinese hospitality in our tropical gardens. Take advantage of our airport transfer, laundry services, and free Wi-Fi for a seamless stay.Book your stay at Sri Bungalows Ubud for a tranquil getaway amidst nature and luxury.

• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the spacious and comfortable rooms, but some mentioned issues with lighting and outdated bathrooms.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - The amenities were generally satisfactory, with highlights on the pools, but there were some concerns about the lack of proper lighting in certain areas.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Mixed opinions on the value for money, with some finding it reasonable for the location while others felt the services could be improved for the price.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and dining services were generally well-received, although some thought there could be improvements in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed their intention to return, praising the property's ambiance and staff hospitality.
