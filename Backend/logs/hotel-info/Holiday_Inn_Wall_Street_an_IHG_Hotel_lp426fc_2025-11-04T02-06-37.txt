Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests mentioning cleanliness as a positive aspect, while others noted issues like a stinky bathroom and lack of cleanliness.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, with friendly staff and helpful responses. Some complaints about maintenance and housekeeping response time.
• Location: 9/10 - Highly praised location near key landmarks, attractions, and public transport, making it convenient for tourists and business travelers.
• Room Quality: 5.9/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with positive comments on comfortable beds and negative feedback on room size, maintenance issues, and noisy air conditioning.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Guests found amenities satisfactory, but some highlighted the lack of certain amenities like a proper breakfast, ice availability, and in-room fridges.
• Value for Money: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location and overall experience.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and food services received generally positive feedback, but some guests wanted more variety and better food options.
• Overall Experience: 7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some specific complaints in other categories.
