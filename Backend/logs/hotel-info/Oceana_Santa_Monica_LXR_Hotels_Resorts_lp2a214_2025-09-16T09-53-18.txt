HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 65 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$200 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money varied among guests, with some feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, though a few suggested more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
