HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Rooms with Nespresso Machine and Free WiFiThe design hotel in Paris’ 10th district, Hôtel Littéraire Arthur Rimbaud, BW Signature Collection, offers chic rooms furnished with a Nespresso coffee machine and complimentary WiFi. The soundproofed rooms, adorned in serene hues, boast flat-screen satellite TVs for entertainment.Fitness Room with Unique ArchitectureGuests can enjoy a workout in the hotel's fitness room, characterized by a distinctive arched, stone ceiling. Stay active and rejuvenated during your stay at this elegant Parisian hotel.Ideal Location in the Theatre DistrictSituated on a charming pedestrian street in the bustling theatre district, this hotel is a stone's throw away from Le Grand Rex Theatre.
