HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsScenic Terrace and Modern AmenitiesThe air-conditioned rooms at The Kayon Resort offer a picturesque terrace, flat-screen TV, and a cozy seating area. Guests can unwind in a beautifully designed space equipped with a CD/DVD player, laptop safe, and a minibar. The marble bathroom provides a luxurious touch with a shower, bathtub, and premium toiletries.Exquisite Dining and RelaxationIndulge in fusion cuisine at KE-PI-TU Restaurant amidst a Balinese royal setting or savor drinks at the on-site bar. Serayu Spa offers rejuvenating treatments, while the beauty salon ensures guests look and feel their best. With a 24-hour front desk and a range of services including room service, laundry, and tour assistance, every need is catered to at The Kayon Resort.Book now to experience unparalleled luxury and hospitality at The Kayon Resort.
