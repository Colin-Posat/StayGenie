GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Location
• Comfortable beds
• Friendly staff
• Clean rooms

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• No spa services
• Noise and congestion issues

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were clean and very comfortable, with many guests mentioning the cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with excellent staff mentioned by multiple guests. However, some guests had complaints about unhelpful concierge and wait times for services.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's location was praised for being central and convenient, with easy access to key landmarks. However, some noise and congestion issues were noted by a few guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were spacious and well-appointed, offering comfort to guests. Some minor maintenance issues were reported by a few guests.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with highlights on the swimming pool and room features. However, some guests mentioned areas for improvement like the gym location and lack of a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with mention of good variety. However, some guests suggested improvements could be made in this area.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with strong praise for the staff, service, and location. Some areas for improvement were noted but did not significantly impact the overall experience.
