HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe InterContinental San Francisco offers spacious rooms equipped with the latest technologies, flat-screen TVs, and in-room minibars. Enjoy modern amenities such as iPod clock radios and coffee machines for a comfortable stay.Convenient LocationSituated near Union Square, guests have easy access to shopping and dining opportunities. The hotel's proximity to the Moscone Convention Center, Chinatown, and the Powell Street Cable Car makes exploring the city a breeze.Exceptional FacilitiesIndulge in 24-hour fitness facilities, including an indoor lap pool, during your stay. Dine at the on-site Luce restaurant, unwind at Bar 888, or browse the gift shop for souvenirs. Experience luxury and convenience at InterContinental San Francisco.Book your stay now to experience the perfect blend of modern amenities and prime location at InterContinental San Francisco!

• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Guests generally found the rooms to be comfortable, but there were occasional issues such as maintenance problems.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - The hotel offered satisfactory amenities, though some guests noted areas for improvement such as the availability of early coffee.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Mixed opinions on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - While breakfast and dining received positive feedback, there were some suggestions for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
