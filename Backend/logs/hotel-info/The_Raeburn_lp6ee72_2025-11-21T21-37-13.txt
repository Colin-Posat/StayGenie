HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Location in StockbridgeThe Raeburn is ideally situated in the charming Stockbridge area of Edinburgh, offering a picturesque setting within walking distance to Princes Street and the New Town.Stylish Georgian PropertyHoused in a beautiful Georgian property, The Raeburn boasts modern design elements such as exposed stone walls, sash windows, and tartan fabrics, creating a unique and inviting atmosphere for guests.Delightful Dining and Bar ExperienceIndulge in British dishes made with local produce at the hotel's smart, brasserie-style restaurant, and unwind with a drink in the bar featuring a cozy fireplace, bespoke chairs, and a terrace with parasols for a relaxing experience.Experience the charm and elegance of The Raeburn during your stay in Edinburgh.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 16 years old stay for £50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for £50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
