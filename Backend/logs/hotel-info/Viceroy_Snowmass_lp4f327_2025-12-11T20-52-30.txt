HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Year-Round ResortDesigner Interiors and High-End AmenitiesIndulge in designer interiors, kitchenette facilities, and Egyptian cotton sheets in our guest bedrooms. Watch a DVD on your high-definition TV or soak in mountain views from your balcony.Gourmet Dining and RelaxationSavor Pan-Latin-inspired cuisine at TORO Kitchen or unwind by the double-sided fireplace in the well-stocked lobby bar. Enjoy the infinity pool with a waterfall and a state-of-the-art fitness center. Pamper yourself with spa treatments and massages.Convenient Location and Eco-Friendly ServicesJust steps from Snowmass Mountain’s ski lifts, our ski-in/ski-out hotel also offers free electric car charging. With Aspen just 14.5 km away and Aspen-Pitkin County Airport 12 km away, your luxury mountain getaway awaits at Viceroy Snowmass.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
No special conditions apply for groups.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 60 per  day.
