HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated just 400 metres from Gare du Nord Train Station, Hôtel Du Brabant offers easy access to transportation hubs and popular attractions like Montmartre.Comfortable AccommodationThe hotel provides simply decorated en suite rooms with flat-screen TVs and elevator access. Guests can enjoy a continental breakfast each morning and benefit from services like internet access.Nearby AttractionsWith Poissonnière Metro Station a short walk away, guests have direct access to iconic landmarks such as The Louvre Museum and Opéra Garnier. Gare de l’Est Train Station is also conveniently located within a 5-minute walk.Book your stay at Hôtel Du Brabant now for a convenient and comfortable experience in Paris!
