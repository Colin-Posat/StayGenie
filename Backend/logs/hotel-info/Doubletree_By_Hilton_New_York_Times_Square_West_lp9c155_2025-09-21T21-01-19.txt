• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, despite some complaints about maintenance issues in rooms.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Staff received mixed reviews, with some guests praising their helpfulness and friendliness, while others noted bad attitudes and unhelpful behavior.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel's central location near key landmarks and public transport was appreciated by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.4/10 - Rooms were described as small and worn out, with some guests experiencing maintenance issues such as broken furniture and appliances.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - While some guests appreciated amenities like the rooftop bar and gym, others mentioned the lack of certain facilities such as a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable for the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on the breakfast and restaurant services, with some praising them while others suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing good location and friendly staff as highlights, despite some shortcomings in room quality and amenities.
