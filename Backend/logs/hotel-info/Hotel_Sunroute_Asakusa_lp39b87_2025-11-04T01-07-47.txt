HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Recent RenovationHotel Sunroute Asakusa is conveniently located just a 7-minute walk from A1 Exit of Toei Asakusa Station. Recently renovated in January 2019, this hotel offers modern comforts and easy access to transportation.Comfortable Non-Smoking RoomsThe hotel's non-smoking rooms feature soothing earth-tone colors and are well-equipped with essential amenities including en suite bathrooms, air conditioning/heating, and refrigerators. Guests can also enjoy free toiletries, a desk, and a flat-screen TV in every room.Relaxing Services and Nearby AttractionsGuests at Sunroute Asakusa Hotel can unwind with a relaxing massage or take advantage of dry cleaning services. The hotel also offers free newspapers, vending machines, and safety deposit boxes. With easy access to Tokyo Sky Tree and popular districts like Ginza and Tsukiji, there is no shortage of activities to enjoy during your stay.Ready to experience comfort and convenience at Hotel Sunroute Asakusa?
