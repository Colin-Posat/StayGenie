HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic 19th-Century BuildingLocated on La Giudecca island in the Venice Lagoon, Al Redentore Di Venezia is a charming retreat housed in a historic 19th-century building. The self-catering apartments offer a unique opportunity to experience the rich history and culture of Venice in a comfortable setting.Modern AmenitiesEach air-conditioned apartment boasts a luxurious bathroom adorned with Travertine marble. The well-equipped kitchenettes include a dishwasher, microwave, and kettle, providing guests with the convenience of preparing their meals. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi available throughout the property.Convenient Shuttle ServiceWith a 24-hour accessibility, Al Redentore Di Venezia ensures a seamless travel experience. Guests can request a shuttle service to and from Marco Polo Airport, the ferry cruise terminal, and Piazzale Roma municipal car park, making transportation hassle-free.Experience the charm of Venice at Al Redentore Di Venezia. Book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.
