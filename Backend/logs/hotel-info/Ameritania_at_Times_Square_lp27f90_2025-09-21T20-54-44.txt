HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations - Experience the epitome of comfort and style in our guest rooms at Ameritania. Boasting designer bath amenities, cable TVs, and elegant decor, each room offers a lavish retreat in the heart of New York City.Prime Location - Situated in the vibrant Theatre District, Ameritania Hotel is a stone's throw away from iconic landmarks like The Ed Sullivan Theater and The Museum of Modern Art. With the 7th Avenue underground station just steps away, exploring the city is convenient and effortless.Proximity to Central Park - Nestled just 805 meters from the sprawling oasis of Central Park, Ameritania Hotel provides the perfect blend of urban excitement and natural serenity. Immerse yourself in the beauty of the park after a day of sightseeing or shopping.Book now to indulge in luxury and convenience at Ameritania Hotel!

Upon check-in photo identification and credit card are required for incidental charges. All special requests are subject to availability upon check-in. Special requests cannot be guaranteed and may incur additional charges.

- Holding fee for guest packages shipped in advance to hotel 48 hours prior to arrival
- Luggage storage (same day) upon arrival and post checkout
- Complimentary High - Speed WiFi for unlimited devices
- 2 complimentary bottled waters daily
- Complimentary local phone calls
- Neighborhood restaurant discount, please inquire at check-in for details

Guests will pay the Residence Fee plus tax directly at the hotel upon departure.
