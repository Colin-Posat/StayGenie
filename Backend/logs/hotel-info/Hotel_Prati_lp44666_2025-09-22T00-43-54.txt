The proximity to the shuttle bus stop to Fiumicino and Ciampino Airport ensures hassle-free travel arrangements.Simple EleganceThe simply furnished rooms at Prati Hotel boast cool tiled floors and wooden furniture, creating a comfortable and inviting atmosphere for guests to unwind after a day of exploration.Retail TherapyLocated in an upscale neighborhood, the hotel provides easy access to premier shopping destinations.
