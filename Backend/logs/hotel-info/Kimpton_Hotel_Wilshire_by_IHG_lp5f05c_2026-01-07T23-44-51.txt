HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodations with EntertainmentThis modern Los Angeles hotel offers rooms with a flat-screen cable TV. An iPod docking station is featured in all rooms and suites at The Kimpton Hotel Wilshire. Select rooms offer area views.Relaxation and ConvenienceA rooftop pool and a furnished sun terrace are available for guest relaxation. En suite bathrooms boast slippers and plush bathrobes for guest comfort. The non-smoking hotel offers a business centre with laptop stations for guest use.Prime Location and ServicesThe Los Angeles County Museum of Art, La Brea Tar Pits, and the Petersen Automotive Museum are within 15 minutes' walking distance away. Universal Studios Hollywood is 30 minutes’ drive from The Kimpton Hotel Wilshire. Paramount Pictures is 6.4 km away.Book your stay at The Kimpton Hotel Wilshire for a luxurious and convenient experience!
