HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience a serene escape at Puri Wulandari, a Boutique Resort and Spa, where luxury meets nature in the heart of Ubud.Indulgent Spa Treatments: Treat yourself to a rejuvenating experience at our spa, featuring massages, body treatments, and facials that promise to refresh your body and mind.Gourmet Dining Options: Savor exquisite culinary delights at Legong Terrace or enjoy 24-hour room service. Unwind with drinks at our bar/lounge or poolside, ensuring your relaxation never skips a beat.Comfort and Connectivity: Our 35 elegantly decorated rooms offer private balconies and modern amenities, including complimentary Wi-Fi, so you stay connected while immersing yourself in luxury.Immerse yourself in this tranquil haven and book your unforgettable stay today!
