HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 5 per  day.

• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with some guests praising the comfort while others criticized old furniture and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on amenities, with some enjoying the pool and breakfast, while others mentioned missing or faulty services like spa and breakfast equipment.
• Value for Money: 5.3/10 - Varied feedback on the pricing, with some guests feeling it was not justified by the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 6.6/10 - Breakfast and other food services received mixed reviews, with some guests appreciating the options while others noted issues like lack of variety and faulty equipment.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing cleanliness, comfort, and location as key factors.
