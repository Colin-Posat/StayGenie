IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Star Grand Hotel and Residences Sydney in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that there is a 0.8% charge when you pay with a (Visa, Mastercard, American Express etc) credit card.

Please note that the property requires a $100 credit card pre-authorisation at time of check-in to cover any incidental charges or damage to the property.

Self parking is available for AUD 40. Valet parking is available from Sunday to Thursday for AUD 50 and Friday and Saturday for AUD 60.

Please note that special conditions apply for reservations of 10 rooms or more. The hotel will contact you with further information.

The accommodation prices shown online are for bookings of up to 9 rooms only, regardless of room type. Room rates and booking terms may be different for group bookings depending on the number of rooms booked and will be cancelled should it exceed the maximum number bookable online. To make a booking of 10 rooms or more please email us directly: Sales@star.com.au

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for AUD 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and charges may be applicable.

• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were comfortable with modern facilities, with some reports of minor inconveniences like strange smells.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Satisfactory amenities were provided, although some guests wished for more options, such as spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling it was slightly overpriced for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and dining options were generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety and better restaurant service.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
