HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in elegance at Best Western Hotel Rivoli, where each room boasts air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and a marble bathroom. Some rooms even feature a wide-screen TV and minibar for added comfort.Gastronomic DelightsStart your day with a delectable breakfast buffet, complete with fresh fruit and pastries. Unwind in the evening at the chic bar and grill, offering a tempting selection of international drinks and snacks.Convenient LocationSituated in Rome’s prestigious Parioli district, our hotel is in close proximity to Villa Borghese, the Auditorium, and the Modern Art Gallery. With excellent public transport links, including buses to Roma Termini Train Station, exploring the city is a breeze.Experience luxury and convenience at Best Western Hotel Rivoli - book your stay now!

• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Guests had mixed feelings about the value for money, with some finding it expensive for the services provided.

• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
