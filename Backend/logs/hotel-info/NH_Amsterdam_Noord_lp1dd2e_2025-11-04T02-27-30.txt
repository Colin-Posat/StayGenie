IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A maximum of 2 children may stay free of charge in their parents' room if the room size allows this.

When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Please note that dogs and cats are allowed upon request and subject to approval. The maximum weight is 25 kg. A charge of EUR 25 per night will be applied (max of 2 pets per room). Guide dogs stay free of charge.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were clean, with cleanliness being consistently mentioned as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was appreciated overall, with friendly staff mentioned frequently. Some guests had complaints about noise and communication.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's proximity to key locations and shuttle bus service were highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, although some guests noted issues with the bed firmness and noise levels.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with mentions of a shuttle service and good breakfast buffet. Some guests noted a lack of variety and noise from the road.
• Value for Money: 7.1/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding the hotel expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and food services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel. Staff friendliness and location were highlighted as key positives.
