Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were clean and well-maintained, with guests consistently mentioning cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8/10 - Friendly and helpful staff were a highlight for many guests, with a few mentions of slow service.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel's proximity to downtown Banff and key landmarks was appreciated by guests, although a few found the edge-of-town location inconvenient.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were comfortable with good amenities, but some guests noted issues such as room size and outdated decor.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the pool and sauna, but some guests desired more modern facilities like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the services provided and others finding it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Restaurant services were generally well-received, with few mentions of high prices or limited options.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the comfortable beds, friendly staff, and convenient location, although some mentioned room size and pricing as drawbacks.
