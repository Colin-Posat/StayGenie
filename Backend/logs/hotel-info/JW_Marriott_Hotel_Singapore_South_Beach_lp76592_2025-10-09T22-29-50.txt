HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in Singapore's Cultural DistrictFeaturing a contemporary design, JW Marriott Hotel Singapore South Beach offers state-of-the-art accommodation conveniently located in the heart of Singapore's arts and cultural district near Marina Bay. The property boasts 2 sky gardens, 2 infinity pools, and a fitness centre for all in-house guests.Modern Amenities and Convenient LocationNational Gallery Singapore is 750 metres away, while iconic landmarks like the Singapore Flyer, Marina Bay Sands, and Gardens By The Bay are within walking distance. With stylish rooms equipped with USB charging sockets, LED night lights, and high-tech bathrooms, the hotel ensures a comfortable stay.Culinary Delights and Event SpacesIndulge in all-day dining, Japanese or Cantonese cuisine, and unwind at one of the 3 in-house bars. For business or events, the hotel offers heritage-listed event spaces and an executive club lounge with work stations.Experience luxury and convenience at JW Marriott Hotel Singapore South Beach. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  SGD 2.40 per  hour.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally described as clean, with some guests mentioning minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Service was appreciated by most guests, although a few had complaints about check-in processes, customer service recovery, and coordination issues.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel is well-located with easy access to key landmarks and public transport, which was praised by many guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally spacious and well-designed, with some concerns about dampness and smell in a few cases.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with a mix of positive feedback on the gym, pool, and breakfast, but also some mentions of limited facilities like showers.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with some suggestions for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, praising the friendly staff and great location.
