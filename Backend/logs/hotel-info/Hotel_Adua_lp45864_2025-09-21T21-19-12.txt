HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Venetian BuildingHotel Adua, a charming property nestled next to the Jewish Ghetto, offers a unique stay in a 17th-century Venetian building. The historical essence of the architecture provides a truly authentic Venetian experience for guests.Convenient LocationSituated just a 4-minute walk from Venezia Santa Lucia Station, Hotel Adua's prime location allows guests easy access to explore Venice. Whether it's strolling through the nearby Jewish Ghetto or venturing into the heart of the city, the hotel serves as the perfect starting point for your Venetian adventures.Helpful Staff and Budget TipsThe friendly team at Hotel Adua goes above and beyond to ensure a pleasant stay, offering valuable tips for exploring Venice on a budget. Their insider knowledge enhances the overall guest experience, making your visit to Venice even more memorable.Experience the charm and convenience of Hotel Adua - book your stay now for an unforgettable Venetian getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 2 years old and over stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

• Room Quality: 5.9/10 - Mixed reviews on room quality, with complaints about outdated furniture, uncomfortable beds, and poor condition of the rooms.
• Amenities: 5.2/10 - Feedback on amenities was below average, with guests mentioning lack of toilet paper, soap, and limited charging spaces for phones.
• Value for Money: 4.3/10 - Most guests found the hotel not to offer good value for money, citing issues such as broken air conditioning, outdated rooms, and poor maintenance.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 4.6/10 - Overall, the hotel received mixed reviews with significant complaints on various aspects, leading to a low average rating for the overall experience.
