HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Outdoor Pool and Free Wi-FiThe Hilton Garden Inn Atlanta Midtown offers a refreshing outdoor pool for guests to enjoy, perfect for a relaxing swim after a day of exploring the city. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi available throughout the property, ensuring you can easily share your travel adventures with friends and family.Comfortable Rooms and Convenient AmenitiesEach room at this hotel is equipped with modern conveniences such as a flat-screen cable TV, alarm clock with an MP3 docking feature, and a work desk. Upgrade to a suite for extra space and added comfort during your stay. Additionally, take advantage of the fitness center, business center, and on-site launderette for all your needs.Great Dining Options and Nearby AttractionsIndulge in delicious meals at The Great American Grille restaurant located on-site, offering a variety of dining options for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, including convenient take-out choices. Explore nearby attractions like the Georgia Aquarium and the World of Coca Cola, both just a short distance away, making your stay at Hilton Garden Inn Atlanta Midtown even more memorable.Book your stay now at Hilton Garden Inn Atlanta Midtown for a delightful and convenient experience in the heart of Atlanta.
