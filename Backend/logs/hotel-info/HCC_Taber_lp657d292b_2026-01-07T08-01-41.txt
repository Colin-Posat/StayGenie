HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Impressive Modernist BuildingHotel HCC Taber, located a short walk from Plaza Catalunya, boasts a stunning Modernist building designed by the renowned architect Domènech i Montaner. The architecture features wrought-iron balconies and large bay windows, adding to its charm.Comfortable Rooms and AmenitiesThe air-conditioned rooms at HCC Taber offer free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and a minibar. The private bathrooms come equipped with a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests.Convenient Dining Options and Nearby AttractionsGuests can enjoy a buffet breakfast with a variety of dishes or opt for à-la-carte options. Additionally, the hotel features a snack bar and room service. Explore the nearby attractions such as the Ramblas, Casa Batlló, and La Pedrera, all within a 10-minute walk from the hotel.Book your stay at Hotel HCC Taber for a memorable Barcelona experience!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for € 70 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
