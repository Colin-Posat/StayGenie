HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views of Marmara Sea: Enjoy elegant rooms at Sidonya Hotel with breathtaking views. Relax in the sauna or dine on the terrace overlooking the sea.Modern Comforts: All rooms are soundproofed with modern décor, air conditioning, and flat-screen TVs. Unwind on parquet floors and enjoy satellite channels at your fingertips.Delicious Dining Options: Indulge in regional delicacies at the à la carte and buffet restaurant. Sip on Turkish tea or choose from a variety of beverages at the bar.Experience convenience at its best with Kadikoy Ferry Port nearby for easy city access. Book your stay at Hotel Sidonya to explore Istanbul's iconic sites effortlessly.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 4 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
