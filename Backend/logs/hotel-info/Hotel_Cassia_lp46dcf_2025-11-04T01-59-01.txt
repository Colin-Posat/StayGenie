HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tranquil Location in Rome's NorthHotel Cassia offers a peaceful retreat in the green and residential northern part of Rome, providing a quiet escape from the bustling city center.Convenient Access to Major AttractionsLocated near attractions like the archaeological park of Veio, Lake Bracciano, and the Vatican City, Hotel Cassia makes exploring Rome's historical and cultural sites effortless for guests.Comfortable Rooms and Exceptional ServiceWith finely decorated rooms and attentive staff, Hotel Cassia ensures a comfortable stay for discerning travelers, promising a memorable experience in the heart of Rome.To experience the tranquility and convenience of Hotel Cassia, book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
