HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Tropical-Style Interiors with Private BalconyDe Wiang Kum Kam offers a unique stay in the Underground City with modern tropical-style interiors and private balconies in each room. Guests can enjoy the cozy atmosphere and take advantage of the free round-trip shuttle service to key locations like the airport and Night Bazaar.Convenient Amenities and ServicesThe hotel provides a range of convenient amenities including free Wi-Fi, on-site parking, and a tour desk. With 24-hour reception, luggage storage, and free bicycle rental, guests' needs are well taken care of. Additionally, enjoy relaxing massage treatments and delicious dining at Yellow Square Restaurant.Prime Location and AccessibilityLocated just a 10-minute ride from Chiang Mai International Airport, Night Bazaar, and the Train Station, De Wiang Kum Kam offers easy access to major attractions. Explore the city effortlessly with free scheduled shuttle services and reach Central Kad Suan Kaew Department Store within 30 minutes.Book your stay at De Wiang Kum Kam for a memorable experience in Wiang Kum Kam area.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests who wish to use the complimentary round-trip shuttle service from Chiang Mai Airport, Chiang Mai Bus Terminal and Chiang Mai Railway Station are kindly requested by the hotel to provide travel details at the time of booking under "Special Requests". Guests can also contact the hotel directly prior to the travel date with information found the booking confirmation.

Please note that the complimentary shuttle service to Night Bazaar runs on schedule. Please contact the hotel for more information.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
People no matter the age stay for THB 500 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
