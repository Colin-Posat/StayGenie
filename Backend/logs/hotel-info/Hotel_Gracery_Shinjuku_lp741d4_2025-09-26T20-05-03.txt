HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Comfortable Rooms and Convenient LocationHotel Gracery Shinjuku, open since April 2015, offers comfortable rooms with free WiFi just a 5-minute walk from JR Shinjuku Station. Seibu Shinjuku Station is also conveniently close, just a 3-minute walk away.Exceptional Services and AmenitiesThe hotel provides 24-hour reception, dry cleaning, laundry, and ironing services. Guests can enjoy a Japanese-style and Western breakfast buffet at the on-site restaurant. The multilingual concierge service and currency exchange machine add to the convenience.Unique Features and Nearby AttractionsNotably, the hotel terrace boasts the iconic Godzilla Head, offering a unique experience. Within walking distance, guests can explore Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden and Shinjuku Golden Gai, making Hotel Gracery Shinjuku the perfect choice for a memorable stay.Book now for an unforgettable experience at Hotel Gracery Shinjuku!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.1/10 - The majority of guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, although some reviews mentioned specific cleanliness issues.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally well-received, with positive comments about the friendly and responsive staff. Some reviews highlighted delays in addressing issues.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's central location in Shinjuku was a standout feature for many guests, offering convenience and easy access to attractions.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Guests had mixed opinions about room quality, with some mentioning comfortable beds and amenities, while others reported maintenance issues and small room sizes.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Some guests praised the amenities like the laundry room, while others mentioned the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Reviews were divided on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high compared to the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and dining services, although there were suggestions for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, although there were isolated negative experiences.
