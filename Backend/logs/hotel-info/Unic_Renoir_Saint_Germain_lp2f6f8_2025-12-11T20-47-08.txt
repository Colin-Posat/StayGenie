HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Luxembourg GardensHotel Unic Renoir Saint Germain is conveniently situated between Saint Germain and the Latin Quarter, a mere 700 metres from the picturesque Luxembourg Gardens. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of Paris right at your doorstep.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesIndulge in the comfort of air-conditioned, soundproof rooms featuring satellite TV, minibar, and en suite bathrooms with showers. With all rooms serviced by an elevator, convenience is key during your stay.Convenient Facilities and ServicesStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast in the bright breakfast room or opt for breakfast in your room. Unwind at the hotel's bar or terrace, and take advantage of the 24-hour reception service. Private parking can be arranged upon request for a hassle-free stay.Experience the best of Paris at Hotel Unic Renoir Saint Germain - book your stay now!
