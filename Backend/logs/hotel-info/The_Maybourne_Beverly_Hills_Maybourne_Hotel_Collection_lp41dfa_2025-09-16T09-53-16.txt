HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in Frette bathrobes, a rooftop pool, and a spa with a sauna and hot tub at The Maybourne Beverly Hills.Exquisite Dining ExperienceSavor California-inspired cuisine at The Terrace while overlooking the beautiful Beverly Canon Gardens, and enjoy crafted cocktails at the Maybourne Bar.Convenient LocationLocated just a short drive from popular attractions like Crypto.Com Arena, SoFi Stadium, and the Hollywood Bowl, The Maybourne Beverly Hills offers easy access to the best of Los Angeles.Experience luxury, fine dining, and convenience all in one place at The Maybourne Beverly Hills.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 65 per  day.
