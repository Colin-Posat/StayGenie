HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesSet in the 9th district, Vintage Hostel Gare du Nord is just 500 meters from Gare du Nord Train Station and a short walk from the Moulin Rouge. Anvers Metro Station is also nearby, offering easy access to Paris's top attractions.Comfortable AccommodationGuest rooms at the hostel come with private bathrooms and some even have LCD TVs. Dormitory rooms are equipped with a hairdryer and provide access to shared bathrooms. The hostel also features a 24-hour reception and free WiFi throughout the property.Leisure and Dining OptionsEnjoy a continental breakfast with croissants and hot beverages each morning in the lounge, which includes a TV and computers. Stay entertained with table football or work out in the free fitness room. Additionally, vending machines and a communal kitchen are available for your convenience.Book now for a delightful stay at Vintage Hostel Gare du Nord!
