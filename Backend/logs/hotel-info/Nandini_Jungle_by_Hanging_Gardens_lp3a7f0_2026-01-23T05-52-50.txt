HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Jungle RetreatSerenity in NatureNandini Jungle by Hanging Gardens is a hidden gem nestled in the Ayung River Valley, offering a tranquil escape surrounded by lush jungle greenery. Guests can unwind at the hillside pool, soak in the outdoor hot tub, or explore hidden paths leading to a jungle river.Exquisite Dining and ActivitiesIndulge in a culinary journey at Wild Ginger Restaurant, savoring a mix of local and international flavors. Engage in resort activities like yoga, trekking, and cycling, or simply relax with daily afternoon tea and a welcome massage. Don't forget to visit the Warung Teh Lounge for a delightful selection of teas.Balinese Charm and ComfortExperience authentic Balinese hospitality in the traditional chalets featuring private balconies with breathtaking jungle views. Each chalet is elegantly furnished with wooden accents, modern amenities, and a luxurious bathroom for a comfortable stay.Book now for an unforgettable jungle retreat at Nandini Bali Jungle Resort.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 150,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 1,936,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
