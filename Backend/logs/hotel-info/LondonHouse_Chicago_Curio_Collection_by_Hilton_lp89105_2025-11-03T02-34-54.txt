HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Bar with Stunning ViewsLondonHouse boasts Chicago's only tri-level rooftop bar, offering breathtaking views of the city skyline and historic landmarks. Guests can unwind with a drink while taking in the beauty of the Chicago River.Luxurious AccommodationsGuest rooms at LondonHouse are spacious and well-equipped, featuring free WiFi, a large work station, and a 55-inch flat-screen TV. Some rooms even offer panoramic views of the Chicago skyline, providing a truly immersive experience for guests.Fine Dining ExperienceIndulge in reimagined regional cuisine and expertly crafted cocktails at on-site LH restaurant. The unique terrace overlooking the river and dining underneath the 23rd story domed cupola create a memorable dining atmosphere.Book your stay at LondonHouse Chicago, Curio Collection by Hilton, and experience luxury and convenience in the heart of the Windy City.
