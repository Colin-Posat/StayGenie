HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views and Resort AccommodationExperience the allure of Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus with its breathtaking views of the Bosphorus Strait and luxurious resort amenities. Enjoy the convenience of being a short stroll away from Taksim Square and indulge in the outdoor and indoor pools, lush gardens, and a traditional Turkish bath.Culinary Delights and Scenic DiningSavor Cantonese and Szechuan delicacies at the Dragon Restaurant, dine with panoramic nature views at the open-air restaurants, and unwind at the Veranda Bar&amp;Terrace overlooking the picturesque Bosphorus. Explore the flavors of Indian cuisine at the Dubb Indian Bosphorus Restaurant, catering to all taste preferences with children's menus and high chairs available.Family-Friendly Fun and EntertainmentCreate lasting memories with your family at Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus, offering playgrounds for toddlers and kids, along with amenities like bottle warmers and baby carriages. With easy access to entertainment options and nearby attractions, your stay promises to be both enjoyable and convenient.Book now to secure your unforgettable stay at Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus, where luxury and convenience meet in the heart of Istanbul.

Outdoor Kids Swimming Pool is closed from Tue 05 Nov 2024 until Thu 15 May 2025
Kindly note that an interior upgrade is currently being undertaken in one the hotel's wings, carried out between 10AM and 5PM. Guests may rest assured that our Garden View Rooms are located in a separate wing, and hence continue to offer the hotel's usual five star service and hospitality.

Guests staying at executive and suite type rooms have privileged access to Executive Lounge. Lounge features special check-in check-out area and a computer area. Tea, coffee and non-alcoholic drinks are served between 07:30 - 23:00. Alcoholic drinks are also available while guests are also served snack certain times of the day.

Please note that the rates on this website are quoted in EUR but that guests are charged in local currency at the front desk. Rates are based on the daily exchange rate.

When guests made a booking for Half Board meal plan “in package with breakfast & dinner", beverages are not included”

Food and beverage services for children under the age of 6 are free of charge. For children between the ages of 7 and 10, food and beverages are offered at a 50% discount.

Guests can benefit from "Hilton Marhaba" programme, including in-room prayer mats, daily Arabic coffee service, Arabic speaking front desk and concierge desk team members, afternoon tea service in the hotel lobby lounge, a selection of Arabic TV channels and Arabic newspapers.

Please note that individuals under the age of 18 cannot be accommodated without their parents.

The hotel offers facilities for younger guests, including a garden with playgrounds for toddlers and kids, outdoor swimming pool and peddle pool. Toys, scooters, bicycles and colouring books are available for free. Bottle warmers, baby carriages, and other similar items are readily available for fee, rent or purchase. Children's menus, high chairs, in-room children's television stations, kids tooth brush, thermometer and bottle warmer, digital sterilizer, and kids check-in desk is available. If you want to benefit from these facilities, please request from the hotel staff.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 12 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children 17 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
