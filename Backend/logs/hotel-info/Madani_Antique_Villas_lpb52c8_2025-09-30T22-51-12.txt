HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsMadani Antique Villas in Ubud offers a tranquil setting with picturesque rice field and pool views. The villas feature elegant decor, spa treatments, and a relaxing hot tub, ensuring a comfortable stay.Relaxation and RecreationEnjoy the outdoor pool, sip on refreshing drinks at the bar, and indulge in delicious packed lunches. The property also offers various activities such as painting, cooking classes, and yoga sessions for a rejuvenating experience.Convenience at Your FingertipsBenefit from shuttle services, tour arrangements, and vehicle hire options provided by the hotel for hassle-free exploration. With Ubud's attractions nearby, including the Palace and Monkey Forest, Madani Antique Villas is the perfect choice for a memorable getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Madani Antique Villas in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that the property offers the following complimentary services:

- Welcome drinks and cold towels upon arrival
- Daily breakfast for 2 persons based on the rates chosen
- 2 bottles of mineral water
- Free WiFi connection throughout the property
- Free scheduled shuttle service to Ubud Centre

The following daily activities based on requested schedule are available with additional charges:

- Painting classes
- Cooking classes
- Yoga and meditation classes
- Morning trekking to rice fields based on requested schedule (optional)
- Making Balinese coconut leaf craft based on requested schedule (optional)
- Making Balinese Canang Sari offerings based on requested schedule

The property can arrange excursions, day tours, as well as car, motorbike and driver hire, with additional charges.

Guests can also enjoy our spa treatment and we do have restaurant on site for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for Rp 200,000 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 8 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for Rp 300,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
