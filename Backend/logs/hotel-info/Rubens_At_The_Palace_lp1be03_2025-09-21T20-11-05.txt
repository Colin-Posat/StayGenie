HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique Hotel Overlooking Buckingham PalaceIndividually Designed Rooms: Each room at The Rubens at the Palace is a unique blend of luxury and comfort, providing a homely feel with thoughtful touches.Culinary Excellence: Indulge in the finest food and beverage offerings in London at the flagship English Grill restaurant, where signature classics and seasonal delights are grilled to perfection in a live-action kitchen.Royal Experiences: From enjoying morning coffee with panoramic views at The Palace Lounge to embarking on an authentic culinary journey at the Curry Room, every moment at The Rubens is filled with royal elegance and top-notch service.Book your stay at The Rubens at the Palace for an unforgettable experience in the heart of London.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please be aware that on arrival at the hotel a credit or debit card or cash deposit must be provided for a pre charge/ authorisation for the accommodation and for any extras. As a standard hotel policy, upon arrival an amount of £100.00 per night will be temporarily pre authorised on your card or taken as cash deposit towards any extras during your stay with us. Anything that is not used will be released from your card upon your departure from the Hotel.
