Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests generally mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, although a few reviews reported some cleanliness issues such as unclean plates and cups.
• Service: 8.6/10 - Service was well-received by guests, with special mentions for approachable staff and accommodating late check-out requests.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised due to its proximity to key landmarks and convenient access to public transport.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, with positive comments on the bed comfort and amenities. However, some guests found the rooms dated.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with mixed reviews mentioning good breakfast options but also a lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - There were mixed opinions on the value for money, with some guests finding the pricing reasonable for the location while others felt it was slightly high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, but some suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
