HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near DisneylandLocated just seconds away from Disneyland, the Embassy Suites by Hilton Anaheim North offers spacious all-suite accommodations perfect for families and leisure travelers. Situated close to major attractions like Knott's Berry Farm and corporate offices, guests can easily explore the best of Anaheim.Complimentary Amenities and ServicesEnjoy free shuttle service to Disneyland and the Convention Center, on-site ticket purchases for theme parks, and a delicious cooked-to-order breakfast. Unwind in the tranquil ambiance of the hotel, featuring a soothing waterfall, koi ponds, and exotic plants.On-Site Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in on-site dining options and evening receptions, providing convenience and relaxation during your stay. Book now to experience a seamless blend of comfort and convenience at the Embassy Suites by Hilton Anaheim North.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.3/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, some guests praised the cleanliness while others mentioned issues like bad odor, dirty carpets, and stained furniture.
• Service: 6.8/10 - Service received mixed feedback with complaints about customer service, staff friendliness, and delayed check-ins.
• Location: 7.2/10 - Most guests found the location convenient, while a few mentioned it being far from attractions or noisy due to freeway proximity.
• Room Quality: 6.5/10 - Rooms had mixed reviews with praise for comfortable beds but criticism for outdated rooms, broken shower heads, and room maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.4/10 - Guests highlighted amenities like breakfast and arcade, while some complained about limited amenities, expensive parking, and lack of room service.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Mixed feedback on pricing, some guests felt it was overpriced for the offered services, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Breakfast and food services received positive feedback overall, with some guests suggesting more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed satisfaction with their stay.
