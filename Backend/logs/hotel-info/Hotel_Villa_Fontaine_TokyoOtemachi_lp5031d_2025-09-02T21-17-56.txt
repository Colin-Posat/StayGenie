HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Comfort and ConvenienceHotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Otemachi offers stylish rooms with free wired internet, flat-screen TVs, and convenient amenities like fridges and electric kettles. Enjoy a comfortable stay with provided nightwear and slippers, along with a well-equipped en suite bathroom.Convenient FacilitiesGuests can take advantage of the coin-launderette, drink vending machines, and the option to borrow a trouser press for in-room use. The 24-hour front desk offers luggage storage and photocopying services for added convenience.Prime LocationLocated just a 5-minute walk from JR Kanda Train Station, this hotel provides easy access to Tokyo Station and various attractions. Explore the city with ease, including a short subway ride to popular destinations like the Imperial Palace and Asakusa area.Book your stay at Hotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Otemachi now for a memorable experience in Tokyo!
