HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury in the Heart of RomeAt Hotel Imperiale by OMNIA hotels, experience the epitome of luxury in a 19th-century building on Rome's iconic Via Veneto. Luxurious Rooms and Amenities await, with fine fabrics, marble interiors, and modern comforts like hydromassage baths, flat-screen TVs, and minibars. Each room is uniquely designed, offering a touch of elegance and comfort.Gourmet Dining and Convenient LocationStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast and dine at the Gazebo restaurant overlooking Via Veneto. Explore the vibrant neighborhood and visit nearby attractions like the Spanish Steps and Villa Borghese park, all within walking distance. Relaxation and Convenience are key, with massage services available and easy access to public transportation.Indulge in a luxurious stay at Hotel Imperiale - book now for an unforgettable Roman getaway.
