HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Moroccan Guest Houses in Marrakech MedinaExperience the charm of Marrakech Medina at our 6 typical Moroccan guest houses, just a short stroll from Jamaâ El Fna Square and Bahia Palace. Each room and suite features a private bathroom for your comfort.Exclusive Spa and Outdoor Plunge PoolsPamper yourself with a rejuvenating massage at our exclusive spa, complete with a Moorish-style indoor hot tub and hammam. Some Riads also boast outdoor plunge pools for a refreshing dip. You have full access to all the facilities across our Riads Collection.Culinary Delights at Riad Si SaidSavor the flavors of Morocco at Riad Si Said restaurant, where guests can relish traditional Moroccan cuisine. Immerse yourself in the rich culinary heritage of the region during your stay.Book now to immerse yourself in the luxury and culture of Marrakech at our exquisite guest houses.
