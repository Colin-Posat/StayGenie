HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Garden View Public Bath:Experience modern comfort at Mitsui Garden Hotel Kyoto Sanjo, where you can unwind in a public bath overlooking a serene Japanese garden.Convenient Location and Amenities:Just a stone's throw away from Karasuma Oike Subway Station, this 4-star hotel offers free WiFi, on-site dining, and easy access to Kyoto's top attractions including Nijo-jo castle and Heian Jingu Shrine.Delicious Buffet Breakfast and Cozy Guestrooms:Start your day with a sumptuous buffet breakfast at Kyoto Dining Mikura and retreat to well-appointed guestrooms featuring a work desk, flat-screen TV, and climate control for a restful stay.Book now to indulge in a blend of modern luxury and traditional charm at Mitsui Garden Hotel Kyoto Sanjo.
