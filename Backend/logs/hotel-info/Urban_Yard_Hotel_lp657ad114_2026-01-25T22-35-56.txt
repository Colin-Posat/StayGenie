HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Elegant Art Nouveau Townhouses:Experience the charm of 2 Art Nouveau townhouses seamlessly blending contemporary style with natural science and elegance. Each room boasts its own unique character, offering a picturesque stay that is both sophisticated and inviting.Japanese Garden Terrace:Relax and unwind on the spacious terrace overlooking a serene Japanese garden, a perfect spot to enjoy a peaceful moment amidst the bustling city. Let the tranquil ambiance elevate your stay and provide a delightful escape from the urban hustle.Convenient Location and Modern Amenities:Benefit from a prime location just a short walk from Brussels' iconic Grand Place and enjoy the convenience of free WiFi throughout the hotel. Indulge in a delicious continental breakfast buffet with warm extras and make use of the stylish lobby and computer corner for added comfort during your stay.Book now to immerse yourself in the unique blend of history and modernity at the Urban Yard Hotel!
