HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Inn in Historic San Francisco NeighborhoodUnique Guestrooms and Exceptional ServicesLocated in one of San Francisco's oldest neighborhoods, Monte Cristo Bed and Breakfast offers a charming stay surrounded by a variety of restaurants and shops. Guests can enjoy unique guestrooms and first-rate services, including a continental breakfast with fresh fruits and gluten-free options. After breakfast, unwind in the cozy guestrooms equipped with flat-screen TVs and complimentary Wi-Fi.Convenient Location for Shopping and DiningSituated within walking distance of Union Street and Fillmore Street, guests can indulge in first-class shopping experiences.
