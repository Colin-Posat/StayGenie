IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
We are excited to inform you that our hotel will be undergoing a renovation of guest rooms. The work will be carried out in phases to ensure a smooth transition and minimal disruption for our guests.
The renovation will be conducted from April 2025 through January 2026, with updates to rooms and halls being completed in sequences to ensure a smooth transition between floors.
The hotel will remain fully operational throughout the renovation, with all services continuing without interruption.
Renovation work will take place from 9:00 a.m. to 5:30 p.m., Monday to Friday, to ensure minimal disruption.
We greatly appreciate your understanding and patience as we work to enhance your future experience with us.

Please note that due to the recent changes in Tax legislation all cash payments are subject to a 2% sales tax.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
