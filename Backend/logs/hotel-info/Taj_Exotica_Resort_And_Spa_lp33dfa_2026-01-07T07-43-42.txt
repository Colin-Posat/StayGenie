HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Escape to the luxurious Taj Exotica Resort and Spa, a tranquil haven located on the stunning waterfront of Emboodhu Finolhu.Revitalize Your SensesIndulge in a full-service spa that offers rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials, all designed to refresh your mind and body.Endless Leisure ActivitiesSoak in the sun at our private beach, take a dip in the outdoor pool, or maintain your fitness routine at our 24-hour health club.Culinary Delights AwaitSavor international cuisine at 24 Degrees or enjoy a night in with 24-hour room service. Quench your thirst at one of our beach bars or poolside bar.Unwind in one of 64 individually decorated guest rooms, fitted with premium amenities and breathtaking views. Book your stay at Taj Exotica Resort and Spa today for an unforgettable escape!

• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
