HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated in Venice’s historic centre, behind Santa Fosca Church, Characteristic Hotel Tintoretto is just a 5-minute walk from Rialto Bridge and the Venetian Ghetto. Situated off the Strada Nuova street and a short walk from the train station, it offers easy access to the Grand Canal and is surrounded by shops and restaurants.Comfortable RoomsThe elegant and en suite rooms at Tintoretto are well-furnished, featuring amenities such as satellite TV and air conditioning. With most rooms offering charming canal views, guests can enjoy a cozy and relaxing stay.Delicious Breakfast and Nearby AmenitiesStart your day with a sweet breakfast served daily at the hotel. Additionally, the S.Marcuola waterbus stop is just a 5-minute stroll away, providing convenient transportation options for exploring Venice.Book your stay at Characteristic Hotel Tintoretto now for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Venice.
