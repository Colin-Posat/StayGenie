HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Convenient LocationHotel Resol Ikebukuro provides a contemporary stay just steps away from Higashi Ikebukuro Station. The hotel boasts a restaurant, massage services, and complimentary WiFi, ensuring a comfortable and connected experience.Well-Equipped GuestroomsEach guestroom at Resol Ikebukuro is equipped with air conditioning, heating, a humidifier, a flat-screen TV, and a tea set. The en suite bathrooms are stocked with toiletries and feature a relaxing bathtub, catering to guests' comfort and convenience.Easy Access to Attractions and AirportsWith its proximity to JR Ikebukuro Train Station, guests can easily explore Ginza and Roppongi. Moreover, the hotel offers convenient access to Haneda and Narita Airports through the Airport Limousine Bus, making travel hassle-free.Book your stay at Hotel Resol Ikebukuro for a seamless blend of modern comforts and convenience.

• Amenities: 7.4/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with particular praise for the availability of pyjamas, amenities at the reception, and daily essentials provided.
