HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the ultimate retreat at Volcano Lodge Hotel &amp; Thermal Experience, where relaxation meets adventure in the heart of La Fortuna.Water WonderlandDip into one of our 4 outdoor swimming pools or unwind in the soothing hot springs and sauna—perfect for rejuvenation after a day of exploration.Culinary DelightsIndulge in delightful meals at Restaurante Sura, or opt for convenient room service. Don’t forget to enjoy a refreshing drink from our poolside bar while basking in the sun.Comfort &amp; ConvenienceOur 66 air-conditioned rooms come with private patios, refrigerators, and LCD televisions, ensuring a cozy environment complemented by complimentary Wi-Fi and full daily breakfast.With so much to offer, make your reservation today for an unforgettable stay!

* Hot springs access available from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
