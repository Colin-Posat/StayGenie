HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stylish Rooms with Modern AmenitiesRiva Surya Bangkok offers chic air-conditioned rooms with complimentary WiFi, flat-screen TV, iPod docking station, and safety deposit box. Some rooms even boast a private balcony for a touch of luxury.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind at the hotel's fitness center or take a dip in one of the two swimming pools designed for both adults and kids. Enjoy the scenic views of the river while savoring authentic Thai, Asian, and European cuisines at the babble &amp; rum Restaurant.Convenient Location and ServicesSituated just minutes away from Khao San Road and The Bangkok National Museum, the hotel provides easy access to key attractions. Benefit from 24-hour front desk assistance, laundry services, and free private parking for a hassle-free stay. Book now to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Riva Surya Bangkok.
