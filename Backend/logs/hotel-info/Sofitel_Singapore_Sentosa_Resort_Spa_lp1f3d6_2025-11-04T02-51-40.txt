HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsNestled amidst tropical woodlands, the Sofitel Singapore Sentosa Resort &amp; Spa offers 211 well-appointed rooms and suites, along with four opulent villas providing a palatial retreat. Each accommodation is elegantly designed for a comfortable and lavish stay.World-Class Spa and DiningIndulge in the largest Sofitel SPA in the world, offering a range of rejuvenating treatments. With three dining establishments and alfresco options, guests can savor exquisite culinary experiences amidst lush greenery and fresh sea breezes.Versatile Event SpacesFrom 12 meeting rooms to two ballrooms, the Celebrations &amp; Events Centre caters to various gatherings. Whether for business meetings or special occasions, the Resort provides an ideal setting for memorable events.Experience luxury, relaxation, and versatility at Sofitel Singapore Sentosa Resort &amp; Spa – book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that guests with pre-paid, non-cancellable bookings must present the same credit card used to make your booking when checking in at the hotel. Please note that the full amount of the reservation will be charged to the credit card upon booking confirmation. Credit cards with declined payments may result in cancellation of the booking at the Resort. If you are making payment using another cardholder's credit card, kindly provide the following to the Resort at least 7 days prior to your arrival: 1) Authorization letter with cardholder's signature 2) Copy of the cardholder's card (front and back of card with cardholder's signature) Please note that Resort may contact the cardholder for verification purposes. Please note that this is a strictly non-smoking Resort. Designated outdoor smoking areas can be found along the walkways near the main entrance of the Resort. Please note that guests should indicate under Special Requests during booking they require an airport transfer (charges apply). Guests can also contact the Resort directly (contact details can be found on the booking confirmation) at least 48 hours before arrival. For bookings that include the Spa Package, please note that the 60-minute massage treatment is only applicable for either So Relaxing Massage or The Sentosa Massage, and is not refundable or exchangeable for other spa treatments. The massage treatment is valid for use during stay period and cannot be amended to other dates if not utilised. You can contact the Resort using the details provided in your booking confirmation.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 120 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
