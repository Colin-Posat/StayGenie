GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Great location
• Delicious food

Areas for Improvement:
• Inconsistent room quality
• Limited amenities
• Lack of gluten-free options

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.21/10 - The rooms were clean, but some reviews mentioned that the room quality was inconsistent.
• Service: 9.68/10 - The staff were friendly, kind, and attentive, with specific commendations for multiple team members.
• Location: 9.52/10 - The hotel is in a great location close to the beach, with a quieter atmosphere compared to other areas.
• Room Quality: 8.17/10 - The room quality varied with some guests dissatisfied with the balcony view and room comfort.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Amenities such as the pools were mentioned as small and not well maintained.
• Value for Money: 7.66/10 - Guests noted feeling charged extra for amenities like umbrellas and resort fees without a good value tradeoff.
• Food and Beverage: 8.71/10 - Dinner experiences were described as delicious, but there were issues for those requiring gluten-free options.
• Overall Experience: 9.18/10 - Overall experience rated highly due to service and some positive aspects, despite some complaints.
