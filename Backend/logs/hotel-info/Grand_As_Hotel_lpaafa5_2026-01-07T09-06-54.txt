HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Sea Views and Turkish HospitalityLuxurious Accommodation: Grand As Hotel, just steps away from the Kadikoy ferry port, offers elegantly furnished air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities for a comfortable stay.Culinary Delights: Indulge in authentic Turkish cuisine at the hotel's restaurant with a diverse à la carte menu and enjoy a hearty buffet breakfast each morning. Unwind at the lobby bar or terrace bar with a wide selection of beverages.Convenient Services: With a 24-hour front desk, free Wi-Fi, and complimentary parking, guests can also take advantage of additional services like room service, laundry, and dry cleaning for a hassle-free stay.Experience the perfect blend of convenience and luxury at Grand As Hotel - book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 18 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
