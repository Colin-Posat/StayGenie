HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationWyndham Anaheim is ideally located just a short walk from Disneyland, making it the perfect choice for families and tourists looking to explore the area. The Anaheim Convention Center and Angels Stadium of Anaheim are also within close proximity, offering easy access to major attractions.Comfortable AccommodationEach room at Wyndham Anaheim is equipped with modern amenities such as Free WiFi, satellite TV, and a cozy seating area. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of a microwave, fridge, coffee maker, and hairdryer in their rooms, ensuring a comfortable stay.Relaxing FacilitiesGuests can unwind and relax at the on-site pool or stay active at the fitness centre. The hotel also offers 24-hour front desk service, along with dry cleaning and laundry services for added convenience. With free parking available, guests can enjoy a stress-free stay at Wyndham Anaheim.Book your stay at Wyndham Anaheim now for a memorable and hassle-free experience!
