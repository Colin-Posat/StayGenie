HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in AmsterdamHotel Pagi is perfectly located opposite the Spui Tram Stop, making it convenient for guests to explore the heart of Amsterdam. Just a short walk from Dam Square and a quick tram ride to Central Station, our hotel offers easy access to the city's top attractions.Renovated Rooms with Canal ViewsExperience comfort and style in our newly renovated rooms, each featuring modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV and private bathroom. Select rooms even offer picturesque views of the charming Amsterdam canals, providing a truly unique stay for our guests.Proximity to Major LandmarksWith the bustling Kalverstraat shopping area just steps away and iconic sites like the Anne Frank House and Amsterdam Hermitage within walking distance, Hotel Pagi is the ideal choice for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the rich culture and history of the city.Experience the best of Amsterdam at Hotel Pagi - book your stay with us today!

Please note that all rooms are accessible via steep stairs only in a building without a lift.

Please note that the full amount must be paid upon check-in.

Please note that a pre-authorization will be done on the credit card used for making the reservation.

For non-refundable reservations, the credit card used for the reservation must be showed upon check-in.

Please provide a copy of the passport of the credit card owner and a copy of both sides of the credit card itself in the following cases:
- When the credit card owner is not present upon check-in
- When the name on the reservation does not match with the name on the credit card used for booking
- When guests wish to use a corporate credit card
