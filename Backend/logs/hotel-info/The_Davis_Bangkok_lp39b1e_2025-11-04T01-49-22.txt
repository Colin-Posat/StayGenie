HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Thai-Style Villas and Outdoor Pools:Experience modern Thai luxury at The Davis with its traditional Thai-style villas and 2 outdoor pools, perfect for a relaxing getaway in the heart of Bangkok.Convenient Location and Complimentary Shuttle Service:Situated in the vibrant Klong Toei District, The Davis is just a short walk from Phrom Phong BTS Skytrain Station and Emporium Shopping Mall. Enjoy the convenience of a free Tuk Tuk shuttle service for easy exploration of the city.Fitness Center with Rooftop Whirlpool and Dining Options:Stay active at the hotel's fitness center with a rooftop whirlpool and separate changing rooms. Indulge in delicious buffet spreads at Gallery Cafe, sip on drinks at The Living Room, or grab coffee at Starbucks, all without leaving the property.Book now to experience the ultimate blend of luxury and convenience at The Davis Bangkok!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for THB 1,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.3/10 - Mixed reviews with some guests reporting cleanliness issues and others finding the rooms clean.
• Service: 7.1/10 - Service quality varied, with some guests praising the friendliness of the staff while others mentioned issues with reception and check-in processes.
• Location: 8.2/10 - Most guests appreciated the central location with easy access to shops and restaurants, although traffic congestion was a concern for some.
• Room Quality: 5.9/10 - Reviews were polarized, with some guests highlighting spacious rooms and comfortable beds, while others complained about old furniture and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 3/10 - Several guests expressed disappointment with the lack of amenities and dated facilities.
• Value for Money: 2.7/10 - Many guests felt that the hotel did not offer good value for the price paid, citing hidden charges and poor customer service.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although there were suggestions for improvement in the variety of offerings.
• Overall Experience: 3.8/10 - Opinions on the overall experience were divided, with some guests having positive stays and others describing negative experiences including noise disturbances and poor customer service.
