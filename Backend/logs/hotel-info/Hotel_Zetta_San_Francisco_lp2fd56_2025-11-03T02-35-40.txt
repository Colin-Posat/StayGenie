HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 65 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness, although some mentioned issues with water pressure and dust in certain areas.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Mixed feedback on service, with some praising the helpfulness of the events team and housekeeping, while others criticized the lack of warmth and knowledge at the reception.
• Location: 8.3/10 - Highly rated location with easy access to key landmarks and public transport, although some guests found the nearby area less desirable.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Spacious and comfortable rooms, though some guests experienced noise from conjoining rooms and minor issues with air conditioning.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Satisfactory amenities, with positive mentions of toiletries provided and a negative mention of limited breakfast options.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Mixed feelings on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was inflated for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Varied feedback on food and beverage, with positive mentions of the continental breakfast and negative mentions of limited gluten-free options.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with praise for the convenient location and comfortable rooms, though some had concerns about value for money and service quality.
