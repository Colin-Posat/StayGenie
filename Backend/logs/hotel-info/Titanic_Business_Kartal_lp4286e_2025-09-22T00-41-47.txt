Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.6/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with a few mentions of room for improvement with welcoming non-Turkish tourists.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, with some guests highlighting the presence of a bathtub.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the swimming pool and spa facilities, although some experienced a sewage smell issue in the spa and jacuzzi area.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Opinions were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was reasonable for the services offered, while others found it a bit high.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety and higher coffee quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
