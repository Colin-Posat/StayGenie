HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Eiffel Tower and River SeineLocated just a 10-minute walk from the iconic Eiffel Tower and Champ de Mars, Campanile Paris 15 - Tour Eiffel offers a prime location for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the heart of Paris.
