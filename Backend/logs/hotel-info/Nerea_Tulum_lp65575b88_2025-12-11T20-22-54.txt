HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and RelaxationNerea Tulum, located steps away from Tankah Bay Beach in Tulum, offers a luxurious stay with free bikes, private parking, and an outdoor pool. The 4-star hotel boasts a garden, terrace, and a hot tub for ultimate relaxation.Exquisite Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at Nerea Tulum with a restaurant serving Mediterranean, Seafood, and Steakhouse cuisine. Enjoy a delectable à la carte or American breakfast, with options for vegetarian, dairy-free, and vegan guests.Exciting Activities and Scenic SurroundingsExplore the beauty of Tulum with activities like snorkeling, canoeing, and cycling, or simply unwind along the picturesque beachfront. Soliman Bay Beach and Tulum Archeological Site are nearby for further exploration.Book your luxurious retreat at Nerea Tulum for a perfect blend of comfort, dining, and adventure.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
