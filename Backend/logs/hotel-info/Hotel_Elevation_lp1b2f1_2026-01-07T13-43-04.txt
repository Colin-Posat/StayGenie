HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Beaches and Golf CoursesLocated in South Lake Tahoe, Hotel Elevation is just steps away from El Dorado Beach and a short distance from Lakeside Beach and Lake Tahoe Golf Course. Guests can also easily access Washoe Meadows State Park and Balloons Over Lake Tahoe.Comfortable Accommodation with Multilingual StaffThe hotel offers free WiFi and parking, with bilingual staff fluent in English and Spanish. Guests can enjoy skiing and other activities in the area. The rooms are designed for comfort and relaxation after a day of exploring the nearby attractions.Proximity to Popular Landmarks and AirportHotel Elevation is close to Thomas F Regan Memorial City Beach, Tahoe Queen, and Bijou Golf Course. For convenience, Reno-Tahoe International Airport is located just 89 km away.Book your stay at Hotel Elevation for a memorable South Lake Tahoe experience!
