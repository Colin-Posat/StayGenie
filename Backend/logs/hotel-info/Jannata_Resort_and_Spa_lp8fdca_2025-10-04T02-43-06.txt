HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsYear-Round Outdoor Pool and Spa TreatmentsThe Jannata Resort and Spa boasts a picturesque year-round outdoor pool with lush greenery views, offering a serene retreat for guests. Indulge in relaxing massages and treatments at the spa center while enjoying the tranquil surroundings.Diverse Activities and Dining OptionsWith a helpful multilingual staff, guests can easily arrange exciting activities like cycling and hiking to explore the area. After a day of adventures, savor Western and Asian cuisine at the Jannata Resort Restaurant, offering both continental and à la carte options for a delightful dining experience.Experience luxury and relaxation at Jannata Resort and Spa - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Beautiful jungle views
• Luxurious rooms
• Delicious food

Areas for Improvement:
• Lack of certain amenities like a heated pool
• Mixed reviews on value for money
• Limited menu variety

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.7/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff mentioned frequently.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport, offering amazing jungle views.
• Room Quality: 8.4/10 - Spacious and luxurious rooms with beautiful views and comfortable amenities, although some guests mentioned minor issues.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Satisfactory amenities including a pool, spa, and transportation services, with some guests noting the lack of certain services like a heated pool.
• Value for Money: 7.8/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling it was worth the price for the experience while others found certain aspects lacking.
• Food and Beverage: 8.3/10 - Delicious food options with attentive service, though a few guests suggested more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff, amenities, and location, making it a highly recommended stay.
