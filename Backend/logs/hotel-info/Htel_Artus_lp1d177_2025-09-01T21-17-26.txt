HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique ExperienceHôtel Artus, nestled in the heart of Saint Germain des Près, offers a chic and luxurious boutique hotel experience on Paris' left bank.Sauna and Modern AmenitiesIndulge in relaxation with the hotel's sauna and enjoy the luxury of rooms and suites adorned with soft pastels, bright fabrics, iPod docking stations, and LCD TVs. The private bathrooms with bathrobes and slippers add an extra touch of comfort.Convenient Location and ServicesBenefit from the convenient location just a minute's walk from Mabillon Metro Station. Start your day with a hot and cold buffet in the modern breakfast room, and stay connected with free Wi-Fi. Explore local attractions easily with transport links and unwind with a massage bookable at the 24-hour reception.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hôtel Artus!
