HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Bosphorus ViewsExperience the ultimate luxury at Conrad Istanbul Bosphorus with its upscale suites and contemporary designed rooms offering breathtaking views of the Bosphorus. Each room is elegantly furnished with modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay.Gourmet Dining and Rooftop BarTreat your taste buds at Manzara Restaurant, where you can enjoy Mediterranean delicacies while taking in panoramic views of the Historic Peninsula and Bosphorus. For a more laid-back experience, head to Summit Bar&amp;Terrace on the 14th floor for a wide selection of beverages and appetisers with stunning city and Bosphorus views.Leisure and Business FacilitiesIndulge in the hotel's leisure facilities including open clay tennis courts, spa, fitness centre, Turkish hammam, and indoor/outdoor pools. With business facilities and meeting rooms available, Conrad Istanbul Bosphorus caters to both your relaxation and professional needs.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Conrad Istanbul Bosphorus and immerse yourself in luxury and comfort.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that the rates on this website are quoted in EUR but that guests are charged in local currency at the front desk. Rates are based on the daily exchange rate.

Children under the age of 6 can benefit from food and beverage services free of charge. For children between the ages of 7 and 10, 50% discount is offered. The regular fee is applied for guests aged 11 or older.

Guests under 18 years of age cannot be accommodated without their parents.

Conrad Istanbul Bosphorus offers 4 different luxury bath amenity brands: Tara Smith Vegan Hair Care, Refinery, Aromatherapy Associates, Shanghai Tang. Guests can choose from four distinct bath amenities.

Guests staying in executive and suite-type rooms have privileged access to Executive Lounge. The lounge features special check-in and check-out area. Tea, coffee and non-alcoholic drinks are served between 06:00 - 23:00. Alcoholic drinks are also available while guests are also served snacks at certain times of the day.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 9 per  day.

• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Guests enjoyed comfortable beds and overall room quality, with some feedback suggesting the need for minor updates.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating the spa and swimming pools, while others mentioned a lack of certain services like a full-fledged spa.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some finding it a bit high for the offerings, while others considered it reasonable given the prime location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and dining services, although some guests desired more variety and better coffee options.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hotel and recommending it for future stays.
