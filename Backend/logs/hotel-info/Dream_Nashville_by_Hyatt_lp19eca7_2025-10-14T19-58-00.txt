HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Landmarked Buildings and Rich Heritage:Dream Nashville is a unique blend of history and modern luxury, housed in 2 historic landmarked buildings in Printer's Alley.Art-Deco Inspired Rooms and Amenities:Each room at Dream Nashville is a stylish retreat, featuring art-deco design, a flat-screen TV, minibar, plush bathrobes, and premium bath amenities for a comfortable stay.Dining and Entertainment Options:Indulge in a culinary journey at the six dining and nightlife venues within the hotel, including the acclaimed Stateside Kitchen. With so much to explore in and around the hotel, from the Country Music Hall of Fame to the Frist Art Museum, your Nashville adventure awaits.Book your stay at Dream Nashville now to experience the perfect blend of history, luxury, and entertainment in the heart of Printer's Alley.
