HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical Center Location:Located a mere 50 meters from the iconic Pantheon in Rome, Hotel Pantheon offers a prime spot in the heart of the historical center. Just a 5-minute stroll will lead you to the enchanting Trevi Fountain, ensuring you're surrounded by the city's rich history and beauty.Classic Elegance:Housed in a charming 17th-century building, the Hotel Pantheon boasts classic and elegant interiors that perfectly blend with the historical surroundings. The 24-hour bar and spacious lounge provide a relaxing ambiance for guests to unwind after a day of exploring the city.Modern Comforts:Each room at the Hotel Pantheon is adorned with top-quality furnishings and modern amenities, including satellite TV, air conditioning, and a minibar. Stay connected with free WiFi throughout the hotel and start your day right with a delicious American breakfast buffet featuring delightful pizzas.Ready to immerse yourself in the charm of Rome?
