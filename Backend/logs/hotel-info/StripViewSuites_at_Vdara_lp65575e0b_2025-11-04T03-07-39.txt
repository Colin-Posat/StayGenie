Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - While some guests found the rooms clean, others reported issues such as dust, dirty earbuds, and stained carpets.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Check-in and check-out processes were smooth, and service was generally appreciated. However, some guests faced delays and were unhappy with the lack of immediate solutions for room issues.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel's prime location near key attractions and connected to other major establishments was a highlight for many guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities in the rooms, like kitchen facilities, but mentioned outdated decor and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The hotel offered a variety of amenities like a pool, spa, fitness facility, and convenience store, although some guests noted the lack of certain services such as a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the cost was justified for the location while others felt it was too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Guests enjoyed the convenience of in-room kitchen facilities but had mixed opinions on the dining options and cleanliness of dining areas.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience despite some room and cleanliness issues, with many praising the location and amenities.
