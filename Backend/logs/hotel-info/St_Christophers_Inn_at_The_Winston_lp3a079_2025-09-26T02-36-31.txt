HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Vibrant Hostel in Amsterdam's City CentreUnique Dormitory and Private Rooms: Each dormitory room at St Christophers Inn at The Winston is individually decorated and equipped with bunk beds, a toilet, and a shower. For those seeking more privacy, private rooms with a personal bathroom are also available.Exciting Amenities and Entertainment: The hostel features the lively Belushi bar and restaurant offering a variety of dishes and drinks. Additionally, guests can enjoy a nightclub, pool table, smoking room, and a garden/terrace for relaxation and socializing.Convenient Location and Nearby Attractions: Situated next to the Red Light District, the hostel is a short walk from Amsterdam Central Station and popular landmarks like Anne Frank House. With direct tram links from Dam Square, exploring the city's tourist attractions is hassle-free.Book now to experience the vibrant atmosphere at St Christophers Inn at The Winston!
