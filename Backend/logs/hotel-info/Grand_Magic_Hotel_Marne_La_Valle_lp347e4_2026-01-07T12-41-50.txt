HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Immersive Experience:The Grand Magic Hotel in Disneyland Paris offers a one-of-a-kind immersive experience for guests of all ages. With its starlight-themed rooms and breathtaking views, it's a magical escape like no other.Exciting Amenities:From a heated indoor pool to a video game room, the hotel provides a range of amenities for relaxation and entertainment. Stay active at the fitness centre or unwind with a cocktail at the Magic'Hall Bar.Delightful Dining Options:Indulge in traditional cuisine at Chez &quot;Maurice&quot; restaurant or enjoy a buffet breakfast and dinner. For a special dining experience, opt for à la carte at &quot;A la table de Monsieur Maurice.&quot; Pleasure and delicacies await at every turn.Immerse yourself in the magic of Grand Magic Hotel and book your enchanted stay now!
