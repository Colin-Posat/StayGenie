Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a major concern, with reports of hair in the shower, visible mold, and outdated facilities.
• Service: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on service, with some guests praising the staff's friendliness and others criticizing slow valet service and staff negligence.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location received positive feedback for its proximity to shops, restaurants, and landmarks despite some complaints about traffic and congestion.
• Room Quality: 6.5/10 - Guests reported a need for renovation, uncomfortable temperatures, and maintenance issues in the rooms.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Satisfactory amenities mentioned, with the pool, gym, and bar area receiving some positive comments, but no spa services were available.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Mixed feedback on pricing, with some guests finding it expensive while others found it justified considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 5/10 - Poor quality food and lack of variety in breakfast and restaurant services were commonly mentioned as negative aspects.
• Overall Experience: 6.5/10 - While some guests had a positive experience, a majority expressed disappointment in the hotel's cleanliness, comfort, and service.
