HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Catalonia Catedral, housed in a traditional Modernist building near Barcelona Cathedral, offers stylish rooms with modern decor and top-notch amenities like smart TVs, Nespresso machines, and complimentary smartphones for guests.Dining with a ViewIndulge in Mediterranean cuisine at the hotel's Ascent Restaurant by Eboca while enjoying stunning views of the Palacio Pignatelli Art Gallery. Afterward, unwind at the lobby bar with complimentary computer and printer access.Prime LocationConveniently situated within walking distance of Las Ramblas, Plaza de Catalunya Square, and Barcelona's Old Port, this hotel is the perfect base for exploring the city. With Jaume I Metro Station just minutes away, the city's attractions are within easy reach.Experience luxury, convenience, and exceptional service at Catalonia Catedral - book your stay now!

Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Only one dog or cat less than 20 kg is allowed per room (on request). Supplement of 22€ night/animal and deposit of 200€.

During weekends, breakfast is open until 12:00.

Credit cardholder must match guest name or provide authorization.

Once the reservation is made, the hotel will get in touch with the customer to agree on how to prepare the prepayment.

Please note that the year-round pool is not heated.
