HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Five-Star AccommodationLocated in Nairobi, The Concord Hotel &amp; Suites offers a lavish experience with an indoor heated swimming pool, spa, and fitness centre. Each air-conditioned room features modern amenities like a flat-screen TV and a minibar for your comfort.Culinary Delights and 24-Hour Room ServiceIndulge in a variety of dining options on-site and take advantage of 24-hour room service for a truly convenient stay. Tea and coffee facilities in each room ensure you can relax with a hot beverage anytime.Convenient Location and Business ServicesSituated near Nairobi National Museum and Westgate Shopping Mall, the hotel also offers a business centre and free private parking. For added convenience, Kenyatta International Conference Centre is just a short distance away.Book your luxurious stay at The Concord Hotel &amp; Suites for an unforgettable experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
