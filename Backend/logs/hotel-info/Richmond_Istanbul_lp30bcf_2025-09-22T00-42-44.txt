HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location on Istiklal AvenueSituated in the heart of Taksim, Richmond Istanbul offers unparalleled access to entertainment, culture, and shopping right on Istiklal Avenue. Surrounded by historical landmarks and churches, the hotel provides a unique blend of heritage and modernity.Comfortable Rooms with Stunning ViewsEach room at Richmond Istanbul features a satellite LCD TV, a cozy seating area, in-room coffee makers, and complimentary WiFi. Guests can enjoy picturesque views of Istiklal Caddesi, the Bosporus, or the Istanbul skyline from the comfort of their rooms.Delicious À La Carte BreakfastStart your day off right with a sumptuous à la carte breakfast menu offered daily at the hotel. Indulge in a variety of delectable options to fuel up for a day of exploring the vibrant city of Istanbul.Experience the perfect blend of convenience and luxury at Richmond Istanbul. Book your stay now to immerse yourself in the rich culture and history of Istanbul.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for € 45 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
