HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodations with Free Wi-FiLocated just a 10-minute drive from JR Fuji Train Station, Kuretake-Inn Fujisan offers simple yet modern accommodations with complimentary Wi-Fi/wired internet access. The air-conditioned rooms are equipped with essential amenities such as a TV, fridge, and electric kettle. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of a humidifier, en suite bathroom, and provided Yukata robes and slippers.Relaxing Public Baths and Free Breakfast BuffetUnwind in the indoor/outdoor public baths after a day of exploring, and start your morning with a delicious complimentary breakfast buffet featuring fresh baked bread, Japanese dishes, and coffee. Additionally, guests have the option to request massages for an extra cost and can even choose their preferred pillow for a comfortable night's sleep.Convenient Amenities and Nearby AttractionsGuests can take advantage of the coin-launderette, borrow a trouser press for in-room use, and utilize the free-use PC. With its proximity to attractions like Fuji Safari Park and JR Shin Fuji Train Station, Fujisan Kuretake Inn provides a convenient and comfortable stay for travelers.
