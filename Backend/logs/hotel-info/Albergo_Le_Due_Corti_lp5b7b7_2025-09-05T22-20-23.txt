HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building with Modern AmenitiesHotel Le Due Corti, nestled by Como's old city walls, offers a blend of history and comfort with its historic building and modern amenities like satellite TV and free Wi-Fi.Convenient LocationSituated just 600 meters from Como Nord Borghi Train Station, guests can easily explore Milan with direct connections. A short walk will lead you to Como's historic center and Lake Como, while buses outside the hotel offer convenient transportation.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by the small pool or take a stroll through the nearby shops and cafes, experiencing the charm of Como. After a day of exploring, return to the tranquility of Le Due Corti for a peaceful night's rest.Book now to experience the perfect blend of history, convenience, and relaxation at Hotel Le Due Corti.
