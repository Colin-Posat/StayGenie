HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationSerotel Lutèce, nestled in the heart of Paris just a short stroll from Jardin du Luxembourg, offers elegant air-conditioned rooms with a touch of classic décor. Some rooms even boast a private balcony for a charming Parisian experience.Chic Dining and RelaxationStart your day with a delightful buffet breakfast in the chic dining area, and unwind later at the hotel's bar or terrace with a view of the serene garden. The perfect way to immerse yourself in the Parisian atmosphere.Convenient LocationWith Censier – Daubenton Metro station nearby, explore Paris with ease, from Opera to Chatelet.
