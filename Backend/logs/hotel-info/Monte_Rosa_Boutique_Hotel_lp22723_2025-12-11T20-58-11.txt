HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern LuxuryThe Monte Rosa, the first hotel in Zermatt, offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern luxury. Located in the heart of the village, its 150 years-old building houses tastefully furnished rooms and suites equipped with cable TV and minibars.Spa Access and Scottish-style BarGuests have complimentary access to the spa and swimming pools at the 5-star Mont Cervin hotel. For a cozy evening, Edward's Scottish-style bar and café serve delicious coffee, cocktails, and a wide selection of drinks. In the summer, enjoy dining with a view of the majestic Matterhorn in the garden.Dine-around ExperienceIndulge in a dine-around experience at 13 partner restaurants and hotels, offering a variety of cuisines including Swiss, Japanese, Italian, and French.Book your stay at the Monte Rosa Boutique Hotel for a delightful blend of history, luxury, and culinary experiences.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.
