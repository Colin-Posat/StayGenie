HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Ornate 19th-Century CharmStep into a world of elegance and history at Hotel Nouvel, where ornate ceilings, cast bronze and crystal lamps, and marble floors adorn this 19th-century building. Each air-conditioned room offers a luxurious stay with modern amenities such as free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and double-glazed windows.Delightful Dining and Convenient ServicesIndulge in Mediterranean cuisine at La Lluna Restaurant, or grab a snack from the vending machines. Let our 24-hour reception assist you with car hire, laundry services, and show tickets. Enjoy the convenience of luggage storage and a reading room with computers during your stay.Prime Location for ExploringLocated just steps away from Plaza Catalunya Square and the Ramblas, Hotel Nouvel is the perfect base for exploring Barcelona. Immerse yourself in the city's culture with Portal de Angel shopping street, Barcelona Cathedral, and the Gothic Quarter all within walking distance.Book your stay at Hotel Nouvel today for a blend of historic charm and modern comforts.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 11 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
