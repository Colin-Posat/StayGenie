HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AccessibilityHotel Grand Pacific is a stylish 4-star hotel located just a short 5-minute walk from Bugis MRT Station. Additionally, it is also conveniently close to City Hall MRT and Bras Basah MRT Stations, making it easy to explore the city.Modern Amenities and ServicesEnjoy free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, an outdoor pool, fitness centre, and 2 restaurants offering a variety of cuisines. The spacious guestrooms feature stylish furnishings, free wired internet, tea/coffee making facilities, and cable TV channels.Additional Services and FacilitiesThe hotel offers currency exchange services, 4 function rooms, and 24-hour front desk luggage storage. Guests can indulge in local and western delights at Sun's Cafe Restaurant and Bar, or savor Chinese cuisine at the Chinese Restaurant.Book your stay at Hotel Grand Pacific for a luxurious and convenient experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 11 years old and over stay for S$ 85 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
