HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Ottoman TouchElegant Rooms with Modern Amenities: Enjoy a stay in air-conditioned rooms featuring an in-room entertainment system, DVD player, iPhone/iPad dock, and a fully stocked minibar. Indulge in the comfort of a pillow menu and a private marbled bathroom with premium toiletries.Delicious Dining and Rooftop Experience: Start your day with a rich breakfast buffet and savor homemade delicacies at Olive Restaurant while taking in stunning views of the Bosphorus and Hagia Sophia.Relaxation and Convenience: Take advantage of the hotel's heated indoor pool, fitness center, Turkish hammam, and sauna. Benefit from 24-hour front desk assistance, tour desk services, and a business center for a seamless stay.Experience luxury and comfort at Hotel Sultania Boutique Class. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Sultanahmet district!
