HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Ciutat de Sant Adrià offers a prime location just 15 minutes from central Barcelona by Metro, providing easy access to the city's vibrant attractions.Comfortable AccommodationThe air-conditioned rooms with private balconies and flat-screen TVs with international channels ensure a comfortable stay. Free Wi-Fi and a complimentary guidebook for Barcelona add to the convenience.Dining and EntertainmentGuests can enjoy a continental breakfast with a variety of options, including coffee, juice, sandwiches, and pastries. The 24-hour bar serves drinks and snacks, while packed lunches are available upon request.Book your stay at Hotel Ciutat de Sant Adrià for a seamless blend of convenience and comfort during your Barcelona getaway.
