HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in the Heart of the French QuarterExquisite Dining and RecreationIndulge in delectable dishes at the on-site restaurant and take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool. Unwind with live jazz music at the bar on select evenings, offering a truly immersive experience.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesRelax in rooms equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, free WiFi, and in-room coffee machine. Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Bourbon Orleans Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of USD 75 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
