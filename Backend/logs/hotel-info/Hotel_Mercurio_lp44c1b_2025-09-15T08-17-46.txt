HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Canal Views and Venetian EleganceHotel Mercurio offers a charming stay just steps away from La Fenice Theatre and St. Mark's Square. Enjoy the picturesque views of the Barcaroli Canal from elegantly decorated rooms featuring LCD satellite TV.Family-Run HospitalityWith a legacy spanning 3 generations, the Mercurio Hotel ensures a warm welcome and attentive service for all guests. The air-conditioned rooms boast classical Venetian furnishings, with some offering delightful canal views.Convenient Location and Cultural AttractionsIndulge in a generous buffet breakfast at San Marco Mercurio and explore nearby attractions like the Guggenheim Museum and the Giglio vaporetto water bus stop within walking distance. Book now for an unforgettable Venetian experience at Hotel Mercurio.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 4 years old and over stay for 30% of the adult rate per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
