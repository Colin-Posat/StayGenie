HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tropical Oasis in UbudLuxurious Accommodation: Immerse yourself in traditional Balinese-style villas with private terraces, spacious bathrooms, and modern amenities including flat-screen TVs and DVD players.Relaxation and Recreation: Unwind by the outdoor pool surrounded by lush tropical gardens, or indulge in a rejuvenating spa treatment. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 500,000 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
