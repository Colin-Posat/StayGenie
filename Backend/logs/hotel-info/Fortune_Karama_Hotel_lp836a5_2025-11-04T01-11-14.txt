Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Guests had mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some mentioning issues like noisy AC and stinking rooms upon change.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Service feedback was varied, with some guests appreciating the friendly staff while others mentioned noise and delays in addressing room concerns.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location near the ADCB metro and grocery stores was praised, despite the noise from nearby bars and clubs affecting some guests.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Room quality received mixed feedback, with complaints about old buildings, noisy AC, and leakage issues balanced by the comfort of beds and availability of amenities upon request.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with mentions of rooms lacking essentials like a safe, iron, or minibar initially, but being provided upon request.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Opinions on value for money were polarized, with some feeling the price justified the services while others found it expensive, especially considering the noise and maintenance issues.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services was given, although variety in food options was suggested for improvement by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 6.9/10 - While most guests had a positive overall experience, the noise from bars and clubs affected sleep quality, leading to mixed opinions.
