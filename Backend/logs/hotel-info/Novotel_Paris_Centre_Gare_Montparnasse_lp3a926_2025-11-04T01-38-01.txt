HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Novotel Paris Centre Gare Montparnasse offers air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities such as a flat-screen HD TV, electric kettle, and minibar. Upgrade to an Executive room for additional perks like free drinks, a Nespresso coffee machine, and plush bathrobes.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in delicious meals at Halle 4 restaurant or unwind at the bar's shaded terrace with a variety of drinks. Enjoy live entertainment and sports events on the large TV screen. The buffet breakfast is a delightful spread of pastries, fruits, eggs, and more, served in the restaurant or on the sun terrace.Convenient FacilitiesBenefit from private parking, a ticket service, free digital newspapers, and an iMac computer in the lobby. Stay connected with free fiber optic WiFi throughout the hotel.Book your stay at Novotel Paris Centre Gare Montparnasse for a luxurious and convenient experience.
