HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Oceanfront Property Just 25 km from Los Angeles, Viceroy Santa Monica offers stunning Pacific Ocean views. Outdoor Retreat Enjoy our two outdoor pools, Pool Cabanas, and Backyard Cabanas for the ultimate relaxation. Convenient Location Explore Santa Monica's attractions like Santa Monica Place and 3rd Street Promenade, or venture to The Getty Museum just a short distance away. Book your stay at Viceroy Santa Monica for a memorable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Viceroy Santa Monica in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please contact the property for policies regarding cots or roll-away beds in the room.

The guest amenities fee includes:
- Welcome beverage upon arrival
- Internet access
- Daily priority bike rentals, based upon availability
- Fitness centre access(Currently Limited Capacity), based on availability
- Water bottles in room - Fitness classes by Beach SoCal Yoga

 Guests travelling with pets are subject to an additional fee of USD 125 per pet per stay.

There is an additional charge of $15 per night to use the electric vehicle charge.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 63.80 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas.
• Service: 8/10 - Staff members were commended for their helpfulness and friendliness, although there were occasional delays in service.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's proximity to key attractions and the beach was highlighted as a positive aspect by many guests.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the comfortable and modern rooms, with some minor issues reported regarding room amenities.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests enjoying the free bike rentals and cozy rooms, while others noted the lack of in-room dining options.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the parking costs were expensive compared to the overall experience.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Overall positive feedback on the food quality and options, though some guests found the breakfast prices to be high.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a pleasant overall experience and expressed a willingness to return to the hotel.
