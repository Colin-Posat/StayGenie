HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AccommodationExperience modern and air-conditioned rooms at our Holiday Inn Express on the Greenwich Peninsula. Enjoy the convenience of a short walk to popular attractions like the O2 Arena and North Greenwich tube station. Stay connected with free WiFi throughout the hotel.Delicious Dining OptionsStart your day right with an inclusive breakfast buffet in the Great Room, offering a variety of fresh pastries and hot items. In the evenings, indulge in pizzas and light meals for a satisfying dining experience without leaving the hotel.Convenient Location and ParkingLocated outside the Congestion Charge Zone, our hotel offers on-site parking through YourParkingSpace. Explore nearby attractions like the National Maritime Museum and easily reach London City Airport. Book now for a memorable stay at Holiday Inn Express London Greenwich.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
If booking a Double Room with Sofa Bed and you require the hotel to set up the extra bed, please inform the hotel via the Special Requests box during the booking process.

Please note that a cot cannot be provided in the Double Room with Sofa bed when the sofa bed is in use due to limited space.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
