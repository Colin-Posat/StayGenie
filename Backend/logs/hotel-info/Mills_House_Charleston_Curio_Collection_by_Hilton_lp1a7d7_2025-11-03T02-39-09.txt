HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsMills House Charleston, Curio Collection by Hilton offers elegant rooms with modern amenities, including a 55-inch flat-screen TV and a work desk.Dining OptionsGuests can indulge in Southern Fine Dining Cuisine at The Iron Rose restaurant or grab a coffee and pastry at The Black Door Cafe, ensuring a culinary delight for every taste.Convenient LocationSituated in Charleston city centre, this hotel is a short walk from the Charleston City Market and close to the picturesque White Point Garden, providing guests with easy access to the city's top attractions.Experience the charm and comfort of Mills House Charleston, Curio Collection by Hilton.
