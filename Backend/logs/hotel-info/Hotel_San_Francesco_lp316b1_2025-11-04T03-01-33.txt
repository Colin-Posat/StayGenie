HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsAt Hotel San Francesco, experience the perfect blend of history and luxury in the heart of Trastevere. Indulge in breathtaking 360-degree views from our rooftop terrace, overlooking the charming Porta Portese market and the picturesque streets of Rome.Chic Accommodations and Spectacular ViewsWake up to the serene sights of the 15th-century courtyard of the San Francesco a Ripa church while enjoying a delicious breakfast in our well-decorated rooms. With amenities like free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and air conditioning, your stay with us promises both comfort and convenience.Unwind in Style and ConvenienceSip on refreshing drinks on our rooftop terrace, where you can marvel at the panoramic views of Trastevere and the Aventine Hill, creating unforgettable memories as the church bells serenade you. Explore Rome effortlessly with excellent tram and bus links just steps away on Viale Trastevere.Experience the magic of Rome at Hotel San Francesco - Book your stay with us today!
