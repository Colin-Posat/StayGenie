HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms and AmenitiesThe Hilton San Francisco Union Square offers modern rooms equipped with a flat-screen HDTV, coffee machine, and luxurious Crabtree &amp; Evelyn bathroom amenities. Enjoy the convenience of an alarm clock with MP3 player connection for personalized entertainment.Health Club and Dining OptionsIndulge in the 2,800 square-foot health club and outdoor whirlpool for relaxation. Experience a culinary delight at Urban Tavern with its extensive dinner menu in an artistic setting. Unwind at the lounge area offering a selection of sophisticated snacks and drinks.Prime Location and Special OfferConveniently located in downtown San Francisco, just a short walk from the Powell Street subway station, this Hilton is near popular attractions. Book now to take advantage of the $25 food and beverage credit, $25 Tower Tours credit, and Premium WiFi included in the mandatory Destination Fee.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note:

There is a per night incidental charge that is refundable after inspection.

Due to fire code policies, 1 extra bed/baby cot is only available in King Rooms. Any type of crib or extra bed needs to be requested from Hilton San Francisco Union Square.

Pets up to 34 kg (75 lbs) are permitted, and charges are applicable. Please notify Hilton San Francisco Union Square if you have further pet questions.

If a guest books a room that offers a breakfast-included rate, this room will provide breakfast for 2 guests. All additional guests will be charged.

Guests must join the Hilton Honors loyalty program in order to receive free WiFi.
