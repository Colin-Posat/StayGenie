Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with some guests praising the friendliness and helpfulness of the staff, while others criticized the language barrier and availability of English-speaking staff.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks, the beachfront, and public transport, making it convenient for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally clean and well-designed, but some guests experienced issues with soundproofing and hot water availability.
• Amenities: 7/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with some guests mentioning the limited breakfast options and lack of hot water in the shower.
• Value for Money: 3.7/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high compared to other properties, while others appreciated the overall experience despite the shortcomings.
• Food and Beverage: 6.2/10 - Breakfast and food quality received mixed feedback, with some guests praising the staff and others expressing disappointment with the limited options.
• Overall Experience: 9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, particularly highlighting the location, cleanliness, and helpful staff.
