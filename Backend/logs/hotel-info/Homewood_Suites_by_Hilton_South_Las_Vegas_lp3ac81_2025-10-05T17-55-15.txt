Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.6/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the rooms, highlighting it as a major positive aspect of their stay.
• Service: 8.4/10 - The friendly and helpful staff were a standout for many guests, although some mentioned issues with the layout and maintenance of the hotel.
• Location: 8.9/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and public transport received high praise from guests for its convenience and accessibility.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests generally found the rooms spacious and comfortable, but some reported noise issues and maintenance concerns.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - While some guests appreciated the amenities like the gym and pool, others were disappointed by the lack of spa services and noisy air vents.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, especially regarding breakfast offerings.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some guests desiring more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - The majority of guests reported a positive overall experience, with many expressing a willingness to return and recommend the hotel to others.
