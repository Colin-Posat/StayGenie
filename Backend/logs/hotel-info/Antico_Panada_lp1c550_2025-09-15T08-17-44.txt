HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic CharmAntico Panada, dating back to 1889, offers a unique stay in 2 historic buildings just 30 meters from Saint Mark's Square. Experience the elegance of air-conditioned rooms with refined furniture and Murano lamps, coupled with free Wi-Fi throughout.Prime LocationOverlooking Calle degli Specchieri, a direct path to the Basilica of San Marco, the hotel provides easy access to the city. With the water-bus stop close by at Calle Vallaresso, exploring Venice is convenient for guests.Venetian HospitalityIndulge in the stylish white and red marble interiors and start your day with a delicious continental buffet. Unwind at Bar Ai Speci, an old Venetian inn serving classic aperitifs, espresso, and cocktails until midnight.Book now for an unforgettable Venetian experience at Antico Panada!

Category Ratings:

• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable for most guests, but there were complaints about dark rooms and maintenance issues in some accommodations.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While some guests enjoyed the provided amenities like breakfast, others mentioned issues with air conditioning and limited amenities.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions on value varied, with some finding the price reasonable for the location and others feeling it was too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast was generally appreciated by guests, though some mentioned a desire for more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the location and staff, despite some issues with room quality and amenities.
