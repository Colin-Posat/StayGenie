HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover an oasis of tranquility at Komaneka at Bisma, where luxury meets the beauty of Ubud.Indulge in RelaxationUnwind at our full-service spa offering rejuvenating massages and body treatments tailored to soothe your senses. Enjoy two spectacular outdoor swimming pools, a modern fitness center, and bicycles for rent, ensuring your every leisure need is catered to.Savor Culinary DelightsExperience the rich flavors of Indonesian cuisine at Seneng Kitchen Restaurant, with serene garden views, or opt for the convenience of 24-hour room service. Enjoy your favorite beverages at our bar/lounge or poolside bar, making every moment a celebration.Comfort and ConvenienceStay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi in all 32 air-conditioned rooms, equipped with LCD televisions and luxurious bathrooms. Additional amenities include a business center, complimentary valet parking, and a shuttle service to explore nearby attractions effortlessly.Don't miss your chance to experience luxury in Ubud—book your stay today!
