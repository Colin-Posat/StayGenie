HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesExperience comfort and style at Courtyard Miami Airport South, where every room is equipped with luxury bedding, a cable TV, and coffee-making facilities. Relax in our outdoor pool or hot tub, and enjoy the convenience of free WiFi throughout the hotel.Culinary DelightsIndulge your taste buds at the Cane Fire Grille Restaurant, known for its delectable seafood and steak dishes. For a more casual dining experience, head to Champion Sports Bar for American favorites and a wide selection of beers to choose from.Convenient LocationConveniently situated just a 5-minute drive from Miami International Airport, our hotel also offers a free airport transfer service for our guests. Explore nearby attractions such as Dolphin Mall, Miracle Mile, and Downtown Coral Gables during your stay.Book now and elevate your Miami experience at Courtyard Miami Airport South!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were clean and well-maintained, with several guests specifically mentioning the cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with guests highlighting helpful and professional staff. However, there were occasional complaints about delayed or lackluster service.
• Location: 7.7/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its proximity to key landmarks, public transport, and the airport by several guests.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Guests found the rooms to be comfortable and well-equipped, although some mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with some guests appreciating the gym and pool, while others noted the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed opinions on the value for money, as some guests felt the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, although some guests suggested improvements in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
