HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Stunning ViewsThe family-run Bristol hotel in Zermatt offers a peaceful retreat in the heart of the city, conveniently located between the train station and the Klein Matterhorn cable car. Wake up to breathtaking views of the iconic Matterhorn from most rooms, making your stay truly unforgettable.Convenient Winter Sports FacilitiesWith easy access to all winter sport facilities and cable cars on foot, Hotel Bristol is a paradise for snow enthusiasts. Rent or buy skis on-site, purchase lift tickets at the front desk, and catch the ski bus right outside the hotel for a seamless winter adventure.Relaxation and EntertainmentUnwind in the hotel's spa area with a sauna and steam bath, or enjoy a refreshing drink at the cosy bar. The spacious terrace with deckchairs is perfect for soaking up the sun. Plus, guests can take advantage of the free playroom, internet corner, and shuttle service for a truly hassle-free stay.Book your stay at the Bristol hotel now to experience the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and relaxation.
