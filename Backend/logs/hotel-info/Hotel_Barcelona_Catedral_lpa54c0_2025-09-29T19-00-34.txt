Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were noted as clean, spacious, and well-maintained, but a few guests mentioned minor issues with bed linens feeling scratchy.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Friendly and helpful staff were highlighted, with fast check-in, excellent information provided, and assistance with guest needs. However, some guests reported unresolved AC issues and inadequate gym facilities.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The central location close to key landmarks, restaurants, shops, and transport options was praised by many guests.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable, well-equipped, and modern, with some mentioning spaciousness and amenities like windows for fresh air.
• Amenities: 7.6/10 - Guests appreciated the provided amenities like pools and breakfast but noted some areas for improvement, such as AC issues and limited spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.4/10 - Opinions on value varied, with some feeling the price was high for services offered while others found it reasonable considering the location and facilities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety or noted tight pool space.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive experience, praising the hotel's overall offerings like location, staff, cleanliness, and comfortable amenities.
