HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in the Heart of IstanbulModern Amenities for Your Comfort: Enjoy the outdoor pool, spa, fitness centre, and modern meeting rooms during your stay. The luxury rooms are decorated in light colours and feature marble bathrooms with a rain shower.Culinary Delights and Cozy Atmospheres: Indulge in a wide culinary selection at '34' restaurant offering Turkish and Mediterranean cuisine. Relax at the Mezzanine Lounge &amp; Bar or The Library Bar for a cozy atmosphere.Relaxation and Recreation: Unwind at the Gaia Spa&amp;Fitness Centre with modern spa treatments or enjoy a barbecue by the pool at the Gazebo. Don't miss out on the floodlit tennis court for some active fun.Book your stay at Grand Hyatt Istanbul for a luxurious and unforgettable experience in the vibrant city of Istanbul.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Grand Hyatt Istanbul offers airport shuttle service with a private limousine at a surcharge. Please inform the hotel in advance in order to benefit from this service. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

Extra beds do not include breakfast service. Breakfast for extra bed is offered at an additional fee.

Guests are kindly requested to show the credit card used for reservation upon check-in.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 7 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
