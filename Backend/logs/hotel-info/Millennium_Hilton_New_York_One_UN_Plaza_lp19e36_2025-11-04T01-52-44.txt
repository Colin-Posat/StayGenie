HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Millennium Hilton New York One UN Plaza, located in Manhattan’s East Side, offers elegantly designed guest rooms equipped with 42-inch flat-screen TVs, desks, and safety deposit boxes. Enjoy breathtaking views of the East River or the iconic New York City skyline from the comfort of your room.Dining OptionsIndulge in a variety of culinary experiences at this hotel, including a grab and go café, a full-service restaurant, and an executive lounge. Whether you're in the mood for a quick bite or a gourmet meal, the hotel has you covered with its diverse dining options.Prime LocationConveniently situated just 644 meters from the Grand Central Terminal, and in close proximity to famous landmarks such as the United Nations Headquarters, Times Square, and the Empire State Building, this hotel is the perfect choice for travelers looking to explore the best of New York City.Experience luxury, convenience, and exceptional service at Millennium Hilton New York One UN Plaza.
