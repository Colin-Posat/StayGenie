HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 2-Star Michelin RestaurantIndulge in the culinary delights of Le Meurice with its renowned 2-star Michelin restaurant led by the esteemed chef Alain Ducasse. The grand dining area, inspired by the opulence of Château de Versailles, offers a gastronomic experience like no other.Elegant 18th Century DecorExperience the charm of a bygone era in each air-conditioned and soundproofed room, adorned with Louis XVI furniture and modern amenities. The classic decor with a contemporary twist and marble bathrooms provide a luxurious stay in the heart of Paris.Relaxing Spa and Fitness CentreUnwind and rejuvenate at the on-site spa and fitness centre by Valmont. Enjoy a range of massage treatments and wellness facilities, ensuring a tranquil escape from the bustling city.Immerse yourself in luxury at Le Meurice and elevate your Parisian experience. Book now for an unforgettable stay in the City of Light.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests have access to high-speed Wi-Fi in all areas, free of charge.

Pets are not permitted in the bar, the restaurant or on the terrace. For guests bringing pets, the property will charge a daily cleaning fee of EUR 35 upon check-out.

You are required to present a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that special requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Please note that the Daily rate is in Euro including VAT 10% and Employee Contribution 5%.

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 105 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
