HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tranquil Rice Field ViewsLocated in Pengosekan, Bhuwana Ubud Hotel and Farming offers stunning views of rice fields, providing a serene escape for guests seeking tranquility and nature's beauty.Fresh Farm-to-Table DiningIndulge in delicious meals at the on-site restaurant, where ingredients are sourced fresh from the hotel's garden. Experience a culinary journey of Indonesian, Balinese, and Western cuisine amidst the lush surroundings.Relaxing AmenitiesUnwind in the outdoor swimming pool, enjoy free Wi-Fi access throughout the property, and explore the area with complimentary bicycle rentals. The modern Balinese-style rooms with large windows offer a comfortable retreat after a day of adventure.Experience the perfect blend of nature, comfort, and culinary delights at Bhuwana Ubud Hotel and Farming. Book your stay now for a rejuvenating getaway.
