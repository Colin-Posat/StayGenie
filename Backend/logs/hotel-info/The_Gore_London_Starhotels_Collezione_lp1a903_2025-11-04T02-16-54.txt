HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and LuxuryThe Gore London - Starhotels Collezione, established in 1892, is a boutique hotel in South Kensington with 50 lavishly-appointed bedrooms steeped in history. Each room is uniquely furnished with hand-picked pieces, ornate beds, and Italian luxury linens from Frette.Culinary DelightsIndulge in classic British cuisine at the on-site restaurant or sip on fine spirits at The Bar 190, famous for hosting The Rolling Stones' album launch. Enjoy the extensive collection of whiskies and rums in a setting rich with musical history.Prime LocationLocated near top attractions like Kensington Palace, Hyde Park, and world-renowned museums, The Gore offers easy access to the best of London. Explore upscale shopping in Knightsbridge and enjoy the vibrant nightlife of the West End just minutes away.Ready to experience luxury and history at The Gore London? Book your stay now!
