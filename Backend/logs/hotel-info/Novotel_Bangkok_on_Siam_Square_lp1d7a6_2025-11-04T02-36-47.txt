HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities - Novotel Bangkok on Siam Square offers a range of luxurious amenities including a large outdoor pool, sauna room, day spa, and fitness center with activity classes.Convenient Location - Situated just a minute's walk from Siam BTS Skytrain Station, the hotel is in close proximity to popular shopping centers like MBK, Siam Paragon, and Central World, making it a convenient choice for travelers looking to explore the city.Culinary Delights - Guests can indulge in a variety of culinary experiences at The SQUARE restaurant, which serves international favorites and delicious Asian cuisine.Experience luxury, convenience, and culinary delights all in one place at Novotel Bangkok on Siam Square. Book your stay now to enjoy a memorable experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 16 years old and over stay for THB 1,412 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
