HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsLocated just steps away from the iconic Blue Mosque, Saba Sultan Hotel offers elegantly decorated rooms with modern Turkish touches and panoramic city views from its rooftop terrace. The carefully curated rooms feature parquet floors, large windows, and amenities like LCD TVs, free Wi-Fi, and coffee/tea facilities. Some rooms even boast charming French balconies for a touch of romance.Delicious Breakfast and Convenient ServicesStart your day with a traditional Turkish breakfast served in the cozy breakfast room, complete with olives and freshly baked bread. Take advantage of the hotel's bicycle rental service to explore the nearby attractions when the weather is pleasant.Prime Location with Easy Access to AttractionsConveniently situated near the Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace, Saba Sultan Hotel offers easy access to Istanbul's top landmarks. With Atatürk Airport just a short drive away and free parking on-site, your stay is guaranteed to be hassle-free.To experience luxury and comfort in the heart of Istanbul, book your stay at Saba Sultan Hotel now.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
