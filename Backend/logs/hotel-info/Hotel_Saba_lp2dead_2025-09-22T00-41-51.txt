HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in IstanbulLocated just steps away from the iconic Blue Mosque, Hotel Saba offers a convenient and comfortable stay in the heart of Istanbul. Explore nearby attractions like the Spice Bazaar, Topkapi Palace, and Suleymaniye Mosque with ease.Modern Amenities and ServicesExperience a range of amenities including free WiFi, concierge services, and a 24-hour front desk. Relax in non-smoking rooms equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms. Some rooms even boast balconies with stunning city views.Delicious Halal BreakfastStart your day right with a complimentary halal breakfast served at the hotel. Fuel up before heading out to discover the wonders of Istanbul.Ready to immerse yourself in the beauty of Istanbul? Book your stay at Hotel Saba now for an unforgettable experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
