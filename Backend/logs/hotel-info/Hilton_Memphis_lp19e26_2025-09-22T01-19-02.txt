• Limited parking

• No spa services
• Inconsistent cleanliness
• Maintenance issues
• Value for money

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness with some guests mentioning issues like dirty windows and rooms, while others found the hotel clean.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally appreciated, but some guests experienced issues with front desk staff friendliness and room readiness.
• Location: 8.2/10 - Guests liked the hotel's proximity to key locations like the airport and downtown Memphis.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but there were complaints about amenities, room cleanliness, and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests had mixed reviews on amenities, with some highlighting positive aspects like the pool and breakfast, while others mentioned shortcomings like limited hooks in bathrooms.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Guests had varied opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price was justified and others finding it expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, but some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience but highlighted areas for improvement related to cleanliness, service, and value for money.
