HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Spectacular ViewsRooftop Terrace and Outdoor Pool: Enjoy breathtaking views of Mexico City while taking a dip in the outdoor pool on our rooftop terrace.Wellness Area and Gym: Relax and rejuvenate in our wellness area featuring a hot tub, steam bath, and a variety of massages. Stay active at our on-site gym.Convenient Location: Explore the nearby National Auditorium and Chapultepec Park, or indulge in shopping at Presidente Mazarik Street. With free private parking and easy access to public transportation, Las Suites is the perfect choice for your stay in Polanco.
