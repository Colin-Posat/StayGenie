HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in BarcelonaConveniently situated in the heart of Barcelona, Hotel Rec Barcelona - Adults Only offers air-conditioned rooms, an outdoor pool, free WiFi, and a shared lounge. Guests can enjoy a city view from selected rooms and unwind at the terrace and bar. Private parking is also available on site.Comfortable Rooms and AmenitiesEach room at Hotel Rec Barcelona features a desk, private bathroom with free toiletries, bed linen, and towels. Guests can start their day with a continental, vegetarian, or gluten-free breakfast at the property.Multilingual 24-Hour Front DeskThe hotel's staff, fluent in Catalan, German, English, and Spanish, are available round the clock to assist guests with local tips and information. Nearby attractions include Picasso Museum, Santa Maria del Mar, and Cathedral of Barcelona.Book your stay at Hotel Rec Barcelona - Adults Only for a memorable Barcelona experience!
