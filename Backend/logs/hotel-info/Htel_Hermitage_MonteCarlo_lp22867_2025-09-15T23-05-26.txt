Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.

Category Ratings:

• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with special mention to the valet team.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to key landmarks and offers a convenient location for exploring Monaco.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned noise issues in certain rooms.
• Amenities: 7.9/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the spa, but concerns about the lack of bidets and the closure of the spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high compared to services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, but some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
