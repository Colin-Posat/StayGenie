IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The property can be reached by a private and a shared speedboat.

Private Speedboat Transfers: 
From July 1, 2025 to July 31, 2026 the charges are as follows:
- Adult: USD 1608.80 per boat per way, with a maximum of 10 people.
- Child: USD 1608.80 per boat per way, with a maximum of 10 people. This rate is not included in the booking price and will be charged additionally.

Shared Speedboat Transfers: 
From July 1, 2025 to December 31, 2026 the charges are as follows:
- Adult (12 years and above): USD 270.27 per person.
- Child (2–11 years): USD 105 per child. This rate is not included in the booking price and will be charged additionally.

The Green Tax of USD 12 per person per day will be collected directly from the guests during their stay. 

Additional Mandatory Gala Dinner fees for Christmas Eve and/or New Year's Eve will be applicable to every adult and every child. Guests will be asked to pay the following charges at the property: 

Christmas Eve Gala dinner on breakfast meal plan: USD 280 per adult, USD 100 per child (2-11.99 yrs). New Year's Eve Gala dinner on breakfast meal plan: USD 510 per adult, USD 160 per child (6-11.99 yrs)

All bookings include a Spa Credit of USD 30 per adult per stay and a Candy Spa Credit of USD 25 per child per stay. Credits are valid during the stay and applicable for bookings until year 2026. Terms and conditions apply.This property does not accommodate bachelor(ette) or similar parties.
