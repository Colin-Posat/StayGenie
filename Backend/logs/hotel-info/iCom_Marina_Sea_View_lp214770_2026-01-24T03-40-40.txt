HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover a tropical haven at iCom Marina Sea View, where beachfront relaxation meets modern comfort.Serene AccommodationsUnwind in our 36 individually furnished guestrooms, featuring private balconies, minibars, and Smart televisions. Enjoy complimentary wireless internet access while you take in the breathtaking ocean views.Culinary DelightsIndulge in delicious meals at our two on-site restaurants, including the vibrant Outside Hut. For a cozy night in, room service is available during limited hours, along with snacks from our coffee shop/café.Amenities for Every OccasionConnect with fellow guests at our complimentary daily reception or enjoy a refreshing drink at the bar/lounge. With express check-out, luggage storage, and a roundtrip airport shuttle service, your convenience is our priority.Don’t miss out on your perfect getaway—book your stay at iCom Marina Sea View today!
