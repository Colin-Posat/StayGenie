HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Serene ViewsVilla Wastra presents lavish rooms amidst lush greenery, offering serene views of the natural surroundings. Guests can unwind with soothing massage treatments and enjoy a refreshing swim in the pool.Convenient Location for ExplorationSituated just a short drive from Kintamani, guests can marvel at the stunning vistas of Mount Batur and Lake Batur. Additionally, the proximity to Ubud Art Market and Ubud Monkey Forest provides ample opportunities for cultural exploration.Modern Amenities and Personalized ServiceThe rooms at Villa Wastra are elegantly furnished with modern decor, featuring private bathrooms and some with the added convenience of a kitchen and private balcony. Guests can enjoy complimentary wired internet, relax in the common area with a TV and library, and benefit from services like car rental assistance and room service.Book now to experience luxury amidst nature at Villa Wastra!

HOTEL POLICIES:

Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for Rp 350,000 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for Rp 350,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 350,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
