HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation with Free Wi-FiHotel Monterey Hanzomon, located just a stone's throw away from Hanzomon Subway Station, offers contemporary rooms with complimentary Wi-Fi. The sleek design and convenient amenities provide a comfortable stay for all guests.Convenient Location for SightseeingSituated near the Imperial Palace and Yasukuni Shrine, the hotel is a perfect base for exploring Tokyo's cultural landmarks. With easy access to train stations like Shibuya and Oshiage, guests can effortlessly navigate the city.Delicious Buffet BreakfastStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast featuring local delicacies and Western favorites in the stylish dining area. The perfect way to fuel up before a day of sightseeing in Tokyo.Experience the modern comfort and convenience of Hotel Monterey Hanzomon - book your stay now for an unforgettable Tokyo adventure!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
There is no space for a baby cot or extra bed in the Single Room.

When booking for 16 people or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Please note that the hotel will undergo a planned blackout due to a statutory inspection of the electrical equipment at the following time: from 11:30 to 17:00 on Monday, November 10, 2025. 
During this period, there is a hot water supply available at the property. Please note that the following facilities are unavailable: the air conditioning, in-room lighting, TVs, power outlets in guest rooms, the internet, the hotel's multi-story parking lot, and the elevators are not available, and guests must use the emergency stairs. 
Please note that late check-out after 11 and early check-in before 17 are unavailable on that day.An accommodation tax per person, per night is not included in the price and must be paid at the property.
