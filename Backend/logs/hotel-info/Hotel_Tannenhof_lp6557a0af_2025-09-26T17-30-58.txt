HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ZermattLocated in the heart of Zermatt, Hotel Tannenhof offers a convenient base for your stay. Just 600 meters from Zermatt Railway Station, this 3-star hotel is perfect for exploring the city on foot.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesEach room at Hotel Tannenhof is thoughtfully equipped with a flat-screen TV, coffee machine, and free WiFi. The property also offers allergy-free rooms and a ski pass sales point, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free stay.Dining Options and Nearby AttractionsIndulge in a variety of cuisines at the hotel's restaurant, including Italian and Mediterranean dishes. With the Matterhorn Museum just 200 meters away and skiing and cycling opportunities nearby, there's plenty to explore during your stay at Hotel Tannenhof.Ready to experience comfort and convenience in Zermatt? Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests consistently appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, although some minor maintenance issues were mentioned.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was highly praised by guests, with standout mentions for friendly and helpful staff, although some complaints about wait times were noted.
• Location: 8.9/10 - The hotel's central and convenient location near key landmarks and amenities received high acclaim from guests.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable, modern, and spacious, although some guests pointed out the occasional noise disturbance and issues with pillows and duvets.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the provided amenities satisfactory, with positive mentions for the ski room, cleanliness, and friendliness of staff, though some suggested improvements in storage and drying facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.3/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money aspect, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, particularly in relation to room size and dining experiences.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some guests suggesting a wider variety of food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, praising various aspects such as modernity, cleanliness, friendly staff, and location convenience.
