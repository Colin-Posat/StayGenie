HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Art Deco StyleHotel Serhs Rivoli Rambla boasts a stunning Art Deco style, blending classic elegance with modern flair. The theatrical interiors, clever lighting, and contemporary furniture create a unique ambiance that is sure to impress guests.Scenic Views and DiningIndulge in picturesque views over the Gothic Quarter from the terrace as you enjoy a cup of espresso. Return for lunch to savor views of the historic urban gardens. The 128 Rambla Restaurant offers a diverse menu of Mediterranean and international cuisine, while the buffet breakfast prepares you for the day ahead.Charming Courtyard and PatioRelax in the beautiful interior courtyard and soak in the serene atmosphere.
