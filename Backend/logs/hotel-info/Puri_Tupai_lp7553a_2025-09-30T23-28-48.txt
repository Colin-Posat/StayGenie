HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsTreat yourself to a stay in one of the 6 individually decorated guestrooms, featuring private pools and LED televisions. Rooms have private balconies or patios. Complimentary wired and wireless internet access, along with satellite programming on 32-inch flat-screen televisions, provide entertainment.Exceptional AmenitiesIndulge in massages and facials, or relax by the pool. Enjoy a meal at the restaurant, have a drink at the bar/lounge, or order room service. Take advantage of complimentary breakfast served daily, as well as concierge services, shuttle rides, and a free reception.Prime LocationLocated in Ubud, Puri Tupai is in a rural area near popular attractions like Ubud Monkey Forest and Campuhan Ridge Walk. With a convenient distance to the airport, this upscale hotel offers both tranquility and easy access to city must-sees.Book your stay at Puri Tupai now for an unforgettable experience in Ubud!

* One child 6 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding. Children may not be eligible for complimentary breakfast.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* The property is professionally cleaned.
* Contactless check-in and contactless check-out are available.
