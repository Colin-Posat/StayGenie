HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Modernist Building and Art Nouveau DecorHotel HCC Regente, nestled in a beautifully restored traditional Modernist building, offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern comfort. The Art Nouveau décor and wrought-iron balconies add a touch of elegance to the property.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsSituated on the iconic Rambla de Catalunya, HCC Regente provides easy access to popular attractions such as Las Ramblas and Gaudí’s Casa Batlló. With Passeig de Gràcia Metro Station just a stone's throw away, exploring the vibrant city of Barcelona is a breeze.Comfortable Rooms and Varied BreakfastEach air-conditioned room at the Regente is equipped with satellite TV, free wired internet, and a minibar for utmost convenience. Start your day right with a delicious breakfast served in the hotel's restaurant, ensuring a pleasant stay from the moment you wake up.Experience the perfect blend of history and modernity at Hotel HCC Regente. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Barcelona!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for 100% of the adult rate per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
