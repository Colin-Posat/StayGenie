IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Zermatt is car-free. Cars can only go to Täsch (3 miles from Zermatt). There are many parking lots and multi-story parking lots there. Trains leave for Zermatt every 20 minutes.

Please inform the property upon arrival by phone if you wish to use the free pick-up service, which runs between 8 am and 9 pm. Outside of these hours, it is possible to reach the hotel with a 10-minute walk or with an official taxi.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 9 years old to 15 years old stay for CHF 50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 8 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 9 years old to 15 years old stay for CHF 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 16 years old and over stay for CHF 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
