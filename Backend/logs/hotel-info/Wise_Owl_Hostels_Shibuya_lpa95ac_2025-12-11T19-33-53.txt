HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Hostel in TokyoWise Owl Hostels Shibuya, opened in April 2017, offers a convenient and comfortable stay in Tokyo's Meguro District. With Simmons beds in all rooms, free WiFi, and a shared mini-kitchen, guests can enjoy a cozy and relaxing atmosphere.Convenient LocationSituated within walking distance of Shinsen and Ikejiri-ohashi Stations, exploring Tokyo is easy from this hostel. Additionally, with Shibuya Station nearby and various transportation options, visiting attractions like Meiji Jingu Shrine is a breeze.Amenities and AccessibilityFrom a designated smoking area to a nearby convenience store, Wise Owl Hostels Shibuya ensures guests have everything they need for a pleasant stay. Book now and experience the vibrant city of Tokyo from this welcoming hostel.
