HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortSet in a typical Parisian building from the 19th century, Hôtel Gustave blends historic charm with modern comfort. The décor, inspired by the Belle Epoque and Universal Exhibition, creates a unique and elegant atmosphere throughout the property.Convenient LocationLocated in the 15th district of Paris, just a short 10-minute walk from the iconic Eiffel Tower, Hôtel Gustave offers easy access to popular attractions. With Dupleix Metro Station only 100 meters away, exploring the city is convenient for guests.Personalized Service and AmenitiesGuests can enjoy soundproofed en suite rooms equipped with satellite TV, air conditioning, and a minibar. The hotel also offers free WiFi, a cold breakfast box served in-room or to go, a laundry service, an honesty bar, and a lift for easy access to all guest rooms.Experience the perfect blend of history and modernity at Hôtel Gustave. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Paris!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
