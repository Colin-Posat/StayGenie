HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationCasual Colours Barcelona is ideally situated just a short walk from Barcelona Sants Railway Station and Avinguda Diagonal, making it a perfect base for exploring the city's main attractions.Comfortable AccommodationThe hotel offers cozy rooms with simple yet attractive décor, each equipped with air conditioning, a TV, and a minibar, providing a comfortable retreat after a day of sightseeing.Great ServicesGuests can enjoy a daily buffet breakfast and benefit from the helpful 24-hour reception staff who can assist with restaurant reservations and provide valuable insights on the city's highlights.Book your stay at Casual Colours Barcelona now for a memorable and convenient experience in Barcelona!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that when booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

When traveling with pets, please note that an extra charge of 10 EUR per night applies. Please note that a maximum of 1 pet is allowed per room.

Bright studio on the 5th floor of Parque Santiago II, in the very heart of Playa de las Américas. The balcony offers a side view of the sea and the nearby bars. Please note: due to the central location, nighttime noise is possible, although the balcony doors have been renewed to reduce it.

The studio has recently been updated with new, high-quality mattresses for better comfort.

The apartment includes:

Equipped kitchen (stovetop, fridge, kitchenware, coffee maker – no oven, no dishwasher)

Washing machine

Smart TV, free Wi-Fi

Hairdryer, iron

⚠️ Important information:

Located on the 5th floor with elevator.

No private parking (free public parking usually available around the complex).

Heated saltwater pool in the complex – sun loungers and parasols available for a fee (approx. €3.50 per lounger + €1.50 parasol).

This is a private flat, not a hotel → no daily cleaning.

For stays over 7 nights: mid-stay change of bed linen and towels.

Guests under 25 are not allowed.

Self check-in after mandatory ID registration.

Office hours: Mon–Fri 9:30–17:30. Out-of-hours assistance: €50 surcharge.
