HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Barcelona’s Gothic QuarterHostal Fernando is conveniently located just 100 meters from Las Ramblas and Plaça Sant Jaume, making it the perfect base for exploring the vibrant city of Barcelona.Comfortable Accommodation with Essential AmenitiesThe air-conditioned rooms and dormitories at Hostal Fernando are equipped with free Wi-Fi, TV, safe, and private bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests.Convenience and Services at Your FingertipsGuests can enjoy the convenience of a simple restaurant and bar on-site, as well as easy access to a variety of dining options nearby. Additionally, the tour desk, bicycle and car rental services make exploring Barcelona's top attractions like Barcelona Cathedral, Picasso Museum, and Boquería Market a breeze.Book your stay at Hostal Fernando now for a memorable Barcelona experience!
