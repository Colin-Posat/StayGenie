Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Food & beverage services at this property may be limited or unavailable due to Coronavirus (COVID-19).
Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), this property is taking steps to help protect the safety of guests and staff. Certain services and amenities may be reduced or unavailable as a result.
A damage deposit of USD 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note if booking 5 or more rooms, different policies apply. Contact property after booking for details.

Parking costs USD 36.00, plus tax per night. Large vehicles require a surcharge of USD 3.00, plus tax. Oversized vehicles cost an additional USD 3.00 per day, plus 12% sales tax in addition to the prevailing valet parking daily rate.

Parking rates are subject to change without notice, and special valet parking rates apply during special events. Please call the front desk 30 minutes in advance to allow ample time for the valet.

Due to renovation of the May Baily's Place Bar and our Coffee Shop, the breakfast will be a limited continental breakfast in our Patio Room until renovations are complete.
