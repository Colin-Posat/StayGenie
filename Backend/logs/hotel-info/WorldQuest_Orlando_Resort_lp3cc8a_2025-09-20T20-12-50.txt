HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Condo-Style Resort in Orlando/Lake Buena VistaSpacious Suites with Modern Amenities: Enjoy fully-stocked kitchens, separate living areas, and jetted tubs in our two and three-bedroom condo-style suites. Relax with DVD players, flat-screen TVs, and complimentary WiFi during your stay.Fantastic On-Site Facilities: Stay active at our fitness center, unwind in the heated pool and hot tub, or grab a drink at the seasonal Tiki Bar. Take advantage of laundry facilities, a 24-hour food market, and free shuttle service to EPCOT®.Convenient Location: Just 3.2 km from Walt Disney World®, with easy access to other major theme parks and the Orlando Premium Outlets. Book now for an unforgettable stay in the heart of the magic!
