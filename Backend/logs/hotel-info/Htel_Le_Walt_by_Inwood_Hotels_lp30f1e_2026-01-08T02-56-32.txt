HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Eiffel Tower ViewsHotel Le Walt offers a luxurious 4-star experience in Paris’ 7th arrondissement. Some rooms provide stunning views of the iconic Eiffel Tower, along with modern amenities like air-conditioning, flat screen TVs, and minibars.Charming Garden Terrace and Personalized ServiceGuests can unwind in the hotel's private garden terrace, perfect for enjoying a relaxing drink or a delightful breakfast. The option to have breakfast served in your room adds a touch of personalized service to your stay at Le Walt.Pet-Friendly with Prime LocationConveniently located near famous landmarks like Champ de Mars and the Grand Palais, Hotel Le Walt welcomes pets of all sizes for a small additional fee. Explore Paris with your furry companions by your side at this elegant hotel.Book your stay at Hotel Le Walt for an unforgettable Parisian experience!
