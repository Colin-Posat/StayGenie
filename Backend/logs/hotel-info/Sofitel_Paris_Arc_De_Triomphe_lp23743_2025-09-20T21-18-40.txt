HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience contemporary design and luxury at Sofitel Paris Arc De Triomphe, located steps away from iconic landmarks. Indulge in Bose audio speakers, Nespresso machines, and Balmain toiletries in each room. The suites boast flat-screen TVs and elegant bathrooms with mosaic tiles, offering both a walk-in shower and a freestanding bath.Gastronomic DelightsDelight your taste buds at &quot;Les Cocottes&quot; restaurant by Chef Christan Constant, serving authentic bistro cuisine with seasonal ingredients. Unwind in the hotel lounge by the fireplace or sip on creative cocktails at Bar Sorsi crafted by our expert barmen.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy complimentary WiFi, private parking spaces, and proximity to Charles de Gaulle-Etoile and Ternes Metro Stations for easy exploration. Book now for a sophisticated Parisian getaway at Sofitel Paris Arc De Triomphe.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 150 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
