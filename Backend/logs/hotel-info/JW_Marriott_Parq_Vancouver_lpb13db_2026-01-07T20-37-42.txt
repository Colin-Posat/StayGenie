HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe JW Marriott Parq Vancouver offers elegant and spacious rooms with modern amenities such as Internet TV, Wi-Fi, and 24/7 room service. Some rooms even provide stunning views of False Creek or the city and stadium.Dining and EntertainmentGuests can indulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's eight diverse restaurants and lounges, including establishments led by renowned mastermind Elizabeth Blau. The Spa by JW offers a range of beauty and spa treatments, with an outdoor rooftop hydrotherapy tub boasting panoramic city views.Convenient Location and ServicesLocated in Vancouver's entertainment district, the hotel is near popular attractions like Rogers Arena and BC Place. The concierge desk provides information on local tours and activities, while amenities such as a 24-hour fitness centre and on-site parking add to the convenience.Book now to experience luxury and convenience at JW Marriott Parq Vancouver.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and costs  CAD  14.95 per  24 hours.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  CAD 50 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for CAD 78.75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
