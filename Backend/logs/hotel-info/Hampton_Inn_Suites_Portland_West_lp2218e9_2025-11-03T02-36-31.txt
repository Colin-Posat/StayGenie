HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible).

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Guests appreciated the spacious rooms and stocked amenities, but some mentioned issues with refrigerators not working properly.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - The pool and breakfast buffet were highlights, but some guests wished for additional offerings such as a hot tub and larger refrigerators.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding the price reasonable for the location while others considered it high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - The breakfast buffet was well-received, with some guests appreciating the variety of options available.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the cleanliness, friendly staff, and convenient location.
