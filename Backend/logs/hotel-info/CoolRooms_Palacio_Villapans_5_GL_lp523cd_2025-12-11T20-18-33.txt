HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 18th-Century Palace in SevilleRooftop Sun Terrace and Plunge Pool: Enjoy stunning views from the rooftop terrace and take a refreshing dip in the plunge pool.Elegant Rooms with Modern Amenities: Experience a blend of historic charm and contemporary comfort in the spacious rooms equipped with satellite TV, air conditioning, and luxurious bath amenities.Andalusian Dining and Courtyard: Indulge in traditional cuisine at the restaurant or savor tapas on the garden terrace surrounded by marble columns.Experience the allure of Seville at CoolRooms Palacio Villapanés.
