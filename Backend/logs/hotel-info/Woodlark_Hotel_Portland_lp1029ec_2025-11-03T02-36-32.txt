HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsWoodlark, nestled in Downtown Portland, offers air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and city views. Each room is designed for comfort and style, ensuring a relaxing stay.Convenient FacilitiesGuests can enjoy the convenience of a fitness centre, free WiFi, and a 24-hour front desk ready to assist with any needs. The property also features a restaurant and a shared lounge, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring the city.Prime LocationLocated just 400 metres from Powell's City of Books and 700 metres from Portland Art Museum, Woodlark is the ideal choice for travelers interested in culture, city walks, and exploring Portland's vibrant downtown scene.Book your stay at Woodlark now for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Portland!
