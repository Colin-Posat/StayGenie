HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Garden ViewsStep into a world of elegance at Hotel Bel-Air where each room offers stunning garden views, large windows, and modern furnishings. Relax in style with plush white bedding, spa-style bathrooms, and the option of cozy bathrobes in adult or children's sizes.Amenities GaloreIndulge in the hotel's outdoor pool, state-of-the-art fitness center, and the charming presence of resident swans in the garden pond. Unwind at the full-service spa or enjoy a drink at the Bar &amp; Lounge, complete with a fireplace, grand piano, and picturesque garden views.Culinary Delights and BeyondExperience the vibrant California lifestyle at the Restaurant at Hotel Bel-Air, where Mediterranean influences create a culinary masterpiece. With the renowned shopping of North Rodeo Drive just a short distance away, your stay promises luxury and convenience.Ready to experience the epitome of luxury at Hotel Bel-Air? HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 70 per  day.
