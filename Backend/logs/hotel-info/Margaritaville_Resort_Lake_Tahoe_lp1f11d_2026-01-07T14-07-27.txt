HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the perfect oasis at Margaritaville Resort Lake Tahoe, where relaxation meets adventure in stunning South Lake Tahoe.Recreational AmenitiesDive into fun with our indoor pool, unwind in the hot tub, or energize yourself at our fully-equipped fitness center. Our resort also offers ski storage to ensure you're ready for your next day on the slopes.Dining ExperiencesIndulge your taste buds at JWB Prime, one of our two exquisite restaurants, or enjoy in-room dining with our limited-hours room service. Savor delightful snacks at the coffee shop or unwind with your favorite drink at one of our three bars and lounges.Comfortable AccommodationsRetreat to one of our 399 elegantly designed guestrooms featuring pillowtop beds, Smart TVs, and complimentary Wi-Fi. With thoughtful details like down comforters and Egyptian cotton sheets, a restful stay is guaranteed.Book your escape today and experience all that Margaritaville Resort Lake Tahoe has to offer!
