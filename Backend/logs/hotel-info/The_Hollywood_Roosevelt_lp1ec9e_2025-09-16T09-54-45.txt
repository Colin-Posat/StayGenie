HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationIndulge in luxury at The Hollywood Roosevelt, located just 300 metres from Dolby Theater. Each room is elegantly designed with a desk, private bathroom, and flat-screen TV. Some rooms even offer a terrace for a stunning view.Fantastic AmenitiesEnjoy a refreshing dip in the outdoor swimming pool, stay active in the fitness centre, or relax in the garden. With a restaurant, room service, and a 24-hour front desk, your every need is catered to. Free WiFi, an ATM, and concierge services are also available for your convenience.Entertainment and ConvenienceHave a blast playing table tennis or explore the nearby hiking trails. Unwind at the in-house bar or get work done in the business area. With Capitol Records Building and Hollywood Bowl close by, there's never a dull moment at The Hollywood Roosevelt.Book now for an unforgettable stay at The Hollywood Roosevelt!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 600 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Be aware during the summer months through September 7th that live music is played during the day and evening Friday through Saturday and official hosted pool parties occur Sundays from 14:00 to 18:00, as well as our Night Swim on Tuesday evenings from 20:00 to 02:00.

Reservations of 9 or more rooms will be subject to different policies and deposit. Please contact the property for more details.

Pool is undergoing restoration from January 3rd to January 18th 2024.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 70 per  day.
