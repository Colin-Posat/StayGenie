HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated in Venice's tranquil Dorsoduro neighborhood, Hotel Messner is a stone's throw away from the Salute water bus stop, offering easy access to key attractions.Charming AccommodationsThe hotel boasts elegantly furnished rooms, each equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, satellite TV, and a private bathroom. Guests can choose from rooms overlooking the canal, the garden, or the annex building.Delightful Dining ExperienceIndulge in a delectable dining experience at the on-site restaurant, serving a variety of meat and fish dishes complemented by a fine selection of wines. The bar and outdoor dining area provide a perfect setting to unwind after a day of exploration.Ready to experience the charm of Hotel Messner? Book your stay now and immerse yourself in the beauty of Venice.

• Room Quality: 1.5/10 - Reviews consistently criticized broken facilities, mold, bedbugs, and unbearable conditions in the rooms.
• Amenities: 1/10 - The lack of proper amenities and the presence of broken facilities led to extremely negative feedback from guests.
• Value for Money: 1.8/10 - Many guests felt cheated by the poor quality and conditions of the hotel, leading to a low rating for value for money.
• Food and Beverage: 1/10 - No positive feedback was mentioned regarding food and beverage services, with focus instead on the numerous issues with the accommodations.
• Overall Experience: 1.3/10 - The majority of guests had an extremely negative overall experience, leading to strong recommendations against staying at the hotel.
