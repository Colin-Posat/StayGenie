Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3.5/10 - Cleanliness received low ratings across multiple reviews, with guests complaining about unclean rooms, dirty fixtures and fittings, and moldy air conditioning.
• Service: 7.3/10 - Service was generally positively mentioned, with guests appreciating the staff's friendliness and helpfulness. However, there were some complaints about unhelpful or unfriendly staff members.
• Location: 7.8/10 - The hotel's location was praised for being safe and conveniently located near public transport. However, some reviews mentioned the area as rough and not ideal for walking at night.
• Room Quality: 5.5/10 - Room quality received mixed reviews, with guests mentioning small rooms, thin walls, noisy air conditioning, and outdated furniture as issues.
• Amenities: 5.8/10 - Amenities were rated lower, with guests expressing disappointment in the lack of certain services like spa facilities and the overall quality of amenities provided.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Value for money was a common concern among guests, with some feeling that the price was high for the services offered and the quality of the accommodation.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed feedback, with positive comments about breakfast buffet but some guests wished for more variety and better quality beverages.
• Overall Experience: 6.5/10 - The overall experience varied among guests, with some having a positive stay while others mentioned significant issues with cleanliness, room quality, and value for money.
