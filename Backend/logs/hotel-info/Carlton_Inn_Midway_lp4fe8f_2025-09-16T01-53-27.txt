HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in ChicagoLocated just 8 km from Guaranteed Rate Field, Carlton Inn Midway offers a convenient stay near major attractions like United Center and Willis Tower. With the Midway International Airport only 3.1 km away, our hotel is the perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers.Comfort and ConvenienceEnjoy a comfortable stay with amenities such as a fitness centre, free parking, and a shared lounge. Stay connected with free WiFi throughout the property and indulge in complimentary premium movie channels in your room. Start your day right with continental and buffet breakfast options available every morning.Additional ServicesBenefit from a 24-hour front desk, luggage storage, and a complimentary airport shuttle. For your convenience, we offer a business centre and vending machines with a selection of drinks and snacks on site. Book your stay at Carlton Inn Midway for a memorable Chicago experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 16 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
