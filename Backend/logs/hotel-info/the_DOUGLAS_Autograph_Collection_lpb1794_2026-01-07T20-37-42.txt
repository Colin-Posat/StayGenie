HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique Hotel in Vancouver's Entertainment DistrictThe DOUGLAS, Autograph Collection offers a sophisticated stay with modern décor, a 30,000-square-foot rooftop park, Parq Casino, and the stylish D/6 Bar and lounge.Elegant Rooms and AmenitiesAll rooms feature air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, Internet TV, and illy coffee and tea facilities. The private bathrooms boast luxurious amenities, and guests can enjoy 24/7 room service.Convenient Facilities and Nearby AttractionsGuests have access to a fitness center, spacious conference space, and a 24-hour front desk. Explore nearby attractions like Rogers Arena, BC Place, and False Creek, all within 500 meters.Ready to experience luxury and convenience in Vancouver? HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and costs  CAD  14.95 per  24 hours.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  CAD 50 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for CAD 78.75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
