HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Okura Tokyo, with its two buildings, offers a blend of modern amenities and traditional Japanese hospitality. From the serene garden to the sophisticated bar, every corner exudes elegance.Exclusive Club Lounge and City ViewsIndulge in the breathtaking cityscape views from the Prestige Tower located on the high floors. Opt for the Club Lounge Access in the Heritage Wing for a premium experience, including a complimentary welcome drink and access to the Club Lounge.Convenient Facilities and ServicesWith a 24-hour front desk, concierge, and room service, your every need is anticipated and promptly met. The hotel also provides a buffet breakfast, ensuring a delightful start to your day.Book now to experience the epitome of luxury at The Okura Tokyo.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
