HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious and Convenient LocationHotel Mascagni, located in Rome's historic centre, offers a luxurious stay just a 5-minute walk from Termini Train Station. The contemporary design and 1920s style bathrooms provide a unique and upscale experience for guests.Delicious Breakfast and Helpful StaffStart your day with a delicious continental buffet breakfast featuring homemade cakes and bread. The 24-hour multilingual staff are ready to assist with booking tickets, tours, and recommending the best dining spots in Rome.Near Major AttractionsExplore Rome's iconic landmarks with ease - the Trevi Fountain, Spanish Steps, and Vatican City are all within walking distance or a short metro ride from the hotel.Experience luxury, convenience, and exceptional service at Hotel Mascagni - book your stay now for an unforgettable Roman getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
