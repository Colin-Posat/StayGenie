HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to the Astro Palace Hotel &amp; Spa, your luxurious sanctuary in the heart of Santorini.Spa RetreatIndulge in rejuvenating treatments at our full-service spa. Unwind with soothing massages, uplifting body treatments, and refreshing facials designed to melt your stress away.Culinary DelightsSavor authentic Greek cuisine at Pleiades, one of our two exquisite restaurants.
