HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near the BeachHotel Mediterráneo, just 250 meters from Guardamar del Segura’s beaches, offers the perfect seaside retreat. Explore the sandy shores and enjoy the coastal breeze at your doorstep.Modern Rooms and AmenitiesRelax in air-conditioned rooms with contemporary décor, equipped with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi access throughout the hotel. For added security, safes are available for a small fee.Convenient Access to AttractionsDiscover Reina Sofía Park just a short walk away or tee off at one of the 4 nearby golf clubs. With Torrevieja 12 km away and Alicante a 45-minute drive, Hotel Mediterráneo is your gateway to the best of the Costa Blanca.Experience seaside luxury at Hotel Mediterráneo - Book your stay now!
