Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests mentioned clean rooms as a positive aspect, although some reviews highlighted minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.6/10 - Staff friendliness and service at the hotel were highly appreciated by guests.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel is situated in a great location close to key landmarks and public transport, although some guests noted noise issues due to downtown traffic.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and spacious, while some guests mentioned outdated facilities and thin walls.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Guests appreciated the pool and gym facilities, but some reviews pointed out the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied regarding the pricing, with some guests feeling it was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, with a few guests suggesting more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
