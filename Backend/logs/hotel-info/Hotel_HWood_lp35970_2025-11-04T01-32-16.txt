• Amenities: 5/10 - The amenities provided were lacking, with reports of limited toiletries, towels, and cleanliness in common areas.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was fair for the location, while others found it expensive for the services offered.
