HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tropical Beachfront ParadiseNestled in Singapore, the Shangri-La Rasa Sentosa is a tropical haven perfect for families and sun-seekers.Family-Friendly AmenitiesEnjoy a variety of sea sports, pools, and a 24-hour gym with lush views. Children can have a blast at the water slides, splash pad, and activity centers while parents unwind at Chi, The Spa.Dining and LeisureIndulge in dining at Silver Shell Cafe or Trapizza. End the day with sunset drinks at Siloso Beach Bar. Unwind in spacious guestrooms with private balconies overlooking the South China Sea.Book now for an unforgettable beachfront experience at Shangri-La Rasa Sentosa, where relaxation and adventure await!
