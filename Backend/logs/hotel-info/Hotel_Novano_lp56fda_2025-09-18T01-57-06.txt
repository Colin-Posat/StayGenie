HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Novano offers spacious rooms merging Ottoman and modern designs, featuring luxurious fabrics, parquet floors, air conditioning, and minibar.Culinary DelightsIndulge in Turkish and international dishes at the on-site à la carte restaurant, along with a breakfast buffet. Explore nearby local taverns and international dining options.Convenient LocationSituated just 150 meters from the Blue Mosque, with Topkapi Palace and the Grand Bazaar within walking distance. The 24-hour reception staff can assist with local attractions, and an airport shuttle is available. Book your stay at Hotel Novano for a memorable Istanbul experience.
