HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Literary Theme Inspired by Marcel ProustHotel Litteraire Le Swann, BW Premier Collection pays homage to Marcel Proust, offering a unique literary experience in a 19th-century building. The rooms are modern and feature amenities like TV, minibar, and complimentary hot drinks.Convenient Location and ServicesSituated just a short walk from Saint-Lazare Train Station, guests enjoy easy access to popular attractions like Galeries Lafayette. The hotel offers a 24-hour reception, free newspapers, and a dry-cleaning service. Metro Station Europe nearby provides direct links to key landmarks.Art Deco Breakfast ExperienceIndulge in a diverse buffet breakfast daily under an exquisite Art Deco glass ceiling. Immerse yourself in the ambiance of the era while starting your day on a delightful note.Book your stay at Hotel Litteraire Le Swann, BW Premier Collection for a literary escape with modern comforts and convenient services.
