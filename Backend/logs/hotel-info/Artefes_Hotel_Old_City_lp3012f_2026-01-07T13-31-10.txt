HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Ottoman Mansion with Stunning ViewsLuxurious Amenities: Enjoy free WiFi, cable TV, and a minibar in our air-conditioned rooms with traditional Ottoman-style décor. Relax on our rooftop terrace overlooking the Sea of Marmara and Bosphorus.Convenient Location: Explore Istanbul’s top attractions within walking distance, including Topkapi Palace, Sultan Ahmet Mosque, and Hagia Sophia. The hotel is just minutes away from the Kennedy Circle tram stop for easy access to Sarayburnu.Personalized Service: Start your day with a delicious Turkish breakfast buffet and let our tour desk help you plan your itinerary. Experience the charm of a 19th-century Ottoman mansion with modern conveniences.Book now to experience the perfect blend of history and luxury at Artefes Hotel Old City.
