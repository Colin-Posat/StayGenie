HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in Sydney CBDSituated in the vibrant heart of Sydney CBD, Rydges World Square offers luxury accommodation with modern amenities. Guests can unwind in the fitness centre, sip on cocktails at the bar, and indulge in delicious meals at the restaurant.Convenient LocationAdjacent to the World Square Shopping Centre, our hotel is a short stroll from Chinatown and a brief walk from Darling Harbour and the Sydney Convention Centre. With easy access to public transport, exploring the city is a breeze for our guests.Modern Facilities and Dining OptionsOur air-conditioned rooms are well-equipped with essentials like a minibar and WiFi access. The fully refurbished fitness centre boasts state-of-the-art equipment, while Amber Restaurant and Bar offer a delightful dining experience with a range of culinary delights.Experience luxury and convenience at Rydges World Square - book your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
A damage deposit of AUD 300 is required on arrival. This will be collected as a cash payment. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full in cash, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that there is a 1.50% charge when you pay with a credit card.

You must show a valid photo ID upon check-in. A record and/or copy of this identification may be retained by Rydges World Square to minimise the risk of fraudulent credit card usage and for verification purposes with your credit card provider in the event of a credit card charge dispute.

You must show a valid credit card upon check in. If the guest cannot provide a credit card, a AUD $300 security deposit is required by by cash or EFTPOS.

Please note that the guest registering at check in must be at least 18 years of age.

Please note that there is a $1000 limit on charges to your room. In the event a room account exceeds this figure, a payment will be requested to bring the account back to zero.

Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  AUD 65 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for AUD 80 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for AUD 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7.1/10 - Rooms were described as spacious with comfortable beds, but there were complaints about noise from air conditioners and outdated facilities.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, particularly highlighting the free parking option, although some mentioned issues with noisy air conditioners.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Breakfast received positive comments for variety and quality, though there were complaints about cold food and limited options.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience, with praise for the location, staff friendliness, and room cleanliness.
