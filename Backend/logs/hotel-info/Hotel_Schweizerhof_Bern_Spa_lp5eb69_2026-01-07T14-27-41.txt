HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and Cutting-edge FacilitiesHotel Schweizerhof Bern &amp; Spa offers luxurious rooms with elegant interior design and cutting-edge facilities. Each room features a minibar, Nespresso coffee machine, and state-of-the-art entertainment. The large bathrooms include a rain shower, bathtub, towel warmer, bathrobe, and slippers for ultimate comfort.Wellness Centre and Rooftop TerraceEnjoy the 500 m² wellness centre with a hot tub, sauna, hammam, and 24-hour fitness room. Relax on the trendy rooftop terrace with stunning views of Bern. Indulge in innovative cuisine at Jack’s Brasserie or savor a cocktail at the lobby lounge bar or Sky terrace.Convenient Location and Premium ServicesCentrally located near Bern Railway Station, Hotel Schweizerhof offers valet parking and free public transport for easy exploration. Don't miss the opportunity to experience luxury and relaxation at Schweizerhof Bern &amp; Spa.

Opening hours at the spa:
Monday to Thursday: 3.00 pm to 8.00 pm, Friday 10.30 am to 9.00 pm, Saturday 9.00 am to 9.00 pm and Sunday 9.00 am to 8.00 pm.

Friday to Sunday we have Spa Slots that may be reserved in advance.

Please note that children under 12 years are only allowed in the spa area from 16:00 until 17:00 and need to be accompanied by an adult. Children from 12 to 16 years of age in the company of an adult have unlimited access to the spa area.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  CHF 50 per  day.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 15 years old stay for CHF 65 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for CHF 65 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
