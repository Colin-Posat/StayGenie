IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Kimpton Palladian Hotel, an IHG Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
WiFi is free for IHG Rewards members only. Fees apply to non-members to access the WiFi. Please see the Policies section for further details.

Kimpton Hotels considers 10 or more rooms a group. Kimpton Hotels reserves the right to cancel 10 or more rooms booked online. Please contact the hotel directly if you are booking 10 or more rooms.

A guest amenities fee of $25 (plus tax) per night includes: A $10 daily food and beverage credit to be used at Shaker + Spear or Front Desk Bodega (cannot combined, bundled, or rolled over), Complimentary Palladian postcard and postage, Unlimited local and long distance phone calls, Upgraded WiFi speed throughout the hotel's guest rooms and public spaces for up to 5 devices (Normally a $14.99 charge).
