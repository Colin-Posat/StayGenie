Please inform Exhibition Court Hotel 4 in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of GBP 20 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Exhibition Court Hotel 4 offers cozy and comfortable ensuite air-conditioned bedrooms that perfectly blend old-world charm with modern amenities. Each room is equipped with in-room controlled heating and air-conditioning, ensuring a pleasant stay. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a Smart TV, USB and type C sockets, and complimentary WiFi.

In addition to the comfortable rooms, guests also have access to a reception room where they can indulge in 24/7 Hilton's coffee machine, serving delicious lattes and cappuccinos.

Located in one of Kensington's historic white stucco buildings, the hotel is ideally situated just a short walk away from some of London's most iconic attractions, including the Natural History Museum, the Victoria and Albert Museum, Buckingham Palace, Hyde Park, Oxford Street, and even Chelsea Football Club.

Group bookings (more than 2 rooms) are not accepted for a one-night stay.
Check in after midnight will incur a fee of £20
