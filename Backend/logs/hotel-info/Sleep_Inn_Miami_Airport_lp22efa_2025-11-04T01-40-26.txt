Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Overall, guests praised the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas, with some minor complaints in a few reviews.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Service was generally appreciated, but there were some mixed reviews about the response times and language barriers with staff.
• Location: 7.6/10 - The hotel's proximity to key locations and transport options was well-received, although some guests mentioned noise disturbances from the airport.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests found the rooms to be comfortable, but a few highlighted issues such as noisy air conditioning and limited bathroom space.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - While some guests appreciated the amenities like cleanliness and convenience, others expressed disappointment in the shuttle service and lack of soundproofing.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some feeling it was justified for the location, while others found it expensive compared to the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Reviews for the food and beverage services were mixed, with some guests enjoying the breakfast and others mentioning issues such as limited space and food running out quickly.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, but there were notable complaints about noise levels, cleanliness, and service responsiveness.
