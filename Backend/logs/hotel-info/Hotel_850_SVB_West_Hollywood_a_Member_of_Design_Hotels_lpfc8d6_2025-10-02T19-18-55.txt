HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation:Hotel 850 SVB West Hollywood at Beverly Hills offers 4-star accommodation with modern amenities including free WiFi, an iPad in every room, and room service. Relax in comfort with air conditioning and enjoy the convenience of a private bathroom and flat-screen TV.Convenient Location:Located just a short distance from top attractions like Petersen Automotive Museum and LACMA, this non-smoking property provides easy access to the best of Los Angeles. Explore the city with complimentary bikes or unwind on the terrace overlooking the vibrant surroundings.Exceptional Service:Start your day with a delicious continental breakfast and rely on the attentive staff for any assistance you may need, available 24/7 at the front desk. Book your stay at Hotel 850 SVB West Hollywood at Beverly Hills for a memorable and seamless experience.
