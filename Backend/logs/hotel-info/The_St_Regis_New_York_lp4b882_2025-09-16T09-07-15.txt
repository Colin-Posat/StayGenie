Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on cleanliness, with some praising the hotel as super clean and others mentioning stains and peeling wallpaper in the rooms.
• Service: 5.7/10 - Service received a low rating due to multiple complaints about cold and unfriendly staff, lack of explanations, and poor service at various hotel amenities.
• Location: 8.4/10 - Guests appreciated the hotel's central location, considering it a major advantage.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Room quality received a mid-range rating due to mixed reviews on room conditions and delays in room readiness.
• Amenities: 6.4/10 - Amenities were criticized for poor service and lack of distinction or benefits for hotel guests at food and beverage venues.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Guests had varied opinions on the value for money, with some feeling disappointed with the services compared to price, while others appreciated the location and historic charm.
• Food and Beverage: 6/10 - Food quality and service received low ratings, with complaints about poor quality, limited options, and unfriendly service at various dining venues.
• Overall Experience: 6.3/10 - Overall experience was dampened by poor service, cleanliness issues, and unmet expectations.
