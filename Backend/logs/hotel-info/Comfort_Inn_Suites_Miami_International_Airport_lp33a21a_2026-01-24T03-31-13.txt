HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesComfort Inn &amp; Suites Miami International Airport offers a range of luxurious amenities for guests to enjoy, including an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, and shared lounge. The hotel's sun terrace provides the perfect spot to relax and soak up the Miami sunshine.Convenient ServicesGuests can take advantage of convenient services such as private parking, airport transfers, and a 24-hour front desk. Free WiFi throughout the property ensures guests stay connected during their stay. The business centre and vending machines with snacks and drinks add to the overall convenience of the hotel.Prime LocationLocated just a few steps from Miami International Airport, Comfort Inn &amp; Suites is also close to popular attractions like Marlins Park, Adrienne Arsht Center for the Performing Arts, and American Airlines Arena. Experience comfort and convenience at our hotel.
