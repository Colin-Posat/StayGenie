HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover a tranquil oasis in the heart of Bangkok at The Continent Hotel Sukhumvit, where luxury meets convenience.Relaxation and WellnessIndulge in our full-service spa, offering rejuvenating massages and pampering treatments. Unwind by the spacious outdoor pool or elevate your fitness routine at our state-of-the-art fitness center.Culinary DelightsSavor innovative Fusion cuisine at Medinii, our on-site restaurant, or opt for cozy room service during limited hours. Don’t forget to unwind at our stylish bar/lounge or poolside bar after a day of exploring.Ultimate ComfortChoose from 153 elegantly designed air-conditioned rooms equipped with modern amenities. Enjoy complimentary internet, 40-inch LCD TVs, and ensure your comfort with private bathrooms and minibars.Experience luxury and convenience in the heart of Bangkok. Book your stay today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 11 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* Parking height restrictions apply.
