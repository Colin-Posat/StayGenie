HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Pool and Sun Terrace:Hotel Lleó features a charming rooftop pool with a sun terrace and loungers, offering a perfect spot to unwind and soak up the Barcelona sun.Elegant Decor and Spacious Rooms:Step into elegance at Hotel Lleó where polished floors, glass accents, and inviting lighting create a sophisticated ambiance. The spacious air-conditioned rooms are equipped with modern amenities such as fridges and flat-screen TVs.Convenient Location and Dining Options:Located next to Plaza Catalunya and Las Ramblas, this hotel is a prime choice for exploring Barcelona's attractions. Indulge in Mediterranean cuisine at the on-site restaurant or enjoy a relaxing drink at the lounge area and snack bar.Experience the allure of Barcelona at Hotel Lleó - book your stay now for a memorable getaway!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff, although some guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport, making it ideal for exploring the city.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were spacious and clean, with comfortable beds, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests praising the pool area, but others mentioned issues like lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the services offered while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests wished for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others due to its great location and friendly staff.
