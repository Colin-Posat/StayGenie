Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned specific issues like carpet smell and maintenance problems.
• Service: 6.7/10 - Service received mixed reviews. While some guests found the staff helpful and friendly, others reported bad experiences, especially in handling complaints and issues.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was widely praised for its convenience, proximity to attractions, and availability of nearby dining options.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally described as clean and comfortable, with positive comments on room size and breakfast quality. Some guests reported issues with air conditioning.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities offered received mixed feedback, with some guests enjoying the breakfast and clean rooms, while others expressed dissatisfaction over the lack of responses to maintenance issues like malfunctioning air conditioning.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on the value for money were divided, with some feeling the prices were slightly high given the services offered, while others found it reasonable, particularly because of the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Food and beverage services generally met guests' expectations, with positive feedback on breakfast quality and convenience, although suggestions were made for improvements such as variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience with the hotel, appreciating aspects like cleanliness, location, and breakfast quality, though some expressed discontent with service-related issues and inadequate responses to complaints.
