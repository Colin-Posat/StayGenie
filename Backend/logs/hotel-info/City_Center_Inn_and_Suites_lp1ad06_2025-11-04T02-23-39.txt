HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in San FranciscoLocated in the heart of San Francisco, City Center Inn &amp; Suites offers a convenient stay just minutes away from major attractions like Moscone Center, Union Square, and Oracle Park.Comfortable and Convenient AmenitiesEach guest room is equipped with modern conveniences including a flat-screen LED TV, free WiFi, and in-room coffee maker. Guests can also enjoy the complimentary private limited parking and multilingual staff services.Proximity to Popular AttractionsWith Oracle Park, Bill Graham Civic Auditorium, and City Hall within walking distance, guests can easily explore the best of San Francisco. Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform City Center Inn and Suites in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
A damage deposit of USD 150 is required. The host charges this 14 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
All Major Credit/Debit Cards will be accepted at check-in. Parking is limited and is filled on a first-come, first-serve basis. Guests under the age of 18 are only allowed to check in with a parent or official guardian. Additional charges for more than 4 guests will apply for all suite categories. Prior to check in time for early arrivals and late departures who choose to store their luggage/baggage at our hotel. Guests may only use existing bedding, no alternative accommodations will be provided. Guests may not create alternative bedding.
