HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsLocated in the heart of Mexico City, Hotel Templo Mayor offers a unique blend of historic charm and modern comforts. Housed in an 18th-century building, the property is just a short walk from iconic landmarks such as the Metropolitan Cathedral. The rooms are elegantly decorated with hand-painted Talavera tiles and feature amenities like cable TV and rain showers, providing a luxurious experience for guests.Central Patio and Cultural ProximityThe hotel boasts a central patio where guests can relax and unwind, adding to the tranquil ambiance of the property. With the Antiguo Colegio de San Ildefonso Museum right next door, guests have easy access to cultural experiences.Prime Location for ExploringHotel Templo Mayor's prime location makes it a convenient choice for travelers. Situated near major transportation hubs, including the Metrobus and Zócalo Metro Station, guests can easily explore the vibrant city of Mexico. Book your stay now and immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of this dynamic destination.
