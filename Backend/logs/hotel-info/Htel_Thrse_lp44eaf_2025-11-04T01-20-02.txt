CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Location: 9.1/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks, restaurants, and public transport was a standout feature for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the comfort of the rooms and amenities, with some noting minor issues such as small room sizes.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The hotel's amenities were generally satisfactory, with mentions of complimentary tea time and quality bathroom products.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews were received regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location but others finding it high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.9/10 - Breakfast and F&B services were well-received, with positive comments on the buffet breakfast and the quality of service.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighted by the great location, friendly staff, and comfortable rooms.
