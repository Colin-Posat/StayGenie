HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Buildings and Prime LocationHotel St. Pierre French Quarter, with 2 separate pools, is nestled in historic buildings just a stroll away from vibrant Bourbon Street and Louis Armstrong Park.Comfortable Rooms with ViewsGuest rooms feature modern amenities like a flat-screen cable TV, microwave, and refrigerator. Some rooms even offer serene views of the inner courtyards.Convenience and AccessibilityEnjoy round-the-clock assistance at the 24-hour front desk and take advantage of the on-site ATM. With the Morial Convention Center and Mercedes-Benz Superdome nearby, plus easy access to the airport, your stay is as convenient as it gets.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hotel St. Pierre French Quarter!
