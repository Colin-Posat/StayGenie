HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Japanese Garden ViewExperience a stay like no other at The Prince Kyoto Takaragaike, designed by Togo Murano. Immerse yourself in spacious rooms overlooking a serene Japanese garden. Relax in the large en suite bathroom or unwind while enjoying satellite TV with nature views.Convenient Location and Exceptional DiningLocated just a 3-minute walk from Kyoto International Conference Centre, our hotel offers easy access to the city. Indulge in culinary delights at one of our four restaurants serving Chinese and Japanese cuisine. For a quick bite or souvenir shopping, visit our on-site gift shop.Explore with Ease and Enjoy Personalized ServicesDiscover Kyoto with ease as Kokusai Kaikan Subway Station is a short walk away. Take advantage of sightseeing assistance at the concierge desk or rent electric bicycles for a personalized tour. With a business services centre, we cater to all your needs.Book now for an unforgettable stay at The Prince Kyoto Takaragaike.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform The Prince Kyoto Takaragaike, Autograph Collection in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
When booking for 15 guests or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Meals for children using existing beds are available at an extra charge.

Free luggage delivery service between the hotel and the JR Kyoto Station's Welcome Counter is available until 14:00.

Guests staying at the Rooms with Lounge Access and Suites are offered Ryoal Lounge access with drinks and snacks.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  JPY 1500 per  stay.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for ¥6,325 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
