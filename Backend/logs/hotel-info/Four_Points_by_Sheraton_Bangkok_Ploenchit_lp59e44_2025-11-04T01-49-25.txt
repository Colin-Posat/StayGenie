HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenience and Modern Rooms:Novotel Bangkok Ploenchit Sukhumvit, located in the heart of Bangkok, offers modern rooms with glass-walled bathrooms, cable TV, and minibar. The hotel's prime location near the Ploenchit BTS Skytrain Station provides easy access to the city's business districts and popular shopping centers like Central Embassy and Central Chidlom.Leisure Facilities and Services:Guests can unwind at the outdoor pool and fitness facilities on the 9th floor, complete with sauna rooms and personal trainers. The hotel also features 12 multi-function rooms for meetings and events, a concierge counter for sightseeing arrangements, and services like childcare, laundry, and currency exchange.Dining Experience:Indulge in all-day dining and hearty buffets at The SQUARE on the 8th floor, or savor light bites and drinks at Dee Lounge &amp; Beer Garden with live music entertainment. Certified chefs prepare a Halal menu, ensuring a diverse culinary experience for guests.Book your stay at Novotel Bangkok Ploenchit Sukhumvit for a seamless blend of convenience, leisure, and culinary delights.
