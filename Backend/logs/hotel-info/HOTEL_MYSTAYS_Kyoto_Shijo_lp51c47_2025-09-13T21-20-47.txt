HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Centrally Located Luxury AccommodationHotel MyStays Kyoto-Shijo, recommended by the Michelin Guide, offers a prime location just a 6-minute walk from Shijo Subway Station.Comfort and ConvenienceRooms at Kyoto-Shijo MyStays are equipped with modern amenities including free WiFi, LCD TV, air purifier, and a safe. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of a refrigerator and electric kettle in their rooms.Culinary Delights and Cultural ExperiencesIndulge in local Kyoto cuisine at Kyo Washoku Dining Chacha and savor the traditional flavors of &quot;Obanzai&quot; during breakfast buffets. Don't miss the chance to explore nearby attractions like Nijo Castle, Kiyomizu-dera Temple, and Heian-Jingu Shrine.To experience luxury, comfort, and authentic Kyoto hospitality, book your stay at Hotel MyStays Kyoto-Shijo today!
