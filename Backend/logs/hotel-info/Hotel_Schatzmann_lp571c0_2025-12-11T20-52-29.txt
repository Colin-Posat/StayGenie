HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Scenic Mountain RetreatNestled in a picturesque mountain landscape just a stone's throw away from Vaduz, Hotel Schatzmann is a serene escape offering complimentary WiFi and parking facilities.Outdoor Adventure AwaitsExplore the nearby Triesenberg village within a 10-minute drive or head to the Malbun ski and hiking area just 20 minutes away. Golf enthusiasts can tee off at the Haag/Gams course, a short 15-minute drive from the hotel.Comfort and ConvenienceWith a selection of 10 standard rooms and 15 comfort rooms and suites, guests can indulge in either 3-star or 4-star accommodations. Even standard room guests can savor a delicious breakfast spread. Take advantage of the complimentary business corner and secure parking in the hotel's private garage.Book your tranquil mountain getaway at Hotel Schatzmann today!

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for CHF 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
