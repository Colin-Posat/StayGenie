Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but some noted that rooms were a little outdated.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, especially in the restaurant where guests felt like family.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel is located close to key landmarks and public transport, although some guests mentioned a sketchy area and a view of the freeway.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were spacious, well-stocked, and had Netflix available, but some guests mentioned old, outdated carpets.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with highlights on the gym and pool, but some guests mentioned a lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value varied, with some guests feeling the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others found it suitable for the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with comments on great food, but some guests wanted more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
