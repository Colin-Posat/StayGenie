HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique Villas with Private Pools:Experience the ultimate relaxation at Satori Villas, nestled in the serene surroundings of Ubud's lush rice fields. Each boutique villa comes with a private pool, terrace, and garden views, offering a tranquil retreat for your getaway.Personalized Services and Amenities:Enjoy the convenience of free WiFi, in-house chef services, and a dedicated staff to cater to your every need. Indulge in a hot tub session, request grocery delivery or packed lunches, and let the chef curate a personalized menu for in-villa dining, ensuring a truly luxurious stay.Exploration and Adventure:Take advantage of bike and car hire services to explore the vibrant area of Ubud, popular for cycling. With a free scooter shuttle service and key attractions like Ubud Monkey Forest and Elephant Cave nearby, Satori Villas offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.Book your stay at Satori Villas now for an unforgettable luxury experience in Ubud!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Satori Villas Bali in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that this property is located about 300 metres from the main road where cars are not allowed. There is a scooter shuttle service available to city centre.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for Rp 100,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  IDR 25000 per  day.
