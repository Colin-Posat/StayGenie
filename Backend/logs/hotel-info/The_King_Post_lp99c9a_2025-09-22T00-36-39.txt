HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Swahili Architecture: The King Post in Nairobi offers a one-of-a-kind experience with its castle-like building showcasing Swahili architecture infused with Arabic, Portuguese, and Indian influences.Cozy Apartments with Old World Charm: The apartments at The King Post blend simplicity with elegance, featuring mahogany and oak furnishings, fully furnished kitchens, and draped beds with mosquito nets, providing a touch of old world charm.Relaxation and Recreation: Enjoy a range of amenities including an outdoor pool, private parking, free WiFi, a fitness centre, and the option to indulge in massages and beauty treatments.Book your stay at The King Post now to immerse yourself in a unique blend of history, comfort, and relaxation in Nairobi.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

Housekeeping and maid service is provided daily, except on Sundays and public holidays. One linen and two towel changes per week.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for US$50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
