IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of EUR 200 is required. The host charges this 7 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
For group reservations, 100% of the total amount will be charged to secure your booking.

When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

For all bookings made with prepayment conditions, guest will have to provide the credit card used to make the booking and a valid ID upon check in. Please note that if the guest knows in advance that providing the same credit card as during the booking process will not be possible, he will have to contact the hotel prior to arrival using the contact details provided on the confirmation.

Please note that the hotel's reception is located on the first floor. The lift starts on the first floor. Staff are available to assist with luggage.

A pre-authorisation corresponding to the total amount of the stay will be made at the time of booking.

The credit card used to make the reservation must be presented upon arrival. The cardholder’s name must match the photo ID.

For reservations with prepayment, the credit card used to make the reservation and a corresponding photo ID will be requested at check-in.
