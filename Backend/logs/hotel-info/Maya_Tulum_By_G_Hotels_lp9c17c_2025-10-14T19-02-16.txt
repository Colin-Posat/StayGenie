HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationMaya Tulum By G Hotels, located in the heart of Tulum, offers air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities like a coffee machine, minibar, and flat-screen TV. Guests can unwind in comfort and style during their stay.Relaxing FacilitiesEnjoy a range of facilities including a fitness centre, indoor pool, and a garden to relax in. The hotel also features a restaurant and bar where guests can indulge in delicious meals and drinks.Convenient LocationSituated in the Zona Hotelera district, Maya Tulum By G Hotels is just steps away from South Tulum Beach. HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 17 years old stay for US$20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 17 years old and over stay for US$100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.2/10 - Mixed reviews about cleanliness. Some guests mentioned rooms were clean, while others expressed concerns about maintenance issues.
• Service: 2.7/10 - Service received predominantly negative feedback. Guests complained about slow response times, rude staff, and unhelpfulness.
• Location: 5/10 - Location received mixed feedback. While close to key landmarks, the presence of rotting seaweed and unpleasant smells affected the overall experience.
• Room Quality: 3.3/10 - Room quality had mostly negative reviews, citing maintenance issues, rusty doors and windows, as well as cleanliness concerns.
• Amenities: 3.6/10 - Amenities received low ratings with guests mentioning broken taps, exposed plumbing, and slow service for basic needs.
• Value for Money: 2.5/10 - Guests felt the hotel was overpriced for the services offered, particularly noting the lack of value for money and additional charges.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews for food and beverage, with some positive comments on breakfast and variety, while others mentioned limited options.
• Overall Experience: 3.3/10 - Overall experience received negative feedback, with guests expressing disappointment, safety concerns, and lack of response to issues.
