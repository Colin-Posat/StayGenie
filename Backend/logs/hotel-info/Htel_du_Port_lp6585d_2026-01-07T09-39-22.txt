HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Marina Hotel ExperienceCharming Rooms with Wooden DecorExperience the cozy warmth of our rooms adorned with wooden decor, providing a tranquil retreat during your stay at Hôtel du Port. Each room and suite offers a private bathroom with soothing earthy tones, ensuring your comfort and relaxation.Convenient Location for ExploringSituated at the Nogent sur Marne marina, just 15 km from Paris, our hotel offers easy access to the city and beyond. With private parking available and the RER E a short 10-minute walk away, exploring the Île-de-France region is a breeze. Additionally, the RER A, located 20 minutes away on foot, provides direct access to iconic landmarks like the Arc de Triomphe and the bustling La Defense district.Book your stay at Hôtel du Port now for a memorable and convenient experience in the heart of Nogent sur Marne.
