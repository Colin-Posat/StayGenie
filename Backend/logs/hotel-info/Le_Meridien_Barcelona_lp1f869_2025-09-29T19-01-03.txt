HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations - Le Méridien Barcelona is a favorite among celebrities, offering stylish rooms with high-end amenities like flat-screen TVs and iPod docks.Dining Experience - Indulge in a delicious international breakfast at The Méridien and savor creative Mediterranean cuisine at CentOnze, all while enjoying stunning views of Las Ramblas.Relaxation and Entertainment - Unwind at the on-site spa with a variety of treatments or explore the nearby Palau Güell for free, adding to the overall luxurious experience of your stay.Experience the glamour and comfort of Le Méridien Barcelona - book now for an unforgettable stay!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Le Meridien Barcelona in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Cots are available upon request and are free of charge. However, bedding for cots is not provided and must be provided by the guest.

Please note that guests must present the credit card used to make the reservation on arrival. If you are not the owner of the credit card used to make the reservation, please contact the property in advance.
