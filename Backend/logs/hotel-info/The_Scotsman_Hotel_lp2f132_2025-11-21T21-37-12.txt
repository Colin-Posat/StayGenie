Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, although some reviews noted minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with a few complaints about wait times but mainly positive feedback.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport, highly praised by most guests.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Rooms were spacious, comfortable, and nicely decorated, with some comments about rooms being too hot or a bit tight.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with mixed reviews mentioning the gym, pool, and breakfast services, but also complaints about limited options and stock.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Some guests found the price slightly high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on food quality, with some positive comments on specific items but also complaints about limited options, raw food, and service issues.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, praising staff friendliness, comfortable beds, and great location.
