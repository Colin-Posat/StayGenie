HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Convenient LocationExperience comfort and convenience at Kyoto Tokyu Hotel, just 5 minutes from Kyoto Station. Enjoy modern rooms with Tempur comfort pillows, satellite LCD TV, and free WiFi. Relax with a drink from the minibar or unwind in the private bath.Dining Options and Fitness FacilitiesIndulge in diverse culinary experiences at 3 restaurants offering Western, Japanese, and Chinese cuisine. Keep fit at the on-site gym or savor drinks at The Terrace in the serene courtyard.Exceptional Services and Nearby AttractionsBenefit from complimentary access to the fitness center, 24-hour concierge service, and front desk assistance. Explore Nishi Honganji Temple nearby or take a short ride to Gion and Kiyomizu-dera Temple.Book now for a memorable stay at Kyoto Tokyu Hotel!
