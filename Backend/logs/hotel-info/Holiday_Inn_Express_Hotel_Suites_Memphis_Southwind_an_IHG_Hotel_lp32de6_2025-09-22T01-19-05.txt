Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests mentioned overall cleanliness as a positive aspect, although some reviews noted issues with cleanliness in specific areas.
• Service: 7.6/10 - Service was appreciated overall, with positive comments about staff friendliness. However, some guests experienced issues such as unavailability of employees and rude behavior.
• Location: 7.8/10 - The hotel's location was generally praised for its safety and proximity to key areas. Some reviews mentioned being far from town as a minor drawback.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable with clean linens and beds, but a few reviews noted issues such as old or stained pull-out mattresses.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews about amenities provided. While some guests appreciated the offerings, others highlighted missing items like breakfast or issues with the quality of amenities.
• Value for Money: 5.2/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money. While some guests found the pricing reasonable for the location, others felt that the services did not justify the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 5.7/10 - Breakfast and food-related services received mixed feedback, with a common complaint about the quality and availability of breakfast options.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, with good customer service and comfortable amenities outweighing specific drawbacks.
