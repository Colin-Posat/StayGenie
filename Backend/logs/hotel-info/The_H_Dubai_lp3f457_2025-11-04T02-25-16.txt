HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover a luxurious retreat at The H Dubai, where comfort meets sophistication in the vibrant Trade Center area.Indulge in RelaxationUnwind at our full-service spa, offering a variety of treatments including soothing massages and rejuvenating facials, ensuring a rejuvenating escape from the hustle and bustle of Dubai.Gastronomic DelightsSavor international cuisine at Eat &amp; Meat, one of our 12 dining options, or opt for 24-hour room service for a cozy night in. With multiple bars and lounges, including a poolside bar, your choices for refreshment are endless.Convenience and ConnectivityStay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and enjoy our fully-equipped business center. Whether you're here for work or pleasure, our concierge services and complimentary shuttle make exploring Dubai effortless.Elevate your experience and book your stay at The H Dubai today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* Up to 2 children 12 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* A car is not required for transportation to and from this property.
* Cashless payment methods are available for all transactions.
