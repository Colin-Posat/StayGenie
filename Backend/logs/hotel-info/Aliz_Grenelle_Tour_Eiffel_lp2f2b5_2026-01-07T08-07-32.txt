HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with AmenitiesThe hotel Alizé Grenelle, centrally located in Paris, offers modern rooms with a TV, Wi-Fi access, trouser press, and alarm clock. Each room features a private bathroom with a hairdryer for your convenience.Convenient LocationJust a 15-minute walk from the iconic Eiffel Tower and a stone's throw away from Beaugrenelle Shopping Centre, the hotel's location is ideal for both sightseeing and shopping enthusiasts.Exceptional ServicesEnjoy a buffet breakfast in the dining room or have it served to your room. With a 24-hour reception, lift access to all rooms, and the Charles Michels Metro Station nearby, Alizé Grenelle ensures a comfortable and convenient stay in Paris.Book now to experience the perfect blend of modern comfort and convenience at Alizé Grenelle Tour Eiffel.
