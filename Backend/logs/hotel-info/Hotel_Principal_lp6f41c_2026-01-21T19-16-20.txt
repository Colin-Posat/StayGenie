HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in the Heart of Mexico CityConvenient Location: Situated just 300 metres from San Juan de Letrán Metro Station, The Principal Hotel offers easy access to key attractions like the National Art Museum and Palacio de Bellas Artes Museum within a 5-minute walk. Constitution Square is also a mere 500 metres away.Culinary Delights: Indulge in a gastronomic experience at Parrilla Leonesa Restaurant, serving a delectable array of Mexican and international dishes along with scrumptious breakfast options. Don't forget to unwind at the hotel's bar or grab a quick snack from the convenient vending machine.Comfortable Stay: Each room is equipped with cable TV, free Wi-Fi, and a private bathroom, ensuring a cozy and relaxing stay for all guests.Experience luxury and convenience at The Principal Hotel - book your stay now!
