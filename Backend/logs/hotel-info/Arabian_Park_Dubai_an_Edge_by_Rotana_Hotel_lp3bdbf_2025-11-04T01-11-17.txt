IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of AED 300 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
All guests staying are required to provide their original passport or UAE identity card.

Arabian Park Dubai has shuttle service to to La Mer Beach & to Dubai Mall

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for AED 46 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for AED 92 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
