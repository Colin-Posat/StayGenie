HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Waterfront Stay with Stunning ViewsState-of-the-Art Amenities: The W Hoboken boasts 50-inch TVs, coffee machines, Bluetooth clock radios, and USB ports in every room. Indulge in bathrobes and designer amenities, with select rooms offering breathtaking views of the Manhattan skyline and Hudson River.Relax and Rejuvenate: Unwind at the full-service spa or stay active at the fitness center. Need to catch up on work? The hotel offers a full-service business center. For a cozy evening, head to the Living Room for board games, books, snacks, and drinks.Culinary Delights: Enjoy delicious regional cuisine at Halifax, the on-site restaurant open daily for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.Experience luxury and convenience at the Hoboken W - Book your stay now!
