HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationGuests at ANA Crowne Plaza Hotel Kyoto can expect a comfortable stay in rooms equipped with air-conditioning, heating, free internet, a minibar, and a flat-screen TV. The en suite bathrooms feature a relaxing bathtub for a soothing experience.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a variety of cuisines at the hotel's 5 restaurants, including authentic French cuisine at The Rose Room, Japanese teppanyaki at NIJO, and Chinese dishes at Karin restaurant. Enjoy refreshing beverages in the lobby bar overlooking a traditional Japanese garden.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind in style with facilities such as an indoor pool, sauna rooms, and a lounge for refreshments. Additionally, laundry and dry cleaning services are available for convenience.Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and comfort at ANA Crowne Plaza Hotel Kyoto.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Shuttle bus operates from 8:00 to 19:45, every 15 minutes.
Please note, this schedule is subject to change. Please ask at the hotel for more detailed information.

The hotel's pool and sauna facilities are accessible at a charge. Please contact the hotel directly for more information.

Guests with a tattoo may not be permitted to use the property’s pools or other facilities where the tattoo might be visible to other guests.

Please note that parking is free until 13:00 on the check-out date. After 13:00 on the day of check-out, charges apply.

Please note that for "Rooms Selected at Check-In" rates, guests may not be able to chose smoking or non-smoking preferences due to availability.

<Children and Extra Beds>
- For children aged 0 to 5 years, breakfast is free. Children who are 6-12 years old and sleeping on an existing bed may stay without charge. Please note that breakfast is not included in this case but can be added for an additional fee.
- Please include all children who require an extra bed in the total number of adults when making a reservation.
- Please contact the property directly to use an extra bed or baby bed for an additional fee. Please note that some requests may not be accommodated depending on availability.
