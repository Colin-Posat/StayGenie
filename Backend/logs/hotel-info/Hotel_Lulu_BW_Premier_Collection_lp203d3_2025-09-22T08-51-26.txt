• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with some guests suggesting more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience despite some challenges, and would consider staying again due to the convenient location and friendly staff.
