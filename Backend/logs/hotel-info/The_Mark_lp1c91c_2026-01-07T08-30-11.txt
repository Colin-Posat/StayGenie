HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the charm and luxury of The Mark, your oasis in the heart of New York City.Indulgent Amenities: Treat yourself with soothing onsite massages or energize your day at our 24-hour fitness center. With complimentary high-speed internet and concierge services, we ensure your stay is both relaxing and seamless.Exceptional Dining: Delight your palate at The Mark Restaurant or enjoy the unique ambiance of our two bars. Room service offers convenience for those who prefer dining in, with a delicious continental breakfast available daily.Elegant Accommodations: Sink into comfort in one of our 152 individually designed rooms, featuring premium bedding, iPod docking stations, and luxurious bathrooms with designer toiletries and deep soaking tubs.Elevate your New York experience – book your stay at The Mark today!
