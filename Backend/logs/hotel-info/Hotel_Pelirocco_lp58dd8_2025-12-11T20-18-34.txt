Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel.

• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were comfortable and themed, but a few guests noted the need for updates in decor and amenities.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities provided, with positive mentions of breakfast and quirky decor, but some guests desired more facilities like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some guests feeling it was reasonable for the location while others found it high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and bar offerings received positive feedback, with some suggestions for improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
