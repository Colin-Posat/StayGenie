Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Some guests praised the cleanliness, while others mentioned issues with cleanliness such as dirty floors and unclean pool.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Mixed reviews on service with instances of accommodating and friendly front desk staff, but also complaints about a front desk mistake and inadequate housekeeping.
• Location: 8.3/10 - Most guests appreciated the great location with proximity to dining options and attractions.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Spacious and comfortable rooms were a common positive point, although some guests noted outdated and unclean room issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities mentioned, with highlights on breakfast, pool, and comfort, but also complaints about lack of hot water and missed room details.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed reactions on value for money with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided and outdated rooms.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast was a highlight for many guests, while others mentioned the need for more variety and cleanliness in the restaurant.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Positive overall experiences were common among guests, with praise for cleanliness, breakfast, location, and friendly staff.
