PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 76.50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities provided, with some guests liking the gym and pool while others wished for more comprehensive spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.6/10 - Opinions on pricing varied, with some guests feeling it was justified by the location while others thought it was slightly high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback from most guests, with some suggesting more variety would enhance the experience.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
