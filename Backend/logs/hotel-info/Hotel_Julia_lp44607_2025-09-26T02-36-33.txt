HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Trevi FountainHotel Julia, nestled in a historic 19th-century building just steps away from the iconic Trevi Fountain, offers a peaceful oasis in the heart of Rome.Charming Rooms with Unique DesignsIndulge in the comfort of our uniquely designed rooms, each equipped with modern amenities including free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and air conditioning. Some rooms even feature a convenient fridge for your snacks and beverages.Explore Rome with EaseImmerse yourself in the bustling neighborhood filled with shops and eateries, and easily navigate the city with free city maps available at our reception.
