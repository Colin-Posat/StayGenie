HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsYurbban Passage Hotel, located in the heart of Barcelona, offers elegantly designed rooms with modern amenities, including flat-screen TVs and private bathrooms. Some rooms even have a cozy seating area and a charming terrace or balcony for a relaxing stay.Relaxation and WellnessIndulge in ultimate relaxation at the hotel's outdoor pool and spa center, where guests can unwind and rejuvenate. Pamper yourself with complimentary bathrobes and slippers provided in each room, ensuring a luxurious and comfortable experience.Convenient ServicesWith free WiFi throughout the property and a 24-hour front desk, Yurbban Passage Hotel prioritizes convenience for its guests. Start your day right with an authentic Catalan breakfast and explore the city with ease using the hotel's bike hire service.Book now for a memorable stay at Yurbban Passage Hotel in Barcelona!
