Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Most guests commented on the overall cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned maintenance issues in the rooms.
• Service: 7.1/10 - Service received mixed reviews with some praising the staff friendliness and efficiency while others had complaints about responsiveness.
• Location: 8.1/10 - Guests appreciated the central location of the hotel, making it convenient for accessing various amenities and transport options.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable but with mixed feedback on outdated decor and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - While some guests highlighted the gym and pool, others mentioned the lack of spa services and limited amenities.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some finding the price reasonable for the location while others felt it was high for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, but some guests desired more variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
