HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Seafront Retreat with Stunning ViewsUnique Décor and Modern Amenities: The Central Palace Bosphorus offers luxurious rooms with elegant wallpaper, parquet floors, and nostalgic wrought iron beds. Enjoy soundproofed rooms with comfortable mattresses for a peaceful night's sleep. Some rooms even feature a spa bath and breathtaking views of the Bosphorus.Gourmet Dining and Scenic Terrace: Indulge in Turkish and international cuisines at Bosphorus Cafe Restaurant with an à la carte menu. Start your day with a delicious open buffet breakfast. Relax on the terrace or in the beautiful garden surrounded by colorful flowers.Convenient Location and Exceptional Service: Located in Tarabya district, the hotel is close to fish restaurants and just a drive away from Istanbul's airports. Benefit from 24-hour room service and free WiFi during your stay at The Central Palace Bosphorus.Experience luxury and comfort at The Central Palace Bosphorus - Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
