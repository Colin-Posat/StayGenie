HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience a blend of comfort and sophistication at Kent Hotel Istanbul, where every detail is designed for your relaxation.Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in our 94 elegantly designed guestrooms, featuring LCD televisions, minibars, and designer toiletries. Enjoy seamless connectivity with complimentary wireless internet, ensuring you stay entertained and connected throughout your stay.Culinary DelightsSavor delectable dishes at our renowned restaurant, CALCEDOINE, or unwind with 24-hour room service. Whether it’s a quick coffee at the café or a refreshing drink at the bar/lounge, we’ve got your culinary cravings covered.Convenient AmenitiesWith a business center, meeting facilities, and express check-in/check-out, we cater to both leisure and business travelers.
