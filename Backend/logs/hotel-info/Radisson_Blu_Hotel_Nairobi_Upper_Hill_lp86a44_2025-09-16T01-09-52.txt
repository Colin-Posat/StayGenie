HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.7/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel, with multiple reviews mentioning clean rooms and facilities.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally well-received by guests, with compliments for friendly and helpful staff, although a few reviews mentioned some issues with billing consistency.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was highlighted positively, being conveniently close to key landmarks and transport options.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and modern, although some reviews cited minor issues like small wardrobes.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - Guests found the facilities satisfactory, with some positive mentions of the pool and gym, but a few complaints about lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others considered it reasonable for the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Positive feedback for the breakfast and restaurant services, though some guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and indicated a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
