HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central LocationHotel Arco di Travertino is ideally located just a short walk from Rome's Metro A, offering easy access to iconic landmarks such as the Spanish Steps and the Vatican. With convenient bus links to the historic center, exploring the city is a breeze.Comfortable AccommodationExperience a stay in a historic villa where each room is equipped with modern amenities including free Wi-Fi, a minibar, and air conditioning. Relax and unwind in the comfort of your room after a day of sightseeing.Convenient ServicesStart your day right with a delicious buffet breakfast served daily. Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi available in the hotel's lounge, perfect for planning your itinerary or catching up on emails.Book your stay at Hotel Arco di Travertino for a memorable and convenient Roman getaway.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
