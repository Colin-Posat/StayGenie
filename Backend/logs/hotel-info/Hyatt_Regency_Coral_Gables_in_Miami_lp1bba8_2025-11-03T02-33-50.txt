HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the charm of Coral Gables at our hotel inspired by the Alhambra Castle. Enjoy upscale amenities like a 55-inch flat-screen TV, iPod docking stations, and a minibar in every room. Relax on your private patio or balcony after a day of exploring the nearby Miracle Mile and Giralda Plaza.Gourmet Dining OptionsSavor delicious meals at Two Sisters Restaurant or sip on handcrafted cocktails at Bar 50. For a more casual experience, lounge by the pool with a refreshing drink. Indulge in our room service for a cozy night in at the Hyatt Regency Coral Gables.Convenient LocationLocated just a short distance from downtown Miami, our hotel offers easy access to the University of Miami and Miami International Airport. With over 100 restaurants and shops within walking distance, your stay with us promises convenience and luxury.Book your stay at the Hyatt Regency Coral Gables now for an unforgettable experience in Florida.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), wearing a face mask is mandatory in all indoor common areas.
Please note rooms booked with "Breakfast Included Rate" only include breakfast for 2 adults. Additional fees apply to additional guests including children. 

Please note only dogs can be accommodated upon request for a surcharge. Contact property for more details. 

Please note that smoking at the property is not allowed. Smoking at the pool or balconies will result in USD 250 fine. The designated smoking area is located by the front drive.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Friendly staff
• Spacious rooms
• Excellent service

Areas for Improvement:
• Expensive buffet breakfast
• Lack of amenities like free parking and spa services
• Maintenance issues in rooms and common areas

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests mentioned the cleanliness of the rooms as a positive aspect, but some cleanliness issues like dirty whirlpool area were noted.
• Service: 8.5/10 - The staff's friendliness and assistance were highly appreciated, although some guests encountered issues with responsiveness and service speed.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its proximity to restaurants, bars, and landmarks, providing a convenient and enjoyable stay.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally described as spacious, comfortable, and well-equipped, with minor complaints about old furniture and maintenance needed in some areas.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - While some guests appreciated certain amenities like the pool and room credits, others felt that additional offerings like food variety and free parking were lacking.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Mixed reviews on the perceived value for money, with some guests feeling the services did not justify the cost, especially with additional charges like parking fees.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Overall positive feedback on the breakfast and dining services, though complaints about buffet prices and limited food options were highlighted.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience despite some concerns, with particular mentions of the wonderful location and friendly service.
