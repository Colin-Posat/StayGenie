HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in the Heart of UbudUnwind in Comfort and StyleGriya Sriwedari offers air-conditioned units with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, a safety deposit box, and a private bathroom. Relax on the balcony with quiet street views or cozy up by the outdoor fireplace.Explore and IndulgeGuests can enjoy mountain and garden views, perfect for a tranquil retreat. Take advantage of the picnic area, coffee shop, and the option for bike or car hire to explore the popular skiing and cycling spots nearby.Convenience and Serenity CombinedWith free WiFi, a terrace, and soundproof rooms, Griya Sriwedari ensures a peaceful stay. Don't miss the chance to experience the beauty of Ubud while staying in comfort at our guest house.
