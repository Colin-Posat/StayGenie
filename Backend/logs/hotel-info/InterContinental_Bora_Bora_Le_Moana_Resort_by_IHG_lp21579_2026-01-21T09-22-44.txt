HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover paradise at InterContinental Bora Bora Le Moana Resort, where luxury meets the serenity of nature.Wellness Oasis Indulge in our full-service spa, offering rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials. After pampering yourself, soak up the sun on our pristine private beach or dive into the sparkling outdoor pool.Culinary Delights Savor international cuisine at Noa Noa, our beachfront restaurant featuring an inviting bar/lounge and picturesque poolside views. Enjoy alfresco dining or room service during limited hours, ensuring a unique culinary experience.Comfort and Convenience Each of our 64 elegant guestrooms includes private balconies or patios, flat-screen televisions, and complimentary high-speed internet. With a 24-hour front desk and tailored concierge services, your every need is catered to.Escape to blissful tranquility—book your unforgettable stay today!
