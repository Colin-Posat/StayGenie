HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Square, a 5-star boutique hotel in Paris, offers elegant rooms and suites with modern amenities, including air-conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and free Wi-Fi. The marble bathrooms with bathtub and shower add a touch of luxury to your stay.Relaxation and EntertainmentGuests can unwind in the hotel's two lounge areas, featuring plasma-screen TVs with cable channels. For those looking to host private events, the lounges are available upon request. Additionally, the 200m² Spa Nuxe provides various well-being treatments for ultimate relaxation.Convenient LocationSituated opposite the Eiffel Tower, Hotel Square is within walking distance of Beaugrenelle Shopping Centre and Rue de Passy. With nearby metro and RER stations, exploring Paris is easy. Don't miss the chance to book your stay at this exquisite hotel.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Adults  stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
