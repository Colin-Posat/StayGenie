HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Infinity Pool with Greenery ViewsExperience serenity at B Saya Villas in Ubud, Bali, where an infinity pool overlooks lush greenery. Indulge in a meal at the on-site restaurant and enjoy complimentary WiFi and parking during your stay.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsEach air-conditioned room features a terrace or balcony, with some units offering a dining area and refrigerator. Rest easy in a private bathroom stocked with plush towels and complimentary toiletries.Convenient Services and ActivitiesFrom arranging laundry service to providing tickets for local attractions, our staff ensures a seamless stay. Take advantage of airport transfers, a free shuttle to Ubud Centre, and daily morning yoga sessions at the villas.Book now and immerse yourself in the tranquility of B Saya Villas.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for Rp 350,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
