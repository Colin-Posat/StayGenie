CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 17 and older are allowed.
Children 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children 17 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Adults  stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
