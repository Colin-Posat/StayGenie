HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationSamsara Ubud, located just 7 km from Tegallalang Rice Terrace, offers a sustainable 5-star experience. The property features an infinity pool, fitness centre, and private parking, ensuring a lavish stay for guests.Wellness RetreatIndulge in ultimate relaxation with spa facilities, wellness packages, and yoga classes available at the villa complex. Guests can unwind in the serene garden or explore the area through cycling with complimentary bicycle use provided.Modern AmenitiesEach air-conditioned unit is thoughtfully equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower. Some units even feature a fully equipped kitchen for added convenience.Book now for a luxurious and rejuvenating stay at Samsara Ubud!
