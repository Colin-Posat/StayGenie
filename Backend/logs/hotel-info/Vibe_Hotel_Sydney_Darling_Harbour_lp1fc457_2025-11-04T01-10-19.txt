Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Overall, guests appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms. However, some reviews mentioned issues with dust and cleanliness in certain areas.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally good, with many guests praising the friendly staff and helpful service. However, a few guests had complaints about noise levels and delayed responses.
• Location: 9.1/10 - The hotel's location received high praise from guests, with many highlighting its convenience and proximity to key attractions and transportation options.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests found the rooms comfortable and well-appointed. Some complaints mentioned small room sizes and issues with soundproofing.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided met expectations, with some guests enjoying the facilities such as the cafe and rooftop bar. However, there were complaints about limited room service options and noise disturbances.
• Value for Money: 7.6/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the accommodation offered great value, while others mentioned concerns about cleanliness and noise levels.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage options were generally well-received, with guests appreciating the breakfast choices and cafe offerings. Some guests suggested improvements in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, citing factors like location, cleanliness, and friendly staff. However, some mentioned issues with noise, cleanliness, and room size.
