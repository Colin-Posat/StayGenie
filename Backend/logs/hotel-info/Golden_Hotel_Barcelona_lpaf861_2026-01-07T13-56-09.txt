HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesGolden Hotel Barcelona offers a sustainable stay with a Biosphere certificate. Guests can unwind on the rooftop terrace with a refreshing outdoor swimming pool, jacuzzi, and bar. The property also features a gym, a restaurant, and a shared lounge for relaxation.Convenient LocationLocated just 1.4 km from Sagrada Familia and 1.5 km from Port Olympic, Golden Hotel Barcelona provides easy access to Barcelona's top attractions. Explore the city with ease, as Sagrada Familia and Picasso Museum are within a short distance from the hotel.Special Offers and ExtrasEnjoy a complimentary spa entry at Hotel &amp; Spa Villa Olimpic@ Suites complex with each stay. Start your day right with a delicious buffet breakfast served every morning. The multilingual staff ensures a seamless experience for guests throughout their stay.Experience luxury and convenience at Golden Hotel Barcelona. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Barcelona.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
