HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Tokyo DomeHotel Wing International, located just a 5-minute walk from Korakuen Subway Station, offers modern rooms with free internet and flat-screen TVs. Enjoy the convenience of being only 300 meters from Tokyo Dome and a short taxi ride to the Imperial Palace and Yasukuni Shrine.Comfortable Rooms with AmenitiesRelax in the modern rooms featuring full private bathrooms with bathtubs, a fridge, and a writing desk. Start your day with a hot cup of tea in the comfort of your room.Additional Services and FacilitiesIndulge in Shiatsu massage and acupressure services offered at the hotel. Benefit from coin-operated laundry machines, free internet terminals, and a daily Continental breakfast served in the lobby.Book your stay at Hotel Wing International for a comfortable and convenient experience.
