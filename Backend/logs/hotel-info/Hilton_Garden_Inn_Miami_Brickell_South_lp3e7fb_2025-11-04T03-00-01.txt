HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at the Hilton Garden Inn Miami Brickell South.Exceptional AccommodationsRelax in one of our 129 well-appointed guestrooms, featuring modern amenities such as refrigerators and plasma televisions. Enjoy free wired and wireless internet, iPod docking stations, and comfortable private bathrooms with complimentary toiletries.Recreational PursuitsTake a refreshing dip in our outdoor pool or maintain your workout regimen in our 24-hour fitness center. For those traveling with family, babysitting services are available for a surcharge, allowing you to unwind fully.Dining and Entertainment OptionsSavor delicious meals at our on-site restaurant or order in with room service, followed by delightful drinks at our bar/lounge or poolside bar. Start your day right with our buffet breakfast served on weekends until 11 AM.Unlock the door to your Miami getaway and book your stay today!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.5/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness. Some guests found the cleanliness to be acceptable, while others mentioned cleanliness as an issue.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Service received positive feedback, with guests appreciating the friendly and helpful staff. However, some guests mentioned issues with service quality.
• Location: 8.3/10 - Location was a highlight, with many guests praising the convenient proximity to key landmarks, public transport, and shopping areas.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and equipped with amenities like a Keurig machine, microwave, and mini-fridge. However, some guests reported minor issues like broken coffee machines and room discrepancies.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities were satisfactory for most guests, with positive mentions of the breakfast buffet and poolside bar service. Some guests noted the lack of certain amenities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money. Some guests felt the price was reasonable, while others considered it high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed reviews, with positive mentions of breakfast buffets but also criticisms of limited variety and high prices.
• Overall Experience: 6.8/10 - Overall, guests had a mixed experience. While some had positive stays and praised the staff and service, others had negative experiences with cleanliness and amenities.
