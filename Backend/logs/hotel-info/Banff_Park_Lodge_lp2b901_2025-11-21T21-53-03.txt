HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 16 years old to 17 years old stay for CAD 25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for CAD 10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  CAD 14 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with positive comments about the gym and pool, but some guests desired additional spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some guests feeling it was slightly high for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, although there were suggestions for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and indicated they would recommend the hotel.
