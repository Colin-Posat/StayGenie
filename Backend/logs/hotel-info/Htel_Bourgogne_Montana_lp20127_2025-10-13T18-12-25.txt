HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Hôtel Bourgogne &amp; Montana, a former 18th-century family mansion, offers luxury rooms and suites with free Wi-Fi access. Each room features an LCD TV with satellite channels, free bathrobes, and slippers, with some boasting elegant period-style furniture.Convenient LocationSituated on a quiet square opposite the National Assembly, the hotel is just a short stroll away from iconic landmarks such as Place de la Concorde, Tuileries Gardens, and the Eiffel Tower. The Invalides RER Station nearby provides easy access to popular attractions like Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Louvre Museum.Relaxation and DiningGuests can unwind in the sauna, which can be privatized for a relaxing 45-minute session. Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast in the bright breakfast room and enjoy a selection of vintage wines at Montana’s bar in the hotel’s elegant lounges.Book your stay at Hôtel Bourgogne &amp; Montana for an unforgettable Parisian experience.
