HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Modern AmenitiesLocated just a minute walk from Shiomi Train Station, APA Hotel &amp; Resort &lt; Tokyo Bay Shiomi &gt; offers western-style rooms with free wired internet and WiFi access. Each room features an original mattress for optimal comfort, a flat-screen TV, and a refrigerator. Guests can also enjoy the spacious public bath free of charge, along with well-equipped bathrooms and convenient amenities.Convenience and ServicesThe hotel provides photocopying/fax services and massage service upon request, ensuring a stress-free stay for guests. Additionally, a convenience store on the 1st floor caters to any immediate needs, offering added convenience during your stay.Dining ExperienceIndulge in a delightful buffet at the La Veranda restaurant, where breakfast is served to kick start your day with a delicious meal.Book your stay at APA Hotel &amp; Resort &lt; Tokyo Bay Shiomi &gt; for a comfortable and convenient experience.
