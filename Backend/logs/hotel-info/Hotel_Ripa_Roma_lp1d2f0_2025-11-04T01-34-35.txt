HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in TrastevereHotel Ripa Roma is a modern hotel situated in the sought-after area of Trastevere, just 50 metres from a tram stop offering easy access to Rome's historical center.Comfortable AccommodationThe spacious and air-conditioned rooms and suites at Hotel Ripa Roma are well-equipped with amenities such as satellite TV, minibar, and en suite bathrooms with complimentary toiletries. Plus, most rooms feature a charming balcony.Convenience and DiningEnjoy the convenience of free WiFi throughout the hotel and a complimentary gym for guests. Indulge in a delicious breakfast served in your room and savor Roman specialties and international cuisine at the on-site Ripa Place restaurant.Ready to experience the charm of Trastevere at Hotel Ripa Roma? CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 10 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
