HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Riverside Charm and City ConvenienceNestled in a serene street by the picturesque Amstel River, Hotel de Munck offers a tranquil escape just moments away from bustling city hotspots. Private Retreats for All - Each guest room features a private bathroom, ensuring a comfortable and exclusive stay for all visitors. Families can also opt for specially designed rooms to suit their needs perfectly.Accessible Attractions - A mere 3-minute stroll to tram stop Oosteinde provides easy access to the vibrant Leidse Square, while the nearby Utrechtsestraat beckons with its array of enticing shops and eateries. Blooming Oasis - During summer, the garden blooms with a riot of colorful flowers, adding a touch of natural beauty to your urban retreat.Indulge in Riverside Tranquility and Urban Excitement at Hotel de Munck. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hotel de Munck in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that this hotel only has steep stairs and no lift.

Please note that for non refundable bookings, guests are required to show the credit card used to make the reservation. If this is not possible, guests can pay upon arrival or by bank transfer.
