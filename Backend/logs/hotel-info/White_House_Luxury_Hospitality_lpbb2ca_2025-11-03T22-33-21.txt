HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 25 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for € 50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 80 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for € 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Guests mentioned varying levels of cleanliness in their reviews, some positive and some negative.

• Location: 5.4/10 - Location received mixed reviews, with some guests finding it convenient while others were disappointed by the lack of nearby attractions.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Guests generally found the rooms spacious and comfortable, despite some maintenance issues reported.
• Amenities: 5.8/10 - Amenities were deemed satisfactory by some guests, while others highlighted restricted services like food delivery and additional charges for pool towels.
• Value for Money: 4.5/10 - Several guests expressed dissatisfaction with the value for money, citing high prices for services and additional unexpected charges.
• Food and Beverage: 3.2/10 - Breakfast received extensive negative feedback for its limited selection, additional charges for basic items like eggs and juice, and overall inadequate quality for the price point.
• Overall Experience: 3.7/10 - Overall experience was disappointing for many guests due to communication challenges, unexpected charges, inadequate amenities, and unsatisfactory value for money.
