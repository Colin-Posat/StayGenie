HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern AmenitiesReverb by Hard Rock Atlanta Downtown boasts a range of modern amenities, including a fitness centre, shared lounge, and a terrace for guests to enjoy during their stay. The hotel's on-site restaurant offers a delightful dining experience for visitors, while the 24-hour front desk and ATM ensure convenience and accessibility for all guests.City Views and ComfortGuests can relax in rooms equipped with air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a safety deposit box for their valuables. Some rooms even provide stunning city views, adding a touch of luxury to your stay at this 4-star hotel.Prime LocationConveniently situated just 1.5 km from the city centre and 600 metres from Mercedes-Benz Stadium, Reverb by Hard Rock Atlanta Downtown is surrounded by popular attractions such as State Farm Arena and Georgia World Congress Center. Book your stay now and experience the vibrant heart of Atlanta!

• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were mostly comfortable and well-equipped, though some reported maintenance issues and lack of amenities like water boilers/kettles.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with positive mentions of the ice and sparkling water but complaints about broken coffee makers, phones, and Wi-Fi issues.
• Value for Money: 5.6/10 - Wide range of opinions on pricing, with some guests feeling it was too expensive for the services provided and additional charges like parking fees, while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, with some guests wanting more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would consider returning, despite a few negative aspects.
