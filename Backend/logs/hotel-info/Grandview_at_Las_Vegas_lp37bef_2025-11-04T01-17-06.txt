Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Service was generally appreciated with friendly staff, although there were some complaints about check-in times and room cleanliness.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to key landmarks and public transport, ideal for both leisure and business travelers.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were spacious and well-appointed, but some guests reported issues with bed comfort and cleanliness.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with a range of facilities including pools and mini-golf, although some areas like spa services were lacking.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests finding it a bit high for the service quality, while others considered it reasonable considering the offerings.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining services were generally well-received, although some guests expressed a desire for more variety and on-site dining options.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - While most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, there were some issues reported related to room cleanliness and service quality.
