HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationCanalta Lodge, just a short drive from Banff city centre, offers a cozy retreat with easy access to local attractions like Snowy Owl Sled Dog Tours and Mount Norquay Ski Resort.Comfortable AccommodationEach air-conditioned room at Canalta Lodge features a seating area, cable HDTV, and a private bathroom with modern amenities. Guests can also enjoy complimentary WiFi and ironing facilities during their stay.Exceptional AmenitiesStart your day with a delicious coffee and breakfast at the Happy Camper coffee barista. Take advantage of ski storage, fitness facilities, and the convenience of paid underground parking. Plus, unwind at the nearby Red Earth Spa for the ultimate relaxation.Book your stay at Canalta Lodge for a memorable and convenient experience in Banff!
