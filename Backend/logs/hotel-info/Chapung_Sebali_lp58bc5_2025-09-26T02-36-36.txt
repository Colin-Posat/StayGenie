HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Villas with Private PoolsChapung Sebali offers spacious villas with private pools, perfect for a relaxing and indulgent stay. Enjoy the ultimate privacy and comfort in these beautifully designed accommodations.Exciting Activities and AmenitiesFrom yoga classes to rice field trekking, horse-riding, and cycling, there is no shortage of activities to keep you entertained. Unwind at the famous Jungle Fish pool bar or take a refreshing dip in the infinity pool overlooking the valley.Convenient Services for a Seamless StayStay connected with free WiFi, explore the area with bicycle rentals and shuttle services, and indulge in a massage or arrange day trips at the tour desk. With babysitting services available, both parents and children can make the most of their time at Chapung Sebali.Experience luxury and relaxation at Chapung Sebali - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for Rp 450,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 900,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for Rp 450,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for Rp 900,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were described as spacious, modern, and comfortable, with a few mentions of minor issues.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities such as the pool, spa, and restaurants, though some felt there were missing facilities like a treadmill in the gym.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling it was overpriced and others finding it justified for the experience.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - The food and beverage offerings were generally well-received, with compliments on taste and variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had an exceptional overall experience, praising the hotel's ambiance, service, and amenities.
