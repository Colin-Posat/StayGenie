HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesSituated in downtown Miami, the Hampton Inn is just steps away from the Brickell Metromover Station, making exploring the city a breeze. Enjoy a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool and stay connected with complimentary WiFi.Comfortable Rooms and ServicesRelax in rooms equipped with modern conveniences like a flat-screen TV, coffee facilities, and a work desk. Benefit from the fitness and business centers, as well as the helpful concierge desk for arranging activities.Dining and EntertainmentStart your day right with a delicious hot breakfast and unwind in the evening with a drink from the Lobby Bar. Don't forget to visit the on-site convenience store and enjoy complimentary coffee throughout the day.Book your stay at Hampton Inn Miami now for a memorable experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  USD 39 per  day.
