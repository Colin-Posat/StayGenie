Modern Amenities and ComfortExperience comfort and convenience at Hilton Garden Inn Sacramento Airport Natomas. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect in their reviews.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests. Some mentioned specific staff members who provided excellent service.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location was praised for its proximity to key landmarks and public transport, though some reviews mentioned it was a little out of the way.
• Room Quality: 7/10 - Guests found rooms spacious and comfortable, but there were complaints about maintenance issues and room type allocations.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Guests were satisfied with the amenities provided, with positive mentions of the gym and pool, but some guests desired additional services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests wished for more variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
