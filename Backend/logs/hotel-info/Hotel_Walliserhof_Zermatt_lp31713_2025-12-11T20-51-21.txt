HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Valais Chalet StyleDiscover the allure of Hotel Walliserhof Zermatt, centrally located just 300 metres from the train station. Embrace the typical Valais chalet architecture while enjoying modern amenities such as a spa area with sauna and hot tub.Comfortable Rooms and Delectable DiningIndulge in comfort in our well-equipped rooms featuring a TV, desk, and luxurious bathroom amenities. Start your day with a delicious breakfast and savor regional and international cuisine for lunch and dinner at our restaurant with a terrace overlooking Bahnhofstrasse.Proximity to AttractionsConveniently situated, our hotel is a short distance from Matterhorn Glacier Paradise Station and the Zermatt-Furi Ski Lift, making it an ideal choice for adventurers and leisure travelers alike.Book now to experience the perfect blend of tradition and luxury at Hotel Walliserhof Zermatt.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform the hotel if you are traveling with children and include their number and age.

Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.

Please note this is a cash-free property. All payment is by card.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for CHF 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 16 years old and over stay for CHF 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 8.84/10 - Good value for the quality advertised, especially given the positive comments on the restaurant.
• Food and Beverage: 8.89/10 - Breakfast received great reviews, while dinner was praised for its quality.
• Overall Experience: 9.36/10 - Overall experiences shared were highly positive, with many expressing interest in returning.
