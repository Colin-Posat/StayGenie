Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, but a few mentioned minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service received high praise from guests for being helpful and accommodating.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The convenient location near key attractions and public transport was a highlight for many guests.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, although some guests noted the lack of certain amenities.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities with some guests appreciating the pool and gym, while others desired more services such as spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Opinions varied on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high compared to the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, although some guests desired more variety and better pricing.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would consider staying again, citing the staff and location as highlights.
