HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Impressive Views and Modern DesignExperience the contemporary Hotel Barcelona Condal Mar, managed by Meliá, housed in a unique circular building just a short stroll from Mar Bella Beach. Enjoy spacious soundproofed rooms with top amenities like air conditioning, satellite TV, and free Wi-Fi. Take in breathtaking views of Barcelona from the rooftop pool and stay active at the 24-hour gym.Gastronomic Delights and RelaxationIndulge in a variety of cocktails at the lobby bar, lounge in style at the snack lounge, and sip on refreshing drinks at the poolside bar. Start your day right with a buffet breakfast in the restaurant. For a truly relaxing stay, take advantage of the special late check-out service and locker facilities.Convenient Location and Easy AccessWith proximity to Maresme Forum Metro Station and bus stops, exploring Barcelona is a breeze. Whether you prefer Metro, Bus, or Tram, the city center is just a short ride away. Book your stay at Hotel Barcelona Condal Mar now for a memorable Barcelona experience.
