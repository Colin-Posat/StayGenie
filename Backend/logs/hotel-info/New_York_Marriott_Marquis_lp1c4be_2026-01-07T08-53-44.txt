HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the epitome of comfort at New York Marriott Marquis with rooms featuring Marriott’s signature bedding, marble bathrooms, and high-tech amenities like flat-screen TVs with Netflix app and iPod clock radios.Unmatched ConvenienceIndulge in the convenience of onsite dining options, complimentary WiFi, and a range of amenities including a concierge lounge, cash machine, and onsite shops. The hotel's proximity to iconic landmarks like Radio City Music Hall and Rockefeller Center ensures you're at the heart of all the action.Spectacular Views and Event SpacesElevate your stay with panoramic views of Times Square from the lounge, or host a grand event in the expansive Broadway Ballroom.
