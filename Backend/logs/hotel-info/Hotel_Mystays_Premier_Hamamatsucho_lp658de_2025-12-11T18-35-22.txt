HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Mystays Premier Hamamatsucho offers spacious and comfortable guest rooms with modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs, air purifiers, and memory foam pillows. The soothing color schemes create a relaxing atmosphere for guests to unwind after a busy day exploring the city.Convenient LocationLocated just a short walk from JR Hamamatsucho Station and with easy access to Haneda Airport, our hotel is the perfect choice for travelers seeking convenience. Additionally, the nearby Daimon Subway Station provides quick transportation to popular destinations like Roppongi and Tokyo Tower.Exceptional ServicesEnjoy the convenience of a 24-hour front desk, free luggage storage, and in-room massage services. Indulge in a delicious breakfast at the on-site Japanese-style restaurant &quot;Morimoto&quot; before starting your day of adventure in Tokyo.Book your stay at Hotel Mystays Premier Hamamatsucho now for a memorable and comfortable experience in the heart of the city.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note, the credit card that was used to guarantee the reservation will be charged with applicable cancelation charges in the event of cancelation after the cancelation deadline.

On-site multi-story car parking accommodates cars up to 61 in high. Please note that if you need parking for a large car, you may be directed to a paid public parking nearby.

For guests booking a meal inclusive rate, a breakfast buffet is served between 07:00-10:00. (Their last order is 9:30).

Alipay is accepted at the property.

Guests staying in the Comfort King room can rent an exercise bike at an additional charge per day, if a request is made directly to the property in advance. Please note requests are subject to availability as the number of bicycles are limited.

Please note, guest room temperatures are centrally controlled. Guests cannot switch between air conditioning and heating.
An accommodation tax per person, per night is not included in the price and must be paid at the property.
