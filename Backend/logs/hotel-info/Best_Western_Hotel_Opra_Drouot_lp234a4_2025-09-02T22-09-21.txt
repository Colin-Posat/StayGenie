HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ParisLocated in the stylish 9th district of Paris, Hotel Opera Drouot offers a convenient stay just a short stroll away from the renowned Galeries Lafayette department store. With a prime location in central Paris, guests can easily explore the city's attractions and shopping destinations.Comfortable and Convenient AmenitiesThe air-conditioned guest rooms at Hotel Opera Drouot are designed for comfort and convenience. Enjoy soundproofed rooms with free internet access, satellite TV, and private bathrooms with a hairdryer. The presence of a lift ensures easy access to all rooms, making your stay hassle-free.Delightful Breakfast and Nearby AttractionsStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast served in a charming breakfast room under an arched ceiling. Additionally, the hotel's proximity to Opéra Garnier and the Grands Boulevards Metro Station, just 270 meters away, offers easy access to explore iconic Parisian landmarks like the Champs Elysées.Book your stay at Hotel Opera Drouot for a memorable Parisian experience.
