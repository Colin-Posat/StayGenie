HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in Saint Louis DistrictLogis La Residence Du Berry offers a luxurious stay in the heart of the Saint Louis district, just steps away from the iconic Chateau de Versailles. Each guestroom, accessible by a lift, features modern amenities including a flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi.Entertainment and RelaxationGuests can unwind with a game of billiards, enjoy a refreshing drink at the bar, or surf the internet in comfort. The hotel provides a perfect blend of entertainment and relaxation for all guests.Convenient Location for Business and LeisureConveniently situated near Versailles Rive Gauche Train Station, Palais des Congrés, and Versailles Chantiers SNCF Station, Logis La Residence Du Berry is ideal for both business and leisure travelers. Don't miss the chance to experience comfort and convenience at our hotel.
