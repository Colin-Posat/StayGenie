HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Venetian Hotel in the Heart of the CityEnchanting Location: Hotel Da Bruno is ideally situated between the iconic Rialto Bridge and St. Mark's Square, offering guests the perfect starting point to explore Venice's treasures.Family-Owned Tradition: With a rich history spanning 3 generations, our dedicated team is committed to providing a warm and welcoming stay, ensuring you have all the information and assistance you need to make the most of your visit.Quaint Venetian Experience: Nestled in a traditional Venetian building on a picturesque narrow street, Hotel Da Bruno immerses you in the authentic Venetian lifestyle. While the lack of a lift adds to the charm, some rooms boast a private terrace for a touch of luxury.Book your stay at Hotel Da Bruno now for an unforgettable Venetian experience!
