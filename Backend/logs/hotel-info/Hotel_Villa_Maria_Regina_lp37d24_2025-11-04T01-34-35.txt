HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tranquil Oasis in Rome's Elegant Residential AreaSurrounded by a private green park, Hotel Villa Maria Regina offers a peaceful stay just outside Rome's historic center. Convenient Location and Amenities The 4-star hotel provides free private parking and is located near the Vatican City and Roma Golf Club. Modern Comforts and Services Recently renovated, the hotel boasts comfortable rooms with modern amenities, a fitness center, and meeting rooms with natural lighting.Experience a serene retreat at Hotel Villa Maria Regina and book your stay now to enjoy luxury and convenience in Rome.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 4 years old to 9 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
