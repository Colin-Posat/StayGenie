HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Elegant RoomsLocated between Piccadilly and Regent Street, The Dilly offers spacious and elegantly furnished rooms with private bathrooms, bathrobes, and slippers.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy free newspapers, a refrigerator, and 24-hour room service. The hotel is a short walk from popular shopping destinations like Oxford Street and Bond Street.Health Club and Dining OptionsThe Health Club features a steam room, state-of-the-art gym, squash courts, and a dance studio. Guests can dine at the Terrace restaurant with an outdoor terrace or savor Indian cuisine at Madhu's restaurant in the Oak Room.Book your stay at The Dilly for a luxurious and convenient experience in the heart of the city.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please note that, for security reason, guests are required to provide at check-in the credit card used at the time of booking.

Third-party forms are not accepted, please contact the property for instructions on how to settle the bill.

Connecting Room Policy: Connecting rooms are available but not guaranteed and need to be requested.

 Dogs are accepted. Food, water bowls and a floor mat will be provided.

If your bring your pet with you, please note that a £35 cleaning fee will be applied.

Cash paying guests must provide valid identification upon check-in (Passport or Driving license for UK citizens, Passport or EU ID for EU citizens and Passport for any other citizens).

Swimming Pool Admittance Policy: Children under 16 years of age are permitted in the swimming pool Monday - Friday between 07:00 - 21:30 and on weekends from 08:00 - 20:00.

Please note that a deposit equal to the room, tax and GBP 100 per day will be collected at check-in.

Cash is not accepted at the hotel

PARKING:

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for £100 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
