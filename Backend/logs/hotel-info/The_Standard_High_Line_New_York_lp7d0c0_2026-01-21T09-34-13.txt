HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsFeaturing a roof terrace with a hot tub, The Standard, High Line New York offers a luxurious stay in the Meatpacking District. Enjoy breathtaking city or river views from rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows. Relax with amenities like a flat-screen TV, overstocked minibar, and a private bathroom with premium toiletries.Exceptional Dining and Entertainment OptionsIndulge in new American cuisine at The Standard Grill or sip cocktails at the rooftop bar, Le Bain, overlooking the city. For a more laid-back experience, visit The Standard Biergarten for beer, sausages, and a game of table tennis.Convenient Amenities and ServicesWith a 24-hour front desk, on-site bars and restaurants, complimentary bike hire, and pet-friendly rooms, The Standard, High Line New York ensures a comfortable and memorable stay for all guests.Book now for an unforgettable experience at The Standard, High Line New York.
