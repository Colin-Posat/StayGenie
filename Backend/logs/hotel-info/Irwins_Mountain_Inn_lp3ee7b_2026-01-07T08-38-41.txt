There must be a name attached to each room.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 2 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 16 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for CAD 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but some guests mentioned maintenance issues and outdated decor.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with some guests mentioning the lack of certain facilities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally positive, though some guests desired more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, with particular praise for friendly staff and great location.
