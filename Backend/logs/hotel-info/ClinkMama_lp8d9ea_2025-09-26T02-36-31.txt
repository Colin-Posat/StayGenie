IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Food & beverage services at this property may be limited or unavailable due to Coronavirus (COVID-19).
Please inform ClinkMama in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Check-in time is from 4pm to 10pm. Check out time is before 11am.

Guests can stay for a maximum of 7 days in a 28-day period.

Please note we do not accept bookings of groups larger than six people. If you decide to make a booking for your group anyway, we retain the right to cancel your booking without a refund.

We are cashless hostels.

Please note that children under 18 years old must be accompanied by an adult. Please note that guests who bring children under 16 years old have to book a whole dorm or a private room. Please note that children of 16 and 17 years old can stay in a shared dorm, but must be accompanied by an adult.

Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 4 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
