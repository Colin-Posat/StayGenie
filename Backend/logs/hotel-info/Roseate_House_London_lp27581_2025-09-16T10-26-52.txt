Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of GBP 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note, if you are a non-UK Visa, Mastercard and American Express cardholder, where available, the amount of stay can be charged in your local currency. The card used to make the reservation must be presented at check-in. The details must match those on the photo ID card presented at check-in. 

Advanced purchase booking or any prepaid reservations must be presented upon check-in for verification against your photo ID such as a Passport or National ID card. The card holder name and photo identification must match. 

Failure to comply with the hotel check-in policies, may result in the reservation cancellation. If the booking is made by a third party, please contact the property directly to make alternative arrangements.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 15 years old stay for £35 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for £40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
