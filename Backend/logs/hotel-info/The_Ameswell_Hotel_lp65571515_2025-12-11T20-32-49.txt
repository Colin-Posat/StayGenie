HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesThe Ameswell Hotel in Mountain View offers a 5-star experience with an outdoor swimming pool, fitness center, garden, and shared lounge. Guests can enjoy free WiFi, a restaurant, and a bar during their stay.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsEach air-conditioned room at The Ameswell Hotel features a desk, kettle, fridge, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a shower. The rooms are elegantly furnished with bed linen and towels for a relaxing stay.Convenient Location and AssistanceSituated just 4 km from Shoreline Amphitheatre, the hotel also provides easy access to San Jose Convention Center and the airport. Guests can rely on the reception for helpful information and guidance during their visit.Experience luxury and convenience at The Ameswell Hotel. Book your stay now!
