HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Boasting a seasonal outdoor pool, Hotel Roma Aurelia Antica features an à la carte restaurant and a sun terrace. Set in a private park, this property provides free on-site parking.Luxurious AmenitiesAll air conditioned, guest rooms of the Roma Aurelia Antica have a minibar, and a TV with satellite and pay-per-view channels. Some rooms also feature a tea/coffee maker.Exquisite Dining ExperienceThe restaurant has a pool-side area plus a romantic terrace, and serves classic Italian cuisine as well as Spanish specialities. Catering for banquets and private functions is also available.State-of-the-Art Sports CentreA complete sports centre is located next to the hotel and includes a gym, swimming pool, sauna, tennis courts, and football pitches.Book your stay at Hotel Roma Aurelia Antica and indulge in luxury and relaxation.
