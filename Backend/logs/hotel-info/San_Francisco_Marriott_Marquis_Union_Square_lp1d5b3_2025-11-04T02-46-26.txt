HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations:Experience luxury at its finest at San Francisco Marriott Marquis, located next to the Moscone Convention Center. The stylish interior of our rooms, equipped with flat-screen TVs, air-conditioning, and city views, ensures a comfortable and upscale stay.Convenient Facilities:Stay on top of your fitness routine at our on-site fitness center. Whether you're in town for business at the convention center or exploring nearby attractions like the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art and Union Square, our hotel offers the perfect location for all your needs.Delicious Dining Options:Start your day right with a visit to B55 Craft House and Kitchen for a delicious breakfast. With convenient facilities, luxurious accommodations, and delectable dining options, San Francisco Marriott Marquis is your ideal choice for a memorable stay in the heart of downtown San Francisco.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note: When breakfast is included in your meal plan, it will be provided in B55 Craft House and Kitchen for 2 adults and 2 children under 12 years old are registered in the same room. All further guests are subject to additional charges.

Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount.

 Our daily destination fee includes: $25 USD Daily Food & Beverage Credit*, 2 Dynamic Stainless Steel Water Bottles, 2 hour bike rental (once per stay), 2 Round trip Cable Car Tickets (once per stay), Daily Enhanced High Speed Internet Access. Please visit our Front Desk in the lobby for more information about these items/experiences. *Food and Beverage credit is not cumulative and cannot be accumulated for multi-day stays (credit applies to room charges only.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 5.2/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money, with complaints about additional parking fees and undisclosed charges.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and bar services received positive feedback, but some guests desired more variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 7.4/10 - While most guests had positive experiences, there were notable complaints about rude staff and hidden fees.
