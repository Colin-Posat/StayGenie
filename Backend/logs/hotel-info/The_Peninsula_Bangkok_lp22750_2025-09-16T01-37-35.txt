HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Accommodation with River ViewsThe Peninsula Bangkok - SHA Extra Plus offers luxury guest rooms with stunning views of the Chao Phraya River. Each room is equipped with modern amenities and elegant decor, ensuring a comfortable stay.Top-Notch Facilities for Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind at the outdoor pool, stay active at the fitness centre, or pamper themselves at the spa and sauna. The hotel provides a range of facilities to cater to every guest's needs for a truly relaxing and enjoyable stay.Culinary Delights with Scenic Dining OptionsIndulge in a variety of dining experiences at the hotel's restaurants. From international cuisine at The Lobby to authentic Thai dishes at Thiptara Restaurant, guests can savor delicious meals while enjoying picturesque views of the river.Book your stay at The Peninsula Bangkok now to experience luxury and comfort at its finest!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 12 years old and over stay for THB 2,500 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
