HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - Some guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, while others cited room maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was highly appreciated by most guests for being friendly and attentive.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and public transport was praised by all guests.
• Room Quality: 8.5/10 - Rooms were described as spacious, clean, and comfortable by the majority of guests.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities offered, but some mentioned the lack of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6/10 - Opinions varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 8.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, although there were suggestions for increased variety.
• Overall Experience: 9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a desire to return to the hotel.
