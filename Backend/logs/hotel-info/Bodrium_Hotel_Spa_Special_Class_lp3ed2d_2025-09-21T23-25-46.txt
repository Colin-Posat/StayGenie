HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience unparalleled luxury at Bodrium Hotel &amp; Spa - Special Class, where relaxation meets sophistication.Indulge in RejuvenationRelax and unwind at our exquisite spa, offering a range of massages, body treatments, and facials that will leave you feeling refreshed. Enjoy our outdoor pool, 24-hour health club, and sauna for the ultimate leisure experience.Gastronomic DelightsSavor delectable dishes at TYRO BAR&amp;LOUNGE or indulge in room service during limited hours. Our complimentary buffet breakfast ensures you start your day right, while our poolside bar provides the perfect setting for a refreshing drink.Comfort and ConvenienceOur 50 elegantly designed air-conditioned rooms feature premium Tempur-Pedic beds, private balconies, and modern amenities including complimentary Wi-Fi. Enjoy hassle-free services with express check-in and a 24-hour business center to cater to all your needs.Don’t miss your chance to unwind in our exceptional paradise—book your stay today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* Cashless payment methods are available for all transactions.
* Contactless check-in and contactless check-out are available.
