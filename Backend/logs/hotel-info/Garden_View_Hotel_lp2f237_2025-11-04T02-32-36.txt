GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:

• Outdated rooms with maintenance issues

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but there were also reports of unclean bathrooms and dirty windows in some rooms.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, although some complained about noisy rooms and difficulties in getting a good night's sleep.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's proximity to public transport and key landmarks was praised by many guests, making it convenient for travelers.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally spacious and comfortable, with amenities like tea facilities and a safe; however, there were reports of tired-looking rooms with outdated furniture and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with some praising the luggage storage and the attached restaurant, while others noted the lack of charging options for non-UK devices and broken USB sockets.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was reasonable given the location, while others thought it was expensive for the relatively outdated rooms and decor.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests desired more variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some minor issues with room quality and amenities.
