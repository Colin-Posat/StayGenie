Luxurious AmenitiesRosen Shingle Creek offers a top-rated golf course, a full-service spa, and gourmet dining options, making it the perfect destination for a relaxing and indulgent getaway.Outdoor OasisGuests can enjoy four outdoor pools, scenic walking trails, and a golf academy, providing ample opportunities for leisure and recreation amidst the beautiful 230-acre property.Culinary DelightsFrom the legendary steakhouse, A Land Remembered, to Italian cuisine at Cala Bella, guests can indulge in a variety of dining experiences without leaving the resort.Experience luxury and relaxation at Rosen Shingle Creek - book your stay now for an unforgettable Orlando retreat.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
The lead guest must be at least 18 years old and present a valid, government-issued photo ID with a matching credit or debit card with a security chip to cover incidentals upon check-in.

All special requests are subject to availability upon check-in. Special requests cannot be guaranteed and may incur additional charges.

Valet parking is available for an additional fee.

Oversized vehicles such as buses, trailers, and RVs will pay additional parking fees per day.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  USD 34.08 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$28.13 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
