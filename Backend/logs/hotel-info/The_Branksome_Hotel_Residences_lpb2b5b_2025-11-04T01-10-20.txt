HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation OptionsThe Branksome Hotel &amp; Residences in Sydney offers a variety of upscale accommodation options, from studios to spacious 2-bedroom suites, all featuring fully equipped kitchens and laundry facilities for your convenience.Exceptional AmenitiesExperience luxury at its finest with a digital concierge, motorised black-out blinds, and a TV with unlimited movie options. Indulge in the on-site restaurant offering East-meets-West cuisine, relax at the bar, or enjoy the swimming pool, sauna, fitness centre, and children's playground made from natural timber logs.Convenient Location and FacilitiesLocated just 5 minutes from Sydney Airport, this hotel also provides state-of-the-art meeting rooms with audiovisual equipment for successful conferences. With nearby dining and shopping options, The Branksome Hotel &amp; Residences is the perfect choice for your Sydney stay.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and charges may be applicable.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for AUD 30 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for AUD 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for AUD 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
