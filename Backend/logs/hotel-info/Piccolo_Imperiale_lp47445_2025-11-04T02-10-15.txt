HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationPiccolo Imperiale offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and Sky TV, providing a comfortable and lavish stay.Convenient LocationLocated near Castel Sant’Angelo and Lepanto Metro Station, guests have easy access to explore Rome with transportation options at their doorstep.Exclusive ServicesGuests can enjoy a shuttle service to Fiumicino Airport and even opt for a limousine service to the city center with an on-board apéritif, ensuring a luxurious and convenient travel experience.To experience luxury and convenience in the heart of Rome, book your stay at Piccolo Imperiale today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
If you require an invoice when booking a prepaid rate, please write this request and your company details in the <b>Ask a question</b> box.
Please inform Piccolo Imperiale in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A surcharge of EUR 15 applies for arrivals after check-in hours. All requests for late arrival are subject to confirmation by the property.

Please note the shuttle and the limousine service come at extra costs.
