HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsLocated centrally on the vibrant Las Vegas Strip, Nobu Hotel at Caesars Palace offers contemporary rooms with upscale amenities such as a 55-inch flat-screen TV, iPod docking station, and free Wi-Fi. Guests can indulge in complimentary Natura Bisse toiletries and enjoy room service during their stay.Exquisite Dining and EntertainmentGuests can savor the culinary delights of world-renowned Chef Nobu at the onsite Nobu Restaurant and Lounge, known for its Japanese cuisine. Additionally, the hotel features high-end shops, a casino, and a spa offering rejuvenating body treatments and massages. Valet parking adds convenience to the upscale experience.Unwind and ExploreAfter a day of excitement, guests can relax at the outdoor swimming pool or sauna. With priority reservations at Nobu Restaurant and proximity to attractions like the Fremont Street Experience, Nobu Hotel at Caesars Palace is the epitome of luxury in Las Vegas.
