HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Lake Views and Historic EleganceThe Chicago Drake hotel offers breathtaking views of Lake Michigan and a rich history dating back to 1920. Enjoy luxurious guest rooms with deluxe bedding and marble bathrooms, along with modern amenities like flat-screen HD TVs and iPod docking stations.Convenient Amenities and ShoppingGuests can explore the arcade level shops, grab essentials at the convenience store, and make use of foreign currency exchange services without leaving the hotel. Additionally, indulge in afternoon tea or cocktails at the elegant Palm Court.Prime Location for ExploringLocated just minutes from Oak Street Beach, Navy Pier, Lincoln Park Zoo, and the John Hancock Center, The Drake Hilton Hotel is the perfect base for experiencing the best of Chicago. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
