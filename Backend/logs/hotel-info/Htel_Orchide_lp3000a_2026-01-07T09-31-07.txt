HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Floral Courtyard and Stylish RoomsHotel Orchidée in Paris offers a unique experience with its charming floral courtyard and stylish rooms. Enjoy the picturesque views from some rooms overlooking the tranquil courtyard.Convenient AmenitiesEach room is well-equipped with a private bathroom, cable TV, and a welcome tray upon arrival. Accessibility is made easy with a lift to all rooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Ideal Location and TransportationLocated in the Montparnasse district, the hotel is near Metro Station Pernety for easy access to popular destinations like Invalides and Saint-Lazare. Additionally, being close to the TGV train station and airport shuttle stop makes traveling hassle-free.Book your stay at Hotel Orchidée now for a delightful Parisian experience!

GROUPS:

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
