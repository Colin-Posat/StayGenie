HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsSoulshine Bali, nestled in Ubud, offers a tranquil retreat with rooms featuring air conditioning, private bathrooms, and stunning views of rice fields and jungle. Some rooms even boast mosquito nets and spa baths for added relaxation.Exceptional Dining and WellnessIndulge in delectable meals at the on-site restaurant or opt for intimate room service. For wellness enthusiasts, pampering massages await at the spa center. Additionally, guests can explore special diet menus, packed lunches, and a variety of souvenirs at the gift shop.Convenient Amenities and ServicesWith free WiFi, private parking, and the option for bike hire, car rental, and airport transfers, Soulshine Bali ensures a seamless stay for all guests. Explore nearby attractions like Elephant Cave and Ubud Market, making every moment memorable.Ready to experience the serenity of Soulshine Bali? Book now for an unforgettable getaway.

Soulshine is Bali’s first Sound+Wellness resort that is deeply rooted in barefoot luxury, with a mix of mindfulness and pure fun! Located just 10-minutes from central Ubud, Soulshine is a place for you to find your balance, reset, relax, dance, celebrate life, and connect with others. The 33-room upscale resort is a place where people from all over the world can enjoy the culture and beauty of Bali, share delicious food and drinks, be immersed in great music, and form lifelong friendships. The new jungle-facing Panorama Suites are breathtaking! Every room, suite and penthouse has a Marley turntable and a fine set of Marshall speakers, as well as a selection of vinyls, and an acoustic guitar that you can play throughout your stay, and be moved by the sound and the gorgeous jungle view

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 1 and older are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 2 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Adults  stay for Rp 750,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
