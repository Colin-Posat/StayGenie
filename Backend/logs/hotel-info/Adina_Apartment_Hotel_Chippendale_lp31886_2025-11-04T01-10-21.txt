HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Serviced Apartments with Outdoor Plunge PoolAdina Apartment Hotel Chippendale offers contemporary serviced apartments surrounding a peaceful courtyard with a refreshing outdoor plunge pool, perfect for relaxation.Convenient Amenities and FacilitiesEnjoy the convenience of a kitchen or kitchenette, flat-screen TV, air conditioning, and in-room safe in each apartment. Some units also boast a private balcony and spacious living/dining areas for added comfort.Ideal Location for Exploring SydneySituated just a short walk from the University of Sydney, with easy access to Redfern Railway Station and bus stops, the hotel provides a convenient base for exploring all that Sydney has to offer.Book your stay at Adina Apartment Hotel Chippendale now and experience modern comfort and convenience in the heart of Sydney.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.
Please note that property do not accept cash. A valid credit card under the traveler's name is required on check in.

Please note that a 1.5% surcharge applies for payments made with credit card.

Please note guests receive 100MBs of free WiFi per room, per 24 hours. Additional data is available for AUD 9.95 for the remainder of the 24 hours, for up to 4 devices per room.

In accordance with the NSW Public Health Order, from 11 October 2021, hotel teams will be required to confirm the vaccination status of guests. This will be done during the booking process and on check in. The hotel team is required to check vaccination certificates of guests for both vaccination doses or medical exemption forms at the hotel and in the restaurant and bars. For more information, please contact the property using the contact details found on the booking confirmation.

Please note that  the property underwent a full refurbishment in December 2021.

Please note bookings over 7 nights will only receive a weekly housekeeping service.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for AUD 75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  AUD 30 per  day.

• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were comfortable and well-equipped, with positive comments, but maintenance issues and street noise in one review affected the average rating.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities with positive mentions of gym and pool, but some guests noted the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Varied opinions on pricing, with some feeling it was high for the services provided while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally satisfactory, with some suggestions for improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, contributing to the high rating.
