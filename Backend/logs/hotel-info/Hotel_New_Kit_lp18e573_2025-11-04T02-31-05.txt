Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.4/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on cleanliness with some mentioning cleanliness as positive while others noted issues with maintenance.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff mentioned by multiple guests. However, some complaints about wait times were reported.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel's proximity to public transport and key landmarks was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and well-equipped. However, some guests mentioned issues such as lack of windows and noisy ventilation.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, with positive mentions of breakfast, gym, and pool. However, concerns were raised about the lack of spa services and room size.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services. Some guests suggested more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
