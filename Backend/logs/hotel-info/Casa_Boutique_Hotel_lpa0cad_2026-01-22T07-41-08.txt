HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Boutique Hotel in Miami Beach's Art Deco DistrictLocated in the heart of Miami Beach's historic Art Deco District, Casa Boutique Hotel offers a perfect blend of style and comfort. Just steps away from Ocean Drive and the beach, our guestrooms are equipped with modern amenities including flat-screen TVs and safety deposit boxes. Enjoy the convenience of ironing facilities and a private bathroom during your stay.Exceptional Services and AmenitiesExperience top-notch services at Casa Boutique Hotel, including free WiFi, a 24-hour front desk, and a private beach club offering 2 complimentary beach chairs. Explore the vibrant neighborhood with our bike hire services or take a leisurely stroll to nearby attractions like Lincoln Road and the Miami Beach Convention Center.Convenient Location and AccessibilityWith Miami International Airport just 15 km away, Casa Boutique Hotel is easily accessible for travelers. Book your stay now and indulge in the charm of South Beach at our boutique hotel.
