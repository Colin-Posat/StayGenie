HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel Carlton offers spacious, Venetian-style rooms with elegant interior design, Murano glass lamps, and antique furniture. Each room is air-conditioned and equipped with modern amenities for a comfortable stay.Gourmet Dining OptionsIndulge in an American breakfast each morning and savor Italian and international dishes at La Cupola restaurant. Enjoy drinks and snacks at Bar Carlton Café throughout the day, all while overlooking the stunning Grand Canal.Convenient Location and ServicesLocated just minutes away from Santa Lucia Train Station and Piazzale Roma car park, Hotel Carlton provides easy access to Venice's top attractions. Take advantage of free access to the Venice Casino and the assistance of the hotel's helpful staff for booking restaurants and tickets.Ready to experience the charm of Venice at Hotel Carlton On The Grand Canal?
