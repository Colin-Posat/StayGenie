HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Serene Japanese Garden Setting:Immerse yourself in the tranquility of a 400-year old Japanese garden surrounding Hotel New Otani Tokyo.Convenient Central Location:Located in central Tokyo, just a short walk from 5 subway lines, Hotel New Otani Tokyo provides easy access to popular areas like Ginza, Shibuya, and Shinjuku in under 10 minutes.Diverse Dining Options:Indulge in a culinary journey with 37 dining choices at Hotel New Otani, offering a wide range of international and Japanese cuisine, from French and Italian to traditional tempura and teppanyaki.Experience the beauty, convenience, and culinary delights at Hotel New Otani Tokyo – book your stay now.
