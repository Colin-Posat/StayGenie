HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central LocationHotel Nazionale boasts the most central accommodation in Venice, just a stone's throw away from Santa Lucia Railway Station and Piazzale Roma bus station. With the nearby vaporetto pier Ferrovia, guests can easily explore all the famous landmarks in Venice.Diverse Dining and ShoppingSituated in Cannareggio Sestiere, guests at Nazionale can enjoy a wide array of restaurants to suit every palate and budget. Exclusive shops, boutiques, clubs, and discos are also within reach, offering a vibrant experience for all visitors.Concierge ServicesThe hotel's 24-hour reception desk is dedicated to ensuring guests have a seamless stay. From providing information on tours, events, and museums to arranging visits to Murano's glass furnaces, the concierge is committed to enhancing your Venice experience.Book your stay at Hotel Nazionale for a memorable Venetian adventure!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
In the event of early departure, the hotel will charge the entire amount of the booked stay.

The lift runs from the first floor upwards. Gluten-free menus are available in the restaurant on request.
