HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Shangri-La Bosphorus, Istanbul offers elegantly decorated rooms with modern amenities, boasting contemporary décor with Asian highlights. Each room features high-tech products, free high-speed internet, and luxurious marble bathrooms with Bvlgari toiletries. Suites include separate living rooms and two terraces with stunning Bosphorus views.Exquisite Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at IST TOO, offering a selection of world cuisines, or savor authentic Chinese delicacies at the signature Shang Palace. The Lobby Lounge provides a unique experience with Kung-Fu Tea Master performances and a wide variety of Chinese teas and pastries.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind at CHI, The Spa, which includes an indoor pool, sauna, Turkish Bath, hot tub, and steam rooms. Private spa suites are also available for a truly luxurious experience.Experience luxury and relaxation at Shangri-La Bosphorus, Istanbul.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for € 45 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
