CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for US$12 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.

Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 8/10 - Guests found the rooms spacious, clean, and comfortable, with only minor maintenance issues reported.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - While most guests were satisfied with the amenities provided, some mentioned a desire for additional services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some feeling the price was slightly high for the offerings, while others found it reasonable considering the location and overall experience.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Overall, guests had positive feedback on the breakfast and dining options, though some wished for more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
