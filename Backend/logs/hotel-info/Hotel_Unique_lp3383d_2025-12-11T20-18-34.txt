HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Exquisite Architecture and Stunning ViewsHotel Unique, designed by architect Ruy Ohtake, offers breathtaking views of São Paulo and Ibirapuera Park in the trendy Jardins area. The rooftop pool and Skye Restaurant &amp; Bar provide a luxurious experience with a modern touch.Luxurious AccommodationThe 5-star hotel features modern furniture, a hot tub, and upscale amenities in each room. From air conditioning to an LCD TV and JBL speaker, guests are pampered with comfort. Suites offer a spacious seating area for relaxation.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentIndulge in contemporary cuisine at Skye Restaurant &amp; Bar while enjoying lounge music and city views. The lobby bar offers a selection of exotic drinks amidst stylish design objects.Book now to experience the unique blend of luxury and sophistication at Hotel Unique.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for R$ 600 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  BRL 80 per  day.
