HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the charm of Barcelona at Hotel 1898, where history meets elegance in the heart of La Rambla.Rooftop OasisTake a dip in our stunning rooftop pool, surrounded by a sun terrace that invites relaxation while you soak in panoramic views of the vibrant city.Culinary DelightSavor exquisite Mediterranean cuisine in our stylish restaurant, or unwind at our bar-restaurant terrace, where traditional tapas create a culinary journey for your taste buds.Historic EleganceHoused in a beautifully restored 19th-century building, our hotel boasts a Speciality Monument status, blending unique architectural heritage with modern comforts for an unforgettable stay.Don't miss the chance to book your stay at Hotel 1898, where comfort and culture await you.
