HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Airport Shuttle Service and On-Site DiningThe Best Western Plus Vancouver Airport Hotel offers a 24-hour on-call airport shuttle service and on-site restaurants for your convenience. With Vancouver International Airport just 3 km away, your travel needs are well taken care of.Modern Amenities and Comfortable AccommodationsEnjoy air-conditioned rooms equipped with a coffee maker featuring Seattle's best coffee, a 43-inch flat-screen TV with pay-per-view movies, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Each room also includes a mini-refrigerator and in-room safe for your comfort.On-Site Facilities and Nearby AttractionsTake advantage of the 24-hour gym, business centre, ATM machine, and more at the hotel. Explore nearby attractions like VanDusen Botanical Garden and Queen Elizabeth Park, with easy access to downtown Vancouver. Book your stay at the Best Western Plus Vancouver Airport Hotel for a memorable experience.
