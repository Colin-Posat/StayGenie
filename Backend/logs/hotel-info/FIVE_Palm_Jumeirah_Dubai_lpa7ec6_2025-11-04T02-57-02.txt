Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.8/10 - Some guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, while others complained about noise and dirtiness.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service was generally praised, especially the friendly staff, although there were complaints about long check-in times and issues with reservations.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel's location was highlighted as a positive aspect, with guests appreciating its proximity to attractions and public transport.
• Room Quality: 6.5/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable by some, but others mentioned maintenance issues and dissatisfaction with room allocation.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests enjoying the pool and restaurants, while others noted the lack of spa services and noise.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.1/10 - Mixed reviews contributed to an average overall experience rating, with some guests having a great stay and others experiencing issues with service and cleanliness.
