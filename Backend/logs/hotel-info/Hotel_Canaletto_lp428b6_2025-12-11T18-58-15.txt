HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortDiscover the allure of Hotel Canaletto, once the residence of the renowned Canaletto painter, located near San Lio Church in the heart of Venice. Immerse yourself in Venetian elegance with Wi-Fi access and rooms offering picturesque views. Indulge in a delightful buffet breakfast served in two elegant dining rooms, or savor a drink at the outdoor tables.Convenient Location and AccessWith the Rialto Bridge just 200 meters away and Piazza San Marco within walking distance, Hotel Canaletto offers easy access to Venice's iconic landmarks. The nearby vaporetto provides swift connections to Santa Lucia Station and public car parks, ensuring a seamless travel experience.Effortless ExplorationEmbark on a hassle-free journey to Hotel Canaletto by taking water bus 2 towards Rialto and alighting at the Rialto stop, just a short 5-minute walk from the hotel. Elevate your Venice experience by booking your stay with us today.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
No parking available.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
