HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the ultimate getaway at Chatrium Hotel Riverside Bangkok, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of the city.Recreational DelightUnwind by our stunning outdoor pool, take a refreshing sauna, or break a sweat in our 24-hour fitness center. Whether you seek relaxation or activity, our amenities cater to every need.Culinary IndulgenceSavor a delightful international culinary experience at the River Barge Restaurant or order in from our 24-hour room service. Enjoy snacks at our café or sip drinks by the poolside bar as you soak in the vibrant atmosphere.Comfort and ConvenienceOur 396 chic guest rooms are designed with your comfort in mind, featuring kitchenettes, premium bedding, and complimentary high-speed internet. With thoughtful amenities and easy airport access, your stay will be as enjoyable as it is seamless.Book your unforgettable stay today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
* One child 6 years old or younger stays free, per registered adult, when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* A car is recommended for transportation to and from this property.
* Parking height restrictions apply.
* Cashless payment methods are available for all transactions.
* This property welcomes guests of all sexual orientations and gender identities (LGBTQ+ friendly).
