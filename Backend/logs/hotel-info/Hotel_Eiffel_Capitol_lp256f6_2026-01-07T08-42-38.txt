HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central LocationSituated on a peaceful street near the Eiffel Tower, our hotel offers convenience and charm for your Parisian getaway. Dupleix Metro Station is just steps away, ensuring easy access to iconic landmarks like the Arc de Triomphe.Comfortable AccommodationOur modern guest rooms are designed for relaxation, featuring complimentary WiFi, satellite TV, and cozy tea/coffee facilities. You'll feel at home with a private bathroom equipped with a hairdryer for your convenience.Delightful Dining OptionsStart your day with a delicious breakfast served in the breakfast room or opt for a cozy in-room dining experience. Unwind with a refreshing drink at the hotel bar, adding a touch of luxury to your stay.Book now to experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Eiffel Capitol hotel.
