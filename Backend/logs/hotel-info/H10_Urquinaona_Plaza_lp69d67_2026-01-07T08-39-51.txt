HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 19th-Century Boutique Hotel in the Heart of BarcelonaChic Rooms with Modern Amenities: Enjoy smart, contemporary décor in our chic rooms equipped with free Wi-Fi, Nespresso coffee maker, and flat-screen TV.Roof Terrace with City Views: Relax on our roof terrace with a small seasonal pool and take in stunning city views. Perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring.Convenient Location: Just a stone's throw away from Plaza de Catalunya and Las Ramblas, with easy access to Urquinaona Metro Station and major attractions like Palau de la Música Catalana and the Born district.Book your stay at H10 Urquinaona Plaza for a luxurious and convenient Barcelona experience.
