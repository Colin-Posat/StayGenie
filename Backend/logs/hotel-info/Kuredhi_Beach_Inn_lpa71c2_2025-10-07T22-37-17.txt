HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience comfort and style at Kuredhi Beach Inn in Maafushi. Each air-conditioned room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, a kettle, and a private bathroom. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
