HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsExperience the ultimate stay at Sir Adam Hotel, located in the iconic A'DAM tower overlooking the IJ-river and Amsterdam's historic city center. Indulge in a delicious burger at The Butcher Social Club or unwind in the comfort of your room with modern amenities and scenic views.Unique Experiences and FacilitiesPurchase tickets for the A'DAM LOOKOUT observation deck, work at The Hub's co-working space, or host events in The Deck's flexible studios. Enjoy gourmet burgers and cocktails at The Butcher Social Club, available for breakfast, lunch, and terrace dining.Wellness and ConvenienceStay fit with guided classes and fitness instructors in the hotel's gym area. Explore the city with bike rentals or visit nearby attractions like Dam Square and Beurs van Berlage. Book now for a memorable stay at Sir Adam Hotel, part of Sircle Collection.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Sir Adam Hotel, part of Sircle Collection in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Breakfast is free of charge for children up until 2 years old (including). Children between 3 and 11 years old (including) receive a 50% discount on breakfast. From 12 years on the breakfast price is equal to that of an adult.

When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Please note that children can only stay in existing bedding. There is no space for baby cots or extra beds in all room types.

The maximum occupancy per room is 2 persons (either 2 adults, or 1 adult and 1 child).

Please note that breakfast is not included in the price in certain rate types. In these cases, breakfast has to be booked separately against a charge.
