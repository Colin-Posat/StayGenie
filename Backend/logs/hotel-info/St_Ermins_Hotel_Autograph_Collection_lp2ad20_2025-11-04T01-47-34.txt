• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with occasional complaints about dinner quality and lack of variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Majority of guests had a positive overall experience, with highlights on location, service, and cleanliness.
