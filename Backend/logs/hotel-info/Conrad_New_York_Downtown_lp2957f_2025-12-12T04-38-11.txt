GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location

Areas for Improvement:
• Expensive breakfast
• Limited parking
• Mixed service feedback
• Value for money concerns
• Maintenance issues

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 6.5/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests appreciating it and others having complaints about staff behavior.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its proximity to key landmarks and the Hudson River.
• Room Quality: 7.3/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable but some mentioned small room sizes and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests praising the rooftop bar and charging options while others mentioned the lack of parking facilities.
• Value for Money: 4/10 - Many guests felt the price was not justified by the services provided, leading to low ratings in this category.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, although some guests expressed disappointment in the mini fridge charges.
• Overall Experience: 6.7/10 - The overall experience had mixed reviews, with both positive and negative feedback influencing the rating.
