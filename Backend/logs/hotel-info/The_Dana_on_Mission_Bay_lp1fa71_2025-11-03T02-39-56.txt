HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tropical Oasis by Mission BayWaterfront Dining and RelaxationIndulge in a waterfront dining experience at our contemporary restaurant, unwind by one of our 2 outdoor pools or rejuvenate in one of the 2 hot tubs. Don't miss out on the seasonal pool bar for refreshing drinks under the sun.Comfortable Guest Rooms with Marina ViewsEnjoy modern amenities in our guest rooms including a flat-screen TV, microwave, and refrigerator. Some rooms offer picturesque views of the marina. Relax in our plush bathrobes and make use of the complimentary toiletries provided.Concierge Services and Recreational ActivitiesLet our dedicated staff enhance your stay by arranging tours, dinner reservations, and attraction tickets. Explore the area with ease by renting a bike or car, and partake in activities like cycling, fishing, and canoeing.Book your stay at The Dana on Mission Bay for an unforgettable tropical getaway by the bay!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 29 per  day.
