HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Modern AmenitiesP-Park Residence (Srinakarindra-Suvarnabhumi) offers modern serviced apartments easily accessible by the BTS skytrain and MRT. Rooms come with free Wi-fi and cable TV. The apartments offer a gym and pool.Spacious Rooms with Private BalconiesP-Park's rooms offer large living spaces with private balconies. A minibar and tea/coffee maker are provided, while private bathrooms offer shower facilities.Exceptional Services and Dining OptionsP-Park Residence provides laundry service and 24-hour security. A restaurant serving authentic Thai cuisine is available. Alternatively, guests can request for room service. Free parking is available in the building.Experience comfort and convenience at P-Park Residence - book your stay now!
