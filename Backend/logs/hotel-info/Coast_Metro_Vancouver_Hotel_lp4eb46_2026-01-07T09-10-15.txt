HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 10 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 11 years old to 17 years old stay for CAD 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 8 years old and over stay for CAD 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Location: 7.9/10 - The central location of the hotel near key amenities in Vancouver was praised, despite some noise disturbances and the need for transportation to downtown.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Guests enjoyed the spacious and well-decorated rooms, complimenting the comfortable beds and modern amenities, while some noted the lack of nearby restaurants.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating the common room with a coffee machine and outdoor spaces, while others desired more extensive offerings like breakfast and spa services.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Opinions on value for money were varied, with some feeling the price was justified by the location and service, while others believed additional amenities would enhance the overall value.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and dining options, though some guests wished for more variety and a simpler breakfast offering.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff, cleanliness, and comfortable rooms, with a few mentioning noise disturbances and minor inconveniences.
