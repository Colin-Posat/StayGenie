HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationThe Westin Lake Las Vegas Resort &amp; Spa offers spacious guest rooms with flat-screen TVs, Westin Heavenly Bath toiletries, and cozy bathrobes for a comfortable stay. Enjoy a complimentary cup of in-room coffee to start your day refreshed.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at the resort's restaurants. From Japanese and Asian-fusion cuisine at Marssa Steak &amp; Sushi to American dishes with Mediterranean flavors at Rick's Cafe, there's something for every palate. Sip on exotic cocktails at Sandsa Bar &amp; Grill or enjoy a signature drink at Arabesque Lounge.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind at the 2 outdoor pools, water slide, and private beach. Explore the picturesque lake with kayak or canoe rentals. Treat yourself to a rejuvenating massage at g Aveda Spa Lago, offering customizable spa packages and premium facials.Book your stay at The Westin Lake Las Vegas Resort &amp; Spa for an unforgettable experience!
