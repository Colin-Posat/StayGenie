HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Boutique Hotel with Rooftop Infinity PoolNaumi Hotel Singapore offers a luxurious boutique experience with a stunning rooftop infinity pool showcasing breathtaking city skyline views. The stylish rooms, complete with Nespresso machines and complimentary WiFi, provide the perfect blend of comfort and sophistication.Personalized Service and Convenient LocationEnjoy personalized service at Naumi Singapore, conveniently located just a 5-minute walk from City Hall MRT Train Station. Situated amidst charming shop houses and local eateries, the hotel is also a short drive from the Marina Bay Financial Center, making it an ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers.Dining and Entertainment OptionsIndulge in a delightful buffet breakfast at the lobby and unwind at the daily happy hour offering a selection of beers, drinks, and local snacks. For more dining and entertainment choices, head to Cloud 9 Pool &amp; Bar for a variety of beverages and delectable canapes.Ready to experience the luxury and convenience of Naumi Hotel Singapore? IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Naumi Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of SGD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
The name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property.  The payable total price may be increased by 1% due to the GST increase in 2024.
