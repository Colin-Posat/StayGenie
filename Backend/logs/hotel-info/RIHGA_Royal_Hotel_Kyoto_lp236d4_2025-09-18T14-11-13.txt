HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Japanese TouchesExperience the elegance of RIHGA Royal Hotel Kyoto, just minutes away from JR Kyoto Train Station. Indulge in the contemporary decor with Japanese influences in every room, complete with modern amenities like a satellite TV and Laura Ashley bath products.Culinary Delights for Every PalateSavor the flavors of Japan at Aoi Teppanyaki Restaurant or enjoy authentic French cuisine at Top of Kyoto with breathtaking views from the 14th floor. With a total of 8 restaurants on-site, including Chinese dishes and a cozy bar, your culinary desires are sure to be satisfied.Convenient Location for Exploring KyotoExplore the nearby temples such as Nishi Hongan-ji and To-ji, or take a short train ride to Fushimi Inari Taisha Shrine. With complimentary shuttle service to JR Kyoto Station, your adventures in Kyoto are just a step away.Book your stay at RIHGA Royal Hotel Kyoto now and immerse yourself in luxury and convenience during your Kyoto getaway.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for ¥1,650 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for ¥3,630 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally praised, with some suggestions for improvement in variety and portion size.
