HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience comfort and elegance in every room at Sheraton Maria Isabel Hotel Towers. Enjoy custom-designed plush mattresses, spacious bathrooms, and modern amenities including air conditioning and cable channels.Exceptional FacilitiesIndulge in a variety of facilities such as an outdoor pool, fitness center, and tennis court during your stay. Unwind at Jorongo bar with live mariachi music or savor award-winning Italian cuisine at Ristorante Amici.Prime LocationConveniently situated in Mexico City's financial district on Paseo de la Reforma Avenue, our hotel offers easy access to iconic landmarks like the Angel de la Independencia Monument and the U.S. Embassy. Explore the city's attractions with the Historic Centre just a short drive away.Book your stay at Sheraton Maria Isabel Hotel Towers for a memorable and luxurious experience.
