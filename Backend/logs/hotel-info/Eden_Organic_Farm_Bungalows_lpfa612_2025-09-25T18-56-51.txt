HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Outdoor Views and Amenities - Enjoy stunning views from the terrace and garden at Eden Organic Farm &amp; Bungalows. Stay connected with complimentary wireless internet and explore the gift shops and picnic area for a relaxing time outdoors.Local Cuisine and Services - Indulge in a satisfying meal at the on-site restaurant and start your day with a complimentary local cuisine breakfast. Benefit from amenities such as luggage storage, lockers, and a convenient roundtrip airport shuttle for a surcharge.Comfortable Accommodations - Feel at home in the air-conditioned rooms with minibars, private balconies, and patios. Stay connected with complimentary wireless internet and unwind in modern bathrooms with free toiletries and hair dryers.Immerse yourself in the beauty of La Fortuna at Eden Organic Farm &amp; Bungalows. Book your stay now to experience relaxation and nature at its finest.
