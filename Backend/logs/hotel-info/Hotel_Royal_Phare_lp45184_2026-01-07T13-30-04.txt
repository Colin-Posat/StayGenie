HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Eiffel TowerHotel Royal Phare offers a prime location on Paris’s Left Bank, just a 5-minute walk from the iconic Eiffel Tower. Enjoy stunning views of Paris and stay connected with free WiFi in our soundproofed rooms.Modern Comforts and AmenitiesExperience modern decor and convenience in our guest rooms, each equipped with a TV offering satellite and cable channels.
