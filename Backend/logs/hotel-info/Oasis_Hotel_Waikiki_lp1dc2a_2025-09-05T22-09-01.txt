HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 10 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  USD 45 per  day.

• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Spacious and comfortable rooms were well-liked, while some guests mentioned noise issues and maintenance concerns.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with positive highlights on the pool area and pet-friendly policy, balanced by concerns about the lack of shade at the pool.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Varied opinions on value for money, with some guests finding the hotel prices reasonable while others felt it did not match the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Breakfast options received mixed feedback, with some guests criticizing the lack of variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and highlighted the friendly staff, clean rooms, and convenient location, though some issues like noise and breakfast quality were mentioned.
