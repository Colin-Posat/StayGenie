HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location on Las Ramblas and Plaza CatalunyaLocated in the heart of Barcelona, Hotel Continental offers charming rooms with free high-speed Wi-Fi and a fridge. The pink-themed air-conditioned rooms feature a flat-screen TV, private bathroom, and microwave for guest convenience.24-Hour Free Continental BuffetGuests can enjoy a complimentary continental buffet available round the clock. Additionally, a microwave and crockery set in each room allow guests to store and enjoy food from the buffet at their leisure.Convenient Amenities and ServicesWith a 24-hour reception, currency exchange, and car rental facilities, Hotel Continental ensures a seamless stay for guests. Explore nearby attractions easily, with Catalunya Metro Station just a minute away.Book now for a delightful stay at Hotel Continental Barcelona, where comfort meets convenience.

HOTEL POLICIES:

Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 5 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests finding it expensive for what was offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and food options received mixed reviews, with some guests praising the variety and others finding it lacking.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel, highlighting the location and service.
