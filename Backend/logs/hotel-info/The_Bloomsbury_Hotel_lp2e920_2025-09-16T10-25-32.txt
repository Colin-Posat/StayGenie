HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in the Heart of LondonThe Bloomsbury Hotel is nestled in the vibrant theatre district, offering easy access to London's top shows and attractions. With Tottenham Court Road tube station and The British Museum just steps away, exploring the city is a breeze.Luxurious Amenities for a Comfortable StayIndulge in luxury with plush bedding, cozy bathrobes, and Malin &amp; Goetz toiletries in every room. Stay entertained with flat-screen TVs and stay refreshed with complimentary tea, coffee, and water. Plus, stay connected with free Wifi throughout the hotel.Exquisite Dining ExperiencesSavor delicious meals at The Bloomsbury Club Bar &amp; Dining Room or unwind at the Dalloway Lounge and Terrace. Whether enjoying a meal indoors or under the stars on the terrace with retractable roofing, culinary delights await.Experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at The Bloomsbury Hotel. Book your stay now for an unforgettable London getaway.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for £50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Location: 8.8/10 - The hotel's proximity to key landmarks and public transport was widely acknowledged and praised for its convenience.
• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Guests found the rooms spacious, comfortable, and well-equipped, with minor maintenance issues mentioned by a few reviewers.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While some guests appreciated amenities like the gym and pool, others noted the absence of spa services as a drawback.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Guest opinions varied on the value for money, with some considering the price high for the services offered and others finding it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some guests suggesting more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 8.8/10 - The majority of guests reported a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, with particular praise for cleanliness, location, and service.
