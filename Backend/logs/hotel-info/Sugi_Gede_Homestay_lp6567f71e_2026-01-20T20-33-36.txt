HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for Rp 50,000 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 100,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 150,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
