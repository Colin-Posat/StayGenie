HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical Charm and Modern ComfortNestled in the heart of Kumkapi district in İstanbul, Ahmet Efendi Konağı offers a perfect blend of history and modernity. Just 800 meters from the iconic Grand Bazaar, this hotel provides a serene retreat with a sun terrace offering panoramic city views.Cozy Rooms and Thoughtful AmenitiesEach room at Ahmet Efendi Konağı features a kettle, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries. Slip into the provided slippers and unwind after a day of exploration, knowing that comfort awaits.Convenient Location and Exceptional ServiceConveniently located near Sultahahmet Square, The Hagia Sophia Museum, and Blue Mosque, this hotel is an ideal choice for travelers seeking to immerse themselves in İstanbul's rich history. With a 24-hour front desk and a delicious breakfast served at the on-site café, your stay at Ahmet Efendi Konağı promises both convenience and luxury.Book your stay now and experience the magic of İstanbul at Ahmet Efendi Konağı.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 5 years old and over stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
