HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Scenic Location and Nearby AttractionsThe Comfort Inn Downtown offers a picturesque view of the Mississippi River and is conveniently situated near popular attractions like Mud Island River Park, Beale Street, and the National Civil Rights Museum.Convenient Amenities and ServicesGuests can enjoy a range of amenities including a fitness center, rooftop deck with river views, and a complimentary hot breakfast. Business travelers will appreciate the free high-speed internet, business center, and spacious meeting facility.Comfortable Accommodations with River ViewsThe hotel's spacious guest rooms feature modern conveniences like coffee makers, microwaves, and refrigerators. Business class suites go a step further with additional amenities like wet bars, DVD players, and stunning views of the Mississippi River.Book your stay at the Comfort Inn Downtown for a memorable experience in historic downtown Memphis!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
