HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
City Views and Modern ComfortsHotel Nishimura, nestled in Fuji, offers stunning city views and modern amenities. The air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and private bathrooms ensure a comfortable stay.Relaxing Shared LoungeGuests can unwind in the shared lounge, perfect for socializing or enjoying a quiet moment. The multilingual staff, fluent in English and Japanese, adds a personal touch to your experience.Convenient LocationSituated near Shimizu Station and Shizuoka Airport, Hotel Nishimura provides easy access to nearby attractions. Don't miss the opportunity to indulge in sea views and impeccable service at our 3-star hotel. Reserve your stay now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
On-site parking is limited and is available on a first-come, first-serve basis. Private parking is located within a 2-minute walk from the property. Please note, additional fees may apply. Please contact the property in advance if guests plan to arrive in a nonstandard-sized car. Contact details can be found in the booking confirmation.

Housekeeping will be conducted between 10:00AM and 15:00PM. For guests staying multiple nights and remaining in their rooms during this time, room cleaning will not be performed. Please note that only towel replacement and garbage collection will be carried out.Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.
