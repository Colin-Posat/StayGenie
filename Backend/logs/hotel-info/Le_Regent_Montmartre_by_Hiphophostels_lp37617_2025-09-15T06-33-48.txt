IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
A damage deposit of EUR 200 is required. The host charges this 7 days before arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
All guests under 18 years old must be accompanied by an adult and cannot be accommodated in a dormitory.

Bed linen are provided while bath towels can be rented on site at an additional cost (EUR 2.50/day).

Guests are kindly advised that groups with participants under 16 years of age are not accepted. If you request beds for a group of students who are between 16 and 18 years of age you must inform us at the time of booking. Failure to comply with these conditions may result in cancellation of the reservation.

Each guest must show a valid photo ID and the credit card used to make the reservation upon check-in. The name on the credit card must match the guest's name.

When booking 5 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Please note that property do not accept reservations for 7 persons and more.
