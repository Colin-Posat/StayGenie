HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Panoramic ViewsRyumeikan Tokyo offers modern rooms with free WiFi and wired internet access, featuring panoramic city views from a restaurant. The sound-proof rooms boast 2 pillow types, extra-long beds, flat-screen TVs, air purification/humidifiers, and optional in-room computer rentals. Guests are pampered with free toiletries, slippers, and a hairdryer for a comfortable stay.Convenient Amenities and ServicesThe hotel provides mobile phone rentals, quick laundry service, and travel information. Enjoy the convenience of a launderette, drink vending machines, and a 24-hour front desk offering DVD/CD player rentals, photocopying services, and daily English newspapers. Indulge in in-room massages for an extra touch of relaxation.Central Location for ExploringConveniently located just a 3-minute walk from Tokyo Station and Nihombashi Subway Station, Ryumeikan Hotel Tokyo offers easy access to popular shopping districts like Akihabara and Ginza. Explore Shibuya, Roppongi, and Shinjuku with a quick 30-minute train ride. Book now for an unforgettable Tokyo experience at Ryumeikan Tokyo.
