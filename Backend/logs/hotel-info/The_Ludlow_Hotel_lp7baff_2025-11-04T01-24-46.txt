HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spectacular Views and Luxury AccommodationsExperience breathtaking views of New York City's iconic skyline at The Ludlow Hotel. Each room is elegantly furnished with hardwood floors, silk rugs, and Moroccan lamps, creating a luxurious and cozy atmosphere.Top-Notch AmenitiesIndulge in a relaxing bath in the marble mosaic en suite bathroom, complete with a deep soaking bathtub and brass rain shower. Enjoy local products from the minibar and take advantage of complimentary WiFi access in every guestroom.Convenient Location and ServicesLocated in the vibrant Lower East Side neighborhood, our hotel offers easy access to popular attractions like the Tenement Museum and the New Museum. With a 24-hour fitness center, trellis-covered garden, and lobby bar, The Ludlow Hotel ensures a memorable stay in the heart of Manhattan.Book now to experience luxury and comfort at The Ludlow Hotel.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note, a $75.00 per night deposit will be charged upon check-in for incidentals. Any remaining funds will be made available up to 30 days after check-out.

Please note, the Ludlow Loft is the only room type that can accommodate an extra roll away bed ($35/night plus tax).

Please note, the Mini Studio and Queen Studio cannot accommodate a crib.

Please note that for non-refundable reservations, prepayment will be required for the total price of the reservation and it will be charged immediately after completion of the booking.
