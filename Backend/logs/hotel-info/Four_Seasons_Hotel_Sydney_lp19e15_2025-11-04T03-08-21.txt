HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Panoramic ViewsSituated in Sydney CBD, Four Seasons Hotel Sydney offers stunning panoramic views of Sydney Harbour, including the iconic Opera House and Circular Quay. The carefully designed rooms feature high-quality amenities and the Deluxe Full Harbour Room provides unobstructed views for a truly memorable stay.Culinary Delights and Elegant DiningIndulge in modern Australian cuisine at Mode Kitchen &amp; Bar or sip on handcrafted cocktails at Grain Bar. The hotel offers a delightful culinary experience with dishes made from quality local produce, ensuring a memorable dining experience for all guests.Wellness and RelaxationUnwind at the state-of-the-art fitness centre or pamper yourself at the therapeutic spa and wellness centre. From skin treatments to relaxing massages, guests can rejuvenate and relax during their stay. Additionally, Chinese-speaking staff are available to cater to your needs, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable visit.Book your stay at Four Seasons Hotel Sydney for a luxurious and unforgettable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
You must show a valid credit card upon check in. This credit card must be in the same name as the guest's name on the booking confirmation.

When booking 3 rooms or more, or booking a room for 7 nights or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Same Credit card number that booking has been made in must be visible on check in.

Please note that there is a 2% charge when you pay with a (Visa, Mastercard, American Express etc) credit card.

Hotel welcomes dog only. For any room booking, guests are allowed to bring a dog weighing less than 11.5kg. A pet service fee of AUD 150 applies and includes the set up of pet bedding, bowls, and toilet pets. Please contact our Reservations Team to further discuss additional terms and conditions of pet stays.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 8 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  AUD 89 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 150 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 8.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received overall, with a few suggestions for improvement.
• Overall Experience: 9.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and praised the hotel's location and staff.
