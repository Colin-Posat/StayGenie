HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Charming CourtyardLocated just a 5 minutes’ walk from Mexico City Cathedral and the Zócalo Main Square, Hotel Isabel boasts a prime location for exploring the city. The central courtyard with columns adds a touch of charm to your stay.Comfortable Rooms with Various AmenitiesEach room at Hotel Isabel is equipped with a laptop safe, sofa, and cable TV for your convenience. Choose from rooms with private or shared bathrooms, and some even offer a balcony for a lovely view.Convenient Services and Dining OptionsEnjoy the convenience of a restaurant and bar on-site, along with room service and the option for packed lunches. Need assistance? The hotel offers luggage storage, a tour desk, and an airport shuttle service for an added fee.Book your stay at Hotel Isabel now for a memorable experience in Mexico City!

Reservations of more than 1 room will require a deposit or transfer. Reservations of more than 5 nights will require a deposit or transfer. Reservations made for a high season will require a deposit or transfer. The establishment will contact you to provide you with bank details. The allocation of balconies in some rooms is subject to availability at check-in. Visitors are not allowed in the rooms, access to the room is only for registered guests.
