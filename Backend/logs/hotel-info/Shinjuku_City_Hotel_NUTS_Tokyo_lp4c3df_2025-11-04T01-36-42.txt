HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Comfortable StayLocated just a 2-minute walk from Shinjuku Gyoenmae Subway Station, Shinjuku City Hotel N.U.T.S Tokyo offers cosy accommodation with free broadband internet access and spacious en suite bathrooms.Explore Nearby AttractionsGuests can enjoy a flat-screen satellite TV, refrigerator, and wake up to complimentary tea or coffee. Within walking distance are Shinjuku Gyoen, Kabukicho area, and easy access to Shibuya and Ginza areas via the nearby Shinjuku San-chome Station.Additional AmenitiesThe hotel features an internet corner, laptop borrowing service, bicycle hire, and daily breakfast served in-room upon request. Don't miss the chance to experience comfort and convenience at Shinjuku City Hotel N.U.T.S Tokyo.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  JPY 3000 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for ¥3,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
