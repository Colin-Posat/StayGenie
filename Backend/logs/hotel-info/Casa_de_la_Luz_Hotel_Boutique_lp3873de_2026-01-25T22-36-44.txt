HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Architecture and Prime LocationCasa de la Luz Hotel Boutique, with its historic 1500s architecture, offers a charming stay in Mexico City. Situated in front of Plaza Primo de Verdad and just 800 meters from Zocalo Square, the hotel provides stunning city views from its bar and terrace.Deluxe Amenities and ServicesEnjoy a taste of Mexico at the on-site restaurant, take advantage of the 24-hour front desk and room service, and stay active in the fitness center. With free WiFi throughout the property and family rooms available, every need is catered to with utmost care.Convenience and ExplorationFrom making tour arrangements to utilizing the business center, Casa de la Luz Hotel Boutique ensures a seamless stay. Explore nearby attractions like the Metropolitan Cathedral and Templo Mayor, then unwind with a meal at the hotel. Book now for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Mexico City.
