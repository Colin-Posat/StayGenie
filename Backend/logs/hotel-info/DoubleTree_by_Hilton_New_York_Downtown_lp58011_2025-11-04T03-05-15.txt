Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests had mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some mentioning the hotel was very clean, while others complained about specific issues like overwhelming chlorine smell and thin walls.
• Service: 6.7/10 - Service feedback was varied, with reports of unfriendly reception staff, language barriers with cleaning personnel, and slow response times concerning elevators.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for its proximity to public transport stations and key landmarks, providing guests with convenience and a sense of security.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and clean, with positive remarks about bed comfort, internet quality, and room size. However, noise disturbance from neighboring rooms and outside sources was reported.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Amenities were satisfactory overall, with particular praise for the gym facility. However, complaints were made about the lack of spa services and the requirement to join Hilton Honors for free internet access.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Guests had differing opinions on the value for money, with some feeling that the price was too high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable given the hotel's location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Feedback on food and beverage services was generally positive, with compliments on breakfast offerings and nearby dining options. However, suggestions for improvement included more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, citing factors like location, cleanliness, and friendly staff as key highlights. Some negative aspects mentioned included elevator wait times and noise disturbances.
