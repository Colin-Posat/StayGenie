HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Prime LocationOpened in 1308, Albergo Cavalletto &amp; Doge Orseolo is a historic gem nestled behind St. Mark's Square. The elegant rooms and private pier offer a glimpse into Venice's rich past.Venetian Elegance and ComfortExperience classic Venetian style in every room at Albergo Cavalletto, complete with satellite TV and minibar. Some rooms even boast picturesque views of gondolas in the Orseolo Basin.Culinary Delights and SightseeingIndulge in a varied breakfast buffet and savor typical Venetian cuisine at the hotel's renowned restaurant. With Correr Museum and St. Mark's Basilica just steps away, your Venetian adventure awaits.To experience the allure of Venice from a historic vantage point, book your stay at Albergo Cavalletto &amp; Doge Orseolo today.
