HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations Overlooking a Private ParkExperience the charm of Hôtel de Berri, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Paris, just steps away from the iconic Champs-Elysées. Indulge in the elegance of guest rooms overlooking a century-old private park, featuring parquet floors, marble fireplaces, and modern amenities like Apple TV and automated lighting.Exquisite Dining OptionsSavor a diverse breakfast selection and Italian cuisine at the hotel's restaurant, or unwind with a drink at the stylish bar. With continental, American, Asian, and gluten-free options available, every palate is catered to at Hôtel de Berri.Convenient Location for Paris ExplorationExplore Paris effortlessly with the Arc de Triomphe within walking distance and Georges V Metro Station nearby, providing easy access to renowned attractions like the Louvre and Tuileries Garden. Book your stay now at Hôtel de Berri for an unforgettable Parisian experience.
