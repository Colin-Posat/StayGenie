HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationThe Romer Waikiki at The Ambassador is ideally situated on Oahu, with easy access to Waikiki Beach and close proximity to transportation options like the Waikiki Trolley and bus routes.Comfortable AccommodationsEach room at the hotel features modern amenities such as a flat-screen cable TV, refrigerator, and air conditioning. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of ironing facilities, a walk-in shower, and a hair dryer in the en suite bathroom.On-Site OfferingsGuests can relax by the outdoor pool, grab a coffee at the bistro, or pick up snacks and essentials at the convenience store. The hotel also provides a launderette and surfboard storage for added convenience.Experience the best of Waikiki at Romer Waikiki at The Ambassador - book your stay now for a memorable Hawaiian getaway!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 51 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 2 years old and over stay for US$100 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
