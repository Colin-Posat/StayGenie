HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel NuVe Urbane, nestled in the vibrant Lavender district, offers a luxurious stay with air-conditioned rooms featuring flat-screen TVs and complimentary minibars. The en suite bathrooms are stocked with free toiletries, ensuring a comfortable and convenient experience for guests.Convenient LocationSituated just 550 meters from the renowned 24-hour Mustafa Centre and a short 7-minute walk to Lavender MRT Station, our hotel provides easy access to popular attractions like Bugis Village, Arab Street, and Haji Lane, all within a 1 km radius. The proximity to Changi Airport, only 19.3 km away, makes travel hassle-free for our guests.Relaxation and DiningUnwind by taking a refreshing dip in the swimming pool or indulge in delectable meals at the on-site restaurant. With free WiFi available throughout the property and a 24-hour front desk for any assistance needed, Hotel NuVe Urbane ensures a memorable and seamless stay for all guests.Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at Hotel NuVe Urbane in Singapore.

A damage deposit of SGD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 10 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7/10 - Rooms were generally clean and spacious, but some guests noted maintenance issues and noise disturbances compromising comfort.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with positive feedback on the pool and snacks, but some guests mentioned missing umbrellas, pool area maintenance, and difficulty in accessing pool towels.
• Value for Money: 7.1/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price was justified for the location, while others mentioned noise issues affecting the overall experience.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, but some guests suggested more variety could be offered.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and found the hotel suitable for their stay, with minor complaints affecting their ratings.
