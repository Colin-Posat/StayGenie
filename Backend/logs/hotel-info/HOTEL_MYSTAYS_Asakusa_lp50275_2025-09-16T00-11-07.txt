HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient AmenitiesHotel MyStays Asakusa offers rooms with a kitchenette, en suite bathroom, and free wired internet, providing guests with a comfortable and convenient stay.Dining OptionsGuests can enjoy the convenience of cooking their own meals in-room or visit the Indian restaurant and bar Doli located on the ground floor, offering a variety of dining options.Nearby AttractionsLocated within walking distance of the historical Asakusa area, the National Sumo Stadium at Ryogoku, and just a short bus ride away from the iconic Tokyo Skytree Tower, guests at Hotel MyStays Asakusa can easily explore the vibrant city of Tokyo.Book your stay at Hotel MyStays Asakusa for a memorable and convenient experience in Tokyo!

Housekeeping service is not included in the room rate and is subject to additional costs. This service needs to be requested 7 days in advance of your arrival.
