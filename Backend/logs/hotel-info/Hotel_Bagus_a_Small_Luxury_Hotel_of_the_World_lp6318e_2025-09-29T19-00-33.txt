HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Pool and Bar with City ViewsHotel Bagués offers a luxurious experience with its rooftop pool and bar, providing stunning city views for guests to enjoy.Unique Masriera Museum Jewellery in Every RoomEach stylish room at Hotel Bagués showcases a unique item of jewellery from the prestigious Masriera Museum, adding a touch of elegance and history to the stay.Central Location and Modern AmenitiesConveniently situated on Las Ramblas in the Gothic Quarter, the hotel is just a short walk from Barcelona Cathedral and Plaza Catalunya Square. Guests can indulge in modern fusion cuisine at El Regulador Bistro or enjoy 24-hour room service.Experience the charm and sophistication of Hotel Bagués - book now for an unforgettable stay!
