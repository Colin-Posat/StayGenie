HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience a stay of comfort and elegance at Omni Boston Hotel at the Seaport. Each air-conditioned room features free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom, ensuring a relaxing retreat during your visit.Exceptional AmenitiesIndulge in the hotel's outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, and terrace for stunning views of the city. Enjoy a delicious meal at the on-site restaurant or unwind in the hot tub. Additionally, take advantage of services like room service, a 24-hour front desk, and currency exchange for a seamless stay.Convenient LocationLocated just 2.5 km from Carson Beach, our 4-star hotel is near popular attractions like the Boston Tea Party Ship &amp; Museum. With easy access to Logan Airport and various points of interest, Omni Boston Hotel at the Seaport is the perfect choice for your Boston getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.
