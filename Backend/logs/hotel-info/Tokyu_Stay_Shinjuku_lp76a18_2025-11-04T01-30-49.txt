HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern and Convenient AccommodationTokyu Stay Shinjuku, opened in May 2015, offers a modern stay in the heart of lively Shinjuku. Just steps away from the subway station, the hotel provides free WiFi and 24-hour front desk services with luggage storage.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsThe rooms at Tokyu Stay Shinjuku are furnished with white and light-wood decor, featuring amenities like a flat-screen TV, fridge, and electric kettle. Some rooms even come with a microwave and air purifier/humidifier for added convenience.Complimentary Services and Nearby AttractionsGuests can enjoy free rental services including a washing machine, tumble dryer, and more. With a variety of dining options nearby and attractions like Shinjuku Gyoen Park within walking distance, there's plenty to explore during your stay. Book now for a memorable experience!
