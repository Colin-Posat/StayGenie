HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location in De Pijp DistrictLocated in the lively De Pijp district, easyHotel Amsterdam offers economic rooms within walking distance of Museum Square and the RAI Convention Centre.Functional Rooms with Essential AmenitiesAll rooms at easyHotel feature private en suite bathrooms, air-conditioning, and free WiFi. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that for group reservations of 6 rooms or more, different policies apply. Please contact the accommodation for more information.

It is possible to get an early check-in from 11AM and a late check-out until 1PM, subject to availability.
The cost of an early check-in from is €20 and the cost of a late check out is €20 per room. You can request a late check-out at our reception

The hotel operates with a unique concept which means that the rooms are small and simply equipped.

Please note that this property does not offer extra bed or cots as it can not be accommodated in the rooms.

During your stay your room will not be cleaned and your linen will not be changed. If you would like this service, it is available for a small surcharge.

Guests are kindly requested to note that the windows in your room cannot be opened.

There is no restaurant or bar in the hotel, but there are vending machines for snacks and hot drinks available.

Guests are advised to pay with card.

Please note that the minimum check-in age is 18 years.

Large dogs over 60cm are not permitted.

No more than two dogs per room, and dogs must always be supervised by their owners.

Surcharge of €10 per dog. Guide dogs are welcome free of charge.

Please note that your room can be cleaned every 6th day for free. Extra room clean costs €20.

Children (under the age of 18) are welcome as long as they are accompanied by a legal guardian over the age of 18. You may be required to show proof of guardianship and age, so please come prepared.
