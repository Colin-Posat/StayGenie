Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 2/10 - Guests consistently complained about cleanliness issues such as lack of maintenance and delays in room cleaning.
• Service: 2.9/10 - Service was a major issue with guests reporting rude and unhelpful staff, lack of maintenance, and delays in room service.
• Location: 7.3/10 - The convenient location was one of the few positive aspects mentioned by guests.
• Room Quality: 3.8/10 - Rooms were criticized for being subpar with complaints about cleanliness, maintenance, and overall quality.
• Amenities: 5.3/10 - Amenities were not highlighted positively by guests, with complaints about limited services and lack of important facilities like a spa.
• Value for Money: 3/10 - Many guests felt that the high charges, additional fees, and lack of transparency in pricing made the hotel poor value for money.
• Food and Beverage: 5.5/10 - Few positive remarks about food and beverage services, with complaints about high prices and lack of variety.
• Overall Experience: 1.7/10 - Overall, the majority of guests had a negative experience, citing issues with cleanliness, service, room quality, and value for money.
