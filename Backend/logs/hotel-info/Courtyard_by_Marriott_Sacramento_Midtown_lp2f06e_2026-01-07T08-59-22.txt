HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Self-Catering Facilities: Enjoy the convenience of self-catering in our modern rooms equipped with a mini-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker.The Bistro Restaurant &amp; Bar: Indulge in delicious meals and snacks at our bistro open throughout the week, offering healthy options, ready-to-eat items, and Starbucks® Coffee.Convenient Location: Explore the historical Midtown District and easily reach Central Sacramento, just a 10-minute drive away.
