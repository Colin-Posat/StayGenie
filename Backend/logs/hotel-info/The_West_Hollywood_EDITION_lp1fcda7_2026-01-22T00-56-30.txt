HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation:The West Hollywood EDITION, located in Los Angeles, offers a 5-star experience with free bikes, a fitness centre, and a shared lounge. Guests can enjoy the convenience of a restaurant, room service, and a 24-hour front desk, all complemented by free WiFi throughout the property.Top-Notch Amenities:This hotel boasts a business centre, concierge service, and currency exchange for a seamless stay. The terrace and billiards offer additional recreational options for guests to unwind and have a great time.Convenient Location:Situated just a short distance from Petersen Automotive Museum, Los Angeles County Museum Of Art / LACMA, and Dolby Theater, The West Hollywood EDITION provides easy access to popular attractions. Book now for an unforgettable stay in the heart of Los Angeles!
