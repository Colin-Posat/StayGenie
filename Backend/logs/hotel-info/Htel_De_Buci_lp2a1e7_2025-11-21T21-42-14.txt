HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms with Parisian CharmExperience the allure of Paris in the heart of the Latin Quarter at Hôtel De Buci. Indulge in luxuriously decorated rooms adorned with antique furnishings and velvet curtains. Each room offers Cable TV and a minibar for your convenience.Elegant Breakfast and 24-Hour BarStart your day with a delightful buffet breakfast in the elegant breakfast room. Unwind at any time with the hotel's 24-hour bar, perfect for a late-night drink or a quick refreshment after a day of exploring.Prime Location and Concierge ServiceConveniently situated in the Saint Germain area, Hôtel De Buci allows easy access to Parisian landmarks like the Louvre and Luxembourg Gardens. Take advantage of the concierge service for personalized travel tips and recommendations.Book your stay at Hôtel De Buci now for an unforgettable Parisian getaway!
