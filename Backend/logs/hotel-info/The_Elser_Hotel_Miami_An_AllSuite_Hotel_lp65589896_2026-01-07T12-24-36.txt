HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesThe Elser Hotel Miami offers a range of luxurious amenities including an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre, and terrace. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of private parking, free WiFi, and a 24-hour front desk with concierge service.Elegant and Comfortable RoomsEach guest room at The Elser Hotel Miami is designed for comfort and style, featuring air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. Certain rooms even boast city views and balconies, providing a touch of elegance to your stay.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary experience at the hotel's restaurant, offering a variety of American, Italian, and Mediterranean cuisine. Vegetarian, dairy-free, and halal options are also available upon request, catering to every guest's palate.Experience luxury, comfort, and exceptional service at The Elser Hotel Miami. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Miami.
