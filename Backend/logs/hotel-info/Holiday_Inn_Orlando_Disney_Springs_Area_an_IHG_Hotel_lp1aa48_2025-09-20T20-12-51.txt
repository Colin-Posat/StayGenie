HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesThis Lake Buena Vista hotel, nestled within the Walt Disney World® Resort and a short stroll from Disney Springs, offers a perfect blend of comfort and convenience. Guests can relish complimentary WiFi, an on-site restaurant, and easy access to nearby attractions like Epcot.Comfortable AccommodationsEach guest room at Holiday Inn Orlando – Disney Springs™ Area is equipped with modern essentials including a flat-screen cable TV, microwave, and refrigerator. Stay connected with wired internet and keep valuables secure in the in-room safe.Delightful Dining and RecreationIndulge in delectable meals at Palm Breeze's Restaurant &amp; Lounge, open daily for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. For a quick bite, the Grab-N-Go outlet offers coffee and snacks. Kids dine free, and guests can unwind at the 24-hour fitness centre, outdoor pool, hot tub, and games room.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Holiday Inn Orlando – Disney Springs™ Area!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
The resort fee includes:

- Hourly transportation to all 4 theme parks (reservations required)
- Coffee in the lobby (07:00-11:30)
- Afternoon cookies and lemonade in the lobby
- 2 bottles of water per day
- Local and 800-number calls
- Refrigerators and microwaves in every guestroom
- 2 drinks valid for cocktail, house wine or beer
- Computer and printing access
- 24-hour access to the fitness center

Please note Breakfast Included rate includes breakfast for 2 adults. Additional charges apply for further guests.

The Kids Eat Free offer is available for kids 11 and under and is limited to up to four (4) children per dining family, per meal OR up to two (2) children per parent/adult, per meal. Additional terms and conditions apply.

We appreciate your understanding. Please contact our front desk for more information.

For stays of less than two nights there will be no housekeeping service provided. We will provide full cleaning on the third day of your stay and each day after until checkout.

Contact the front desk at any time to request extra towels and amenities, we will be happy to deliver them to your room.
