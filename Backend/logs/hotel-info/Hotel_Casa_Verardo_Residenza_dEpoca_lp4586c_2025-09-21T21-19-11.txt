HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm in the Heart of VeniceLuxurious Accommodations: Hotel Casa Verardo offers air-conditioned rooms with original period furniture, providing a unique and elegant stay. The annex building features rooms with contemporary décor, catering to diverse preferences.Scenic Dining Experience: Indulge in a buffet breakfast in the elegant dining hall or on the terrace overlooking the picturesque canals during summer. The perfect way to start your day in Venice.Convenient Location: With a water bus stop just a short walk away, accessing major transportation hubs and iconic landmarks like the Rialto Bridge is effortless. Make the most of your Venetian getaway at Hotel Casa Verardo.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
