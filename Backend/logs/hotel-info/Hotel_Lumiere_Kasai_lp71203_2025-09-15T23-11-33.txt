HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Comfortable StayHotel Lumiere Kasai, just a 3-minute walk from Kasai Subway Station, offers cozy guestrooms with free wired internet. Enjoy a complimentary breakfast each morning.Well-Equipped Rooms and ServicesRooms are furnished with a flat-screen TV, fridge, electric kettle, and tea set.
