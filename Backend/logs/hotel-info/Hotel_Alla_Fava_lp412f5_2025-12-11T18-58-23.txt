HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Canal View Rooms and Prime LocationHotel Alla Fava offers charming en suite rooms with air conditioning and flat-screen TVs, overlooking a picturesque Venetian canal and a quiet square. Just a stone's throw away from the iconic Rialto Bridge, immerse yourself in the beauty of Venice right from your window.Convenient Access to Venetian LandmarksSituated a mere 5 minutes' walk from the renowned St. Mark's Square, Alla Fava Hotel places you in the heart of Venice's vibrant atmosphere. Surrounded by popular shops, cafés, and restaurants, experience the essence of Venetian culture at your doorstep. Easily accessible by Vaporetto, with the Rialto stop nearby, explore the city with ease.Personalized Service and Luggage StorageEnjoy personalized service at Hotel Alla Fava, where the reception and luggage storage are available upon request. Your comfort and convenience are our top priorities during your stay in Venice.Book now to experience the allure of Venice at Hotel Alla Fava.
