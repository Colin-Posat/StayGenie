HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in our spacious guest rooms featuring stone gas fireplaces, Keurig coffee machines, and marbled bathrooms with heated floors. Select rooms even boast heated toilet seats and spa tubs for the ultimate relaxation experience.Top-Notch AmenitiesEnjoy our full-service spa, rooftop terrace, fire pits, and lake-view patio. Stay active in our fitness center, unwind in the hot tub, or take a dip in the heated swimming pool. Free snowboard and ski storage is available, and don't forget to explore our gift shop for souvenirs.Culinary DelightsSavor wood-fired and Mountain Chic California Cuisine at Jimmy’s Restaurant, open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. For a more private dining experience, opt for in-room dining and enjoy delicious meals in the comfort of your own space.Experience luxury and comfort at The Landing Resort and Spa.
