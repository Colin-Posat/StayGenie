HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and Stunning ViewsStep into luxury at Oceanside Hotel in Miami Beach, where an outdoor pool and cityscape vistas await. Just a short stroll away, the sandy beach beckons with complimentary beach towels for your relaxation.Elegant Rooms and Convenient FacilitiesIndulge in comfort in our air-conditioned rooms, each equipped with free WiFi and a flat-screen TV.
