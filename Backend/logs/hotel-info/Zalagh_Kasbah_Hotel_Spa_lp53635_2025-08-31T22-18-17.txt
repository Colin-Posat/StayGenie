HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsLocated beside the beautiful Agdal Gardens, the Zalagh Kasbah Hotel &amp; Spa offers modern 4-star accommodations with spacious rooms featuring stunning views of the Atlas Mountains from large balconies. Each room is equipped with a minibar and a flat-screen TV, ensuring a comfortable stay for every guest.Relaxation and DiningGuests can unwind in the outdoor swimming pool, fitness room, or the spa centre with a hot tub. The hotel's restaurant serves delectable international cuisine, while the snack bar offers quick bites. Don't miss the daily breakfast served in the breakfast room to start your day right.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy free Wi-Fi in public areas and take advantage of the free private parking on site. With a short 11-minute drive to Menara Airport, the Zalagh Kasbah Hotel &amp; Spa is the perfect choice for your stay in southern Marrakech.Ready to experience luxury and relaxation? HOTEL POLICIES:

Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Beautiful decor
• Helpful service
• Comfortable rooms
• Great shower pressure

Areas for Improvement:
• Some noisy guests affecting sleep quality
• Limited gluten-free breakfast options
• Room maintenance issues

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but there were some complaints about noisy guests affecting sleep quality.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests, with particular praise for the friendly and helpful staff members like Chaima, Najwa, and Kenza.
• Location: 7.9/10 - The hotel's location was praised for being quiet, clean, and beautiful, but some guests found it too far from the city center.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally described as comfortable and luxurious, with some mentions of noisy doors and minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory, with positive comments on the courtyard and pool views, but some guests desired more gluten-free options for breakfast.
• Value for Money: 7.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests finding it excellent and others mentioning high prices for services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed reviews, with positives on burgers at the snack bar but some dissatisfaction with breakfast variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had positive overall experiences, citing friendly staff, beautiful decor, and a generally pleasant stay.
