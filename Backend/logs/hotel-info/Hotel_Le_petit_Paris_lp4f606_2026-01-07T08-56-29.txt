HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 18th-Century Hotel in the Heart of the Latin QuarterHistorically Inspired Rooms with Modern Amenities: Indulge in the charm of French historical periods while enjoying modern comforts like flat-screen TVs, minibars, and free WiFi in each air-conditioned, soundproofed guest room.Cozy Bar and Lounge, Buffet Breakfast: Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast in the contemporary breakfast room, unwind with a drink at the bar or lounge, and soak in the charming ambiance of Hotel Le Petit Paris.Convenient Location and Services: Benefit from a 24-hour reception, ticket service, tour desk, and a small courtyard.
