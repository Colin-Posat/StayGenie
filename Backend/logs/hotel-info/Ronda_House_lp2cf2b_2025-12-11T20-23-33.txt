HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationRonda House, located just off Ronda Sant Antoni Avenue, is a mere 10 minutes' stroll from the vibrant Las Ramblas.Charming Decor and AmenitiesThe hotel boasts bright and simple décor, offering air-conditioned rooms with fully equipped private bathrooms. Guests can start their day with a delightful buffet breakfast at the on-site café, serving a variety of coffees and pastries.Nearby Attractions and AccessibilityWith the MACBA Art Gallery a short 5-minute walk away and the Sant Antoni Metro Station a mere 200 meters from the hotel, exploring Barcelona's iconic sights such as Gaudí’s Sagrada Familía and Casa Batlló is convenient and easy.Ready to experience Barcelona from the comfort of Ronda House? HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  EUR 20 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
