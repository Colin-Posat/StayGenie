HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for £90 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for £240 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  GBP 75 per  day.
