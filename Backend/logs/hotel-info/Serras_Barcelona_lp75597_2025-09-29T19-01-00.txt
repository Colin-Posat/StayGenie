HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 110 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 45 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were clean and well-maintained, with guests consistently mentioning cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests, with mentions of helpful and friendly staff. Some guests highlighted specific staff members like Sandra for exceptional service.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and tourist spots was a major highlight for guests, making it convenient for exploring the city.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as spacious, comfortable, and well-supplied with necessary amenities. Some minor maintenance issues were mentioned by a few guests.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with mixed feedback highlighting the gym and pool positively, but some mentioning the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with positive comments on food quality. Some guests suggested more variety could be added.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a desire to return, recommending the hotel to others.
