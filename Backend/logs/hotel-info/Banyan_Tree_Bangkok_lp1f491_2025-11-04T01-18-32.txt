HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and Stunning ViewsBanyan Tree Bangkok offers luxurious rooms with stunning views of the city or Chaophraya River. The spacious rooms and suites feature wooden flooring, large windows, a seating area, coffee/tea maker, and satellite TV. Guests can also enjoy complimentary non-alcoholic beverages from the minibar and take advantage of 24-hour room service.Exceptional Dining Options and Rooftop ExperienceExperience a culinary delight at Banyan Tree Bangkok with 7 dining options, including the stylish rooftop restaurant and bar - Vertigo and Moon Bar. Enjoy panoramic views while sampling a variety of western, Thai, and Chinese dishes. Don't miss the opportunity to embark on a dinner cruise along the Chaophraya River.Wellness and Fitness FacilitiesRelax and rejuvenate at the hotel's health club, offering a well-equipped fitness centre, steam room, and sauna. Unwind by the 4m x 25m swimming pool or indulge in a spa treatment. Airport transfers are also available for your convenience.Book your luxurious stay at Banyan Tree Bangkok for a memorable experience in the heart of Bangkok's central business district.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of THB 6000 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 14 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that the name of the credit card holder must be the same as the guest's name and credit card must be presented to the property upon check-in. For the cardholder's protection, If the cardholder is not present, full payment will be taken on the presented card and the pre-payment will be credited back to the original card. Additional authorizations for incidental charges can be processed on separate card.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 12 years old and over stay for THB 2,354 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
