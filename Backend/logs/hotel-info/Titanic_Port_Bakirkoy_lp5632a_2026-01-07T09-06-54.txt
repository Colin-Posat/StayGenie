HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsElegant Rooms and Fine DiningIndulge in traditional Turkish cuisine at Alesta Restaurant or savor Italian pastries at Titanic Coffee. Enjoy buffet-style meals with breathtaking views of the Sea of Marmara and Atakoy Marina.Relaxation and Wellness AmenitiesUnwind with holistic therapies and massages in the wellness center, complete with a hammam, hot tub, and sauna.
