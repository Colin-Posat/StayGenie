HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Terrace with Stunning ViewsThe Knickerbocker, located on Times Square, offers a breathtaking rooftop terrace with over 370 square meters of indoor and outdoor space. Enjoy panoramic views of the city skyline while sipping cocktails at St.Cloud.Luxurious Room AmenitiesEach room at the hotel is equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, laptop safe, mini fridge, and a desk. Relax in comfort with city views, plush bathrobes, and free toiletries in the en suite bathroom.Convenient Location and Dining OptionsWith Bryant Park just a short walk away and the New York Public Library within minutes, guests have access to iconic NYC landmarks. Indulge in delectable cuisine at Charlie Palmer at The Knick and unwind with cocktails at St.Cloud or Charlie Palmer lounge.Book your stay at The Knickerbocker for a truly unforgettable New York City experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Knickerbocker in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of USD 300 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed within 7 days of check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note, guests must be at least 21 years old to check-in.

Tickets, which can be purchased for an additional fee, are required for entry to St Cloud Rooftop on New Years Eve.

Please note that group policy applies when more than 7 rooms are booked. This includes a cancellation policy of 30 days prior to arrival instead of 24 hours.
A mandatory fee is applicable per room per night. It includes the following:
• Unlimited WiFi for all devices
• Unlimited local, long-distance and international calling
• Unlimited access to nytimes.com and ft.com
• Wardrobe storage and nightly shoeshine
• 24-hour access to fitness centre
• Two bottles of water daily

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$75 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 165 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with multiple mentions of exceptional staff members providing excellent care.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel is located in the heart of New York City, offering easy access to key landmarks and transportation.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Rooms were spacious, quiet, and well-equipped, although some guests noted lighting issues.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities provided, particularly the rooftop bar and dining options, though some mentioned the absence of in-room coffee.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on perceived value for money, with some guests finding the price reasonable for the location while others considered it high.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, although a desire for more variety was expressed by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
