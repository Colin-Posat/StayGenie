Prime Location near The Louvre and Notre DameLocated just a 5-minute walk from The Louvre, Hotel Ducs de Bourgogne offers a prime location in the heart of Paris, with Notre Dame Cathedral just 1 km away.Elegant and Comfortable GuestroomsExperience luxury in every detail with the Louis Philippe style décor, wooden furniture, and sumptuous fabrics of the guestrooms. Enjoy modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, work area, and elevator access, with some rooms specially designed for guests with reduced mobility.Exceptional Services and AmenitiesIndulge in a buffet breakfast served in a renovated cellar, request room service for drinks and snacks, and benefit from a 24-hour reception with a dedicated concierge service.
