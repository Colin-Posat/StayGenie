HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesSercotel Riscal in Puerto Lumbreras offers a delightful stay with a seasonal outdoor pool, fitness center, and a charming garden. Enjoy the convenience of air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and a 24-hour front desk for all your needs.Culinary Delights and OptionsIndulge in a buffet breakfast each morning and savor Spanish cuisine at the in-house restaurant. With vegetarian and vegan options available, there's something for everyone's taste buds.Outdoor Activities and ExplorationExplore the surroundings with cycling and hiking activities offered nearby. Don't miss the stunning mountain views from select rooms, adding a touch of nature to your getaway.Book your stay at Sercotel Riscal now for a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure!
