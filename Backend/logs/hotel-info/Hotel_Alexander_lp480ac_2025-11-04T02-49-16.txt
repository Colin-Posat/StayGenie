HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Boutique Hotel in the Heart of AmsterdamCourtyard Garden and Convenient LocationLocated just a 5-minute walk from Vondelpark and Leidseplein, Hotel Alexander boasts a charming courtyard garden with a seating deck. Perfect for relaxing after a day of exploring the city. Situated on a quiet street, the hotel is also conveniently close to tram and bus stops, making it easy for guests to navigate the city.Comfortable Rooms and Modern AmenitiesThe simple yet functional rooms at Hotel Alexander are equipped with a TV and modern bathrooms, providing guests with a comfortable stay. Additionally, the hotel offers a computer with free internet access in the lobby, ensuring guests can stay connected during their visit.Concierge Services and Nearby AttractionsWith a 24-hour reception, tour desk, and bicycle rental service, Hotel Alexander goes above and beyond to cater to its guests' needs. Explore nearby attractions such as the Van Gogh Museum and the Rijksmuseum, both within a 10-minute walk. Don't miss the opportunity to indulge in luxury shopping at the nearby PC Hooftstraat.Experience the charm of Hotel Alexander and book your stay now for an unforgettable Amsterdam getaway.
