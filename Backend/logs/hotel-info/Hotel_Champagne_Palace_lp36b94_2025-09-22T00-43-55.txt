HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building and Elegant Decor - Hotel Champagne Palace, nestled in the heart of Rome, offers a unique stay in a historic building adorned with original paintings and ancient furnishings.Comfortable Rooms with City Views - Enjoy air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV, some offering stunning city views. Each room boasts a private bathroom with tiled floors for your comfort.Convenient Location and 24-Hour Reception - Just 200 meters from Termini Station, our hotel is a short walk from iconic landmarks like the Roman Forum and the Coliseum. With a 24-hour reception, we are always here to assist you during your stay.Experience the charm of Rome at Hotel Champagne Palace. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

Food & beverage services at this property may be limited or unavailable due to Coronavirus (COVID-19).
Due to Coronavirus (COVID-19), this property is taking steps to help protect the safety of guests and staff. Certain services and amenities may be reduced or unavailable as a result.
The Regulation on the Rome Tourist Tax (resolution no.29 of 8 April 2021) provides for the payment of € 6.00 per person per night (excluding minors within the tenth year of age; sick people and those who assist patients hospitalized in facilities health, etc) for all non-resident guests in Rome staying in private apartments, including accommodation for tourist use.
The tourist tax will be paid by the guests exclusively at the time of check-in and will not be charged in advance by the hotel. The taxes on the room, VAT, etc, have nothing to do with the contribution of the Municipality of Rome.
