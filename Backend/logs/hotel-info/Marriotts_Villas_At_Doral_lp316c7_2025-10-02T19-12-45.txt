HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Villas with Modern AmenitiesLocated in Miami, Marriott's Villas At Doral offers spacious air-conditioned villas with fully equipped kitchens, free WiFi, and private balconies. Guests can enjoy the outdoor pool, gym, and breakfast bar in a separate living area.Relaxation and RecreationThe resort features a lap pool, massages, skin treatments, and a picnic area with barbecue facilities. Golf enthusiasts can benefit from arranging tee times at the Doral Golf Resort &amp; Spa.Convenient Dining OptionsIndulge in American cuisine poolside, accompanied by a full cocktail menu at Deco Bar and Grill. For quick bites and essentials, the 24-hour MarketPlace Express convenience store is available on-site.Experience luxury and convenience at Marriott's Villas At Doral.
