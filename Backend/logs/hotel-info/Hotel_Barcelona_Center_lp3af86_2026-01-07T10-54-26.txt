HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime LocationLocated just 400 metres from Barcelona’s iconic Passeig de Gràcia Avenue and a short 15-minute walk from Plaza Catalunya, Barcelona Center Hotel offers the perfect base for exploring the vibrant city of Barcelona.Modern ComfortsExperience comfort in the modern rooms equipped with satellite TV, free WiFi, minibar, safe, and a private bathroom with amenities and a hairdryer.Dining and RelaxationIndulge in traditional Spanish cuisine at the hotel's restaurant, unwind at the bar, or soak in the rooftop hydromassage tub. The terrace offers a perfect spot to enjoy the Mediterranean breeze.Ready to experience Barcelona in style? Book your stay at Barcelona Center Hotel now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  EUR 30 per  day.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
