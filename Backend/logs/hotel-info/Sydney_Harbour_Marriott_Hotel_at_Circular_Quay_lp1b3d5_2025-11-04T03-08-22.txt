HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsSydney Harbour Marriott at Circular Quay offers elegantly appointed rooms with breathtaking views of the harbour, city skyline, or the iconic Opera House. Each room is furnished with plush 300 thread-count linens and cozy down-filled comforters for a truly comfortable stay.Convenient Location for Exploring SydneySituated in the heart of Sydney CBD, our hotel is within walking distance to major attractions such as the Opera House, The Rocks, and the Sydney Harbour Bridge. Explore the vibrant city with ease, with popular shopping destinations like Pitt St Mall just a stone's throw away.Relaxation and Wellness AmenitiesUnwind and rejuvenate at our day spa, take a refreshing swim in the indoor pool, or maintain your fitness routine at the well-equipped gym. After a day of sightseeing, guests can indulge in a soothing sauna session for the ultimate relaxation.Experience luxury and convenience at Sydney Harbour Marriott at Circular Quay. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Sydney getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please note that there is a 1.95% charge when you pay with a credit card.

Guests are kindly requested to inform the hotel in advance of their preferred bedding configuration. This can be noted in the Special Request Box during booking or by contacting the hotel using the contact details found on the booking confirmation.

Breakfast inclusive rates are applicable for a maximum of two guests only, irrespective of room confirmation showing three or four guests. Breakfast for third and fourth guests is available at AUD $48 per person, per day.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  AUD 110 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 110 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.
