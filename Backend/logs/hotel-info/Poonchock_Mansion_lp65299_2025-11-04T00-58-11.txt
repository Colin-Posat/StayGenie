HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Convenience:Poonchock Mansion is centrally located in the Business District, just a short walk from Phaholyothin MRT Station, making it a convenient choice for both business and leisure travelers.Well-Equipped Rooms:The hotel offers spacious rooms with a kitchenette, dining area, microwave ovens, kitchenware, and a fridge. Some rooms even feature luxurious bathrooms with bathtubs, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests.Amenities and Services:Poonchock Mansion boasts a fitness centre, disabled-friendly facilities, a business centre, and a concierge desk, catering to the diverse needs of its guests and providing a seamless and enjoyable experience throughout their stay.Experience the perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and excellent service at Poonchock Mansion. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The hotel requires prepayment via bank transfer. Guests will receive a direct email from the hotel within 48 hours of booking with the bank account detail of the hotel. To confirm the reservation, payment must be made within 48 hours once email is received.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for THB 300 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
