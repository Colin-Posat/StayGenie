HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location: Hotel Marc'Aurelio is ideally situated just 500 metres from Rome’s Cornelia Metro Station, providing easy access to Vatican City within 10 minutes by car.Comfortable Accommodation: The air-conditioned rooms offer a classic style with modern amenities including free WiFi, TV, and minibar. The wooden floors and private bathroom with a bath or shower ensure a comfortable stay.Free Parking and 24-Hour Reception: Enjoy the convenience of free parking on-site and a 24-hour reception for any assistance needed during your stay.Experience the best of Rome with us at Hotel Marc'Aurelio. Book now for a memorable stay!

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

INTERNET:
