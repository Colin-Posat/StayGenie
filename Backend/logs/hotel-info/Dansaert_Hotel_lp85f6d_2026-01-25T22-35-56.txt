HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant heart of Brussels at Dansaert Hotel, where modern comforts blend seamlessly with an unbeatable location.Contemporary Comforts: Indulge in one of our 41 stylish guestrooms, each equipped with plasma televisions and complimentary Wi-Fi, ensuring you stay connected while enjoying your favorite shows.On-Site Dining Delights: Satisfy your cravings at our cozy coffee shop or take advantage of our room service during limited hours. Don’t miss our delightful buffet breakfast served on weekends!Convenience at Your Fingertips: Enjoy our concierge services, express check-in/out, and easy access to a roundtrip airport shuttle. Explore the nearby attractions like the Grand Place and Brussels Stock Exchange, all within a short stroll.Book your stay today and immerse yourself in the charm of Brussels!
