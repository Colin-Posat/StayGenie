HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and Convenient LocationHotel Angeleno offers a range of upscale amenities, including a gym, outdoor pool, and an outdoor fireplace for relaxation. Guests can also enjoy free WiFi, a complimentary shuttle service, and a daily wine hour. The hotel's convenient location between Beverly Hills and Santa Monica makes it an ideal choice for exploring Los Angeles.Innovative Services and Dining ExperienceGuests can take advantage of innovative services such as Bluetooth media streaming and the option to call an Uber billed directly to their room. The penthouse-level West Restaurant and Lounge serves fresh Californian cuisine with stunning views of the city, providing a unique dining experience with new American influences.Complimentary Transportation and EntertainmentAngeleno Hotel offers complimentary transportation to popular attractions like the Getty Center museum and the University of California - Los Angeles. Guests can also attend daily wine hours and easily access public transportation for further exploration. Experience the best of Los Angeles at Hotel Angeleno.
