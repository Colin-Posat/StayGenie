HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience luxury at its finest at Virgin Hotels Nashville, located just 1.2 km from Vanderbilt University. The 4-star hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, a fitness centre, private parking, and a restaurant.Unparalleled AmenitiesIndulge in a variety of amenities including a terrace, bar, room service, and a 24-hour front desk. Selected rooms feature balconies with stunning sea views, while all rooms are equipped with a safety deposit box and cozy bed linen.Entertainment and DiningEnjoy an American breakfast during your stay and take advantage of recreational activities such as billiards and table tennis. With popular attractions like Bridgestone Arena and Ryman Auditorium nearby, Virgin Hotels Nashville is the perfect choice for your next getaway.
