HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 2 years old to 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.1/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.8/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests for being caring, courteous, fantastic, and amazing.
• Location: 9.1/10 - The hotel is centrally located, close to key landmarks and shopping malls, making it ideal for tourists.
• Room Quality: 8.1/10 - Rooms were clean, spacious, and comfortable, with some minor maintenance issues reported.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - The gym and pool facilities were highlighted positively, but some guests mentioned the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some finding the price reasonable and others considering it high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 8.1/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel to others, praising the staff, location, and facilities.
