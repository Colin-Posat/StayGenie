* Pool access available from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM.

• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling that the price was slightly high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location and offerings.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - The food and beverage options received positive feedback overall, with guests enjoying the variety and quality of meals provided. Some suggested additional menu options for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience at the hotel, expressing satisfaction with the facilities, services, and staff interactions. The majority would recommend the hotel to others.
