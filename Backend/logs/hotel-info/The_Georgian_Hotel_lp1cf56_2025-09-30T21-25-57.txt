HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Georgian offers 84 well-appointed guest rooms and 28 spacious suites, many with breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean. Each room features fine Italian linens and Marshall Bluetooth speakers, while suites come equipped with Victrola record players and curated vinyl records from Ubiquity Records.Unique ExperiencesImmerse yourself in culture at The Georgian with exclusive spaces like The Library Room, The Writer’s Room, and Gallery33. Discover curated art, enjoy private dining, or host meetings in these distinctive settings. Additionally, guests can explore Santa Monica with complimentary Priority Bicycles or maintain their fitness routine at the 24-hour fitness center.Enchanting AtmosphereExperience a stay like no other at The Georgian, where luxury meets creativity and history blends with exceptional service. Join us in Santa Monica’s iconic hotel, where every moment is filled with charm and the promise of unforgettable stories waiting to be told.Book now to indulge in a stay at The Georgian and create your own memorable moments amidst the allure of this enchanting destination.
