HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with BalconiesHotel Gran Playa offers newly renovated rooms with modern amenities including air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms. Most rooms also feature a private balcony and complimentary safety deposit box, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Convenient LocationSituated just 500 meters from Gran Playa Beach, Hotel Gran Playa provides easy access to the beautiful sandy beaches of Costa Blanca. Additionally, the hotel is a short walk away from Pola Park Theme Park and a salt mine nature reserve, offering guests a variety of nearby attractions to explore.Relaxing Outdoor Pool and Free WiFiGuests can unwind by the outdoor pool or enjoy a refreshing drink at the hotel's café and bar. With free WiFi available throughout the property, staying connected and sharing holiday memories is convenient for all visitors.Experience a relaxing and convenient stay at Hotel Gran Playa. Book now to enjoy a memorable vacation on the Costa Blanca!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 5 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
