HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to UMi Tulum, your beachfront oasis nestled in the heart of Tulum, where relaxation meets adventure.Private Beach &amp; Outdoor PoolSpend your day unwinding on our exclusive private beach or take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool, surrounded by lush landscapes.Gourmet Dining ExperienceSavor delightful meals at our on-site restaurant or grab a quick snack at the coffee shop/cafe. Indulge in room service during limited hours and wind down with your favorite drink at the bar/lounge.Comfort &amp; ConvenienceOur 14 uniquely decorated guestrooms feature modern amenities such as minibars, espresso makers, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Enjoy a complimentary continental breakfast daily and benefit from our 24-hour front desk service, airport shuttle, and free self-parking.Don’t miss out on an unforgettable stay at UMi Tulum – book your getaway today!
