Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, with a few mentions of rooms not being cleaned or maintained properly.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service received high praise, particularly for the staff's friendliness and helpfulness, although there were a few instances of unaccommodating behavior.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and ease of access to public transportation were highlighted positively by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but some guests mentioned issues with maintenance and noise levels.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with mixed reviews highlighting the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was justified while others found it expensive for the offerings.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Food options received mixed feedback, with some guests praising the quality while others thought it was overpriced for what was offered.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the comfort, location, and service provided by the hotel.
