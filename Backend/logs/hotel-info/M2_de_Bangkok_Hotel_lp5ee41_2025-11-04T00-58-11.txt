Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 4.5/10 - Cleanliness was mentioned positively in one review but was a concern in another, resulting in a lower rating.
• Service: 3/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with a notable negative experience involving unhelpful staff, impacting the rating negatively.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The location was consistently praised for its proximity to various amenities, including Voice Space and a dentist.
• Room Quality: 3.8/10 - Overall poor room quality with complaints about a stench in the room, a non-functional TV, and issues with the room's safe and shower.
• Amenities: 2.5/10 - Guests expressed dissatisfaction with the amenities, facing issues like a locked safe, no signal on the TV, and poor bathroom fixtures.
• Value for Money: 2.9/10 - Given the issues reported with the room and services, guests perceived the value for money as low.
• Food and Beverage: 1/10 - Food and beverage quality was not specifically mentioned, but negatives in other areas likely detract from the overall dining experience.
• Overall Experience: 5/10 - Mixed overall experiences were reported, with some guests likely to recommend based on location and staff, while others faced multiple issues.
