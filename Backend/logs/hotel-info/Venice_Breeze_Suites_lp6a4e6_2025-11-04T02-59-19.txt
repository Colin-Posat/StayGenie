HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Beachfront Studios with Rooftop DeckLocated in a Venice beachfront building, Venice Breeze Suites offers studios with free WiFi and full kitchens. Enjoy the proximity to Ocean Front Walk and its vibrant shops and street performers. The highlight of the property is the rooftop deck with barbecue facilities, lounge chairs, and a wet bar for a relaxing stay.Modern Kitchen and Ocean ViewsEach studio is designed with a modern open layout kitchen featuring white cabinets, black counters, and stainless steel appliances. The en suite bathroom includes free toiletries, and select suites offer exposed brick walls, ocean views, and separate seating areas for a comfortable stay.Convenience and Nearby AttractionsGuests can unwind on the rooftop deck or use the on-site launderette. Additionally, fax and photocopying services are available. Explore Santa Monica Pier and 3rd Street Promenade just a short drive away from Venice Breeze Suites.Book your beachfront getaway now at Venice Breeze Suites!
