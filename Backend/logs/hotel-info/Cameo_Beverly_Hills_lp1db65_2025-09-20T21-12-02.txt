HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Venetian-inspired rooms at Cameo Beverly Hills offer a blend of elegance and comfort. Featuring modern amenities like a 42-inch flat-screen TV and floor-to-ceiling windows, each room provides a relaxing retreat with stunning views of Los Angeles and Beverly Hills.Gourmet Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at The Restaurant at Cameo Beverly Hills, where Italian cuisine meets classic Cipriani dishes. From a special menu for children to Sunday brunch accompanied by live jazz music, there's something to satisfy every palate. Don't forget to visit the poolside bar for a delightful dining experience under the California sun.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind by the outdoor pool or pamper yourself with a massage at the on-site spa. Stay active in the fitness center or simply bask in the sun on the sundeck. With amenities like day beds, cabanas, and dry cleaning services, your stay at Cameo Beverly Hills promises both relaxation and convenience.Experience the allure of Beverly Hills at Cameo Beverly Hills - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests enjoyed spacious and comfortable rooms, but there were complaints about outdated decor and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating the gym and pool, while others criticized the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally received positive feedback, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
