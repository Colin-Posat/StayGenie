HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel M offers value-for-money accommodation in Old Chiang Mai, opposite Thapae Gate. Explore historic temples and the Sunday Walking Street just a 10-minute stroll away.Modern AmenitiesStay in modern, air-conditioned rooms equipped with a bathroom, minibar, cable TV, and a safe. Enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi, parking, bathrobe, and slippers during your stay.Exceptional ServicesExperience friendly staff ready to assist with car rental, airport shuttle, laundry, and dry cleaning services. Indulge in Western and Thai cuisine at Brix Restaurant and Bar, with the option for in-room dining.Book your stay at Hotel M Chiang Mai for a memorable experience near Chiang Mai International Airport.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 1 year old to 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 1 year old and over stay for THB 800 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
