Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Most guests highlighted the cleanliness of the rooms, although some mentioned issues with bathroom smell in one of the rooms.
• Service: 7.8/10 - Guests praised the staff for being friendly and efficient, with only a few complaints about limited English proficiency.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's central location near public transportation was well-received, making it convenient for guests to explore the area.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally described as clean and spacious, with a few mentions of uncomfortable pillows and small room size.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities provided, such as the shower and accessibility to public transportation, but some mentioned the lack of coffee machines and in-room irons.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was too high for what was offered, while others found it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Generally positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some mentioning the lack of variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel, although there were a few isolated complaints about room size and amenities.
