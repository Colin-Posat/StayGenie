HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Ski-In/Ski-Out SuitesLocated at the base of Heavenly Ski Basin in the Eldorado National Forest, Marriott’s Timber Lodge offers a cozy retreat with free Wi-Fi. The well-appointed suites feature a comfortable sitting area and a fully equipped kitchen or kitchenette, complete with a convenient microwave.Dining and Concierge ServicesIndulge in delectable meals at Fire and Ice, the on-site grill serving lunch and dinner. Need assistance? The hotel's concierge desk is at your service, providing valuable information on local attractions and activities to enhance your stay.Convenient LocationSituated just a 35-minute drive from the bustling Carson City, guests can explore a variety of shopping, dining, and entertainment options. Additionally, golf enthusiasts can tee off at the renowned Edgewood Golf Course, located only a mile away.Book your stay at Marriott’s Timber Lodge for an unforgettable experience amidst the beauty of South Lake Tahoe!
