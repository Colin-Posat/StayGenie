HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  CAD 25 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for CAD 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Friendly staff
• Convenient to key attractions
• Good breakfast

Areas for Improvement:
• Room cleanliness concerns
• Maintenance issues
• Value for money discrepancies
• Inconsistent amenities

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.3/10 - Guests had mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some mentioning rooms were clean while others noted issues like stains and dirt.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, with many praising the friendly and helpful staff.
• Location: 9.1/10 - The hotel's location was a standout feature, being close to key landmarks and attractions.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable by some, but there were complaints about small sizes, maintenance issues, and outdated decor.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Amenities received mixed feedback, with some guests highlighting positive aspects like breakfast, while others mentioned issues with hot water and lack of toiletries.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was high compared to the quality of services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Food and beverage offerings had mixed reviews, with breakfast service receiving both praise and criticism for variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 6.8/10 - Most guests had a moderately positive overall experience, with some pointing out specific issues that impacted their stay.
