HOTEL DESCRIPTION:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect. However, there were some complaints about cleanliness issues such as the aircon and fittings in one review.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated by most guests, with attentive staff and a friendly attitude. However, there were mentions of lackadaisical staff and unhelpful behavior in one review.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport, making it convenient for guests to explore the area.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were praised for being spacious, quiet, and well-decorated. However, there were some complaints about the aircon being loud and causing discomfort in one review.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with positive feedback on the gym, pool, and a library concept. However, there were mentions of limited mini bar options and a small pool size.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions were mixed regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the location and amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with praise for the central location and the pool. However, there were comments about limited breakfast options in one review.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing a cool decor, great music-inspired elements, and a convenient location.
