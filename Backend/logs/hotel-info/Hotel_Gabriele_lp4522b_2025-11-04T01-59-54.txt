HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Gabriele is perfectly situated in the Central Station district of Rome, just steps away from major attractions like Rome Termini Train Station, Santa Maria Maggiore, and Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station.Modern AmenitiesEnjoy the convenience of free WiFi, room service, and a tour desk at this 1-star hotel. The air-conditioned rooms are well-equipped with a desk, kettle, fridge, oven, safety deposit box, TV, and a private bathroom with a bidet.Nearby AttractionsExplore Rome easily with the hotel's proximity to Repubblica - Teatro dell'Opera Metro Station, Cavour Metro Station, and Quirinal Hill. Plus, with Rome Ciampino Airport just 14 km away, your travels are made simple.Experience the best of Rome at Hotel Gabriele.
