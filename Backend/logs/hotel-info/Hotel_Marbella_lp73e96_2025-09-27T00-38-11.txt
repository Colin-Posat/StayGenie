HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with AmenitiesHotel Marbella in Roma, Mexico City offers modern, air-conditioned rooms with flat-screen TVs, spacious work desks, and free WiFi. The king room even features a comfortable seating area for relaxation.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsSituated just 50 meters from Centro Medico hospital, the hotel is centrally located with Parque Delta shopping center and Pabellón Cuauhtémoc plaza within walking distance. Explore Mexico City's historic center, a short 15-minute drive away.Dining and Business FacilitiesIndulge in international cuisine at the on-site restaurant, or take advantage of the business center, bar, and meeting facilities. With room service available, Hotel Marbella ensures a comfortable and convenient stay.Book your stay at Hotel Marbella for a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and excellent service.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for MXN 376 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally clean, although some guests mentioned minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Friendly and helpful staff, with quick assistance for most issues. Some guests experienced delays in service.
• Location: 8.3/10 - Great location near key landmarks and accessible by public transportation. Noise levels from nearby roads might be a concern for light sleepers.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Comfortable and spacious rooms, with some maintenance issues reported such as loud AC units and faulty shower heads.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities, with highlights on cleanliness and functionality. Some guests missed having a variety of services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining options generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the location, cleanliness, friendly staff, and comfortable rooms.
