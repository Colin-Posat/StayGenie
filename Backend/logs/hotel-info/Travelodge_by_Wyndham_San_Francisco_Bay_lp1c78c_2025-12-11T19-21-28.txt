HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLess than 1.6 km from Fisherman's Wharf, Travelodge by Wyndham by the Bay offers easy access to San Francisco's top attractions. Explore the city hassle-free with popular destinations just a stone's throw away.Comfortable AccommodationRelax in air-conditioned rooms equipped with a cable TV, work desk, and coffee-making facilities. Enjoy the convenience of a private bathroom with a provided hairdryer. Stay connected with complimentary WiFi available in all guest rooms.Nearby LandmarksImmerse yourself in San Francisco's charm with the historic cable cars and the famous Lombard Street just minutes away. Explore Chinatown a short 10-minute drive from the motel, or venture to the iconic Golden Gate Bridge located 5 km from the property.Book your stay at Travelodge by Wyndham by the Bay for a memorable San Francisco experience.
