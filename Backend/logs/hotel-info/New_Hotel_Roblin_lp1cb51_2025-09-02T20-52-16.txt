HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationNew Hotel Roblin offers air-conditioned rooms and suites decorated by the renowned interior designer Flamant. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and free Wi-Fi, ensuring a comfortable stay for every guest.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy additional facilities such as photocopying and fax services, available at an extra cost. The hotel also features a stylish English-style bar, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring the nearby attractions.Prime LocationSituated just steps away from the prestigious shopping area of rue du Faubourg St-Honoré, New Hotel Roblin is in close proximity to iconic landmarks like the Champs Elysées, Opera Garnier, and the Louvre museum. Experience the best of Paris right at your doorstep.Book your stay at New Hotel Roblin now and indulge in luxury and convenience during your trip to the City of Lights.
