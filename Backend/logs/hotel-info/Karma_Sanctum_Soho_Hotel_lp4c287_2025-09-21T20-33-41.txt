HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Garden and CinemaThe Sanctum Soho Hotel in London's West End offers a unique experience with its rooftop garden featuring a 24-hour bar and an onsite cinema. Guests can unwind in style and catch a movie under the stars, creating unforgettable memories during their stay.Wild Heart Restaurant and Hot TubIndulge in exquisite dining at the Wild Heart Restaurant, where vintage-glamour décor meets modern British cuisine. After a delicious meal, guests can relax in the outdoor hot tub, enjoying the ultimate in luxury and comfort at this boutique hotel.Luxurious Rooms and Modern AmenitiesEach individually styled room at Sanctum Soho Hotel boasts a bespoke design, free WiFi, and a plasma flat-screen TV. With amenities such as a minibar, iPod docking station, and Templespa toiletries in the modern en-suite bathrooms, guests are treated to a truly lavish experience.Experience the epitome of luxury and style at Sanctum Soho Hotel - book now for an unforgettable stay in the heart of London's vibrant West End.
