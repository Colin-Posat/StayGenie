HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsThe Ahwahnee, nestled in Yosemite Village, offers 4-star accommodation just a stone's throw away from El Capitan. Indulge in the outdoor pool, relish delectable meals at the restaurant, and unwind in the shared lounge.Modern Amenities and Exceptional ServicesEnjoy the convenience of free WiFi, room service, and a 24-hour front desk. The hotel also provides an ATM, concierge service, and luggage storage for a hassle-free stay.Exciting Activities and RelaxationEngage in thrilling outdoor adventures like hiking, skiing, and fishing around Yosemite Village.
