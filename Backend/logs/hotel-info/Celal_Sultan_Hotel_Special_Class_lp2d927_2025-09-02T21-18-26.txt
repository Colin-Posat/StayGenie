HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Breathtaking ViewsSituated in Istanbul’s historic Sultanahmet district, Celal Sultan Hotel offers tastefully decorated rooms with free WiFi and a minibar. The highlight is the terrace with panoramic views of the city and the Bosphorus.Culinary Delights and Cozy AtmosphereGuests can savor Turkish cuisine on the open-air terrace overlooking the Bosphorus and the Hagia Sophia. The hotel also boasts a cosy bar serving refreshing cocktails.Convenience and HospitalityWith a 24-hour reception, Celal Sultan Hotel assists in arranging tours and offers services like airport shuttle, car rental, and laundry. Explore nearby attractions like Topkapi Palace, the Blue Mosque, and Hagia Sophia on foot.Book your stay at Celal Sultan Hotel for an unforgettable Istanbul experience.
