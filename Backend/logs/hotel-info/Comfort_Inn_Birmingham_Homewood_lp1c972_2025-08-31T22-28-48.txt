HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsComfort Inn Birmingham Homewood hotel is ideally situated near Interstate 65 and close to Birmingham-Shuttlesworth International Airport. Explore nearby attractions like the Birmingham Zoo and the Birmingham Civil Rights Institute museum.Fine Features and AmenitiesEnjoy complimentary high-speed Internet, free local and long distance calls, a seasonal outdoor pool, and a fitness center. Start your day with a hot breakfast including eggs, meat, yogurt, and more. Business travelers will appreciate the public computer, meeting facilities, and in-room amenities like refrigerators and ergonomic chairs.Guest Room Comforts and Additional ServicesRelax in spacious rooms equipped with conveniences like refrigerators, microwaves, and coffee makers. Non-smoking rooms are available, and some rooms feature sofa sleepers. Laundry facilities, a newsstand, and ample parking, including space for trucks and RVs, add to guest convenience.Book your stay at Comfort Inn Birmingham Homewood for a memorable and comfortable experience.
