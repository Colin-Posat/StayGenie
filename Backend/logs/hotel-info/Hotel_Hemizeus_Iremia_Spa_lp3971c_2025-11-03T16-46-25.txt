IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hotel Hemizeus & Iremia Spa in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
A damage deposit of CHF 500 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.

Please call the hotel from the free phone at the hotel information board by the train station. The hotel can pick you up for free between 08:00 and 18:00 (after 18:00 only if the porter is available.)

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for CHF 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 13 years old stay for CHF 60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Children from 14 years old to 16 years old stay for CHF 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
