HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Transportation LinksThe Patra is strategically situated near transportation links and entertainment options, making it a perfect choice for travelers looking for convenience and accessibility. The hotel's proximity to Ramkhamhaeng Airport Link Station provides easy connections to both the airport and central Bangkok, ensuring a seamless travel experience.Comfortable Accommodation with Modern AmenitiesThe rooms at The Patra are designed in a contemporary style, featuring wooden furnishings and a range of amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. From air conditioning and wireless internet access to a small kitchenette and room service, guests can enjoy a pleasant and convenient stay.Dining Options and RelaxationStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast at the hotel before exploring the city. After a day of sightseeing, indulge in a variety of dishes at Maneeya Restaurant, followed by a relaxing evening at Patra Lounge with a wide selection of drinks and soothing music.Book your stay at The Patra now for a memorable and convenient experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for THB 600 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
