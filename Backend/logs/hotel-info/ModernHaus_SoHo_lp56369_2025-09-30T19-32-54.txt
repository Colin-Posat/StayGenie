HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the epitome of modern luxury at ModernHaus SoHo, where rooms feature floor-to-ceiling glass walls, wood floors, and upscale amenities like Nespresso coffee and Le Labo bath products.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in culinary delights at Jumpin Jacks and savor craft coffee by day and signature cocktails by night, all while enjoying panoramic views of the Urban Garden and Avenue of the Americas.Rooftop OasisUnwind at the rooftop lounge, Jimmy, with breathtaking views of the Hudson River and a seasonal outdoor pool, offering a perfect retreat from the bustling city below.Ready to elevate your stay in SoHo? Book now at ModernHaus SoHo and immerse yourself in luxury and sophistication.

Guests must be 18 years of age or older to check-in.
Please note that the amount for the first night and taxes will be pre-authorized on the credit card.
Please note that the outdoor pool is seasonal, open from Memorial Day to Labor Day.
Hotel guest have exclusive access to the pool from 08:00 to 16:00 Monday-Friday and from 08:00-14:00 on Saturday and Sunday.
Rooftop wellness programming is offered to all guest on select days.
