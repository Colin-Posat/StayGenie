HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Hotel in the Heart of MontmartreLuxurious Amenities: Enjoy air conditioning, soundproofed rooms, and complimentary WiFi at Hôtel des Arts Montmartre. The rooms feature a TV, private bathroom, and some offer stunning views of Montmartre and Paris rooftops.Delicious Breakfast: Start your day with a delightful buffet breakfast served daily in the dining lounge.Convenient Location: Situated just a short walk from iconic landmarks like Sacré-Coeur Basilica and Moulin Rouge, our hotel offers easy access to Abbesses and Blanche Metro Stations. Explore Paris with ease from our centrally located hotel.Experience the charm of Montmartre at Hôtel des Arts Montmartre. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Parisian getaway.
