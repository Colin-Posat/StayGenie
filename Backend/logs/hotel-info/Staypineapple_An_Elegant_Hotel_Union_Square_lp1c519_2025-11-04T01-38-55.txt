HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Modern ComfortsStaypineapple, a 1913 historic hotel near Union Square, seamlessly blends modern decor with 1930s Victorian elegance. Each room features a flat-screen TV, en suite bathroom with a hairdryer, and a coffee machine for your daily brew.Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy a coffee and treats reception at check-in and stay connected with free WiFi throughout the hotel. Need assistance? Our concierge services and 24-hour reception are here to help. Plus, make use of the lobby computer for any business needs.Explore San FranciscoImmerse yourself in the vibrant city - visit Chinatown just 1 km away or take a leisurely 8-minute walk to Grace Cathedral. Book your stay at Staypineapple now and experience the best of San Francisco.
