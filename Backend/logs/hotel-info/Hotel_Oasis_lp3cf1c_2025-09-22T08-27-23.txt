HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms and Convenient LocationHotel Oasis, a contemporary gem near França Railway Station and Barcelona’s vibrant Born district, offers stylish air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV and private bathrooms.Seasonal Terrace with Outdoor PoolEnjoy the seasonal terrace with a refreshing outdoor pool, perfect for unwinding under the sun. Indulge in a variety of hot drinks and cocktails at the Oasis café-bar throughout the day.Proximity to Beach and AttractionsWith Barceloneta beach, lively bars, and the iconic Santa Maria del Mar Church just a stone's throw away, and easy access to Barceloneta Metro Station and Ciutadella Park, Hotel Oasis is your gateway to the best of Barcelona.Book now to experience the perfect blend of modern comfort and prime location at Hotel Oasis.
