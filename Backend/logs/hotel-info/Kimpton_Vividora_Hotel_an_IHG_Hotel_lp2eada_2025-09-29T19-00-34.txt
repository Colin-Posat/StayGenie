HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Mediterranean ExperienceElegant Design and Amenities: The Kimpton Vividora Hotel offers contemporary rooms with yoga mats, smart TVs, and exclusive bathrobes. Guests can enjoy a Mediterranean restaurant, cafeteria, terrace bar, and rooftop pool.Culinary Delights: Indulge in Mediterranean cuisine at Fauna restaurant or sip on locally sourced coffee and cocktails at Got Coffee &amp; Cocktails. Don't miss the free Social Wine Hour every evening.Prime Location: Situated in the Gothic Quarter, near popular attractions like Barcelona Cathedral and Plaza de Catalunya, this pet-friendly hotel is perfect for exploring the city. Book now for an unforgettable stay in Barcelona!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Kimpton Vividora Hotel, an IHG Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you a fee of 150€.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 38 per  day.
