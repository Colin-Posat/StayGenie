HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and Convenient LocationThe Anaheim Marriott hotel boasts marble bathrooms, flat-screen TVs, and a pool, providing a touch of luxury. Situated near the Anaheim Convention Center and Disneyland, it offers convenience for both business and leisure travelers.Culinary Delights at nFuse and StarbucksIndulge in California microbrews, wines, and cocktails at the on-site restaurant, nFuse. With a menu inspired by global cultures, it promises a delightful dining experience. Additionally, satisfy your coffee cravings at the Starbucks located within the hotel.Explore Anaheim and BeyondLocated in the vibrant Anaheim Resort District, the hotel is a short distance from attractions like Anaheim GardenWalk. Enjoy easy access to Huntington Beach and the Pacific Ocean for a well-rounded stay.Book your stay at Anaheim Marriott for a blend of luxury, convenience, and culinary delights.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
