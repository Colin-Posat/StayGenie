HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and Prime LocationHilton Bangkok Grande Asoke, nestled in vibrant Sukhumvit, boasts 325 modern rooms and suites with cutting-edge technology and free WiFi. Situated near top attractions and transportation, guests can easily explore the city's best offerings. The hotel is a stone's throw away from Asoke BTS and Sukhumvit MRT stations, ensuring convenient travel. Enjoy proximity to Queen Sirikit National Convention Centre and premier shopping destinations like Terminal 21 and The Emporium.Top-Notch Amenities for a Relaxing StayThe air-conditioned rooms feature flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms with bathtubs, safety deposit boxes, and minibars. Unwind at the fitness center, spa, or swimming pool. Benefit from services like laundry, meeting rooms, and a 24-hour front desk.Culinary Delights and EntertainmentIndulge in a seafood buffet at Atelier, Mediterranean cuisine at Tapas Vino, or pastries at Flourworx. Relax at inBlu with live music, snacks, and drinks in indoor and outdoor settings.Book your stay at Hilton Bangkok Grande Asoke for a memorable experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for THB 1,500 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
