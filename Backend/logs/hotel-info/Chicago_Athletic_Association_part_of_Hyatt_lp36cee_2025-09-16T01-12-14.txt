HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Bar with Stunning Views:Enjoy breathtaking views of Millennium Park from the rooftop bar at the Chicago Athletic Association, part of Hyatt. The perfect spot to unwind and take in the city skyline.Unique Dining Options:Indulge in a variety of dining experiences, from the popular Shake Shack to the seasonal cuisine and cocktails at Cindy's. Room service is also available for guests to enjoy delicious meals in the comfort of their own room.Entertainment and Wellness:Experience the social games room, grand ballroom with stunning stained glass windows, and modern fitness center. Stay connected with complimentary WiFi, explore the city with bike rentals, or have a friendly game at the indoor bocce ball court.Book now to experience the vibrant atmosphere and top-notch amenities at Chicago Athletic Association, part of Hyatt.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
