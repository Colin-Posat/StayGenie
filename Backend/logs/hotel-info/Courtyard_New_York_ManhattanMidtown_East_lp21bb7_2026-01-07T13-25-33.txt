HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
City Convenience and ComfortLocated in East Midtown Manhattan, Courtyard New York Manhattan offers a prime location just a short walk from Fifth Avenue and Central Park. The hotel features spacious rooms with city views, a work desk, and room service from nearby restaurants.Top-Notch AmenitiesGuests can enjoy free Wi-Fi in the lobby, a fitness center with city views, and a business center. The 12th floor boasts several shared seating areas, perfect for relaxing or socializing.Dining DelightsIndulge in breakfast items and Starbucks coffee at the on-site Table 866 restaurant. For other dining options, the hotel's proximity to Fifth Avenue provides a plethora of choices.Book your stay at Courtyard New York Manhattan for a perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and city living.
