HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesHotel Resol Trinity Kyoto offers a garden, a restaurant, and a bar for guests to enjoy during their stay. The 4-star hotel provides free WiFi, a 24-hour front desk, and convenient luggage storage space.Central LocationSituated in the heart of Kyoto, guests are only 700 meters away from the Kyoto International Manga Museum. Heian Shrine and Shoren-in Temple are also within a few kilometers from the hotel, allowing easy access to popular attractions.Nearby AttractionsExplore the vibrant surroundings with popular points of interest such as Samurai Kembu Kyoto, Gion Shijo Station, and Kyoto Shigaku Kaikan Conference Hall just a stone's throw away. Don't miss the chance to experience the best of Kyoto during your stay at Hotel Resol Trinity Kyoto.
