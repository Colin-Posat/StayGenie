HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Indulge in luxury at The Ritz-Carlton, Istanbul—a serene oasis in the heart of the city.Spa RetreatPamper yourself with a rejuvenating experience at our exquisite spa, offering a selection of massages, body treatments, and facials. Let the stresses of everyday life melt away in our tranquil environment.Culinary DelightsSavor international cuisine in our five distinct restaurants, or enjoy 24-hour room service for a dining experience in the comfort of your room. Relax by the poolside bar with a refreshing drink or indulge in a snack at our coffee shops.Modern ComfortsRetreat to one of our 239 air-conditioned rooms featuring plush Tempur-Pedic beds, state-of-the-art entertainment, and luxurious bathrooms with deep soaking bathtubs. Stay connected with complimentary wired and wireless internet access throughout your stay.Embark on an unforgettable getaway and book your room today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* The seasonal pool will be open from May 01 to October 31.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 6 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.

• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling the experience justified the cost while others sought additional features.
• Food and Beverage: 8.3/10 - Guests appreciated the diverse breakfast options and attentive restaurant service, though a desire for more variety was expressed.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to return to the hotel.
