• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and dining options was praised, making it convenient for exploring the area.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Guests enjoyed the spacious and comfortable rooms, but some noted issues like a hard and uncomfortable bed.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with mentions of a gym and pool but also complaints about limited parking and no included breakfast.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Guests had varying opinions on value for money, with some finding the price reasonable and others considering it a bit high for the offerings.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety in the food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing factors like the great location, friendly staff, and comfortable rooms.
