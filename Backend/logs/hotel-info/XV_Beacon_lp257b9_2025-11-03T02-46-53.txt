HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsTreat yourself to a stay in one of the 63 guestrooms at XV Beacon, featuring fireplaces and flat-screen televisions. Enjoy complimentary wired and wireless internet access, with additional entertainment from cable programming.Top-Notch AmenitiesThe hotel offers a fitness center, complimentary Wi-Fi, and concierge services, as well as babysitting services, on-site shopping, and a banquet hall. Guests can dine at the on-site restaurant with a bar/lounge or opt for 24-hour room service.Convenient LocationLocated in the heart of Boston, XV Beacon is within walking distance to significant landmarks like Boston Common and Faneuil Hall Marketplace.
