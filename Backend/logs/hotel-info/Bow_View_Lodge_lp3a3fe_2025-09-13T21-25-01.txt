HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Riverside Retreat with Modern AmenitiesScenic Views and Comfortable AccommodationsIndulge in picturesque river views from most guest rooms at the Bow View Lodge. Each room is equipped with essential amenities like a cable TV, seating area, and private bathroom for a comfortable stay.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind and rejuvenate with access to a swimming pool, fitness centre, and sauna nearby. The 24-hour front desk ensures your needs are met promptly, while the on-site launderette adds to your convenience.Explore Banff's BestConveniently located just a short walk from Banff city centre, our lodge offers easy access to attractions like the Banff Centre for Arts and Creativity and the Cave and Basin National Historic Site.
