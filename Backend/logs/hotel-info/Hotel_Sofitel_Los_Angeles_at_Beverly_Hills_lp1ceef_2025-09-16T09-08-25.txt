HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and AmenitiesSofitel Los Angeles at Beverly Hills offers 295 rooms on 10 floors, including 26 suites and two Presidential Suites. Each room features Sofitel's luxurious signature bedding, state-of-the-art technology, French bath amenities, and rain showers. Guests can indulge in the SoBed sleep system, 47” TV’s, Wi-Fi, 24-hour in-room dining, minibar, safe deposit box, and movies on demand.Wellness and EntertainmentExperience a 360 ° wellness journey at the hotel, including an outdoor heated pool, Sofitel Fitness, a spa with indulgent cosmetology treatments, and Le Salon celebrity hair salon. Enjoy the exclusive &quot;ALL Be Well&quot; in-room wellness program in partnership with Three Sages. Unwind with live performances and entertainment at Riviera 31 Lounge Bar, voted the best Hotel Bar in West Hollywood.Sustainability and RecognitionAs a pioneer in sustainability, Sofitel Los Angeles is Green Seal Certified and proudly holds Planet 21 Platinum and 5 Keys by Green Key certifications. Experience luxury, sustainability, and entertainment all in one place at Sofitel Los Angeles at Beverly Hills.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.5/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the hotel and rooms.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was a highlight for many guests, with friendly and attentive staff.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and shopping centers was greatly appreciated.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were spacious and well-kept, although some guests mentioned issues with room views and darkness.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - While some guests enjoyed the amenities, others noted a lack of food options and ongoing construction affecting the experience.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was somewhat high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience, emphasizing the location and friendly staff.
