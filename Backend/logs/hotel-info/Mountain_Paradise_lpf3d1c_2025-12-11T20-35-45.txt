HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Mountain Getaway with Stunning ViewsScenic Relaxation: Indulge in the sauna and steam room while taking in the tranquil surroundings and breathtaking Matterhorn Mountain views.Cozy Rustic Charm: Each room offers a balcony, seating area, and private bathroom. Some rooms boast unobstructed views of Winkelmatten and Mount Matterhorn, all elegantly furnished with wood-paneled ceilings and beams.Convenient Amenities: Enjoy a complimentary breakfast buffet in the spacious dining room with panoramic windows or on the outdoor terrace. Stay connected with free Wi-Fi and unwind with drinks at the on-site bar.Experience the perfect blend of luxury and nature at Mountain Paradise in Zermatt. Book now for an unforgettable mountain retreat.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that Zermat is a car-free resort. You can only drive as far as Täsch and proceed by shuttle train.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for CHF 70 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for CHF 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for CHF 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
