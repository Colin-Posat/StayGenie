Areas for Improvement:
• Limited balcony space
• Cleaning process concerns
• Pricing for amenities
• Lack of information on amenities

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the property. Some minor concerns were raised regarding cleaning processes and open doors after cleaning.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally praised, especially the friendly staff and quick check-in process. Some guests felt that more information could have been provided about hotel amenities during check-in.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was a significant highlight, being close to key attractions and offering beach access. Guests enjoyed the quiet location away from the busy town.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were spacious, comfortable, and generally clean. Some rooms had limited balcony space with views of the parking lot, which was a downside for some guests.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities like the pool area and fire pit were well-received. Some guests suggested improvements such as more chairs around the fire pit and better communication about hotel amenities.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on the value for money, with some feeling it was worth the cost and others expressing disappointment, particularly citing room balcony space and parking issues.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining options received positive feedback, with some guests mentioning the availability of coffee, s'mores, and community events. However, there were suggestions for more variety in food offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the unique atmosphere and amenities. Some mentioned minor concerns like crowded hot tubs and lack of detailed information during check-in.
