HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel Danieli, Venice offers elegant and spacious 5-star accommodations facing the Venetian lagoon, providing a truly exquisite stay for guests. Each room and suite is equipped with modern amenities such as LCD satellite TV, air conditioning, and luxurious Italian-marble bathrooms, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing experience.Spectacular Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at the rooftop restaurant, Terrazza Danieli, where guests can enjoy not only delicious cuisine but also breathtaking views of the lagoon. The combination of delectable dishes and panoramic scenery creates a dining experience that is both memorable and enchanting.Historic CharmSteeped in history and Venetian grandeur, Hotel Danieli showcases majestic architecture dating back to the 14th Century, offering guests a unique glimpse into the city's rich past. The blend of historic charm and modern luxury makes it a truly exceptional destination for travelers seeking a one-of-a-kind experience.Experience the epitome of luxury and history at Hotel Danieli, Venice. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Venetian getaway.

GROUPS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 150 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
