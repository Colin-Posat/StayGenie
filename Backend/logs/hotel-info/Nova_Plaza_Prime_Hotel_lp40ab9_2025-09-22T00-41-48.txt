HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 7 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
