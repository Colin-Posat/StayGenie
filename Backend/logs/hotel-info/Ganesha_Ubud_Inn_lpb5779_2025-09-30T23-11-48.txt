HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tranquil Oasis Amidst Rice FieldsNestled in Ubud, Ganesha Ubud Inn offers a serene escape surrounded by lush rice fields. Traditional Javanese Charm infuses each room, featuring wooden décor and private terraces. Guests can unwind with Nature Views from the terrace or garden, and stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi.Indulge in a delightful Culinary Experience, with daily breakfast served in the dining area or in-room. Conveniently located, the inn is a short drive from Monkey Forest and Ubud Art Market. Book your stay at Ganesha Ubud Inn for a peaceful retreat in the heart of Ubud.
