HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesInterContinental - Los Angeles Downtown offers a luxurious experience with an outdoor pool, on-site restaurant, and free WiFi throughout the property. The guest rooms are equipped with modern amenities like a TV, coffee machine, and complimentary toiletries. For added luxury, select rooms feature separate showers and bathtubs, as well as stunning views of Los Angeles.Rooftop Bar with Spectacular ViewsIndulge in a cocktail at Spire 73, the hotel's rooftop bar boasting the title of the tallest open-air bar in the Western Hemisphere. Enjoy panoramic views of Los Angeles while sipping on your favorite drink. Additionally, guests can dine at the property's on-site restaurants, Dekkadance and La Boucherie, offering a culinary experience with unparalleled views of the L.A. skyline.Convenient LocationLocated in the heart of Downtown Los Angeles, the hotel is within walking distance of Microsoft Theater and Staples Center. With Los Angeles International Airport just 18 km away, InterContinental - Los Angeles Downtown offers both luxury and convenience for your stay.Book now for an unforgettable stay at InterContinental - Los Angeles Downtown.
