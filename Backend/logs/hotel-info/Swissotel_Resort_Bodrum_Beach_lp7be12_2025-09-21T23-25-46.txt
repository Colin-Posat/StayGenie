HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Welcome to Swissotel Resort Bodrum Beach, your idyllic escape by the sea!Spa ParadiseIndulge in ultimate relaxation at our full-service spa, where rejuvenating massages, luxurious body treatments, and soothing facials await you.Dining DelightsSavor international cuisine at Café Swiss or enjoy 24-hour room service in the comfort of your room. Don’t miss our delightful buffet breakfast available daily.Comfort and ConvenienceUnwind in one of our 74 air-conditioned rooms, each featuring a private balcony and panoramic views. Enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi and a minibar for your convenience.Your seaside sanctuary is just a booking away—secure your room today and experience the beauty of Bodrum!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* The seasonal pool will be open from May to September.
* Reservations are required for massage services. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 11 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
