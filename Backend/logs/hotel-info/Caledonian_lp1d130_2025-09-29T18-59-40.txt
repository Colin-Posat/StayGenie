HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location:The Caledonian boasts a prime location on Barcelona's Gran Vía, just a short stroll from Plaza de Catalunya and Las Ramblas. With Universitat Metro Station only 100 meters away, exploring Gaudí's architectural wonders is a breeze.Comfortable Accommodation:Enjoy a cozy stay in the Caledonian's air-conditioned rooms, featuring charming decor and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms even offer picturesque views of Gran Vía, providing a true Barcelona experience.Exceptional Services:Experience top-notch hospitality with services like a 24-hour front desk, express check-in/out, and convenient luggage storage. Start your day right with a delicious buffet breakfast served on-site, or explore the nearby dining options for a taste of local cuisine.Book your stay at the Caledonian now for an unforgettable Barcelona getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
To take into account the heating and air conditioning system is centralized, therefore it cannot be modified. Air conditioning is available only for the summer months from April to September. Heating is available only for the winter months from October to March.
