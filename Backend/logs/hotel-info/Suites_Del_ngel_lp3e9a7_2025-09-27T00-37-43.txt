HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Suites with Kitchenettes and AmenitiesSuites del Ángel, located in the heart of Mexico City, offers modern suites with kitchenettes, air conditioning, and cable TV. Each suite features a seating area with a sofa bed, perfect for relaxation after a day of exploring the city. The kitchens are equipped with a hob, microwave, and fridge, providing guests with the convenience of preparing their own meals.Convenient Location for ExploringSituated just 240 metres from the Angel of Independence Monument, guests can easily access iconic landmarks and vibrant areas such as Paseo de la Reforma Avenue and Zona Rosa within minutes. Additionally, being 1.3 km away from Bosque de Chapultepec, guests have the opportunity to explore the various museums and attractions in the area.Free WiFi and Easy Airport AccessGuests can stay connected with complimentary WiFi available in all rooms. With Mexico City International Airport only 15 km away, traveling to and from the hotel is convenient for guests. Book your stay at Suites del Ángel for a comfortable and well-connected experience.
