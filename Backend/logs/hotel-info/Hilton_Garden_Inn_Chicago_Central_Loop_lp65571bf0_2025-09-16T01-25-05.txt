Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Overall, guests positively mentioned the cleanliness of the hotel, although some reviews noted minor issues like low water pressure.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was a highlight for most guests, with many mentioning the friendly and helpful staff. However, one review pointed out a lack of apology for an overcharging issue.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and transportation options received high praise from guests, making it a convenient choice for travelers.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were generally described as comfortable and well-designed, with some guests praising the luxury and elegance. However, there were complaints about noisy traffic and room maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews were received for amenities, with some guests appreciating the comfortable beds and spacious layouts, while others mentioned the limited spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on the value for money were divided, with some feeling the price was justified by the location and services, while others found it expensive, especially considering parking limitations.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Reviews on food and beverage services were generally positive, highlighting the breakfast options and restaurant quality, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing factors like convenient location and friendly staff. However, a few negative incidents impacted certain reviews.
