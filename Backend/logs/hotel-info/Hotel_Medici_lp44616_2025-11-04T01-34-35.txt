HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 19th Century BuildingHotel Medici offers a luxurious stay in an elegant 19th-century building, just a stone's throw away from Rome's renowned Villa Borghese Park. The modern and spacious rooms, coupled with complimentary Wi-Fi, ensure a comfortable and convenient stay for guests.Tranquil Garden OasisGuests can unwind in the hotel's serene garden oasis, complete with a charming patio where they can enjoy refreshing beverages during the spring and summer months. The perfect spot to relax after a day of exploring the bustling city.Convenient LocationConveniently situated just 100 meters from Porta Pia, Medici Hotel is surrounded by authentic restaurants and is a hub for city tours and public transportation. With iconic landmarks like the Spanish Steps within walking distance, and Termini Train Station a mere 1.5 km away, the hotel serves as the ideal base for exploring Rome.Experience luxury, tranquility, and convenience at Hotel Medici. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Roman getaway.
