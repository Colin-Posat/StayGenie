IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note, when booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.﻿

Please note that the pool is open from 10:00 to 20:30. All guests under 18 years must be accompanied by an adult.

Gluten-free breakfasts are available under previous request. You can use the Special Requests box when booking or contact the property.

Please note, reservations for 2 adults and 1 or 2 children can only be accommodated in the Family Room.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
