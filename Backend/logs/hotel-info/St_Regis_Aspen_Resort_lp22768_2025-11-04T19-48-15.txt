HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsSt. Regis Aspen Resort, nestled at the base of Aspen mountain, offers elegantly furnished rooms with modern amenities, including air conditioning, cable TV, and plush bathrobes.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a gastronomic experience with 5 unique dining options at the resort. From mountain cuisine at The Velvet Buck to handcrafted cocktails at Mountain Social, there is something to satisfy every palate.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind at the Remède Spa with a rejuvenating facial or massage, or take a dip in the outdoor pool. For adventure enthusiasts, the resort provides ski and bike equipment hire, along with proximity to skiing, golfing, and scenic trails.Experience the epitome of luxury and leisure at St. Regis Aspen Resort. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 60 per  day.

• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as spacious and comfortable, with some minor maintenance issues mentioned by guests.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the provided amenities satisfactory, with some mentioning the gym and pool, while others noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the price, with some feeling it was high for the services offered and others finding it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, though a desire for more variety was expressed by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
