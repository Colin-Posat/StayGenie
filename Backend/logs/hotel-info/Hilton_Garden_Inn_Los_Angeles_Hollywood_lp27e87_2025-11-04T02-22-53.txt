Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Guests generally appreciated the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas, with some minor mentions of maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Friendly and helpful staff received positive feedback, with some instances of staff going above and beyond to assist guests.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's proximity to key attractions and public transportation was praised, making it convenient for guests to explore the area.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Comfortable beds and well-equipped rooms were highlighted, with occasional noise issues mentioned for rooms facing the street.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The hotel offered satisfactory amenities, with positive mentions of the pool and convenience of in-room facilities, although some guests desired additional services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on pricing, with some guests feeling it was reasonable considering the location, while others found certain costs like parking to be high.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and dining options were generally well-received, though a desire for more variety was expressed by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, citing friendly staff, comfortable accommodations, and convenient location.
