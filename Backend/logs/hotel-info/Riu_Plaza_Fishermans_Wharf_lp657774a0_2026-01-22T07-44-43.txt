HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Riu Plaza Fisherman's Wharf offers spacious rooms with deluxe bedding, a 49-inch flat-screen TV, and ironing facilities. Enjoy a comfortable stay with amenities like a safety deposit box and tea/coffee-making facilities.Exciting AmenitiesRelax by the outdoor pool or gather around the fire pits for a cozy evening. Take advantage of the concierge service for wine and bay tours. Stay connected with free WiFi in the lobby and make use of the business center and event facilities during your stay.Delightful Dining OptionsIndulge in delicious American buffet breakfasts to kickstart your day. Throughout the day, enjoy the Grab &amp; Go menu or unwind at the hotel bar with a selection of drinks and snacks. Northpoint, the hotel's bar, is open from 6 pm to midnight for your convenience.Book your stay at Hotel Riu Plaza Fisherman's Wharf for a memorable experience near Pier 39!
