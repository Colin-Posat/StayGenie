HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Premium AmenitiesSonesta Select Las Vegas Summerlin offers stylish rooms with free WiFi and premium cable TV, just a short distance from the vibrant Las Vegas Strip. The modern interiors, large work desks, and convenient tea/coffee facilities ensure a comfortable stay.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind by the outdoor pool or enjoy a workout at the fitness center. Additionally, the hot tub provides the perfect way to relax after a day of exploring Las Vegas. Laundry facilities are also available for added convenience.Convenient LocationLocated near the Cheyenne Commons shopping center and only 5 miles from North Las Vegas Airport, Sonesta Select Las Vegas Summerlin offers a prime location for both leisure and business travelers.Book your stay at Sonesta Select Las Vegas Summerlin for a memorable Las Vegas experience!
