HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Miami Convention Center and BeachesAdjacent to the Miami Convention Center, Comfort Inn &amp; Suites Downtown Brickell-Port of Miami offers a prime location just minutes away from the stunning beaches of Miami.Comfortable Accommodations with City ViewsThe hotel provides spacious and air-conditioned rooms with cable TV, ironing facilities, and picturesque views of the city or Miami River, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Convenient Amenities and ServicesGuests can enjoy a daily breakfast, relax by the outdoor pool with a sundeck, and access weekday newspapers in the 24-hour lobby. Shuttle services to the Port of Miami and proximity to Miami International Airport add to the convenience of staying at this hotel.Experience comfort and convenience at Comfort Inn &amp; Suites Downtown Brickell-Port of Miami.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that rooms with river view may have limited availability and are subject to a charge.

Guests under the age of 21 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

The hotel charges an additional service fee of 15$ per night + taxes not included in the original room price and it must be paid upon arrival.

Resort fee includes breakfast, access to the pool, internet access, and gym access.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
