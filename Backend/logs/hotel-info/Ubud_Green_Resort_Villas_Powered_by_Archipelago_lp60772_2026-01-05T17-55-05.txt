HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Villas with Stunning ViewsModern Amenities and Relaxing SpaGourmet Dining and Convenient Shuttle ServiceExperience eco-friendly luxury just minutes away from Ubud Market at Ubud Green. Indulge in spacious villas with private balconies overlooking picturesque rice fields. Enjoy 5-star facilities including a top-notch spa and complimentary Wi-Fi.Modern villas at Green Ubud boast stylish decor, natural light, and premium amenities. Some even feature outdoor hot tubs for ultimate relaxation.Savor delectable Indonesian and international cuisine at Firefly Restaurant, offering floating breakfast and a range of beverages. Unwind with a garden stroll or book a tour at the on-site desk.Don't miss the convenience of a shuttle to Ubud and the option to rent a bike for exploring. Book your luxurious getaway at Ubud Green now for a memorable stay.
