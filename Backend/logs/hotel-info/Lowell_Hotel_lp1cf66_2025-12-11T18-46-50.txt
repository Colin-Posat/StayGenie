HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury AccommodationsThe Lowell, a historic 17-story building established in 1927, offers elegantly designed rooms and suites with marble bathrooms. Enjoy a nightly turndown service and complimentary New York Times newspaper during your stay.Charming AmenitiesExperience the charm of wood-burning fireplaces and beautifully landscaped terraces at The Lowell, providing stunning views of the city. Indulge in exquisite dining options overseen by renowned Restaurateur Charles Masson at Majorelle and Jacques Bar.Prime LocationLocated just steps away from Central Park and Madison Avenue, The Lowell offers easy access to luxury shopping destinations like Hermès and Barneys New York. Explore nearby cultural attractions such as Lincoln Centre and Carnegie Hall.Book your stay at The Lowell for a luxurious and memorable experience in the heart of Manhattan.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
