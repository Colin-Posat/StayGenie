HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Pratunam Market DistrictThe Seasons Bangkok Siam Hotel offers clean and comfortable accommodations with free WiFi access in the lobby. Situated near the vibrant Pratunam market district, guests can easily immerse themselves in the bustling local scene.Delicious Dining Options and Relaxing AmenitiesStart your day right with a scrumptious breakfast at the hotel's restaurant, serving a variety of Thai and international dishes. After a day of exploring the city, unwind at the cozy coffee shop or bar. The hotel ensures a relaxing stay with its 24-hour room service and basic amenities in the classically-styled rooms.Proximity to Key Attractions and Transportation HubsExplore nearby attractions such as Suanpakkad Palace and Suansantiparb Park, just a short walk away. With Victory Monument BTS Skytrain Station and a bus stop in close proximity, convenient transportation options are readily available for guests to discover Bangkok easily.Book your stay at The Seasons Bangkok Siam Hotel for a memorable and hassle-free experience.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 4 years old to 11 years old stay for THB 300 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 6 years old and over stay for THB 706 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
