HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Indoor Water Park with Thrill Rides and SlidesGreat Wolf Lodge Southern California boasts an 84-degree indoor water park with 16 thrilling rides and water slides, making it the perfect destination for water enthusiasts. Your reservation includes access to this exciting water park, ensuring endless fun for all ages.Family-Friendly Amenities and EntertainmentFrom specialty children's rooms to a kid's spa and character appearances, this lodge offers a wide range of family-friendly amenities. The XD movie theatre, bowling alley, and zero-depth-entry playgrounds cater to guests of all ages, promising a memorable stay for the whole family.Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated just 3.6 km from Disneyland and in close proximity to Disney California Adventure and Knott's Berry Farm, Great Wolf Lodge Southern California offers easy access to top attractions. With airports nearby, including John Wayne Airport and Los Angeles International Airport, your vacation begins the moment you arrive.Book your stay at Great Wolf Lodge Southern California for an unforgettable family getaway filled with excitement and relaxation.
