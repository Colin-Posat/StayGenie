HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Colonial-Style AccommodationThis former monastery, dating back to 1592, has been transformed into a luxurious colonial-style accommodation, offering a unique stay experience in Cusco's historical city center. The property's design, centered around a courtyard, provides a tranquil and elegant atmosphere for guests to enjoy.Fine Dining and Leisure ActivitiesIndulge in Andean cuisine at Tupay and Illariy Restaurants, savor fine wines at the lobby bar, and take advantage of room service for a relaxing dining experience. For those seeking adventure, the hotel offers leisure activities such as horse riding, mountain biking, and treks to Machu Picchu, ensuring a memorable stay filled with exploration and excitement.Warm Welcome and Convenient LocationUpon arrival, guests are greeted with fresh fruit and coca tea, a perfect introduction to the Peruvian hospitality. Conveniently located just 2 blocks away from Plaza de Armas Square, with easy access to the airport and train station, Belmond Monasterio Hotel is the ideal choice for a luxurious and culturally enriching stay in Cusco.Book your stay at Belmond Monasterio Hotel now for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Cusco.
