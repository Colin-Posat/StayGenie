HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in Miami BeachLocated in the heart of Miami Beach, Bentley Hotel South Beach offers a luxurious stay with top-notch amenities. Chic Rooms with City Views Each room is equipped with modern essentials like a kitchenette, flat-screen TV, and private bathroom. Upgrade to a room with a balcony to enjoy stunning city views. Relaxation and Convenience Unwind by the outdoor swimming pool or indulge in the hot tub. With concierge services and a 24-hour front desk, your comfort is our top priority. Explore Miami Beach Situated just steps away from South Pointe Park Beach, our hotel is also close to Lummus Park Beach and Fisher Island Beach. Don't miss the chance to experience the best of Miami Beach at Bentley Hotel South Beach.

• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and dining services received positive feedback, although some guests suggested more variety and an improvement in quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hotel's atmosphere, staff, and amenities.
