HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsInterContinental Tokyo Bay offers spacious rooms with stunning views of Tokyo Bay or Sumidagawa River. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as an LCD TV, fridge, and a luxurious bathroom with toiletries.Gourmet Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's 8 restaurants, including 'Chef's Live Kitchen' with sea views, 'La Provence' serving Southern French cuisine, and 'Zillion' offering healthy Italian dishes. Enjoy drinks at the trendy 'Hudson Lounge' and 'New York Lounge'.Convenient Location and ServicesLocated near Takeshiba Station and Hamamatsu-cho Train Station, the hotel provides easy access to major attractions. Benefit from 24-hour concierge services, free WiFi, and facilities like a hairdresser and private parking.Experience luxury and convenience at InterContinental Tokyo Bay. Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience!
