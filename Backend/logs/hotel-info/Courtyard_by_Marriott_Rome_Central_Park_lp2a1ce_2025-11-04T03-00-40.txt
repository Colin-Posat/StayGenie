HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations with Stunning ViewsModern Amenities and Convenient LocationDelicious Dining Options and Breakfast BuffetExperience the elegance of Courtyard by Marriott Rome Central Park, boasting rooms with breathtaking views of St. Peter’s Dome. Conveniently located near Gemelli Hospital and Sacro Cuore Catholic University, our hotel offers easy access to public transport for exploring Rome's iconic landmarks. Unwind in modern rooms equipped with essential amenities like Wi-Fi and enjoy a delicious buffet breakfast each morning. Indulge in delectable dishes at Moscati restaurant or grab a snack from the mini market. Book your stay now for a memorable Roman getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  EUR 25 per  day.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
Wired internet is available in the hotel rooms and costs  EUR  0.35 per  minute.
