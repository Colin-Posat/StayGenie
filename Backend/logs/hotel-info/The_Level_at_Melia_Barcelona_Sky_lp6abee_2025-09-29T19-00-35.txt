HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Spa and Wellness AmenitiesThe Serena SPA Wellness at The Level at Melia Barcelona Sky offers a lavish experience with a sauna, Turkish baths, bithermal shower, and a heated pool with whirlpool. Guests can also enjoy discounted massages, ensuring a relaxing stay.Modern Rooms with Premium FeaturesThe hotel's modern, air-conditioned rooms boast free WiFi, a 55-inch flat-screen satellite TV, a coffee maker, and a minibar. Each room includes a private bathroom with a rain-effect shower, bathrobe, and slippers, providing guests with ultimate comfort and convenience.Stunning Views and Personalized ServicesGuests can unwind on the 24th floor at Skyline 24 with a cocktail while enjoying panoramic city views. The hotel also offers a personalized reception with flexible check-out times and assistance in arranging tickets for excursions, shows, and car rentals.Book your luxurious stay at The Level at Melia Barcelona Sky for an unforgettable experience.
