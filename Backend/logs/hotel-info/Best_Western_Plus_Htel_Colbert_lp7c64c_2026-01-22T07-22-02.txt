GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Friendly staff
• Comfortable rooms
• Good location

Areas for Improvement:
• Poor dinner service
• Noisy surroundings

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.34/10 - While the rooms were described as clean, some guests mentioned noise issues that could impact the perception of cleanliness.
• Service: 8.81/10 - The service was often described positively, with friendly staff; however, there were some mentions of unhelpful restaurant staff.
• Location: 8.85/10 - Multiple reviews praised the hotel's proximity to the station and center.
• Room Quality: 9.3/10 - Rooms were repeatedly described as comfortable and well-equipped, with good amenities.
• Amenities: 9.33/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities provided, including a mini-kitchen; however, some desired more options.
• Value for Money: 8.79/10 - Generally, guests felt that the experience matched the cost, especially given the comfort of the rooms.
• Food and Beverage: 7.34/10 - The food quality was criticized in several reviews, specifically regarding dinner services.
• Overall Experience: 8.81/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience, despite some isolated issues.
