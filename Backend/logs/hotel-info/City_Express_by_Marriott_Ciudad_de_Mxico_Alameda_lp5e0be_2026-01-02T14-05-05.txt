HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Nearby AttractionsLocated in a prime spot in Mexico City, the City Express Ciudad de México Alameda offers easy access to popular attractions such as the Palace of Fine Arts, Metropolitan Cathedral, and Alameda Park. Guests can also explore various museums including the Anthropology Museum, all just a stone's throw away.Proximity to Major VenuesWith the hotel being only 40 minutes from Arena Ciudad of Mexico and 30 minutes from Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez, Palacio de los Deportes, and Foro Sol, guests have the advantage of attending events with great ease.Comfort and ConvenienceAfter a long day of sightseeing or attending events, guests can unwind in spacious rooms and enjoy delicious meals through the hotel's 24-hour room service. Experience relaxation and convenience at City Express Ciudad de México Alameda.
