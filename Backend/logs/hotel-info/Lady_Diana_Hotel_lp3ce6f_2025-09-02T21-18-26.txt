HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Diana hotel, nestled in the heart of Sultanahmet, offers spacious rooms adorned with wooden furniture and modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests.Tranquil Spa ExperienceUnwind at Lady Diana’s Turkish spa after a day of exploration. Indulge in rejuvenating treatments, relax in the sauna, or cleanse your body in the hammam for the ultimate pampering session.Spectacular Rooftop DiningExperience a culinary delight at the rooftop restaurant with stunning views of the iconic Blue Mosque. Enjoy a mix of Turkish and international cuisine while basking in the beauty of Istanbul’s skyline.Book now to revel in the opulence and convenience that Hotel Diana provides, just steps away from Istanbul's renowned landmarks.
