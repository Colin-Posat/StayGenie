HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Accommodations with High-Tech FeaturesHotel Beaux Arts Miami, located in Miami city centre, offers luxurious rooms equipped with an Apple iPad, espresso machine, and iPod docking station. Each room boasts a flat-screen TV, sofa, and breathtaking views, ensuring a comfortable and high-tech stay.Gourmet Dining Options and 24-Hour Room ServiceIndulge in a culinary experience at Beaux Arts with its three restaurants, including Boulud Sud and L'Atelier, offering a variety of cuisines. Start your day at Intermezzo coffee house or ThreeFortyFive for breakfast, and enjoy the convenience of 24-hour room service for a delicious meal anytime.State-of-the-Art Facilities and Relaxation AmenitiesGuests can take advantage of top-notch facilities like an NBA-approved basketball court, Jim McLean Indoor Golf School, and virtual bowling. Unwind at the outdoor pool, hot tub, and sauna for ultimate relaxation during your stay at Miami Beaux Arts.Book now to experience luxury, technology, and relaxation at Hotel Beaux Arts Miami!
