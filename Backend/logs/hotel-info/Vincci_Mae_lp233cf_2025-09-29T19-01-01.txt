HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Terrace with Plunge Pool: Enjoy breathtaking views of Diagonal Avenue and Tibidabo Mountain from the terrace featuring a refreshing plunge pool.On-Site Restaurant and Bar: Indulge in delectable cuisine and drinks without leaving the comfort of Vincci Mae.Chic and Comfortable Rooms: Relax in stylish rooms equipped with modern amenities like air-conditioning, flat-screen TV, and a minibar. The elegant private bathrooms offer complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer.Experience the essence of Barcelona at Vincci Mae. Book now to enjoy luxury and convenience at its best.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.1/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness positively, but some reviews highlighted issues with room cleanliness.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was appreciated overall, but there were a few complaints about maintenance and response times.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's central location was praised by most guests for its convenience and accessibility.
• Room Quality: 6.7/10 - Room quality received mixed reviews, with some guests noting comfortable beds but others mentioning maintenance issues and lack of amenities.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with positive comments on the rooftop restaurant and terrace, but some complaints about missing complementary water and spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location, while others found it too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, although suggestions for more variety were made by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, despite some specific issues mentioned in other categories.
