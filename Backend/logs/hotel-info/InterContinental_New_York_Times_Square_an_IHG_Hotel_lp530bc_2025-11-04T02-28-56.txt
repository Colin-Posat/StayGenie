HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Stunning ViewsExperience the breathtaking Manhattan skyline, Hudson River, and Broadway Theater District from our 36-story hotel. Each room boasts floor-to-ceiling windows, a flat-screen TV, and a spa-inspired bathroom for the ultimate relaxation.State-of-the-Art AmenitiesEnjoy modern comforts including a spacious work station, coffeemaker, minibar, and hairdryer in every room. Our 24-hour gym on the third floor offers panoramic views of 44th Street, equipped with top-of-the-line treadmills and free weights.Convenient Location and DiningWith a Subway Station a stone's throw away and Times Square within walking distance, explore the best of New York City right at your doorstep. Indulge in craft cocktails and a delectable menu at The Stinger Cocktail Bar &amp; Kitchen for a memorable evening.Book now for an unforgettable stay at InterContinental New York Times Square!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
