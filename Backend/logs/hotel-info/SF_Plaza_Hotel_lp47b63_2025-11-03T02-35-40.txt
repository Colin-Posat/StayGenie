• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Cleanliness ratings were mixed with some guests praising the cleanliness while others reported cleanliness issues such as dirty rooms and hair in bathrooms.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Service received mixed reviews with compliments for friendly staff and helpful reception, but some complaints about cleaning service, delayed requests, and unhelpful staff.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The location was a popular positive aspect with guests appreciating its proximity to key landmarks and business areas like Chinatown and CBD.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Room quality ratings varied with guests mentioning comfortable beds but also reporting issues like old properties, uncomfortable chairs, and unclean rooms.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Amenities like gym and pool were appreciated by some guests, while others mentioned the absence of desired amenities like spa services and breakfast.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location, while others found it expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed feedback with positive comments on breakfast and restaurant services, but also suggestions for more variety.
• Overall Experience: 6.6/10 - Overall guest experiences ranged from positive recommendations to strong criticisms about cleanliness, room quality, and lack of amenities.
