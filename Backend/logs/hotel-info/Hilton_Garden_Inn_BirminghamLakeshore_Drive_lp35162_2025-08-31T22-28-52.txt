HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Modern AmenitiesJust outside historic downtown Birmingham, the Hilton Garden Inn Birmingham / Lakeshore Drive offers free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and an indoor pool. The spacious rooms include work desks, MP3 connections, and kitchen appliances for added convenience.Fitness and Business FacilitiesGuests can stay active at the on-site fitness center and utilize the hotel's business center equipped with free Wi-Fi. Whether traveling for leisure or work, the hotel provides facilities to meet various needs.Dining Options and Nearby AttractionsIndulge in a delicious breakfast at the Great American Grill or unwind with drinks at the Pavilion Lounge. With the Birmingham Zoo and Oxmoor Valley Golf Course just a short drive away, there's plenty to explore near the hotel.Book your stay at Hilton Garden Inn Birmingham / Lakeshore Drive for a comfortable and enjoyable experience.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.2/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness. Some guests mentioned clean lobby, while others reported issues with room cleanliness and dirty fitness room.
• Service: 7/10 - Overall mixed feedback on service. Positive mentions of helpful staff, but complaints about delayed dinner service and cold indoor pool.
• Location: 8.1/10 - High rating for location. Guests appreciated the proximity to stores, food, and event venues.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally spacious and comfortable, with some reports of cleanliness issues like hair in the shower and toilet.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities, with positive mentions of a smoking area, pet-friendly policy, and digital key access.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money. Some guests felt the price was fair, while others mentioned issues with breakfast charges and lack of included services.
• Food and Beverage: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on food and beverage. Some guests were disappointed with limited breakfast options and no dinner menu.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to visit again.
