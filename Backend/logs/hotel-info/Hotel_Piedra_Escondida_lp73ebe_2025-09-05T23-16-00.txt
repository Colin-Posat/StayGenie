HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Accommodation in TulumSeaside Views and Comfortable Rooms: Hotel Piedra Escondida offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, private bathrooms, and sea views from each room's patio. You can also enjoy the safety of a safety deposit box and some rooms even feature a balcony.Convenient Amenities: The hotel provides a garden, free private parking, shared lounge, and terrace. Guests can indulge in delicious meals at the on-site restaurant and take advantage of services like room service, concierge assistance, and currency exchange.Ideal Location: Situated just 2.5 km from Las Palmas Beach, with Tulum Archeological Site and Parque Nacional Tulum nearby, Hotel Piedra Escondida is the perfect retreat. Book now for a memorable stay in Tulum!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7/10 - While some guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, others reported issues like a dirty beach and finding a cockroach in the room.
• Service: 8/10 - Staff was generally described as helpful and professional, with multiple mentions of accommodating service. Some guests appreciated the patience and customer-oriented approach.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location by the beach and proximity to restaurants and shops was highly praised by guests, providing a secluded and relaxing atmosphere.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were considered comfortable with good sea views, although some guests noted issues with the bed size and maintenance.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Positive mentions of amenities like breakfast and beach access, with some criticism regarding the beach cleanliness and lack of available espresso.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed opinions on pricing, with some feeling the hotel was not worth the cost while others considered it reasonable for the location and services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - The restaurant received positive feedback for its menu and delicious food, although there were some suggestions for improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the beautiful setting, helpful staff, and beachfront location.
