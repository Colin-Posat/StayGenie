HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated just a 5-minute walk from Sumiyoshi and Nishi Ojima subway stations, APA Hotel Tokyo Ojima offers easy access to transportation, making it a perfect choice for travelers looking to explore Tokyo.Modern AmenitiesThe hotel features newly refurbished rooms equipped with an LCD TV, video-on-demand, personal fridge, and comfortable slippers. Guests can enjoy free WiFi throughout the property and experience a restful sleep on the original mattress designed for optimal weight distribution.Accessible AttractionsWith its proximity to Kinshicho and JR Kameido Station, guests can easily reach Tokyo Skytree and other popular attractions. Additionally, the hotel provides convenient access to airport limousine buses for Narita and Haneda international airports, ensuring a hassle-free travel experience.Book your stay at APA Hotel Tokyo Ojima for a comfortable and convenient Tokyo experience!
