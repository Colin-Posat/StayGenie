HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the elegance and charm of Waldorf Astoria New York, where luxury meets convenience in the heart of the city.Unwind in LuxuryRelax at our full-service spa, indulging in rejuvenating massages, or simply savor the tranquil ambiance that surrounds you.Culinary DelightsWith three exquisite restaurants, including Lex Yard, and 24-hour room service, dining here is a sumptuous experience. Don’t forget to start your day with a delightful breakfast served daily!Modern ConveniencesStay connected with complimentary wireless internet, and enjoy our well-equipped business center and meeting facilities, perfect for both leisure and corporate travelers.Embrace the allure of New York City and make your reservation at Waldorf Astoria New York today!
