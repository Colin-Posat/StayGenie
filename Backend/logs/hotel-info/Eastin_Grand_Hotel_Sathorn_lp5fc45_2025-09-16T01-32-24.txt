HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Convenient LocationThe Eastin Grand Hotel Sathorn offers a luxurious stay with modern amenities and a direct connection to Surasak BTS Skytrain Station. The outdoor infinity-edge pool, well-equipped fitness center, and multiple dining options provide guests with a truly indulgent experience.Elegant Rooms and Culinary DelightsThe spacious air-conditioned rooms are designed with a contemporary touch and feature top-notch facilities including a flat-screen TV, minibar, and hot-water showers. Guests can savor Chinese, Cantonese, and Italian cuisines at the hotel's restaurants, promising a culinary journey to remember.Additional Services and FacilitiesIn addition to the exceptional accommodation and dining options, the hotel offers free WiFi, parking, a business center, and services like currency exchange and babysitting. With laundry, ironing services, and a variety of beverages available, every guest's needs are catered to seamlessly.To experience luxury and convenience at its best, book your stay at Eastin Grand Hotel Sathorn today.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of THB 1000 is required on arrival. This will be collected as a cash payment. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full in cash, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that the name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property or guest may be asked to pay with alternative method.

Please note that all guest rooms and hotel public areas are non-smoking. Dedicated smoking areas are provided for guests who wish to smoke in front of the hotel. Guests are not permitted to smoke in hotel guest rooms and hotel public areas as per Thai law and are subject to a fine if they do so.

Please note that an additional charge for extra bed for Superior Double or Twin Room and Superior Sky Double or Twin Room is THB1,766 per room per night. and an extra bed is charged at THB2,943 per room per night for Executive Superior Sky and Executive Deluxe Double Room. If an extra bed is required, the charges are to be paid upon check-in.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 15 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 16 years old and over stay for THB 2,943 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
