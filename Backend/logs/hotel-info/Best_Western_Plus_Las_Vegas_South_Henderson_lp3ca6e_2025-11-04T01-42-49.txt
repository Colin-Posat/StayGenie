Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.4/10 - Mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some guests praising the hotel's cleanliness, while others reported issues such as dirty rooms and stained carpets.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service was generally positive, with guests appreciating friendly staff and quick maintenance responses. However, there were complaints about rude staff members at check-in.
• Location: 7.9/10 - Positive feedback on the hotel's location, close to key areas and convenient for travelers. Some guests enjoyed the proximity to shopping and dining options.
• Room Quality: 6.7/10 - Mixed feedback on room quality, with comments on spacious rooms and comfortable beds, but also reports of maintenance issues like malfunctioning refrigerators and dirty bathrooms.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Satisfactory amenities overall, with mentions of the gym and pool as positives. However, some guests noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Varied opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was reasonable due to the location, while others found it too high for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally received positive feedback, but some guests desired more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some specific complaints about various aspects.
