HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and River ViewsLocated in the heart of the Historic District overlooking the scenic Savannah River, the River Street Inn offers a unique blend of history and comfort.Elegant Guestrooms and AmenitiesIndulge in the charm of hardwood floors, cozy bath robes, and luxurious canopy beds in select rooms. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for US$20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.

• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with praise for the gym and pool, but some guests noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some feeling it was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
