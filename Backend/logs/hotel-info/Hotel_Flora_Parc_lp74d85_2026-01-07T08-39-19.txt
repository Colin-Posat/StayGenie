HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spacious Accommodations for FamiliesHotel Flora Parc offers large, air-conditioned rooms with balconies, wooden floors, and modern bathrooms, perfect for families looking for comfort during their stay.Convenient LocationSituated in central Castelldefels, just a short drive from Barcelona and close to the beach, guests can easily explore the vibrant city or relax by the sea.
