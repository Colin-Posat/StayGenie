HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the enchanting charm of Istanbul at the Adamar Hotel - Special Class, your gateway to the city's rich history and stunning sights.Stylish RoomsIndulge in one of our 25 uniquely decorated guestrooms, each equipped with minibars and LCD televisions. Enjoy complimentary wireless internet and designer toiletries that create a perfect blend of comfort and luxury.Gourmet DiningSatisfy your culinary cravings at our on-site restaurant, or opt for the convenience of 24-hour room service. Begin your day with a delightful complimentary buffet breakfast and unwind with a refreshing drink at one of our two bars.Prime LocationNestled just steps away from iconic landmarks like the Basilica Cistern and Hagia Sophia, our hotel makes it easy to explore the best of Istanbul. With a roundtrip airport shuttle available, your journey begins seamlessly.Book your unforgettable stay at Adamar Hotel now and immerse yourself in the beauty of Istanbul!
