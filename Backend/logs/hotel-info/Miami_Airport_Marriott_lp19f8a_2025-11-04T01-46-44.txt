HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThis Miami Airport Marriott offers a luxurious stay with a free transfer service to the nearby airport, ensuring a hassle-free arrival and departure. The hotel boasts modern rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and spacious work areas, providing both comfort and convenience for guests.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind and rejuvenate at the outdoor heated pool or stay active at the well-equipped fitness centre, offering a range of exercise options. Whether you prefer a leisurely swim or an intense workout, this hotel caters to all your relaxation and fitness needs.Dining ExcellenceIndulge in a culinary experience at the on-site restaurants, Cane Fire Grille and Champions Sports Bar, serving delicious meals throughout the day. For added luxury, guests can upgrade to M Club Lounge access, where they can enjoy a blend of work-friendly spaces and socializing opportunities.Book your stay at Miami Airport Marriott now and experience the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and luxury during your visit to South Florida!

• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money with some guests feeling the breakfast was overpriced, impacting their overall perceived value.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Positive feedback on food quality and service in the restaurant, with some guests praising the variety and service level.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, despite some specific criticisms, and would recommend the hotel to others.
