HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Japanese Traditional Design and CourtyardHotel Niwa offers an elegant accommodation with touches of Japanese traditional design, including a Japanese-style courtyard. Bright light filters through paper sliding screens in the modern rooms, creating a serene atmosphere.On-Site Dining and Relaxation LoungeIndulge in delicious Japanese kaiseki meals at Yukuri or enjoy casual French delicacies at Dining Lieu. The hotel also features a Relaxation Lounge with massage chairs, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring the city.Convenient Amenities and LocationGuests can take advantage of the free WiFi, fitness centre, and 24-hour business centre. With its proximity to JR Suidobashi Station and Jimbocho Subway Station, Hotel Niwa Tokyo is the ideal base for your Tokyo adventures.Book now to experience the perfect blend of Japanese tradition and modern comfort at Hotel Niwa Tokyo!
