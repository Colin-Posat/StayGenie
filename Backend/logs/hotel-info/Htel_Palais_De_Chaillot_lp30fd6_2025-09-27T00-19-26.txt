HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Comfort and ConvenienceExperience the epitome of modern comfort at Hôtel Palais De Chaillot, just steps away from the iconic Trocadéro. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and air-conditioned rooms with private bathrooms.Delectable Dining OptionsIndulge in a delightful buffet breakfast featuring fresh juices, homemade pastries, and a selection of hot dishes. A surcharge of EUR 50 applies for early check-in and/or late check-out. All requests for late arrival are subject to confirmation by the property.
