HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for AED 150 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Proximity to Dragon Mart
• Free parking
• Free water
• Friendly and helpful staff
• Cleanliness
• Ease of access to public transport

Areas for Improvement:
• Expensive food menu
• Reception staff conduct
• Room maintenance issues
• Housekeeping service
• Smell of cigarette in lobby
• Perceived favoritism towards certain nationalities

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.1/10 - Rooms are generally described as clean, adding to the positive impressions of the hotel.
• Service: 7.6/10 - Staff, particularly Mr. Venu, received high praise for their professionalism, although there were a few concerns regarding respectfulness and housekeeping.
• Location: 7.9/10 - Proximity to Dragon Mart and the bus station was frequently cited as convenient, although some guests found the location to be less ideal.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - The rooms were equipped with standard amenities, but there were reports of maintenance issues like broken telephones and fridges.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Amenities such as free parking and free water were appreciated, but guests suggested the addition of USB points and noted the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.4/10 - Many guests felt the hotel provided good value, especially given its amenities and staff service, though there were some comments about higher priced food.
• Food and Beverage: 6.5/10 - Overall satisfaction with food services, but with a few guests finding the options expensive or lacking variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - The majority of guests seem to have had a very positive experience, with strong recommendations to stay at the hotel.
