• Value for Money: 5.5/10 - Varied opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling the hotel did not meet expectations for the price paid, while others found the location justifies the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 6.3/10 - Mixed reviews of the food and beverage services, with some guests praising the breakfast and restaurant, while others mentioned limited food offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Overall positive guest experiences, with many guests recommending the hotel to others despite some challenges in specific categories.
