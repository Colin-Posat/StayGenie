IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to check-in before 23:00. Please note that the room may be resold after that time. If you cannot change your arrival time, please contact the hotel before 23:00 local time.

Please note that hotel guests have access to free parking for the duration of their stay only.

Please note that there is a free private shuttle service from Orly Airport that operates from 05:00 to 13:30 every 30 minutes with a break between 12:00 and 13:00. Guests are kindly requested to call the hotel upon arrival after baggage pick-up. Please note the shuttle has 8 spaces. The shuttle operates on call only between 15:00 and 23:30.

Please note that children aged between 5 and 10 years benefit from a reduced breakfast price of EUR 5.95 per child per day.

Early check-in is possible at an extra charge and upon availability.Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
