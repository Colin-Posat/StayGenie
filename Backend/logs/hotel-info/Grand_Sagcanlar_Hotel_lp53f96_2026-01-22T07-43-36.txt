HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Marmara Sea Views can be enjoyed from the roof terrace of Grand Sagcanlar Hotel in Yenikapı district. The hotel's proximity to Yenikapı Ferry Terminal and key attractions like Sultanahmet Square and Taksim Square make it an ideal choice for travelers.Modern Rooms with Free WiFi and satellite TV offer a comfortable stay for guests. The contemporary decor and well-equipped bathrooms enhance the overall experience.Convenient Location and Services such as the transfer service to Ataturk International Airport and easy access to interconnected metro lines add to the convenience. Additionally, the ticket service provided by the hotel simplifies travel arrangements for guests.Experience comfort, convenience, and breathtaking views at Grand Sagcanlar Hotel. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 2 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
