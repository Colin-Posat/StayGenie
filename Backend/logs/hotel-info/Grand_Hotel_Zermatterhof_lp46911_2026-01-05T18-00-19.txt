HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Heritage Hotel in ZermattExquisite Dining Experiences: Indulge in award-winning cuisine at Alpine Gourmet Prato Borni, Brasserie Lusi, and saycheese! Restaurant. Enjoy daily afternoon tea and live piano music at Stars Bar.Ultimate Relaxation: Unwind in the Vita Borni spa with a pool and sauna. Rooms offer stunning mountain views, with some overlooking the iconic Matterhorn. Enjoy complimentary tea, coffee, and minibar treats.Exclusive Events and Amenities: Host events in modern banquet rooms, relax in the smoker's lounge, and explore the hotel garden and terrace. Member of “Preferred Hotels &amp; Resorts” and “Swiss Deluxe Hotels”.Book your stay at Grand Hotel Zermatterhof for a truly unforgettable experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 9 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 100 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 8 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 9 years old to 12 years old stay for CHF 140 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for CHF 200 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
No parking available.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.
