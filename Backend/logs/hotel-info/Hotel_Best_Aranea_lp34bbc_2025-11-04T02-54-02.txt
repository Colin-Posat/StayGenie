HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Sagrada FamiliaHotel Aranea is conveniently situated just a short walk from Gaudi’s iconic Sagrada Familia, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore Barcelona's famous landmarks.Comfortable and Modern AccommodationsThe hotel offers modern, air-conditioned rooms with double-glazed windows, ensuring a peaceful stay. Guests can relax and unwind in the cozy rooms equipped with satellite TV, a safe, and a minibar.Fantastic Amenities and ServicesEnjoy complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the property, a lovely roof terrace with stunning city views, and a delicious buffet breakfast at the Aranea restaurant. The 24-hour reception staff are also on hand to assist with any inquiries, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable stay.Book your stay at Hotel Aranea now to experience the best of Barcelona!

Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.

When booking 3 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

For reservations of more than 8 persons, or more than 7 nights, a prepayment is required. Once the reservation has been made a representative will contact the guest to arrange this prepayment.

Please note that when booking a rate where payment is due before arrival, the property will send detailed payment instructions and a link to a secure payment platform.
