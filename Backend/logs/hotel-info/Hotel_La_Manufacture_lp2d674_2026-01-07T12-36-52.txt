HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm in Central ParisLuxurious Accommodations: Step into a 19th-century building and experience the comfort of soundproofed guest rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi. Each room boasts modern decor and essential amenities like a private bathroom and air conditioning.Convenient Facilities: Start your day with a delicious buffet breakfast at Hotel La Manufacture before exploring the vibrant Butte aux Cailles area just a short walk away. Need to stay connected? Utilize the computer with internet access available for guests after breakfast.Prime Location: Located near Place d’Italie Metro Station, our hotel provides easy access to the bustling Latin Quarter, ensuring you can make the most of your Parisian adventure.Book your stay at Hotel La Manufacture now for a memorable Parisian getaway!
