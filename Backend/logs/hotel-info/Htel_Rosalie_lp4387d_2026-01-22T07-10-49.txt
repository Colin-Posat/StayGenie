HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Design and TerracesThis recently renovated design hotel in the Gobelins district offers a unique experience with its duplex terrace and private shaded rooftop terrace, providing stunning views of the Eiffel Tower and Parisian cityscapes.Modern AmenitiesEach room is equipped with free Wi-Fi, auto-controlled air conditioning, a flat-screen satellite TV, tea and coffee making facilities, an ensuite private bathroom, and exceptionally comfortable bedding. Additionally, private parking is available on-site for your convenience.Convenient LocationSituated just 120 meters from Place d’Italie Metro Station, guests can easily explore the vibrant Rue Mouffetard and its array of restaurants and market stalls. The hotel's proximity to Paris's main Asian quarter, the Cité de la Mode et du Design, and other cultural attractions makes it an ideal choice for travelers. Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience.
