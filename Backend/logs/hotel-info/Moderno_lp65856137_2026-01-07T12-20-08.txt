HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Las RamblasHotel Moderno is conveniently situated just 20 meters from Barcelona's iconic Las Ramblas, providing easy access to the heart of the city.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsEach room at Hotel Moderno is designed to ensure a cozy stay with amenities like heating, air conditioning, soundproofing, cable TV, a fridge, and a private bathroom, offering a comfortable retreat after a day of exploring.Convenient Services and AmenitiesGuests can take advantage of the City Tour offer to discover Barcelona, a ticket service for convenience, and complimentary use of computers in the lobby. The 24-hour front desk, along with express check-in and check-out options, cater to the needs of every traveler.Experience the best of Barcelona at Hotel Moderno. Book your stay now for a memorable visit.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note, when booking more than 8 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Please note the name on the credit card used must be the same as the (main) guest name on the booking. If the guest staying at the hotel is not the one making the booking, or when making a payment in advance with a company credit card, the credit card holder has to send a signed letter to the hotel authorizing the charge, specific for the price of the reservation, along with copy of the credit card and/or an ID document.

Cots are available on request and subject to availability (please make a request in the Comments box when booking).

Please note in the Comments Box whether you would prefer a window with Ramblas view or a quiet, interior view.

The restaurant is open every day.
