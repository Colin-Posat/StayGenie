HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesGoodwood Park Hotel offers a range of luxurious amenities including 2 pools, a spa, and free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel. Guests can also enjoy free private parking, making their stay convenient and stress-free.Dining Options GaloreWith 6 dining options to choose from, guests at Hotel Goodwood Park can indulge in a variety of culinary experiences. From western and local dishes at The Coffee Lounge to European specialties at Gordon Grill, there's something to satisfy every craving.Prime Location for Shopping and NightlifeConveniently located near Far East Plaza Shopping Centre and Orchard MRT Station, guests can easily explore the vibrant shopping and nightlife scene of Orchard Road. For a night out, Clarke Quay and Boat Quay are just a short taxi ride away.Book your stay at Goodwood Park Hotel now to experience luxury, convenience, and culinary delights all in one place.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for S$ 83.93 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
