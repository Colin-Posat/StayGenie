HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant allure of Las Vegas at Downtown Grand, your gateway to the heart of the city!Entertainment Galore: Try your luck at our thrilling casino, relax by the outdoor pool, or energize yourself in our state-of-the-art 24-hour fitness center. Dive into an unforgettable experience that caters to all your recreational needs.Culinary Delights: Savor delightful American cuisine at Freedom Beat and discover our variety of dining options including three restaurants and a cozy coffee shop. Wind down your day with a refreshing drink at the bar or the inviting poolside bar.Convenience at Your Fingertips: Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi, take advantage of our business center, and enjoy dry cleaning services. With comfortable rooms featuring pillowtop beds and modern amenities, your stay will be both relaxing and productive.Book your unforgettable getaway at Downtown Grand Las Vegas today!
