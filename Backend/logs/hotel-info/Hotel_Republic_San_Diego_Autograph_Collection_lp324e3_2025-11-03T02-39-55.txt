HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Vibrant Lounge, Restaurant, and Art GalleryHotel Republic San Diego, a Marriott Autograph Collection, is a vibrant destination just a short walk from Little Italy. The property boasts a lively lounge, a stylish restaurant, and a contemporary art gallery, offering guests a unique and artistic experience during their stay.Pet-Friendly Rooms and Gourmet DiningGuests can enjoy eco-friendly accommodations that welcome pets, along with amenities like a flat-screen HDTV and gourmet in-room dining. The hotel's focus on sustainability and comfort ensures a relaxing and enjoyable stay for both guests and their furry companions.Topside Terrace Kitchen &amp; Bar and 24-Hour Fitness CentreIndulge in Californian cuisine at the hotel's dining venues, including the Topside Terrace Kitchen &amp; Bar with its stunning city views. For those looking to stay active, the 24-hour fitness centre provides modern equipment for a convenient workout session.Experience the perfect blend of art, dining, and relaxation at Hotel Republic San Diego. Book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway!

People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
