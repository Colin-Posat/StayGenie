HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationIndulge in comfort at The Sebali Resort, nestled in Ubud just a stone's throw away from major attractions. Each air-conditioned room is elegantly furnished with modern amenities and a private terrace for a relaxing stay.Relaxation and DiningUnwind by the outdoor swimming pool or pamper yourself with a soak in the hot tub. Enjoy delicious meals at the on-site restaurant, and don't miss the daily breakfast options ranging from à la carte to American classics.Convenience and ExplorationWith a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, and free WiFi, your stay is made seamless. Explore the area with ease by renting a bicycle or car, perfect for visiting nearby landmarks like Saraswati Temple and Monkey Forest Ubud.Book now for an unforgettable experience at The Sebali Resort!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children are not allowed.
Adults  stay for Rp 750,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
