HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Stunning Views and Convenient LocationThe Select Inn Ryde offers stunning views of the adjacent golf course from floor-to-ceiling windows. Located on Victoria Road, this hotel is just a short drive away from Macquarie University and Sydney city centre.Modern Amenities for ComfortAll rooms at The Select Inn Ryde are equipped with air-conditioning, soundproof glass, a lounge area, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Guests can also enjoy tea and coffee making facilities, a refrigerator, and a private bathroom for their convenience.Scenic Surroundings and Relaxing StayWith some rooms overlooking the picturesque Ryde-Parramatta Golf Course, guests can enjoy a peaceful and relaxing stay at The Select Inn Ryde. Book your stay now for a delightful experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform The Select Inn Ryde in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note that there is a 1.5% charge when you pay with a Visa or Mastercard credit card.
Please note that there is a 3% charge when you pay with an American Express credit card.

If you expect to arrive after hours, please contact the property in advance to arrange after hours check-in.

During COVID19 our Reception and Operational hours have changed to:

Monday to Saturday 8am-8pm
Sunday and Public Holidays 8am-5pm

All rooms are non smoking, if evidence of smoking is found an AUD $200 fee will be charged.

Please note that this property is a two level motel with no lift access, access via stairs only.

PAYMENTS
If your booking has not already been pre-paid, we will require payment at check in. No cash payment accepted at the property.
