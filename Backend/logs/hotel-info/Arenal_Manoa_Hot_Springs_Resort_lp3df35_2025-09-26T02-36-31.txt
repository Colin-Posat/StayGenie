HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience a tranquil retreat at Arenal Manoa &amp; Hot Springs Resort, where comfort meets luxury in the heart of La Fortuna.Rejuvenating Spa ExperienceIndulge in the ultimate pampering with our exceptional spa services, offering massages, body treatments, and invigorating facials designed to refresh your body and mind.Inviting AmenitiesDive into relaxation with 2 outdoor swimming pools and soothing hot springs, or take advantage of complimentary Wi-Fi and room service to elevate your stay.Culinary DelightsSavor delicious meals at our on-site restaurant or enjoy a refreshing drink at the bar/lounge while basking in the serene poolside atmosphere.
