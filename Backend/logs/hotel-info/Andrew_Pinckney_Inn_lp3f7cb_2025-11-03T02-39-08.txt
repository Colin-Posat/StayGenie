HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Boutique HotelStep back in time at this charming boutique hotel built in 1840, just 1 km from the College of Charleston. Enjoy a complimentary breakfast on the rooftop terrace and stay connected with free WiFi throughout the property.Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in Gilchrist &amp; Soames toiletries, unwind with an iHome docking station, and relax in a small seating area in every guest room. The Italian Travertine porcelain tiles and granite countertops in the bathrooms add a touch of elegance. Upgrade to a suite for a cozy sofa bed.Convenient ServicesTake advantage of the 24-hour reception and knowledgeable concierge desk to plan your activities, from horse-drawn carriage tours to museum visits. Valet parking is available for a daily fee, ensuring a hassle-free stay.Ready to experience historic charm and modern comfort? Book your stay at Andrew Pinckney Inn today!
