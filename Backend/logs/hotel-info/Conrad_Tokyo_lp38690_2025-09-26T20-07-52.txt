HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation and Award-Winning DiningConrad Tokyo, located in the vibrant heart of Tokyo near Ginza, offers spacious rooms with modern decor and stunning city views. Indulge in award-winning dining experiences at the 5 on-site restaurants, including the renowned China Blue.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind at the 29th-floor spa with panoramic city views or take a dip in the picturesque indoor pool. Stay active at the spacious fitness center overlooking Tokyo. The Mizuki Spa offers a range of rejuvenating massages for ultimate relaxation.Convenient Location and Exceptional ServiceWith easy access to Shiodome Subway Station and JR Shimbashi Train Station, exploring Tokyo is a breeze. Enjoy the convenience of being a short ride away from Tokyo Big Sight Exhibition Centre and the Imperial Palace. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for ¥10,120 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
