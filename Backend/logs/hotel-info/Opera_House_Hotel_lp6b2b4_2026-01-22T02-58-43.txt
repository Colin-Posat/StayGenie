HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the epitome of comfort and style at the Opera House Hotel in the Bronx. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, microwave, and refrigerator. The custom desk and chair provide a perfect workspace, and the ihome systems with USB plugins offer convenience for all guests.Convenient LocationSituated just 160 meters from the subway station, our hotel offers easy access to explore New York City. With Yankee Stadium, The Bronx Zoo, and other attractions just a stone's throw away, you'll never run out of things to do during your stay.Exceptional ServicesFrom the business center to the 24-hour front desk and concierge services, we ensure that your every need is met promptly and efficiently. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our dedicated staff is committed to making your stay unforgettable.Book your stay at the Opera House Hotel now and indulge in a blend of luxury, convenience, and top-notch service.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
