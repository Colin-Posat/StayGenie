HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Retreat in UbudMandapa, A Ritz-Carlton Reserve offers a tranquil escape just a short drive from Downtown Ubud, surrounded by lush greenery and stunning views.Exquisite AccommodationsIndulge in air-conditioned villas and suites featuring modern amenities, including a flat-screen TV, minibar, and luxurious en suite bathrooms with separate bath and shower facilities. Enjoy complimentary WiFi and 24-hour butler service for a truly pampering experience.Exceptional Facilities and DiningRelax by the outdoor pool, rejuvenate at the spa, or stay active at the fitness centre. With multiple dining options like Kubu Restaurant and The Pool Bar, guests can savor a variety of cuisines in beautiful settings.Experience luxury and serenity at Mandapa, A Ritz-Carlton Reserve - book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 726,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 1,452,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 7.7/10 - Mixed opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high relative to services, while others found it justified given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Overall positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, though a desire for more variety was noted by some.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hotel and its amenities, recommending it to others.
