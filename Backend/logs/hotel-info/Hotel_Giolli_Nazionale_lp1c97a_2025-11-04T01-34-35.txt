HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location:Hotel Giolli, situated on Via Nazionale near Roma Termini Railway Station, offers easy access to Rome's top attractions like the Opera House and Trevi Fountain.Comfortable Rooms:Guests can enjoy spacious rooms at Giolli Hotel, featuring amenities such as Wi-Fi, air conditioning, satellite TV, and minibars. The eco-friendly wood parquet floors and balconies with street views add a touch of luxury to the stay.Excellent Services:With a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, and assistance in booking museum tickets, the hotel ensures a seamless experience for guests. Start your day with a buffet breakfast in the panoramic dining hall and unwind at the bar or lounge.Experience the best of Rome at Hotel Giolli - book your stay now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 30 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
