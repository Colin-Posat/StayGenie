HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsSoHo 54 Hotel, a 4-star gem in the heart of NYC, offers a serene garden and a charming terrace for guests to unwind. The rooms boast modern amenities including flat-screen TVs, air conditioning, and some even feature a private balcony for a touch of luxury.Convenience at Your FingertipsGuests can enjoy the convenience of a business center, an ATM, and complimentary newspapers during their stay. The 24-hour front desk staff are always on hand to assist with any inquiries, ensuring a seamless experience for all visitors.Prime LocationLocated just a short stroll from Bloomingdales and with easy access to LaGuardia Airport, SoHo 54 Hotel is the perfect choice for travelers seeking both convenience and comfort in the bustling city.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.3/10 - Several guests reported issues with cleanliness, including dirty rooms, bathrooms, and bed linens.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Though some guests praised the friendly staff, others encountered rudeness and poor hospitality.
• Location: 9.3/10 - The hotel's prime location near key landmarks and public transport received widespread appreciation.
• Room Quality: 6.3/10 - Guests expressed dissatisfaction with room quality, citing issues like small rooms, noisy surroundings, and outdated interiors.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests appreciating daily cleaning and complimentary water, while others noted limited offerings.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with concerns over WiFi quality, room size, and lack of breakfast options.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Positive feedback on the availability of coffee and the hotel's proximity to food options, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Despite mixed reviews in individual categories, most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
