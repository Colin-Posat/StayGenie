HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsBeverly Wilshire, a 4 Seasons Hotel, offers elegant rooms with modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs, free WiFi, and stunning views of Rodeo Drive or the Hollywood Hills. The property's Mediterranean-style pool and hot tub with private cabanas provide the perfect spot for relaxation.Fine Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at the hotel's various dining options. From the michelin star-rated steakhouse CUT Lounge to the Mediterranean flair of THE Blvd and the casual Pool Bar and Café, guests can savor a range of delectable dishes and beverages in picturesque settings.Wellness and LeisureUnwind at The Spa with rejuvenating massages and organic treatments, or stay active at the fitness centre equipped with top-notch gym facilities. Complimentary fresh fruit, juice, and water are available, while meeting centers and cabanas by the pool offer convenience and luxury.Book now to experience the epitome of luxury at Beverly Wilshire, a 4 Seasons Hotel.
