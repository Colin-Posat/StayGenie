Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.8/10 - Guests had mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some noting clean rooms and others mentioning issues like pungent chemical smell and room odor.
• Service: 6.5/10 - Service received mixed feedback, with guests appreciating the helpful staff in some instances and criticizing the front desk employees in others.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised, with guests highlighting the convenience and proximity to key attractions.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms received mixed feedback, with positive comments on comfort and cleanliness, and negative remarks on the lack of amenities like a microwave.
• Amenities: 6.2/10 - Guests had varied opinions on amenities, with some appreciating the restaurant and others mentioning the absence of a swimming pool or spa services.
• Value for Money: 5.6/10 - Some guests felt the price was not justified due to issues like limited elevator access and maintenance challenges, impacting the overall value.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, with positive feedback on the restaurant and wait staff.
• Overall Experience: 6.2/10 - Guests had a mixed overall experience, with positive mentions of location and food overshadowed by concerns about cleanliness and service.
