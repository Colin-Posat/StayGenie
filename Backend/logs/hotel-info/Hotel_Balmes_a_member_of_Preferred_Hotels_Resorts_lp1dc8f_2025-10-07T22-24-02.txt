HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in BarcelonaLocated just a 5-minute walk from the iconic Passeig de Gràcia Boulevard, Hotel Balmes offers convenience and style in central Barcelona.Modern Rooms and AmenitiesEnjoy comfortable and contemporary rooms with parquet floors, private bathrooms, flat-screen TVs, and free Wi-Fi. The hotel also boasts a swimming pool, gym/fitness center, and the uniquely decorated B Bar showcasing African art.Concierge Services and Nearby AttractionsTake advantage of the 24-hour reception staff to book tickets for tours and shows. Explore nearby attractions such as Gaudí’s Casa Batlló, La Pedrera, Las Ramblas, and the Gothic Quarter, all within walking distance.Experience the best of Barcelona at Hotel Balmes - book your stay now!
