HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 6 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  GBP 19 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for £30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Location: 6.5/10 - The location had mixed feedback, convenient for some due to proximity to transport, but noisy for others.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Guests had varying opinions on room quality, praising cleanliness and beds but mentioning maintenance issues like outdated features and plumbing problems.
• Amenities: 7/10 - The hotel amenities were deemed sufficient by most guests, with no specific amenities standout or lacking based on the provided reviews.
• Value for Money: 7.9/10 - Many guests felt the hotel offered good value for money, although some pointed out high prices for certain services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and dining services were generally appreciated, notwithstanding a few comments on variety and cost.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - The overall experience was positive for most guests, with several willing to recommend the hotel despite some criticisms.
