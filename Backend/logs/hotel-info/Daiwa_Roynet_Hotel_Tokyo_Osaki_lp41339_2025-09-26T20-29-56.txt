HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location &amp; ConnectivitySituated just 200 meters from JR Osaki station, Daiwa Roynet Hotel Tokyo Osaki provides easy access to explore Tokyo. Guests can reach popular destinations like Shibuya and Shinjuku within minutes via the efficient train network.Modern AmenitiesThe hotel offers free Wi-Fi and wired internet in rooms and the lobby, ensuring guests stay connected throughout their stay. Rooms are equipped with an LCD TV with satellite channels, a private bathroom with a bathtub, and a refrigerator for added convenience.Fitness &amp; RelaxationFor those looking to stay active, the hotel features a well-equipped gym and an indoor swimming pool. Guests can also unwind with a variety of massage services available on-site.Book your stay at Daiwa Roynet Hotel Tokyo Osaki for a seamless blend of comfort and convenience.
