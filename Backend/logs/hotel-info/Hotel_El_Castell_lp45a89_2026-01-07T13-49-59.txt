HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Panoramic Views and Tranquil SurroundingsHotel El Castell offers a serene escape with its hillside location on the outskirts of Barcelona, providing breathtaking panoramic views and a peaceful setting surrounded by lush forests.Convenient Location and Accessible AmenitiesSituated just a short drive from Barcelona El Prat Airport, guests can easily reach the hotel. Additionally, the proximity to a sports centre and spa offers opportunities for relaxation and recreation within walking distance.Elegant Rooms and Dining ExperienceThe spacious and stylish rooms at Hotel El Castell provide comfort and luxury, complemented by amenities such as satellite TV. Guests can indulge in exquisite dining at the popular restaurant Blanc&amp;Negre, or enjoy a relaxed meal at the on-site café, with the option to dine al fresco on the charming terrace.Book your stay at Hotel El Castell for a memorable blend of luxury, convenience, and natural beauty.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible).

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.
