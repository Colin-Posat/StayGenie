HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationThe Hotel de Bellevue is perfectly located between Montmartre and Parc de la Villette, offering easy access to key attractions in Paris. Just a short walk from Gare du Nord and close to a Metro station, exploring the city is a breeze.Comfortable AccommodationExperience a peaceful stay in soundproofed guest rooms equipped with modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV, free WiFi, and a work desk. The elevator ensures convenience for all guests.Delightful Dining OptionsStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast in the charming 18th-century cellar, featuring a variety of fresh and savory options. The hotel's 24-hour reception and available seminar rooms add to the convenience of your stay.Book your stay at the Hotel de Bellevue now and enjoy a memorable experience in the heart of Paris.
