HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationSituated next to Fabra i Puig Station, Ibis Barcelona Meridiana provides easy access to Plaza Catalunya in just a 10-minute Metro ride.Modern AmenitiesEnjoy air-conditioned rooms equipped with flat-screen satellite TV, ensuring a comfortable stay. The 24-hour bar offers a variety of snacks and drinks for your convenience.Entertainment and ShoppingAdjacent to the Heron City Shopping Centre, our hotel allows guests to explore various dining options, cinemas, and even a gym. Additionally, indoor public parking is available at an extra cost.Ready to experience Barcelona with us? Book your stay at Hotel Ibis Barcelona Meridiana now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that parking in the public car park can not be pre-paid.

Children up to 12 years old will stay for free but the meal (breakfast, dinner, lunch) is not included.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
