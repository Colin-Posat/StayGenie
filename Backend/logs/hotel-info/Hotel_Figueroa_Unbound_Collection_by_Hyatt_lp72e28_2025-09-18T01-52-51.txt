HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Accommodation in the Heart of Downtown LATranquil Outdoor Pool and Gourmet DiningHotel Figueroa offers a serene outdoor pool oasis amidst the bustling city, perfect for relaxation. Indulge in Mediterranean cuisine at Breva or sip on creative cocktails at The Alta Room, all within the hotel's historic Spanish architecture.Elegant Rooms with Modern AmenitiesEach room at Hotel Figueroa boasts rich décor and features free high-speed WiFi, William Roam bath amenities, and cutting-edge technology like smart TVs and iPads for guest requests. Enjoy a comfortable stay just steps away from the vibrant LA Live entertainment complex.Convenient Location and Exceptional ServiceSituated across from Microsoft Theatre &amp; Staples Centre, and a short walk from the Staples Center, the hotel offers unmatched convenience for guests. Experience personalized service and luxury at the only independently owned luxury hotel in downtown LA.Book your stay at Hotel Figueroa for a truly unforgettable experience in Los Angeles.
