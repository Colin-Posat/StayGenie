HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Convenient LocationHotel East 21 Tokyo boasts 8 dining options, a sprawling outdoor pool, and easy access to Toyocho Subway Station. The elegantly decorated rooms with free WiFi and European flair provide a comfortable stay.Convenient Transportation and Nearby AttractionsLocated just minutes away from Toyocho Station, guests can effortlessly explore key destinations like Otemachi and Nihonbashi. With Tokyo Disney Resort a shuttle ride away and iconic landmarks like Tokyo Sky Tree Tower within reach, the hotel offers the perfect blend of leisure and convenience.Premium Amenities for a Relaxing StayIndulge in The East Health Club's fitness facilities, indoor pool, and rejuvenating sauna. For a fee, unwind in the outdoor pool or enjoy personalized concierge services. End your day with breathtaking city views at Panorama Lounge or savor delectable cuisines at the on-site restaurants.Book your stay at Hotel East 21 Tokyo for an unforgettable experience!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Advance reservations are required to use the free shuttle to Tokyo Disney Resort. Reservations can be made 24 hours a day from our hotel's official website.

The paid The East Health Club's fitness room and indoor pool is not available for children under 18 years of age.
Please check the official website for details such as The East Health Club's business hours and fees.

Please note that the on-site parking requires additional fees for large vehicles such as buses. Reservation is required for parking for vehicles that have a height over 6.9 feet.

The outdoor pool is only open during the summer and is subject to an additional fee.
The outdoor pool is only available to children aged 3 years and older. The night pool is only available to guests aged 16 years and older.
