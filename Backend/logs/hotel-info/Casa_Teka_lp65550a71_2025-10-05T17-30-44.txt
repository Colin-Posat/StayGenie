HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation:Experience comfort and style at Casa Teka in Tulum. Our 4-star hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, private bathrooms, and room service. Some rooms even feature a kitchen for your convenience.Relaxing Amenities:Unwind in our garden, terrace, or with free bike rentals to explore the beautiful surroundings. Enjoy a delicious meal at our on-site restaurant or sip a drink at the bar. Our 24-hour front desk and currency exchange services ensure a seamless stay.Convenient Location:Located just steps away from South Tulum Beach, Casa Teka is also close to the Tulum Archeological Site. With a paid airport shuttle service to Cozumel International Airport, your travel arrangements are hassle-free. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for US$42 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 16 years old stay for US$68 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
