HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationLiberty Guesthouse Maldives offers air-conditioned rooms with terraces, some boasting stunning sea views. Each unit is elegantly furnished with a seating area, ensuring a comfortable stay.Family-Friendly AmenitiesFamilies are catered to with family rooms, indoor play area, and children's playground. The property also provides a baby safety gate, ensuring a worry-free stay for parents.Dining and RecreationIndulge in a variety of dining options, including American cuisine at the on-site restaurant. CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 2 years old to 6 years old stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$3 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 10 years old stay for US$25 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 11 years old to 17 years old stay for US$35 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for US$15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
