HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Savoy Hotel, with its rich history dating back to 1889, offers 195 guest rooms and 73 suites exquisitely decorated in Edwardian or Art Deco style. Each room features elegant marble bathrooms and many boast stunning views of the River Thames, ensuring a comfortable and sophisticated stay.Culinary ExcellenceIndulge in a culinary journey at The Savoy's renowned restaurants and bars. From the three Gordon Ramsay restaurants to the iconic American Bar and the stylish Beaufort Bar, guests can savor a variety of exquisite dining experiences. Don't miss the chance to enjoy Afternoon Tea in the Thames Foyer, a quintessentially British tradition.Prime LocationConveniently located in the heart of London, The Savoy provides easy access to top attractions like The British Museum and The Royal Opera House. With Covent Garden just a short walk away and designer shopping districts within reach, the hotel offers the perfect blend of luxury and convenience.Book your stay at The Savoy Hotel for an unforgettable experience in the heart of London.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 16 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  GBP 60 per  day.
