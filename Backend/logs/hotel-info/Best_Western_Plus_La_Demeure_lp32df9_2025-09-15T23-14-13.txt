HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Haussmanian BuildingSet in a typical Haussmanian building near Rue Mouffetard, Best Western Plus La Demeure offers a charming stay just steps away from Jardins des Plantes and the Natural History Museum. The property boasts free WiFi, a wellness and fitness centre with a multi-sensory shower, and the option for relaxing massages.Elegant Rooms and SuitesExperience comfort in soundproofed rooms and suites featuring air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and Nespresso coffee machines. Each room includes a private bathroom with free toiletries, an electric kettle, and a complimentary bottle of water upon arrival.Delicious Breakfast and Relaxing LoungeStart your day with a buffet breakfast or opt for an express breakfast at a reduced rate. The ground-floor lounge, adorned with warm colors and Parisian decor, invites you to unwind with a drink.Book your serene getaway at Best Western Plus La Demeure now!
