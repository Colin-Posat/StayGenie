HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Elegance: Step into a 16th-century palatial home turned 5-star hotel, nestled in a natural forest with yew, cherry, pine, and holly trees.Luxurious Accommodations: Experience stylish rooms with breathtaking views and enjoy the top-notch facilities and services expected from a 5-star establishment.Culinary Delights: Indulge in exquisite dining at the chic restaurant, adding a touch of gastronomic pleasure to your stay.Immerse yourself in the charm of Luces, a quaint Asturian village, and explore its unique granaries and traditional cottages. With easy access from the A-8 Motorway, a memorable stay in Lastres is just a drive away. Book now for an unforgettable experience.
