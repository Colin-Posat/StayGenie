HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsLocated just 2 blocks from the Magnificent Mile, the Ivy Boutique Hotel in downtown Chicago offers spacious rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows and upscale amenities. Each room features modern dark wood furniture, automatic window drapery, a 42-inch flat-screen TV, and an iPod docking station. Some rooms even include a separate living room for added comfort.Dining and EntertainmentGuests can indulge in morning breakfast and evening cocktails at the DiVine Lounge. For breathtaking city views, the outdoor Sky Terrace is the perfect spot to relax during warm weather months.Wellness and EventsEnjoy complimentary access to CorePower Yoga and Planet Fitness during your stay. With over 3,000 square feet of meeting and banquet facilities, the hotel is ideal for both leisure and business travelers.Book your stay at Ivy Boutique Hotel for a luxurious and convenient Chicago experience.
