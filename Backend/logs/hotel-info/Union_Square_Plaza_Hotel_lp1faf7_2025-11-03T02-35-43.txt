HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in Theatre DistrictLocated in San Francisco’s vibrant Theatre District, Union Square Plaza Hotel is just steps away from popular theaters and Union Square shopping. The famous Powell Street Cable Car is conveniently close, offering easy access to explore the city.Comfortable Accommodations with Free WiFiEach guest room is thoughtfully furnished with a satellite TV and hardwood desk, ensuring a comfortable stay for every guest. Stay connected with complimentary WiFi throughout the hotel.Convenient Concierge ServicesEnjoy the convenience of 24-hour reception and concierge services to assist with all your needs during your stay.
