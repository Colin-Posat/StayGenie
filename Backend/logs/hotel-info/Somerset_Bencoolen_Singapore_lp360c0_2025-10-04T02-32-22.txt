HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Apartments in Central Business DistrictModern Interiors and Convenient AmenitiesThe spacious apartments at Somerset Bencoolen boast stylish modern interiors flooded with natural light. Guests can enjoy fully equipped kitchens, home entertainment systems, and free internet access in every room.Recreational Facilities and ServicesGuests can unwind at the outdoor pool, rooftop garden, or well-equipped fitness centre. Families will appreciate the children's playground, while travelers can make use of services like pocket WiFi rentals, laundry, and dry cleaning. Complimentary housekeeping services ensure a comfortable stay, with the convenience of a taxi stand at the property entrance.Prime Location and AccessibilityLocated just steps away from Bencoolen MRT Station, the hotel offers easy access to Dhoby Ghaut MRT Station, Singapore Art Museum, Orchard Road, and Changi International Airport. Experience luxury and convenience at Somerset Bencoolen.

Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.

People no matter the age stay for S$ 129.60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
