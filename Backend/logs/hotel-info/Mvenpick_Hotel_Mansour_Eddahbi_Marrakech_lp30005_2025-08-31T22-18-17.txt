HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationIndulge in comfort and style at Mövenpick Hotel Mansour Eddahbi Marrakech, offering elegant rooms with modern amenities. Unwind in a cozy seating area and pamper yourself in a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries.Relaxation and WellnessTake advantage of the spa center, sauna, and outdoor pools for a rejuvenating experience. HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 4 years old and over stay for MAD 450 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, with only a few minor mentions of cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service received high praise from guests, with special mention of staff members who went above and beyond to assist.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's prime location near key landmarks and public transport was highlighted as a major positive aspect by guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Although rooms were generally comfortable, some guests reported minor issues such as maintenance problems or insufficient lighting.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfaction with amenities varied, with some guests praising the gym and pool while others noted a lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.7/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was justified by the location while others found it too high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, although some suggested an improvement in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing a combination of factors like cleanliness, service, and location.
