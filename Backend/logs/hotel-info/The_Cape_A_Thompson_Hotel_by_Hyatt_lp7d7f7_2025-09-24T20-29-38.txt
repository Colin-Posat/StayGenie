HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesThe Cape in Cabo San Lucas offers two swimming pools, an outdoor spa, and two restaurants on-site. Guests can indulge in ocean views from their private balconies and enjoy the convenience of concierge services.Relaxation and RecreationUnwind in the Jacuzzi, stay active in the fitness center, or sip on cocktails at the bar. The resort also provides a tour desk for exploring the area and venues for various events.Convenient LocationLocated near the Marina of Cabo San Lucas and just 33 km from San Jose Downtown, The Cape offers a prime location for both leisure and business travelers.Book your stay at The Cape now for a luxurious and convenient experience in Cabo San Lucas!
