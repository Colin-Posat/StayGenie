HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 3 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  AUD 65 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.4/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the rooms and common areas.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Staff were highly appreciated for their friendliness and professionalism, though some noise complaints were noted by a few guests.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's central location near key landmarks and easy access to transportation options received high marks from guests.
• Room Quality: 8.8/10 - Spacious, comfortable rooms with modern amenities were consistently praised, with some guests noting noise issues in certain rooms.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Some guests enjoyed the free minibar contents, while others commented on noise disturbances and lack of certain amenities like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some finding the price reasonable given the location, while others felt services did not justify the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and dining services generally well-received, though pricing concerns and limited variety were mentioned by some guests.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and expressed a willingness to return, citing various positives like location, staff, and room quality.
