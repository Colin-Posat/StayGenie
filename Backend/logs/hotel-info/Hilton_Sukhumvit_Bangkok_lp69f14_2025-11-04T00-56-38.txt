Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 5 years old and over stay for THB 1,765.50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests praising the gym and pool, while others expressed a desire for additional amenities like spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with some guests suggesting more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
