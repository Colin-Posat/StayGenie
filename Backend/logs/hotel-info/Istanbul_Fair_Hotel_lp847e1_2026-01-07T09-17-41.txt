HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation near Ataturk AirportComfortable Rooms with AmenitiesThe simply and modernly decorated rooms at Airport Inci Hotel offer a range of amenities including air conditioning, flat-screen satellite TV, safe box, electric kettle, and a minibar. Guests can also enjoy the convenience of a private bathroom with complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer.Convenient Location for Business and TravelLocated just 3 km from Ataturk Airport, with easy access to CNR Expo Fair Centre and Tüyap Fair Convention and Congress Centre, our hotel is ideal for business travelers. Additionally, with universities and public transportation nearby, exploring Istanbul is a breeze.Exceptional Services and DiningStart your day with a delicious open buffet breakfast and take advantage of our 24-hour front desk. For added convenience, we offer airport transfer services upon request. Book your stay at Airport Inci Hotel for a comfortable and hassle-free experience.
