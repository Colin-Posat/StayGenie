HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious 5-Star Mont Cervin Palace in ZermattRelaxation and Indulgence: Enjoy the pool, 1,700 m² spa, and beauty treatments.Breathtaking Views and Comfort: Most rooms face south with Matterhorn views, some with hot tubs or fireplaces.Fine Dining and Entertainment: From rich breakfast buffets to gala dinners, Grill Le Cervin, and stylish bars.Book your stay at the iconic Mont Cervin Palace for an unforgettable luxury experience in Zermatt.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that Zermatt is a car-free village. You can park your car in Täsch (indoor parking) and continue to Zermatt by train or taxi.
Please note that (Spa Nescens) is only available during the winter months to mid-March.
