HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Exhibition Centre and Metro StationLuxurious Rooms and Suites with Modern AmenitiesEasy Access to City Centre and Business DistrictExperience comfort and convenience at Mercure Paris Porte De Versailles Expo, just steps away from the Exhibition Centre and Metro station. Indulge in a stay in one of our luxurious rooms or suites, equipped with top-notch amenities including air conditioning, satellite TV, and some with minibars and Nespresso coffee machines. With easy access to the city centre and business district via the T3 Tramway Line, and being close to the Parc des Princes Stadium, your stay with us will be both relaxing and productive. Don't miss out on the chance to book your stay at our hotel and make the most of your time in Paris.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
An extra bed for a person over 16 can be installed in the room on request and at an additional cost.

Please note that children under 12 years old can enjoy breakfast for free.

In the Half Board rates, breakfast for children is offered but dinner is subject to an additional charge on site.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
