HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Indulge in Nature's BeautyNestled just 5 minutes from Yosemite National Park, our Cedar Lodge offers a serene escape with both indoor and outdoor swimming pools.Comfort and ConvenienceEach room is thoughtfully equipped with air conditioning, a TV, refrigerator, and coffee-making facilities. Enjoy the rustic charm of period wood furnishings and vibrant accents during your stay.Delight Your Taste BudsSavor delectable American cuisine at our on-site restaurant, open daily for dinner. Unwind at the Canyon Bar &amp; Grill for a relaxing evening after a day of exploring the wonders of Yosemite.Book your stay now to experience the perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and natural beauty at Yosemite Cedar Lodge.
