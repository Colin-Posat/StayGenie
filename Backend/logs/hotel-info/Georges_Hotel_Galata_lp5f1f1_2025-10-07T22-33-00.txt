HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical Charm and Modern LuxuryGeorges Hotel Galata, nestled in a majestic historical building in the heart of Istanbul, offers spacious balconies with stunning views and personalized service.Fine Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in local wine at the rooftop restaurant, savor traditional Turkish cuisine at Restaurant 24, and enjoy live jazz performances at the hotel's bar. Don't miss the legendary Sunday Brunch experience!Prime Location and ConvenienceSituated in the trendy Galata district, the hotel is a short walk from iconic landmarks like Istiklal Avenue and the Galata Tower. With the Old City just 2 km away, explore Istanbul's rich history with ease.Book now to experience the perfect blend of history and luxury at Georges Hotel Galata!

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  EUR 15 per  day.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for € 95 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for € 40 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 95 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
