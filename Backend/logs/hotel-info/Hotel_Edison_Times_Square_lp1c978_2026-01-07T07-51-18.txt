HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm in Times SquareStep back in time at the iconic Hotel Edison, a historic gem in the heart of Times Square. The Art Deco influences in the lobby and exterior transport you to the glamorous 1930s era.Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in comfort with flat-screen cable TVs, air conditioning, and elegant bathrooms stocked with complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer in every Classic and Signature room and suite.Unmatched ServicesExperience top-notch concierge assistance, 24-hour business and fitness centers, and event spaces like the Edison Ballroom. Enjoy delectable dining at Bond 45 and Friedman's, or unwind at the nearby Rum House for live entertainment.Book now to immerse yourself in the rich history and modern luxury of The Edison Hotel in Manhattan.
