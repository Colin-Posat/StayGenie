HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in Paris's Latin QuarterHotel Central Saint Germain is ideally situated just 300 metres from Cluny Sorbonne Metro Station, making it a perfect base for exploring the vibrant Latin Quarter. Guests can easily access iconic landmarks like Notre Dame Cathedral and the Pantheon within minutes on foot.Comfortable Rooms with Modern AmenitiesThe hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi and satellite TV, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests. Each room is carpeted and features a private bathroom with a hairdryer, providing convenience and relaxation after a day of sightseeing.Proximity to Charming Cafes and GardensWithin a short walk, guests can immerse themselves in the Parisian lifestyle by visiting the numerous cafes and bars along Rue de Buci. Additionally, the enchanting Luxembourg Gardens are just 350 metres away, offering a serene retreat in the heart of the city.Ready to experience the charm of Paris at Hotel Central Saint Germain? CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
