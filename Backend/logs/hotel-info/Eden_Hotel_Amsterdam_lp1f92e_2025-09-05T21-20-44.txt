HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Architecture and Room VarietyWith a history dating back to 1977, Eden Hotel Amsterdam boasts a distinctive feature - each room is uniquely designed due to the gradual expansion of the hotel's buildings. Navigating through the maze of corridors adds a touch of adventure to your stay, ensuring a one-of-a-kind experience. Some rooms offer picturesque views of the canals and lift-up bridges, providing a true essence of Amsterdam.Central Location and City AccessNestled in the heart of the city centre, our hotel offers easy access to the renowned Rembrandt Square and is a short tram ride away from the central station. Moreover, with a ticket service available on-site, guests can effortlessly kickstart their city exploration right from our doorstep. Within a leisurely 20-minute walk, cultural gems like the Rijksmuseum and Van Gogh Museum await your discovery.Concierge Services and Travel AssistanceAt Eden Hotel Amsterdam, our dedicated concierge ensures a warm welcome and is readily available to enhance your stay. From arranging rental bikes to providing insider tips on navigating the city, our team is committed to making your Amsterdam experience seamless. Additionally, with Schiphol Airport a convenient 22 km away, your travel convenience is our priority.Experience the charm of Amsterdam at Eden Hotel - Book your stay now!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 12 years old stay for € 9.50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
