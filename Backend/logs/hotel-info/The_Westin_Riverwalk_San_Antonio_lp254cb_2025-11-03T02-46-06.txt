Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness with some guests mentioning cleanliness as a positive aspect and others pointing out dirty restrooms and outdated rooms.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was generally appreciated by most guests, with a few complaints about staff behavior and room visits for noise disturbances.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location on the riverwalk was highlighted positively by many guests for its proximity to key landmarks, although some mentioned it was on the unpopular side of the river.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable with spacious beds, but some guests mentioned issues with older facilities and noise disturbances like loud elevators and doors.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities with some guests appreciating the gym and pool while others noted the lack of ice machines, malfunctioning air conditioners, and elevators.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price did not match the experience, especially in terms of amenities like ice machines and air conditioners.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Generally positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with only a few guests suggesting more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the friendly staff and great location, although some mentioned issues like noisy elevators and the need for room fans.
