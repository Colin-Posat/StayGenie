HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe eco-friendly Hélios Opéra hotel offers stylish and comfortable rooms equipped with modern amenities such as air-conditioning, free WiFi, and flat-screen TVs. Upgrade to Deluxe or Executive rooms for added luxuries like Nespresso Machines and plush bathrobes.Delicious Breakfast OptionsStart your day with a delightful buffet breakfast featuring local and organic products, as well as a cheese plate. Enjoy a cup of coffee under the hotel's beautiful stained-glass roof.Convenient Location and ServicesLocated just a short walk from popular attractions and a metro station, Hélios Opéra provides easy access to Paris landmarks. Take advantage of the 24-hour reception, tour desk, and helpful multilingual staff to make the most of your stay.Experience the perfect blend of comfort and convenience at Hélios Opéra - book your stay now for an unforgettable Parisian getaway.
