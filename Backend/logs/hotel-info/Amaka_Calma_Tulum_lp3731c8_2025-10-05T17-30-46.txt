HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationAmaka Calma Tulum, located in Tulum, offers 4-star accommodation with modern amenities including free bikes, a refreshing outdoor pool, and a fitness centre. The air-conditioned rooms are well-equipped with a flat-screen TV, safety deposit box, and a private bathroom, ensuring a comfortable stay.Delicious Dining OptionsStart your day with a buffet or American breakfast served at the hotel. For those looking to explore local cuisine, the hotel is conveniently located near various dining options.Recreational ActivitiesGuests can enjoy playing table tennis or cycling in the surrounding area. The hotel's friendly staff, fluent in English and Spanish, are available 24/7 at the reception to assist with any inquiries.Experience luxury and comfort at Amaka Calma Tulum. What Guests Love:

• Limited staff availability

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a major issue, with multiple reviews highlighting dirty and unkept conditions.
• Service: 5.9/10 - Mixed reviews on service quality, with some guests praising friendly staff while others noted issues with limited staff availability and cleanliness.
• Location: 6.7/10 - Location received mixed feedback, with some guests considering it adequate while others mentioned it was far from amenities.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Rooms were generally described as spacious and comfortable, although there were complaints about maintenance issues such as funny smells and broken items.
• Amenities: 3.6/10 - Amenities were criticized for being poorly maintained and dirty, with guests mentioning a lack of necessary items and cleanliness issues.
• Value for Money: 5.8/10 - Mixed opinions on the value for money, with some guests feeling it was subpar due to cleanliness and maintenance issues.
• Food and Beverage: 3.5/10 - Food and beverage options were heavily criticized for poor quality and cleanliness, with guests mentioning unwashed dishes and unappetizing food.
• Overall Experience: 4.2/10 - Overall experience was negative for most guests, with many mentioning unsatisfactory cleanliness, maintenance issues, and lack of basic facilities.
