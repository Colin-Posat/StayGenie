Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Generally positive feedback on cleanliness, but a few instances of cleanliness issues reported like dirty vents and blood stains on bed and pillows.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Staff were mostly described as friendly and helpful, but some complaints about noise during breakfast and unfulfilled yoga lessons.
• Location: 8.6/10 - Highly praised for its great location close to the beach and key landmarks.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on room quality with some guests appreciating comfy beds and curtains, while others mentioned small, windowless, and subpar rooms.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities with mentions of good kitchen and common areas, but complaints about small bathrooms and non-functioning wifi.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Varied opinions on value for money, with some guests feeling it was overpriced for the experience offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and dining services, but some guests desired more variety and better cleanliness in food preparation areas.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and were satisfied with their stay, although there were some issues reported like cleanliness and room quality.
