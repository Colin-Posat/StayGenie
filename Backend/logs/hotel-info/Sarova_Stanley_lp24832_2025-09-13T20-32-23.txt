HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 12 years old stay for US$50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for US$70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Value for Money: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some finding the rates reasonable for the location and services, while others felt it was on the higher side.
• Food and Beverage: 6.9/10 - Food quality received mixed feedback, with some praising breakfast and restaurant options, while others found it lacking in variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had an excellent overall experience, highlighting positive aspects like friendly staff, comfortable rooms, and convenient location.
