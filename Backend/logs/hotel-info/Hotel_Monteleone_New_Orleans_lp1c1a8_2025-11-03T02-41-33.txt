HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at Hotel Monteleone, nestled in the heart of vibrant New Orleans.Unwind in StylePamper yourself at our exquisite spa where you can indulge in rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials. Enjoy the serene outdoor pool and state-of-the-art health club, perfect for relaxation and fitness.Savor Culinary DelightsDine at Criollo Restaurant, specializing in local cuisine, or take advantage of our 24-hour room service for a luxurious dining experience. Enjoy crafted cocktails at our stylish bar or poolside lounge.Comfort and ConnectivityOur spacious 570 guestrooms are equipped with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs, refrigerators, and complimentary Wi-Fi, ensuring you stay connected and comfortable during your visit.Don't miss out on an unforgettable stay—book your getaway at Hotel Monteleone today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* Children 17 years old and younger stay free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* This property welcomes guests of all sexual orientations and gender identities (LGBTQ+ friendly).

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with some guests highlighting the friendly and attentive staff, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport, making it convenient for guests to explore the area.
• Room Quality: 8/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, with guests particularly enjoying the amenities such as the comfortable beds and shower pressure.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the pool, bar, and restaurant services, while others mentioned the lack of certain services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Opinions on the value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling that the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable given the prime location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, with guests enjoying the variety of options available, although some suggested room for improvement.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, praising the hospitality, location, and overall atmosphere.
