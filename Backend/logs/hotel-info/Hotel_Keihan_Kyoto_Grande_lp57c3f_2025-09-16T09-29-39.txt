HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and ServicesHotel Keihan Kyoto boasts a prime location directly connected to JR Kyoto Train Station, offering easy access for travelers. Enjoy the convenience of massage services and a 24-hour front desk for all your needs.Modern AmenitiesExperience comfort in the hotel's modern rooms equipped with free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, heating, and essential amenities like an electric kettle, fridge, and flat-screen TV. The en suite bathroom provides toiletries and a bathtub, along with complimentary bath amenities.Dining and Leisure OptionsIndulge in Mediterranean cuisine paired with fine wines at restaurant Octavar. Unwind with a drink from the vending machine, explore Kyoto through provided tour books, or utilize services like luggage storage and dry cleaning. Book your stay now for a memorable experience at Hotel Keihan Kyoto.
