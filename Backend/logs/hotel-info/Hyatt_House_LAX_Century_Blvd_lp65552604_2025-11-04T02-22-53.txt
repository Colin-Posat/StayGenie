• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were clean, comfortable, and spacious, with some issues reported about water pressure and requests for specific room setups.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests appreciating the pool, gym, and free breakfast, while others mentioned limited pool area and lack of certain services like spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on pricing, with some guests feeling it was expensive, while others appreciated the value, especially considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, but some suggested more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, with a few concerns raised about noise, hygiene, and lack of communication from management.
