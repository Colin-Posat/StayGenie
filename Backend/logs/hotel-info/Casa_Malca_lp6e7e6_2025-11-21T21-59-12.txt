HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for US$250 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Spacious rooms
• Comfortable beds

Areas for Improvement:
• Chaotic location
• Misleading information about amenities
• Poor service and cleanliness

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 3/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a major issue, with complaints about neglected areas and dirty surroundings.
• Service: 5.7/10 - Service was neutral at best, with guests describing it as impersonal and not welcoming.
• Location: 6.8/10 - The hotel's location was criticized for being chaotic, with narrow roads, loud beach clubs, and poor surroundings.
• Room Quality: 6.5/10 - While rooms were spacious and had comfortable beds, guests highlighted various maintenance issues and impractical fittings.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Guests were disappointed with the amenities provided, mentioning a lack of proper services and misleading information.
• Value for Money: 3.6/10 - Many guests felt the hotel was overpriced and did not offer value for the amount charged.
• Food and Beverage: 5.7/10 - While breakfast and restaurant services were advertised as complimentary, guests felt misled by additional charges and lack of variety.
• Overall Experience: 5/10 - Overall, the majority of guests had a disappointing experience and would not recommend the hotel to others.
