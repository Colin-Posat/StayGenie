HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Airport Accessibility:Located next to Haneda Airport, The Royal Park Hotel Tokyo Haneda offers seamless access to the International Terminal, making it a perfect choice for travelers with early or late flights.Diverse Dining and Services:From the on-site restaurant TAILWIND serving all meals to the business center and bar, guests can enjoy a range of amenities. Currency exchange, dry cleaning, and 24-hour front desk services ensure a comfortable stay.Luxurious Room Features:The rooms are equipped with modern conveniences like air conditioning, soundproofing, and an LCD TV. Guests staying in Premium Floor rooms can indulge in special amenities like pyjamas, beauty supplies, and a coffee maker.Book now to experience the convenience and luxury of The Royal Park Hotel Tokyo Haneda!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests mostly appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel, with minor mentions of cleanliness issues like old carpet and dust in some rooms.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was generally well-regarded, with staff being polite and helpful. Some guests mentioned minor issues like confusion with the kiosk check-in process.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel's location within the airport terminal received high praise for its convenience and proximity to departure gates and airport amenities.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were described as clean, comfortable, and functional, though there were mixed reviews on the size of the rooms and amenities like towels and pillows.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests mentioning the nice breakfast and efficient room design. However, there were occasional mentions of limited towels and uncomfortable Japanese style pillows.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the room rates were expensive, especially with additional charges for early check-in, while others appreciated the convenience and location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Generally positive feedback on breakfast and restaurant services, with some guests suggesting more variety. However, a comment noted the expensive breakfast options.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting the convenience, friendliness of staff, and comfortable beds. However, there were minor complaints about amenities, check-in times, and room size.
