Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel facilities, with multiple mentions of it throughout the reviews.
• Service: 8.2/10 - The helpful and friendly staff received positive comments overall, though some guests noted issues with slow service.
• Location: 8.5/10 - The convenient and central location near key attractions and public transportation was highlighted as a major positive aspect by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - While rooms were generally comfortable, some guests observed maintenance issues and small room sizes.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Satisfactory amenities were provided, with mixed reviews mentioning the gym and pool positively but also noting the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, though some guests suggested increasing variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - The majority of guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
