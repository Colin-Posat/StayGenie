HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children 6 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for € 35 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for € 35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable with cleanliness generally praised, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.5/10 - Service was highly appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff mentioned frequently. Some minor complaints about slow service.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel's location was a standout feature, with guests finding it close to major attractions but away from the hustle, and with good accessibility to public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned the need for updates and improvement in certain areas.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the gym and pool satisfactory. However, some pointed out the lack of spa services as a downside.
• Value for Money: 5.7/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money aspect, with some feeling the price was high for the services offered and others finding it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested more variety would enhance the experience.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
