HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern and Stylish AccommodationsExperience the elegance of our bright, air-conditioned rooms with wooden floors and flat-screen satellite TV. Choose rooms with stunning views of the sea or city for a truly memorable stay.Luxurious AmenitiesIndulge in our hotel's sauna, steam baths, and well-equipped gym for an additional fee. Treat yourself to a relaxing massage or explore the city with our convenient bicycle rental service. Our 24-hour reception ensures all your needs are met promptly.Prime LocationConveniently situated in the 22@ Technological District, Hotel 4 Barcelona is a short walk from Bogatell Beach and Llacuna Metro Station. Explore nearby attractions like Ciutadella Park and the Olympic Port, then unwind at our Mediterranean restaurant and stylish bar.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hotel 4 Barcelona!

For reservations for more than 8 people, or more than 7 nights, a prepayment is required. Once the reservation has been made a representative will contact the guest to arrange this prepayment.

The hotel reserves the right to charge the total amount to the credit card provided in case the amount exceeds EUR 800.

Please note that use of the gym, sauna and Turkish baths carries a surcharge.

Please note, when booking more than 3 rooms and/or 10 people, different policies and additional supplements may apply.﻿

Please note that when booking a rate where payment is due before arrival, the property will send detailed payment instructions and a link to a secure payment platform.

Other services that can be requested and are subject to availability include early check-in, late check-out, gluten-free breakfast, breakfast bag (for early check out), in-room breakfast, and cribs. These services may have an additional cost.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
