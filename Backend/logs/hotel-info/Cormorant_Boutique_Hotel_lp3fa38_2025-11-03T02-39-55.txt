Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests mentioned clean rooms as a positive aspect, while some noted minor maintenance issues.

• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Spacious and comfortable rooms with some noise issues mentioned due to location or other guests.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Satisfactory amenities with positive comments about the gym and pool, although the lack of spa services was a drawback for some guests.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the pricing, with some guests considering it slightly high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received, but some guests suggested more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, with a few noting noise-related issues.
