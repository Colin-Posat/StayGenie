Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.9/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel, although some mentioned minor maintenance issues in rooms.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Staff were considered friendly and helpful by most guests, with positive mentions of bartenders and restaurant waitress.
• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's proximity to the airport and public transportation was seen as a major advantage by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.4/10 - Mixed feedback on room quality, with some guests praising the comfort and others pointing out the need for renovations.
• Amenities: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests highlighting the lack of room service and outdated bathrooms.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and dining services received positive feedback overall, with some concerns over pricing.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
