HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Downtown Hotel with Spa Services and On-Site DiningContemporary Guestrooms with Premium AmenitiesThe Sofia Hotel offers non-smoking guestrooms equipped with iPod docking stations, flat-screen TVs with HBO and ESPN, and in-room spa services. Guests can enjoy complimentary Sofia bottled waters, a coffee machine, refrigerator, and microwave, as well as unlimited local and toll-free calls.Gourmet Dining and Wellness AmenitiesIndulge in delectable meals at Currant American Brasserie, offering brunch, dinner, room service, and happy hour. The hotel also features a concierge desk, 24-hour business center, and a 24-hour yoga center with exercise classes. Guests can partake in guided walking tours and explore the area on complimentary urban cruiser bicycles.Convenient Location with Nearby AttractionsLocated near the Gaslamp Quarter and Amtrak Station, The Sofia Hotel is just a short drive from San Diego Zoo and the airport. Little Italy is also within easy reach, making it a perfect choice for travelers seeking a blend of luxury and convenience.Experience the sophistication and comfort of The Sofia Hotel - Book your stay now!
