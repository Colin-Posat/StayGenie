HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Tropical GetawayPanoramic Views and Convenient LocationLocated just a 3-minute walk from Palawan Beach, Amara Sanctuary Resort on Sentosa Island offers stunning views of the South China Sea. Situated close to Universal Studios Singapore and Resorts World Sentosa, the resort provides easy access to entertainment options.Elegant Accommodations and DiningThe air-conditioned rooms feature stylish wooden furnishings, LED TVs, and large windows. Shutters restaurant serves modern Singaporean cuisine with an open-concept kitchen and outdoor terrace.Convenience and ComfortGuests can enjoy 4 outdoor swimming pools, a business center, and free parking. The resort also offers a free shuttle service to Harbourfront and a 24-hour front desk for tour arrangements.Experience luxury and convenience at Amara Sanctuary Resort Sentosa.
