HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the epitome of comfort at Hyatt Union Square New York, offering pet-friendly rooms, stylish studios, and elegant suites with private terraces. Each room is thoughtfully designed with premium amenities like white linens, a porcelain sink, and a flat-screen TV for your relaxation.Fitness and DiningStay active at our 24-hour Life Fitness™ gym equipped with complimentary towels, fruits, water, and headphones for a rejuvenating workout session. After exercising, indulge in delectable American cuisine at Bowery Road, curated by Chef Ron Rosselli, right within the hotel premises.Convenient LocationSituated a stone's throw away from Union Square Park and with easy access to New York University and Mount Sinai Beth Israel Centre, our hotel offers the perfect blend of tranquility and convenience. Plus, with a subway station just steps away, exploring iconic landmarks like Washington Square Park is a breeze.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Hyatt Union Square New York!
