HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 22 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.7/10 - While the room was clean overall, a review mentioned finding a few bugs in the room.
• Service: 10/10 - Numerous reviews highlighted exceptional service by staff like Candi, Mike, and others, making guests feel at home.
• Location: 9.7/10 - The hotel is ideally located within walking distance of Disneyland, enhancing the overall experience.
• Room Quality: 8.7/10 - Rooms are noted as older and in need of an update, though large and comfy.
• Amenities: 8.7/10 - Basic amenities are available, but some guests noted that coffee availability could be improved.
• Value for Money: 8.7/10 - Some guests felt the hotel was costlier compared to the facilities offered.
• Food and Beverage: 9.2/10 - Food quality was positively reviewed, although there were a few complaints about bugs.
• Overall Experience: 9.9/10 - Guests mostly had wonderful experiences due to the staff's friendliness and helpfulness.
