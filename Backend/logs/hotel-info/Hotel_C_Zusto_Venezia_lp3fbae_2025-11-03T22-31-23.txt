HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Building in Venice:Experience the charm of Venice at Hotel Cà Zusto, located in a historic building in the Santa Croce area. Just a short walk from the Riva di Biasio Vaporetto water bus stop, immerse yourself in the rich history of the city.Elegant Rooms with Modern Amenities:Indulge in luxury at Cà Zusto with elegantly designed rooms that seamlessly blend bright colour schemes, antique furniture, and modern amenities. Enjoy your stay with flat-screen TVs, minibars, and private bathrooms for your comfort.Convenient Location and Services:Start your day with a delicious Italian breakfast featuring pastries and hot drinks. Benefit from free Wi-Fi in the lobby, 24-hour front desk assistance for tourist information, and a peaceful location just 600 meters from Santa Lucia Train Station.Book your stay at Hotel Cà Zusto for a memorable Venetian experience!
