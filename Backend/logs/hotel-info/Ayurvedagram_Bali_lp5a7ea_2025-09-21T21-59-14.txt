HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Ayurvedagram Bali - The Heart of Healing - offers a serene escape surrounded by lush landscapes. Immerse yourself in traditional Ayurvedic healing practices, yoga, and the essence of balance.Breathtaking Scenery and Diverse Accommodations: Experience our 25-room wellness resort nestled in 7,000 square meters of beauty. Choose from a range of rooms, including large villas, secluded nature suites, pool rooms, and deluxe terrace rooms.Satvik Cuisine and Holistic Healing: Indulge in our satvik cuisine, prepared mindfully to satisfy your senses. Explore our well-appointed spaces, yoga shala, Buddha circles, and exceptional Ayurvedic treatments for holistic rejuvenation.Discover a holistic sanctuary in the heart of Ubud, Bali, where your well-being is our priority. Book your stay at Ayurvedagram Bali for a transformative healing experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Ayurvedagram Bali in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Payment before arrival via bank transfer is required. The property will contact you after you book to provide instructions.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
In accordance with government guidelines to minimise transmission of the Coronavirus (COVID-19), this property may request additional documentation from guests to validate identity, travel itinerary and other relevant information, during dates where such guidelines exist.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 5 and older are allowed.
Children from 5 years old to 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for Rp 450,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Adults  stay for Rp 650,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
