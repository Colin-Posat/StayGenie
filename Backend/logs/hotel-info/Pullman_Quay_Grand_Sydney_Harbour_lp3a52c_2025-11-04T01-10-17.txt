HOTEL POLICIES:
CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for AUD 0 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for AUD 0 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for AUD 55 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
