PETS:
Pets are allowed. No extra charges.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.1/10 - Guests had mixed reviews about cleanliness, with some mentioning issues with room smell and dirty towels.
• Service: 7.5/10 - Overall, service received positive feedback, though some mentioned rude staff interactions.
• Location: 8.8/10 - The location near Venice Beach was highly praised by many guests for its convenience and safety.
• Room Quality: 7.2/10 - Rooms were generally clean and equipped, although some guests reported issues with small size and unpleasant smells.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - The rooftop bar and lounge were highlights for many guests, while some mentioned the lack of a pool as a downside.
• Value for Money: 4.5/10 - Several guests felt the hotel was overpriced and did not offer good value for the money, especially considering additional charges for amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 6.6/10 - Feedback on breakfast and restaurant services varied, with some guests questioning the quality and pricing.
• Overall Experience: 6.3/10 - Opinions on the overall experience were polarized, with some guests having positive memories while others strongly advised against staying at the hotel.
