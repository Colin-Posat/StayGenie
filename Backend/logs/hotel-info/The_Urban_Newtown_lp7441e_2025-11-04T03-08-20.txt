HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Industrial-Style Studios in Vibrant NewtownConvenient Location: Situated in the lively suburb of Newtown, The Urban Newtown is a short walk from Enmore Theatre and Newtown train station, with easy access to the University of Sydney and Sydney Airport.Modern Amenities: Enjoy free WiFi, air-conditioning, flat-screen TV, microwave, espresso machine, refrigerator, kitchen with stove top, and a private bathroom with rain shower and toiletries.Near Major Attractions: Explore Sydney easily with the hotel's proximity to Sydney Opera House, just a 20-minute drive away.Book your stay at The Urban Newtown for a unique industrial-style experience in the heart of Sydney!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform The Urban Newtown in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of AUD 250 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note that there is a 2% charge when you pay with a Visa or Mastercard or American Express.

All international cards will be charged and refunded in AUD currency, and the hotel is not responsible for any fluctuations in exchange rates.

You must show a valid photo ID and credit card upon check-in.

Please note guests under 18 years of age must be accompanied by an adult.

Property is dog friendly, but only the King Suite room and not any other rooms at AUD 40 per stay for one night stays and AUD 80 for more than one night stays. Please contact the property.

The bedding configuration request is subject to availability only.

Public parking is available on site (reservation is needed) and costs AUD 65 per day.
