HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHotel Genova, located near the Basilica of Santa Maria Maggiore, offers elegant rooms with parquet floors and marble bathrooms. The soundproof guest rooms, decorated in warm colors, feature amenities such as a flat-screen TV, work desk, and complimentary toiletries.Culinary DelightsExperience Mediterranean cuisine made with seasonal ingredients and international cocktails at the Orange Bar. Start your day right with a delicious breakfast served in the welcoming dining room each morning.Convenience and ServiceJust a 2-minute walk from Roma Termini Train Station, Hotel Genova's lobby boasts marble floors and pillars. The attentive staff at the front desk is ready to assist with tourist information. Stay connected with Wi-Fi access available throughout the hotel.Book now to indulge in luxury and convenience at Hotel Genova!
