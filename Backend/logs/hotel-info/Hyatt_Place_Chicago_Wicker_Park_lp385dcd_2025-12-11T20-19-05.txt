HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodations with City ViewsHyatt Place Chicago Wicker Park offers air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and private bathrooms. Enjoy city views from select rooms and make use of the on-site fitness centre and terrace.Convenient Amenities for Business and LeisureThis 3-star hotel provides a shared lounge, evening entertainment, and a 24-hour front desk. Start your day with an American breakfast and unwind at the on-site bar. Stay productive at the business area and explore nearby attractions like Lincoln Park Zoo and United Center.Central Location with Easy AccessSituated just 3.6 km from Lincoln Park Zoo, this hotel is also close to United Center and 360 Chicago.
