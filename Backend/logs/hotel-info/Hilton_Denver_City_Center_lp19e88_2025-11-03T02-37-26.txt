HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 64 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Service: 7.3/10 - Mixed feedback on service quality, with some guests praising staff friendliness and efficiency, while others complained about unhelpful reception staff.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to key landmarks, events, and facilities.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but some guests experienced maintenance issues like condensation on windows.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities with some guests enjoying the gym and bar, while others criticized the lack of variety and spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Guests had varying opinions on the pricing, with some feeling it was high for the basic services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally well-received although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and indicated they would recommend the hotel to others.
