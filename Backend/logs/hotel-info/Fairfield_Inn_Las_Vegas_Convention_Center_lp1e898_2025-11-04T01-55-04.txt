HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location:Situated within 3 km of the vibrant Las Vegas Strip and McCarran Airport, Fairfield Inn Las Vegas Convention Center offers easy access to key attractions.Comfortable Accommodation:Guests can unwind in smoke-free rooms featuring modern decor, luxury bedding, and a 50” HDTV. Additional conveniences like ironing facilities, tea/coffee maker, microwave, and mini fridge are provided for a relaxing stay.Relaxing Amenities:Enjoy the outdoor pool area and sundeck for a leisurely break. Benefit from express check-in/check-out services and start your day with a complimentary grab and go breakfast, ensuring a pleasant stay.Book your stay at Fairfield Inn Las Vegas Convention Center for a memorable experience!
