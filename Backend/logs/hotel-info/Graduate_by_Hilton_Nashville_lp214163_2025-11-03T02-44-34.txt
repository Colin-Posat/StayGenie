IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform Graduate by Hilton Nashville in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Pool reservations are required through White Limozeen in advance.

Please note The Governor’s Pool is an extension of White Limozeen and not a hotel amenity. Reservations are required for everyone, including hotel guests. Space is limited – visit Resy to book.

To ensure best-in-class service and a memorable stay, we charge a nightly $20 amenity fee, plus tax. During your stay, you'll receive:

• In-room RightWater canned water, replenished daily
• Complimentary bike rentals, available at the Front Desk
• Access to Peloton bikes in our hotel fitness center
• Complimentary Wi-Fi.
