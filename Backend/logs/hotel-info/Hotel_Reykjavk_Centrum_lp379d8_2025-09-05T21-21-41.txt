HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Viking Exhibition CentreHotel Reykjavik Centrum offers a one-of-a-kind experience with its Viking exhibition centre, showcasing Iceland's rich history. Explore the on-site exhibition center honoring the Viking-era longhouse ruins and Iceland's oldest human remains.Award-Winning DiningIndulge in traditional Icelandic dishes and an extensive wine list at Fjalaköttur, the hotel's award-winning restaurant. For a more casual setting, visit Uppsalir, the hotel's own café bar, and enjoy a relaxing atmosphere.Modern AccommodationsChoose from comfortable hotel rooms or self-service studios, all featuring modern décor, fresh bathrooms, and wooden floors. Enjoy in-room amenities such as satellite TV and tea/coffee facilities for a relaxing stay.Experience history, indulge in exquisite dining, and enjoy modern comforts at Hotel Reykjavik Centrum. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Icelandic getaway!
