HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Rooftop Pool and Fitness CentreCitadines Raffles Place Singapore boasts a stunning rooftop pool and a modern fitness centre, offering guests the perfect opportunity to unwind and stay active during their stay.Convenient Amenities and ServicesWith a lift, shared kitchen, sun terrace, and a 24-hour front desk, guests can enjoy convenience and comfort throughout their visit. Free WiFi is also available property-wide for added connectivity.Comfortable and Well-Equipped UnitsEach air-conditioned unit at the aparthotel is thoughtfully furnished with essential amenities such as a desk, kettle, safety deposit box, and a flat-screen TV. The private bathrooms feature a bidet, and some units even offer a balcony or patio for guests to enjoy.Experience the perfect blend of convenience and luxury at Citadines Raffles Place Singapore. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
