HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Waterfront Location in Downtown MiamiMiami Marriott Biscayne Bay offers a prime location in the heart of downtown Miami, providing stunning bay and city views. Enjoy waterfront dining and explore nearby attractions such as museum park and the FTX arena.Luxurious Accommodations with Bay ViewsExperience comfort and elegance in our guest rooms featuring wired Internet, work desks, and marble bathrooms. Upgrade to rooms with private balconies and breathtaking bay or city views for a truly unforgettable stay.Concierge Services and Fine DiningLet our Concierge desk enhance your stay by arranging transportation and exciting water activities. Indulge in sea-to-table coastal cuisine at Gold Coast Kitchen + Cocktails, our waterfront restaurant offering the freshest ingredients.Book now to experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at Miami Marriott Biscayne Bay!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.
Please note that the breakfast-included rate covers breakfast for 2 adults and 2 children under 12 years old. Fees apply for additional guests. Contact hotel for details.

Valet parking is available for an additional fee.

Port of Miami is 2.5 miles away.

Destination Fee Includes:
Enhanced High-Speed Internet, Daily F&B Credit, Two Bike Rentals (up to 2 hours per stay), Enhanced High Speed Wireless Internet
