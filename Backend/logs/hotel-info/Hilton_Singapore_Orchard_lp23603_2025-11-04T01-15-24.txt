HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 143.88 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  SGD 3.80 per  hour.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.6/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a strong positive aspect of their stay.
• Service: 8/10 - Overall, the service was appreciated by guests, with particular praise for friendly staff members.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's central location near public transportation and key landmarks received high praise from guests.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Guests found the rooms spacious and comfortable, with a few mentioning minor noise issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - While some guests appreciated amenities like the gym and pool, others noted a lack of variety and services such as spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.3/10 - Opinions on value for money varied, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - The quality of breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, although some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
