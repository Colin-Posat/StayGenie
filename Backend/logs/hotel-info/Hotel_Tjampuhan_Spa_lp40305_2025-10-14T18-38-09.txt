HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the serenity of Bali at Hotel Tjampuhan Spa, your oasis in the heart of Ubud.Stunning Views and Ideal LocationNestled amidst lush tropical greenery, our hotel offers breathtaking views of Gunung Lembah Temple and the tranquil rivers that flow nearby. Just a short distance from local attractions like Ubud Market and Museum Puri Lukisan, you’ll enjoy easy access to the best of Bali.Luxurious Rooms with ComfortOur Balinese-style accommodations feature both ceiling fans and air-conditioning for your comfort. Each room boasts a private terrace with stunning garden and river views, creating the perfect backdrop for relaxation.Wellness and Adventure AwaitsIndulge in our rejuvenating spa treatments, relax in the hot/cold spring water bath, or explore Ubud at your own pace with bicycle or car rentals. Plus, benefit from our complimentary shuttle service for easy travel.Escape to paradise and book your unforgettable stay at Hotel Tjampuhan Spa now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Upon check-in, customers are required to present the confirmation page, and the credit card used to pay for the booking. The holder of the credit card used to pay for the booking should be part of the traveling party.

If you are making payment using another cardholder's credit card, kindly provide the following to the hotel prior to your arrival:
- Authorization letter with cardholder's signature
- Copy of the cardholder's card (front and back of card with cardholder's signature)
- Copy of the credit card holder's passport.

The information may be sent to the hotel via e-mail or facsimile. Please note that hotel may contact the cardholder for verification purposes. The hotel will only accept credit card for deposit payment to secure the booking instead of debit card.

Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children ages 2 and older are allowed.
Children 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 2 years old to 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 550,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
