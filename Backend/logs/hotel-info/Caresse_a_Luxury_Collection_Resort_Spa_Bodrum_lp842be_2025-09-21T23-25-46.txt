HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Escape to luxury at Caresse, a Luxury Collection Resort &amp; Spa in the heart of Bodrum, where coastal sophistication meets unparalleled relaxation.Spa BlissIndulge in soothing treatments at our world-class spa, offering massages, body treatments, and rejuvenating facials that promise to revitalize your spirit.Culinary DelightsSavor culinary excellence at Buddha Bar Beach, one of three exquisite dining options, or enjoy 24-hour room service for a taste of paradise without leaving your balcony.Amenities GaloreRelax by the indoor pool, unwind in the hot tub, and stay connected with complimentary wired internet. For added convenience, our business center and event facilities cater to all your needs.Book your unforgettable getaway now and experience the best of Bodrum!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* The seasonal pool will be open from April to October.
* Reservations are required for massage services and spa treatments. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 6 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
