HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Scenic GardenHotel Grand Fuji, nestled in Fuji, offers a serene escape with a beautiful garden and convenient amenities. The air-conditioned rooms are well-equipped with modern comforts like a coffee machine, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom.Convenient Facilities and ServicesGuests can enjoy free WiFi, luggage storage, and complimentary private parking. The on-site restaurant serves a delicious daily breakfast with continental, American, or Asian options. The hotel's proximity to Shimizu Station and Shuzen-ji Temple makes it an ideal choice for travelers.Relaxing Stay with Excellent HospitalityImmerse yourself in comfort at Hotel Grand Fuji, where every detail is designed to enhance your stay. Book now to experience luxury and tranquility in the heart of Fuji.
