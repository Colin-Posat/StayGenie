HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Canal-View Rooms and Historic CharmPalazzo Priuli, nestled in a 14th-century building along 2 canals, offers a unique stay with canal-view rooms and a blend of historic charm. The Murano glass lamps, marble stairs, and antique furniture create an old-fashioned and intimate atmosphere that transports guests back in time.Complimentary Amenities and ServicesGuests can enjoy complimentary drinks and snacks in the lobby each afternoon, along with a free welcome drink upon arrival. The 24-hour bar ensures that guests can unwind at any time, while the buffet breakfast featuring delicious Italian pastries is the perfect start to the day.Convenient Location and Modern ComfortConveniently located just a 5-minute walk from St. Mark's Square and the Bridge of Sighs, Hotel Palazzo Priuli offers air-conditioned rooms furnished with antiques and traditional Venetian décor. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as satellite TV, air conditioning, and free Wi-Fi, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable stay.Experience the historic charm and modern comfort of Palazzo Priuli – book your stay now for an unforgettable Venetian getaway!
