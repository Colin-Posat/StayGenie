HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for THB 1,765.50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Areas for Improvement:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.4/10 - The cleanliness received mixed reviews, with some guests mentioning clean rooms and others pointing out maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was highly appreciated by most guests, with mentions of friendly staff going above and beyond.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and amenities was highlighted positively.
• Room Quality: 6.9/10 - Room quality received mixed feedback, with some guests mentioning spaciousness and others noting the need for updates.
• Amenities: 6.3/10 - Guests mentioned a mix of satisfactory and lacking amenities, with some positive comments on the gym and pool.
• Value for Money: 4.7/10 - Guests had varied opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high for the offered services.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Food and beverage services generally received positive feedback, although some guests wanted more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, despite mixed feedback in other categories.
