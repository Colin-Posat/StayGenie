HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the ultimate luxury at Trump International Hotel &amp; Tower New York, where sophistication meets convenience in the heart of the city.Indulge in Relaxation: Pamper yourself at our serene spa, offering an array of treatments, massages, and facials designed to rejuvenate your mind and body. Unwind further with access to our sauna and 24-hour health club.Culinary Delights Await: Savor gourmet cuisine at Jean-Georges, one of our two renowned restaurants, or indulge in the convenience of room service for a cozy night in. Don’t miss our delectable full breakfast served on weekends.Unmatched Comfort and Connectivity: Our 176 elegantly appointed guestrooms feature modern amenities, including flat-screen TVs, iPod docking stations, and complimentary internet access, ensuring you remain connected while enjoying your stay.Book your exceptional getaway today and immerse yourself in the luxury of New York City!
