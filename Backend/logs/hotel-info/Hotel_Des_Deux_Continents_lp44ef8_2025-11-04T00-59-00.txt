IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Hotel Des Deux Continents in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please note that some of the hotel rooms are not serviced by a lift and that there is no luggage porter in the hotel.

All rooms are non-smoking only. If guests do not respect this rule, the property may charge 1 extra night's cost as a penalty.
