HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Stay in the Heart of BarcelonaElegantly Decorated Rooms and Seasonal Outdoor PoolExperience the epitome of luxury at Cotton House Hotel. Indulge in elegantly decorated rooms, enjoy free WiFi, and take a refreshing dip in the seasonal outdoor pool.Charming Historical Building with Modern AmenitiesImmerse yourself in the rich history of the former headquarters of the Association of Cotton House Manufacturers. With beautiful lounges, a library, and a modern gym and massage room on the sixth floor, the hotel seamlessly blends old-world charm with contemporary comfort.Culinary Delights and Convenient LocationSavor delectable cuisine at the stylish bar and restaurant, or sample Spanish Tapas on the terrace. Located just minutes from Plaza Catalunya and Passeig de Gracia Metro Station, the hotel offers easy access to top attractions and transportation hubs.Book now for an unforgettable stay at Cotton House Hotel, Autograph Collection, A Marriott Luxury &amp; Lifestyle Hotel.
