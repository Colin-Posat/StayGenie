HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Indulge in a tranquil escape at Visesa Ubud Resort, where luxury meets nature in the heart of Ubud.Relaxation AwaitsUnwind at our exquisite spa offering an array of massages, body treatments, and facials designed to rejuvenate your spirit. Enjoy our stunning outdoor pool and well-equipped fitness center, or explore the surroundings with rented bicycles.Culinary DelightsSavor local and international flavors at Lesung Restaurant or opt for the convenience of in-room dining. Join us for daily complimentary receptions and toast to your holiday at one of our two inviting bars.Comfort and ConvenienceOur well-appointed rooms feature private balconies, wireless internet, and premium bedding for a restful stay. Enjoy complimentary shuttle services within 1 km, along with on-site parking and business amenities.Book your unforgettable getaway today!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
* Pool access available from 7:00 AM to 7:30 PM.
* Reservations are required for massage services, spa treatments, and private bath/onsen. Reservations can be made by contacting the hotel prior to arrival, using the contact information on the booking confirmation.
* One child 5 years old or younger stays free when occupying the parent or guardian's room, using existing bedding.
* Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
* No pets and no service animals are allowed at this property.
* Contactless check-in and contactless check-out are available.
