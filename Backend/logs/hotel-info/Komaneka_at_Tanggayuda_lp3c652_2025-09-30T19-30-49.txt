HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the serene luxury of Komaneka at Tanggayuda, a haven nestled in the heart of Ubud.Indulgent WellnessPamper yourself at our exclusive Mango Tree Spa, where you can unwind with rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials, ensuring your body and mind find harmony.Delightful DiningSavor exquisite Indonesian cuisine at Batukaru Restaurant or relax in your room with 24-hour room service. Don’t forget to enjoy a refreshing drink at our inviting poolside bar after a long day.Convenient AmenitiesStay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi and take advantage of our fitness center, outdoor swimming pools, and complimentary area shuttle services to explore nearby attractions effortlessly.Book your unforgettable getaway at Komaneka at Tanggayuda today!

• Cleanliness: 8.5/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, with rooms being spotless and well-maintained.

• Room Quality: 8/10 - Rooms are spacious, well-appointed, and come with unexpected amenities such as plug converters, complimentary fruit, and cookies. Guests appreciated the attention to detail in room maintenance.
• Amenities: 7.5/10 - The hotel provides a range of complimentary activities, from yoga classes to Balinese cultural experiences. Some guests mentioned the availability of free activities as a highlight.
• Value for Money: 7.5/10 - While the hotel is reasonably priced for a 5-star property, some guests felt that additional amenities or services could have enhanced the overall value.
• Food and Beverage: 8/10 - Guests enjoyed the quality and variety of food offerings, although some suggested improvements in the timing of drink service and the availability of a separate bar area.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a memorable and positive experience at the hotel, praising the personalized service, amenities, and tranquil ambiance.
