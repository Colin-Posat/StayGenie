HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Beautiful Garden Views and Complimentary Amenities:Enjoy the scenic garden views at Le Hua while staying connected with free Wi-Fi and benefiting from concierge services. Additionally, indulge in a picnic at the picnic area or grab a snack from the on-site vending machine.Convenient Shuttle Services:Explore the nearby beach or shopping center hassle-free with the hotel's shuttle service available for a surcharge. For your convenience, there is also a grocery/convenience store on-site, perfect for any last-minute needs.Comfortable Rooms and Featured Amenities:Relax in one of the 24 air-conditioned rooms with private balconies, minibars, and LED televisions. Unwind with complimentary wireless internet and catch up on the news with free newspapers in the lobby. Book your stay now at Le Hua for a memorable experience in the heart of Phuket.
