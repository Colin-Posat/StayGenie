HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Yet Traditional DecorMitsui Garden Hotel Kyoto Shinmachi Bettei, opening in March 2014, seamlessly blends modern amenities with traditional Japanese décor. The air-conditioned rooms boast comfortable Serta beds and a flat-screen TV, providing a cozy retreat for guests.Convenient AmenitiesGuests can enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the property and unwind in the public bath. The hotel also offers a coin-launderette, dry cleaning services, and a 24-hour front desk for added convenience. Additionally, drink vending machines and massage services are available on-site.Culinary DelightsIndulge in a Japanese breakfast at the hotel's restaurant, featuring local ingredients such as Kyoto vegetables. The perfect way to start your day before exploring nearby attractions like Nijo Castle and Kyoto Imperial Palace.Book your stay at Mitsui Garden Hotel Kyoto Shinmachi Bettei for a harmonious blend of comfort and tradition.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests with tattoos may not be permitted to use the property’s public bathing areas or other facilities where the tattoos might be visible to other guests.
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Please note that room windows do not open. Room windows are opaque, not offering outside views.
