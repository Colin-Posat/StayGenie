HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Studios with Pool and Beach AccessExperience the perfect getaway at Inn at the Beach in Venice, Florida. Our studios offer a blend of comfort and convenience with a full kitchen, patio, and dining area. Enjoy a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool or unwind in the hot tub. Just a short walk of 60 meters will lead you to the pristine beach for a day of sun and sand.Convenient Amenities and ServicesIndulge in our thoughtful extras including an en suite, plush towels, and crisp linens. Seek recommendations from our 24-hour reception for the best local sightseeing spots. Take advantage of free WiFi, parking, and even a vending machine during your stay.Nearby Attractions and AccessibilityLocated just 3.8 km from Lake Venice Golf Club and 34 km from Sarasota Bradenton Airport, our prime location offers easy access to both leisure and travel. Book your stay now for a memorable experience at Inn at the Beach.
