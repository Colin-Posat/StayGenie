HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Turkish Artisan WorksExquisite Dining Experience at Seasons RestaurantIndulge in the rich Turkish theme and neoclassical details of the rooms at Four Seasons Hotel Istanbul at Sultanahmet. Enjoy the luxury of an espresso machine, flat-screen TV with CD and DVD players, and a marble bathroom with a deep soaking tub.Experience a culinary journey at Seasons Restaurant, located within a glass pavilion in the courtyard garden. Delight in Turkish art and Ottoman period features while savoring breakfast, lunch, dinner, or the popular Sunday brunch.Relaxation and Views at A’YA LoungeUnwind at A’YA Lounge on the rooftop, offering stunning views of the Old City. Enjoy a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages along with savory snacks like mussels dolma and Turkish puff pastry.Book now for a luxurious stay at Four Seasons Hotel Istanbul at Sultanahmet and immerse yourself in Turkish hospitality and luxury.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that only dogs that are 7 kg or less are allowed at the hotel.

Please kindly be informed that smoking is prohibited in this property. Smoking in non-smoking rooms are subject to an extra charge to cover deep-cleaning expenses.

The name on the credit card used for the booking should correspond to the guest staying at the property. For reservations made by a third party, you will need to complete an authorization form and present a copy of the person’s ID and credit card.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not possible).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.
