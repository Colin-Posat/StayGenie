HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in Eixample DistrictLocated in the heart of Barcelona's Eixample district, Catalonia Diagonal Centro offers easy access to popular sights and attractions. Just 300 meters from Diagonal Metro Station, guests can explore the city with convenience.Modern Amenities and Comfortable AccommodationThe hotel boasts modern and comfortable rooms equipped with smart TVs featuring Chromecast apps. Guests can unwind in stylish bedrooms with contemporary wooden furniture and marble en suite bathrooms. Free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel ensures connectivity during your stay.Exploration and RelaxationExplore Gaudi's famous creation, La Pedrera, and the vibrant Ramblas area nearby. After a day of sightseeing, relax by the seasonal outdoor pool or take a short stroll to the trendy Gràcia neighborhood with its eclectic shops and dining options.Book your stay at Catalonia Diagonal Centro for a perfect blend of convenience and comfort.
