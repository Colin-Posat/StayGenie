HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsHôtel Vic Eiffel, situated in the heart of Paris, offers elegantly designed air-conditioned rooms equipped with modern amenities such as a Nespresso coffee machine and flat-screen TV. The en suite bathrooms are stocked with complimentary toiletries and a hairdryer for your convenience.Convenient LocationConveniently located in the 15th district, a short stroll from the iconic Eiffel Tower, this hotel is a perfect choice for travelers interested in exploring Paris. With Sèvres-Lecourbe Metro Station right next door, accessing famous landmarks like Champs-Élysées and Montparnasse Train Station is a breeze.Exceptional ServicesIndulge in a delicious breakfast spread every morning or dine at nearby restaurants for a taste of authentic French cuisine. Benefit from 24-hour front desk assistance, luggage storage, and free Wi-Fi access throughout your stay at Hôtel Vic Eiffel.Book your stay now and experience the charm of Paris with Hôtel Vic Eiffel.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed) and charges may be applicable.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children from 4 years old to 12 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for € 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
