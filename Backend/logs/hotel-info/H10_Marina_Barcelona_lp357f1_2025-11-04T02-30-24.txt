HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesThe H10 Marina Hotel Barcelona offers a gym, spa, and free Wi-Fi for guests to enjoy during their stay. The rooftop pool and sun terrace provide stunning views of the city and sea, creating a relaxing atmosphere.Culinary DelightsIndulge in delicious meals at the Dionissos restaurant, serving both à la carte and tapas options. For a cozy evening, guests can explore the extensive wine list at Bar La Llum. Room service is also available for those who prefer to dine in the comfort of their room.Relaxation and RecreationGuests can unwind at the hotel's spa for an additional fee, which includes access to a sauna, steam baths, and indoor pool. Pamper yourself with massage and beauty services. Additionally, the hotel's proximity to Bogatell Beach and easy access to transportation make it a convenient choice for travelers.To experience luxury, relaxation, and culinary delights at H10 Marina Hotel Barcelona, book your stay now.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Please inform H10 Marina Barcelona in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
If breakfast is reserved directly at the hotel the price is 19 EUR per person, per night.

If you want to enjoy our SPA it is very important to make the reservation in advance. The hotel will not be able to guarantee availability if there are last minute reservations.
The Spa rate is 16 € per person (90 minutes water circuit).
Please note that children between the age of 6 and 16 are only admitted in the spa between 11:00 and 13:00. Children under the age of 6 are not permitted in the spa area.

Double rooms can only accommodate a baby cot, not an extra bed. Maximum capacity is 2 adults and 1 baby.

Please note, when booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

For non-refundable rates, the hotel reserves the right to request, at check-in, both the credit card used to pay in advance and the cardholder’s ID.
