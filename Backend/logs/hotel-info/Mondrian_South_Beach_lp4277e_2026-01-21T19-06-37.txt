HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Design by Marcel WandersExperience the exclusive blend of luxury and artistry at our Miami Beach hotel, meticulously designed by the renowned Marcel Wanders. Each corner showcases custom-designed furniture and artwork, creating a truly one-of-a-kind stay.Luxurious Suites with Modern AmenitiesIndulge in the comfort of our modern suites, featuring full kitchenettes, 42-inch plasma TVs, and iPod docking stations. Enjoy stunning garden or Biscayne Bay views while surrounded by elegant black and white decor and custom furnishings.Relaxation and Dining OptionsUnwind by the main outdoor pool amidst lush gardens, or enjoy a rejuvenating treatment at the Spa at Baia Beach Club. Treat your taste buds at Baia Beach Club Miami, offering delectable American cuisine and sushi with breathtaking views of Biscayne Bay.Book now for an unforgettable stay at our Miami Beach haven, just moments away from Lummus Park Beach and Lincoln Road Mall.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 17 years old and over stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 9.45/10 - The pool and bay view were positively highlighted as main attractions.
• Value for Money: 7.91/10 - The resort fee and overall experience were felt to be overpriced compared to similar hotels.
• Food and Beverage: 8.96/10 - Food quality was described as delicious, contributing positively to the experience.
• Overall Experience: 8.93/10 - Mixed reviews led to a somewhat average overall experience despite certain highlights.
