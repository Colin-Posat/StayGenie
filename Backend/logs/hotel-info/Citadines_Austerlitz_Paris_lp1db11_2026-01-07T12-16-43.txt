HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated between the vibrant Latin Quarter and Bibliothèque François Mitterrand, Citadines Austerlitz Paris offers easy access to the city's attractions. Just a short walk from Campo Formio Métro station and Gare d'Austerlitz, exploring Paris is a breeze.Comfortable AccommodationWith 50 well-equipped apartments, including studios and a 2-room option, guests can enjoy a cozy stay with fully equipped kitchens and private bathrooms. The bright breakfast room overlooking the garden provides a refreshing start to the day.Modern AmenitiesGuests can take advantage of the on-site business center with complimentary Wi-Fi, a convenient laundry room, and parking spaces (subject to availability). Within walking distance to Place d'Italie and Jardin des Plantes, the hotel offers both comfort and convenience.Ready to experience Paris? Book your stay at Citadines Apart'hôtel Austerlitz now!

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
When booking more than 7 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Reception opening times: everyday from 07:00 to 22:00. The hotel has video surveillance outside of these hours, as well as a security agent.

A daily cleaning service is possible at an additional cost. This cost will vary depending on the size of the apartment and the length of your stay.

Please note that the property provides 3 private parking spaces that are subject to availability. Extra costs of EUR 25 per night, EUR 110 per week and EUR 310 per month apply.

Rooms for guests with reduced mobility are available according to availability and must be confirmed with the property after booking. Payment will be requested upon arrival.

10 € /pet/day - Payment at your arrival.This property does not accommodate bachelor(ette) or similar parties.
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
