HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for Rp 600,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 650,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:

• Location: 7.5/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and public transport was a positive for most guests, although some found it slightly far from the city center.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and clean, with a few mentions of minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - Satisfactory amenities were provided, including a gym and pool, but some guests expressed a desire for more diverse services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 5.6/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally appreciated, although some guests suggested improvements for more variety and better quality.
• Overall Experience: 6.1/10 - While most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel, there were some mixed reviews reflecting varying opinions.
