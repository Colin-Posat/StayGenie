HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Rooms with Stunning ViewsExperience luxury at The LINE Hotel LA with modern rooms featuring floor-to-ceiling windows offering breathtaking views of Los Angeles. Each room is equipped with custom furnishings, flat-screen TV, and a mini-bar stocked with American and Korean snacks.Dining Experience at Openaire RestaurantIndulge in a unique dining experience at Openaire Restaurant, offering a seasonal menu with ingredients from land and sea. Enjoy poolside dining in the heart of Koreatown in a shareable format that will tantalize your taste buds.Convenient Location and AmenitiesConveniently located near Hollywood, The LINE Hotel LA offers an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a retail store by Poketo. Guests can also enjoy free yoga classes and explore the area on bicycles. Book now for a memorable stay in the vibrant city of Los Angeles.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on cleanliness, with some guests praising it and others noting issues with dirty carpets and electrical plugs not working.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service received positive feedback for being welcoming and friendly, but some guests experienced delays during check-in.
• Location: 9/10 - Location was highly praised for being in the heart of Koreatown with convenient access to amenities and cultural experiences.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were generally well-liked for their comfort and amenities, although some guests expressed disappointment with room size and missing features like a bathtub.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Amenities were appreciated, with positive mentions of the pool, gym, and quick elevators, but some guests found the furniture worn and the hotel in need of a refresh.
• Value for Money: 5.9/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered, particularly concerning parking fees.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, although some guests desired more breakfast variety and onsite food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, especially praising the location and friendly staff, with some minor complaints about noise levels and value for money.
