HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming Accommodation near the Eiffel TowerLe Tourville Eiffel offers charming accommodation with a neo-classic décor, just a 10-minute walk from the Eiffel Tower.Luxurious Room FeaturesGuest rooms are air-conditioned, individually decorated, and feature a flat-screen satellite TV with international channels. Some rooms offer a private terrace, while suites boast a relaxing spa bath. All rooms provide bathrobes and slippers for added comfort.Convenient AmenitiesStart your day right with a delicious continental breakfast served in the communal lounge. For those in a hurry, there is also the option for a take-away breakfast. After a day of exploring, unwind at the hotel's bar, the perfect spot to relax and socialize.Experience Paris in style at Le Tourville Eiffel - book your stay now!
