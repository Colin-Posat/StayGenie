HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation with Free BikesHotel Ma'xanab Tulum, located just steps away from South Tulum Beach, offers 5-star accommodation with free bikes for guests to explore the beautiful surroundings. The air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and private bathrooms provide a comfortable stay, with some rooms even boasting terraces and sea views.Dining Options and Fitness CentreIndulge in authentic Mexican cuisine at the hotel's restaurant, which also caters to vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free preferences. After a flavorful meal, guests can stay active at the fitness centre or take a refreshing dip in the outdoor swimming pool.Concierge Services and Tour OrganizingExperience top-notch hospitality with room service, concierge assistance, and tour planning available at Hotel Ma'xanab Tulum. Explore nearby attractions like Tulum Archeological Site and Parque Nacional Tulum with ease, making your stay both relaxing and adventurous.Book your stay at Hotel Ma'xanab Tulum now to enjoy a blend of luxury, convenience, and personalized service.
