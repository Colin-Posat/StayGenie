Category Ratings:

• Amenities: 7.5/10 - Satisfactory amenities were provided, but some guests mentioned missing features like an available tub and swimming pool.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed opinions on pricing value, with some finding it high for the services offered, while others considered it reasonable.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Breakfast and dining options were generally well-received, although some guests suggested improvements in variety.
• Overall Experience: 9/10 - The majority of guests had an exceptional overall experience and expressed a strong willingness to recommend the hotel.
