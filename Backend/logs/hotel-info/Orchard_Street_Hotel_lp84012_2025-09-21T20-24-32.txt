HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Located in Lower East Side in Manhattan, guests at the Orchard Street Hotel will find many restaurants and shopping destinations in close proximity. An onsite rooftop lounge offering views of the city gives guests a place to relax in the evening.Luxurious Room Features: All rooms at Orchard Street Hotel come with walnut hued furnishings and wall-mounted HD TVs. City views are offered on the higher floors.Convenient Amenities: Daily housekeeping, a 24-hour business centre, and front desk are provided. Complimentary WiFi is available.Nearby Attractions: Just 1.1 km from Little Italy, with easy access to the F train at 2 Avenue station, guests can explore with ease.Book your stay at Orchard Street Hotel for a memorable experience in the heart of Manhattan!
