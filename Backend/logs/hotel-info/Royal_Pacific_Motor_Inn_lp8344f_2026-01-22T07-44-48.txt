IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that the maximum length of stay at this property is 21 days.

Parking policy:
• One standard sized parking space per guest room is included for free.
• Additional vehicles are subject to fees.
• You may be asked to give your car keys to the valet/front desk if the upper lot is full so that we may move cars in the morning.

Parking space vehicle size limits:
• Height: 6 ft., 6 in. (78 in. or 6.5 ft)
• Length: 17 ft. (204 in. or 17 ft)
• A space is NOT guaranteed for vehicles that exceed the size limits ("oversize") and you may be referred to our partner off-site facilities, which will charge fees.
• Examples of oversize vehicles include large vans, full-size pickup trucks, campers, large trailers, or similar vehicles.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 0 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$10 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
