HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Elegance with Modern ComfortsNestled between the River Thames and Trafalgar Square, this 4-star hotel seamlessly blends history with contemporary style. The Grade I listed building boasts stunning views of the London Eye and is a stone's throw away from iconic landmarks like the National Gallery and Covent Garden.Luxurious AmenitiesThe Victorian-inspired bedrooms offer a perfect fusion of elegance and modernity, complete with complimentary Wi-Fi, Elemis toiletries, a 49-inch LCD TV, and refreshing mineral water.Fine Dining and Scenic ViewsIndulge in exquisite culinary delights at the One Twenty One Two Restaurant, serving up a delectable array of dishes and wines.
