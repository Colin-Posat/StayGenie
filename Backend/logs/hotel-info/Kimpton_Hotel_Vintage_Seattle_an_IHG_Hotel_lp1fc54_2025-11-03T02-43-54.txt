HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsFurnished in rich tones inspired by Washington Wine Country, Kimpton Hotel Vintage Seattle offers stylish rooms with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, in-room safe, and minibar. Guests can indulge in premium bath products and cozy bathrobes during their stay.Wellness and FitnessStay active during your visit with access to the 24-hour fitness center or unwind with the &quot;Om Away From Home&quot; in-room yoga program. Enjoy the Yoga Channel available on every TV, ensuring a relaxing and rejuvenating experience.Convenient LocationIdeally situated in the heart of Seattle, our hotel is just a short walk from Pike Place Market and close to many popular attractions including Pioneer Square and the Waterfront Neighborhood. Book your stay now at Kimpton Hotel Vintage Seattle for a memorable and luxurious experience.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Guests reported generally clean rooms, although a few mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Overall, the staff was helpful and accommodating, but some guests had complaints about slow response times and lack of cleaning services.
• Location: 7.8/10 - The hotel's location was positively highlighted for its convenience to attractions, but some guests felt it was lacking nearby amenities.
• Room Quality: 7.6/10 - Rooms were described as spacious and comfortable, yet there were complaints about noisy pull-out beds and uncomfortable mattresses.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - The provided amenities were satisfactory, but there were comments about the lack of certain services such as spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 5.2/10 - Several guests felt that the hotel was overpriced for the services provided, including issues with room cleanliness, bed comfort, and customer service.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - While breakfast was generally well-received, some guests expressed concerns about limited selection and inconsistent quality.
• Overall Experience: 7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience but with mixed feelings about specific aspects of the hotel.
