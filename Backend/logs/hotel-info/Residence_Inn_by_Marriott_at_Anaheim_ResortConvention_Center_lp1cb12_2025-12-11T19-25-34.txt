HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spacious Suites and Disney Themed RoomsExperience the magic just 1 mile from Disneyland Adventure Park at our Residence Inn. Our warmly decorated rooms feature granite bathroom countertops and fully equipped kitchens. Choose from a variety of suites, including Disney themed suites and kids' suites with bunk beds.Convenient Amenities for FamiliesEnjoy free access to the fitness center, rooftop pool with cabanas, and daily breakfast during your stay. Our onsite convenience store and concierge desk are available to assist with all your needs.Ideal Location for Business and LeisureWith a meeting room available and close proximity to the Anaheim Convention Center and Riverview Golf Course, our hotel is perfect for both business and leisure travelers. Book now and make the most of your Anaheim visit!

What Guests Love:
• Location proximity to theme parks
• Friendly staff
• Clean rooms

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• No spa services
• View and noise issues in rooms
• Mixed reviews on value for money

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were clean overall, but some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Service was highly appreciated with friendly and helpful staff, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel's close proximity to theme parks and shops was a major highlight for guests.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were spacious and well-equipped, but some guests faced issues with the view and noise from machinery.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, notably the spa/pool deck area, but some guests mentioned the lack of variety in breakfast.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews regarding the value for money, with some guests finding it reasonable given the location, while others felt it was expensive for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests desired more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the location, cleanliness, and friendly staff.
