HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and AmenitiesLocated in the heart of the 15th district of Paris, just a short walk from the iconic Eiffel Tower, ibis Paris Tour Eiffel offers a 24-hour reception, free Wi-Fi, and convenient luggage storage. With Cambronne Metro Station a mere 150 meters away, exploring the city is effortless.Comfortable Rooms and Delicious BreakfastEach room at ibis Paris Tour Eiffel is equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, wardrobe, and a private bathroom with a hairdryer. Start your day with a delightful buffet breakfast featuring a variety of sweet and savory options, freshly baked pastries, and French Madeleine cakes.Nearby Attractions and Easy AccessImmerse yourself in the beauty of Paris with the River Seine and Hotel des Invalides just a short stroll away. Additionally, the Champs de Mars RER Station is nearby, providing direct access to the magnificent Château de Versailles.Book your stay at ibis Paris Tour Eiffel now for an unforgettable Parisian experience!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Rooms and hotel's overall cleanliness have been positively highlighted by many guests.
• Service: 6.8/10 - Mixed feelings about service were reported; some guests were satisfied while others mentioned indifference from staff.
• Location: 9/10 - Guests have consistently rated the location highly due to its proximity to public transportation and key attractions.
• Room Quality: 6.4/10 - While some guests found rooms to be comfortable, concerns were raised about size, lack of amenities, and the absence of air conditioning.
• Amenities: 5.9/10 - Guests appreciated some amenities but felt the lack of basics such as kettles, fridges, and English TV channels was a drawback.
• Value for Money: 6.2/10 - Some guests felt the prices were reasonable for the location, while others expressed concerns about the cost given the room issues and lack of service.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - While the variety and quality of breakfast were generally praised, some guests felt it was overcrowded and too expensive when purchased separately.
• Overall Experience: 7.4/10 - Most guests had a good overall impression but acknowledged areas where improvements could enhance their stay.
