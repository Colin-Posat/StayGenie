HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Panoramic Views and Unique SuitesExperience breathtaking panoramic views of Fira Bay and the volcano from our traditional suites perched on the cliff's edge. Each suite boasts uniquely shaped king size beds and a furnished balcony or terrace for enjoying Santorini's famous sunsets.Convenient Amenities and ServicesEnjoy modern conveniences such as wireless internet, satellite TV, and free Wi-Fi throughout the property. Let our helpful staff assist you with airport shuttle service, bicycle or car rentals, ensuring a seamless stay.Prime Location and Relaxing PoolSituated on the Caldera Cliffs, our property's vertical spread leads to a stunning swimming pool overlooking the Aegean Sea and the Volcano. With Fira just a stone's throw away, explore the old quarter or embark on boat excursions easily.Book your stay at Porto Fira Suites for an unforgettable Santorini getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are kindly requested to let the property know their arrival details (including flight number and airline company or ferry boat name), so the property can arrange porterage of your luggage from Metropolis Church in Fira to Porto Fira Suites.

Please note that guests must climb 86 stairs in order to reach the property. There is no elevator at the property.

The property is spread vertically on the Caldera Cliffs and the Hotel's Swimming pool is located on the lowest level, overlooking the Aegean Sea and the Volcano!"
