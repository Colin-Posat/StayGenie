HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Beach-Front Rooms with Stunning ViewsIndulge in the beauty of Tulum Beach with our beach-front rooms featuring terraces and a lounge area. Each room is elegantly decorated with a minimalist touch, providing a serene atmosphere for your stay. Enjoy the sound of the waves and the breathtaking views right from your room.Relaxing Amenities for a Tranquil StayUnwind by the outdoor pool, pamper yourself at the onsite spa, or explore the onsite shop for souvenirs. Whether you prefer lounging on the beach or taking a dip in the pool, our hotel offers the perfect blend of relaxation and luxury amenities.Culinary Delights and Beachside RefreshmentsSavor a delicious full breakfast at Ziggy's restaurant or Fresco's, and sip on refreshing drinks at Ziggy's Beach Club while enjoying the stunning ocean views. Our beach club offers a variety of snacks to keep you energized throughout the day.Experience the ultimate beach getaway at The Beach Tulum Hotel. Book now for an unforgettable adults-only retreat by the sea.

• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Spacious and comfortable rooms contributed to a pleasant stay, although minor maintenance issues were occasionally reported.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the provided amenities satisfactory, with some highlighting the gym and pool while others noted the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the pricing, with some guests feeling the cost was justified given the location, while others believed it was slightly high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were well-received overall, although a few guests suggested more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, citing a welcoming atmosphere and enjoyable stay.
