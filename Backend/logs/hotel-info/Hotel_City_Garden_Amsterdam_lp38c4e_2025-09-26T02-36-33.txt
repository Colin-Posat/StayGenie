IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
A damage deposit of EUR 50 is required on arrival. This will be collected as a cash payment. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full in cash, subject to an inspection of the property.
Please note in case of a non-refundable booking, the total price of the reservation may be charged anytime after booking. It will be charged in the guest's local currency.

Please note that guests are required to show the credit card used to make the booking upon arrival. If this is not possible, guests should have an authorization form, signed by the credit card holder if he/she is not traveling.

Please note that Hotel City Garden reserves the right to pre-authorize your card prior to arrival.

When booked 5 rooms or more, it will be considered as a group reservation. In this case the hotel reserves the right to ask a prepayment as a guarantee for the reservation. The prepayment amount is 10% of the total reservation value, acceptable by bank transfer only. Credit card payment is not acceptable for guarantee of reservation. Please note that such reservation will not be confirmed before the hotel receives the prepayment.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.4/10 - Overall, guests appreciated the cleanliness of the hotel. However, some reviews mentioned issues such as bed bugs and dusty rooms.
• Service: 7.1/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests praising friendly staff while others expressed dissatisfaction with their interactions.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was a standout feature for many guests, offering easy access to museums, tram stops, and parks.
• Room Quality: 6.4/10 - Room quality varied widely across reviews, with positive comments on spaciousness but negative remarks on uncomfortable beds and maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - Guest opinions on amenities were mixed, with some mentioning positive aspects like free coffee but others noting the lack of air conditioning and spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Perceptions of value for money were diverse, with some guests feeling the hotel was overpriced while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Food and beverage services generally received positive feedback, particularly regarding buffet breakfast and restaurant offerings.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience and would consider staying at the hotel again, despite some specific complaints.
