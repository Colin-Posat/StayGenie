HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationExperience luxury at its finest at The Shay, a Destination by Hyatt in Los Angeles. Indulge in air-conditioned rooms equipped with modern amenities like a flat-screen TV, fridge, and a private bathroom.Top-Notch FacilitiesEnjoy a range of facilities including a fitness centre, restaurant, bar, and room service during your stay. Unwind in the shared lounge or on the terrace, and make use of the safety deposit box and 24-hour front desk for added convenience.Convenient LocationSituated just 5 km from Petersen Automotive Museum, with easy access to Los Angeles County Museum Of Art and Venice Beach Boardwalk, The Shay offers the perfect blend of comfort and convenience. Book now for an unforgettable stay!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  USD 50 per  day.
