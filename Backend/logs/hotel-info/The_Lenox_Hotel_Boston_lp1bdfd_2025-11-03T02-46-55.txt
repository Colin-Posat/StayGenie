HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant charm of Boston at The Lenox Hotel, your premier destination for comfort and luxury in the heart of the city.Unmatched AmenitiesIndulge in our top-notch offerings, including a 24-hour fitness center and bike rentals for exploring the bustling streets. Enjoy seamless connectivity with complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the property, as well as attentive concierge services to cater to your every need.Culinary DelightsSavor delectable meals at our three on-site restaurants, including Willow &amp; Ivy, or enjoy the convenience of 24-hour room service. Start your day off right with our full breakfast buffet, ensuring you have the energy to explore Boston.Convenient Business FacilitiesWith 3000 square feet of versatile meeting space and a 24-hour business center, The Lenox is the perfect venue for conferences or special events. Our express check-in and limo service add an extra touch of ease to your stay.Don't miss out on an unforgettable experience—book your stay at The Lenox Hotel Boston today!
