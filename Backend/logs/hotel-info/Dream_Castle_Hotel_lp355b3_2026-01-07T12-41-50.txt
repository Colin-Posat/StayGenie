HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Step into a world of elegance and comfort at Dream Castle Hotel, where luxury meets family-friendly excitement just minutes from Disneyland® Paris.Relax and UnwindIndulge yourself at our full-service spa offering rejuvenating massages, body treatments, and facials. For those seeking recreation, dive into our indoor pool, unwind in the hot tub, or detox in the sauna.Savor Exquisite DiningTreat your taste buds at Le Bosquet Gourmand, our in-house French restaurant, or enjoy a delightful meal with room service. Start your day right with a complimentary buffet breakfast served daily.Convenient AmenitiesComplimentary Wi-Fi keeps you connected, while our concierge services ensure a stress-free stay. With spacious conference facilities and an airport shuttle, we cater to both leisure and business travelers.Don’t miss out on an unforgettable stay—book your getaway at Dream Castle Hotel today!
