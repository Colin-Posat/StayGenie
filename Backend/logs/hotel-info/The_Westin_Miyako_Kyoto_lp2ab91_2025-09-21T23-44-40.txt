HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Westin Miyako in Kyoto offers elegantly furnished rooms with modern amenities including a minibar and LCD TV. Enjoy a comfortable stay surrounded by wooden furniture and complimentary water bottles.Wellness and Leisure FacilitiesIndulge in relaxation at the indoor pool, fitness center, and spa treatments available at the hotel. Take advantage of the free WiFi throughout the property and unwind in style during your stay in Kyoto.Convenient Location and DiningBenefit from the hotel's prime location near Keage subway station and the iconic Gion district. Explore various dining options ranging from Buffet to Teppanyaki cuisines without leaving the comfort of the hotel.Experience luxury and convenience at The Westin Miyako - book your stay now for an unforgettable experience in Kyoto!

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but some reviews highlighted minor cleanliness issues.
• Service: 8.8/10 - Service was generally appreciated by guests, with many highlighting the helpful and friendly staff.
• Location: 7.5/10 - The hotel's location received mixed reviews, with some guests finding it convenient while others mentioned it was a bit far from downtown Kyoto.
• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Rooms were generally praised for being spacious, cozy, and comfortable, although some guests had issues with the lack of closet space.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the spa, fitness center, and pool; however, some guests noted the lack of variety in food options.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Guests had mixed opinions on the value for money, with some feeling the price was high compared to other hotels they stayed in.
• Food and Beverage: 7.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, although some guests felt there could be more variety in the offerings.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, with high praise for the staff and amenities.
