Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was highly appreciated with staff going above and beyond, ensuring a top-notch experience for guests.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and the jungle was greatly valued by guests.
• Room Quality: 8.3/10 - Spacious and comfortable rooms were a highlight, with minor maintenance issues mentioned by some guests.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Satisfactory amenities were noted, with a mix of positive feedback on the gym and pool and some complaints about the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some guests feeling the price was slightly high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services generally well-received, though some guests desired more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.6/10 - Most guests had a highly positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
