HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for CAD 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  CAD 30 per  day.

Category Ratings:

• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's central location near water taxi and main shopping street was highly praised by guests.
• Room Quality: 6.7/10 - Room quality received mixed reviews with mentions of clean and spacious rooms, but also complaints about room layout and poor design.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities provided, including parking facilities, cleanliness, and safety measures.
• Value for Money: 5.3/10 - Mixed feedback on value for money, with some guests feeling the premium price did not match the overall experience.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested room for improvement in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience with the hotel, despite some issues with service and room quality.
