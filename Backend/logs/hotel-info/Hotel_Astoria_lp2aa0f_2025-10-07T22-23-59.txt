HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience the perfect blend of Art Nouveau charm and modern comfort at Astoria Hotel. Relax in air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, and elegant wooden floors. Indulge in the convenience of a safe, minibar, and modern bathrooms with a hairdryer.Rooftop Pool and GymUnwind and enjoy stunning views from our rooftop pool or stay active at the well-equipped gym. Whether you prefer a refreshing swim or a workout session, our facilities cater to your relaxation and fitness needs.Culinary DelightsSavor traditional Catalan cuisine at our restaurant, or opt for the convenience of room service. With a terrace and bar on-site, you can enjoy delicious meals and drinks in a cozy ambiance.Book your stay at Astoria Hotel now for a memorable and delightful experience!

Category Ratings:

• Service: 8.1/10 - Staff service was highlighted as friendly and attentive by many guests, with a few exceptions mentioning lack of staff presence at certain points.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The central location of the hotel received positive feedback from guests, with mentions of proximity to key landmarks and quiet surroundings.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Overall, guests appreciated the comfortable beds and room cleanliness, with some noting small room sizes.
• Amenities: 7.8/10 - Guests mostly enjoyed the amenities such as the pool and gym, though some mentioned the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with some finding the prices reasonable for the location and service, while others felt it was too expensive.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, though suggestions for more variety were noted.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive experience and would recommend the hotel, with minor issues such as room sizes and bathroom conditions.
