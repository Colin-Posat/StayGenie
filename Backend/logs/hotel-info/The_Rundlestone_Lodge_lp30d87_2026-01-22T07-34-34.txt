HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Breathtaking Mountain ViewsNestled in the heart of Banff Avenue, The Rundlestone Lodge offers stunning views of the Canadian Rockies.Modern Comforts and AmenitiesEach guestroom is equipped with a 42” HDTV, a refrigerator, and tea &amp; coffee making facilities. Relax in comfort with a work desk, seating area, and some rooms even feature a private balcony.Relax and RejuvenateUnwind by the indoor pool, cozy up by the grand fireplace in the lobby, or stay active in the fitness centre. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Groups: Bookings of 3 rooms or more will be classed as a group reservation and charged a 1 night plus tax as a deposit. A group cancellation policy of 30 days prior arrival will be applicable. Changes or cancelations will need to be made at least 30 days in advance or the 1 night deposit will be forfeited. There must be a name attached to each room.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
