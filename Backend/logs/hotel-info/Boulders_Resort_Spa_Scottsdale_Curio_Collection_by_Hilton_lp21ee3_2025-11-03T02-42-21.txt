HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and AmenitiesLocated in the stunning Sonoran Desert foothills of Scottsdale, Arizona, The Boulders Resort &amp; Spa, Curio Collection by Hilton offers a lavish retreat for those seeking relaxation and recreation. The resort boasts spacious accommodations with modern amenities, including flat-screen TVs, private balconies or patios, and free WiFi.World-Class Golf and Spa ExperiencesGuests can enjoy two championship golf courses, four swimming pools, and a state-of-the-art fitness center with whirlpool tubs and saunas. After a day of activities, unwind at the full-service spa offering massages, yoga, and pilates classes. Indulge in gourmet dining options at the resort's seven restaurants, including Palo Verde serving delicious southwestern dishes.Explore the Desert OasisConveniently located near hiking trails and popular attractions like Desert Botanical Gardens, The Boulders Resort &amp; Spa is the perfect destination for a memorable desert getaway. Book now for an unforgettable holiday experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$50 per person per stay when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
