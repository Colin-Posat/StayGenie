HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Discover the serene ambiance of Ubud Village Hotel, perfectly nestled just a 10-minute stroll from Ubud Market and Ubud Palace.Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in our elegantly designed deluxe and suite rooms, featuring dark wood furnishings, marble floors, and relaxing views. Each room is equipped with modern amenities, including a minibar and satellite TV for your entertainment.Relaxation and WellnessUnwind at our scenic spa center or take a refreshing dip in one of our two beautiful swimming pools. Your well-being is our priority, with a 24-hour on-call doctor available for peace of mind.Gourmet Dining ExperienceSavor an exceptional dining journey at Maluku Restaurant, where a delightful array of Asian and Western dishes awaits you, all while overlooking our stunning fish pond.Experience luxury and comfort in Ubud Village Hotel; book your stay today!

HOTEL POLICIES:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
