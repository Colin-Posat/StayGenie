HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and SpaBackstage Boutique SPA Hotel, centrally located in Zermatt, offers uniquely furnished rooms with fireplaces and free WiFi. Each room boasts a private balcony, CD/DVD player, and bathrobes. The majority of rooms feature a sofa and freestanding bathtub with stunning views of the Swiss Alps.Relaxing Spa FacilitiesIndulge in the spa area with a hot tub, sauna, steam bath, and massage room. Rejuvenate your senses and unwind in the tranquil ambiance of the spa.Award-Winning Dining and Convenient LocationSavor exquisite cuisine at the Michelin-starred restaurant After Seven by Ivo Adam. With a terrace and bar for relaxation, the hotel is also close to Gornergrat and Sunnega railways, ski bus stops, a tennis court, and a skating rink. Book your stay now for a memorable experience at Backstage Boutique SPA Hotel.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Backstage Boutique SPA Hotel in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Please note that Zermatt is a car-free resort. Cars can be parked at the nearby town of Täsch (at a cost), where there is a regular train service running to Zermatt.

Please note that cats are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for CHF 70 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
