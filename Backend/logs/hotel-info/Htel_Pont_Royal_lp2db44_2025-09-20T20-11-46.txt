HOTEL DESCRIPTION:

Indulge in complimentary Wi-Fi throughout your stay and enjoy convenient amenities like a hair salon and gift shop right on-site.Culinary DelightsSavor exquisite meals at our restaurant or take advantage of our 24-hour room service.
