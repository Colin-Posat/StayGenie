HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Nice is ideally situated just 300 meters from Piazza Tasso, making it a perfect choice for exploring Sorrento. With the bus and railway stations within walking distance, guests can easily navigate the city.Comfortable AccommodationEnjoy a pleasant stay in our air-conditioned rooms equipped with satellite TV. Additionally, indulge in a delicious continental buffet breakfast to start your day off right.Book your stay at Hotel Nice now and experience the perfect blend of convenience and comfort.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. No extra charges.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 20 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children from 2 years old to 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for € 35 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People 3 years old and over stay for € 35 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
