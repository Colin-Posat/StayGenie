IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Guests who wish to go on a rickshaw tour must notify the property at least 3 days prior to the date of arrival. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

A free taxi transport is available from JR Kyoto Station with an advance reservation. Guest who wish to use this service must notify the property at least 3 days prior to the date of arrival.
Please be informed that your transportation to/from the hotel may be delayed depending on traffic conditions during the autumn season.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  JPY 2200 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 2 years old and over stay for ¥8,855 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Guests consistently mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, although a few guests had complaints about wait times.
• Location: 8.4/10 - The hotel is located in close proximity to many key landmarks and public transport.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 5.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
