HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed).

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 275,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
You haven't added any cots.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Beautiful location
• Friendly staff

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• Lack of spa services

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.5/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, although some reviews reported issues like dirty mattresses and bed sheets.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with many guests praising the friendly and helpful staff. However, a few reviews mentioned delays in service.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location in the middle of rice fields was well-received, offering a tranquil and beautiful setting. Some guests found it a bit far from major attractions.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as spacious, beautifully decorated, and comfortable, with some reports of minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with positive mentions of the pool and garden. Some guests noted the lack of spa services as a drawback.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests finding the prices high compared to the offered services, while others thought it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Feedback on breakfast and restaurant services was generally positive, although some guests suggested more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the peaceful atmosphere, friendly staff, and beautiful location.
