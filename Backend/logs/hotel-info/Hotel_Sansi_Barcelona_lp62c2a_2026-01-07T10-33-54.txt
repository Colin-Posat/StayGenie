HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Sansi Barcelona is ideally located in the heart of Barcelona's Eixample district, just steps away from popular attractions like Plaça Catalunya and Casa Batllo. With Passeig de Gracia Metro Station nearby, exploring the city is a breeze.Modern AmenitiesEnjoy the comfort of air-conditioned rooms equipped with a desk, safety deposit box, flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom with a relaxing bath. Stay connected with complimentary WiFi throughout the hotel.Nearby AttractionsImmerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Barcelona with attractions like Boqueria Market, Tivoli Theatre, and Palau de la Musica Catalana within walking distance.
