HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodations and Convenient LocationNestled between Kampong Glam and Bugis, Andaz Singapore offers a unique stay with locally-inspired designs and breathtaking city views. The hotel's prime location near Bugis MRT Station and Bugis Junction makes exploring Singapore a breeze.Unparalleled Dining ExperienceIndulge in a culinary journey at Andaz Singapore with a variety of dining options to choose from. Whether it's savoring Singapore street food at Alley on 25, enjoying premium cuts at 665°F steakhouse, or relishing refined Cantonese cuisine at 5 ON 25, there's something to satisfy every palate.Rooftop Bar with Stunning ViewsUnwind at the rooftop bar, Mr. Stork, and sip on your favorite drink while marveling at panoramic views of downtown Singapore. The perfect spot to relax and soak in the vibrant cityscape.Experience luxury and convenience at Andaz Singapore – book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for S$ 0 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for S$ 0 per person per stay when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for S$ 100 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not possible) and costs  SGD 2.57 per  hour.
