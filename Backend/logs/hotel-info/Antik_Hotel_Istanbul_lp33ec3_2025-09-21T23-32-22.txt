HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical CharmAntik Hotel, located in the heart of Istanbul, offers a unique experience inspired by the city's rich history. Each floor of the hotel represents a different period of Istanbul's past, providing guests with a glimpse into the city's cultural heritage.Panoramic Views and Rooftop TerraceGuests can unwind at the rooftop terrace with a refreshing drink in hand, while taking in the breathtaking panoramic view of the Marmara Sea. The perfect spot to relax and soak in the beauty of Istanbul.Convenience and ComfortWith amenities like free WiFi, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV in every room, Antik Hotel ensures a comfortable stay for all guests. The 24-hour front desk, concierge service, and on-site dining options add convenience to your Istanbul adventure.Experience the historical charm, stunning views, and convenience at Antik Hotel. HOTEL POLICIES:

PETS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 6 years old to 11 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.2/10 - Guests consistently praised the cleanliness of the rooms.
• Service: 8.4/10 - Staff were generally friendly and helpful, with some exceptions noted.
• Location: 8.7/10 - The hotel's location near key attractions and public transportation was highly appreciated.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were described as comfortable and clean, with occasional size and maintenance issues noted.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests missing spa services and higher-end toiletries.
• Value for Money: 7/10 - Opinions varied on the value offered for the price, with some guests feeling it was reasonable and others expecting more.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Breakfast and restaurant options received generally positive feedback, though diversity could be improved.
• Overall Experience: 8.4/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed willingness to recommend the hotel to others.
