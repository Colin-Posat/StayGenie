HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location at Tokyo StationHotel Metropolitan Tokyo Marunouchi is a mere 1-minute walk from Otemachi Station, offering unparalleled convenience for travelers. Situated in the heart of the city, guests can easily access various attractions and transportation hubs.Luxurious AmenitiesThe hotel boasts air-conditioned guestrooms with a modern interior, each equipped with a fridge and a flat-screen TV. Guests can indulge in massage services, utilize the 24-hour business center, and enjoy complimentary WiFi throughout the property.Spectacular Dining ExperienceTreat yourself to exquisite French and Japanese dishes at the upscale TENQOO restaurant and bar on the 27th floor, providing stunning views of the bustling Tokyo cityscape. Immerse yourself in a culinary journey without leaving the hotel premises.Experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at Hotel Metropolitan Tokyo Marunouchi. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Tokyo getaway.
