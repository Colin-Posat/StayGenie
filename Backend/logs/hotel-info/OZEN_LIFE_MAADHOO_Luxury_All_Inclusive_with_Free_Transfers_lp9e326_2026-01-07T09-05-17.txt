HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious All Inclusive ExperienceOZEN LIFE MAADHOO - Luxury All Inclusive offers a premium experience with a private beach area, year-round outdoor pool, and a children's playground. The INDULGENCE Plan includes fine dining, top-shelf spirits, water sports, and spa or dive experiences.Unique Dining OptionsIndulge in specialty fine dining at the underwater restaurant, M6m, offering modern seafood cuisine with stunning views of marine life. Degustation menus with wine pairings are available for a memorable culinary experience.Exciting Activities and FacilitiesGuests can enjoy water sports, free bike hire, and curated excursions like snorkeling and sunset fishing. The resort also provides a 24-hour front desk, shared lounge, and a gift shop for a seamless stay.Experience luxury and adventure at OZEN LIFE MAADHOO - Luxury All Inclusive.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for US$6 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for US$175 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 5 years old to 11 years old stay for US$175 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
