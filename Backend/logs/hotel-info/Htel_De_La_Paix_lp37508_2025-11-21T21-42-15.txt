IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
For the apartment:
Check-in is from 17:00 and check-out is until 12:00.
Cleaning is available at an additional cost.

Please note that parking is limited and should be reserved in advance. You can do this by leaving a message in the Special Requests box or calling the hotel directly. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

Please note that your credit card will be pre-authorized for the first night, to guarantee the reservation.

If adult guests get the paid breakfast option, breakfast for children under 7 years old will be free of charge.

Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot in the double rooms only, upon prior request. Please contact the property in advance if you will be coming with children.

The credit card used to make the booking must be presented upon arrival. The city tax is also due upon arrival.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
