HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Eiffel Tower and Metro StationLocated in the heart of the 7th district in Paris, Hôtel Le Beaugency offers a prime location just 1 km from the iconic Eiffel Tower and a stone's throw away from Ecole Militaire Metro Station. Guests can easily explore the city's renowned attractions with convenient access to public transportation.Charming Rooms with Modern AmenitiesThe air-conditioned guest rooms at Hôtel Le Beaugency are tastefully decorated with wooden furnishings and vibrant fabrics, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Each room is equipped with a minibar and a modern en suite bathroom with a hairdryer for added convenience.Delicious Continental Breakfast and Nearby AttractionsStart your day with a delicious continental breakfast featuring French pastries and hot beverages, served amidst a relaxing ambiance. Additionally, the hotel's proximity to Varenne Metro Station and the Rodin Museum allows guests to easily explore the city's cultural and historical treasures.Ready to experience the charm of Paris?
