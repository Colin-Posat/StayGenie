HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Disney Concierge Desk and Free BreakfastLocated just 1.6 km from Disneyland, Embassy Suites by Hilton Anaheim South offers a unique experience with a Disney concierge desk for all your theme park needs. Start your day with a complimentary cooked-to-order breakfast before heading out to explore the local activities.Garden Atrium and Fitness FacilitiesUnwind in the 14-story garden atrium filled with tropical plants, waterfalls, and koi ponds. Stay active at the state-of-the-art fitness room or take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool during your stay at this hotel.Convenient Dining Options and Nearby AttractionsIndulge in delicious meals at the on-site Serengeti Restaurant and explore a variety of dining and shopping options within walking distance. With the Anaheim Convention Center nearby and Knott's Berry Farm just 9.6 km away, there's never a dull moment at Embassy Suites by Hilton Anaheim South.
