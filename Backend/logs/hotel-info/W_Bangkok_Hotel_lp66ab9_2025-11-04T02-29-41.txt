HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Accommodation with Modern AmenitiesW Bangkok Hotel, located just a 3-minute walk from Chong Nonsi BTS Skytrain Station, offers a luxurious stay with contemporary amenities. The stylish rooms feature flat-screen TVs, iPod docking stations, and en suite bathrooms with premium toiletries.Rejuvenating Spa and Outdoor PoolGuests can unwind with rejuvenating spa treatments or take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool. The hotel's fitness centre is perfect for those looking to stay active during their stay. Additionally, the 24-hour reception ensures that guests' needs are always taken care of.Diverse Dining OptionsIndulge in a culinary journey at W Bangkok Hotel with a variety of dining options. From international cuisines at The Kitchen Table to modern Thai seafood dishes at Paii, there is something to satisfy every palate. End your day with live music and drinks at The Bar at The House on Sathorn.Ready to experience luxury and comfort at W Bangkok Hotel? Book your stay now for an unforgettable experience!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in public areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
People no matter the age stay for THB 1,121 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
