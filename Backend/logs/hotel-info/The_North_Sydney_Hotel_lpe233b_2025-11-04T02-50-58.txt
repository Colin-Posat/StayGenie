HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient AmenitiesThe North Sydney Hotel offers a range of convenient amenities for guests to enjoy, including an on-site bar, bistro, gaming area, outdoor smoking area, and communal living room with a kitchen stocked with complimentary essentials.Modern ComfortsEach room at The North Sydney Hotel is equipped with modern comforts such as free WiFi, a flat-screen Smart TV, individually controlled air-conditioning and heating, complimentary toiletries, and daily housekeeping services.Ideal LocationLocated just 1.9 km from Luna Park Sydney and 2.3 km from Harbour Bridge, The North Sydney Hotel provides easy access to some of Sydney's top attractions. Book your stay now and experience comfort and convenience in the heart of the city.

In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that all rooms are strictly non-smoking. Smoking is allowed in the outdoor area.

Please note that there is a 1.5% charge when you pay with a Visa & Mastercard credit card.

Please note that there is a 2% charge when you pay with an American Express and Diners credit card.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Private parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is needed) and costs  AUD 35 per  day.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for AUD 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for AUD 30 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for AUD 30 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Adults  stay for AUD 50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - Rooms were comfortable and quiet, with some guests highlighting the authentic feeling and decor.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with positive mentions of the onsite restaurant and simple breakfast options. However, some guests noted the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests finding the price reasonable for the location, while others felt it was slightly high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received by guests, although a few suggested an increase in variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
