HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Courtyard New York Manhattan/Upper East Side offers elegantly decorated rooms with modern amenities, including free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. Enjoy a comfortable stay with coffee-making facilities and a work desk in each room.Convenient FacilitiesTake advantage of the fitness centre, 24-hour reception, and fully equipped business centre during your stay. Additional services like dry cleaning and self-service lockers ensure a hassle-free experience for guests.Prime LocationSituated in Manhattan’s Upper East Side, the hotel is a short walk from iconic attractions such as the Guggenheim Museum and Central Park. With easy access to public transportation, exploring popular destinations like Times Square and Yankee Stadium is a breeze.Book your stay at Courtyard New York Manhattan/Upper East Side for a memorable and convenient experience in the city.
