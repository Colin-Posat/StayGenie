HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe Ritz-Carlton Key Biscayne, Miami offers suites with separate living rooms and balconies, providing a spacious and comfortable stay for guests. Marble bathrooms with lavish amenities ensure a relaxing experience.Exquisite Dining OptionsFrom oceanfront dining at Lightkeepers to the Cuban-inspired Rumbar, the hotel offers a diverse culinary experience. Guests can savor modern-coastal dishes and a wide selection of rums in a glamorous setting.Pampering Spa and RecreationWith 25,000 square feet of spa facilities, including saunas and a eucalyptus steam room, guests can indulge in a rejuvenating spa experience. The hotel also features outdoor pools, tennis courts, and a fitness center for leisure and relaxation.Experience luxury and relaxation at The Ritz-Carlton Key Biscayne, Miami. Book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The resort fee includes the following: 
- Welcome drinks one time upon arrival for 2 guests 
- Daily tennis court usage for one hour, a can of balls, refreshments, and towels for two guests (ADA Accessible) 
- Daily fitness class for two guests or in room Fitness Kit (ADA Accessible) 
- Daily one hour non motorized beach activity (beach wheelchair, kayak, SUP, snorkel/fins) for two guests.
Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In the event of an early departure, the property will charge you the full amount for your stay.

• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Rooms were described as clean, spacious, and suitable for families, although some areas showed signs of age.
• Amenities: 7.9/10 - Overall, guests found the amenities satisfactory, with some areas like the lounge access and e-bikes receiving special mention.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the value for money aspect, with some guests feeling the price was justified for the experience offered, while others thought it could be improved.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - The quality and variety of food received positive feedback, although some mentioned crowded bar areas during peak times.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the magical holiday ambiance and attentive staff.
