• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some finding the price high for the services offered and others considering it reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received generally positive feedback, although some guests felt there could be more variety.
