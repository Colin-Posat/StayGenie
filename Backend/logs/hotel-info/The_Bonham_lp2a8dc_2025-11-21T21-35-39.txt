HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
City Views and Central LocationLocated in Edinburgh's West End, The Bonham Hotel offers stunning city views from the majority of its Victorian townhouse bedrooms. With popular attractions like Princess Street, Calton Hill, and Edinburgh Castle just a short walk away, guests can easily immerse themselves in the heart of the city.Luxurious Rooms and DiningEach room at The Bonham features modern amenities such as a flat screen TV, free WiFi, and an en-suite bathroom with complimentary toiletries. Guests can enjoy 24-hour room service and indulge in traditional European cuisine at the hotel's restaurant, along with a variety of classic cocktails and afternoon tea at the bar.Convenient Transport OptionsWith Edinburgh Airport just a 20-minute drive away, guests have easy access to and from the property. Alternatively, the Edinburgh Tram and Airlink bus provide convenient transport options for exploring the city. Book your stay at The Bonham Hotel for a memorable Edinburgh experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please note that The Bonham is strictly a nonsmoking property.

Please note that The Bonham Hotel has a car park to the rear of the building, which is used on a first come first serve basis. Parking tickets are issued on arrival in the car park and should be paid for at reception.

When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

Payment will be taken upon arrival for all reservations.

Please note that dinner rate plan is a GBP 30 per person allowance.

The Hotel has Electric Vehicle Charging available at £4.00 per hour booking required 24 hours in advance.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Adults  stay for £60 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
