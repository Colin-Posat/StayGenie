HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location:Located just a minute walk from Toyocho Train Station, Sotetsu Fresa Inn Tokyo-Toyocho offers easy access to transportation, making it a perfect base for exploring Tokyo and beyond.Comfortable Rooms and Amenities:Enjoy a restful stay in air-conditioned rooms featuring Sealy brand beds, ensuring a good night's sleep. The rooms are equipped with modern conveniences such as a fridge, electric kettle, and a flat-screen TV with video-on-demand. Plus, unwind in the en suite bathroom with provided amenities and a humidifier for added comfort.Additional Services:Indulge in a relaxing massage, take advantage of the coin-launderette, or utilize services like laptop rentals and dry cleaning offered by the 24-hour front desk. IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Sotetsu Fresa Inn Tokyo-Toyocho in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
The full amount of the reservation must be paid at check-in.

Guests who plan to arrive after check-in hours (midnight) must inform the property in advance. Contact details can be found in the booking confirmation. If the property is not informed, the booking will be treated as a no-show.

Early check-in and late check-out is available for extra charges. Maximum late check-out time is 14:00.

The property is a 1-minute walk from Toyocho Station on the Tokyo Metro Line, exits number 1 or 4. Exit 4 does not have a lift.

Smoking preferences are subject to availability. Please note that if there is no availability, your request will not be granted. For more information, please contact the hotel directly.

If you request a non-smoking room, you may get a room where smells have been neutralised with a deodorising spray.

Please note the hotel cannot arrange any external reservations such as tours, shows or restaurants.

Luggage storage is available for guests before check-in and after check-out. This is available only on guests’ check-in/check-out dates. Please note that the property cannot store large luggage, large amount of luggage, or items requiring refrigeration. For more information, please contact the property directly.

If you wish to send luggage or mail to the property prior to your arrival, please fill out your name and check-in date on each item. Items can be stored for 1 week prior to guests’ arrival. Please note that the property cannot store large luggage, large amount of luggage, or items requiring refrigeration. The property cannot pay on behalf of the guest when items are delivered. For more information, please contact the property directly.

Please note that there are several Sotetsu Fresa Inn properties within Tokyo and in other areas. Please double check the hotel name on your booking confirmation prior to travelling, and make sure you arrive at the right hotel. You will not be accommodated if you arrive at a different Sotetsu Fresa Inn from the one you have booked.
