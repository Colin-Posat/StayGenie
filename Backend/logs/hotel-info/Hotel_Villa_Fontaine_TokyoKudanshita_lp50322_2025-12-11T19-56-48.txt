HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Facilities and Convenient LocationHotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Kudanshita, just a 3-minute walk from Jimbocho Subway Station, offers stylish rooms with free wired internet and dark, relaxing colors.Entertainment and ComfortEach room is equipped with a flat-screen TV with video-on-demand, a fridge, and an electric kettle for your convenience. Enjoy toiletries and a hairdryer in the en suite bathroom.Additional Amenities and ServicesTake advantage of free luggage storage, dry cleaning, and laptop rental services. The hotel also features a coin launderette, vending machines, and a daily breakfast buffet in the lobby.Experience comfort and convenience at Hotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Kudanshita.
