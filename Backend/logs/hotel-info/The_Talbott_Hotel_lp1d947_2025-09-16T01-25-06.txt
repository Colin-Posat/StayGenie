HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxury Accommodations in the Heart of Downtown ChicagoElegant Rooms with Modern Amenities: Each room at The Talbott Hotel features a flat-screen TV with HD channels and individual climate control. Suites offer additional living space for a comfortable stay.Farm-to-Table Dining Experience: Indulge in farm fresh local artisan cheeses and charcuterie at The Talbott Restaurant. Enjoy a variety of American bistro-inspired dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. In-room dining services are also available for your convenience.Convenient Location and On-Site Facilities: Explore the iconic Magnificent Mile just steps away. Take advantage of the state-of-the-art fitness center, flexible meeting space, and laundry services. With top attractions nearby, including Navy Pier and Millennium Park, The Talbott Hotel offers the perfect blend of luxury and convenience.Book your stay at The Talbott Hotel for an unforgettable experience in Chicago!
