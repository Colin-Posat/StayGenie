Breathtaking Mountain ViewsNestled in the heart of Banff Avenue, The Rundlestone Lodge offers stunning views of the Canadian Rockies.Modern Comforts and AmenitiesEach guestroom is equipped with a 42” HDTV, a refrigerator, and tea &amp; coffee making facilities.
