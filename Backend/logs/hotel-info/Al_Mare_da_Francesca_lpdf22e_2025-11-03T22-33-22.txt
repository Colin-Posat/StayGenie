HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation by the SeaInfinity Pool and Sun TerraceAl Mare da Francesca offers guests access to an inviting infinity pool and a relaxing sun terrace, perfect for soaking up the Mediterranean sun in style.Convenient Amenities and ServicesGuests can enjoy air-conditioned rooms with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms, and even sea views in select units. The property also features a shared kitchen, a coffee shop, and the option for packed lunches, ensuring a comfortable stay.Explore the SurroundingsSituated near beautiful beaches like Pittulongu Beach and Spiaggia del Pellicano, guests can easily explore the stunning coastline. Additionally, with a bicycle rental service available, discovering the charming town of Olbia is convenient and enjoyable.Book your stay at Al Mare da Francesca now for a memorable seaside getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform Al Mare da Francesca in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
This property will not accommodate hen, stag or similar parties.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
No internet access available.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  EUR 10 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
People no matter the age stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.
