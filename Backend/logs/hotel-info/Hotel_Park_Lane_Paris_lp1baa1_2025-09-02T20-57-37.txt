HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsIndulge in the comfort of our 4-star boutique hotel, just steps away from Galeries Lafayette. Relax in guest rooms with private balconies and top-notch amenities like a flat-screen TV, iPhone station, and espresso machine.Exquisite Dining and ServicesSavor cocktails at our 24-hour bar or enjoy a buffet breakfast in our charming breakfast room. Take advantage of our tour desk, ticket service, and fitness room, with massages available for ultimate relaxation.Convenient Location and AccessibilityExplore Paris with ease from our centrally located hotel, with Miromesnil Metro Station nearby. With 24-hour reception and free WiFi, your stay at Hotel Park Lane Paris will be seamless and unforgettable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 80 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for € 80 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
