Charming 3-Star Hotel Near The Champes-ElyséesCourtyard Garden Oasis: Enjoy a tranquil stay in our air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi access and a lovely courtyard garden, perfect for relaxing after a day of exploring.Convenient Amenities: Benefit from a minibar, safety deposit box, and elevator access in all guest rooms.
