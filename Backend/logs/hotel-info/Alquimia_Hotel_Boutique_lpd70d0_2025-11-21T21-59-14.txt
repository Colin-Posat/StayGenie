Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but a few instances of cleanliness concerns were noted.
• Service: 7.6/10 - Service was appreciated overall, with some complaints about slow service at the restaurant.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel is situated in a great location close to key amenities, attractions, and the beach.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were well-decorated and comfortable, although there were some negative comments about room size and bathroom setup.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests praising the beach club and free beach access, while others found the lack of in-room amenities disappointing.
• Value for Money: 6.1/10 - Some guests felt the hotel was overpriced for the offerings provided, particularly in comparison to other hotels in the area.
• Food and Beverage: 6.5/10 - Mixed reviews on food quality and dining options, with some guests mentioning limited dining hours and mediocre food.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the location, service, and ambiance despite some shortcomings in amenities and value for money.
