HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Garden Hotel RetreatExceptional Amenities: Hotel de l’Ours Preles offers a range of amenities including a garden, free private parking, a shared lounge, and a terrace. Enjoy the convenience of an ATM, a business center, and complimentary WiFi throughout the property.Culinary Delights: Indulge in a culinary journey at the on-site restaurant serving French, local, and international cuisine. Special dietary needs are catered to with vegetarian, dairy-free, and vegan options available upon request.Recreational Activities: Unwind with a game of table tennis or explore the scenic surroundings through hiking and windsurfing. The hotel's prime location provides easy access to local attractions such as the International Watch and Clock Museum and Forum Fribourg.Book your stay at Hotel de l’Ours Preles for a delightful blend of luxury and comfort.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 13 years old to 17 years old stay for CHF 15 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
