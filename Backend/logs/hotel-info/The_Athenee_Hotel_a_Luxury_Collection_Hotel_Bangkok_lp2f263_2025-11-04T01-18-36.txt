HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Tropical OasisThe Athenee Hotel, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Bangkok, offers a luxurious 5-star experience within 350 meters of BTS Ploen Chit. The hotel features a tropical lagoon-style pool, 8 dining options, and free WiFi.Elegant AccommodationsExperience Thai colonial designs in rooms and suites equipped with a 48-inch LED TV, minibar, and plush seating area. The en suite bathroom offers free toiletries and a separate bath and shower for relaxation.Exceptional Dining and ConvenienceIndulge in international, French, Chinese, Thai, and Japanese cuisines at the 5 dining venues. Enjoy pampering spa treatments, a 24-hour fitness center, and easy access to premier shopping malls and attractions nearby.Book your luxurious stay at The Athenee Hotel, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Bangkok, for a truly unforgettable experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for THB 2,119 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
