HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spectacular Views and Central LocationEnjoy stunning views of Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, and the Bosphorus from our rooftop terrace at Hali Hotel, ideally situated in Sultanahmet. Explore nearby attractions like Hagia Sophia and Topkapi Palace just 500 meters away.Charming Ottoman Decor and Modern ComfortExperience the blend of tradition and modernity in our air-conditioned rooms adorned with locally-produced Ottoman carpets and equipped with flat-screen TVs. Relax in the warm ambiance and enjoy the convenience of en suite bathrooms.Delightful Dining and Convenient AmenitiesStart your day with a traditional Turkish breakfast featuring fresh fruits and Turkish coffee in our dining hall. Unwind at the roof-top bar with panoramic views or opt for room service. With a prime location near Cemberlitas Hammam and the T1 tram stop, exploring Istanbul is a breeze.Ready to experience the beauty of Istanbul? Book your stay at Hali Hotel now!

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for 50% of the adult rate per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
