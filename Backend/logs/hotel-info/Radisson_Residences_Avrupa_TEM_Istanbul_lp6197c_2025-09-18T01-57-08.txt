HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 7 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
Children from 7 years old to 12 years old stay for € 15 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Room Quality: 8.2/10 - Rooms were spacious, comfortable, and modern, with occasional mentions of minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 7.4/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with highlights on the gym, pool, and modern facilities, but some guests mentioned the lack of spa services and high prices for certain items.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - Mixed feedback on pricing, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location and amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, but there were occasional comments about limited food variety and high prices for breakfast items.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the cleanliness, room quality, and friendly staff, but some pointed out shortcomings in service and pricing.
