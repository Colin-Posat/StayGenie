Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.3/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, although some rooms had issues with spiders and hairs in the bathroom.
• Service: 8/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff noted in most reviews.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel is well-located near public transport and key landmarks, making it convenient for guests to explore the area.
• Room Quality: 7.8/10 - Rooms were described as spacious and comfortable with some minor issues such as cramped toilet areas and a lack of wardrobe space.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting room features like TV channels from different nationalities but also pointing out issues like limited wardrobe space.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions varied on the value for money, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and food services received mixed reviews, with some guests enjoying the variety while others found it lacking.
• Overall Experience: 7.6/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some issues with room quality and amenities.
