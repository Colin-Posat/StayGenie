GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Great location
• Friendly staff
• Beautiful lobby and artwork
• Great rooftop bar

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Inconsistent cleanliness
• Lack of certain amenities like a pool or gym

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Overall, guests generally mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, although some reviews highlighted subpar cleanliness in certain areas.
• Service: 8.3/10 - Service was highly appreciated by guests, with many mentioning the friendly and helpful staff. However, a few reviews expressed dissatisfaction with specific service instances.
• Location: 9.1/10 - The hotel's location was a standout feature, with guests consistently praising its proximity to key landmarks, restaurants, and shops, making it easily accessible and convenient.
• Room Quality: 7/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but some guests reported issues such as small room sizes, musty smells, and subpar cleanliness.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - While some guests appreciated the amenities like the rooftop bar, others expressed disappointment with the lack of certain facilities like a pool, gym, or spa services.
• Value for Money: 4.5/10 - Guests had mixed feelings about the value for money, with some feeling that the services offered did not justify the price paid, especially in relation to breakfast quality and included amenities.
• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Feedback on food and beverage services varied, with some guests enjoying the offerings while others felt that there was room for improvement in terms of variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Despite some drawbacks, most guests had a positive overall experience and expressed a willingness to recommend the hotel to others based on the location and friendly staff.
