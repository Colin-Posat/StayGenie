HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Suites with Stunning ViewsLocated in the heart of Coconut Grove, Mutiny Luxury Suites Hotel offers a heated outdoor pool overlooking Sailboat Bay. Each suite is equipped with a kitchen, sofa bed, 2 flat-screen TVs, and a Bose® Bluetooth® docking station. Indulge in international cuisine at Table 14 Bar &amp; Restaurant or enjoy room service for a fee. Stay active at the fitness centre, relax in the sauna, or unwind in the hot tub. With free WiFi, massages, and 24-hour room service available, every need is catered to. Explore nearby attractions like Viscaya Museum &amp; Gardens or take a short drive to Miami city centre. Book now for a memorable stay at Mutiny Luxury Suites Hotel.
