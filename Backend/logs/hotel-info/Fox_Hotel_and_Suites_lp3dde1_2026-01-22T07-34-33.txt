HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Contemporary Hotel in Historic BanffLocated in historic Banff, Alberta, the Fox Hotel and Suites offers a modern stay with a touch of charm. Enjoy a complimentary shuttle to downtown and the Banff Ski Gondola, making exploration easy and convenient.Unique Amenities for Relaxation and DiningUnwind in the cave-inspired hot pool or indulge in a delicious meal at Chili’s Restaurant and Grill right on-site. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, and cozy amenities like a coffee maker and sofa bed.Convenient Facilities and Fitness OptionsStay active at the on-site gym with floor-to-ceiling mirrors and modern equipment. For added convenience, guest laundry facilities are available, ensuring a stress-free stay.Experience the best of Banff at Fox Hotel and Suites, book your stay now for a memorable getaway.
