HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationHotel Monterey Ginza is perfectly situated just steps away from Ginza-Itchome Subway Station and a short walk from JR Yurakucho Station, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore Tokyo with ease.Modern AmenitiesEnjoy the comfort of Western-style rooms equipped with free high-speed wired internet, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, and en suite bathrooms with luxurious toiletries provided. The hotel also offers laundry services and parking for your convenience.Delicious Dining OptionsIndulge in French cuisine at Escale Restaurant or savor a buffet-style breakfast in the Banquet Room, providing a delightful culinary experience during your stay.Experience the perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and culinary delights at Hotel Monterey Ginza. The accommodation tax will be collected at the hotel separately from the accommodation fee.
