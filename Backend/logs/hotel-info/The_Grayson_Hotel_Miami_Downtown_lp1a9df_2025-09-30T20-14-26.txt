- Yoga class on Saturday and Sunday
- Access to Nikki Beach – 10% discount at Nikki Beach Miami Beach restaurant, Complimentary beach towels and lounge chairs (additional requirements needed)
- Digital Newspaper

• Value for Money: 5.9/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
