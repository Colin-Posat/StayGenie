HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Contemporary Accommodation with a Touch of Parisian CharmChic Rooms with Modern Amenities: Hotel de France Quartier Latin offers elegantly decorated rooms with large mirrors, windows, and free Wi-Fi access. Enjoy a cozy stay with views over the charming streets of Paris.Delicious Breakfast Options: Start your day in the modern breakfast room with a delightful continental breakfast. For a more intimate experience, savor your meal in the comfort of your own room.Explore Paris with Ease: Take advantage of the hotel's convenient location near Rue Mouffetard and Censier-Daubenton Metro Station. With a small library offering guidebooks, venture out to iconic landmarks like Jardin des Plantes and Le Louvre.Experience the allure of Paris at Hotel de France Quartier Latin. Book your stay now for an unforgettable getaway.
