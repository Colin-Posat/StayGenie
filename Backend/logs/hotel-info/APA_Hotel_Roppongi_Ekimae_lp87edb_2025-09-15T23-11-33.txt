Food & beverage services at this property may be limited or unavailable due to Coronavirus (COVID-19).
When booking for 15 guests or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

In case of no show, 100% of the first day will be charged. If credit card was not provided, the invoice will be issued and mailed to the guest.

Guests arriving after check-in hours (00:00) must inform the property in advance. If the property is not informed, the reservation will be cancelled.

Please note, guest room temperatures are centrally controlled. Guests cannot switch between air conditioning and heating.

Please note that the hotel will undergo emergency equipment maintenance (Power cuts throughout the building due to electrical equipment inspections in accordance with statutory inspections) on the following dates/times: 25th November 2024, 12:00-14:00. (This may vary slightly depending on inspections).
During this period, guests can stay in their rooms during the work, though, all electrical facilities in the building, including lifts, interior lighting, air-conditioning and refrigerators will not be available.
