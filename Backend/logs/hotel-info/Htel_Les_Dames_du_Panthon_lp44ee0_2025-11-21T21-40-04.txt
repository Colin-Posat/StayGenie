HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm in the Latin QuarterStep into the past at Hôtel Les Dames du Panthéon, nestled in an 18th-century building across from the iconic Panthéon. Admire the classic French décor in each elegant guest room, some offering stunning views of the Panthéon.Delightful Dining and RelaxationStart your day with a delicious buffet breakfast served in the charming dining room or opt for a cozy in-room experience. Unwind with a drink at the bar while catching up on the latest news with complimentary newspapers.Convenience and ComfortEnjoy modern amenities like free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a minibar in all rooms. Benefit from a 24-hour reception and a convenient ticket service for a stress-free stay.Ready to immerse yourself in Parisian elegance? Book your stay at Hôtel Les Dames du Panthéon now.
