HOTEL DESCRIPTION:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests mentioned the cleanliness as a positive aspect, though a few reviews pointed out maintenance issues.
• Service: 7.4/10 - Service received mixed reviews, with some guests appreciating it while others complained about wait times and lack of luggage assistance.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location was highly praised for being close to key landmarks, public transport, and ideal for late arrivals from the train station.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally described as spacious and comfortable, with specific praise for the beds. However, noise levels and small room sizes were mentioned as drawbacks.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others expressed disappointment over the lack of spa services and limited breakfast options.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Opinions on the value for money were divided, with some feeling the prices were high for the services offered, while others found it reasonable considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.1/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback overall, with some guests suggesting more variety could be added.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the staff friendliness, comfort, and convenient location, with a few exceptions mentioning noise levels and small elevator size.
