HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Accommodation in the Heart of ParisHotel Marignan Champs-Elysées, nestled between Avenue George V and Avenue Montaigne, just steps away from the iconic Champs-Elysées, offers a unique design experience in Paris’s 8th district.Elegant Rooms with Eiffel Tower ViewsIndulge in the contemporary Parisian ambiance of the air-conditioned rooms, each resembling chic apartments. Some rooms boast private terraces overlooking the majestic Eiffel Tower, perfect for savoring meals with a view.Exquisite Dining Experience at Ma DameSatisfy your culinary cravings at Ma Dame restaurant, serving a blend of traditional French cuisine and innovative dishes made from fresh, seasonal ingredients. Don't miss the chance to sip on refreshing drinks and cocktails in the stylish bar.Experience the epitome of luxury and convenience at Hotel Marignan Champs-Elysées. Book now for an unforgettable stay in the City of Lights.

Please note that a 3% service charge will be added to your bill upon departure.

• Food and Beverage: 6.8/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
