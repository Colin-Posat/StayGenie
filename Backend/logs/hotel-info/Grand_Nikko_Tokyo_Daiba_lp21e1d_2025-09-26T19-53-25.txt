HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsExperience elegance in every corner of Grand Nikko Tokyo Daiba's spacious guestrooms. Adorned with European-style decor, enjoy modern amenities including free wired internet access, flat-screen TVs, and plush feather pillows.Breathtaking ViewsIndulge in stunning panoramic views of Tokyo Bay, the Rainbow Bridge, and Tokyo Tower from the comfort of your room. The upper floors offer a unique vantage point to admire the city's iconic landmarks and beautiful skyline.Culinary DelightsSavor a culinary journey at the hotel's diverse dining options. Whether you prefer Teppanyaki, sushi, or international cuisine, each restaurant promises a delightful experience with a backdrop of the bustling city.Book now to elevate your stay at Grand Nikko Tokyo Daiba and create unforgettable memories.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
As a result of Coronavirus (COVID-19), this property has temporarily suspended its shuttle services.
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
The property will contact the guests after booking on instructions about payment. Guests must make the payment within the instructed date or guest reservations may be cancelled.

Additional fees may apply to breakfast for a child staying in an existing bed. Please contact the property for details.

Different policies and additional supplements may apply for group bookings.

Please note that guests under 20 years old are not permitted to enter the lounge after 18:00.

Guests with a tattoo may not be permitted to enter the facility.

Please be informed that hotel member benefits and flight mileage will not be credited to guests booking from this website.

Airport limousines to/from  Haneda airports are available at a fee. Please visit the hotel's website for more information.

Guests must be 16 years of age or older to access Le Club Fitness Facilities including its indoor pool and sauna.

• Room Quality: 8/10 - Rooms were spacious and comfortable, although some guests mentioned minor maintenance issues.
• Amenities: 6.8/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory, with some guests highlighting the gym and pool, while others mentioned the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.6/10 - Some guests felt that the price was a bit high for the services offered, while others thought it was reasonable given the location.
• Food and Beverage: 6.7/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although a few guests thought there could be more variety.
• Overall Experience: 8.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
