HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and ServicesThe La Quinta Inn &amp; Suites by Wyndham Las Vegas Airport South hotel is perfectly situated just 1.8 miles from the Harry Reid International Airport (LAS) and a short 4.5 miles from the vibrant Las Vegas Strip. Enjoy the ease of complimentary 24-hour shuttle service to and from the airport, along with free parking for all guests.Relaxing AmenitiesUnwind and recharge in style with our 24/7 fitness center, outdoor pool, and hot tub. Each well-appointed guest room features modern conveniences like a Keurig™️ coffee maker, work desk, ironing essentials, and a flat-screen HDTV. Start your day right with our complimentary Bright Side Breakfast®.Stay Connected and ProductiveStay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi access and make use of our business center for any work needs. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, our hotel ensures a comfortable and seamless stay for all guests.Book your stay at La Quinta Inn &amp; Suites by Wyndham Las Vegas Airport South now for a memorable and stress-free experience.

GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:
• Clean rooms

• Comfortable beds
• Quiet

Areas for Improvement:
• Limited parking
• Expensive breakfast
• No spa services
• Bathroom cleanliness issues
• Maid entering room without knocking
• Maintenance issues in rooms
• Lack of amenities variety
• Mixed perceptions on value for money

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the overall cleanliness of the hotel rooms and facilities, although there were some isolated complaints about room cleanliness in specific cases.
• Service: 7.6/10 - Service received positive feedback with guests praising the friendly staff and quick check-ins, but there were a few incidents of poor service like unannounced maid entries.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's proximity to key locations like the airport and car rental services made it a convenient choice for many guests.
• Room Quality: 7.4/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable, but there were reports of maintenance issues like dirty bathrooms and worn-out furniture.
• Amenities: 7/10 - Guests had mixed opinions about the amenities, with some enjoying the pool and breakfast, while others found them lacking in quality and variety.
• Value for Money: 6.8/10 - There were conflicting views on the value for money, with some guests feeling the prices were justified by the location, while others thought the services did not match the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested improvements in the variety of food options.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, highlighting cleanliness, comfortable beds, great location, and friendly staff as key positives.
