HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Venetian ExperienceSet in Venice, the family-managed Hotel Concordia overlooks Saint Mark’s Basilica. The entrance is in Calle Larga, a picturesque street with restaurants and bars. Rooms feature 18th-century Venetian-style furnishings.Modern ComfortsThe rooms feature air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and satellite TV. The bathrooms are in marble and hard stone.Convenient AmenitiesIndulge in the charm of Venice while enjoying modern comforts and convenient amenities at Hotel Concordia. Book now for an unforgettable stay!
