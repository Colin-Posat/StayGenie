HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient LocationLocated in the heart of San Francisco, just a short stroll from Union Square, HI San Francisco Downtown Hostel offers a prime spot for shopping and dining enthusiasts.Modern and Practical AccommodationEach room at the hostel is thoughtfully designed with large windows, cool tones, and contemporary furnishings. Some rooms even feature private en suite facilities for added comfort.Abundant AmenitiesGuests can enjoy full access to common areas such as a well-equipped kitchen and a cozy lounge. Stay connected with complimentary WiFi, keep belongings secure with provided lockers, and take advantage of on-site laundry facilities.Book now to experience the vibrant city life of San Francisco at HI San Francisco Downtown Hostel.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Food & beverage services at this property may be limited or unavailable due to Coronavirus (COVID-19).
Please Note: Guests under the age of 18 are only allowed to check in with a parent or official guardian.

Please note: The property will try to keep people travelling together in the same room. However, this might not always be possible. If you have a special request concerning this, please email the hostel directly right after booking.

Please note: Hostel can only honor online bookings of 9 guests maximum. If you have 10 or more guests in your booking, please contact the hostel as different policies apply.

Guests may only stay up to 14 nights in a calendar year. Please note, reservations do not have to be for consecutive dates.

Please note: Parties travelling with children under the age of 12 must stay in a private room or rent out an entire dorm room.

The hotel's lift will be closed for maintenance from early December 2024 through February 2025. During this time, all hostel areas will be accessible only by stairs.
