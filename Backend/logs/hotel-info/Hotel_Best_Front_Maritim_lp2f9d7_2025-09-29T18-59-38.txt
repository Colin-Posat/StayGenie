HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Beachside Hotel with Free WiFi and GymConvenient Location and Modern AmenitiesLocated just 100 meters from Barcelona’s Nova Mar Bella Beach, Hotel Front Maritim offers free WiFi and a gym with a sauna. The modern rooms feature a flat-screen TV, work desk, and a private bathroom with free toiletries. With the Fòrum and CCIB Convention Centre within a 15-minute walk, the hotel provides a convenient stay for both leisure and business travelers.Delicious Dining and Shopping OptionsIndulge in a wide range of modern Mediterranean cuisine at the hotel's restaurant. For shopping and dining, the Diagonal Mar Shopping Centre is only 700 meters away. With Selva de Mar Metro Station a short 10-minute walk and easy access to bus stops, exploring Barcelona from the hotel is a breeze.Book your stay at Hotel Front Maritim for a perfect blend of comfort and convenience.
