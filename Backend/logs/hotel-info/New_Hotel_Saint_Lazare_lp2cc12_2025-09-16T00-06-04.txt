HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in ParisSituated in the heart of the 8th district in Paris, the hotel is conveniently located just 550 metres from Saint-Lazare Train Station, making it easily accessible for travelers.Comfortable and Well-Equipped RoomsThe soundproofed guest rooms at New Hotel Saint Lazare offer a peaceful retreat with amenities such as free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms. Some rooms even provide picturesque views of the Belle Époque-style courtyard.Convenient Amenities and ServicesGuests can enjoy a buffet breakfast in the charming interior patio and benefit from a 24-hour reception. Additionally, the proximity of Liège Metro Station, only 50 metres away, allows for quick and direct transportation to iconic Parisian landmarks like the Champs Elysées.Book your stay at New Hotel Saint Lazare for a delightful Parisian experience.

• Room Quality: 6.6/10 - Rooms varied in size and quality, with mentions of comfortable beds but also complaints about small, stuffy rooms and noise issues.
• Amenities: 6.6/10 - Mixed reviews on amenities, with some guests highlighting the breakfast selection while others mentioned a lack of facilities such as soundproof rooms and proper ventilation.
• Value for Money: 6.5/10 - Opinions on pricing varied, with some guests feeling it was reasonable for the location while others thought it was not justified by the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Guests generally appreciated the included breakfast, but there were some complaints about the variety and quality of food provided.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience, citing the friendly staff and convenient location as key highlights, despite some room and facility issues.
