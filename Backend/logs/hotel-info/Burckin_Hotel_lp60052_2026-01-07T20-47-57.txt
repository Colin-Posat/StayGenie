HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Spectacular Views and Central LocationLuxurious Rooms and AmenitiesCulinary Delights and Relaxing AtmosphereExperience breathtaking views of the Blue Mosque, Hagia Sophia, and the Bosphorus from the rooftop terrace of Burckin Hotel. Centrally located in Sultanahmet, our elegantly decorated rooms offer modern amenities such as LCD TVs and free WiFi. Indulge in traditional Turkish cuisine at our restaurant or unwind in the Turkish-style tea lounge. With a 24/7 front desk, our helpful staff can assist with all your needs. Explore the historic quarter with iconic landmarks just steps away. Don't miss the chance to stay at Burckin Hotel for a memorable Istanbul experience.
