HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant heart of Barcelona at Catalonia Gran Vía BCN, where comfort meets convenience.Recreational AmenitiesDive into relaxation with our outdoor pool and invigorate your body at our well-equipped fitness center. Whether you want to unwind or energize, our offerings cater to all your lifestyle needs.Culinary DelightsSavor delicious bites at our snack bar/deli or enjoy room service during limited hours. Start your day right with our buffet breakfast, available on weekdays and weekends, ensuring you have the perfect fuel for exploring the city.Modern ComfortRetreat to one of our 63 air-conditioned rooms, complete with minibars and complimentary Wi-Fi. Enjoy entertainment with satellite programming and pamper yourself with luxury toiletries in private bathrooms.Book your stay today and dive into the culture and excitement that Barcelona has to offer!
