HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationHotel Savoy offers modern and elegant rooms in the heart of Bern's old town. Each non-smoking room is thoughtfully designed with memory foam pillows, a Nespresso coffee machine, and a rain shower or bathtub in the ensuite bathroom.Convenient LocationSituated within walking distance of the main station and popular shopping streets, guests can easily explore the city's main attractions and dining options on foot. Additionally, Bern Belp airport is just a short drive away.Modern AmenitiesGuests can enjoy complimentary WiFi, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and air conditioning in every room. The hotel also provides a laptop safe, a minibar, and tea making facilities for ultimate comfort.Experience luxury and convenience at Hotel Savoy. Book your stay now for an unforgettable Bern getaway.
