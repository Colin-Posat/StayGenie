HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Vibrant Marina District LocationTrendy Marina District bars, boutiques, and restaurants are just steps away from Hotel Del Sol. Enjoy a playful modern decor and complimentary daily breakfast in all rooms, along with free WiFi and an outdoor pool.Charming Room AmenitiesEach room at Hotel Del Sol offers a flat-screen TV and a range of amenities for your comfort. HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

PARKING:
Public parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 40 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 17 years old and over stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
