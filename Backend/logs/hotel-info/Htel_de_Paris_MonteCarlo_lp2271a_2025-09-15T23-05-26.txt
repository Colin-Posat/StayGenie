HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historic Charm and Luxury in Monte CarloWine Cellar and Piano Bar: Indulge in the charm of a 1864 building with a wine cellar and an American-style piano bar at Hôtel de Paris, just steps away from the iconic Casino de Monaco.Michelin-Star Dining: Experience culinary excellence at the renowned &quot;Louis XV-Alain Ducasse à l'Hôtel de Paris&quot; restaurant, boasting 3 Michelin stars for its exquisite cuisine.Exclusive Amenities: Enjoy complimentary access to the luxurious Thermes Marins Monte-Carlo spa and other Monte-Carlo Société des Bains de Mer properties with a guest pass. Plus, convenient transportation options including free shuttles to the private beach make your stay unforgettable.Book now to immerse yourself in the opulence and history of Hôtel de Paris in Monte Carlo.
