HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location and Modern AmenitiesLocated just a 10-minute walk from The Royal Observatory, the ibis London Greenwich offers a stylish stay with a 24-hour front desk and on-site parking. The bright rooms feature high-speed internet, work desks, and satellite TVs, providing both comfort and convenience.Dining and Entertainment OptionsGuests can unwind at the contemporary bar, enjoying a selection of coffee, wine, and light snacks. The on-site Banana Tree serves a delicious breakfast with hot options and a continental buffet. With the O2 Arena just a short drive away, entertainment is never far from reach.Nearby Attractions and TransportationExplore Greenwich Market and the iconic Cutty Sark, both within a 5-minute walk. With Greenwich DLR and Railway Stations nearby, and South Bank a short drive away, the hotel is well-connected for both leisure and business travelers.Book your stay at ibis London Greenwich for a memorable experience!
