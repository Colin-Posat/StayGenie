HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location and Stunning ViewsThe Hotel Ambassador Zermatt is conveniently located just 100 meters from the Zermatt train station and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Matterhorn from every balcony.Luxurious AmenitiesGuests can unwind in the large heated indoor pool, relax in the whirlpool, and rejuvenate in the sauna. The free high-speed WiFi ensures you stay connected throughout your stay.Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in a culinary journey at the Tapas &amp; Wine restaurant offering Swiss and Spanish delicacies. Afterward, head to the inviting lounge and bar for a wide selection of drinks to enjoy a relaxing evening.Ready to experience luxury and comfort in Zermatt? Book your stay at Hotel Ambassador now.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 8.1/10 - Guests consistently mentioned the cleanliness of the hotel as a positive aspect, with only minor complaints about certain rooms.
• Service: 8.1/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with friendly and helpful staff. Some guests had minor issues with staff efficiency during busy times.
• Location: 8.6/10 - The hotel's close proximity to major transportation hubs and attractions was highlighted by guests as a major benefit of staying at the property.
• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Guests found the room quality to be comfortable overall, but there were some complaints about maintenance issues and disruptions to views in some rooms.
• Amenities: 6.7/10 - While some guests appreciated the gym and pool facilities, others expressed dissatisfaction with the lack of spa services and in-hotel dining options.
• Value for Money: 6.2/10 - Mixed reviews were received regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling that the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - The breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although some guests suggested improvements in variety and service speed.
• Overall Experience: 8.3/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel, with many indicating they would recommend it to others. There were a few isolated incidents that affected the experience for some guests.
