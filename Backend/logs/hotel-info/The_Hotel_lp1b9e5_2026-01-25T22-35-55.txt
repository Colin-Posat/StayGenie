HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the perfect blend of luxury and convenience at our exquisite 4-star hotel in the heart of Brussels.Unwind in StyleIndulge in total relaxation at our rejuvenating spa, featuring a range of massages, body treatments, and facials that promise to pamper you from head to toe.Culinary DelightsSavor delectable dishes at The Iris restaurant while enjoying stunning garden views.
