HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AmenitiesHotel Granvia Kyoto offers an indoor swimming pool, a fitness centre, and a variety of dining options. Guests can also enjoy the steam room, hot tub, and a collection of over 1000 art pieces.Chic and Modern RoomsThe spacious rooms at the hotel are elegantly decorated with modern facilities. Each room features large windows, an LCD TV, extra-long beds, and a well-equipped bathroom. Complimentary WiFi is available throughout the property.Convenient LocationSituated just a 3-minute walk from Kyoto Tower, the hotel is centrally located near Kyoto Station's shopping mall and museum. With easy access to popular attractions like Arashiyama and Fushimi Inari Shrine, Hotel Granvia Kyoto is the perfect choice for travelers looking to explore the cultural wonders of Kyoto.Book your stay at Hotel Granvia Kyoto now to experience luxury and convenience in the heart of Kyoto!

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 1 year old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 6 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 7 years old and over stay for ¥9,055 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
