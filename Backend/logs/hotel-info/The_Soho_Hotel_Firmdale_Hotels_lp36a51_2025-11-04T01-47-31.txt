HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Oxford StreetThe Soho Hotel is conveniently located just a short 5-minute walk from the bustling Oxford Street. With Piccadilly Circus and Leicester Square within a 10-minute walk, guests have easy access to major attractions and transportation hubs.Luxurious Amenities and ServicesEnjoy a fully equipped gym with a personal trainer on-site for your fitness needs. Indulge in beauty treatments like massages and facials in the hotel's treatment room. Each room is equipped with modern comforts including a flat-screen TV, minibar, and air conditioning.Fine Dining and Award-Winning BarSavor delectable modern European cuisine in the hotel's restaurant, along with a delightful breakfast menu and afternoon tea. Unwind with a drink in the award-winning bar, perfect for a relaxing evening.Book your stay at The Soho Hotel now for a luxurious and convenient experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  GBP 60 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 17 years old stay for £78 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for £78 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
