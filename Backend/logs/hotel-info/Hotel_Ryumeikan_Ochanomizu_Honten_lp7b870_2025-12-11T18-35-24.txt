HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Sophisticated Guest Rooms with Modern Facilities and Traditional Japanese AestheticsHotel Ryumeikan Ochanomizu Honten, with a history dating back over a century, offers elegantly renovated guest rooms blending modern amenities with traditional Japanese design. Each spacious junior suite is equipped with a flat-screen TV, Blu-ray/DVD player, coffee machine, and luxurious bath amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay.Convenient Location for Easy Access to Tokyo's Top DestinationsSituated just a 1-minute walk from Shin-Ochanomizu Subway Station and a short stroll to JR Ochanomizu Station, our hotel provides quick access to iconic landmarks like Akihabara, the Imperial Palace, and Tokyo Shinkansen Station. Explore the vibrant city with ease from our centrally located property.Unique Dining Experience at 1899 Ochanomizu RestaurantIndulge in fusion-style Japanese cuisine paired with premium green tea at our on-site restaurant, 1899 Ochanomizu. Immerse yourself in the rich culinary heritage of Japan without leaving the comfort of the hotel.Book your stay at Hotel Ryumeikan Ochanomizu Honten for a memorable blend of tradition and modernity.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Room rates differ according to room size.

Please note only children over 13 years old can stay at this hotel.

The nearest Ochanomizu Train Station does not have elevators or escalators. Guests with heavy luggage are advised to take a taxi from Tokyo Station, about a 15-minute ride away.

In order to prepare special amenities for men and women, guests are kindly requested to indicate the gender of each guest staying in the room in the Special Requests box when booking.Please inform  of your expected arrival time in advance. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly using the contact details in your confirmation.
An accommodation tax per person, per night is not included in the price and must be paid at the property.
