HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationTanah Gajah, a Resort by Hadiprana offers spacious rooms with private terraces and semi-outdoor bathrooms. Enjoy modern amenities like a 32-inch flat-screen TV and Bose home theatre system. Some rooms even boast a private pool with an outdoor dining area.Unique Activities and FacilitiesGuests can partake in cooking classes, hot air balloon rides, and explore the organic farm. The resort also features a gift shop, car rental, business services, and a 24-hour concierge. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.Delightful Dining ExperienceIndulge in Indonesian and Western cuisine at The Tempayan and Panen Padi Lounge while overlooking the Gunung Agung Volcano. Afterward, unwind at The Bird Lounge with cigars and drinks next to the Tanah Gajah Aviary.Book your tranquil escape at Tanah Gajah, a Resort by Hadiprana for a truly unforgettable experience.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 1,600,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 12 years old to 17 years old stay for Rp 2,000,000 per person per night when using an existing bed.
Children from 3 years old to 11 years old stay for Rp 1,140,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for Rp 1,500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
