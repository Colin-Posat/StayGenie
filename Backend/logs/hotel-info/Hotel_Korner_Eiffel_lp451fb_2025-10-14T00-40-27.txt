HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Charming 7-Floor Hotel Near Eiffel TowerCozy Rooms with Modern AmenitiesGuests at Hotel Korner Eiffel can unwind in simply decorated rooms equipped with a flat-screen TV, satellite channels, and a private safety box. A hairdryer is also provided for convenience.Delicious Buffet Breakfast and 24-Hour ReceptionStart your day with a delightful buffet breakfast featuring homemade jams, a variety of teas, and a Nespresso coffee machine. The hotel's reception desk is open 24/7 to assist you during your stay.Convenient Location for Exploring ParisLocated just a 20-minute walk from the Eiffel Tower, the hotel offers easy access to the Beaugrenelle Shopping Mall, Rue du Commerce, and Metro Station Charles Michels for exploring The Latin Quarter and Avenue des Champs-Elysées.Book your stay at Hotel Korner Eiffel for a memorable Parisian experience!
