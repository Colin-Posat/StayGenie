HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location in Hell's Kitchen DistrictModern Amenities and ServicesClose Proximity to Major AttractionsBook your stay at DoubleTree by Hilton New York Times Square South for a luxurious experience in the heart of Hell's Kitchen. Enjoy air-conditioned rooms with cable TV and a 24-hour front desk. Take advantage of the business center and ATM services during your stay. With Bryant Park, Empire State Building, and New York Public Library just a short distance away, you'll be at the center of it all. Explore Macy's, Madison Square Garden, and Times Square, all within reach. Don't miss out on this prime location near Teterboro Airport. Experience the best of New York City at DoubleTree by Hilton.
