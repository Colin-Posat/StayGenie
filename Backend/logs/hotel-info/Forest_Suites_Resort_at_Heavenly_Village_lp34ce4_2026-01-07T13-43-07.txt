HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Escape to the enchanting Forest Suites Resort at Heavenly Village, where comfort meets adventure in the heart of South Lake Tahoe.Relaxation at its BestUnwind in one of our two inviting hot tubs or stay active in our fully-equipped health club. With our concierge services, arcade/game room, and special wedding services, you’ll find everything you need for a perfect getaway.Savor Every BiteStart your day with a complimentary buffet breakfast served daily, and indulge in delectable snacks at our on-site deli. Enjoy your favorite cocktail at the bar/lounge, perfect for winding down after a day of exploration.Ideal for Business and LeisureWith over 3,000 square feet of versatile meeting space and modern business amenities, we cater to both corporate events and leisure stays. Our air-conditioned rooms feature microwaves and flat-screen televisions for your convenience.Book your stay today and create unforgettable memories at Forest Suites Resort at Heavenly Village!
