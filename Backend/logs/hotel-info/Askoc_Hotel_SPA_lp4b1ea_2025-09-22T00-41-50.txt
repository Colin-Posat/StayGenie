HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Historical LocationAskoc Hotel, renovated in 2013, is nestled in the historical heart of the city, mere steps away from iconic landmarks like Hagia Sophia and the Grand Bazaar.Modern Rooms and Wellness CenterThe hotel boasts modern rooms equipped with top-notch amenities like LED TVs, free Wi-Fi, and a wellness center featuring an indoor pool, sauna, and Turkish bath - perfect for relaxation.Culinary Delights and Bosphorus ViewsIndulge in Turkish and Mediterranean cuisine on the terrace while taking in breathtaking views of the Bosphorus. • Amenities: 7/10 - Overall, amenities were satisfactory, with positive mentions of the spa, pool, and breakfast, but some guests desired more options and higher quality services.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Opinions were divided on value for money, with some feeling the price was justified by the location while others considered it overpriced for the services provided.
• Food and Beverage: 7.6/10 - Breakfast and dining services received positive feedback overall, though some guests suggested improvement in variety and quality.
• Overall Experience: 8.2/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the staff, location, and cleanliness.
