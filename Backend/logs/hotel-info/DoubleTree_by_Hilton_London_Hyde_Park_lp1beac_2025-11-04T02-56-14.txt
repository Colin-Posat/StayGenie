HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious AccommodationsThe 4-star DoubleTree by Hilton London - Hyde Park boasts modern air-conditioned rooms featuring elegant decor, Hypnos beds with Egyptian cotton sheets, and convenient amenities like a work desk and mini fridge.Dining Experience at Urban MeadowEnjoy a unique dining experience at Urban Meadow, where wall-to-ceiling windows offer stunning views while you indulge in stylish restaurant fare with a rustic edge. Savor grab-and-go items, sharing platters, and tapas-style dishes sourced from local producers.Convenient LocationSituated just a 3-minute walk from Bayswater and Queensway Tube Stations, this hotel provides easy access to top attractions like Paddington Train Station for Heathrow Airport transfers and popular shopping destinations like Oxford Street.
