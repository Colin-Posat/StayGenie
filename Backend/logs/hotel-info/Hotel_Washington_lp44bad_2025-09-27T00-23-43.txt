HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location in RomeHotel Washington is conveniently situated in the heart of Rome, just 300 metres from Rome Termini Metro Station. Guests can easily explore popular attractions like Santa Maria Maggiore and Vittorio Emanuele Metro Station.Cozy AmenitiesThe hotel offers a shared lounge, a terrace, and a bar for guests to unwind and socialize. Enjoy room service for added convenience during your stay. The property's non-smoking policy ensures a comfortable environment for all visitors.Convenient ServicesTake advantage of the tour desk to plan your sightseeing adventures. For guests flying in or out, the hotel provides a paid airport shuttle service to Rome Ciampino Airport. Book your stay at Hotel Washington for a memorable Roman getaway.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
