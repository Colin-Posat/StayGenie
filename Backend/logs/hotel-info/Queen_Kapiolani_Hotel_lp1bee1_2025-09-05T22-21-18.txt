HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Panoramic Views and Spacious Rooms:Queen Kapiolani Hotel offers stunning panoramic views of Diamond Head from its outdoor pool and sun terrace. The spacious rooms are elegantly decorated and equipped with modern amenities like a 49-inch flat-screen TV and a refrigerator.Convenient Amenities and Services:Enjoy the convenience of beach towels, beach chairs, WiFi, and access to a business center during your stay. The hotel also provides local and toll-free calls, as well as an in-room security deposit box for your peace of mind.Prime Location for Exploration:Located just 1 block from Waikiki Beach, Queen Kapiolani Hotel is the perfect base for exploring Honolulu. With Honolulu Zoo and Kapiolani Park across the street and Ala Wai Golf Course nearby, there is no shortage of activities to enjoy during your stay.Book your stay at Queen Kapiolani Hotel now to experience the best of Waikiki Beach and Diamond Head views!

Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
A damage deposit of USD 100 is required on arrival. This will be collected by credit card. You should be reimbursed on check-out. Your deposit will be refunded in full via credit card, subject to an inspection of the property.

-Yoga On-The-Go (carrying bag, mat, jump rope, foam roller, fitness cables)
-24-Hour fitness centre access and discounted yoga classes
-Daily surf demonstrations (Hans Surf School)
-In-room Chromecast streaming device
-High-speed WiFi
-24-Hour business centre access
-Guest printing services (print from laptop or smart device to the business centre printer)
-Beach chairs, beach towels and sunscreen (sample in-room & dispenser at pool)
-Ice and water stations
-Complimentary QK postcards
-In-room digital safe and refrigerator
-Unlimited local and 1-800 calls

An incidental deposit via credit card is required at check-in and refundable at check-out less any incidental charges incurred during your stay.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$55 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 50 per  day.

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - The rooms were described as clean and comfortable by most guests. Some mentioned minor issues with maintenance.
• Service: 7/10 - Overall, the service was appreciated by guests, with some noting friendly and accommodating staff. However, there were complaints about rude behavior from valet staff.
• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's location was highlighted positively, being in close proximity to key landmarks and offering views of the ocean and Diamond Head.
• Room Quality: 5.5/10 - While some guests praised the comfort of the rooms, others noted outdated bathrooms, misleading room pictures, and issues with noise levels.
• Amenities: 7.7/10 - Guests appreciated the amenities provided, such as the gym, pool, beach equipment, and sustainability initiatives with reusable water bottles. However, valet parking issues were reported.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews were received regarding the value for money, with some guests feeling the hotel was worth the price while others cited issues that impacted their stay.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Most guests enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant services, with some suggesting more variety could be added.
• Overall Experience: 6.8/10 - Overall, the guests had a mixed experience, with some highlighting positive aspects while others mentioned issues that affected their stay. Several guests expressed hesitation to revisit.
