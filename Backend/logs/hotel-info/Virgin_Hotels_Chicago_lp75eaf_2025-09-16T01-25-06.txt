HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Experience the vibrant energy of Chicago at our stunning Art Deco hotel, where luxury meets convenience in the heart of the city.Recreational AmenitiesUnwind after a long day at our on-site nightclub or maintain your fitness routine in our state-of-the-art 24-hour fitness center. Plus, with complimentary Wi-Fi and dedicated concierge services, your every need is catered to.Culinary DelightsSavor exquisite Italian cuisine at Miss Ricky's or enjoy a delightful snack from our coffee shop. A cooked-to-order breakfast is available on weekends, ensuring a perfect start to your day.Meeting and Event SpaceWith over 1,430 square feet of versatile meeting space, we offer the ideal setting for your corporate events or celebrations. Enjoy express check-in and check-out for seamless planning.Book your unforgettable stay today and immerse yourself in the best of Chicago!

* Contactless check-out is available.
