Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests complimented the clean rooms, but some mentioned issues like a smelly room and mold in the pool area.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Staff was generally friendly and helpful, though some complaints about noise and a negative incident involving a guest feeding a dog in the hotel.
• Location: 8/10 - The hotel's proximity to the University and key landmarks was appreciated by guests as a great advantage.
• Room Quality: 8/10 - Spacious and clean rooms were a highlight, but issues such as loud A/C units and unpleasant odors were mentioned.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Amenities were satisfactory, with guests mentioning a clean pool but also the lack of spa services.
• Value for Money: 7.6/10 - Mixed reviews on value for money, with positives about the location and negatives like running out of waffle mix.
• Food and Beverage: 7.2/10 - Positive feedback on breakfast and dining, but some guests desired more variety and mentioned disruptive incidents.
• Overall Experience: 7.9/10 - Most guests had a pleasant overall stay, but a few reported issues such as noise disturbances and unpleasant odors.
