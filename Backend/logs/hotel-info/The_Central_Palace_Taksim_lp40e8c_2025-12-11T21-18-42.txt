HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and Wellness FacilitiesThe Central Palace, just steps away from Taksim Square, offers opulent rooms blending Ottoman decor with modern amenities. Relax in deluxe bathrooms with spa baths and enjoy the comfort of air conditioning.Gourmet Dining and Fitness OptionsIndulge in gourmet cuisine with a focus on healthy ingredients at the hotel's restaurant. Stay active at the state-of-the-art gym with cardio equipment. Unwind at the spa featuring a sauna, whirlpool, and traditional Turkish hammam.Convenient Location and Complimentary AmenitiesExplore Istanbul easily with access to public transportation from Taksim Square. Enjoy free WiFi, parking, and proximity to major attractions and shopping districts like Nisantasi. Book now for a luxurious stay at The Central Palace.

HOTEL POLICIES:
INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Free public parking is possible at a location nearby (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are not allowed.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 3 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 4 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 5 years old to 12 years old stay for € 20 per person per night when using an existing bed.
You haven't added any extra beds.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
