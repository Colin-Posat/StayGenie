HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Convenient Location near Tokyo SkytreeRichmond Hotel Premier Tokyo Schole, open since December 2015, is just a 2-minute walk from Oshiage Subway Station, making it the perfect base for exploring Tokyo. Guests can easily visit the iconic Tokyo Skytree and Tokyo Solamachi for shopping.Modern Amenities for ComfortEach room at the hotel features free WiFi, a flat-screen TV, Video on demand (VOD), and a handy smartphone for guests' convenience. Unwind in rooms with a seating area and enjoy the complimentary toiletries in the en suite bathroom.Dining and ServicesIndulge in a wide array of dishes at the on-site restaurant and start your day with a delicious breakfast buffet. The hotel also offers photocopying services, meeting rooms, and a drinks vending machine for your convenience.Book your stay at Richmond Hotel Premier Tokyo Schole for a memorable Tokyo experience.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
An accommodation tax per person per night is not included in the price and needs to be paid at the property.
Please inform Richmond Hotel Premier Tokyo Schole in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
Please be informed that rooms do not offer views of Tokyo Skytree.

Please note it may take a few days to respond to emails. Should the matter be urgent and you require immediate assistance, please call the property. Contact details can be found on the booking confirmation.

Please note that early check-in before 14:00 is not available.

When booking 5 rooms or more, different policies and additional supplements may apply. Please contact the property directly for more details.

Please note that breakfast for children sleeping in an existing bed who are 7-12 years old can be provided for an additional fee. Please contact the property directly for details.

Please note that there will be a water outage on 29/10/2024 from 00:00 to 03:00.
