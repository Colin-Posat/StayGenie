IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
The Club Lounge is opened from Monday to Sunday. Breakfast service remains at The Poet Restaurant.
 WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.
Please note that reduced parking fees apply during weekends.
Breakfast is free for children under 6 and there is a 50% discount for children between 6 and 12 years old.
The swimming-pool is open for children between 07:00-18:00 and exclusively for adults from 18:00-22:00 on weekdays and from 08:00-18:00 for children and exclusively for adults from 18:00-20:00 on weekends.
The swimming-pool operating hours may change without prior notice during the festive season and public holidays.
The Family room is exclusively designed for families (up to 2 adults and 2 children). Any reservations made for 4 (four) adults will result in an additional charge of EUR 20 upon check-in.Pet fee of EUR 50 per stay and EUR 10 per night, with a maximum of 2 pets per room.Guests are required to show a photo ID and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
Guests under the age of 18 can only check in with a parent or official guardian.
