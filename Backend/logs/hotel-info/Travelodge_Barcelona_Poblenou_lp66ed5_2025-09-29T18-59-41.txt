HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Hotel with Spacious Rooms:This modern hotel boasts 250 spacious and fully equipped rooms, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.Convenient Amenities:Enjoy the convenience of a 24-hour Snack-Bar offering buffet breakfast, light meals, vending machines, and private paid parking. The hotel is also perfectly adapted for people with reduced mobility.Ideal Location and Connectivity:Located in the vibrant Poblenou neighborhood, surrounded by restaurants, bars, and shops, and just 600 meters from Bogatell beach. With free high-speed Wi-Fi in common areas and easy access to the city center and Port Olímpic, the hotel is perfect for leisure and business travelers alike.Book now to experience the best of Barcelona at Travelodge Barcelona Poblenou.

• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - Rooms are generally clean with daily towel changes noted, but some guests reported lobby and toilet odors.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Staff was found to be friendly and helpful, despite some complaints about wait times and unavailability of basic items.
• Location: 8.5/10 - Most reviews noted the hotel's convenient location near restaurants, shops, and public transport.
• Room Quality: 6.8/10 - Guests appreciated comfy beds, but many mentioned issues with missing amenities and lack of maintenance.
• Amenities: 5.9/10 - Common complaints about lack of kettles, WiFi charges, and other facility shortcomings overshadowed the satisfaction with beds and room silence.
• Value for Money: 6.4/10 - Mixed feelings on pricing, with some guests finding it reasonable and others critiquing the cost against the provided amenities and services.
• Food and Beverage: 7/10 - While breakfast was generally appreciated, monotony and extra charges for drinks were negatives.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - Despite some grievances, most guests enjoyed their stay and complimented the hotel's location and staff.
