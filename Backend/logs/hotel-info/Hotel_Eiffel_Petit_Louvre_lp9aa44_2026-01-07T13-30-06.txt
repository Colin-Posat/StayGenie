HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Central Location near Iconic LandmarksHotel Eiffel Petit Louvre is perfectly situated in Paris’s 15th arrondissement, just a short stroll away from the Eiffel Tower, Trocadéro, and The River Seine, making it an ideal choice for travelers looking to explore the city's most famous attractions.Comfortable and Convenient AmenitiesEnjoy a peaceful stay in our sound-proofed guest rooms, each equipped with modern conveniences like free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, and en suite bathrooms. The hotel also offers a 24-hour reception desk, elevator access to all rooms, and a delicious continental breakfast served daily.Easy Access to Transportation and ServicesWith Metro Station Dupleix only 10 meters away, guests have direct access to key landmarks like the Arc de Triomphe and Montparnasse Train Station. Additionally, take advantage of our luggage storage, ticket reservation service, and proximity to Paris Expo Porte de Versailles for a seamless travel experience.Ready to experience the charm of Paris?
