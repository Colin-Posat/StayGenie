HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Elegance and ConvenienceHotel Santa Marina, a former convent near the Rialto Bridge, offers a perfect blend of history and modern comfort. The marble floors and Murano glass furnishings in the guest rooms exude charm, while free Wi-Fi keeps you connected.Personalized ServiceThe dedicated staff goes above and beyond to ensure a memorable stay. From providing insider tips to offering a helpful city map, they are committed to making your trip exceptional.Terrace Breakfast with a ViewIndulge in a delicious buffet breakfast on the terrace overlooking Santa Marina Square. It's the perfect way to start your day before exploring nearby attractions like St. Mark’s Square.Ready to experience the charm of Hotel Santa Marina?
