HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and ServicesThe Sunset Tower Hotel in West Hollywood offers a luxurious experience with a rooftop pool, full-service spa, and spacious guest rooms with modern amenities like flat-screen TVs and Nespresso coffee machines. Enjoy nightly turndown service with freshly baked cookies for the perfect touch of indulgence.Fitness and Wellness OptionsGuests can stay active at the on-site fitness center and rejuvenate at the renowned Joanna Vargas Spa, known for its skincare and body treatments. Whether you prefer a workout or a relaxing spa day, this hotel has you covered.Dining with a ViewExperience exquisite dining at The Tower Bar, serving California cuisine with a European flair. Enjoy panoramic city views from the restaurant or opt for a more casual meal at the poolside dining area. Indulge in culinary delights while taking in the vibrant atmosphere of West Hollywood.Book your stay at The Sunset Tower Hotel for a truly unforgettable experience in the heart of Los Angeles.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 5 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in the hotel rooms and is free of charge.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed) and costs  USD 70 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for US$35 per person per night when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 14 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children from 15 years old to 17 years old stay for US$50 per person per night when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$55 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
