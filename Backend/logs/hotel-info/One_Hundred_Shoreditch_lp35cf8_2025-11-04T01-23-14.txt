Unique Location in ShoreditchOne Hundred Shoreditch stands out as a vibrant hub in the heart of Shoreditch. With its prime spot on Shoreditch High Street, the hotel offers 258 stylish bedrooms, a variety of dining options, a chic cocktail bar by Mr Lyan, and a stunning rooftop bar with panoramic views of East London.Relaxing RetreatEscape the hustle and bustle of the city in the thoughtfully designed bedrooms and suites at One Hundred Shoreditch. HOTEL POLICIES:
PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

GROUPS:
When booking more than 9 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Private parking is possible on site (reservation is needed) and costs  GBP 55 per  day.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 12 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People 13 years old and over stay for £50 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
