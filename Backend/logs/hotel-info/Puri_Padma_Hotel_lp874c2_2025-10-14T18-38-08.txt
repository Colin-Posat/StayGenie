Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.8/10 - Rooms were clean overall, with some minor maintenance issues reported by a few guests.
• Service: 8.1/10 - The staff received positive feedback for being friendly, attentive, and helpful, although there were occasional complaints about the speed of service.
• Location: 8.2/10 - The hotel's location was praised for being close to key landmarks, offering great views, and providing easy access to transportation.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, though some guests suggested improvements such as larger rooms or additional amenities.
• Amenities: 7.1/10 - The amenities provided were satisfactory overall, with mixed reviews highlighting the gym and pool positively, while some guests mentioned the absence of certain services like a spa.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money were varied, with some guests finding the price reasonable given the location and services, while others felt it was slightly high.
• Food and Beverage: 7.4/10 - Guests generally enjoyed the breakfast and restaurant offerings, although some suggested an increase in variety or improvement in certain menu options.
• Overall Experience: 8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience at the hotel and would recommend it to others, citing staff friendliness, location, and cleanliness as key highlights.
