HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Tranquil Riverside Retreat:The Lokha Ubud Resort, Villas &amp; SPA offers a serene escape by the riverside, just a short drive from Elephant Safari Park.Luxurious Amenities:Indulge in elegant teak furnishings, a flat-screen cable TV, and a lavish bathtub in each spacious unit. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, a gym, outdoor pool, and a spa on-site, along with free shuttle service to Central Ubud and daily afternoon tea.Family-Friendly Activities:From village walks to fruit carving, this resort offers a range of activities for all ages. Kids can have a blast at the kids' club while adults unwind in the library or by the pool.Experience the ultimate blend of luxury and tranquility at The Lokha Ubud Resort, Villas &amp; SPA. Book now for an unforgettable getaway.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
Please inform The Lokha Ubud Resort, Villas & SPA in advance of your expected arrival time. You can use the Special Requests box when booking, or contact the property directly with the contact details provided in your confirmation.
Guests are required to show a photo identification and credit card upon check-in. Please note that all Special Requests are subject to availability and additional charges may apply.
In response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), additional safety and sanitation measures are in effect at this property.
Please note that infants between 0-2 years old is free using baby cot that is subject to availability.

Extra adult guests comes at an additional charge for extra bed and including breakfast. Please note that maximum capacity of 3 persons is applicable for all room types.

Children below 12 years old is free by sharing existing bed with parents and breakfast comes at 50% of the full price. Children using extra bed comes at an additional charge at children's rate including breakfast.

HOTEL POLICIES:

WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 5 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for Rp 500,000 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
