HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Villas with Stunning ViewsModern Amenities and Relaxing SpaGourmet Dining and Convenient Shuttle ServiceExperience eco-friendly luxury just minutes away from Ubud Market at Ubud Green. Indulge in spacious villas with private balconies overlooking picturesque rice fields. What Guests Love:

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.2/10 - Guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, but several reviews highlighted issues with insects and cleanliness in some areas of the hotel.
• Service: 8.2/10 - Service was appreciated by most guests for its friendliness and helpfulness. However, there were complaints about the speed and quality of service in some instances.
• Location: 8.3/10 - The hotel's location near key landmarks and public transport was praised by guests as convenient and ideal for exploration.
• Room Quality: 6.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and spacious, but many reviews indicated issues with maintenance, cleanliness, and the condition of amenities.
• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Guests had mixed feelings about the amenities provided, with some appreciating the pool and gym while others criticized the lack of spa services and overall quality.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions on the value for money varied, with some guests finding the prices reasonable considering the location, while others felt the services did not justify the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services were generally well-received, although there were some comments about limited choices and quality.
• Overall Experience: 7.5/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others, despite some issues with cleanliness and room quality.
