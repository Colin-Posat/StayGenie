HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Prime Location near Hidalgo Metro StationExperience the convenience of staying at Hotel Fontan Reforma Centro Historico, located just steps away from Hidalgo Metro Station. Explore the historic city center, Alameda Park, and Colonial Art Gallery within walking distance.Dining Options and AmenitiesIndulge in delicious meals at the on-site restaurant offering a lunch buffet and à la carte dinner menu. Enjoy the comfort of air-conditioned rooms with satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, and a heating system for a cozy stay.Nearby Attractions and EntertainmentDiscover a variety of dining and entertainment options near the hotel, with the Palace of Fine Arts and Franz Mayer Museum a short distance away. Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture and history of Mexico City during your stay at Hotel Fontan Reforma Centro Historico.Book your stay now to enjoy these exceptional amenities and explore the city's attractions effortlessly.
