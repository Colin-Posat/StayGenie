HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Amenities and Convenient LocationThis Greenwich Village hotel, just steps away from Washington Square Park, offers a luxurious stay with a spa, restaurant, and complimentary WiFi. Guests can enjoy in-room safety deposit boxes, flat-screen TVs, and even iPads at the reception. Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 5.7/10 - While some guests mentioned cleanliness as a positive aspect, others reported issues with filth in the rooms.
• Service: 7.7/10 - Service was generally appreciated, with comments about friendly employees and helpful bellhops. Some guests mentioned challenges in reaching the general manager.
• Location: 9/10 - The hotel is praised for its convenient location near key landmarks and public transport, making it easy for guests to explore the city.
• Room Quality: 7.7/10 - The rooms were well-appointed and comfortable, with positive feedback on amenities like air conditioning and bedding. However, some guests reported cleanliness issues.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Guests found the amenities satisfactory, with a mix of positive comments on the quality of services such as check-in ease and availability of vending machines.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Mixed reviews on the price, with some guests feeling it was expensive for the quality offered, while others appreciated the value, especially considering the location.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - The continental breakfast and coffee received positive feedback. Some guests mentioned a lack of variety in food options.
• Overall Experience: 8.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, praising the hotel for its amenities, location, and overall comfort.
