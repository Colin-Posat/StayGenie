HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Beachfront PropertyExperience the ultimate getaway at Casa Malca, situated along a pristine beach in Tulum, bordering the Sian Ka’an Biosphere Reserve.Elegant AccommodationIndulge in our stylishly decorated rooms and suites, each boasting serene views of either the lush gardens or the turquoise Caribbean Sea.Convenient LocationExplore Tulum town and the ancient Mayan ruins just a short distance away, while enjoying the seclusion of our beachfront oasis.Book now to immerse yourself in luxury at Casa Malca and create unforgettable memories by the sea.

HOTEL POLICIES:
GROUPS:
When booking more than 4 rooms, different policies and additional supplements may apply.

PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

PETS:
Pets are allowed. Charges may be applicable.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
Children up to and including 11 years old stay for free when using an available extra bed.
People 12 years old and over stay for US$250 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.
