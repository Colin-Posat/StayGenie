HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Hotel Phoenix in ZermattLocated in the serene Wiesti quarter, Hotel Phoenix offers a blend of rustic charm and modern comfort. Cozy Rooms with Modern Amenities include flat-screen TVs, safes, and complimentary Wi-Fi in public areas. The use of wood, stone, and glass creates a unique ambiance for a peaceful night's sleep. Scenic Views and Relaxing Spaces allow guests to enjoy the Matterhorn vista and unwind in the bar or lounge. Wake up to a Delicious Buffet Breakfast before exploring Zermatt's picturesque surroundings. With its proximity to the train station and local shops, Hotel Phoenix is the ideal choice for your stay in Zermatt.
