HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Modern Accommodation near Public TransportationHotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Kayabacho is conveniently located just a 5-minute walk from Kayabacho Subway Station, making it easy for guests to explore the city.Convenient Amenities for a Comfortable StayGuests can enjoy free wired internet in their rooms, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The rooms are equipped with essential amenities such as a flat-screen TV, fridge, and electric kettle.Additional Services for a Hassle-Free ExperienceThe hotel offers services like luggage storage, a coin-operated launderette, and drink vending machines. Guests can also start their day with a simple breakfast buffet at the lobby.Book your stay at Hotel Villa Fontaine Tokyo-Kayabacho now for a convenient and comfortable experience.
