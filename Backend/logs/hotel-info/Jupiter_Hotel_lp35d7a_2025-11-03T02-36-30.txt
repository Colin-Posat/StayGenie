HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Unique Design and AmenitiesExperience the artistic flair of Jupiter Hotel with chalkboard doors and painted murals in every room. Enjoy environmentally friendly bath amenities and modern comforts like a 32-inch flat-screen TV.On-Site Dining and EntertainmentIndulge in delicious meals at DougFir Restaurant &amp; Lounge, offering a diverse menu for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Take advantage of happy hour specials and late-night dining options, all within the hotel premises.Convenient LocationLocated just minutes away from the Oregon Convention Center and a short drive to downtown Portland, Jupiter Hotel provides easy access to popular attractions like the Moda Center and Memorial Coliseum. Book now to experience the perfect blend of style, convenience, and entertainment.
