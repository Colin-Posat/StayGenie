GUEST SENTIMENT ANALYSIS:
What Guests Love:

• Limited parking

Category Ratings:
• Cleanliness: 7.6/10 - The majority of guests praised the hotel for its cleanliness, with some mentioning minor issues such as mold in the bathroom.
• Service: 7.9/10 - Customer service received positive feedback, with guests appreciating pleasant staff and seamless check-in experiences. However, there were some complaints about slow response times to maintenance issues.
• Location: 8.1/10 - The hotel's location was generally well-received for its proximity to key areas and attractions, although some mentioned noise disturbances and the need to drive to nearby eateries.
• Room Quality: 7.9/10 - Guests found the rooms to be comfortable and spacious, with notable features like anti-fog mirrors with Bluetooth speakers. However, some noted maintenance issues like non-functional bathroom lights and uncomfortable pillows.
• Amenities: 7.2/10 - Mixed feedback on amenities, with some guests enjoying features like a snack bar and friendly staff, while others mentioned issues like cold swimming pool water and the absence of spa services.
• Value for Money: 6.9/10 - Opinions on value for money were divided, with some considering the prices reasonable compared to downtown options, while others felt the services did not justify the cost.
• Food and Beverage: 7.3/10 - Food and beverage services were generally well-received, with positive comments on breakfast and restaurant options. However, a few guests desired more variety in the menu.
• Overall Experience: 8.1/10 - Most guests reported a positive overall experience, highlighting spacious rooms, comfortable beds, and convenient location. Recommendations to others were common.
