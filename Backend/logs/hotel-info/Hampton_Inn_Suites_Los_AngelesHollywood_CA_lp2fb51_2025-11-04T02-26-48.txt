• Room Quality: 7.5/10 - Rooms were generally comfortable and clean, although some reviews highlighted noise issues and maintenance problems.
• Amenities: 7.3/10 - Amenities provided were satisfactory but varied, with some positive comments about breakfast and pool, while others criticized the lack of variety and quality.
• Value for Money: 7.3/10 - Mixed feedback on the value for money, with some guests feeling the pricing was justified given services, while others found it expensive for what was offered.
• Food and Beverage: 6.4/10 - Food and beverage services received mixed reviews, with complaints about breakfast quality and variety, along with some positive mentions about included breakfast and dining services.
• Overall Experience: 7.7/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience, appreciating the staff friendliness and cleanliness, although with some reservations about noise and pricing.
