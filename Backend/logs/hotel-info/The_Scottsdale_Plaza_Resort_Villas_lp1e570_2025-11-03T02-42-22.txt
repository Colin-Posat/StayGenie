Scottsdale Plaza Resort is committed to providing its guests and associates with a smoke-free environment. By acknowledging the foregoing, you agree to waive any future claims related to the presence of the sensor in a room you may book. Tampering with the sensor is strictly prohibited.

HOTEL POLICIES:
PARKING:
Free private parking is possible on site (reservation is not needed).

PETS:
Pets are allowed on request. Charges may be applicable.

INTERNET:
WiFi is available in all areas and is free of charge.

CHILDREN AND EXTRA BED POLICY:
Children of any age are allowed.
Children up to and including 2 years old stay for free when using an available cot.
Children up to and including 17 years old stay for free when using an existing bed.
People no matter the age stay for US$25 per person per night when using an available extra bed.
Any type of extra bed or child's cot/crib is upon request and needs to be confirmed by management.
Supplements are not calculated automatically in the total costs and will have to be paid for separately during your stay.

• Amenities: 6.9/10 - Satisfaction with amenities varied, with some guests enjoying the pool and gym, while others desired more services like spa facilities.
• Value for Money: 6.7/10 - Opinions on value for money were mixed, with some guests feeling the price was high for the services offered.
• Food and Beverage: 7.5/10 - Breakfast and restaurant services received positive feedback, although some guests suggested more variety.
• Overall Experience: 7.8/10 - Most guests had a positive overall experience and would recommend the hotel to others.
