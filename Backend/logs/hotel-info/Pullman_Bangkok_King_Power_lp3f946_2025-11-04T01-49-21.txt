HOTEL DESCRIPTION:
Luxurious Rooms and Free WiFiPullman Bangkok offers spacious and luxurious rooms with free WiFi throughout the property. The rooms feature modern furnishings, floor-to-ceiling windows, and luxurious marble bathrooms with rain showers. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV and a safe for your convenience.Convenient LocationSituated within walking distance of Phayathai Airport Rail Link and Victory Monument BTS Station, King Power Pullman Bangkok provides easy access to various popular shopping areas like MBK, Chatuchak Weekend Market, and Siam Square. The hotel is also just a 30-minute drive from Suvarnabhumi Airport, making it a convenient choice for travelers.Dining and Leisure FacilitiesIndulge in a culinary journey at Pullman Bangkok's 6 trendy dining venues offering a variety of Asian and international cuisine. Don't miss the international buffets at Cuisine Unplugged. After a day of exploring the city, unwind at the beautiful outdoor swimming pool or pamper yourself with a relaxing massage at the first-rate spa.Book your stay at Pullman Bangkok now to experience luxury and convenience in the heart of the city.
